A wasteland is a desolate biome added in 24w14potato. It generates in the Potato dimension.


== Description ==
No trees generate in the wasteland. Surface of this biome is covered in a variety of blocks, including peelgrass and corrupted peelgrass blocks, slime blocks, as well as green, gray and light gray blocks of potato peels. Brown and red mushrooms, potato flowers and normal potato plants grow on top of peelgrass blocks.
All water in wasteland, either naturally generated or placed by players, is poisonous: all entities in it (except toxifins and plaguewhales) are given the Poison effect for 2 seconds. Rain in wasteland has the same effect. However, this only applies to the potato dimension itself, as upon generating a single biome wasteland world, water and rain do not have this effect in the Overworld. Water placed inside of a cauldron is not poisonous.
Water that naturally generates in the wasteland biome is replaced with waterlogged light blocks, causing the water to glow.
Many terrain features are unique to wastelands. These include:

"Large potato columns" - columns of blocks of black potato peels, based on large basalt columns.

"Large potatostone" - a spire of terre de pomme with varying height, based on large dripstone.

"Small debris columns" - a small column of ancient debris.

"Twisted potato" - a powerful potato with weak roots growing from it.
