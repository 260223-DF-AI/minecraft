A version of Indev 0.31 was released on January 4, 2010, at 21:54 UTC. It was announced on The Word of Notch at 23:06 UTC, by which time the next version had already been released.
This is the first version released in 2010.


== Additions ==


=== Gameplay ===
Sounds

Added new sounds from the Freesound project.


== Changes ==


=== Gameplay ===
Hotbar

Added 99 torches to the hotbar.
Sounds

Switched to Paul Lamb's sound engine.
