v0.15.2 alpha is a minor update to Pocket Edition, Gear VR Edition, and the Windows 10 Edition Beta released on July 7-8, 2016 to fix some of the bugs and crashes in v0.15.1. For iOS, this includes bug fixes from 0.15.1.


== Fixes ==
10 issues fixed
From released versions before 0.15.0

MCPE-4267 – Slow game startup (it takes a while to show anything).
MCPE-8143 – Can click invisible cancel button during terrain generation.
MCPE-11279 – Activator rail sounds like wool.
MCPE-12153 – Villagers don't farm land but take up seeds and crops.
From the 0.15.0 development versions

MCPE-14665 – Quitting in a 2 block tall space will move the player west when the world is reloaded.
MCPE-14882 – Sometimes, launching the player on a sticky piston with a slime block makes the player glitch through blocks.
MCPE-15104 – Player standing on the first block in a horizontal line of blocks attached to a sticky piston teleports to the end of the line when the sticky piston is activated.
From the 0.15.0

MCPE-15892 – Equipping a horse immediately after taming it can crash the game;
Other

If your Realm expires, you should now get a screen asking if you want to renew.
Fixed a crash on Android when it sent previous offline data.
