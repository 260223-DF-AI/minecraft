Beta 1.2.13.11 is the fifth beta version for Bedrock Edition 1.2.13, released on March 20, 2018, which adds an option to swap A/B and X/Y buttons on controllers and fixes bugs.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Settings

Added two options to swap A/B and X/Y buttons on controllers, which can be found in Controller Settings.


== Fixes ==
Fixed the cause of several crashes.
When Experimental Gameplay is enabled, tridents can now be enchanted.
Mobs will once again not spawn on transparent blocks like glass and fences.
Top snow falling on a top snow block no longer loses one layer.
Ladders can once again be placed in the same block occupied by a player.
