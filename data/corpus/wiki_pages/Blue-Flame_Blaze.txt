The Blue-Flame Blaze (, Aoi Hono no Bureizu?) was a super-hot blaze encountered by Nico White's group. It acted as a minor antagonist in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Blue-Flame Blaze resembled a regular blaze, but blue. It had a Mod Rune on its forehead, indicating that it possessed the Inferno ability.


== Powers and abilities ==
Like others blazes, the Blue-Flame Blaze could fly, and had the ability to shoot fire.
It possessed the Mod Power "Inferno", causing its body to be heated to extreme temperatures. This causes the fire it shot to be similarly hot, and causes the Blue-Flame Blaze to constantly exude an aura of extreme heat, enough to melt Nether Bricks and instantly incinerate arrows. Its body temperature was even hotter than lava, causing it to be cooled when covered in it.


== Biography ==
The Blue-Flame Blaze spawned from the Monster Spawner that Nico White's party was using to farm Blaze Rod. Its heat melted their defenses, and the blaze began to attack them, following them out of the Nether Fortress. Eventually, Nico came up with a plan to defeat it, and lured it to where Platinum was waiting; she broke a block above its head, releasing lava that flowed over it. The lava was enough to cool the blaze's temperature to a reasonable level, so Nico crafted the Frostgate, freezing the blaze in ice and killing it.
