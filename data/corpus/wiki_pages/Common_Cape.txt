The Common Cape is a cape given to all players who use their Microsoft account to log into Bedrock Edition at any point from May 6, 2025 to May 5, 2026.


== Appearance ==
The cape design is based on the grass block. A green semicircle with a darker gradient towards the center appears at the top of the cape, representing the grass. A brown base with silver dots represents the dirt.


== Distribution ==
Players who used their Microsoft account to log into Bedrock Edition on any platform during the distribution period to received this cape in both Bedrock Edition and Java Edition. A Microsoft account is required to obtain this cape, even on platforms where using one is not required to play the game.


== History ==


== Trivia ==
This is the first cape (excluding the duplicate version of the Pan Cape) to be assigned of a different rarity other than Legendary.
