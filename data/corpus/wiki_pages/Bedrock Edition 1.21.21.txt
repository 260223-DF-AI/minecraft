1.21.21 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on August 14, 2024 for all platforms except Windows and Nintendo Switch, August 15, 2024 for Windows, and August 20, 2024 for Nintendo Switch which fixes bugs and improves reliability of connecting to Realms.


== Fixes ==
4 issues fixed
From released versions before v1.21.0

MCPE-93646 – When using a dispenser with glowstone in it at a player, it will add the glowstone to the player's helmet inventory slot.
MCPE-151620 – Freezing/drowning effect does not stop when exiting powder snow/water by sleeping in a bed if the powder snow/water is one block above the ground.
MCPE-176316 – Riding horses, donkeys, or mules teleports the player.
From released versions before v1.21.21

MCPE-185246 – Realms, Marketplace and other services inaccessible.
Others

Improved reliability of connecting to Realms.
