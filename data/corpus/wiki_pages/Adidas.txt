Adidas AG is a German multinational apparel and footwear corporation. On several occasions, the company has collaborated with Minecraft, releasing specialized items and releases on the Marketplace.


== Products ==


== Marketplace releases ==


=== adidas Adventurers ===

Released on June 3, 2025, adidas Adventurers is an add-on dedicated to various Adidas products, providing various buffs such as haste.


=== Three Stripes City ===

Released on November 18, 2025, Three Stripes City is a dynamic world themed around Adidas.
