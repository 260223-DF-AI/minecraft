This page lists raw vanilla configurations for each biome with Vibrant Visuals, including atmospherics, color grading, and lighting.


== Atmospherics ==
Some values are key framed. This means that there are multiple values depending on the time of day, where 0 is noon, 0.25 sunset, 0.5 midnight, 0.75 sunrise, and 1 the next noon. Values are linearly interpolated between key frames, meaning that they are constantly changing.
The images on the right are all settings applied to the same spot for comparison with default color grading and atmospherics; in-game it will appear differently due to other effects. Even if some biomes only use unique zenith colors at night, for example, the atmospherics during the entire day appear differently due to blending and interpolation. The images show snapshots of the most notable combinations; not all are shown.
