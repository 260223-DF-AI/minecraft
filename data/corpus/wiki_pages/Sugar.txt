== Usage ==


=== Crafting ingredient ===


=== Brewing ingredient ===


=== Horses ===
Sugar can be fed to horses to heal 1HP, speed growth by 30 seconds, and increase taming probability by 3%. In Java Edition, due to a bug, skeleton horses (in their hostile state; being ridden by skeletons) can also be fed sugar.

== Trivia ==
According to Education Edition, the chemical formula of Minecraft sugar is C6H12O6; while this could make it any number of simple sugars, it confirms it is not actually sucrose (common table sugar).
