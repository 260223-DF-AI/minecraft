The Curve-Ball Ghast (変化球のガスト, Henkakyū no Gasuto?) was a wild ghast encountered by Nico White's group. It acted as a minor antagonist in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Curve-Ball Ghast largely resembled a regular ghast. However, it had a Mod Rune on one of its right tentacles, indicating that it possessed the Curve Ball ability.


== Powers and abilities ==
Like others ghasts, the Curve-Ball Ghast could fly, and had the ability to shoot fireballs.
It possessed the Mod Power "Curve Ball", allowing it to remotely control the trajectory of the fireballs it shot out. It used this control to make its fireballs curve around their targets, making them impossible to reflect back.


== Biography ==
The Curve-Ball Ghast ran into Nico White's party while flying through the Nether. It immediately attacked them, successfully knocking Gray off the bridge they were building and attempting to do the same to Nico. As the Curve-Ball Ghast used its Mod Power to repeatedly hit Nico, it was hit from underneath by one of Gray's arrows, allowing Nico to get close and attack it with his sword, killing it.
