Many historical versions in the Minecraft Launcher are labeled as "old_alpha", despite not all actually being from the Alpha development phase (though this may be due to a more general use of the software development term "alpha"). This term may refer to:

Pre-Classic, the first private development phase of Minecraft.
Classic, originally named "Minecraft Alpha".
Indev, where Minecraft was now "in development".
Infdev, infinite world generation development.
Alpha, the development phase of Minecraft preceding Beta.
