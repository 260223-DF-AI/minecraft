19w12b is the twenty-eighth snapshot for Java Edition 1.14, released on March 21, 2019, which fixes some bugs.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Glass

Mobs no longer suffocate in glass.


=== Mobs ===
Iron golems

Spawn chance has been slightly increased:
Now requires 5 villagers to meet instead of 10.
Villagers that are recently hurt by zombies have a higher chance of spawning an iron golem.
Villagers

No longer look at players in Spectator mode.
