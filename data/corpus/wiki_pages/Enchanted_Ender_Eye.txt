The Enchanted Ender Eyes, or Enchanted Eyes of Ender, are a set of five enchanted eyes of ender, pivotal to the second season of Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Appearance ==
Enchanted Ender Eyes look like their non-enchanted versions before Texture Update.


== Properties ==
They don't do anything on their own, but collecting all five enchanted eyes and placing them in a circle will open a portal from the Mini-Series overworld to the game overworld.


== History ==
It is unknown who or when enchanted the five eyes of ender, but since then Zeus has hidden them in various places in the Greek Isles that Poseidon cannot enter and has ordered various greek myths to guard them. After Poseidon began sponsoring CommuniKate's show, and the cast of the show, along with CommuniKate and Zed, were teleported to the Greek Isles, Poseidon forced CommuniKate, Zed, Durango and Quinn to bring him all the enchanted eyes for a short amount of time in exchange for the lives of the rest of the show's participants. The heroes find and bring all the eyes to Poseidon, but he tricked them and all the other participants of the show will still be eaten by the Kraken. However, all the heroes escaped and began to try to prevent Poseidon from taking over the Overworld, which attracted the attention of Zeus, who took the eyes of nder from Poseidon.
