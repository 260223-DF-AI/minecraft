15w35d is the seventeenth snapshot for Java Edition 1.9, released on August 28, 2015 to fix some crashes that occurred in 15w35c.


== Fixes ==
3 issues fixed
From the previous development version

MC-87563 – Punching a wolf crashes game.
MC-87567 – Riding a pig with a carrot on a stick and clicking the right side of the mouse crashes the game.
MC-87569 – Killing a named silverfish crashes game.
