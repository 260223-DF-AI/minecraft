Powder may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Items ===
Blaze Powder - an item crafted from blaze rods used mainly in brewing and crafting eyes of ender.
Gunpowder - an item dropped from creepers, used to craft TNT and in brewing.
Dyes have an internal name "item.dyePowder.name", as shown by a glitch in early Pocket Edition and the statistics in early Java Edition


=== Blocks ===
Concrete Powder - a block crafted from sand, gravel and dye that turns into concrete when it touches water.
Powder Snow - a block variant of snow.


== Minecraft Dungeons ==
 Shock Powder - an artifact used to stun mobs
