A potato stem is a joke log-type block, only present in 24w14potato.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Potato stems can be broken using any tool, but an axe is the fastest. Additionally, swords speed up the breaking time.


=== Natural generation ===
Potato stems generate naturally as part of potato trees, as well as in potato villages and potato mineshafts.


== Usage ==


=== Crafting ingredient ===

Potato stems cannot be stripped with an axe and cannot be crafted into wood or hyphae, unlike all other log-like blocks.
