The Armorsmith is a blacksmith specializing in the forging of armor, appearing in Lego Minecraft and several books based off it.


== Appearance ==
The Armorsmith is a human with tan skin, brown hair, and navy eyes with eyebrows. His wardrobe consists of golden goggles on his forehead, periwinkle shirt, brown overalls, and dark gray gloves. On his back is long, braided hair with a yellow hair tie.


== Personality ==

An adventurer at heart, the Armorsmith is a hardworking individual, as dedicated to his craft as he is eager to explore.
