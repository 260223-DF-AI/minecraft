The Halloween Charity Skin Pack, also referred to as Halloween Skin Pack, is a skin pack that contains 55 Halloween-themed skins designed by various indie game developers. It was available exclusively in Xbox 360 Edition for a limited time between October 26 and November 26, 2012. All proceeds from the skin pack, $770,448 in total, were donated to several charities, including Macmillan Cancer Support, Child's Play, SANDS Lothians and Block by Block.
Some skins featured in this skin pack were later re-released as part of the Halloween Mash-up.
