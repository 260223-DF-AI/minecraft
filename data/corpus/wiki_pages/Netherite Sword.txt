A netherite sword is a late-game variant of sword obtained by upgrading a diamond sword using a netherite ingot and netherite upgrade at a smithing table. It is the highest tier of sword, dealing more damage than a diamond sword and having higher durability.


== Obtaining ==


=== Upgrading ===
Like other diamond items, a diamond sword can be upgraded to a netherite sword, increasing its durability and attack damage, as well as granting resistance to fire and lava when dropped as an item.


== Usage ==


=== Breaking ===

A sword can also be used to destroy certain blocks 50% quicker, sometimes much quicker than with fists. Using a sword to destroy any block that doesn't break instantly by hand decreases its durability by 2; this includes bamboo, despite that the sword is the fastest tool for breaking it.
If a sword is enchanted with Silk Touch, either using Creative or commands, cobwebs hit by the sword drop the cobwebs themselves instead of the usual string, because a sword is classified as the proper tool for cobwebs.


==== Creative mode ====
In Creative mode, swords are unable to break blocks. However, they can still break placed entities such as minecarts, paintings, item frames,‌[Java Edition  only] and armor stands.


=== Attacking ===

A netherite sword is used to deal damage to mobs and entities by attacking them. The netherite sword has 8HP base damage in Java Edition and 9HP base damage in Bedrock Edition, and can be enchanted to deal more damage. === Repairing ===


==== Combining ====


==== Anvil repair ====

A netherite sword can be repaired in an anvil by adding netherite ingots with each ingot restoring 25% the sword's maximum durability, rounded down. Two netherite swords can also be combined in an anvil. Both methods preserve the netherite sword's enchantments.
