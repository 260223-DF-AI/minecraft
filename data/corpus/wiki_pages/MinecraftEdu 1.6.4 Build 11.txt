1.6.4 Build 11 is the eleventh build for MinecraftEdu 1.6.4, released on March 7, 2014. It is based on Java Edition 1.6.4.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Line wrap to MOTD and Welcome Message textareas.


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Welcome message color changed to gold.


== Fixes ==
3 bugs fixed

Exp bar was being shown even if some GUI was open.
Students weren't able to click containers on top of disallow blocks.
Both online and local mod lists didn't update if you deleted a mod when the Launcher was open. It still showed you had a mod installed even when you deleted it.
