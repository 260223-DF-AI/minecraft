WildBrain Studios is the in-house television studio arm of the Canadian entertainment company WildBrain based in Vancouver, which was originally established in 2016 as DHX Studios. The company is currently producing the upcoming Minecraft animated series with Mojang Studios for the Netflix streaming service. Prior to being involved with Minecraft, they were famously known for producing children's shows, including Blaze and the Monster Machines and The Mr. Peabody & Sherman Show, the latter of which was also a Netflix exclusive.
