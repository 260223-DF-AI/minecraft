Sacha is a character in the Minecraft Mini-Series and part of Team Tundra with Rudi.


== Appearance ==
Sacha has short black hair tied up, blue eyes with a scar across one, and copper colored skin. She wears a blue jumper, a green coat or jerkin laced up or fastened the middle, a white shawl, and brown gloves, boots, and belt.


== Biography ==
Before the series began, Sacha was a tundra hunter.
Sacha was once teleported from her home along with the others by CommuniKate using a command block on the show. She and Rudi won some challenges, and in the final challenge, she with other prisoners was saved from obsidian prison and they sabotaged their way back home, but were teleported to the Greek Isles. Poseidon teleported Sacha to a sand pillar in the Kraken Sea along with the other heroes, and she spent most of the season there. Then at the end, all the heroes escape and try to stop Poseidon. After a while, Zeus comes, defeats Poseidon, and asks heroes to help him. After helping the god, Sacha and Rudi apparently returned home.


== Personality ==
Sacha is serious, ambitious, and likes to do things effectively. She likes the snow and tundra, but dislikes creepers, distractions, and danger.


== Relationships ==


=== Rudi ===
Him and Sacha are teammates, always working together to win.


== Trivia ==
Sacha is based on the "Tundra Hunter" from Mattel which in turn was based on a skin by the same name in the Biome Settlers Skin Pack 1.
