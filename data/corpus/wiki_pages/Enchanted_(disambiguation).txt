Enchanted may refer to:


== Minecraft ==
Enchanting, which can make items enchanted.
Enchanted Book, used on an anvil with an item to enchant it.
Enchanted Golden Apple, a variant of golden apple.
Enchanted, a featured server.

Enchanted Golden Poisonous Potato, a variant of golden poisonous potato which also grants Luck and Bad Luck.


== Minecraft: Story Mode ==
Enchanted Flint and Steel, a flint and steel able of lighting tall fire and activating portals.
Iron Sword, which can be enchanted.
Diamond Sword, which can be enchanted.


== Minecraft Dungeons ==
Enchantments, a mechanic used to enhance armor and weapons and/or add properties to them.
Enchanted mobs, a type of mobs which come with enchantments applied.
Enchanted Grass, the artifact used to summon enchanted sheep.
Enchanted Sheep, a summonable pet with three random variants.
