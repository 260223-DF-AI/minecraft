Fallen trees are features that resemble a tree lying on the ground after falling over.


== Generation ==
Fallen trees consist of a single upright log, making the stump, and a log (composed of a row of log blocks) lying on its side, making the trunk. The trunk is often found 1–2 blocks from the stump. 
The stump often has vines growing on its sides. 1–2 red or brown mushrooms can sometimes be found growing on the trunk.
Only oak, spruce, birch, tall birch, and jungle trees can generate as fallen trees.
Fallen trees can be found in all biomes where their standing tree variants grow, except the following:

Meadow
Bamboo Jungle
River
Grove
Flower Forest
Has fallen birch trees but not fallen oak trees.
