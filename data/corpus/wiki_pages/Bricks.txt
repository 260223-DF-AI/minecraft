Bricks are a decorative building block.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Bricks can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, it drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Bricks naturally generate in ocean ruins, plains village armorer houses, and trail ruins.


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==
Bricks have the same blast resistance as other stone-based blocks.
