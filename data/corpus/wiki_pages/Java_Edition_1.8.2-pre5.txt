1.8.2-pre5 is the fifth pre-release for Java Edition 1.8.2, released on January 28, 2015.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Beacons

Now ignore bedrock above them, when determining a clear view to the sky.


=== Mobs ===
 Mooshrooms

Changed Mushroom Model.
