1.95 was an update for PlayStation 4 Edition released on September 9, 2019, which fixed bugs.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Wood

Can now be used to craft planks (MCCE-10011).
Redstone

Can now be placed on glass (MCCE-10012).


== Fixes ==
5 issues fixed

Fix for crash when pushing a button into the top of a door.
Fix for a crash when navigating the fireworks crafting menu.
Fixed stone mason having wrong price for chiseled bricks (MCCE-9978).
Fixed rail blocks not dropping when the block below them is destroyed (MCCE-9653).
Fix for stone masons wanting glazed terracotta instead of offering it.
