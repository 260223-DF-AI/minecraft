Mojang shorts are short videos posted to platforms such as YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram, typically taking the form of a machinima or animation. They can range wildly in subject, from promoting updates or game drops, to being renditions of popular memes of the time or original comedy skits featuring Minecraft characters.

== Trivia ==
In several shorts, many villagers appear, consistently without their robes.
Throughout multiple videos, characters are depicted speaking, without any consistent voice.
WHAT WOULD YOU DO?, Efe appears to have a voice, speaking with a masculine, young voice. In another short, FIRST TIME IN THE DEEP DARK, Efe is depicted with a gruff voice.
