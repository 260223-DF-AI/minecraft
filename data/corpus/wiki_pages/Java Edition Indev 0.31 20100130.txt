A version of Indev 0.31 was released on January 30, 2010, at 01:09 UTC. It was announced on The Word of Notch at 01:22 UTC.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
 Feather

Dropped 0–2 by mobs with the "Beast Boy" skin upon death.
 Sulphur

Dropped 0–2 by mobs with the "Black Steve" skin upon death.
 String

Dropped 0–2 by mobs with the "Steve" skin upon death.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Plants

Now display similarly to items in the inventory.
 Gear

Now visible in the inventory.
 TNT

Crafted with sand and sulphur.

 Stone slabs

Crafted with cobblestone.


=== Items ===
General

Animated textures (currently fire and gears) now display in the inventory.
 Arrows

Crafted with stick, iron ingot and feather.

 Bow

Crafted with string and stick.

 Golden axes, pickaxes and shovels

Crafted with gold ingot and stick.


=== Mobs ===
 Mob

Now has a third skin, commonly known as "Beast Boy".


=== World generation ===
Re-added the infinite lava sea at the bottom of the map.
This time it is infinitely deep.


== Removals ==
Score

Removed the score in the top right of the screen.
Statistics

Player statistics are now removed from the inventory.


== Fixes ==
4 bugs fixed
From 20100122-2251

Fixed an infinite reach bug.
From 20100128-2200

Fixed graphical glitch with using swords.
From 20100129-1447

Dropped stacks of items now smelt as separate items (previously only 1 item would smelt and the rest disappeared).
Right-clicking on stacked crafted items takes out all of them instead of just one.
From 20100129-2332

Mobs can be hit through blocks


== Bugs ==
3 bugs reported

The number on hotbar items also pulses when new items are picked up.
The X-ray effect caused by having the player's head in a block is now permanent as long as the player stays in a block.
Bows disappear from the hotbar when used without arrows.
The screen appears as black on MacOS.
