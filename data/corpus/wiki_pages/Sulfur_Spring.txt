A sulfur spring is a feature consisting of a mound of sulfur on the surface of the Overworld above sulfur caves.


== Description ==
Sulfur springs generate only above sulfur caves on the surface in any overworld biome. They are small, volcano-shaped common features, mostly made of sulfur blocks with a pool of water at the top containing potent sulfur and magma blocks in a configuration suitable for geysers.
They come in 10 variants across four sizes, with rarity increasing as size increases.
