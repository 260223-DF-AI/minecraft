Minecraft Live Official Trailer - September 2025 is the announcement trailer for Minecraft LIVE  September 2025.


== Summary ==
A copper golem is seen taking a redstone torch out of a copper chest with Alex seen behind the chest directing the copper golem forward, with many other copper golems carrying various different redstone-related items and a bucket of axolotl. Eventually the camera zooms out and shows many different copper golems on the same path. At some point the copper golems make it outside, where they pass a wandering trader and trader llama along with various other mobs. We then see the copper golems walking along a mountainside where they pass by a rabbit and an allay carring a slime block. The copper golems are then seen trudging across jagged peaks with a large group of goats. In a badlands biome we see Zuri mining terracotta with the copper golems in the background. A happy ghast carrying a boat with a copper golem inside floats by, and Zuri stops to admire it. The camera then shows the copper golem with the redstone torch and a copper golem with clay riding in a boat carried by a happy ghast. The copper golem drops the torch and then dives after it, plunging into the ocean and landing on a shipwreck. The copper golem begins to oxidize and is confused by it. The copper golem emerges with a squid on its head, shakes it off and continues on. The copper golems then reach a jungle and encounter three frogs, one of each variant. The copper golem continues to oxidize and stops for a second, before being pushed to continue by the other copper golems. 
The copper golems reach an intersection directed by an iron golem. The copper golem then completly oxidizes and turns into a copper golem statue, holding up the entire line of copper golems and causing them all to fall over. Steve rushes in with a copper axe to revive the copper golem, who is then waxed by Efe. Once revived, the copper golem and the line of copper golems behind it continue. Once they reach a desert all of the copper golems start depositing their items into chests. The camera zooms to reveal all of the mobs featured in the trailer earlier and eventually, Kai, Sunny, Ari, Zuri, and Makena building a huge red wall using scaffolding. Once finished, Makena takes the redstone torch out of a chest, pats the copper golem on its head, and places the torch down. The torch activates a long line of redstone dust and redstone repeaters, which the camera follows. As the camera follows the line of redstone, it passes by many mobs. Eventually, the end of the line is reached, and the Minecraft Live logo is powered. All of the mobs and players celebrate as fireworks go off, ending the trailer.

== Trivia ==
Many past trailers and short films are referenced during the trailer.
The wandering trader and trader llama was a reference to A Wandering Trader Springs to Life!
The allay with a slime block, the goats, and the frogs marching were a reference to the Minecraft Live 2022 trailer.
The camel and snifflet were a reference to A Tale of Two Friends.
The heroes constructing a sign of the logo is a callback to Minecraft Live is back for 2023!
