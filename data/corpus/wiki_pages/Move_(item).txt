Move is a joke item added in 26w14a.


== Obtaining ==
It is automatically obtained and appears in the hotbar when the player starts up a world in the Herdcraft Update. It also appears in the hotbar after the player dies.


== Usage ==
Right-clicking living blocks with this item will add them to a group; left-clicking on a block will send the selected living blocks to that block. If the player sends multiple living blocks to the same block, the living blocks will make a stack.
