Weakness is an effect that decreases attack damage. It is required to cure zombie villagers.


== Effect ==
Melee damage inflicted by the affected entity is reduced by 4HP  level in Java Edition. Negative levels increase melee damage dealt.
In Bedrock Edition, melee damage under the Weakness effect can be found through the equation BaseDamage0.8level+0.8level10.4 where BaseDamage is the default attack damage with whatever weapon or tool is used to attack.
Attacks that deal 0HP or less due to Weakness will fail to connect entirely, making them do no knockback. However, by use of damage-increasing enchantments such as Sharpness, Smite, Bane of Arthropods, or Impaling, attacks will always connect. This effect is also used to cure zombie villagers, preceding the golden apple. The effect can be removed by drinking milk. In  Minecraft Education and Bedrock Edition when education options are enabled, drinking elixir also removes Weakness.


== Exceptions ==
Weakness does not affect the damage of spear charge attacks, or the damage of ranged attacks.


== Causes ==


== Immune mobs ==
Withers, ender dragons, and parched are immune to Weakness.
Any mob that cannot attack or only attacks with projectiles is unaffected by Weakness.
