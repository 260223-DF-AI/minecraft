A grave is an item introduced in 25w14craftmine. A poppy and skeleton skull is seen in the items texture.


== Obtaining ==
The grave is only obtainable via the /give command.


== Usage ==
The grave only serves as the icon of the "Oops" advancement, and does nothing else.
