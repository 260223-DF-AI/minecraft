A Minecraft Tale of Two Villages is a trailer promoting the Minecraft Education map of the same name


== Summary ==
The video opens on the border between a mainland of villagers and a small island with illagers and a ravager as both peoples argue. In order to stop the fight, Agent in between digs a canal, allowing the illager island to dry up a little and connect to the mainland as a metaphor of unity. They continue digging between a fisherman and a pillager fighting over a fish, dropping it onto the canal as other fish follow. Back onto the battlefield, a cleric throws toward the illagers their iron shovel which, with Agent's touch, transforms into three sunflowers that land into the ravager's mouth. On a beetroot farm where a farmer and a pillager are next to, Agent harvests a beetroot and gives it to the farmer, who in turns gifts it to the pillager. They then move on to an apple tree where a nitwit struggles to reach the apples. To help them, Agent gets them on a ravager's back, feeding it an apple, before the video shifts to a demonstration of the map.
