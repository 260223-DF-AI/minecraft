Potato may refer to:


== Minecraft ==
Potato, an item that can be eaten by the player, used to breed pigs, or smelted into a baked potato
Baked Potato, an item that restores more hunger and saturation than a raw potato
Poisonous Potato, an poisonous item that rarely drops when harvesting a potato crop


=== Jokes ===
24w14potato is a Java Edition snapshot, released as an April Fools' joke for 2024. The snapshot adds many potato-related features.
