A version of Indev 0.31 was released on January 7, 2010, at 18:51 UTC, which added level themes. It was announced on The Word of Notch at 18:52 UTC.


== Additions ==


=== World generation ===
World options

Level theme (normal, hell).
Normal: The default map theme. The map's contents are based off the level type option. The weather is partly cloudy and trees generate.
Hell: Features significantly less lighting during the day. All surface water is replaced with lava. Mushrooms are abundant on the surface.[verify for Java Edition] Surrounding water also becomes lava.


== Changes ==


=== World generation ===
General

Deep floating maps now have layers of islands.
Flowers no longer generate naturally.
Fog is now darker in darker areas.
Mipmapping is now enabled.


== Bugs ==
2 bugs reported

Water texture is glitched by mipmapping.
Looking at trees and looking down on islands drop performance.
