Minecraft Sticker Pack is a sticker pack for iOS and iPadOS developed by Mojang Studios, used in the iMessage service which adds 25 Minecraft stickers for users to use.


== Purchase and availability ==
The Minecraft Sticker Pack is currently available in the App Store for US$1.99.

== Stickers ==
These stickers are listed in the order they appear in the pack and store page with the official name and a content description. Both the animated and static versions are listed besides each other. The static versions have a white outline and loose some of the intended meaning due to the lack of animation.
