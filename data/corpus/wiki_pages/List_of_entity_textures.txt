This page serves as a compact list of all unique mob and entity textures or skins which have ever existed in officially released versions of Minecraft. For an overview on when each texture was changed, see the appropriate History of textures subpages.
Original texture files for Java Edition can be downloaded with latest client.jar, and the entire default resource pack for Bedrock Edition can be downloaded from https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/addons.
