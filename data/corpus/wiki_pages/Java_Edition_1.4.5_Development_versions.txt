== 1.4.5 (pre-release) ==
Pre-release 1.4.5 is the first and only pre-release for Java Edition 1.4.5, released on November 16, 2012.
This pre-release was reuploaded on November 19 to fix MC-2497, and this reupload proceeded to become the full release of 1.4.5.


=== Fixes ===
11 bugs fixed

Fixed missing localization in editing signs UI.
Fixed worn heads clipping through hat layer.
Fixed dispensers firing towards one side.
Fixed wolves shaking fur dry while dying.
Fixed weird random flames from torches and spawners.
Fixed spawning baby wolves from wild ones.
Fixed spawning baby villagers creating farmer baby villagers only.
Fixed IPv6 servers crashing the client.
Fixed the last page of /help missing.
Fixed players not being able to join to LAN worlds.
Fixed entities teleporting to and from origin in multiplayer.
