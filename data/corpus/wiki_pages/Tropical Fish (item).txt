Tropical fish is a food item that cannot be cooked.


== Obtaining ==


=== Mob loot ===
Java Edition:Bedrock Edition:


=== Fishing ===

Tropical fish can be obtained through fishing, with a 1.7% chance per attempt. See fishing for more details.
Tropical fish cannot be caught when fishing in jungle biome and variants.‌[Bedrock Edition  only]


== Usage ==


=== Food ===
Tropical fish restores 1 () hunger and 0.2 saturation.


=== Dolphins ===
A dolphin can be fed tropical fish to increase its trust of the player and cause it to interact with the player more often. However, unlike most other animal mobs, this does not cause them to breed. Additionally, tropical fish can be used to make baby dolphins grow up faster by 10% of the remaining time.
Dolphins swim to the nearest chest in a shipwreck or underwater ruin after they are fed tropical fish. If the chest in the nearest structure is broken, they swim to another structure with a chest.


=== Trading ===


=== Wolves ===
Tropical fish can be used to feed wolves, healing them by 2HP. However, in Bedrock Edition, unlike other wolf food, tropical fish cannot be used to speed up the growth of baby wolves nor used to breed them and can only be fed to a wolf that is not at full health.
