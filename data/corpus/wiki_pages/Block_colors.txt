=== Constant colors ===

== Biome colors ==


=== Grass colors ===
These colors apply to the following blocks:

Grass Block
Short Grass
Tall Grass
Fern
Large Fern
Potted Fern
Bush
Sugar Cane
Pink Petals (stem only)
Wildflowers (stem only)


=== Foliage colors ===
These colors apply to the following blocks:

Oak Leaves
Jungle Leaves
Acacia Leaves
Dark Oak Leaves
Mangrove Leaves
Vines


=== Dry foliage colors ===
These colors apply to the following blocks:

Leaf Litter


=== Water colors ===
These colors apply only to water, and not to its particles.
