1.18.30.0 (Windows, macOS, iOS, iPadOS) or 1.18.30.1 (ChromeOS, Android) is the second beta version for Education Edition 1.18.32, released on June 16, 2022. The update brought features from Bedrock Edition 1.18.30 to Education Edition and added Android, iPhone, and iPod Touch support.


== Additions ==


=== General ===

Added features from Bedrock Edition 1.18.30.
Added Android, iPhone, and iPod Touch support.
