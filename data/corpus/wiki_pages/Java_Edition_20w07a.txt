20w07a is the second snapshot for Java Edition 1.16, released on February 14, 2020, which adds piglins, baby hoglins and some changes to endermen and beacons.
This snapshot contains a bug which causes all villagers with saved memory disappear when upgrading to it, which was fixed in the following snapshot.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
Spawn eggs

 Piglin spawn egg
Spawns piglins.


=== Mobs ===
 Hoglins

Added baby hoglins.
 Piglins

Spawn in crimson forests and nether wastes biomes.
Randomly spawns with golden sword or crossbow.
Adults are hostile; babies are passive.
Don't attack players wearing golden armor.
Adult piglins turn aggressive when a player is mining blocks of gold or opening chests, even if they are wearing gold armor.
The player can barter with them by right-clicking with gold ingots or drop them on the ground, which makes them drop at least one item or block.
Common barters (10109 or about 9.17%) are 14 soul sand, 14 brown mushrooms, 14 red mushrooms, 14 crimson fungi, 14 nether bricks, 38 flint, or 412 rotten flesh.
Uncommon barters (5109 or about 4.59%) are 1 shroomlight, 1 fire charge, 412 gravel, 25 raw porkchops, 27 leather, or 12 warped fungi.
Rare barters (2109 or about 1.83%) are 24 glowstone dust, 13 magma cream, or 24 ender pearls.
Very rare barters (1109 or about 0.92%) are 1 warped nylium, 14 nether quartz, or 1 obsidian.
Attack adult hoglins sometimes.
Baby piglins do not attack and adult piglins do not attack baby hoglins.
Attack wither skeletons.
Avoid soul fire, soul fire torches, soul fire lanterns, and zombie pigmen.
Prefer to hunt and fight in groups.
Convert to a zombie pigman when in the Overworld.


=== General ===
Tags

Added block tag beacon_base_blocks.
Contains block of iron, block of gold, block of diamond, block of emerald, and block of netherite.
Controls what blocks can be used to build a beacon base.
Added block tag wall_post_override.
Contains lantern, soul fire lantern, torch, soul fire torch, redstone torch, and tripwire.
Controls what non-solid blocks can transform walls into their pillared block state.
Added block tag wither_summon_base_blocks.
Contains soul sand and soul soil.
Controls what blocks can be used to build a wither.
Added item tag beacon_payment_items.
Contains iron ingot, gold ingot, diamond, emerald and netherite ingot.
Controls what items can be used as payment in a beacon's GUI. This does not affect the items displayed by the beacon's GUI.
Text

Added text for translation string death.attack.magic.player which appears when a player dies to a potion of Harming after being attacked by a player. It now says "Player was killed by magic whilst trying to escape attacker".
Textures

Added the zombified piglin entity texture.
Added the leather, chainmail, iron, golden, diamond and netherite helmet textures used for piglins.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Beacons

Netherite blocks can be used as the base of a beacon and netherite ingots can be used as the fuel for a beacon.
Soul fire lanterns

Now have an animated texture.
Removed the wood colors from the texture.


=== Items ===
Spawn eggs

Changed the texture of the hoglin spawn egg.
Using a spawn egg on a mob that doesn't have a baby variant now spawns an adult of that mob type.


=== Mobs ===
Endermen

Can now pick up nylium, crimson roots, warped roots, and Nether fungi variants.
No longer pick up netherrack.
Hoglins

Attack players on sight.
Drop pork and sometimes leather.
Breed when fed crimson fungi.
Are hunted by adult piglins sometimes.
Are not hunted by baby piglins and baby hoglins are not hunted at all.
Move away from placed warped fungi.
Have new sounds.
Withers

Can now be summoned using soul soil in the base.


=== Non-mob entities ===
 Armor stands

No longer delete replaceable blocks such as snow, grass, fire etc. when placed inside them.


=== Gameplay ===
Advancements

Piglins and hoglins now count towards the Monster Hunter and Monsters Hunted advancements.


=== Commands ===
/clear

Now clears item in the inventory crafting grid.


=== General ===
Tags

Removed netherrack and added Nether fungi, crimson roots, warped roots, and nylium variants to the enderman_holdable block tag.
