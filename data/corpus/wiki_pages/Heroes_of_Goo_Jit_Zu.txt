Heroes of Goo Jit Zu is a toyline produced by Moose Toys, which has collaborated with Mojang Studios.


== Background ==

Heroes of Goo Jit Zu is a series of stretchable action figures, similar to Stretch Armstrong, with the characters having transparent insides exposing slime inside. The series began with its own roster of unique characters, but branched out to feature characters from other franchises, such as Sonic the Hedgehog, Toy Story, WWE, and One Piece.
