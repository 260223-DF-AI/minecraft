A version of Indev 0.31 was released on January 29, 2010, at 14:47 UTC. It was announced on The Word of Notch at 15:04 UTC, by which time the next version had already been released.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
 Stick

Used to craft tools.
Crafted with planks.


=== Gameplay ===
Crafting

Is now functional.
Smelting 

Done via throwing the item into fire or lava.
Currently the four recipes are:
Coal ore to 1–3 coal.
Iron ore to 1–3 iron ingots.
Gold ore to 1–3 gold ingots.
Emerald ore to 1–3 emeralds.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Block of emerald

Can now be crafted with 4 emeralds.

Block of gold

Can now be crafted with 4 gold ingots.

Block of iron

Can now be crafted with 4 iron ingots.

Torch

Can now be crafted with a stick and coal.


=== Items ===
General

Items now stack up to 64.
Now get picked up quicker and do not perish in fire.
Arrows

Can no longer be crafted.
Axes

Can now be crafted with 2 sticks and 3 planks, cobblestone, iron ingots, or emeralds.

 Flint and steel

Changed texture from .
Pickaxes

Can now be crafted with 2 sticks and 3 planks, cobblestone, iron ingots, or emeralds.

Shovels

Can now be crafted with 2 sticks and planks, cobblestone, iron ingot, or emerald.

Swords

Can now be crafted with 1 stick and 2 planks, cobblestone, iron ingots, or emeralds.

 Gold ingot

Changed texture from .
 Iron ingot

Changed texture from .


=== Mobs ===
  Mob

The Rana model and texture has been replaced with a new model with two separate skins, commonly known as "Steve" and "Black Steve".


=== World generation ===
Starting house

Removed the chests dedicated to TNT and cloth.
Removed all items from chests.


=== Gameplay ===
Hotbar

The player now spawns with a flint and steel.


=== General ===
Controls

Right-clicking an inventory slot now deposits a single item from a hovering stack.


== Fixes ==
1 bug fixed

The game's performance no longer drastically progressively degrades.


== Bugs ==
1 bug reported

Stone tools cannot be crafted.
