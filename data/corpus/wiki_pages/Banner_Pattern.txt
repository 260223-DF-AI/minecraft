Banner patterns are a set of 10 items used to apply unique designs to banners using a loom, and are freely reusable. Most can be crafted, but some are only found in structures or through other means.

== Usage ==


=== Loom ingredient ===
All banner patterns can be used in looms to add customization to banners. The pattern must be combined with 1 banner and 1 dye. Upon usage in the loom, the banner pattern is not consumed.
