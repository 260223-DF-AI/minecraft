Tobias Möllstam is a former front-end web developer of Mojang and managed most of the things that were directly related to the company and its websites. He is a longtime coder and designer for the web, but for the last few years he's been into game development which he studied at the University of Skövde. On November 6, 2014, he announced on Twitter that he was leaving Mojang.
He also has a LinkedIn profile.


== Work ==
Decadence: A total conversion of the Source engine where 4 players on 2 teams shoot each other.
Decadence Server Cloud: An auto-scaling solution for Decadence server owners to host Source games on the Amazon EC2 cloud.
Jordlingar: A contract job for The Museum of World Culture in Gothenburg. (video)
Presidentspelet: A 10-week game project at the University.
PiStats: World of Warcraft add-on that communicates raid stats in real-time to a website.
