An End stone brick wall is a decorative wall variant of End stone bricks that does not generate naturally and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
End stone brick walls can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.

=== Stonecutting ===


== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
End stone brick walls can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
