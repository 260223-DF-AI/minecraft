1.1.4 is an update for Pocket Edition and its derived editions which added new Marketplace content and fixes some bugs and crashes. While it was not the last update to 1.1, it was the last one before the beta builds of 1.2.0 started.


== Additions ==
Marketplace

Battle & Beasts Skin Pack.
Pet Skin Pack by Imagiverse.
Cyber Space Skin Pack by Irish.


== Fixes ==
Fixed lag on Android devices when accessing resource packs.
Fixed some crashes occurring when launching the game.
Fixed a crash when loading into a world while holding a compass or clock.
