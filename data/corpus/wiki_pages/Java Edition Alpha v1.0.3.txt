Alpha v1.0.3 is a version of Java Edition Alpha, released on July 7, 2010 to add new sounds and to change the behavior of mobs.


== Additions ==


=== Gameplay ===
Ambient sounds

Added 13 cave ambient sounds.


== Changes ==


=== Items ===
Diamond axe, hoe, pickaxe, shovel, and sword

Durability has been increased from 513 to 1025.


=== Mobs ===
General

Pathfinding has been improved on hills.
Only exception is for spiders.
Generally more convincing mob idling behavior.
They stand still more often, looking around at their surroundings.
Creepers

Added sounds.

Mob

No longer has movement AI, therefore it is unable to walk around. It can, however, still rise in water and look around.
Skeletons

Added sounds.

Spiders

Added sounds.

Zombies

Added sounds.


=== Gameplay ===
Third-person view

The player's body no longer slowly rotates to face forward while in third person view.
