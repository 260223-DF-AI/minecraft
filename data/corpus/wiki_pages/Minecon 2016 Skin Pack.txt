MINECON 2016 Skin Pack is a celebratory skin pack for MINECON 2016 that includes 14 skins. It includes all skins that were previously part of the Minecon 2015 Skin Pack and was available for free until October 3, 2016 on Legacy Console Editions and October 7, 2016 on Bedrock Edition.
