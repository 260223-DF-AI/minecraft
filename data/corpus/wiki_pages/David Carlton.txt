David Carlton, also known as CornerHard, is a lead software engineer at Mojang Studios. He was a developer of the Bedrock Edition software. He is now the technical lead for the Realms client.

== Trivia ==
David regularly streams Minecraft on Twitch where he plays with viewers in a realms server. He mainly does this to playtest realms and interact with the community.
