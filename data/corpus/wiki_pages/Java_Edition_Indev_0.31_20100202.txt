== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
 Lava

Pushes out items.
No longer generates on the surface of Normal themed worlds.
 Ores

No longer requires a pickaxe to break the block, although a pickaxe breaks it much faster.
Once again possible to mine ores with anything and not just with a pickaxe.
Mineral blocks (iron, gold, diamond) are treated similarly to their corresponding ores.
 Stone

No longer requires a pickaxe to break the block, although a pickaxe breaks it much faster.
Once again possible to mine stone with anything and not just with a pickaxe.
 TNT

Now hurts mobs.
Items are still safe from TNT explosions.
Pushes away entities.
 Water

Generates splash particles when the player is swimming in it.


=== Items ===
General

No longer destroyed in fire or lava.
Shadows display under dropped items.
No longer go into hidden inventory slots once a save is reloaded.
 Swords

Deals less damage.
Tools

Harder materials no longer require better tools.
Mining underwater or in the air is now slower.
Mining tools can now hit mobs. Their damage depends on the type and tier of tool.

=== Non-mob entities ===
General

Changed the pickup animation for arrows and dropped items.
They now get sucked up when the player walks into them.


=== General ===
Splash

Depending on the length of the splash, it changes size in accordance to the default resolution.
