The iceologer was a proposed mob featured in the Mob Vote during Minecraft Live 2020, alongside the moobloom and glow squid. It would have been a hostile icy illager variant that spawned in mountain biomes and attacked players and entities using large chunks of ice.
The iceologer received 34.8% of the votes in the first round and moved onto the second, but then lost with 47.3% of the votes.

== Trivia ==
Sprites of Iceologers appeared as an easter egg in the 2022 Mob Vote Event, 2023 Mob Vote Event, and the Eerie Mojang Office Party server.
The iceologer is the only mob shown dying in a community vote.
