"Biome Fest" is a song composed by C418 that plays when the player is in Creative mode.


== Locations ==


== Track description ==

"Biome Fest" begins with a dreamlike choir of synthesized voices fading in, which makes way to an electronic song with bells and tribal percussion in the background. It ends with the same choir that it begins with, fading out.

=== Raw music files ===
In Java Edition, the music files can be found in .minecraft/assets/objects.
In Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Education, it can be found in:

Mobile: com.mojang/resource_packs/music/vanilla_music/sounds/music/game/creative/
Windows: %PROGRAMFILES%\WindowsApps\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP_<version>_x64_8wekyb3d8bbwe\data\resource_packs\vanilla_music\sounds\music\game\creative
All music files are in Ogg Vorbis.

== Trivia ==
This track was previously used in a video by Notch to demonstrate biomes.
It is also one of the only two tracks on Minecraft - Volume Beta to feature a description of some kind, linking to the video made by Notch.
A remixed version of "Biome Fest" by C418 was released as "Biome Party" for the album 148.
