Piper Warner is the mother of Dri and Jett, serving as a supporting character in Minecraft: The Tournament.


== Appearance ==

Upon being introduced, Piper is preparing to go to bed, already wearing bonnet and PJs.


== Personality ==


== Biography ==

Prior to the events of the story, Granny's house was flooded, forcing her to move in with the family. To give her space, Piper offered to let her sleep in what would normally be her office.
