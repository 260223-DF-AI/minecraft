Anthony Viviano, better known as BigB, is an American YouTuber known for videos on Minecraft and Roblox.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==


=== Downloadable content ===
In 2021, BigB was one of several YouTubers selected to appear in the YouTube Creators Skin Pack, celebrating a trillion views of Minecraft content on YouTube. He would later voice himself in Caves & Cliffs Explorers, reusing the design from the skin pack.


=== MINECON ===
In 2017, Anthony appeared as a guest during MINECON Earth, as well as during the pre-show. He returned the next year as a host.


=== Minecraft: Story Mode ===
During Minecraft: Story Mode - Season Two, Viviano made an appearance as a fictonalized version himself. Unlike previous YouTubers, he was referred to solely as Anthony, as opposed to any of his nicknames.


== Trivia ==
His skin is taken from Skin Pack 5, and is meant to represent Lee Everett from The Walking Dead. Coincidentally, the voice of the character, Dave Fennoy, also appeared in Minecraft: Story Mode.
