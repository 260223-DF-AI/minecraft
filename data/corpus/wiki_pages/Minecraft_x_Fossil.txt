Minecraft x Fossil is a collaboration between Mojang Studios and Fossil, a fashion design company. The collection was announced and released on March 3, 2025. Five products were released as part of the collection. A Twitch WatchTime Quest was also put out to celebrate the partnership between March 7 and March 14, 2025.
