Lush Bath Bomb Add-On is an official add-on made in promotion of bath bombs produced by British cosmetics retailer Lush.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
 Benzoin Sapling
Found in jungles.
Can be placed.
Produces one benzoin when used when ready, then goes into a cooldown before another can be produced.
Can only be placed on dirt, rooted dirt, coarse dirt, and grass blocks.
Affected by gravity.
 Benzoin
Can be bought from merchants for one emerald
 Big Blue Bath Bomb
Crafted using seaweed, salt, lemon, and lavender
"Produces a peaceful liquid" when thrown.
Liquid puddle remains for 60 seconds.
Grants speed and water breathing to players.
Converts lava into obsidian.
Creates a "bubble shield" around players, making them immune to damage for 60 seconds.
