18w08b is the twenty-second snapshot for Java Edition 1.13, released on February 22, 2018, which added three types of fish mobs and fish buckets, a crafting recipe for dried kelp blocks, and changed the player's underwater visibility.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
Fish buckets

 Cod bucket
 Salmon bucket
 Puffer fish bucket
When used, they place a water source block and spawn the corresponding fish inside it.
Unused texture for an upcoming "clownfish bucket" added to the game files.
Spawn eggs

 Cod spawn egg
Spawns cod.
 Salmon spawn egg
Spawns salmons.
 Puffer fish spawn egg
Spawns puffer fishes.


=== Mobs ===
Added fish as mobs.
They need water to live. Outside of water, they flop around before suffocating.
They can be caught with a water bucket.
 Cod
Spawn in cold ocean, normal ocean, and lukewarm ocean biomes.
Swim in groups of up to 9 cod.
 Salmon
Spawn in frozen ocean, cold ocean and river biomes.
Swim in groups of up to 6 salmon.
   Puffer fish
Spawn in lukewarm ocean and warm ocean biomes.
Inflate themselves when a player gets near.
Cause 7 seconds of Poison to nearby players.
They drop 1 pufferfish item when killed.


=== General ===
Dried kelp can now be crafted into dried kelp blocks, which can be crafted back into dried kelp.


== Changes ==


=== World generation ===
Warm and lukewarm oceans now have sand floors.
Warm deep ocean biomes no longer generate.


=== Gameplay ===
Changed natural water visibility.
The longer a player stays underwater, the better they are able to see. (Maximum underwater visibility is given when a player stays underwater for 1 minute)
The Water Breathing status effect and Respiration enchantment no longer grant enhanced vision underwater.
