1.49  is a version of PlayStation Vita Edition released on May 30, 2017.  
[edit]


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
Shulker boxes, concrete, concrete powder, glazed terracotta.


=== Items ===
Shulker shells, iron nuggets.


=== General ===
DLC

Adventure Time Mash-up pack.
Magic: The Gathering Skin Pack.

Temple, a free Glide track.
Controls

Added Pick Block.
Unassigned by default.
Added off-hand slot for maps and arrows.
Sounds

New note block sounds.
New sound effects for filling cauldrons, paddling boats, using a fishing rod, putting an eye of ender into an End portal frame, and eyes of ender exploding or popping back into item form.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks and items ===
Iron and gold armor can now be smelted into nuggets.
More items can now be used as fuel in a furnace.
Using a firework rocket while gliding with elytra will now give the player a boost.
Changed the color of dyes, wool, carpets, and banners.
Reordered all colored blocks and items in the Creative inventory. Banners now have the same order of colors as all other colored blocks and items.
Fishing rods can now hook on entities without causing knockback or pseudo-damage.
Removed the ability to fire bows by using arrows.


=== General ===
The default controls have been changed slightly and the player can now define their own control scheme with the new controls menu.

The player's best run in Glide will now be recorded (as long as it is under 5 minutes) and a ghost will fly alongside them in Solo Mode to help them improve.
Tooltips have been updated.
The death screen in the Glide minigame now displays "You Crashed" instead of "You Died!".
