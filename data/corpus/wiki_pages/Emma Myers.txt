Emma Myers (born April 2, 2002) is an American actress. She began her career as a child actress in 2010 but gained her recognition for starring in the comedy horror series Wednesday and the mystery series A Good Girl's Guide to Murder.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
Myers is the actress portraying Natalie in A Minecraft Movie. She was revealed to be featuring in the movie in late 2023 and made her first appearance as Natalie on September 4, 2024 in the movie's teaser trailer.
Myers also made an appearance during Minecraft LIVE – March 2025, revealing a clip of the film and announcing A Minecraft Movie Live Event. Later in the after show, she played the event map along with Jens Bergensten and Agnes Larsson.
Myers also appeared in a short segment in the video "Minecraft - Shape your world", pressing a button that leads to a piston door that opens.
