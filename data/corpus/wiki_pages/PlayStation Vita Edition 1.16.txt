1.16  is a version of PlayStation Vita Edition released on April 29, 2015.  
[edit]


== Additions ==
Added the Alex skin and some variations of it to the default skins.

Added The Simpsons Skin Pack trial content.


== Changes ==
Shrubs can be placed in flower pots.
Maps placed in item frames now cover the whole block face.
Skulls worn by mobs and players are now displayed on the armor layer. Previously, the skull replaced the mob/player's head texture; the hat layer appeared over the skull.
Sheep named as 'jeb_' cycles now through all possible sheep colors in a fading rainbow effect.
Changed music tracks played in Creative mode and on front-end menus to match the Java version.
Armor no longer turns red when mobs and players are hurt.
A smooth transition is no longer applied to the camera when the player sneaks.
Third person sword blocking position slightly tweaked.
Name of mobs, which are renamed using a name tag is now always visible, even when not looking at it up close.


== Fixes ==
2 issues fixed
MCCE-14 – Cannot look around on PS3.
MCCE-63 – Texture packs not working on PS3.
