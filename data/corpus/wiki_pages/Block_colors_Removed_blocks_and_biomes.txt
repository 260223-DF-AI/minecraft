This page covers block tints which were applied to blocks that no longer exist, as well as applications from biomes which also no longer exist.
None of these removed blocks have ever existed in Bedrock Edition, and all biomes still exist and are listed on Block colors. Shrubs used the same texture and biome coloring as short grass and ferns.

=== Green shrubs ===
Green shrubs were data values of grass from 3 to 15, which from 13w10a up to their removal used the shrub/dead bush texture, but with an added tint.


=== Potted grass blocks, grass and green shrubs ===
Grass blocks, grass and green shrubs would render if placed inside of a flower pot with commands and would use the grass tint of its biome.
Potted grass and green shrubs existed from 13w36a to 14w11b, whereas potted grass blocks existed from 14w06a to 14w11b.


=== Potted leaves and vines ===
Leaves and vines would render if placed inside of a flower pot with commands and would use the foliage tint of its biome.
Potted leaves existed from 14w06a to 14w11b, whereas potted vines existed from 14w06b to 14w11b.
