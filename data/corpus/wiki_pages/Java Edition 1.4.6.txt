1.4.6 is a minor update to Java Edition. It is the final update of 2012, released on December 20. It introduced fireworks, enchanted books, and the Thorns enchantment. It also added a warning for users playing on soon-to-be unsupported systems (PowerPC and Java 5). While it was not the last update to 1.4, it was the last one before the snapshots of 1.5 started. This is the final version released in 2012.
A pre-release version of 1.4.6 was released on December 17, 2012. The release version of 1.4.6 differs from the reupload of the pre-release by fixing a crash issue.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Nether Brick Slabs

Crafted using nether bricks.


=== Items ===
 Firework Star

Used to create firework rockets.
Combine gunpowder with dyes to get a firework star with those colors used for the sparkles.
Add  Glowstone Dust, a  fire charge, a  diamond, a  gold nugget,  feather, or a  mob head to make the firework twinkling, big, trailing, star-shaped, bursting, or creeper-shaped, respectively.
Craft a firework star with dye to change the color that the sparkles fade to.
 Firework Rocket

Non-destructive, rise up in the air, and create colorful patterns when shot by dispensers or placed by players.
Combine  paper with  gunpowder to craft a firework rocket.
Add a firework star to add effects, or gunpowder to increase the flight duration.
 Enchanted Books

Enchanted books to receive a single enchantment on them.
Can be applied to items using anvils, consuming the book.
Enchants can be applied to any item in Creative mode.
Sold by villagers and found in dungeon and village chests.
Can now be found in loot chests and by trading with villagers.
Unbreaking can be applied to anything with durability.
Efficiency and Silk Touch can be applied to shears, allowing the harvesting of cobwebs.
Sharpness, Smite, and Bane of Arthropods can be applied to axes.


=== Gameplay ===
 Thorns

A new armor enchantment.
Deals damage depending on tier to attackers.
Wears armor out faster.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
 Monster Spawners

Can now spawn differently configured mobs with different spawn chances


=== Items ===
 Bows

A high pitched sound is now played when a player hits another player from far away with a bow.


=== Mobs ===
 Ghasts

Fireballs' blast radius can now be customized
 Skeletons, Zombies, and Zombie Pigmen

The armor and enchants they spawn with now are even stronger on harder difficulties.


=== Non-mob entities ===
 Arrows

Now render from farther away.
Items and  item frame entities

Now render in 3D when using fancy graphics.


=== World generation ===
Dungeon

Now have a random chance to have enchanted books in their chests.


=== Gameplay ===
Inventory

Items can now be dragged and dropped.
Long pressing halves the stack before dragging.
Hotbar

Items now show their name when switching in the hotbar.
Can be disabled by changing heldItemTooltips to false in options.txt.
Dropping items while holding ctrl now drops the whole stack.
 Enchantments

Efficiency now has no effect when using tools on the wrong block types.
Fire Protection now extinguishes fire more quickly.
Blast Protection now reduces explosion recoil.
Protection is not as protective as it used to be in order to make specific enchantments more worthwhile.
 Sneaking

Pressing use while sneaking now uses the held item instead of the currently selected block.
Death messages

Now appear in server log files.


=== General ===
Display

Added a default resolution setting to options.txt: overrideWidth:0 and overrideHeight:0.
Options

Added an option to toggle touchscreen mode.


== Removals ==


=== General ===
Distance effects

Falling block behavior has changed - they are no longer capable of infinitely producing dropped items at high coordinate values.
