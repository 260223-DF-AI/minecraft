"Creator (Music Box)" refers to both a song composed by Lena Raine, and a music disc that plays the song when inserted in a jukebox. The music disc is obtained from trial chambers, as a rare drop from decorated pots.

=== Generated loot ===


== Track description ==

The music box version of "Creator" plays the main melody of the song with only a music box and none of the other accompanying instruments in the original track. It also lacks the key change present in the original.


== Usage ==
Like all music discs, using the "Creator (Music Box)" music disc on a jukebox causes the jukebox to play the song.
If the player places a redstone comparator besides a jukebox playing "Creator (Music Box)", it will emit a redstone signal of 11.
