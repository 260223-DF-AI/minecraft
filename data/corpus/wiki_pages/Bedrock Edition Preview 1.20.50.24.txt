Beta 1.20.50.24 (Android) or Preview 1.20.50.24 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox) is the fifth and final beta/Preview version for Bedrock Edition 1.20.50, released on November 8, 2023, which fixes bugs.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Realm

Player can now launch Minecraft directly into a Realm using a link, even when the game isn't running.
Example link: minecraft://connectToRealm?realmId=<Id number>


== Fixes ==
2 issues fixed
From the previous development versions

MCPE-176399 – Armor renders with solid textures when applying armor trim templates.
Other

Fixed a crash that would sometimes occur when loading a world with custom items.
