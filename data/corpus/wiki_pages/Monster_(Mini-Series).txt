A monster is a creature or species in Minecraft Mini-Series that plays an antagonistic role, with little in the way of characterization. 


== Creeper ==

Creepers are self-destructive mobs that explode as a means of attack. They appear as obstacles in the challenges of Birch Island. In The First Night, one of them ignited near Rudi and Sacha managed to rescue him before it exploded. In The Abandoned Mineshaft, another creeper is seen wandering the Abandoned Mineshaft, and was avoided by Team Stone Beach without noticing them.
Creepers have an Athens variant that appeared in the second season of Minecraft Mini-Series with Greek engravings and similar behavior. They appear in The Twist of the Plot and Maze of the Minotaur (episode).


== Enderman ==

Endermen are tall mobs capable of teleporting and carrying blocks from place to place throughout Scatter Island, where they live. In the Ender Cave, they were the main targets of the participants, who had to kill them in order to collect Ender pearls. When all the endermen in the cave were killed, the remaining one was absorbed by The Enderall.


== Husk ==

Husks are a desert variant of zombies inhabiting the Desert Maze as obstacles during the contestants' penultimate challenge alongside black widow spiders. While they display aggressive behavior, none of them harmed any of the contestants and many of them were defeated.


== Iron Golem ==

Iron golems are metallic constructs serving for CommuniKate as security guards for most challenges. They are also responsible for organizing some challenges, such as the Challenge of Ice and Magma. On Arena Island, when the contestants started a revolution against their host, the latter commanded her iron golems to attack them, but they were all stopped by black widow spiders that Durango and Sacha managed to tame.


== Nether mobs ==

Ghasts, blazes, and zombie pigmen are hostile monsters inhabiting the Nether as the intended obstacles for the final challenge. However, the challenge never happened as the participants escaped. The Nether later appears beyond the portal in the Kraken's mouth, revealing the mobs.


== Skeleton ==

Skeletons are undead mobs made entirely out of bone, spawning at night as a threat to the contestants during their first challenge. They are capable of shooting arrows from their bows. A group of them scared away Team Tundra at dusk, and later at night, one skeleton got defeated by Durango.


== Snow Golem ==

Snow golems are sentient snowmen with the head of a carved pumpkin, capable of throwing snowballs at monsters. Creating them was the goal of the Challenge of Ice and Magma that consisted of one team member crafting snow blocks out of snowballs and the other facing an obstacle course to retrieve a pumpkin. Sacha recklessly decided to get her team's pumpkin but failed to reach due to her death by magma blocks and then by starvation. Quinn attempted to reach the end using an ender pearl, but got stopped by CommuniKate. River, however, managed to create a snow golem first, which shot snowballs at strays and Durango's hat.


== Stray ==

Strays are skeletal mobs that inhabit the mountains of Scatter Island and are minor antagonists of The Challenge of Ice and Magma. During the challenge taking place, groups of them stood next to the way to the snow golem creating track as obstacles, shooting arrows of slowness at the participants.


== Vex ==

Vexes are spirits summoned by evokers to assist them. In order to reach the other side of the cave during the second challenge, Eve made use of her evoker status to summon vexes to fly her and Vin to the treasure. However, the vexes were too weak to handle and Vin died to the lava, disqualifying Team Woodland.


== Zombie ==

Zombies are undead monsters that acted as an obstacle during the first challenge. They appear in swarms and walk slowly toward their targets. It is also revealed that they burn in sunlight because, the next day, Zed's umbrella fell off and he caught fire.
