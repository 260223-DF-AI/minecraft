CU41  is a version of Xbox One Edition released on March 28, 2017.

== Additions ==
Added the Glide mini game.
Added new mini game lobby.
Added leaderboards for the Glide mini game.
Added support for up to 16 players in Battle and Glide.
Added the Mighty Morphin Power Rangers Skin Pack.
Added the Mini Game Masters Skin Pack.
