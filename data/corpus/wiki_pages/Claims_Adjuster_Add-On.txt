The Claims Adjuster Add-On is an official Marketplace add-on developed by Minecraft. Released alongside the add-on was the Ice Cube Claims Adjuster, used to promote the release.


== Additions ==


=== Mobs ===
 Adjuster
Spawns at the player's spawn point
"Asks" the player certain questions from a dialogue database
Once dialogue is completed, it will spawn a Claims Adjuster Golem
 Golem
Spawns once completing the questions from the Adjuster
Attacks monsters (except creepers), and hoglins and zoglins (when not in Peaceful mode)


=== Items ===
 Guidebook
Guide to help the player with the add-on
Given to the player upon loading a world with the add-on enabled
 Baseball Bat
A weapon dropped by the Claims Adjuster Golem
Deals 8 damage (8HP)
 Headset
An item dropped by the Claims Adjuster Golem
Equippable in the head armor slot
 Shirt and tie
An item dropped by the Claims Adjuster Golem
Equippable in the chest armor slot
 Stamp
An item dropped by the Claims Adjuster Golem
Deals 8 damage (8HP)
 Trophy 
An item dropped by the Claims Adjuster Golem
Places the trophy block


=== Blocks ===
 Mug
A block dropped by the Claims Adjuster Golem
Always enchanted with Fire Aspect II
 Trophy 
A block dropped by the Claims Adjuster Golem
Placed via the trophy item
