Sebastian Eugene Hansen is an actor best known for playing Henry in A Minecraft Movie. His acting career started in 2018 in Mozart in the Jungle, and he later played Young Scott Landon in Lisey’s Story (2021).
He also asked for the Technoblade tribute in A Minecraft Movie.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
Sebastian is the actor portraying Henry in A Minecraft Movie. He was revealed to be featuring in the movie in late 2023 and made his first appearance as Henry on September 4, 2024 in the movie's teaser trailer.
