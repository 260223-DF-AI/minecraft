1.16.3 Release Candidate 1 (known as 1.16.3-rc1 in the launcher) is the first and only release candidate for Java Edition 1.16.3, released on September 7, 2020, which fixes two bugs.


== Fixes ==
2 issues fixed
From 1.16.2

MC-196449 – Piglins, piglin brutes, hoglins and zoglins have trouble pathfinding to the player when attacking.
MC-198678 – Giving an item and a gold ingot to a baby piglin and killing it duplicates the item.
