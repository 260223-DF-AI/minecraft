Bamboo mosaic stairs are a decorative stairs variant of bamboo mosaics that does not generate naturally and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Bamboo mosaic stairs can be broken with anything, but axes are the fastest.


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==


=== Fuel ===
Bamboo mosaic stairs can be used as fuel in furnaces, smelting 1.5 items per block.
