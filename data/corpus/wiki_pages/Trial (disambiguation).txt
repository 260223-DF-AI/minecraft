Trial may refer to:


== Minecraft ==
Trial Chambers, a series of underground structures.
Trial Spawner, a block found in trial chambers which spawns mobs in waves.
Ominous Trial Spawner, what trial spawners transform into during ominous trials.
Trial Key, an item obtained from trial spawners.
Ominous Trial Key, an item obtained from ominous trial spawners.
Ominous Trials, which are controlled by ominous trial spawners and triggered by the Trial Omen effect.
Tricky Trials, a major update released in 2024
Demo Mode, the trial version of Minecraft.


== Minecraft Dungeons ==
Daily Trial, challenges which change each day
