A potion of Swiftness is a potion that provides Speed when used.


== Obtaining ==


=== Brewing ===


=== Mob loot ===
A witch has an 8.5% chance to drop a potion of Swiftness if it dies while drinking the potion.


=== Generated loot ===
In Bedrock Edition, the cauldron in a swamp hut contains a random potion, with a 25% chance for it to be Swiftness.


== Usage ==


=== Effect ===


=== Base potion ===
Potion of Swiftness is a base for brewing potions of Slowness.
