Blocks are the main components of each level of Minecraft Blast. They are used to destroy, clear or collect blockers by tapping on matching blocks that are next to the blockers.
Different numbers of colored blocks will create different boosters, which can be used for different purposes, where tapping or swiping them to activate will use a move. The blocks can be swiped together, where they will combine their effects.
Blocks of a certain color can be destroyed in order to unlock certain rewards in the crafting masters screen (currently yellow).
