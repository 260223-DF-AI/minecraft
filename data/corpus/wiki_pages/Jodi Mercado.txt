Jodi Mercado is one of the main characters of Minecraft: Woodsword Chronicles.

== Personality ==


== Powers and abilities ==
Jodi is shown to be a very passionate builder, constructing several megastructures through Minecraft. These are typically made out of cobblestone, such as an obelisk, a Moai, and a giant floating cube. Upon coming into contact with the Foundation Stone, her building prowess are increased tenfold, with her gaining a godlike control over the game. On top of being able to build whatever she wants, she gains an omnipresent perspective of the game world, perceiving herself as being every individual block or mob, feeling physical sensations.
