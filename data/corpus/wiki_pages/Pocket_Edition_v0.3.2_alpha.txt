v0.3.2 alpha is a minor update to Minecraft: Pocket Edition that was released on July 16, 2012. It adds notable features such as furnaces, gold, iron, and diamond materials, as well as made other changes and fixes.
v0.3.1 alpha does not seem to exist; the version skipped from v0.3.0 straight to v0.3.2. The second reupload of v0.3.0 may have been retro-conned as v0.3.1, due to it adding features, rather than fixing crashes like most other reuploads.[verify for Bedrock Edition]


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===

 Furnaces

Storage blocks used for smelting of blocks and items.
Can be crafted with cobblestone.
Saplings

Non-solid blocks that can be grown into their respective tree type.
Are occasionally dropped by leaves when they break or decay.
 Glass panes

Transparent blocks that can be used as a more efficient alternative to glass blocks.
Can be crafted with glass blocks.
 Chests

Currently have no functionality.
Are not yet obtainable.
Cannot make double chests yet.


=== Gameplay ===
Smelting

A method of producing refined goods.
Done via a furnace.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
 Leaves

Now occasionally drop saplings when broken.
 Clay

Now drops 4 clay balls when broken.
 Lapis lazuli blocks

Can now be crafted into lapis lazuli.


=== Items ===
 Gold ingots

Now obtained by smelting gold ore.
Can now be crafted into blocks of gold or golden tools.
 Iron ingots

Now obtained by smelting iron ore.
Can now be crafted into blocks of iron, shears, or iron tools.
 Diamonds

Now obtained by mining diamond ore with an iron pickaxe or stronger.
Can now be crafted into blocks of diamond or diamond tools.
 Bricks

Can now be crafted into brick blocks.
 Clay balls

Can now be crafted into clay.
Can be smeltied into bricks
 Lapis lazuli

Can now be crafted into lapis lazuli blocks.
  Diamond, gold, and iron pickaxes,    shovels,    axes, and    swords

Can now be crafted with corresponding materials and sticks.


=== Gameplay ===
Survival

Every available block and item can now be gathered, crafted, or smelted into.
The player no longer starts out with shears, bricks, and glass in infinite stacks.
Creative inventory

== Bugs ==
When equipping leaves in creative mode, the block will occasionally display a glitched crafting table texture


== Fixes ==
5 bugs fixed

Less flickering/Z-fighting on some devices.
Having one of an item or block no longer displays the "1".
Mobs have correct health.
Fixed rendering of fence gate and half block in hand.
Spruce and birch leaves no longer drop a glitched block when cut by shears.
