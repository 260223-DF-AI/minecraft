3.18.26 is an update to the Minecraft Launcher released on August 13, 2025, which makes changes related to Minecoins.


== Additions ==
Minecoins

Added a Minecoin button that displays how many minecoins a player has on the bottom-right corner of the Bedrock Edition play section. It can be acessed from both the release and the preview section.
Added a Minecoin menu in which the user can buy Minecoins directly from within the launcher.
It can be acessed by clicking the "+" sign on the Minecoin button.


== Trivia ==
The addition of the Minecoin button in Launcher 3.18.26 was mentioned on the changelogs of Launcher 3.18.4.
