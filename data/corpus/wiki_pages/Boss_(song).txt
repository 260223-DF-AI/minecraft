"Boss" is a song composed by C418 that plays during the ender dragon fight.
Unlike other songs by C418 in the Minecraft soundtrack, this was neither part of any soundtrack albums nor released as a single.


== Locations ==


== Track description ==

"Boss" is largely based on modified versions of some themes from Minecraft - Alpha Volume. The theme is a very dark ambient piece with an ominous air of desolation and hopelessness.
Throughout the track, you can hear extremely distorted or remixed versions of parts of the tracks "Cat", "Haggstrom" and "Subwoofer Lullaby" from Minecraft - Volume Alpha, while ambient background noises gradually increase in intensity.

In Java Edition, the music files can be found in .minecraft/assets/objects.

Windows: %PROGRAMFILES%\WindowsApps\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP_<version>_x64_8wekyb3d8bbwe\data\resource_packs\vanilla_music\sounds\music\game\end
All music files are in Ogg Vorbis.
