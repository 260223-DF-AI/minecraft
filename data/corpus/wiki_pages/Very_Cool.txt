Very Cool (also referred to as Cool Shirtz) is a clothing company that collaborated with Mojang Studios on a Minecraft-themed collection. The collection was revealed on November 16, 2025 and the first wave was released on November 23, 2025.

== Trivia ==
Thirteen days before the collaboration was announced, the Cool Shirtz social media manager gave a tour of their Minecraft world in a Twitter thread.
The name of the owner's armadillo, "sock", was the answer to a question in the Minecraft x Very Cool Sweepstakes.
