Minecraft LIVE  September 2025 was a Minecraft LIVE event that took place on September 27, 2025, at 17:00 UTC. It featured information about the Mounts of Mayhem game drop, and revealed the release date for The Copper Age drop.

== Summary ==


=== Pre-show ===
There was a 15-minute Community Pre-Show before the main event, featuring content creators discussing Minecraft. The hosts who presented the Pre-Show were YouTubers Aimsey and Shadoune666.


=== Tutorial ===
Due to the increasing popularity of Minecraft, an influx of new players are learning about Minecraft for their first time, as noted by Jens Bergensten. This summons a transition featuring a happy ghast moving upwards to reveal a new excerpt. It is introduced by a new voice, comforting that there is no wrong way to play Minecraft and then follows on the first few steps to craft a crafting table and a wooden pickaxe. The narrator is then interrupted by a voice call from Jens who warns to not dig straight down but instead is hastily stopped by the narrator and digs straight down anyway. This causes the narrator to fall a large distance and finds himself in a trial chamber. Havoc is unleashed in the trial chamber, causing the narrator to struggle and pause the game to look online and research how to defeat a mistakenly named breeze. Following the search, he scrolls pass successive bursts of short-form content admitting he became distracted and forgot his original intention. Finally, he leaves the reimagined version of the Internet, and preaches yet again there is no right or wrong way to play the game, noting the numerous tutorials made by the community if the player ever becomes stuck.


=== The Copper Age ===
After the tutorial, the devs talked about many of the Copper Age features, including what the community has done with the shelf, how they made the sound effects, mentioning "that it has no ambient sound, and that its sounds came from its movements",  and the Copper Golem statue. During this, they showed a list of all the Copper Age features they will add on the 30th. After all of this, they said that the Happy Ghast was cuter than the Copper Golem, cueing the Happy Ghast song.


=== Happy Ghast Music Video ===

The stream transitions into a music video. 
Efe is mining copper ore, only for a happy ghast to appear. In a library Efe hugs the ghast, but gets squished up against bookshelves. The ghast creeps behind a cherry tree, and then unknowingly begins hitting Efe with tentacles. The two begin flying up into the sky, over a badlands biome, and then into a village where Efe steals a bed for no apparent reason. Two warm animals look up at the sky, while two horses race by it, and then Efe gets mobbed by a clutter of spiders in a mineshaft.
In a birch forest, Efe crafts a harness and equips it on the ghast, allowing their friends Kai, Noor, and Alex to fly with them. From there Steve and Alex get their own ghasts, while Efe begins carrying several mobs in boats "for a party." On the ground a villager flees from the boat, and ends up being caught in one against their will. The happy ghast flies with bees, and then serves as an umbrella for Efe, before Efe gets knocked off a mountain by a screaming goat. An armadillo is startled by the suddenly appearing ghast, and then Zuri leaps into water for an unstated reason. From there Efe fights against illagers in a burning forest, again without an apparent context.
The scene cuts to Efe running with a dried ghast from a regular ghast and piglins, before returning to the Overworld, where Efe is ready to kill several cow. Makena tethers a bee to the happy ghast, alongside several other mobs, before they suddenly disappear when Efe flies through an arch into the sky. Suddenly, all nine heroes appear, on their own happy ghasts. Using crossbows, they aim up towards the clouds, and set off a show of fireworks. Efe and the happy ghast fly through, setting off more fireworks, and then return to the arch to fly through it again.


=== Stories from Minecraft ===
The segment transitions from a rotating happy ghast and is then voiced by Agnes Larsson, mentioning her surprise with the community's creativeness. Leading from a short, humorous clip, Agnes introduces three individuals whose life involves the usage of Minecraft outside of the game, first beginning with Tee Nuchit.
Tee Nuchit, an aspiring 19-year-old architect from Sweden, firsts describes an anecdote of a friend inviting him to a Minecraft server. Due to the long distance between the two, Tee wanted to show where he lived and through Minecraft, built his hometown, Falun, except it is set in 1920 CE. He describes the process of building in the game crossing similarities with his sketches of imaginary buildings in real life, which then inspired him to pursue the career of architecture. Without Minecraft, his life "would have have been so much different."
Agnes expresses how the in-game world can be used in a variety of ways, being a "source of inspiration" for the next person, Travess Smalley. An artist from the USA, he creates art that can be "translat[ed] on and off of computers," due to becoming enamoured by the different patterns formed by the rotation of glazed terracotta in a 44 grid which became a theme for some of his works. He continues to describe the game being the stimulus for his ideas describing how the procedural generation of each world caused him to use randomness and chance more in his pieces.
Once again, Agnes describes the limitless opportunities created by Minecraft, revealing an elderly woman from Uruguay. Known online as Aqmadre, she is a Minecraft streamer who first became exposed to the game through her two sons, both of which are also streamers, where she occasionally appeared in the background of their own streams. This caused a fascination to those interacting in the streamers' communities, asking to introduce who she was. She recalled a moment, when first starting to play, where she encountered a creeper who then proceeded to explode in front of her, evoking a scream. Later, she reflects her positive experiences with the game and the community, being able to have the chance to help so many people.


=== Dragon Ball Z DLC ===

Climbing up a mountain, Efe discovers six of the seven dragon balls, illuminating a bright light. Placing down the four-star ball, Efe successfully summons Shenron into the Overworld. Efe proceeds to ask an inaudible wish, which Shenron happily grants, first turning Efe into a super saiyan, before being replaced with Goku. After taking a look at himself, several of the other Z Fighters appear. 


=== Mounts of Mayhem ===

The nautilus, zombie nautilus, spear, and zombie horseman were announced.


=== The Deep Dig ===

An after show called the "Deep Dig" was broadcast following the main show, featuring playthroughs of newly announced content and developer commentary.
