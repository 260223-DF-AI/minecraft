v0.12.1 alpha build 2 is the second build version released for v0.12.1.


== Changes ==
Added tame button for ocelots
Breaking crops will now always drop seeds
Made the flight controls a little bit smaller
Snow Golems now spawn with a wearing pumpkin

MCPE-9320 – Taming Ocelots no tame button

Other
