18w07b is the nineteenth snapshot for Java Edition 1.13, released on February 15, 2018.


== Additions ==
Added the block tag minecraft:waterlogged.
Added the block and item tags: minecraft:rails, and minecraft:sand.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Bubble columns

Reduced upward bubble column item movement speed.
Dried kelp blocks

Are now flammable.


=== Items ===
Scute

Renamed "Turtle Shell Piece" to "Scute".


=== Mobs ===
Spawning

Mobs are no longer able to spawn or replenish their breath in kelp and sea grass.
Turtles

Breeding now requires sea grass instead of raw cod.


=== Gameplay ===
Oxygen no longer regenerates instantly when the player exits water.
