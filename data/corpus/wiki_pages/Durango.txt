Durango is a character in the Minecraft Mini-Series and part of Team Mesa with River.


== Appearance ==
Durango is referred to as a cowboy and looks the part. He has brown hair and a beard, black eyes and light skin. He wears a hat, dark blue shirt with light blue sleeves, brown pants, and light brown shoes.


== Personality ==
Durango can be hot-headed, cocky, materialistic, and competitive, especially when gold is promised. He likes to be in charge, creating plans, and eating meat. In the Challenge of the Spooky Isles he grows a rivalry with Quinn, but in the Mystery of the Greek Isles they put their differences aside to defeat Poseidon's games.


== Biography ==
Before the series began, Durango was shown to rancher in-between interview clips.
Durango was once teleported from his home along with the others by CommuniKate using a command block on the show. He and River won the first and sixth challenges, and in the final challenge, he and the others sabotaged their way back home, but were teleported to the Greek Isles. There, Durango, along with Zed, Quinn and CommuniKate, were forced by Poseidon to bring him the enchanted eye of Ender. After he got the eyes, the god decided to get rid of the heroes with a kraken, but Durango and the others escape and try to stop Poseidon. Durango then fights the Phoenix, and soon Zeus comes, stops Poseidon and asks the heroes to help him. Apparently after helping Zeus, Durango and River returned home.


== Relationships ==


=== Quinn ===
When Quinn and Durango first met they were rivals and didn't like each other but when they wanted to stop CommuniKate they decided to put their differences aside and defeat CommuniKate.


=== River ===
At first, Durango was cold with River, wanted to do everything himself. However, during the series, he goes through a personal journey and becomes her friend, and begins to save her from dangers.


=== CommuniKate ===
Durango didn't have any hatred towards CommuniKate, but on the contrary wanted to win her show, but then wanted to leave it along with the others. In the second season, they were forced to work together, sometimes even agreed with each other.


=== Zed ===
In Season 2, while collecting enchanted eyes of Ender, he wanted to befriend Zed and began copying his zombie behavior.
