"Pigstep" refers to both a song composed by Lena Raine, and a music disc that plays the song when inserted in a jukebox. The music disc is obtained from bastion remnants.

== Track description ==

The title "Pigstep" is a portmanteau of "piglin" and "dubstep".
The song is an intense, somewhat hip-hop-style beat beginning with a repeating cello-like tune and dubstep-style drop, along with more mellow parts reminiscent of a radio. The song was featured in the 2025 production A Minecraft Movie.
The album release of "Pigstep" includes two versions: the mono mix used in the game and a stereo mix with more usage of multi-directional sound.


== Usage ==
Like all music discs, using the "Pigstep" music disc on a jukebox causes the jukebox to play the song.
If the player places a redstone comparator besides a jukebox playing "Pigstep", it emits a redstone signal of 13.

== Trivia ==
Part of "Pigstep" can be heard in Minecraft Dungeons when equipping Golden Piglin Armor.
In A Minecraft Movie, in a flashback of Malgosha's evilness, she is seen dancing to Pigstep when she was doing an audition in Nether's Got Talent.
A remix of "Pigstep" known as "Pigstep (Just Dance Version)" is a playable song in Just Dance +.
