The Mojang Office Cape is a cape which was obtainable by completing all five daily challenges in the Eerie Mojang Office Party.


== Appearance ==
The cape's design includes one large, opened eyeblossom as the centerpiece with additional, smaller eyeblossoms around it. It is displayed on a dark background, mimicking the night when eyeblossoms bloom.


== Distribution ==

The Mojang Office cape was obtained by completing all five daily challenges in the Eerie Mojang Office Party event server.


== History ==


== Trivia ==
It has also been referred to as the Eyeblossom Cape in official media.
