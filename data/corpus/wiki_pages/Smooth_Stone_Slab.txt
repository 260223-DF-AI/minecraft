A smooth stone slab is a decorative slab variant of smooth stone that generates in overworld structures and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Smooth stone slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Smooth stone slabs can generate as part of strongholds and plains, savanna, taiga, and snowy tundra villages. Smooth stone slabs can also generate in woodland mansions, some Overworld ruined portals, and in trail ruins.
