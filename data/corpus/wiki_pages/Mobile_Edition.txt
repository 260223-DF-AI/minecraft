Mobile Edition may refer to:

Bedrock Edition: the current mobile edition of Minecraft that supports crossplay with different platforms.
Minecraft Preview an app that players can use to test out new features that are in development.
Pocket Edition: the discontinued mobile edition that got replaced with Bedrock Edition.
Pocket Edition Alpha: the first and only development phase of Pocket Edition.
Pocket Edition Lite: a free version of Pocket Edition that got discontinued after v0.2.1 alpha2.
Pocket Edition Demo: a demo of Pocket Edition that was never released to the public but playable at E3 2011
China Edition, the mainland China version of Minecraft for Windows, Android, iOS, iPadOS, and macOS.
