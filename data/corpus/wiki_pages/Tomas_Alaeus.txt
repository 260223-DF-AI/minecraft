Tomas Alaeus is a former member of Mojang Studios. He was a developer for Bedrock Edition and mostly developed the Bedrock Edition's Realms.
Tomas Alaeus studied Computer engineering at Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden from 2005 to 2010. After that, he worked as a game developer at the company Fabrication Games in Stockholm for about one year. In March 2012, he changed to the Swedish offshoot of the British game company King. There he worked as game developer, especially on the mobile version of the game Candy Crush Saga, for three years until he came to Mojang in early March 2015. 
He left Mojang in 2020.
