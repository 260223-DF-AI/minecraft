"Kyoto" is a song composed by C418 that plays in the Festive Mash-up pack for Legacy Console Edition and New Nintendo 3DS Edition.


== Track description ==

"Kyoto" is a downtempo song in two main parts. The first part features a piano playing a dark bassline, with a melody and countermelody played by a saxophone and a flute, respectively. At the end of the first part, a triangle plays out the melody before it transitions to the second part, which features synthesized strings and a celesta.


== History ==


== Trivia ==
"Kyoto" refers to the Japanese city of the same name, which was formerly the imperial capital of Japan.
