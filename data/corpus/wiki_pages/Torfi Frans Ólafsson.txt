Torfi Frans Ólafsson is the Senior Creative Director of Entertainment at Mojang Studios, known for serving as a producer on A Minecraft Movie, its sequel, Minecraft animated series, and Hættulegur Hraði. He appeared  in Minecraft LIVE - 2026, introducing Minecraft World. Torfi made a cameo appearance in A Minecraft Movie as an inductee in Natalie's Survival Mode Self-Defense classes.
