A pet bee is a pet variant of the bee introduced in 25w14craftmine.


== Spawning ==
A pet bee is spawned next to the player on mine start when the "Pet Bee" Player Unlock is active.


== Drops ==


=== On death ===
Pet bee have no drops.


== Behavior ==
Pet bees have the same AI behavior as bees: they flying around slowly and aimlessly.
