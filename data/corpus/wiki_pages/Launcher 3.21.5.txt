3.21.5 is an update to the Minecraft Launcher that was released on October 17, 2025.


== Additions ==
Added launcher controller support for controllers and the ROG Xbox Ally.


== Fixes ==
MCL-25778 - Chromebook is misspelled as “Chrombook” on the “Buy Minecoins” page on the bottom of the page
MCL-25777 - Minecoins miscapitalized in one string ("Buy MineCoins")
Various other bug fixes & under the hood improvements.
