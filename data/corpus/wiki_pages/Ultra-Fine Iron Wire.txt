Ultra-Fine Iron Wire (超極細の鉄のワイヤー, Chō Gokuboso no Tetsu no Waiyā) is very thin iron-reinforced thread created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
Due to its nature as a trap, the Ultra-Fine Iron Wire is very difficult to see when strung up. However, it resembles regular thread.


== Functionality ==
When strung up in high quantities, the Ultra-Fine Iron Wire can be used as a trap, entangling its opponents. This is particularly effective against larger opponents.
