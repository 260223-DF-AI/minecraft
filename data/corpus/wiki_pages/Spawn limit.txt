To prevent the game from lagging or running out of usable memory in Legacy Console Edition and in New Nintendo 3DS Edition, most entities have a maximum number of how many can exist in a world. If the player attempts to spawn another mob when the maximum number has been reached, a chat message will appear, explaining that the limit has been reached. Only entities which are in loaded chunks are counted.


== In Legacy Console Edition ==
 

Breedable mobs also have a breeding limit, which is lower.


=== Behavior ===
Spawn eggs and fish buckets cannot be used if the limit for the corresponding mob has been reached. Players are unable to breed animals for which the breeding limit has been reached but may still be able to spawn them using spawn eggs if the spawn limit is not reached yet. Thrown eggs are unable to spawn more chickens if the breeding limit is reached. Mooshrooms cannot be sheared if the breeding limit for animals has been reached. Villagers are unable to turn into a zombie villager if the spawn limit for enemies has been reached and will die regardless of difficulty. When experience orbs are spawned while its limit has been reached, the oldest experience orbs will vanish. If TNT is ignited while the limit for primed TNT has been reached, the TNT will vanish. If the limit for falling blocks has been reached, gravity affected blocks will not fall until the amount of falling blocks decreases. Paintings and item frames also have a rendering cap of 100 on the screen, which is divided when using split screen. Attempting to build a wither, snow golem or iron golem while the limit has been reached, causes the blocks to drop as items. Wandering trader spawn eggs do not spawn the trader llamas if the limit for animals is reached. Spawn limits also limit the amount of entities that can go through portals before the chunks on the other side are loaded. This counter is however not saved upon reloading the world. Monster spawners play a sound when failing to spawn mobs due to spawn limits. Silverfish stone does not spawn silverfish when mined while the spawn limit for enemies is reached. Withers may spawn less than 3 wither skeletons when reaching half health when the enemy spawn limit is almost reached.


=== Bypassing ===
Spawn limits may be bypassed by having entities in unloaded chunks. Spawn limit for villagers can be bypassed by curing zombie villagers. By spawning 5 withers in the End while the ender dragon is not alive it is possible to have 6 boss mobs after respawning the ender dragon. Slimes and magma cubes can still split into smaller ones when killed even if the spawn limit has been reached. Triggering a skeleton horse trap bypasses the limit. Pigs and villagers may be struck by a lightning and turn into zombie pigmen or witches even if the spawn limit for enemies has been reached. Evokers can spawn vexes even when the spawn limit for enemies is reached, bypassing the limit. Additionally, zombies and its variants can bypass the limit by spawning reinforcements. Dragon eggs still fall even if the limit for falling blocks has been reached (but are counted to the limit when falling), meaning that an infinite amount of dragon eggs can fall at once. Ocelot spawn eggs used when there are 88 or 89 animals still have a chance to spawn 2 additional baby ocelots, bypassing the limit. Ender pearls may still spawn endermites when the enemy spawn limit is reached. Zombie sieges are still able to spawn when the enemy spawn limit is reached.


== In New Nintendo 3DS Edition ==
Due to the more limited nature of the New Nintendo 3DS hardware, spawn limits are much stricter than Legacy Console Edition and span fewer categories.
