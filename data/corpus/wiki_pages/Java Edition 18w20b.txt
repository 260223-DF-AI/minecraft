18w20b is the thirty-sixth snapshot for Java Edition 1.13, released on May 16, 2018.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Added translations for potted plants, such as "Potted Oak Sapling", "Potted Cactus", etc.
Added the "All rumors are true!" splash.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
The eye of the conduit now shows whether it is hunting for hostile mobs or not: it shows an open eye when it is looking out for hostile mobs, and a closed eye otherwise.
