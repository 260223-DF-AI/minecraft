TU69  is a version of Xbox 360 Edition released on September 11, 2018.  This update brought the Update Aquatic to consoles, and is the last major themed update released to Xbox 360 Edition.
[edit]

== Changes ==
Fall damage sound now plays upon landing in Creative mode just like in Bedrock Edition.
Elder guardians now drop wet sponges again rather than dry ones.
Hitboxes of tall grass, large ferns, lilacs, peonies, rose bushes, and sunflowers have been made smaller to match Bedrock Edition.
Snow now falls like sand or gravel when the supporting block is removed instead of being destroyed.
Trading with a villager now shows an emerald icon above the villager.
Updated the rain sounds to be quieter and more 'realistic'.
Added sounds for beacons.
A smooth transition is now applied to the camera when the player sneaks, flies with an elytra or swims.
Items in water now float up.
Baby villagers now have larger-sized heads to match Bedrock Edition.
Elytra flight is now disabled when touching water to match Bedrock Edition.
Chat messages for players entering or leaving the End are now also sent to the player entering the End, instead of only to other players.
Undead mobs now sink in water.
Skeleton horses can now be ridden underwater and don't need to be tamed or saddled anymore to match Bedrock Edition.
A squid now emits bubble particles when underwater.
A smooth transition is now applied to the camera when the player enters or leaves a bed.
Elytra requires phantom membrane to repair now in an anvil instead of leather.
Baby zombies now burn in the daylight.
Zombie villagers and wither skeletons now have their own sounds.
