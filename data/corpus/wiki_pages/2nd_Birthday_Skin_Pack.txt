2nd Birthday Skin Pack is a skin pack that includes 25 party-themed skins celebrating the second anniversary of Xbox 360 Edition's release.
It was originally available on Xbox 360 Edition from May 9 to May 18, 2014. From May 7 to May 17, 2015, May 9 to May 16, 2016, and May 9 to May 21, 2017 the skin pack was available again for Xbox 360 and Xbox One editions.
Due to the Better Together Update, it was added in Bedrock Edition on September 12, 2017, although it is only available to players who owned it already when they migrated from Legacy Console Edition.
