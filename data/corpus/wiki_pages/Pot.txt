Pot may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Blocks ===
Decorated Pot, a storage block that holds items.
Crafted from any combination of pottery sherds and bricks.
Flower Pot, a decorative block that holds plants, mushrooms, and Nether fungi.
Crafted from bricks.
Cauldron, a block that holds most liquids.
Crafted from iron ingots.


=== Items ===
Water Bottle  a bottle that holds water.
Crafted from glass.
Honey Bottle  a bottle that holds honey.
Potion, Splash Potion, or Lingering Potion  an item that applies effects when used.
Effects applied through brewing.
