The Translator Cape and the Chinese Translator Cape are capes given to notable contributors on the Minecraft translation project in Crowdin.


== Appearance ==
Both capes look identical when viewed from the back, only differing on their undersides. The Translator Cape depicts a pink "T", whereas the Chinese Translator Cape depicts a Chinese flag, similar to the Japanese flag on the underside of cheapsh0t's Cape.


== Distribution ==


=== Translator Cape ===
The Translator Cape is given to proofreaders notable for their contribution.


=== Chinese Translator Cape ===
The Chinese Translator Cape was given to Powup333, Craft_Lawrence, and sjjklh for their contribution in Chinese.
