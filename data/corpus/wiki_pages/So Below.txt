All music files are in Ogg Vorbis.

== Trivia ==
The title "So Below" comes from the phrase "As above, so below", an alchemical reference, like Raine's other two Nether tracks. Raine's use of the phrase for the title of the track contrasts the "umbral, occult nature of the Nether" to the Overworld "above".
