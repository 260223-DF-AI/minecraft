1.16.101 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition, released for Android, iOS, iPadOS, Fire OS on November 23, 2020, and Nintendo Switch on November 24, 2020, which fixes bugs. This version is still compatible with all Bedrock platforms running on 1.16.100.


== Fixes ==
Fixed players unable to connect to Realms when playing on cellular data on devices with cellular data. (MCPE-55405)
Fixed Minecoins not appearing after purchase until after restarting the game.
