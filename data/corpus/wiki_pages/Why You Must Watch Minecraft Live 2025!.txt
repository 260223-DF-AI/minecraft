Why You Must Watch Minecraft Live 2025! (stylized in all caps) is a video promotion for Minecraft LIVE – September 2025, similar in style and tone to a Minecraft Monthly episode.


== Summary ==
The video opens on Vu Bui reminiscing about a poorly cropped picture of himself at Minecraft LIVE – March 2025, which he immediately concedes is bad and throws away. He explains that Minecraft LIVE will be back for the second time that year, exclusively on the game's YouTube channel. To set the tone, he shows highlights from the prior year, including the announcements of Chase the Skies and Vibrant Visuals, and the releases of Spring to Life, A Minecraft Movie, and A Minecraft Movie Live Event.
He confirms that news on the next update will be announced through the stream once again, which he tried to leak to the press ahead of time. After picking the lock he immediately set off an alarm, and leaves behind evidence in the form of the lockpick while casually walking away. Returning to the present, Vu mentions there will be behind the scenes content on The Copper Age, which causes footage of a dog to appear with no connection to the topic at hand whatsoever. Getting back on track, Vu confirms there will be an "epic community pre-show," and the return of the Deep Dig, a segment where he, creators, and developers will all play the new content. To practice, he logs onto Jens Bergensten's account, and accidentally ruins his Hardcore game.
