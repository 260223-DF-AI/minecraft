Colour the World was a MC Championship Twitch Rivals event, organised in collaboration with Twitch and involving Mojang Studios, the fifty-fifth of its history, hosted as a part of TwitchCon EU in Rotterdam, Netherlands.
At the event, Mojang Studios revealed the name of the Chase the Skies game drop, coinciding with the release of a Minecraft Monthly episode that likewise revealed the drop's name. They also showcased its features and presented a trailer.
