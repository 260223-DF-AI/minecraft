A version of Minecraft Infdev was released on June 30, 2010, at 18:35 UTC. It is the second of two versions released on June 30, 2010, during the Infdev phase of development. Notch retroactively decided upon the release of Alpha v1.0.1 that the versions from this day were now part of the new Alpha phase. This is the last version to include gears.


== Changes ==


=== World generation ===
General

Changed sand and gravel generation.


=== General ===
Coordinates

Spawn Y is set to 64 if it is at or below 0.
