2.17.13 is an update to the Launcher released on February 7, 2024.


== Tweaks & improvements ==
Java and Bedrock Quick Play are now available in the Launcher.
Play a Java Edition or Bedrock Edition game world and a quick play icon for that world will automatically show up in the upper right corner of the game's page in the Launcher.
Various UI & bug fixes to ensure dialogs show up correctly.


== Bug fixes ==

MCL-24049 - Launcher string for deleting quick play entries contains a spelling mistake
