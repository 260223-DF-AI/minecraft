Zetta is a self-proclaimed "potioner" and main protagonist of Minecraft: The Dragon. Born and raised in the desert village of Sienna Dunes, her dreams were constantly dashed by the conservative mindset of the community, including her father.


== Appearance ==

Zetta is a teenage girl, described as having dark skin, curly black hair, a blue tunic, and a leather pack.
