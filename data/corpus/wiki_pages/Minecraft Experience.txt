Minecraft Experience refers to various in-person interactive events run by Experience Mod and Supply+Demand, in conjunction with Mojang Studios.


== Events ==


=== Villager Rescue ===

Minecraft Experience: Villager Rescue is an in-person interactive event where participants in groups of up to 25 go through a mission and scavenger hunt through multiple Minecraft biomes and dimensions to rescue villagers. The first event took place in Dallas, Texas, from October 18, 2024, to January 20, 2025. Additional events have occurred in Toronto, London, and Riyadh, with four events planned for Denmark, Mexico, Singapore, and Chicago. Additional locations are planned across the Americas, Europe, and the Asia-Pacific region. It was announced on May 29, 2024, the final day of the 15 Days of Minecraft.


=== Moonlight Trail ===

Minecraft Experience: Moonlight Trail is a nightly in-person interactive event held outdoors, where participants in groups hike through various Minecraft biomes in a trail that goes up a kilometer in length, leading to a final area where participants battle mobs to restore a lighthouse. The first event is scheduled to take place in Buenos Aires, Argentina.
