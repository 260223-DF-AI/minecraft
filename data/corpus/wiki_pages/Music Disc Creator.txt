"Creator" refers to both a song composed by Lena Raine, and a music disc that plays the song when inserted in a jukebox. The music disc is obtained from trial chambers, as a rare reward given by ominous vaults.

== Track description ==

"Creator" begins with a music box melody that plays the main nine-note melody of the song. There is the sound of a music box being wound up, and after several seconds of more music box notes, the instrument fades out as an accordion-like instrument, strings, and a clarinet overtake it. An electric piano plays the main melody of the song, being joined by an acoustic guitar. Later in the song, an electric guitar takes center-stage, along with an upward key change to the song, with increased drums/percussion. At the end, "Creator" transitions from the acoustic guitar melody back to the music box melody from the beginning.


== Usage ==

Like all music discs, using the "Creator" music disc on a jukebox causes the jukebox to play the song.
If a redstone comparator is facing a jukebox playing "Creator", it will emit a redstone signal of 12.
