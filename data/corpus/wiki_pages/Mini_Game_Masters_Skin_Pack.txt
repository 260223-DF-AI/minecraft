Mini Game Masters Skin Pack is a skin pack themed around several teams of three characters, each embodying a mob partaking in some sort of sport, with battlers wearing armor, gliders wearing flightsuits, and tumblers wearing more conventional athletic gear (somewhat reminiscent of roller derby). This skin pack was released in promotion of the mini games, which were included in the Legacy Console Editions.
