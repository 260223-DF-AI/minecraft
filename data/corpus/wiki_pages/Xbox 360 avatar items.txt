Several Minecraft-themed avatar items were available for purchase on the Xbox 360 Marketplace. These included wearable items, such as masks and t-shirts, and props that played short animations and interacted with the player's avatar. 
Additionally, a creeper cap, a Minecraft-styled watch, and a t-shirt with a porkchop design can be obtained as free awards for completing certain in-game tasks. These items were not purchasable on the Marketplace.

== List of purchasable avatar items ==
These items can no longer be purchased due to the closure of the Xbox 360 Marketplace. They can, however, still be re-downloaded and equipped on an avatar if purchased before the closure.
