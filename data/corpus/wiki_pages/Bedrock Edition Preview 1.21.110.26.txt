Beta 1.21.110.26 (ChromeOS, Android) or Preview 1.21.110.26 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox, PlayStation) is the sixth and final beta/Preview version for Bedrock Edition 1.21.111, released on August 26, 2025, which fixes some bugs.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Settings

Added an option (called "Hide Sky Flashes") to hide End flashes when playing with Vibrant Visuals.


== Fixes ==
4 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.21.111

MCPE-223753 – Copper golem is spawned in the air when built sideways.
MCPE-223754 – Copper golem loses oxidation level when built sideways.
From the 1.21.111 development versions

MCPE-225194 – Non-full blocks can be put on shelves.
Other

Fixed a crash when downloading packs from a multiplayer world on Xbox.
