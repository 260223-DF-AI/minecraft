Apple may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Items ===
Apple – a food item that can be eaten by the player or crafted into golden apples.
Golden Apple – a food item that bestows beneficial effects when consumed (Absorption and Regeneration) and is also used to cure a zombie villager under the Weakness effect into a normal villager.
Enchanted Golden Apple – a rare and uncraftable food item that, upon consumption, grants much more powerful effects than the golden apple.


=== Editions ===
Apple TV Edition – a discontinued derivative of Pocket Edition designed to run on tvOS.


== Minecraft Earth ==
Apple – A food item obtained from oak leaves.


== Minecraft Dungeons ==
Apple – a consumable that can be found within various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.
