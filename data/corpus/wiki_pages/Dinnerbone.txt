Nathan 'Dinnerbone' Adams is a Software Engineer at Mojang Studios who works on Java Edition and the Minecraft Launcher. 


== About ==
A common trope attributed to Dinnerbone is that he is commonly displayed as being upside-down. His Twitter avatar is flipped and his user model (as well as any mob named "Dinnerbone") is rendered upside-down. Shortly after starting Bukkit, Dinnerbone was hired by Curse where he worked until joining Mojang Studios on February 28, 2012 to develop the mod API. In October 2015, he announced that he would be "taking a break from Minecraft PC development for a few months." Later, in February 2016, he further elaborated that though he would continue on other projects at Mojang, he had left the Minecraft team. On February 6, 2017, Dinnerbone confirmed he had rejoined the Minecraft team. On July 30, 2022, he claimed that he had been off work for 1.5 years for his health. On February 18, 2025, he announced that he's coming back on developing Minecraft.


== Biography ==
Nathan Adams is originally from Shrewsbury, United Kingdom. He learned programming through creating MSN bots at age 10. After finishing secondary school, Dinnerbone was rejected from college, so he found a job with a tiny web development company, but was made redundant after a few years. 
In 2013 he was engaged to Aleksandra 'Missmarzenia' Zajac. He announced on August 24, 2016 that he and Aleksandra were expecting a baby boy. The baby was later born on October 2, 2016. In some time between 2016 and 2021 they broke up.

In 2019 he obtained Swedish citizenship.


== Videos ==
Dinnerbone was the face of the migration campaign in 2020. During the campaign Minecraft account on Youtube released two videos featuring him as Minecraft character.

== Trivia ==
Dinnerbone is red-green colorblind, which is the most common type of colorblindness.
This is why rubies were replaced by emeralds.
Dinnerbone's username is a tribute to an old friend of his named Teabone (T-bone).
Dinnerbone's favorite color is blue, his favorite foods are lasagna and pizza, and his "guilty pleasure" is milkshakes.
If the player uses a name tag titled "Dinnerbone", on most mobs it will turn upside-down. This naturally extends to himself. The same effect is applied when naming a mob "Grumm".
Dinnerbone could sometimes be found on the MindCrack server with other YouTubers.
Dinnerbone was a member of the Hermitcraft server during its early days.
Dinnerbone's bodyguard in A Fun Announcement by Dinnerbone flipped him right side up. He remains this way throughout both migration videos.
Dinnerbone makes an appearance in the trailer for Element Animation's "T.E.A.V.S.R.P" (The Element Animation Villager Sounds Resource Pack), which would later be used for an April Fools' Day joke.
Dinnerbone changed his Minecraft account name to GormanRoddy on January 7, 2026 before he changed it back 13 hours later.
