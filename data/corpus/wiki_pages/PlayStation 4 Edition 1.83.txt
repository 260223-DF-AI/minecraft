1.83 was an update to PlayStation 4 Edition released on December 18, 2018, which added some features of the Village & Pillage update, and is equivalent to Bedrock Edition 1.8.0.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Mash-up packs
Added "The Nightmare Before Christmas".
Trophies
Added Expansion Pack 8, which contains six new trophies:
(Silver) Do a Barrel Roll – Use Riptide to give yourself a boost.
(Bronze) Marine Biologist – Collect any fish in a bucket.
(Silver) Ahoy! – Discover a Shipwreck.
(Bronze) Top of the World – Build a tower of Scaffolding that reaches the maximum build height.
(Bronze) Where Have You Been? – Receive a gift in the morning from a tamed Cat.
(Gold) Zoologist – Breed two Pandas with Bamboo.


=== Mobs ===
Pandas
Spawn in jungles.
Eat bamboo and cake items.
Have different personalities – lazy, playful, aggressive, or worried.
Have a rare brown variant.
Stray cats
Added cats as a separate mob from ocelots.
Spawn in villages.
Can be tamed with fish.
Have dyeable collars, much like wolves.
Bring gifts to the player.
Will scare off phantoms.


=== Blocks ===
Bamboo
Can be used to construct scaffolding or feed pandas.
Can be found while fishing in jungles and will appear in some chests.
Natural world spawning will come in a future update.
Scaffolding
A new climbable block that can be crafted using bamboo.


=== Items ===
Dyes
Added white dye, blue dye, brown dye, and black dye.
Version exclusive: Items previously used as dyes (bone meal, lapis lazuli, cocoa beans, and ink sacs) can still be used.
Spawn eggs
Panda
Cat


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
Ocelots
No longer turn into cats, but can still have their trust earned by being fed fish.


=== Blocks ===
Grass block
Now renders 3d on the side.
Packed ice
Can now be crafted with 9 ice.


=== Items ===
Cactus Green
Renamed to green dye.
Dandelion Yellow
Renamed to yellow dye.
Rose Red
Renamed to red dye.


=== General ===
Mobs and players no longer take knockback during their death animation.
Fishing
Catches have been changed in jungle biomes.
Now only raw cod and raw salmon can be caught as fish in a jungle.
Bamboo and cocoa beans can now be caught as a junk item from fishing in a jungle.
Game Chat
Removed Game Chat and its option at the sound settings. Players must now use the console's party chat if they want to do a voice chat.
Creative inventory
Awkward potions are once again obtainable from the Creative inventory.


== Fixes ==
3 issues fixed
Fixed a crash that would occur while near snow biomes.
Fixed the barrier blocks in the tutorial level.
Fixed a crash that would occur when TNT blew up a fence and a shulker box.
