Minecraft World are upcoming theme parks created through a collaboration between Mojang Studios and Merlin Entertainments. The collaboration was announced on November 19, 2024 by Merlin Entertainments and by Mojang Studios at Minecraft Live  March 2026 on March 21, 2026.
The experience is scheduled to launch in the United Kingdom at Chessington World of Adventures in 2027. A United States location is planned for 20262027, with Merlin Entertainments intending to eventually expand the concept to additional destinations worldwide.


== Overview ==
The collaboration will bring Minecraft-themed immersive and interactive entertainment attractions in fixed locations around the globe. This will include digitally enabled real-life experiences with the help of 'Minebands', touchpoints that will allow guests to unlock exclusive in-game content, guest accommodations, retail, food & beverage, and attractions. It is also said to have the largest Minecraft-themed store in the world, and the first-ever minecart roller coaster.
The theme park is now under construction, with YouTubers, including DanTDM, LDShadowLady, Aimsey, Grian, GoodTimesWithScar and SmallishBeans, helping out on research and foundation.
