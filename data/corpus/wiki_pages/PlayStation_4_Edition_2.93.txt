2.93 was an update for PlayStation 4 Edition released on August 13, 2024, which fixed bugs, alongside Bedrock Edition 1.21.20. It is accessible through the Editions button in the PlayStation 4 version of Bedrock Edition.


== Fixes ==
Fixed item and block corruption when converting worlds to the PlayStation 4 version of Bedrock Edition.
