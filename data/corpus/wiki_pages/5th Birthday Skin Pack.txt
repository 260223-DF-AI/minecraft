5th Birthday Skin Pack is a skin pack that includes 28 skins, 25 of which depicting developers at 4J Studios. It was available from May 9 to May 21, 2017 for Xbox 360 and Xbox One editions, celebrating the fifth anniversary of Xbox 360 Edition's release.
Beginning December 19, 2017, this skin pack was released for a limited time on PlayStation 3,  PlayStation 4, and PlayStation Vita editions as the 4th Birthday Skin Pack, and on Wii U and Nintendo Switch editions as the 2nd Birthday Skin Pack.
Due to the Better Together Update, it was added in Bedrock Edition on September 12, 2017, although it is only available to players who owned it already when they migrated from Legacy Console Edition.
