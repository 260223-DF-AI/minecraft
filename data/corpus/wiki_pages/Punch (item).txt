Punch is a joke item added in 26w14a.


== Obtaining ==
It is automatically obtained and appears in the hotbar when the player starts up a world. It also appears in the hotbar after the player dies.


== Usage ==
Used to mine and attack (as most other tools prevent this).

== Issues ==
Issues relating to "Punch (item)" are not maintained on the bug tracker because it is an April Fools' joke, and is therefore not in the newest stable version or snapshot. Issues reported there are closed as "Invalid".
