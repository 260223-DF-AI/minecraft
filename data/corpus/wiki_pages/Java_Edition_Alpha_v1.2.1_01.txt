Alpha v1.2.1_01 is a version of Java Edition Alpha released on November 5, 2010, to fix a bug relating to armor.
This version is duplicated inside the launcher, as attempting to play a version labeled "Alpha v1.2.1" downloads and launches v1.2.1_01 instead.


== Fixes ==
1 bug fixed

Fixed armor not having an effect on the damage taken.
