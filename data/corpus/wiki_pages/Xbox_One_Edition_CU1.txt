CU1  is a version of Xbox One Edition released on September 5, 2014.  
[edit]


== Features ==

Based on Xbox 360 Edition TU16.[verify]

Worlds saved onto cloud storage from  Xbox 360 can be uploaded to  Xbox One Edition.
Worlds have a maximum size of 51205120.
The  Xbox 360 has a maximum world size of 864864.
Render distance is 18 chunks as opposed to 10 chunks on Xbox 360.
