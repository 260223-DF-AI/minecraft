The Artificial Sun (人工太陽, Jinkō Taiyō) is a large artificial light source created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Artificial Sun resembles a massive, featureless Block of Coal, at least three times the size of Nico. Unlike other blocks in the series it is not drawn with a pixelated texture.


== Functionality ==
When ignited, the Artificial Sun causes a blinding flash of light, though only for a second. If ignited while in the air, this flash of light is powerful enough to act as a fake sun, causing undead mobs to burn even at night.
