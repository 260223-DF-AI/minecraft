1.21.5 Release Candidate 2 (known as 1.21.5-rc2 in the launcher) is the second and final release candidate for Java Edition 1.21.5, released on March 24, 2025 to fix the final issues before the Spring to Life drop releases.


== Fixes ==
2 issues fixed
From the 1.21.5 development versions

MC-295665  There is no blending in new chunks
From the previous development version

MC-295688  Piston movement is not applying block effects to entities
