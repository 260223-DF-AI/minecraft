v0.1.2 alpha is the first version released for iOS devices.


== Changes ==
Third person view "Back Only" for all devices.
Mirrored controls for left-handed players.
In-game sound toggle.
The license verification failure screen additionally contains the text "You need to be connected to the internet once while you start the game."


== Fixes ==
Fixed stairs being invisible when located within a chunk section with no other blocks.


== Internal Version ==
The internal version of v0.1.2 alpha is 0.1.2(1025) and has a size of 1.57 MB.


== J-version ==

v0.1.2j alpha was released on Google Play on an unknown release date. The internal version is 0.1.2(1023) and has a size of 1.98 MB.


== J-version demo ==
A free J-version "lite" demo of v0.1.2 alpha was released on Google Play on an unknown release date. In mid-2014, it was removed from the Play/App stores. The internal version is the same as the j-version.


== Demo ==
A free "lite" demo of v0.1.2 alpha was released on Google Play on an unknown release date. In mid-2014, it was removed from the Play store. The internal version is not currently known.
A free "lite" demo of v0.1.2 alpha was also released on the iOS App Store on November 21, 2011. In mid-2014, it was removed from the App store.
