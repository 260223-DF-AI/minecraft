The final version of Indev 0.31 was released on February 6, 2010, at 14:37 UTC, which fixed text rendering causing FPS dropping.
This is the last version of 0.31, as the next version, Indev 20100206-2034, was the first version to remove the "0.31" label, becoming just "Minecraft Indev".


== Fixes ==
1 bug fixed

Fixed text rendering causing FPS dropping.


== Trivia ==
This was the last version without farming.
