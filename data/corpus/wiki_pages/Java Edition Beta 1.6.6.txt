Beta 1.6.6 is a version of Java Edition Beta released on May 31, 2011, which contains minor changes and fixes some of the bugs and crashes in Beta 1.6.5.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Options

Added a "Performance" section in video settings.
Can be set to "Max FPS", "Balanced", or "Power saver".


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Glowstone

Changed the material from "glass" to "stone".
This means the player needs a pickaxe to get resources from it.
This also means the player can now place redstone, torches, levers, etc. on the block.
Now drops 2–4 glowstone dust when broken.
Can now be crafted with 4 glowstone dust.

White wool

Is now crafted using 4 string.


=== Items ===
Bone meal

Can grow grass and flowers on grass blocks.


=== Non-mob entities ===
Boats

Float up to the surface quicker.
When falling down into water, they lose vertical momentum much faster.
Crashing into soul sand on a boat now kills the player, but keeps the boat intact.


== Fixes ==
3 bugs fixed

Fixed beds not working very well in multiplayer.
Destroying a boat no longer spawns the player too low, causing them to sometimes fall through the ground.
⇧ Shift+ clicking a tool into a chest no longer restores it to new.


== Trivia ==
The first version of Xbox 360 Edition (TU0) and MinecraftEdu mod 0.1 is equivalent to this version.
