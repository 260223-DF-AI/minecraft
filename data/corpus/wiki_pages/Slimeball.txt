A slimeball is a crafting ingredient commonly dropped by slimes, and can be sneezed out by pandas.


== Obtaining ==


=== Mob loot ===
In Bedrock Edition, wandering traders have a chance of 8.5% (increased by 1% per level of Looting) to drop 1 slimeball when killed while showing the item as trade offer, if the player holds an emerald.

==== Pandas ====
Baby pandas have a 1700 chance of dropping one slimeball when sneezing.


=== Crafting ===


=== Trading ===
Wandering traders may sell 1 slimeball for 4 emeralds.


== Usage ==


=== Crafting ingredient ===


=== Breeding ===
Slimeballs can be used to breed frogs and reduce the remaining growth duration of tadpoles by 10%. Both mobs also follow a player holding a slimeball.


=== Mob interaction ===

Slimeballs can be fed to small sulfur cubes to speed up their growth, in which they become a large sulfur cube. While holding a slimeball, small sulfur cubes will be attracted towards the player.
