Beta 1.6.2 is a version of Java Edition Beta released on May 26, 2011, to fix an item duplication bug that existed in Beta 1.6 and Beta 1.6.1.


== Fixes ==
1 bug fixed

Fixed an item duplication bug when picking up any equipment that is damaged. In Beta 1.6 and Beta 1.6.1, damaged equipment would make an item with a stack count of 0 when dropped, which the player could pick up infinitely.
