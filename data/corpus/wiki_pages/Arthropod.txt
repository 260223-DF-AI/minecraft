Arthropod is an in-game classification covering all invertebrate, bug-like mobs.


== Properties ==
All arthropods share the two following properties.

Arthropods take extra damage and receive Slowness IV when hit with a weapon enchanted with Bane of Arthropods.
In Java Edition, all of these mobs are part of the #arthropod entity tag. In Bedrock Edition, all of these mobs are part of the arthropod family.
