Paper is an item crafted from sugar cane, required for crafting books, maps, cartography tables, firework rockets and banner patterns.

=== Villager gifts ===
In Java Edition, cartographer villagers may give paper to players with the Hero of the Village effect.


== Usage ==


=== Crafting ingredient ===


=== Cartography table usage ===
Paper can be used to zoom out a map by one level in a cartography table. This method of zooming out map is 8 times more efficient than using a crafting table. In Bedrock Edition, it can also be used to create an empty map or an empty locator map. This is also cheaper than the equivalent crafting recipes.
