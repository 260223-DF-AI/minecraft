Red Nether brick stairs are a decorative stairs variant of red Nether bricks that does not generate naturally and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Red Nether brick stairs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.

=== Stonecutting ===


== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Red Nether brick stairs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
