v0.16.0 alpha build 2 (labelled v0.15.90 alpha build 1 in-game and 0.15.90.1 internally) is the second build version released for v0.16.0, also known as the Boss Update. It removes most of the Education Edition features that were accidentally added in the previous build.


== Changed ==
Border block, chalkboard, camera and portfolio completely removed
Removed the NPC spawn egg from the Creative inventory.
