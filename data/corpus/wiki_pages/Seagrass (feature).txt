Seagrass is a feature that places a large cluster of seagrass in various underwater biomes.


== Generation ==

Seagrass features generate on most ocean floors. They appear in a pseudo-circular pattern and heavily overlap with other clusters in a way that creates large blankets of seagrass. They are composed of seagrass and tall seagrass. The percentage of tall seagrass in the feature is determined by percentage value in the configured feature.
