"Lilypad" is a song composed by Amos Roddy that plays in the Overworld.


== Locations ==


== Track description ==

This track shares same melody as some of Amos Roddy's previous game soundtracks he made. Previously heard in Citizen Sleeper's Relink (Track 20), Possible Futures (Track 23), and Citizen Sleeper 2: Starward Vector's Imagined Futures (Track 34), with electronic instruments and an ambient composition style that resembles the Citizen Sleeper series' music.
