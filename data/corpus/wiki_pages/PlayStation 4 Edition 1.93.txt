1.93 was an update to PlayStation 4 Edition released on June 25, 2019, which fixed bugs.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Mash-up packs
Added the Toy Story mash-up pack.


== Fixes ==
8 issues fixed
Fixed a crash that occurred while near a village.
Fixed a crash that occurred when the player goes from the Nether to the Overworld.
Fixed villagers having a chance to change their trade deals after saving/loading.
Fixed scaling maps that would give you a map far away from your current position.
Fixed iron golems not spawning in loaded villages.
Fixed chests splitting and functioning as separate chests.
Fixed stone slab recipe being incorrect in classic crafting.
Fixed villager trading when one player is in the End or the Nether.


== Trivia ==
The release date of this version coincides with the day that both seasons of Minecraft: Story Mode have been discontinued.
