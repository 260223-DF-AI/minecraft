The Legacy Skin Pack is a skin pack on Bedrock Edition consisting of 22 skins that were previously part of skin packs on Legacy Console Edition. It includes all skins from the Default Skin Pack except Athlete Steve and Athlete Alex. At some point, this skin pack was delisted.
