1.RV-Pre1 is an April Fools' joke pre-release, supposedly the first and only pre-release for 1.RV, also known as the Trendy Update, released on March 31, 2016. This version is a fork of 1.9.2.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 USB charger block

Functions the same as a redstone block, emitting a redstone signal with strength 15.
Crafted from 7 iron ingots, 1 gold ingot and 1 redstone dust.


=== Items ===
 Reality vision

Equipped via the helmet armor slot.
When equipped, it shows a 3D minimap.
Displays a vision overlay (similar to the pumpkin overlay).
Unlike the pumpkin overlay, this cannot be turned off by pressing F1.
Activates a shader when equipped.
Signs display the text "OBEY" while glasses are equipped, a reference to the science fiction movie They Live.
Stackable up to 64.
 Ankle monitor

Equipped via the boots armor slot.
When equipped in Survival mode, they can't be taken off.
Can be taken off in Creative mode.
Gives the wearer Slowness I and prints messages in chat if the wearer is far away from spawn at night, or in another dimension.
The Slowness effect will expire and the printed messages will cease if the wearer returns to spawn, even at night.
Stackable up to 64.
 Smarter Watch

Equipped on the off-hand slot.
Shows the time above the hotbar where item tooltips are shown in human readable format (18:00, and 22:00 for example).
Push notifications in the chat whenever a statistic reaches a significant milestone.
Other messages are shown when the player takes damage and sometimes even shows item specific information in chat when items are crafted.
Stackable up to 64.


== Changes ==
Mobs

Zombies and skeletons now have a chance of being equipped with any of the 3 equipment items added.
When they wear the reality vision, they do not attack the player, even if the player attacks them, nor do they retaliate against wolves, snow golems and iron golems. They also don't burn in sunlight.
Zombies with reality vision still attack villagers.
Zombies with reality vision can be "called" by zombies without reality vision to attack the player, tamed wolves, snow golems and iron golems.
Skeletons do not run away from wolves when wearing reality vision.
Endermen and Creepers now have a chance of being equipped with reality vision
Endermen do not react to being attacked by the player[needs testing], but are still aggravated by looking at them without wearing a pumpkin.
They will still attempt to teleport away from environmental damage, like lava (to varying success).
Creepers are passive, do not retaliate and aren't scared away by ocelots,
They can still be ignited by flint and steel.
Signs

Signs have a different unique texture.
Sign text is now green.
Signs read "OBEY" while reality vision is equipped. This is a reference to science fiction movie They Live.


== Issues ==
1.RV-Pre1 is not supported due to being an April Fools' joke, and therefore issues affecting it will be closed as "Invalid" in the issue tracker.


== Trivia ==
Despite being an April Fools' joke version, this version was released on March 31.
Sounds are saved in minecraft/sounds/buzzwords/sound_name.ogg where sound_name is replaced with the actual name of the sound, for example equipvr.ogg.
The overlay for the reality vision in the assets folder, cantseeshitcaptain.png, is a reference to a popular meme from the TV show Star Trek.
This is a rare occurrence of official swearing in Minecraft.
The shader for the reality vision can be disabled by using F4.
However, when the player goes into third-person view, the shader is activated again.
This version was previously removed from the launcher for some time, but was added back in September 2018.
Curiously, this pre-release uses a #.[letters]-Pre# format instead of a #.##-pre#
This version features in the lowest number of additions and changes among all April Fool's Day joke versions.
