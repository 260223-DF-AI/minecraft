26.3 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on March 2, 2026, which fixes some bugs.


== Fixes ==
3 issues fixed
MCPE-232930  Netherite spear and netherite sword have the same name in French (France).
MCPE-235956  Names of mobs and items added from Mounts of Mayhem are no longer translated in release 26.0.
Other

Fixed a crash that could occur when loading a world or Realm.
