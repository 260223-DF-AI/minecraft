Stony peaks are warmer mountain peak biomes completely made out of stone and calcite strips.


== Description ==
Stony peaks are a warmer variation of both the jagged peaks and the frozen peaks biomes that generates when a mountain peak is in the lukewarm biome temperature range. It generates in savannas, some forests, some plains, and jungles, and it doesn't generate snowy slopes, groves, meadows or cherry groves in the slopes of the mountain, instead, savanna plateaus, forests, and jungles often generate around it. It is mainly covered by stone with large strips of calcite and exposed ores, making it the only biome where calcite generates above ground.
With Vibrant Visuals, stony peaks use warmish atmospherics, lighting, and color grading similar to jungles, although they use default volumetric fog. This makes the night sky appear slightly less dark, and moonlight is much brighter blue.
Stony peaks are the third rarest Overworld biome and the rarest mountain biome, making up around 0.1% of the Overworld by area.
