The etho slab is a joke slab introduced exclusively in Java Edition 2.0. Its name is a reference to the YouTube channel EthosLab.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Unlike normal TNT, etho slabs do not break instantly.


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==
Etho slabs behave like TNT, with the only difference being that upon explosion there is a 25% chance of spawning a falling anvil one block above the nearest player within a 20 block radius, which is a reference to EthosLab's fondness in pranking his friends with a falling anvil.
