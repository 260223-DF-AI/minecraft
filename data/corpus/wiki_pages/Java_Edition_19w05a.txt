19w05a is the nineteenth snapshot for Java Edition 1.14, released on January 30, 2019, which adds wandering traders and trader llamas.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
Spawn eggs

 Trader llama spawn egg
Spawns trader llamas.
 Wandering trader spawn egg
Spawns wandering traders.


=== Mobs ===
 Trader llamas

Are the same as normal llamas but have blue and gold decorations.
Can be equipped with carpets as normal llamas. Original garb reappears when the carpet is taken off.
Hostile towards all illagers (except for ravagers), zombies, zombie pigmen, zombie villagers, husks, and drowned.
Separate entities from llamas.
Temperamental around players.
 Wandering traders

Found in random places around the world.
Provides a random variety of biome-specific item trades.
Trading times are limited, locked trades are not unlockable by conducting other trades.
Has 6 trades, all unlocked up front, once used up cannot be reopened.
Always surrounded by a few trader llamas.
Separate entities from villagers.
Only one exists per world and despawns after 48000 ticks (40 minutes).


=== Commands ===
Two new block tags: dragon_immune and wither_immune.


=== General ===
Sounds

Added new placing and breaking sounds for lanterns.
New placement sounds for the sweet berry bush.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Composters

Changed how often the player fills a layer.
Items which used to have a 10% chance now have a 30% chance; 20% changed to 50%; 50% changed to 65%; and 80% changed to 85%.


=== Items ===
Spawn eggs

Renamed illager beast spawn egg to ravager spawn egg.
Changed the ID of ravager spawn egg to ravager_spawn_egg.


=== Mobs ===
Ravagers

Renamed from "Illager Beast".
ID changed to ravager.
Now have their own sounds on the client side.


=== Commands ===
/forceload query can now be executed by command blocks and functions.
