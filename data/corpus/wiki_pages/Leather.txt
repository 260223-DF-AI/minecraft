Leather is an item used to craft bundles, books, saddles, armor and other items.


== Obtaining ==


=== Mob loot ===
Java Edition:Bedrock Edition:
A fox sometimes spawns holding leather, which always drops upon death. Alternatively, a player can drop a food item, causing the fox to drop the leather in favor of that food.


=== Generated loot ===


=== Crafting ===


=== Fishing ===
Leather can be obtained as a "junk" item from fishing, with a 1% default chance of getting it.


=== Bartering ===
Adult piglins may barter 2-4 leather with a chance of ~8.53% (40⁄469) when given a gold ingot.


=== Villager gifts ===
in Java Edition, leatherworker villagers throw leather at players under the Hero of the Village effect.

=== Repairing ===
Leather is the repair item for the leather armor, and thus can be used to repair the following items in an anvil:

Leather cap
Leather tunic
Leather pants
Leather boots
