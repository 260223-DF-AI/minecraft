Raid Omen is a status effect that causes raids to start.


== Causes ==


== Effect ==
When the Raid Omen expires, a raid begins. In Java Edition the center is the position where the player triggered the start of a raid after the Raid Omen expiration. In Bedrock Edition, it is the village center. See Raid for details. 
In Java Edition, Raid Omen's potency alters the raid composition and rewards. See Raid for details.  
Raid Omen is a status effect and can be removed through all conventional methods, such as drinking milk or dying.
The player can still receive the Raid Omen effect in Peaceful difficulty via commands (all pillager captains despawn in Peaceful), but does not trigger a raid if in a village.


=== Animation ===

In Bedrock Edition, when a player receives the effect, the effect's icon appears on the screen in a short animation, similar to the animation that plays when a totem of undying activates.


== Immune mobs ==
Withers, ender dragons and illagers are immune to Raid Omen.
All mobs except for the player are unaffected by Raid Omen.

== Trivia ==
The immediate effects of a raid omen are entirely negative (summoning waves of destructive illagers) but the effect is considered neutral. This is probably because defeating a raid results in the positive Hero of the Village status effect, offsetting the initial negative effects of the raid.
