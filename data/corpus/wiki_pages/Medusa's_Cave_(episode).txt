Medusa's Cave is the second episode of the second season of Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Summary ==
On Medusa's Island, CommuniKate presents the audience to Medusa's Cave until she gets stopped by Quinn, who reminds her of no longer being the host and of the "bad things that can happen". CommuniKate soon feels guilty for being behind the same thing that happened to Quinn and her friends. Durango, however, finds the adventure exciting, and tries to convince Zed with a series of zombie grunts. Quinn then tells CommuniKate that, although she lost control of the show, she can "start fresh" which, in Kate's viewpoint, is becoming a superstar, which is evidenced by her showing off on a mirror.
In the cave, Durango talks to Zed with grunts, and as the team advanced, they spot a trio of elderly women gathering up. Startled, CommuniKate jumps on Zed and mistakes the women for three Medusas. Quinn corrects her error by telling her that they are the three Stygian witches, which are fabled to be capable of telling the future. Excited, Kate tries asking them about her becoming a superstar or a mega superstar, angering one of them. Quinn immediately informs her that Stygian witches love eating flesh, chasing the team slowly, and wear blindfolds to focus on the future more than the present. Durango soon gives Zed a wooden pickaxe and grunts him to dig a lava ditch to stop the witches and continue.
After reaching the end of the cave, Durango finds the chest holding the first enchanted ender eye, and CommuniKate assumes that it's time to show off in the hidden cameras. Suddenly, Medusa jumps out of nowhere and in front of her, causing her to run out of fear. Quinn warns the rest of her friends not to look onto Medusa's eyes, but Durango and Zed run through anyway. As Medusa flashes her gaze on Zed, he soon steps on a pressure plate, causing a wall of stone bricks to surround him. The same occurs to Durango, causing Quinn's panic.
After CommuniKate ran away of fear, Quinn finds a wooden sword and a shield, which she uses in battle against Medusa. In an interview, CommuniKate affirms that she is no longer a hero and she has "a lot of trouble primping", which soon gets her excited. Meanwhile, as Quinn fights longer, Medusa lands on her head, dropping her items. CommuniKate soon arrives with her mirror in order to cause Medusa to stare at her own eyes and trapping herself. Then, she and Quinn mine the walls to save Durango and Zed, and the former claims the enchanted ender eye.
Meanwhile in Poseidon's Ocean Monument, Poseidon tells his son that everything went according to plan. Saddened by the sight of the prisoners' pillars lowering toward the Kraken, Theseus tells his father that what he did is a little wrong, fearing Zeus' discovery to the plan. He is soon silenced by Poseidon, who tells him that all that matters is that they worked together. He then summons his participants through a prismarine portal to the Sunken Ruins where they try to reach the surface out of fear of drowning.
