Meat is a type of food which can be eaten by a player or wolf. It can refer to the following items:

Beef (Cooked), a meat from cows.
Pork (Cooked), a meat from pigs and hoglins.
Chicken (Cooked), a potentially poisonous meat from chickens.
Cod (Cooked), a meat obtained by killing cod or through fishing.
Salmon (Cooked), a meat obtained by killing salmon or through fishing.
Mutton (Cooked), a meat from sheep.
Rabbit (Cooked), a meat from rabbits.
Rotten Flesh, a meat from zombies, zombified piglins, zoglins, drowned, husks, camel husks, zombie horses, and zombie nautiluses.
Tropical Fish, a rare fish.
Pufferfish, a toxic uncommon fish.
Spider Eye, a toxic meat obtained by killing spiders and cave spiders. This cannot be eaten by wolves, but armadillos can eat it.
