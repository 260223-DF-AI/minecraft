Minecraft x Vibrant Visuals in Marketplace is a trailer promoting various add-ons, texture packs, and mash-ups.


== Summary ==
The video opens inside a frame featuring various mobs and the Marketplace logo before entering the frame. Inside is a village with a pumpkin-shaped house, and the sky changes textures at a fast rate. Afterwards, showcases of various texture packs, then add-ons, are shown.
