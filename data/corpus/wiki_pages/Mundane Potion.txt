A mundane potion is a potion that provides no effects. In Bedrock Edition, it can be used to brew potions of Weakness.

== Usage ==


=== Base potion ===
In Bedrock Edition:
Only usable as an alternative base for potion of Weakness.
Long mundane potions cannot be obtained in Survival mode. It can only be brewed into an extended potion of Weakness.


=== Lingering potion ===
In Bedrock Edition, a lingering mundane potion acts similar to the lingering water bottle, being able to extinguish burning entities and damage endermen, blazes, and snow golems, but without the ability to put out fires.
