26.11 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on March 26, 2026, for Windows only, which fixes a bug with mouse sensitivity.


== Fixes ==
1 issue fixed
MCPE-235748  Mouse camera sensitivity is significantly higher than in previous versions.


== Trivia ==
This is the only hotfix in Bedrock Edition that received its own vanilla resource pack and behavior pack. Apart from required files, they are completely empty.
