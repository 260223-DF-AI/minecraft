Grey DeLisle, born Erin Grey Van Oosbree, is an American voice actress known for her roles in various animated series. Among others, she has voiced Vicky from The Fairly Odd Parents, Azula in Avatar: The Last Airbender, Daphne Blake in the Scooby-Doo franchise, Emily Elizabeth in Clifford the Big Red Dog, Catwoman in the Batman: Arkham series, and the Simpsons characters Martin Prince and Sherri & Terri.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
In 2015, Grey was cast as Ellegaard the Redstone Engineer in Minecraft: Story Mode. A veteran of the Order of the Stone, her role in the story can vary drastically based off the player's choices.


== Trivia ==
One of her most notable roles, Azula, would later appear in the Marketplace map Avatar Legends.
Although she did not take over their voice roles until 2019, Martin, Sherri and Terri all appear in The Simpsons Skin Pack.
