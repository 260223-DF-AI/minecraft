An enchantment tag is a group of enchantments. Enchantment tags are used in loot tables, to configure exclusive enchantments, and other gameplay features.


== List of tags ==


=== curse ===
These enchantments have red colored description and cannot be removed with a grindstone.


=== double_trade_price ===
These enchantments get charged twice as much in villager trades.


=== in_enchanting_table ===
These enchantments can be obtained from Enchanting Table.


=== non_treasure ===
Used in the #in_enchanting_table, #on_mob_spawn_equipment, #on_random_loot, #on_traded_equipment, and #tradeable tags.


=== on_mob_spawn_equipment ===
These enchantments can be found on spawned mobs' equipment.


=== on_random_loot ===
These enchantments can be found on naturally generated equipment from loot tables.


=== on_traded_equipment ===
These enchantments can be found on equipment sold by villagers.


=== prevents_bee_spawns_when_mining ===
Used to prevent bees from coming out when mining Bee Nest or Beehive.


=== prevents_decorated_pot_shattering ===
Used to prevent decorated pots from dropping bricks and sherds when broken with a tool.


=== prevents_ice_melting ===
Used to prevent Ice turning into water instead of dropping itself when broken.


=== prevents_infested_spawns ===
Used to prevent infested blocks from spawning silverfish when broken.


=== smelts_loot ===
Used in entity loot tables as a condition for the minecraft:furnace_smelt function.


=== tooltip_order ===
The order of enchantments to display in item's tooltip. Does not change the order in data component format.


=== tradeable ===
These enchantments can be sold by villagers.


=== treasure ===
Used in the #double_trade_price tag.


=== exclusive_set/armor ===
Enchantments in this tag are mutually exclusive with each other.


=== exclusive_set/boots ===
Enchantments in this tag are mutually exclusive with each other.


=== exclusive_set/bow ===
Enchantments in this tag are mutually exclusive with each other.


=== exclusive_set/crossbow ===
Enchantments in this tag are mutually exclusive with each other.


=== exclusive_set/damage ===
Enchantments in this tag are mutually exclusive with each other.


=== exclusive_set/mining ===
Enchantments in this tag are mutually exclusive with each other.


=== exclusive_set/riptide ===
Enchantments in this tag are mutually exclusive with riptide.


=== trades/desert_common ===
These enchantments can be sold by desert villagers from Novice to Expert levels.


=== trades/jungle_common ===
These enchantments can be sold by jungle villagers from Novice to Expert levels.


=== trades/plains_common ===
These enchantments can be sold by plains villagers from Novice to Expert levels.


=== trades/savanna_common ===
These enchantments can be sold by savanna villagers from Novice to Expert levels.


=== trades/snow_common ===
These enchantments can be sold by snow villagers from Novice to Expert levels.


=== trades/swamp_common ===
These enchantments can be sold by swamp villagers from Novice to Expert levels.


=== trades/taiga_common ===
These enchantments can be sold by taiga villagers from Novice to Expert levels.
