Founder's Cape is a skin pack that consists of two skins equipped with a golden cape depicting a creeper face. During MINECON Live 2019 it was available for free for a limited time in the Marketplace to promote the upcoming launch of the Character Creator. When the Character Creator launched, all owners of this skin pack received the Founder's Cape as a Character Creator item.
