1.21.92 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on June 19, 2025 on most platforms, June 20, 2025 on Xbox and Fire OS and June 24, 2025 for Nintendo Switch. This version fixes some bugs.


== Fixes ==
6 issues fixed
MCPE-220496  Tridents no longer damage mobs after being moved by a piston or trapdoor in certain situations
MCPE-221318  Skyboxes in texture packs do not render
MCPE-222048  Joining a world in splitscreen causes the secondary player to use a guest account
Other

Fixed an issue that could cause players to get disconnected from multiplayer worlds when attempting to add split-screen players.
Items that are less than 16 pixels in width or height now render properly again.
Fixed to restore original behavior where null components in entity definition files were allowed to parse and load correctly.
