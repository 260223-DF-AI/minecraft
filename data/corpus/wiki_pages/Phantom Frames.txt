Phantom Frames is a tie-in DLC to The Garden Awakens, released under the Expect the unexpected campaign, as both an add-on for Bedrock Edition and a data pack for Java Edition.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
 Phantom Frames Add-On‌[BE  only]
Appears in the inventory upon starting the world or activating the add-on.
Gives an explanation of the add-on's features.


=== Mobs ===
  Merchant‌[BE  only]
Spawns during night.
Can be traded with, offering only magical paintings.


==== Entities ====
 Magical Painting
Requires 3x3 wall (similar to a painting) to be placed.
Allows the player to travel to a random location across the Overworld, setting up a permanent link between the two portals.
Have a chance to show a mysterious, white-eyed figure appear for a short time before disappearing.
