v0.2.2 alpha is a minor update to Minecraft: Pocket Edition that was released on March 17, 2012 to fix some of the bugs and crashes in v0.2.1 alpha2. This version was released for Android devices only.


== Fixes ==
Fixed a bug where the game would not open properly on some devices.
Fixed a bug that occurred on the Acer Iconia A500 tablet.


== Internal version ==
The internal version is 0.2.2(2025), and the size is 2.83 MB.
