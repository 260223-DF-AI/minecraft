1.18.32 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released only for Android, Fire, and Nintendo Switch on May 5-6, 2022, which fixes two performance issues. This version is compatible with all Bedrock platforms running on 1.18.30 and 1.18.31.


== Fixes ==
Improved game performance on certain Android devices (MCPE-142934).
Addressed chunk loading and freeze issues in Nintendo Switch (MCPE-154110).
