A version of Minecraft Indev was released on February 7, 2010, announced on TWoN at 10:58 UTC.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Farmland

Non-full blocks now turn it to dirt.
Glass

Is opaque to light coming through it.
Rain no longer goes through glass.
Water

Non-solid blocks get destroyed by water, although breaking them in this way causes nothing to drop.


=== Gameplay ===
Music

Reimplemented music.
Is now ambient.
Starts playing in the spot the player was in and gets quieter when the player moves away from the spot.


== Technical ==
General

Sound system is switched to use OpenAL.


== Bugs ==
1 bug reported

Water no longer spreads.
