McDonald's Add-On is an add-on promoting the McDonald's x A Minecraft Movie collaboration which was obtained by purchasing the adult A Minecraft Movie Meal through the McDonald's app and opting into emails in the app's settings. The add-on code would be sent via email after a meal is purchased. Two million qualifying purchases could be given the add-on. The add-on ceased distribution on May 6, 2025.
