2.1.1143 (Windows), 2.1.1144 (macOS), or 2.1.1139 (Linux) is an update to the launcher released on June 21, 2018.


== Additions ==
Added "Shakespearan English - Kingdom of England" as new language.


== Fixes ==
MCL-5291: Error Code 5 - Problem moving tmplauncher.tmp to Minecraft.exe.
MCL-6129: Outdated digital signature by Mojang for "MinecraftInstaller.msi".
MCL-7387: First login of fresh install is not remembered (Linux launcher only).
MCL-9304: Linux: ICU version issues.
MCL-9319: Launcher crashes after staying on and exiting the game.


== Reupload ==
2.1.11xx was reuploaded on the same day, to fix the missing integration of a dll.
