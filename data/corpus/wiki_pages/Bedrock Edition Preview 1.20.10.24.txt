Beta 1.20.10.24 (Android) or Preview 1.20.10.24 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox) is the fourth beta version, and fourth and final Preview version for Bedrock Edition 1.20.10, released on June 21, 2023, which fixes some bugs.


== Changes ==


=== Items ===
Decorated Pots

Now have information in their description if they are crafted from pottery sherds.


=== Mobs ===
Sniffers

Now can't dig while having the levitation effect.
Now stop digging after their target block is destroyed.


== Fixes ==
5 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.20.10

MCPE-169693 – Sniffers can still dig when using levitation status effect.
MCPE-170031 – Sniffers continue digging after their target block is destroyed.
from v1.20.0

MCPE-169937 – Decorated Pot combined with pottery sherds doesn't have additional information in the description.
MCPE-171429 – Pressure plates have become broken and inconsistent.
MCPE-171798 – Mobs dismount from chest boats.
