13w18b is the fifth snapshot for Java Edition 1.6.1, released on May 2, 2013 to fix some of the bugs and crashes in the 13w18a snapshot.


== Fixes ==
5 bugs fixed

MC-15020  Animations are not working.
MC-15022  FPS drop due to new texture system while looking at horses.
MC-15027  Crash on world load.
MC-15034  Texture pack descriptions do not load.
MC-15036  Riding horses into obstacles crashes the game.


== Trivia ==
As with 13w18a and 13w18c, this snapshot was released on the same day as 1.5.2.
