Shoghi Cervantes is a member of Mojang Studios. Before working on Minecraft, they worked on (and are the founder of) PocketMine, which is a server software for Bedrock Edition.
They currently work for the team of development for Bedrock Edition and formerly worked on Minecraft Dungeons when it was in development.


== Trivia ==
They are red-green colorblind.
A splash in Pocket Edition is titled "Blame shogchips!", referencing a joke site which allowed users to 'blame' Shoghi.
