A pet axolotl is a pet variant of the axolotl introduced in 25w14craftmine.


== Spawning ==
A pet axolotl is spawned next to the player on mine start when the "Pet Axolotl" Player Unlock is active.


== Drops ==


=== On death ===
Pet axolotl have no drops.


== Behavior ==
Pet axolotls have the same AI behavior as axolotl.
