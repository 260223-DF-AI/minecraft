The wooded hills or forest hills was a hills variant of the forest that no longer generates since Caves & Cliffs: Part II, and was removed in Java Edition. 


== Description ==
The wooded hills biome generated as hills inside forests, and sometimes in plains biomes. The biome was similar to forests, with much more hills, cliffs, and floating islands, making it less suitable for survival. Wolves spawned here.
