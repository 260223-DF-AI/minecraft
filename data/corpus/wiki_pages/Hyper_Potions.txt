Hyper Potions is an American EDM band based in California, with Ian Tsuchiura as its only member. The band is known for composing remixes of video game soundtracks, notably composing music for the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise.
In Minecraft, the band had composed a remix of "Steve's Lava Chicken" as Music Disc Lava Chicken. The band has also composed remixes of "Featherfall" and "Precipice" for the Minecraft: Pixel Genesis album, all the remixes in the album Minecraft: Triple Bundle, and remixes of "Broken Clocks" and "Tears" for the Minecraft: Pixel Drift album.
