The windswept forest or extreme hills plus trees is a more humid variant of the windswept hills biome filled with spruce and oak trees.


== Generation ==
Windswept forests generate instead of windswept hills in areas with high humidity. Taigas, snowy taigas, dark forests, windswept hills, old growth pine taigas, old growth spruce taigas, swamps, forests, birch forests and old growth birch forests often border windswept forests, with rarer bordering biomes being jungles, sparse jungles, bamboo jungles and plains. A lush cave may also generate under windswept forests due to its high humidity, but when the biome generates far inland, dripstone caves may generate beneath it instead.


== Description ==
Windswept forests lack a stony terrain surface, but generate hilly and rugged terrain just like regular windswept hills. Tall grass and both spruce and oak trees appear more often, forming a sparse forest, and bushes occasionally spawn in patches around the biome. Dandelions and poppies are the only flowers that grow in windswept forests. This biome is one of the few to contain emerald ore, and coal ore along with iron ore often generates exposed to sunlight. Infested stone with silverfish may also be found here underground. While the increased wood supply can be useful, the risk of fatal fall damage still exists, especially if this variant generates on the edge of a tall hill.  
Llamas inhabit windswept forests, along with the usual farm animals and hostile mobs. Pigs, cows and chickens appear as cold variants here.
With Vibrant Visuals, windswept forests use a coolish humid volumetric fog setting, which slightly fades the distance in an orange tint. Windswept forests have default coolish color grading tints the environment with a colder blue color, and cold sunlight which is slightly brighter at dusk and dawn.
