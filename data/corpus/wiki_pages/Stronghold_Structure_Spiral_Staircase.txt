Stronghold spiral staircases are naturally generated components of strongholds, up to 5 per stronghold can generate.
This room has the entrance at the top and the exit at the bottom; only the entrance to the room generates one of the doors at random, while the exit is always a plain 33 opening. In case of the starting staircase (the one at the beginning of the stronghold), the entrance is also a plain 33 opening.
They are composed of stone bricks and stone slabs.
