Dave Fennoy is an American voice actor, known for his close working relationship with Telltale Games. Among his roles at the company include Lee Everett in The Walking Dead, Finch from Tales from the Borderlands, and Lucius Fox in Batman: The Telltale Series. Outside of video games, he is known for voicing Tetrax in the Ben 10 franchise.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
In 2015, Fennoy was cast as Gabriel the Warrior in Minecraft: Story Mode. A brave warrior known for his tenure in the Order of the Stone, Gabriel's role in the story can drastically change depending on the player's choices.


== Trivia ==
Prior to being cast in Story Mode, one of Fennoy's characters, Lee Everett, received an official skin in Skin Pack 5.
