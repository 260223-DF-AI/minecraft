CU19  is a version of Xbox One Edition released on December 18, 2015.  This version is equivalent to Java Edition 1.8, adding content from the Bountiful Update.
[edit]
This version was referred to in promotional material as the 1.8.8 update.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
Added podzol, granite, polished granite, andesite, polished andesite, diorite, polished diorite, prismarine, dark prismarine, prismarine bricks, red sand, red sandstone, smooth red sandstone, chiseled red sandstone, red sandstone stairs, sea lanterns, wet sponge, slime blocks, coarse dirt, packed ice, acacia leaves, dark oak leaves, acacia saplings, dark oak saplings, and barriers.


=== Items ===
Added armor stand, raw rabbit, cooked rabbit, rabbit stew, raw mutton, cooked mutton, rabbit's foot, rabbit hide, potion of Leaping, potion of Water Breathing, salmon, cooked salmon, clownfish, pufferfish, blue orchid, allium, azure bluet, tulips, oxeye daisy, sunflower, lilac, poppy (replaces rose), rose bush, peony, double tall grass, large ferns, prismarine crystals, and prismarine shard.
Spawn eggs in Creative mode for rabbits, endermites, and guardians.


=== Mobs ===
Endermite, guardian, elder guardian, rabbit, chicken jockey.
Rabbits have new models.[more information needed]


=== World generation ===
Structures

Added ocean monuments
Biomes

Added Mesa, Mega Taiga, Roofed Forest, Birch Forest, Forest, Savanna, Extreme Hills+, Deep Ocean, Snowless Taiga, and 20 new technical biomes.
Terrain generation changes to the Nether.


=== Gameplay ===
Enchantments

Luck of the Sea, Lure, Depth Strider
Creative Inventory

Added silverfish mossy stone brick, silverfish cracked stone brick, and silverfish chiseled stone brick variants of silverfish blocks to the Creative inventory.


=== General ===
Tutorial

Created a new tutorial world.
Terrain generation changes to the Nether.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Activator rails

If powered, they now dismount entities riding minecarts.
All acacia and dark oak related blocks 

Are now obtainable in Survival mode.
Anvil 

Repairing has been rebalanced.
Buttons 

Can now be placed on the top and bottom of blocks.
Cauldrons 

Will put out burning entities if filled with water, taking away one water level each time.
Cracked stone bricks and sponges 

Are now obtainable via smelting.
Dispensers

Can place pumpkins or wither skeleton skulls to spawn iron golems, snow golems, and withers, provided the body is built in a valid configuration.
Furnace

When it runs out of fuel, the smelting progress will rewind at 2 speed.
Empty furnaces will only accept fuel and empty buckets in the fuel slot.
Huge mushrooms

When harvested with Silk Touch enchantment, now drop blocks with corresponding mushroom texture (red or brown) on all sides, rather than the one with spores.
Monster Spawners 

Can now have the mob they spawn change by placing the corresponding spawn egg
Moss stone, mossy stone bricks, and chiseled stone bricks 

Are now obtainable via crafting.
Redstone comparators 

Can now detect the rotation of an item in an item frame and output an equivalent power signal.
Sponges 

Are now obtainable in survival mode and now soak up contiguous water source blocks out to a taxicab distance of 5 when placed, thereupon turning into a new wet sponge.
Wooden fences

No longer connect to Nether brick fences.


=== Items ===
Arrows decelerate much more quickly underwater and extinguish if on fire.
Updated audio for minecarts.
Poppy and dandelion now only yield 1 dye item instead of 2.


=== Mobs ===
Most mobs can now swim.
Babies' growth can be accelerated by feeding them.
Cows

Updated sounds
Skeletons and rabbits 

Now flee from wolves.
Creepers 

Can now be ignited with flint and steel.
Zombies, skeletons, wither skeletons, and creepers drop their head when killed by a charged creeper. Charged creeper explosions do not yield more than one mob head, regardless of how many mobs were killed by it.
Iron golems, snow golems, and withers

Can be spawned by arranging the blocks, in equivalent rotations of the default on the horizontal plane.
This does not affect the orientation of the spawned mob.
Mooshrooms

Updated sounds.
Sheep

Now drop mutton when they die.
Shrubs

Can now drop wheat seeds.
Can no longer be placed in flower pots. Potted shrubs in old worlds are retained.
Snow golems

Can be spawned by arranging the blocks, in equivalent rotations of the default on the horizontal plane.
This does not affect the orientation of the spawned mob.
Spiders

No longer see players through solid blocks.
Tamed ocelots & tamed wolves

Display a death message in chat if they were named with a name tag.
Villagers 

Have additional professions and trades .
Will now harvest crops if they are farmers
Will only breed when willing
Will turn into witches when struck by lightning.
Witches

Can now drink and drop potions of Water Breathing.
Withers

Can be spawned by arranging the blocks, in equivalent rotations of the default on the horizontal plane.
This does not affect the orientation of the spawned mob.
Wither skeletons 

No longer visibly catch fire when going into fire or lava.
Zombies 

Baby zombies now drop experience.
Zombie pigmen 

Adult zombie pigmen will forgive after a short time (and lose the aggression speed boost), but will continue to attack until the player escapes their aggression distance.


=== Non-mob entities ===
 Players 

Now start with an empty map instead of an already drawn map.
 Item frames

Can now rotate in 8 directions.
Are no longer broken with one click when punched if they have an item inside. Now only the item is taken out.


=== World generation ===
Melons can now be found in jungle biomes similar to pumpkins.
Swamp biomes now spawn with blue orchid flowers and the coast is covered in staggered dirt blocks with lily pads between them.
Plains biomes now generate with azure bluets, oxeye daises, tulips, and double tall grass.
Extreme hills biomes now have snow at higher elevations, gravel, flat valleys near sea level, and are slightly higher.
Ocean biomes contains only gravel on the floor instead of sand, dirt, and gravel.
Desert temples now spawn with stained clay instead of dyed wool.
Gravel roads in villages have cobblestone underneath, to prevent them from collapsing into caves.
Chests in Nether fortresses now have the possibility of containing obsidian.


=== Gameplay ===
Fishing

Now has particle effects - bubbles will show up moving towards the bobber just before the player has something on the hook. This has the appearance of a fish swimming towards the hook.
New items can be caught from fishing
Treasure: enchanted fishing rod, enchanted bow, enchanted book, name tag, tripwire hook, lily pad, and saddle.
Junk: damaged fishing rod, water bottle, rotten flesh, string, leather, bowl, stick, bone, tripwire hook, ink sac, and damaged leather boots.
Enchanting

Now consumes lapis lazuli and has been rebalanced.
Enchanted books can receive multiple enchants at once.


=== General ===
Chat messages of the set day and set night host options now say "day" or "night" instead of displaying the time as numbers.
Falling has particle effects.
Particle circumference size depends on distance fallen.
Particle appearance depends on the block landed.
Added end portal frames and all types of logs to the custom superflat block selection menu.
Removed sponges from the custom superflat block selection menu.
Mobs can now spawn above the nether roof.
The End has been downscaled from using overworld size to match its building area of 288x288 blocks.
Nether portals

New Nether portal building rules: any rectangular shape from 45 to 2323.
If the portal in the Nether is big enough, ghasts can travel through.
Will now be activated by any fire block within the frame.
