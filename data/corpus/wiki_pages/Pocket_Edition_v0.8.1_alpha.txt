v0.8.1 alpha is a minor update to Minecraft: Pocket Edition released on December 18, 2013 for Android and on December 19, 2013 for iOS. It fixes some of the bugs and crashes in v0.8.0 alpha. This is the final version released in 2013.


== Changes ==
 Charcoal

Texture has been changed to Java's 1.6 texture.
 Zombie Pigmen

No longer take damage when burning in the daylight.
