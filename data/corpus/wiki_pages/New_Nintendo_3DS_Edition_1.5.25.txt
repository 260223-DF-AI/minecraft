1.5.25 is an update for the New Nintendo 3DS Edition released on June 56, 2018, which added coarse dirt, stained glass, and a store for DLC packs.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Coarse Dirt

Grass cannot grow on it.
Can be crafted from 4 gravel and 4 sand.
 Stained Glass

Comes in 16 different colors.
 Stained Glass Pane

Comes in 16 different colors.


=== General ===
Added the Super Mario mash-up pack.
Cartoon and Fantasy texture packs are available for purchase.
Campfire Tales, City Folk, and Town Folk skin packs are available for purchase.


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Improved stability and performance.


== Fixes ==
Numerous bug fixes from previous builds.
