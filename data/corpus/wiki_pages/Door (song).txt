"Door" is an album-exclusive song composed by C418 that only appears on Minecraft - Volume Alpha.


== Track description ==
"Door" continues off from where "Key" ends, with light piano melodies that segues into a light remix of "Minecraft" played on multiple instruments, including a flute. After the song reaches its peak, a piano plays while a choir plays in the background.
At the end of the song, the piano segues into "Subwoofer Lullaby".
