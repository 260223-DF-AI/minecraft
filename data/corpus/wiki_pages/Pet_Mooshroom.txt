The pet mooshroom is a pet variant of the mooshroom introduced in 25w14craftmine.


== Spawning ==
A pet mooshroom spawns next to the player on mine start when the "Pet Mooshroom" Player Unlock is active.


== Drops ==
Pet mooshroom drops nothing when it's killed.


== Behavior ==
The pet mooshroom is similar to the regular mooshroom, but there are still some differences:

The player's melee attack cannot damage the pet mooshroom.
The player cannot obtain milk bucket by using a bucket on a pet mooshroom.
The player cannot obtain mushroom stew by using a bowl on a pet mooshroom.
The brown variant of pet mooshroom cannot be fed flowers.
