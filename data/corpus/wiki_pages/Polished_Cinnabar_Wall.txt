A polished cinnabar wall is a decorative wall variant of polished cinnabar.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Polished cinnabar walls drop as an item if mined by any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Crafting ===


=== Stonecutting ===


== Usage ==

Unlike most other walls, mobs can be leashed to polished cinnabar walls similar to fences.[Bedrock Edition  only]
