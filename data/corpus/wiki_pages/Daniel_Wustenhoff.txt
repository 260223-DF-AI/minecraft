Daniel "darngeek" Wustenhoff is a former member of Mojang Studios. He was a developer for Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Dungeons.


== Biography ==
Daniel started programming at the age of 13. Having finished playing his video games, he decided to make his own.
Before joining Mojang, Daniel had previously worked on Rayman Legends. In 2014, he graduated from the NHTV IGAD programme.
Daniel quit Mojang in October of 2021 to focus on his mental health.
