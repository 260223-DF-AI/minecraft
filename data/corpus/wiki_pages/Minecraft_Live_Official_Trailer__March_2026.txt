Minecraft LIVE Official Trailer  March 2026 is the official trailer for Minecraft LIVE  March 2026, highlighting several features from Tiny Takeover.


== Summary ==
As the sun rises across several biomes, many mobs travel in a massive herd. A wandering trader climbs up onto a grass hill, mumbling something, before getting headbutted by a baby goat, and then an adult goat, before noticing a brood of chicks passing under their feet. On a sheep, two baby sheep bounce, while in the outskirts of a village several baby villagers play with an iron golem, knocking it down on its chest, as the chicks continue on their trek. At night they pass by a wolf, who howls as they leap off a cliff.
In a grove, a screaming baby goat rams into a grown goat with two chicks on it, followed by many other kids. In an ocean, several glow squids swim to the surface, followed by three gurgles and zombie nautilus jockey. The tiny chicks proceed with their takeover of a village, running through the streets and on roofs, as well as seemingly multiple towns. Even more appear to chase after a farmer, along with a random baby pig. The chicks then somehow manage to appear in the Nether, climbing up a strider past a warped forest.
Back in the Overworld, several dolphins, axolotls, and turtles ridden by chicks continue the voyage through what appears to be a river. On land, many mobs have joined each other, the chicks rushing past a camel, onto grass blocks, and finally onto a llama, as the camera pans to reveal the scale of the exodus. Several parrots fly by, as the Minecraft Live logo appears. As the screen fades to black, the farmer appears on top of the black, still fleeing the chicks and random baby pig.
