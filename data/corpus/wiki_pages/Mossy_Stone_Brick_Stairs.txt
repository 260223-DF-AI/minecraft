Mossy stone brick stairs are a stairs variant of mossy stone bricks that generates in ruined portals and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Mossy stone brick stairs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Mossy stone brick stairs generate as part of ruined portals in the Overworld.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Mossy stone brick stairs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
