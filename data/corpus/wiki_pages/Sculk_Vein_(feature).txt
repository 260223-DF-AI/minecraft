A sculk vein is a feature that consist of several sculk vein blocks. It generates exclusively in the deep dark biome.


== Generation ==
The sculk vein feature generates on the four sides and lower surface of stone-like blocks including stone, andesite, diorite, granite, dripstone blocks, calcite, tuff, and deepslate. It generates only in air and water.
