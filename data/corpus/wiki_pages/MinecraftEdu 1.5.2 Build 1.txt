1.5.2 Build 1 is the first and only build for MinecraftEdu 1.5.2, released on September 13, 2013. It is based on Java Edition 1.5.2.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Bats are affected by Animals & Villagers setting.
Slimes, magma cubes, and ghasts are affected by monsters setting.
Flint & steel doesn't work if Fire & TNT is disabled.


== Fixes ==
3 bugs fixed

Edu keybindings / commands not working.
Servertool not installing in some cases (1.5.1 build 16)
Disabled WorldEdit selection rendering due to instability issues.
