Default Skin Pack (called Standard Skin Pack in New Nintendo 3DS Edition) is a skin pack consisting of 2 skins, included by default in  Minecraft Education and New Nintendo 3DS Edition. Unless the player manually selects a skin from another skin pack, a skin from this pack is automatically applied to them.
Before being replaced by the Character Creator, the skin pack was also present in Bedrock Edition and included the option to select an image file as a custom skin.
