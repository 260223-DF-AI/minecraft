v0.10.2 alpha is a minor update to Minecraft: Pocket Edition released on November 20, 2014 for Android only, to fix some bugs in v0.10.1.


== Fixes ==
Fixed some bugs and crashes.
Fixed a crash when a player's touch input/crosshair intersects a space containing a block with an invalid ID.
Fixed a crash when an arrow, dropped item, falling block, painting, primed TNT, thrown egg, or thrown snowball is in the same space as a block with an invalid ID.
