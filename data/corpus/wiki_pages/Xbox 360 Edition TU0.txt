TU0 is a version of Xbox 360 Edition released on May 9, 2012. This version is based on Java Edition Beta 1.6.6. The internal build date of this version is March 30, 2012, over a month before its release. This was the first version of the Xbox 360 Edition; TU1 was the day-one patch for consoles.


== Changes ==
Menu scroll sound was changed to a higher pitch.
Text is much blurrier.
Added textures from Java Edition 1.2.1 in res/1_2_2, although they remain unused in this version. The textures are used in later versions, first in TU3 and last in TU12.
Minor tooltip changes.
Pause menu is now lower.
