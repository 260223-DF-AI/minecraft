A version of Minecraft Indev was released on February 19, 2010.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Furnace and lit furnace

Used to smelt blocks and items.
Replaces the old smelting system.
Stick, planks and coal can be used as fuel.
Smelted 0.5 items per stick.
Smelted 1.5 items per planks.
Smelted 8 items per coal.
Lit furnaces drop themselves instead of regular furnaces.
Crafted with cobblestone.


=== Items ===
 Flint

Used to craft flint and steel.
Has 10% chance of dropping from gravel.
 Cooked porkchop

New food item.
Restores 8HP health.
 Raw porkchop

New food item.
Can be cooked into cooked porkchops.
Restores 3HP health.

== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Gravel

Now has a 10% chance of dropping flint.
  Lava 

Texture changed from .
Now no longer smelt items.
Log

No longer breaks faster than stone.
Stone type blocks

Now require a pickaxe to drop an item.


=== Items ===
General

Ore can no longer be smelt with fire.
All items now get destroyed by fire and lava.
 Coal

Texture changed from .
 Flint and steel 

Texture changed from .
Is no longer dropped by mobs.
Can now be crafted from one iron ingot and one flint.


=== Mobs ===
Creepers

Now drop 02 sulphur upon death.
Pigs

Now drop 02 raw porkchops upon death.
Skeletons

Now drop 02 arrows upon death.
Spiders

Now drop 02 strings upon death.
Zombies

Now drop 02 feathers upon death.


=== Gameplay ===
Smelting

All smelting recipes produce 1 output item.
Removed coal ore to coal smelting recipe.
Removed gravel to glass smelting recipe.
