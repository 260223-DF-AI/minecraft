13w06a is the ninth snapshot for Java Edition 1.5, released on February 7, 2013 which adds the minecart with spawner.
13w06a was reuploaded later on February 7, 2013, to fix a crash on the loading screen and remove a debugging feature that replaced other minecarts with minecarts with spawners.


== Additions ==


=== Non-mob entities ===
Minecart with spawner

NBT structure is now the same as with normal mob spawners.
Not an obtainable item, can only be added as an entity with external editors.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Fences

The hitboxes of fences with inner corners have changed  they now much more closely match the shape of the fence itself, rather than being its convex hull (a solid cuboid).
Pistons

Now extend instantly if they're not pushing a block.


=== General ===
Lighting

Some new lighting optimizations.


== Removals ==


=== General ===
Distance effects

The hitboxes of fences no longer lose precision at extreme coordinates.
