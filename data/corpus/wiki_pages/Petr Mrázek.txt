Petr 'peterix' Mrázek was a member of the Minecraft: Java Edition development team at Mojang Studios, since June 2018. He was announced on May 14, 2018. He was hired to work on the Minecraft Launcher, especially on Linux. He is known as the main developer of launcher MultiMC, a custom Java Edition launcher originally created by Forkk which he continues to develop after joining Mojang.
