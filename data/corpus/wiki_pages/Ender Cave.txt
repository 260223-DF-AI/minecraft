The Ender Cave is a cave in Scatter Island inhabited by endermen and the main setting of the episode of the same name in Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Description ==
The Ender Cave is a small cave on a stony hill in the taiga part of Scatter Island. The entrance is guarded by two iron golems. Inside, the walls are made of stone and gravel. Three pillars rise out of the ground to serve as stands for the three teams, and are lowered when the challenge begins. At the end of the cave are three hoppers, each dedicated for a team to fill them with Ender pearls. At one side of the cave, a large, collapsible wall hides The Enderall's lair.


== Points of interest ==


=== The Enderall's lair ===
This lair houses The Enderall, a dangerously ravenous, enderman-like being. It was hidden by a wall of stone until CommuniKate pulled the lever and the stone blocks fell over, revealing the lair.


== History ==
The first challenge on Scatter Island took place in the Ender Cave, where the three teams were tasked of gathering the most ender pearls in their team's hopper. The contestants were first annoyed by the endermen's constant teleporting while trying to kill them, but later managed to defeat them, even using ender pearls for their advantage. Because of the tie between the three teams, CommuniKate brings in The Enderall, a dangerous variant of enderman that can swallow anything with its gaping mouth. After a long fight with it, Quinn manages to find its weak spot and kill it, earning many ender pearls and helping her team win the challenge. Later, the participants left the cave and traveled to the mountains.
