Bedrock Editor v0.4.3 is a minor alpha release for the Bedrock Editor released on August 9, 2023, which fixes a bug.


== Fixes ==
Fixed infinite gizmo drag (issue #24) and bugs around gizmo drag size, setting it as a limit to 100 units. How this works: Total volume of 100100100. Ability to create multiple selections within the bounds of the 100100100 total volumes.
