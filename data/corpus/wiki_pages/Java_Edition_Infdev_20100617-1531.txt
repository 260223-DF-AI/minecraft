A version of Minecraft Infdev was released on June 17, 2010, at 15:31 UTC.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Getting stuck inside blocks causes suffocation.
When inside a block the player will be shown a grass block texture, and will be unable to xray through walls anymore.
The players hand now displays again.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Cobblestone

Flowing water that touches flowing lava creates cobblestone, which makes it renewable.
Ladders

Are no longer destroyed by water.
 Lava

Texture changed from .
Lava will destroy any plant (rose, dandelion, sapling, red mushroom, brown mushroom, or wheat) it flows into.
Lava springs don't spread, making infinite lava no longer possible.
Lava no longer generates at the bedrock level.
Obsidian

Flowing water that touches standing lava turns the lava into obsidian.
 Water

Changed texture from .
Water will destroy any plant (rose, dandelion, sapling, red mushroom, brown mushroom, or wheat) it flows into.
Water flows nicer.


=== World generation ===
General

Made massive tweaks to the pending tile tick logic (used for liquids and fire), quite possibly fixing a crash bug.
Removed the infinite lava sea from under the world (removed when updating, replaces layer 0 with the void).
Surface springs.
Caves

Caves are even more regional, with lava areas being possible but rare.
Springs, both water and lava, are more common.
Increased variety in tunnel thickness, increasing the odds of branches.
Added natural gravel and dirt underground for more varied caves.
Ores

Prior to this version, all ores were generated with a random amount of veins regardless to the type of ore.


=== General ===
Layer 0 can now have blocks placed in it.


== Fixes ==
3 bugs fixed

Fixed a few bugs in liquid spreading code.[more information needed]
Fixed a bug where logs would not generate inside of the tops of trees.
Fixed invisible waterfalls.
