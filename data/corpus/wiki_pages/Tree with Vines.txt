Trees with vines (commonly referred to as dying trees) are features that resemble a tree being overtaken by vines.


== Generation ==
Trees with vines appear almost identical to regular trees, but all exposed logs on the main trunk are covered with vines. 
Only oak, spruce, jungle, and dark oak trees can generate as trees with vines. Jungle trees with vines are technically the same feature as jungle trees, which have a ⅓ chance to generate like trees with vines with all logs covered in vines.
Trees with vines can be found in all biomes where their regular variants grow, except groves.
Trees with vines also have a chance to generate from their respective sapling when it grows.
