Potato stairs are a potato variant of the wooden stairs, crafted from the respective potato planks, added in 24w14potato.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Potato stairs can be broken using anything, but axes are the fastest.


=== Natural generation ===
Potato stairs generate naturally as part of potato villages.
