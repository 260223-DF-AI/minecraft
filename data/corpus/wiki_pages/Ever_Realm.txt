The Ever Realm is a fictional Bedrock Edition server appearing in the Minecraft graphic novel series.


== History ==

At an unknown time, a group of kids began invested in the server, creating their own base and going on many adventures. Once their leader, Tyler, moved away from town, they were left with Minecraft as their primary method of hanging out. The group chose to surprise him when he got situated, and promised to complete one of their personal goals: constructing and defeating a wither. However, the wither had other plans, forcing Evan to build it. Although Tyler was able to chime in and defeat it, this incident made him think they bailed on their plans, and grew distrustful of his own pals.


== Properties ==
Although depicted as a server within a video game, the Ever Realm is shown to behave more akin to reality, with players being depicted as lifelike and rarely wearing in-game items. Although areas are made out of identifiable blocks and inhabited by mobs, it does not appear to follow any sort of grid, with objects being of varying degrees of cubic. As well, players on the server are shown to possess far greater power than a real world player, being able to kill most mobs in a single hit, place down objects, permanently break constructs, and even be held captive.
