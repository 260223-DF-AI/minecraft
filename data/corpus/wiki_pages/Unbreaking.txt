Unbreaking is an enchantment that gives a chance for an item to avoid durability reduction when it is used, effectively increasing the item's durability.


== Obtaining ==
Unbreaking can be obtained from enchanting tables, drops from mobs that have spawned in a raid,[Bedrock Edition  only] books obtained by fishing, monster room chests, librarian villagers, and non-village structure loot.


== Usage ==
For tools and weapons, there is a 100%(level+1) chance that using the item reduces durability, which means that the item has a 50%/66.66......%/75% chance of not using any durability. On average, tools last 2/3/4 times as long.

For armors, there is a 60%+40%(level+1) chance a use reduces durability, meaning each durability hit against Unbreaking armor has a 20%/26.66......%/30% chance of being ignored. On average, armor lasts 1.25/1.3636....../1.428571428571...... times as long. Technically, only level below 0 of Unbreaking can lower the reduce-durability chance less than 60%.
When durability is reduced by multiple points, for example,  when hooking a mob with fishing rods or getting blown up with TNT, Unbreaking is applied for each point of decision of actual reduction.
