A cobbled deepslate slab is a decorative slab variant of cobbled deepslate that generates in ancient cities and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Cobbled deepslate slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Cobbled deepslate slabs generate as part of ancient cities.
