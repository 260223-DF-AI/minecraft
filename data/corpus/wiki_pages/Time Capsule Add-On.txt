Time Capsule Add-On is an add-on made to promote a time capsule made as part of the 15 Days of Minecraft event.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
File:Time Capsule Handbook.gif Time Capsule Handbook
A readable menu split between a tutorial, other maps by Oreville Studios, and support.
Recommends the Oreville projects Actions & Stuff, Realistic Biomes, World Builder, and Health Bars.

File:Time Capsule Spawner.png Time Capsule Spawner
Places the entity, triggering an animation of two random characters emerging from the ground, building the time capsule, and then leaving.
Characters include Ari, Alex, Efe, Makena, Noor, Steve, Sunny, and Zuri.[needs testing]


=== Blocks ===
File:Cherry Trapdoor Time Capsule Add-On.png Cherry Trapdoor
Nonfunctional, full block version of a cherry trapdoor.
Used as part of the construction of the time capsule.
File:Hopper Time Capsule Add-On.png Hopper
Nonfunctional, full block version of a hopper.
Used as part of the construction of the time capsule.
File:Ladder Time Capsule Add-On.png Ladder
Nonfunctional, full block version of a ladder.
Used as part of the construction of the time capsule, allowing for the illusion of the two heroes emerging from the ground; it's merely a single block that looks like a dirt chute.


=== Entities ===
File:Time Capsule render.png Time Capsule
Used to contain various items for certain amounts of time. This can be determined by the second, minute, hour, day, month, or year, between both real time and Minecraft Time.
The capsule's coordinates can be hidden, whispered, and announced.​[more information needed]
Consists of grass blocks, pink petals, stone brick stairs, a cherry tree, cherry trapdoor, hopper trapped chest, and ladder. Attempting to break any piece of this will trigger a pop-up asking if the player wishes to remove the structure, however these can still be picking block in Creative.
Upon the time limit being met, an animation of fireworks going off will play, with the item being given lore that displays the time spent in the capsule.​[more information needed]
