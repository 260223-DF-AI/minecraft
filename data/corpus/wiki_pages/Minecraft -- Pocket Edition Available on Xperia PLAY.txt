Minecraft -- Pocket Edition Available on Xperia PLAY is a trailer made to promote the initial release of Pocket Edition, originally exclusive to the Xperia Play.


== Summary ==
The video consists of two builders slowly constructing a replica of a Xperia Play, made up of various blocks that existed at the time, such as glass, black wool, and obsidian. They start at the bottom of the device, and work their way up layer by layer, slowly manifesting identifiable details such as circuits, button holes, and then the screen. Seconds before the construction would have completed, an image of the real device fades in, with the game on the screen and a caption telling people to play it.

== Trivia ==

As opposed to simply two players or animated models of Steve, the builders featured in the video were an actual mob programmed into a version of Java Edition. This feature was never supposed to be released to the public, but would eventually leak in a build with an incomplete version of the Xperia Play build.
