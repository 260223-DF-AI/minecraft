Poisonous potato fries are a joke food item, added in 24w14potato, obtained by smelting poisonous potato sticks.


== Obtaining ==


== Usage ==
To eat poisonous potato fries, press and hold use while it is selected in the hotbar. Eating one restores 10 () hunger and 12 saturation. Unlike unprocessed poisonous potato, fries do not cause Poison.
