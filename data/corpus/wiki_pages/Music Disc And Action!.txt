"And Action!" refers to both a song composed by Tone Deaf Rebellion, and a music disc that plays the song when inserted in a jukebox. The music disc was included in 25w14craftmine on Java Edition.


== Obtaining ==
Two And Action! discs can be obtained from the structure placed in Mines with the "Warden Boss Fight" mine ingredient or the Warden special event.
It can additionally be obtained from a barrel in the "Relax Room" of the Hub.


== Track description ==

"And Action!" is a retro video game style song, using the Microsoft GS Wavetable Synth.


== Usage ==
Like all music discs, using the "And Action!" music disc on a jukebox causes the jukebox to play the song.
If the player places a redstone comparator besides a jukebox playing "And Action!", it will emit a redstone signal of 15.
