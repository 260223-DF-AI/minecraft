2.1.5379 (Windows), 2.1.5380 (macOS), or 2.1.5381 (Linux) is a launcher beta version released on June 25, 2019, which fixed bugs.


== Changes ==
Updated translations.
Changed networking settings to try to alleviate MCL-11112  Launcher cannot establish connection to the internet.
New drop-down component to fix accessibility and selection problems.


== Fixes ==
MCL-11002  Version list not in alphabetical order on play screen.
