Glowing obsidian is an unobtainable decorative block that emits light. It emits obsidian_glow_dust_particle particles at the world origin (0, 0, 0) if placed. 


== Obtaining ==
Glowing obsidian drops itself only when mined with a diamond pickaxe or netherite pickaxe; otherwise, it drops nothing.


== Usage ==
Glowing obsidian can be used for decoration.


=== Light ===
Glowing obsidian emits a light level of 12.

== Trivia ==
Glowing obsidian makes an appearance in Minecraft: Story Mode - Season Two, spawning around the observer when Jesse places the structure block, on the bottom of the obsidian cages Vos was in, and in the Netflix's Minecraft Story Mode Tutorial shortly before Episode 1 starts.
Glowing obsidian is 2 levels brighter than crying obsidian. However, at the time when glowing obsidian was available, crying obsidian was not.
