Legendary Entertainment, LLC, also known as Legendary Pictures or simply Legendary, is an American mass media and film production company based in Burbank, founded by Thomas Tull. The company has often collaborated with major film studios, including Warner Bros. Pictures, Universal Pictures, Sony Pictures and Paramount Pictures, as well as streaming services such as Netflix and Hulu.
Legendary Entertainment served as one of the production companies for A Minecraft Movie, which was released on April 4, 2025.
