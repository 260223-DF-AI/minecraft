Evan, also known by his username xskullxEVANxskullx, is one of the main characters of the Minecraft graphic novel series.


== Appearance ==

At school, Evan has blond hair, and he wears a red shirt, a blue jacket, dark blue pants, and brown shoes.


== Personality ==

Evan is somewhat introverted, being easily subjugated by a bully at one point (though he later develops past this). At one point, Evan claims that he will join a tabletop RPG group over the summer.

== Relationships ==


=== Tyler ===

Evan is consistently shown to be very close with Tyler, arguably closer than any of his other friends.


=== Bully ===
Throughout the second volume, Evan is repeatedly shown being picked on and tormented by a school bully. The reasons given for this animosity are never given, but it is implied to have been ongoing for an extended time before the story. Whenever this bully is around, Evan will often lose his temper or be manipulated into whatever the bully desires at that moment. Over the course of the comic, he develops the courage to stand up to this oppression, him and his friends successfully riding him in the moment.
