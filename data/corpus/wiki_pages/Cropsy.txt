Cropsy is a character waiting for someone or something, first appearing in Campfire Tales Skin Pack.


== Appearance ==
Cropsy resembles a scarecrow, with hay for arms and a melon as a head. Cropsy wears a brown robe with purple sleeves and an apron, possessing a melon-shaped head with a smile and a pair of eyes carved into it. Cropsy also wears a large purple hat with an orange stripe on top.


== Power and Abilities ==
As shown in the series, Cropsy only comes to life at night, and switches off at dawn. Cropsy can hit with great force (for example, when Cropsy hit Quinn, she flew several meters and destroyed a wooden block with her body), and it is also unknown whether Cropsy can be defeated.


== Biography ==
In Episode 3, teams Mushroom, Mesa, Stone Beach, and Tundra arrive at the haunted farm, where they settle in until sunset. After sunset, Cropsy comes to life and hits Quinn with great force, then the heroes hide in the basement of the house until dawn, and Cropsy returns to the place and turns off.
