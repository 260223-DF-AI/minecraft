Add-Ons Studio for Minecraft is an app for iOS developed by 57Digital which was initially released on December 19, 2016. It allows the creation of add-ons for Minecraft: Bedrock Edition.


== Purchase and availability ==
Minecraft: Skin Studio was available for purchase on the App Store for US$3.99.

The app supports the following languages:

English


== Functional ==
The app provides tools for users to create add-ons in Minecraft: Bedrock Edition. In the app, users can create custom mobs and create packs by choosing its name, icon and description and then port the add-on directly to Minecraft or to other apps as a file.
