21w41a is the fifth snapshot for Java Edition 1.18, released on October 13, 2021, that introduces changes to some textures, world generation, and fixes bugs.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===

=== Items ===

Changed the item textures of all doors except crimson and warped from        to       .
Item frames and glow item frames

Changed the item textures from   to  .
Signs

Changed the item textures from         to        .


=== Mobs ===
Wither

Tweaked the wither skull's texture to use the new wither face from the Texture Update.
Zombie villager

Tweaked the armorer zombie villager's and weaponsmith zombie villager's textures to remove stray villager pixels.


=== World generation ===
Amethyst geodes

Now only generate up to Y=30.
Rarity has been increased to match 1.17 levels.
Badlands

Red sandstone now generates naturally once again.
Mineshafts

Now generate higher up in badlands.
Mountains

The snowy slopes and jagged peaks surfaces have been fixed to work like they did in the experimental snapshots, where steep sides of the peaks that facing the east or north are exposed stone.
General

New random number generator is used for Overworld generation.


=== General ===
Chunk render pipeline

Changed the prioritization order.
Title screen

Main menu background is changed to reflect Caves & Cliffs: Part II.
Server

Server classes are now signed.
Sprinting

Slightly increased the angle in which sprinting against a block would not stop the player from doing so.
