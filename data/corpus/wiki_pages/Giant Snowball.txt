Giant snowballs (巨大雪玉, Kyodai Yukidama) are massive balls of snow created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
As the name implies, giant snowballs are giant balls of snow, multiple times taller and wider than a human.


== Functionality ==
Giant snowballs can be used as a weapon to crush others, by rolling them down a slope. However, due to their nature as snow, they are not particularly harmful, and serve primarily as a delaying tactic.


=== Crafting recipe ===


== History ==
While being chased by the Blizzard Stray, Nico White crafted a giant snowball in an attempt to stop it. He rolled the snowball down a hill and into the Stray, though it caused little damage.
