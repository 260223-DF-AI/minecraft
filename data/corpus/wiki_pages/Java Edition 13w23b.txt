13w23b is the twelfth snapshot for Java Edition 1.6.1, released on June 7, 2013 to fix some of the bugs and crashes in the 13w23a snapshot.
13w23b was reuploaded half an hour after release.​[more information needed]


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
Golden apples

Now give the new Health Boost effect.


=== Gameplay ===
Status effects

Added Health Boost effect.
Also, both the attribute and the potion effect now show the hearts.


== Changes ==


=== Items ===
Golden apples

Normal golden apples now give Regeneration II for 10 seconds.


=== Mobs ===
Zombies

New mechanics and AI.
Able to lock onto the player from much further away.
Mobbing zombies can now move when further than 32 blocks from the player.
Spawn additional zombies when not being looked at.


== Fixes ==
4 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.6.1

MC-13177 – New Client and Server Outputs Increase Loading Time.
From the 1.6.1 development versions

MC-16418 – Extra Hearts Not Displayed.
MC-17222 – ALL spawners with ActiveEffects crash the game, breaking hundreds of custom maps and servers.
From the previous development version

MC-17403 – Golden apples only heal one heart compared to two in version 1.5.2.


== Trivia ==
This is the last version in which texture packs existed.
