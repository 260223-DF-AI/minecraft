The Chinese prediction poll was a popularity poll held in China, teasing the announcement at MINECON Earth 2018 of a Chinese "national treasure" coming to Minecraft.
This poll did not affect the development of Minecraft, as it took place too close to the MINECON event; it was purely a prediction of which mob would be added.


== Development ==
The available options were the giant panda, Chinese alligator, golden monkey, white-lipped deer, and river dolphin.
When the poll concluded, the panda won 1st place. Then it was announced a few days later during the livestream, and was added to the game in the Village & Pillage update.
