The 2022 Boss Vote Event was an event server where players could vote for either the Ender Dragon or the Wither.


== Main Event ==


=== Boss Voting ===
Players can vote for a boss by going towards the voting area then pressing the lever.


=== Endercoaster ===
The endercoaster is a roller coaster that tours players around the entire map.


== Mini Games ==


=== Battle Arena ===
Fight through multiple waves of enemies until the timer runs out. The player is given 2 minutes to beat the waves of enemies. Players outside of the arena can give players inside of the arena cookies and enchanted golden apple.


==== Item Shop ====
The Item Shop is a location that is located in the center of the arena. Players can obtain points from defeating hostile mobs, which can then be used to buy items from the Item Shop. The items that are obtainable include the following:

Iron Sword for 10 Points
Diamond Sword for 150 Points
Netherite Sword for 400 Points
Iron Armor for 10 Points
Diamond Armor for 150 Points
Netherite Armor for 400 Points
Enchanted Golden Apple for 150 Points
Potion of Fire Resistance for 50 Points


=== Parkour ===
The parkour built around the battle between the ender dragon and wither.


=== Portal Fall Dropper ===
The dropper is located in the center of the mob voting area. It has multiple obstacles to try and delay the player from dropping quickly such as blocks of diamonds and glass. The dropper can be completed by landing in the pool of water located in the bottom of the dropper.


== Cut Content ==
There is an unused cave which is located under the map.
