Halloween Cosplay, also known as Minecraft x Disguise Halloween Cosplay Skin Pack, is a skin pack that includes 10 Halloween costume-themed skins promoting the collaboration between Minecraft and costume brand Disguise. It is available through a promo code obtained by purchasing a product from the "Minecraft Halloween" collection at Walmart before November 1, 2024. The promo codes were redeemable until April 30, 2025.
