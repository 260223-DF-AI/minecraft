The Festive Skin Pack is a skin pack consisting of 24 Christmas-themed skins. All skins included in this pack were added to the Holiday Skin Pack 2015 for Bedrock Edition, which owners of the Festive Skin Pack on Xbox One,  Nintendo Switch, and PlayStation 4 editions received for free during the migration to Bedrock Edition.
