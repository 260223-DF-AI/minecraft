Alpha v1.0.9 is a version of Java Edition Alpha released on July 21, 2010, that made changes to animations and a sound, and fixed a bug.


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
General

The animation of the player and mobs has been changed so that their arms no longer flail around.
Cows

Two sounds have been removed.


=== General ===

The background of the pause menu has now been darkened.
