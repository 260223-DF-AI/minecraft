LEGO Minecraft Dragon Slayer is a skin pack that consists of the "Dragon Slayer" skin from the LEGO Minecraft set "21151 The End Battle". It is available by redeeming a promo code printed on one of the LEGO bricks included in the LEGO set. The in-game name for this skin pack is "LEGO(R) Minecraft(TM) Dragon Slayer".
