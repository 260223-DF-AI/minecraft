3rd Birthday Skin Pack (released as the 1st Birthday Skin Pack on Wii U and Nintendo Switch editions) is a skin pack that includes 19 skins, 16 of which being skins of Minecraft developers, celebrating the third anniversary of Xbox 360 Edition's release.
It was available on Xbox 360 and Xbox One editions from May 7 to May 17, 2015, May 9 to May 16, 2016, and May 9 to May 21, 2017.
From December 21, 2016 and from December 19, 2017 on, the skin pack was available on PlayStation 3,  PlayStation 4, and PlayStation Vita editions for a limited time. Starting from December 22, 2016, this skin pack was available on Wii U Edition for a limited time. Beginning December 19, 2017, the skin pack was released again on Wii U and Nintendo Switch editions for a limited time.
Due to the Better Together Update, it was added in Bedrock Edition on September 12, 2017, although it is only available to players who owned it already when they migrated from Legacy Console Edition.
