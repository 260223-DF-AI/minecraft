Minecraft x Higround is a merchandise collaboration between Minecraft and gaming keyboard company Higround. Various keyboards, mousepads, and other merchandise were released as part of the collaboration to celebrate Minecraft's 15th anniversary. The collaboration was announced on May 22, 2024, the eighth day of the 15 Days of Minecraft. The merchandise had a limited release on May 29, 2024, at 19:00 UTC and is currently widely available.


== Keyboards ==

Six keyboards were released as part of the collaboration.


== Mousepads ==

Five mousepads were released as part of the collaboration.


== Miscellaneous ==
Four pieces of miscellaneous merchandise were also released.
