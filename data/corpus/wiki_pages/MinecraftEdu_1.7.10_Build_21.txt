1.7.10 Build 21 is the twenty-first build for MinecraftEdu 1.7.10, released on March 3, 2016. It is based on Java Edition 1.7.10.


== Additions ==
Added Danish, Czech, Italian and French (CAN) translations
"Give Mining Turtle to All" button to CCEdu admin menu.


== Fixes ==
2 bugs fixed

Not being able to have more than 10 assignments.
Hosting Service fixes and optimizations.
