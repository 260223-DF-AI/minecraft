Mossy cobblestone stairs are a decorative stairs variant of mossy cobblestone that generates in pillager outposts and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Mossy cobblestone stairs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Mossy cobblestone stairs generate as part of pillager outposts.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Mossy cobblestone stairs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
