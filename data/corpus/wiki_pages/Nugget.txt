Nuggets are minimum pieces of metal that can be crafted into ingots and used in the crafting of name tags. They can also be crafted into other items, depending on the type of nugget.
All nuggets can be obtained by being crafted from ingots or from smelting their respective tools, swords, spears, and armor.
Since all nuggets can be both crafted from and crafted into ingots, which can be further crafted into blocks of metal, one nugget is equivalent of 19 of the corresponding ingot and 181 of the corresponding block of metal.
Nuggets form the item tag #metal_nuggets.
