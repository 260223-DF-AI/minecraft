The giant tree taiga hills or mega taiga hills was a hills variant of the old growth pine taiga that no longer generates since Caves & Cliffs: Part II, and has been removed in Java Edition.


== Description ==
Like all other hills biomes, giant tree taiga hills featured elevated, hillier terrain compared to the tegular giant tree taiga, making the landscape less suitable for shelter. In any other aspect, the giant tree taiga hills generated the same way as the regular giant tree taiga. Podzol, coarse dirt, and rocks all generated on the hills. Wolves, foxes and rabbits‌[Java Edition  only] spawned here.
With Vibrant Visuals, giant tree taiga hills use a coolish humid volumetric fog setting, which slightly fades the distance in an orange tint. Giant tree taiga hills have coolish color grading tints the environment with a colder blue color, and cold sunlight which appears slightly brighter at dusk and dawn.
