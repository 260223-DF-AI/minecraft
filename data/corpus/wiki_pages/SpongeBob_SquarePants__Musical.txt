SpongeBob SquarePants  Musical is an animated short based off of the SpongeBob SquarePants map.


== Summary ==
Instead of a narrative, the video is a series of animated vignettes of various characters, played and repeated in succession to form a song. The video begins on Patrick, presumably thinking, before banging a jar of mayonnaise on a barrel and the wall of the Krusty Krab. SpongeBob flips a krabby patty while on duty alongside Patrick's chatter, annoying Squidward in the room over. Bubble Bass eats a large pile of Krabby Patties, as the cash register opens. Mr. Krabs pushes a wheelbarrow of money outside the establishment.
Bubble Buddy runs around trying to catch a jellyfish in front of Squidward's house. Gary taps his eyes together, Karen pushes Plankton into the air, and Sandy launches a rocket outside her treedome. Goober drives around on a large rock, and squishes two incidentals, horrifying Mrs. Puff. Mermaid Man and Barnacle Boy watch TV together with Old Man Walker. Pearl fires a cannon with the Flying Dutchman. Painty the Pirate chants "ohhhh" from the show's theme song.

== Trivia ==
Due to reused audio from the SpongeBob SquarePants theme, Tom Kenny is inadvertently the only actor to reprise their regular role, as Painty the Pirate. Everyone else is played by a noticeable soundalike.
Pearl and the Flying Dutchman appearing together is a reference to them being added to the map in the same update.
