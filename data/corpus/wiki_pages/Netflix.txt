Netflix is an American streaming service, enabling immediate access to a wide library of films and TV show under a subscription.


== Releases ==


=== Minecraft: Story Mode ===

Minecraft: Story Mode was an interactive special specifically created for Netflix's streaming service. It was based on the season one of the Telltale version. On December 5, 2022, it was delisted and unviewable.


=== Animated series ===

Announced coming off the trails of the 15 Days of Minecraft event, an animated series based off Minecraft will be animated by WildBrain Studios and released on Netflix at an unspecified time.


== Collaborations ==


=== Stranger Things Skin Pack ===
As a promotion for the show's second season, a skin pack based off the Netflix original series Stranger Things was released on October 27, 2017 for Bedrock Edition and Legacy Console Edition, as well as September 19, 2018 for New Nintendo 3DS Edition.


== Trivia ==
Stranger Things was cited as an inspiration for the novel Minecraft: Mob Squad, alongside similar properties such as The Goonies.
