The 15th Anniversary Cape is a cape given to users to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Minecraft, alongside the Purple Heart cape and the Follower's cape.


== Appearance ==
The cape features a light green checkered pattern with a creeper face.


== Distribution ==
In order to get the cape, users had to go to Minecraft.net and claim it from a section on the 15th anniversary page. After doing so, it would appear in both Java Edition and Bedrock Edition.
