19w44a is the eleventh snapshot for Java Edition 1.15, released on October 30, 2019. It makes honey bottles stackable and adds improvements for iron golems, chunks, and certain particles.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Tags

Added the shulker_boxes block tag.


== Changes ==


=== Items ===
Honey bottles

Combining four glass bottles with one honey block in a crafting table now returns four honey bottles.
Are now stackable (up to 16).


=== Mobs ===
Iron golems

Damage progress is now based on the ratio of current health to max health.
Parrots

No longer imitate polar bears, wolves, and zombie pigmen.

Chunks

Particles
