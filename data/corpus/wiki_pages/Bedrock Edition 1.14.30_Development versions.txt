This is a list of Bedrock Edition 1.14.30 development versions.


== beta 1.14.30.51 ==
Beta 1.14.30.51 is the first and only beta version for Bedrock Edition 1.14.30, released on January 29, 2020, which fixes bugs.


=== Fixes ===
Crashes

Fixed several crashes that could occur during gameplay.
256x resolution packs are now restricted on Xbox One to prevent crashing.
Fixed a crash that could occur in certain worlds. (MCPE-59756)
General

Split screen now displays a message to join when the main controller is disconnected.
Gameplay

Auto-jump no longer triggers unexpectedly when trying to walk through open doors. (MCPE-52213)
Crafting multiple honey blocks now returns the correct amount of empty glass bottles on a 2x2 grid. (MCPE-58579)
