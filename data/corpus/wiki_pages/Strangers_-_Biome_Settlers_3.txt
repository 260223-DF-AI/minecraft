Strangers - Biome Settlers 3 is a skin pack themed around the inhabitants of the Farland, ocean, and End biomes. At some point in 2025, the skin pack was delisted from the Marketplace in Bedrock Edition, though it is still available in Legacy Console Edition on some platforms. The skin pack can still be obtained on Bedrock Edition if purchased on Legacy Console Edition.
