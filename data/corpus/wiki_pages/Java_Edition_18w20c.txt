18w20c is the thirty-seventh snapshot for Java Edition 1.13 released on May 17, 2018.
This snapshot primarily focused on bug fixes and stability improvements ahead of the full 1.13 release, resolving several long-standing technical issues with world generation and entity behavior.

== Trivia ==
This version was released 9 years after Classic 0.0.11a, the first publicly released version of Minecraft.
This was one of the final snapshots released before the Update Aquatic (1.13) officially launched.
