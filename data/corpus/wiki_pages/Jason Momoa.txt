Joseph Jason Namakaeha Momoa (born August 1, 1979) is an American actor best known for his roles as Aquaman in films such as Justice League and Aquaman.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==

Jason Momoa is best known in the Minecraft franchise for his role in A Minecraft Movie as Garrett "The Garbage Man" Garrison. He was revealed to be starring in the film in April 2022, and later appeared in a promotional video for the 15 Days of Minecraft along with Jack Black, teasing the film. Prior to the release of the film, the Minecraft Instagram account released unique artwork of his role as Aquaman, in a show of good faith in the actor.
