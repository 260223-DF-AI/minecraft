Hardcoded may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Items ===
Hardcoded item properties — Item properties that are not entirely controlled by data components or datapacks.


=== Entities ===
Commands/damage § Hardcoded behaviors — Behavior of entities when receiving a damage source that cannot be controlled by /damage.


=== Structure ===
Structure definition § Using structure templates — Structure types use specific structure templates, but they use hardcoded relative positioning between those structure templates instead of using jigsaw blocks.
Structure definition § Hardcoded structures — Structure types use code to place the blocks of the structure directly.


=== Resource packs ===
Model § Objects that cannot be remodelled — Entities, blocks and fluids that can't have their model changed in Java Edition with resource packs alone.
Font § Special characters — Use of characters or font that cannot be modified by resource packs.
