test.mclevel is a Java Edition sample world uploaded by Notch on minecraft.net to demonstrate the Indev level format. It can be loaded in any Indev version starting from Indev 0.31 20100122-2251.


== Summary ==
The world is an ordinary normal-sized, square, island Indev world set in the Normal theme. Upon loading the world, the player is located in a grassy field. Some grass blocks in front of the player's starting location were presumably manually removed before the world was saved, as suggested by some patches of dirt on the ground.
The player's inventory contains:

1 iron shovel
1 iron pickaxe
1 iron axe
1 flint and steel
99 white wool
99 glass
99 torches
99 TNT
99 bookshelves
89 water spawners
94 apples
According to the CreatedOn tag in the level data, this world was created on January 21, 2010 at 20:27:07 (UTC).
