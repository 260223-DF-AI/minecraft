Josh 'rockenroll4life' Letellier is a former member of the Minecraft: Java Edition development team at Mojang Studios from July 16, 2018, to April 10, 2019. Previously, he worked at the Minecraft Redmond office.
He also owns a YouTube account rockenroll4life where he uploads Minecraft Let's Plays.
On April 10, 2019, Letellier announced his intended-to-be-temporary departure from Mojang as his residency/work extension was denied by the Swedish Migration Agency.
