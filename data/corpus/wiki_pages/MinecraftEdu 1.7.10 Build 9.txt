1.7.10 Build 9 is the ninth build for MinecraftEdu 1.7.10, released on October 13, 2014. It is based on Java Edition 1.7.10.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Ability to translate to local languages.
Ability to translate worlds into different languages.


== Fixes ==
2 bugs fixed

Java 1.8 not working at all with MinecraftEdu (this fix is also applied to 1.6.4 build 20 and all newer builds)
Some test translations not working.
