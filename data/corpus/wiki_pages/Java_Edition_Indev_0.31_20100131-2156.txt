A version of Indev 0.31 was released on January 31, 2010, at 21:56 UTC, which added the title screen. It was announced on the Word of Notch at 22:01 UTC.
This version was reuploaded for the first time at 22:29 UTC.
This version was reuploaded for the second time at 22:36 UTC.


== Additions ==


=== Mobs ===
General

Readded support for older mob models.


=== General ===
Title screen

Comprises all of the buttons and text for both the main menu and the pause menu.
 Has a game logo made of cobblestone, on a background of dirt, which scrolls slowly.
Splash

 The yellow text "Pre-beta!" pulsates on the menu screen 120 times per minute.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
General

Changed block shading on the sides of blocks.
Brown mushrooms

Now emit light.
 Workbench

Texture changed from  .
Fire

Now deals damage.
Lava

Now deals damage.
Lava spawner

Now creates lava even if any previous attached lava was removed.
Water 

Texture can now be seen while underwater.
Swimming creates bubble particles.
Water spawner

Now creates water even if any previous attached water was removed.


=== Items ===
General

Higher tier tools mine faster.
Tools now take damage when used.
Diamond: 257 durability
Gold: 33 durability
Iron: 129 durability
Stone: 65 durability
Wood: 33 durability
Flint and steel

Removed from the hotbar.
Now has 65 durability.
Swords

Swords are used to deal more damage than punching.[verify for Java Edition]
Diamond Sword: 16HP  8
Golden Sword: 4HP
Iron Sword: 12HP  6
Stone Sword: 8HP
Wooden Sword: 4HP
Swords take 1 damage when hitting a mob and 2 damage when breaking a block.


=== Mobs ===
 Mob

Changed texture from .
The string, feather, or sulphur drop is now chosen randomly upon death.


=== Non-mob entity ===
Players

Punching mobs deals 1HP damage.


=== World generation ===
Bedrock now appears at the bottom-most 2 layers of the world.
The lava sea only generates whenever caves cut through the top layer of bedrock.
The bottom layer of bedrock can very rarely have gaps.


=== General ===
Fullscreen mode now makes the hotbar, GUI's, and text slightly larger.


== Fixes ==
2 bugs fixed

The number on hotbar items no longer pulses when new items are picked up.
Bows no longer disappear when used without arrows.
Other

Minor bug fixes.[more information needed]
