Levitation is a status effect inflicted by shulker bullets that causes entities to uncontrollably float upward.


== Effect ==
Levitation causes the affected entity to float upward. This does not affect swimming or flying.
An affected entity does not take fall damage until the effect wears off, after which the entity falls and takes damage as usual from falling. 
Levitation has no effect on players riding entities. In Java Edition, it also has no effect on entities that are in water or lava.

== Immune mobs ==
Withers and ender dragons mobs are immune to levitation.
Flying mobs, hovering blazes and shulkers are unaffected by Levitation.


== Notes ==
Mobs tied with balloons[edu & BE  only] exhibit behavior similar to if they had a high level of Levitation. However, this mechanic does not use the Levitation effect.
