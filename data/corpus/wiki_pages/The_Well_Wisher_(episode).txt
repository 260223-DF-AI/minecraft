The Well Wisher is the third episode of Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Summary ==
As all the participants reached Eerie Island near dusk, CommuniKate greets the audience as the participants build their camp in the island's farm. She tells the audience that the farm was inhabited by farmers that mysteriously disappeared, leaving only "useless farm animals". Durango then comes in, telling CommuniKate that "[the animals] aren't useless, they're delicious", startling the cow in between.
As the night grew darker, the participants keep building the camp, and the animals mysteriously vanish. Toby walks next to a scarecrow, Cropsy, that scares him, then gets glowing eyes as it comes to life. On his crafting table, Durango crafts a stone axe and Quinn asks if it's because he broke his golden axe. Durango tells her that he doesn't know his strength, before seeking the cow from earlier to hunt it.
He asks where the other animals went, and Toby answered frightenedly that they went "over there". Intrigued, Durango runs between Sacha and Slugger to find out. Thee three get faced by a "devil cow" appearing before them, before a mummy sheep, a skeleton pig, and a wolfman do the same. The trio run away in fear, and Slugger stumbles upon Mo, dropping her items on impact and telling her that the animals are actually monsters.
Then, all the four teams get surrounded by more farm monsters. On a rooftop, CommuniKate assumes the animals turning evil as a fun twist, and Zed agrees. Back on the ground, Durango runs toward a skeleton pig and a mummy sheep to kill them, but River prevents him from "hurt[ing] them", hoping that the farm monsters have a softer side. The scene cuts to Rudi, who asks whether the participants should give the animals a test, before the scene cuts to Quinn who gets flung by Cropsy into her base and breaking a wooden plank. She notices a trapdoor beneath her, which reveals a basement. She tells Rudi, Mo, and Toby to go along with her into the base, before the rest of the participants fall along.
When day arrived, the farm monsters disappeared, the regular animals reappeared, and Cropsy became inactive. Rudi laments that the monsters destroyed the beds into wool and planks last night. Then, CommuniKate calls all the participants for the second challenge. In the hub, she informs Team Mesa that they performed the best last night. Then, she reveals the second challenge, which takes place inside a well fabled to be haunted by a "vengeful spirit", and tells the participants to make a wish when inside. Before entering, Rudi proposes to wish for more wishes, while Slugger wishes for "something to punch".
Inside the well, CommuniKate reveals the rules of the challenge, which consists of placing four of each item inside a puzzle frame without repeating the same item on a row or column. Toby tells that the game reminds him of Sublocku, while Rudi complains about the tiring game. To cheer him and the other participants up, CommuniKate offers to play music for energy, and tells them that the reward will be a jukebox and music discs. Zed takes out a jukebox and CommuniKate inserts one of the music discs, which reveals to play Toby and River's favorite track. She then warns that the water will rise to endanger the participants, and starts the game.
Team Mooshroom inadventently place two beetroots next to each other, a situation quickly solved by Slugger punching one of them. In Team Mesa's puzzle, Durango eagerly throws every item from the chest to the frames, not knowing that he is not allowed to place more than four types of items until River warned him. Then, Rudi tumbles down into the water where The Well Wisher appears behind him, pulling his leg and dragging him underwater. In an attempt to save him, Sacha takes out a fishing rod to pull him out. However, she pulls so hard that she sends Rudi flying, ending up on Team Mesa's puzzle, scattering all its items, and angering Durango. Meanwhile, CommuniKate notices The Well Wisher coming out of the water, not believing that the legend about her came true.
In Team Mooshroom's puzzle, Slugger realizes she has become a noob and descends to a chest so low it is close to the water, where The Well Wisher tries to get her. Quinn immediately intervenes by throwing random items at her to back her away. Team Stone Beach and Team Mooshroom then agreed to help each other, the former telling the latter that they need two melon slices and an apple to complete the puzzle, which they did. 
Disappointed, CommuniKate admits Team Stone Beach has won the challenge, granting them five points. As she bids goodbye to the audience on her boat across the water, The Well Wisher tries to pull Zed underwater, and CommuniKate replies by hitting her with her microphone.
