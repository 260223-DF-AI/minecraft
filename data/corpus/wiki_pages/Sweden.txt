"Sweden" is a song composed by C418 that plays in the Overworld and when the player is in Creative mode in Java Edition.


== Locations ==


== Track description ==

"Sweden" is a Passacaglia, with a changing melody over a repeated bass line. It is a calm track with soft, somber piano chords that loop that are later joined by strings, which somewhat abruptly stop after a while, leaving the piano alone. After a brief pause, pizzicato strings quietly play the melody before the piano comes back and finishes the song with a glockenspiel and harp accompaniment.

=== Raw music files ===
In Java Edition, the music files can be found in .minecraft/assets/objects.
In Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Education, it can be found in:

Mobile: com.mojang/resource_packs/music/vanilla_music/sounds/music/game/
Windows: %PROGRAMFILES%\WindowsApps\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP_<version>_x64_8wekyb3d8bbwe\data\resource_packs\vanilla_music\sounds\music\game\
All music files are in Ogg Vorbis.

== Trivia ==
A remixed version of "Sweden" by C418 was released as "Beta" for the album 148.
The name "Sweden" was based on the country of the same name, Minecraft's country origin and Notch's birthplace.
