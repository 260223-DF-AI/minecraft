The background is a decorative background of a GUI displayed during dimension teleporting, in some menu screens, and the credit sequence, composed of block textures.


== Backgrounds ==
The background is always shown in menus while the game or panorama is not rendered. Backgrounds in certain menus, such as the Realms loading screen or startup splash screen are simply images part of the UI.


=== Dirt background ===
Dirt backgrounds only appear in the credits, on non-Ore UI connection error screens, and on the loading screen when teleporting to the Overworld using a Nether portal. In Java Edition, when teleporting to the Overworld or custom dimensions, a blurred panorama is displayed in the background instead.
Despite the texture of dirt being changed in Pocket Edition v0.2.0 alpha, the dirt backgrounds still use the old texture, except the dirt background when teleporting to the Overworld.


=== Netherrack background ===
A background with netherrack texture is displayed when teleporting to the Nether.


=== End stone background ===
A background with End stone texture is displayed when teleporting to the End.


=== Nether portal background ===


==== Java Edition ====
An animated Nether portal texture is displayed when changing dimension to or from the Nether in Java Edition. However, the texture is stretched to the size of the window and not a repeated texture like on Bedrock Edition.


==== Bedrock Edition ====
A background with unanimated darkened Nether portal texture is displayed in non-Ore UI errors while joining a Realm in Bedrock Edition.


=== End portal background ===

An animated End portal texture is displayed when changing dimension to or from the End. It is also displayed when taking the End portal back to the Overworld after defeating the ender dragon.
