13w48a is the sixth snapshot for Java Edition 1.7.4, released on November 25, 2013.

MC-31651 – Block break animations no longer display.
MC-36159 – Graphical glitch on beacon effects in inventory screen.
MC-36772 – Enchanted items have big enchant around whole item.
From the 1.7.4 development versions

MC-40580 – Changing a control to "Start Stream" key activates streaming.
MC-40621 – Flowing water surface not visible seen from inside flowing water blocks.

MC-40647 – OpenGL greater than 1.5 but can't stream.
