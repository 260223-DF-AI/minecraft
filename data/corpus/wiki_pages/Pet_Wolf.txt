A pet wolf is a pet variant of the wolf introduced in 25w14craftmine. It can be upgraded to become large in size and carry a netherite sword and wear wolf armor.


== Spawning ==
A pet wolf is spawned next to the player on mine start when the "Pet Wolf" Player Unlock is active.


== Behavior ==
The textures of pet wolves are tinted dark gray once submerged in water.


== Usage ==
Can be upgraded to do the following:

Carry a netherite sword.
Transform into a large dire wolf twice the size of a normal pet wolf.
Wear wolf armor.


== Drops ==
Pet wolves drop no experience when they are killed.
Pet wolves wearing wolf armor drop their wolf armor when they die.
