The Copper Age is a game drop that was released on September 30, 2025 as Java Edition 1.21.9 and Bedrock Edition 1.21.111. It introduces copper armor, tools and weapons, the copper golem and associated copper chest, and various copper decorations, as well as shelves.
During development, the update was referred to as Fall Drop 2025 and Drop 3 2025. The final name of the update was revealed in a Minecraft.net article on August 28, 2025.
Experimental features for the update were released prior to the finalized release as part of Bedrock Edition 1.21.100.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Copper bars

A new type of bars.
Oxidizes over time.
Can be waxed like other copper blocks.
Crafted using 6 copper ingots.
 Copper chain

A new type of chain.
Oxidizes over time.
Can be waxed like other copper blocks.
Crafted using a copper ingot and 2 copper nuggets.
 Copper chest

A new type of chest.
Oxidizes over time.
Can be waxed like other copper blocks.
Crafted using a chest and copper ingots.
Can be spawned by placing a carved pumpkin or jack o'lantern on top of a block of copper and also spawning a copper golem on top.
Can be interacted with by copper golems.
 Placing two will create a large copper chest, like the wooden large chest.
 Copper golem statue

After a copper golem reaches full oxidation, it will become a copper golem statue, with a random pose.
Oxidizes over time.
Can be waxed like other copper blocks.
Pose can be changed by interacting with it.
Every pose has a unique redstone comparator signal.
The poses are Standing (default), Running, Sitting, and Star.
Using an axe on a copper golem statue will remove oxidation and eventually bring it back to life.
 Copper lantern

A new type of lantern.
Oxidizes over time.
Can be waxed like other copper blocks.
Crafted using a copper torch surrounded by 8 copper nuggets.
 Copper torch

A new type of torch.
Crafted using a stick, coal and copper nugget.
 Lightning rod

Added oxidized and waxed variants of lightning rods.
 Shelf

A new storage block.
Crafted using stripped logs.
Can store 3 stacks of items.
 When powered by redstone, its front texture changes.
When powered by redstone, interacting with the block will swap the rightmost items in the hotbar with the contents of the shelf.
Can be connected to other shelves to switch out more than just three hotbar slots.


=== Items ===
 Copper armor

Added copper helmet, copper chestplate, copper leggings and copper boots.
Copper armor has higher armor points than leather armor but less than golden armor. Its durability is higher than golden armor but less than chainmail and iron armor. It has an enchantability of 8, less than iron armor (9) and giving it the lowest enchantability out of any armor type.
Provides a total of 10 () armor points.
 Copper golem spawn egg

Used to spawn a copper golem.
Does not generate a copper chest.
 Copper horse armor

A new type of horse armor.
Provides 4 () armor points.
Generates in the same loot tables with the same chances as iron horse armor.
Cannot be crafted.
 Copper nugget

Copper equipment turns into copper nuggets when smelted.
Nine copper nuggets can be turned into a copper ingot, and vice versa.
     Copper tools and weapons

Added copper axes, copper hoes, copper pickaxes, copper shovels, and copper swords.
Copper tools and weapons are crafted and repaired using copper ingots. They do the same amount of damage as stone but have higher durability. Copper tools have higher mining efficiency than stone tools, but less than iron tools. Copper tools have an enchantability of 13, higher than diamond (10) but less than iron (14).


=== Mobs ===
 Copper golem

A passive mob that can be built with a carved pumpkin or jack o'lantern placed on a block of copper, creating a copper chest along with the golem.
Drops between 1 and 3 copper ingots when killed.
Automatically picks up items from copper chests and sort them into chests and trapped chests.
The golem does this by taking an item out and carrying the item in its hand to a chest.
Will only put the item in a chest that is empty or already has 1 or more of the item.
Can pick up a max of 16 items in a stack at a time.
The holding item can be picked.
Can open non-iron doors.
Oxidizes just like copper blocks do, can be waxed, and loses oxidation when struck by lightning. It does not take damage from lightning.
When it fully oxidizes, it becomes a copper golem statue and freezes in a random pose.
 Can have a poppy placed on its head when interacted by an iron golem in which case said poppy can be sheared.
The poppy pops off when the golem gets fully oxidized.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Copper trapdoor

The crafting recipe now uses 4 copper ingots, instead of 6.
The crafting recipe now produces 1 copper trapdoor instead of 2.

Iron chain

Renamed from "Chain" to "Iron Chain" to distinguish it from the copper chain.
Lightning rod

Can now oxidize and can be waxed like other copper blocks.


=== Items ===
                Dyes

Updated all dye textures.
This was done to enhance visual cohesion across the dye set while maintaining distinct shapes to support color blindness accessibility.
Each dye now has a unique shape, and appears on a brown palette.


=== Mobs ===
Iron golem

Can now interact with the copper golem by planting a poppy on them.

== Promotions ==


=== Copper Collection challenge ===

The Copper Collection challenge, also referred to as the Copper Community Challenge, is a community challenge promoting the release of the game drop. Announced on August 29, 2025, the challenge lasted 4 days, ending on September 2, 2025. In order to complete the challenge, players on Bedrock Edition had to collectively mine 50,000,000 (50 million) copper ore. Upon completing the challenge, a character creator item was given out. The Oxidized Copper Collector item was given out to certain Minecraft creators who help promote the Copper Collection challenge.


=== Reward Drops ===
Three special reward drops were available to promote the release of the game drop. One was available through TikTok, and the other two were available through Twitch.


=== The Copper Cape Quest ===

To promote the release of the drop, a special three-part community challenge was run from September 30-October 20, 2025. Players could complete three challenges. By completing the first two challenges, all Bedrock Edition players earned the two character creator items, and all Java Edition players earned a skin pack. The third challenge required individual players' participation in one of three tasks to earn the Copper Cape.

First Challenge: Players must summon at least 75 million copper golems between September 30–October 6, 2025.
Second Challenge: Players must uses their Copper golems need to organize 500 million items between October 7–13, 2025.
Final Challenge: Players must choose either one of the following tasks in order to obtain the cape.
Pick up a copper golem statue and place it somewhere in Bedrock Edition in any game mode. When done, you get your cape by October 31 in the Dressing Room (Bedrock only).
Complete either one of the WatchTime Quests: Watch 3 minutes of any Minecraft livestream on TikTok, or watch 5 minutes of any Minecraft livestream on Twitch. Once you have your code, redeem it at Minecraft by Nov 19.
Claim your code in-person at The Copper Crown event in San Diego, CA between October 17–19 , 2025, then redeem your code at Minecraft by Nov 19.


=== The Copper Crown ===

As part of the drop's promotion, a special edition of MC Championship was announced, organized in collaboration between Mojang Studios and Twitch. As with prior editions, it features several figures from across the community, including Seapeekay, CaptainSparklez, hannahxxrose, and Grian. It took place on October 19, 2025, 18:00 UTC at the 2025 edition of Twitch Rivals.
