McDonald's skins refers to a group of several skins of both McDonaldland characters and original designs themed after Minecraft that were released as part of the A Minecraft Movie Free Skin Offer. Instead of a singular skin pack with all characters, multiple skin packs with individual skins were released. These skins are obtained by purchasing a Minecraft Movie meal for adults at McDonald's and then redeeming the code printed on the character trading card that comes with the corresponding character toy included in the meal.
