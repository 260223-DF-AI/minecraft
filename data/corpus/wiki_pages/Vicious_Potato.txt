A vicious potato is a joke block added in 24w14potato. It can be found in the Potato dimension.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Vicious potatoes drop when broken with any tool or by hand.


=== Natural generation ===
Vicious potatoes generate rarely in the hash biome, in Potato dimension. They usually generate in clusters of 1-10, with each vicious potato sitting atop a small pillar made of gravtater blocks, known as a "venomous column".


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==
Vicious potatoes shoot projectiles (known as vine_projectile in the code) that damage other entities. They shoot when they receive a random tick or a redstone signal, and there is an entity in a 313131 cuboid centered on the vicious potato block. Projectiles deal 5HP damage and fly in a straight line, unaffected by gravity. Although the projectiles cannot pass through solid blocks, a vicious potato still shoots projectiles regardless of any barriers between it and the targeted entity.
A vicious potato attacks all living entities. Mobs don't try to run away from them, and do not consider the vicious potato as an enemy as it isn't an entity. Even mobs with block-breaking abilities, such as withers, don't try to destroy vicious potatoes that attack them.
