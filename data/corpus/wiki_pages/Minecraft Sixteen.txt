Minecraft Sixteen is a font. It is used on a few Minecraft.net articles and on the footer of the Minecraft Experience: Villager Rescue website.


== Definition ==
MinecraftSixteen.ttf
A TrueType font, named "Minecraft Sixteen". It is a less pixelated version of the Mojangles font.
