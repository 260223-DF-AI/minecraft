1.0.2 (Patch 2) is a version of Nintendo Switch Edition released on May 19, 2017.  
[edit]


== Fixes ==
7 issues fixed
MCCE-4634 – Nintendo Switch will not go into sleep mode after using Pro Controller.
MCCE-4640 – Random screenshot spam occurs since installing and playing Minecraft Nintendo Switch Edition.
MCCE-4641 – Minecraft causes Switch to constantly turn itself on, even after application is closed (related to Joy Cons).
MCCE-4651 – Switching A and B does not work on Pro-Controller.
MCCE-4664 – Cannot use Campfire Tales skin pack.
Fix for false presses of "Home" and "Screenshot" buttons.
Added Missing Campfire Tales Skin Pack.
