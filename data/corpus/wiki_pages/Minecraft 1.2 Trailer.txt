The Minecraft 1.2 Trailer is a trailer for Java Edition 1.2.1.


== Summary ==
The video opens with Alsmiffy waking Trottimus and djh3max out of their beds during the night, bashing on the door. Infuriated, they walk down a flight of stairs, and are told "they're coming," referring to an incoming zombie siege. As several zombies break through the door, they try to escape through an emergency exit, complimenting newly installed lights along the way. However, the exit explodes.
As the three escape out of the building, they quickly arrive at a cliff, as they realize they're on a floating island. As such, the only way out is to jump off. As they fall down, djh3max remarks that they can now build twice as high, before they fall into a jungle. Discovering an ocelot, they decide to chase after it. Eventually, they happen upon a structure, and decide to enter into a dirt tunnel, where they set off tripwires. As zombies continue to chase them, they discover a chamber inside the temple overflowing with vines, accidentally setting off a pressure plate, which burns several leaves, revealing a new path.
At the end of the tunnel is a set of pistons rigged to create an iron golem, labeled "village defense." With a flick of a lever, however, the mechanism breaks, dropping a pumpkin. The zombies still approaching, they figure out how to create the golems, and bring several nearby kits to life. The defenses activated, the zombie problem is swiftly resolved, as the camera zooms out of the temple and onto a block built logo for the update.
