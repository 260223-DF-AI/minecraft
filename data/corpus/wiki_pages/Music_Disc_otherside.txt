"otherside" refers to both a song composed by Lena Raine, and a music disc that plays the song when inserted in a jukebox. The music disc is obtained from various structures, but is most commonly found in ancient cities.

== Track description ==

"otherside" starts off as an uplifting and happy retro-style ostinato in a major key. Upon reaching the second half, the song changes to a minor key and progresses into a darker melody, eventually cutting off abruptly. A clock can be heard ticking quickly at the end. The song audibly samples the starting notes of "Sweden" by C418.


== Usage ==
Like all music discs, using the "otherside" music disc on a jukebox causes the jukebox to play the song.
If the player places a redstone comparator besides a jukebox playing "otherside", it will emit a redstone signal of 14.
