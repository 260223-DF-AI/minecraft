Swift Sneak is an enchantment applied to leggings that lessens the movement speed reduction that occurs when sneaking.


== Obtaining ==


=== Loot chest ===

Swift Sneak can be obtained only from loot chests in ancient cities, making it non-renewable. The chests can contain enchanted books with any level of Swift Sneak.


== Usage ==


=== Speed ===
Swift Sneak increases the player's sneaking speed by 15% per level. At Swift Sneak level 3, the player's crouch speed equals 75% of their normal walking speed. Swift Sneak 4 and 5 can further improve crouching speed, but the crouching speed can never be faster than the player's walking speed. Past Swift Sneak 4, the player can also sprint while they are already sneaking, even without the use of MC-152728.‌[Java Edition  only] The player can sprint while sneaking with Swift Sneak 3 by pressing sprint.
This enchantment also increases the player's movement speed while they are crawling.
