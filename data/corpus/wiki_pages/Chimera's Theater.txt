The Chimera's theater is a large theater run by the Chimera and the setting of Poseidon's fourth challenge in Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Description ==
The Chimera's theater is an open sandstone theater court resembling a small amphitheater, dotted by evenly placed white concrete squares on the court flooring. It is surrounded by a round sandstone and stone staircase for the audience. The scene resembles a Greek temple in appearance, with small staircases leading to it and an enchanted ender eye chest in the middle. A scoreboard transported by the dryads can appear on the scene, split in two representing both sides. Each side has three item frames dedicated to place star points in.


== History ==
After the players managed to collect the third enchanted ender eye from the Harpy's guard, Poseidon teleported them to the Chimera's theater to claim the fourth eye. There, Theseus somehow managed to teleport River and Toby from their sand pillars, giving them items to help them before leaving.
After a while, the Chimera and his servant dryads showed up, requesting the players that they participate in a set of three theater arts challenges: improvisation, mine and movement, and acting, the last one earning three points rather than the usual one. He earned a point for each of the two first challenges, but after he got impressed by CommuniKate's chicken-like acting, he eventually lost, giving the players the enchanted ender eye.
