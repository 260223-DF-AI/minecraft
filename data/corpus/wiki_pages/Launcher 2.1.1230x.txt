2.1.12307 (Windows), 2.1.12308 (Linux), or 2.1.12306 (macOS) is an update to the launcher released on February 24, 2020. This version added a section to sign up for the Minecraft Dungeons beta.


== Additions ==

Section Minecraft Dungeons
Contains a sign-up button and a few videos from Minecraft Dungeons.
