Flow armor trim is a type of smithing template used in smithing tables to add a trim to armor. It is consumed when used, but can be duplicated using an existing template, a breeze rod, and seven diamonds.


== Obtaining ==
Flow armor trims are found in ominous vaults in trial chambers. Once obtained, it can be duplicated using the crafting recipe below.
