The potato font, referred to internally as potatis.ttf, is a font used in the first iteration of 24w14potato as well as in the launcher for a short period.


== Definition ==
potatis.ttf
A TrueType font, named "Potatis". It is used for most common characters, however, there are two characters (U+D83E, U+DD54) that are mapped to a graphic of a poisonous potato.


== Supported characters ==

ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ1234567890-=`\|/?[]{}!$%^&*()#~:;<>,.
