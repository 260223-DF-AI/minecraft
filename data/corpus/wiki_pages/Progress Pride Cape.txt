The Progress Pride Cape is a cape that was given out to players who redeemed it for free while it was available.


== Appearance ==
The cape is a pixelated adaptation of Daniel Quasar's Progress Pride Flag design. It features 6 vertical stripes, colored red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple from left to right, resembling a rainbow pattern from the traditional rainbow flag. Hovering above are a series of downward-pointed chevrons, colored white, pink, light blue, brown, and black from top to bottom. This pattern is repeated with the elytra, instead being mirrored on both wings.


== Distribution ==
The cape was obtainable for free through the Marketplace during the Pride 2023 Sale event as part of a collection of Character Creator pieces. Players were not given the cape automatically.
The cape is only available in certain regions of the world.


=== Availability ===

The cape is region-locked, meaning it could not be claimed in certain countries. The exact list of restricted countries is unknown due to not being public.
