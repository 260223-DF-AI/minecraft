Copper nuggets are minimum pieces of copper that can be obtained by smelting copper tools, weapons, or armor or by crafting it with a copper ingot. One copper nugget is effectively worth one-ninth of a copper ingot.

=== Smelting ===

Durability and enchantments do not affect the ability to smelt copper tools, weapons, or armor into copper nuggets.
