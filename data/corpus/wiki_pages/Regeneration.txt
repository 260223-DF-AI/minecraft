== Immune mobs ==
Ender dragons are immune to Regeneration.
Mobs and players that have full health are unaffected by regeneration.
Mobs in the ignores_poison_and_regen entity tag[Java Edition  only] or the undead family[Bedrock Edition  only] are immune to Regeneration.
