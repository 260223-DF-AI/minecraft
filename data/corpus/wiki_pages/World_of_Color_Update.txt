The World of Color Update is the name for Java Edition 1.12, a major update released on June 7, 2017.
The official name for the update, the World of Color Update, was revealed on March 13, 2017, in a Reddit post by Owen Jones.
The first pre-release, 1.12-pre1, was released on May 10, 2017. This was followed by the second pre-release on May 11, the third on May 17, the fourth on May 18, the fifth on May 19, the sixth on May 29, and the seventh on May 31.


== Additions ==

New blocks
 Bed in various different colors
 Concrete
Obtained by having concrete powder come in contact with water.
 Concrete Powder
Crafted using four sand, four gravel, and the corresponding dye color.
Is affected by gravity like sand and gravel.
Upon contact with water, it converts over to concrete.
 Glazed Terracotta
Obtained by smelting the corresponding stained terracotta color.
Can be oriented in four different directions.
Will not stick to slime blocks if placed on the side, top, or bottom.
New items
 Knowledge Book
Only available through commands.
Used to unlock recipes to players who may have limited crafting recipes available.
Spawn egg:
 Parrot Spawn Egg
New mobs
 Illusioner
Only available through commands.
Will attack any players and villagers nearby.
Will summon duplicates of itself and inflict players with Blindness.
 Parrot
Spawns in jungles.
Flies around but sits when tired.
Can be tamed using seeds.
Can imitate sounds of nearby hostile mobs.
Will dance when music is being played in a jukebox.
Comes in five different colors: Red, Blue, Green, Cyan, and Gray.
Drops feathers when killed.
Using a cookie on the parrot will instantly kill them.
New gameplay
Advancement
Replaces the previous achievement system added since Java Edition Beta.
Is a data-based system that can be customized via JSON files.
Has five tabs with the ability to add more:
Minecraft: The heart and story of the game.
Adventure: Adventure, exploration, and combat.
Nether: Bring summer clothes.
The End: Or the beginning?
Husbandry: The world is full of friends and food.
Advancements are capable of providing players rewards, such as experience points.
A number is shown indicating how much a player has towards completing a specific advancement.
Some advancements are indicated as a goal or a challenge.
Completing a challenge advancement rewards experience and plays a unique jingle.
Recipe book
Shows a list of recipes that can be done through a crafting table.
Can be activated by clicking on the green-colored book.
When a recipe is clicked on, the materials required are placed on the crafting table.
Other additions
Narrator  says what is typed in chat out loud (Ctrl + B to turn on).
4 new commands: /advancement (replacing /achievement), /function, /recipe, /reload
New function system that loads a list of commands upon usage.
The hotbar can now be saved and loaded in Creative mode (C + number to save and X + number to load).
New sounds.


== Notable changes ==
The colors of banners, shulker boxes, and wool now has a new palette.
Stricter NBT syntax in commands.


== Further revisions ==
1.12.1 updated the game credits and fixed a security issue.
1.12.2 changed the name from Minecraft to Minecraft: Java Edition.


== Trailer ==

A trailer documenting some of the World of Color Update's creations was released on Mojang's official YouTube channel on June 7, 2017.
