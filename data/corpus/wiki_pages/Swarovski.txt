Swarovski is a producer of jewelry, watches and crystal decorations that collaborated with Mojang Studios on a Minecraft-themed collection. The collection was revealed in May 2025 at the MCM Comic Con London and released in June 2025.


== Merchandise ==


== Character Creator item ==

Upon the purchase of any of the four figurines, the player receives a Character Creator item.
