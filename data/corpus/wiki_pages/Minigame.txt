Minigame may refer to:

Server § Minigame – a short game contained within Minecraft, having different gameplay elements from the main game itself. They are usually hosted within servers, with popular examples being sky wars, bed wars, spleef, capture the flag, and etc.
Mini games – game modes exclusive to Legacy Console Edition.
