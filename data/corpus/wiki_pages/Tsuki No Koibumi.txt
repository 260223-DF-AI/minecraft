"Tsuki No Koibumi" (月の恋文, lit. "The Moon's Love Letter") is a song by C418 featuring Laura Shigihara. The lyrics are in Japanese and are sung by Laura Shigihara. The song is about reuniting once more. It plays during the end credits of Minecraft: The Story of Mojang. Another version of the song, titled "Tsuki No Koibumi 2", was released in 2015 with a new arrangement by C418, while the original vocals were retained.
