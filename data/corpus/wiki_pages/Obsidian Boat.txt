== Usage ==
Unlike other boats, obsidian boats sink in water instead of floating and are resistant to fire and lava. This makes it somewhat prototypical of Striders.


== Sounds ==
Obsidian boats use the "Friendly Creatures" sound category for entity-dependent sound events, although they do not have any sounds of their own.

== Issues ==
Issues relating to "Obsidian Boat" are not maintained on the bug tracker because it is an April Fools' joke, and is therefore not in the newest stable version or snapshot. Issues reported there are closed as "Invalid".
