A Minecraft Adventure was a 2-part-series by Element Animation for Minecon 2016 in September 30, 2016 and October 7, 2016.


== Summary ==


=== Part 1 ===
Ella and Tim go through a portal to the Overworld and Ella teaches him how to play, despite knowing very little about it.
