13w02b is the fourth snapshot for Java Edition 1.5, released on January 11, 2013 to fix some of the bugs and crashes in the 13w02a snapshot.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Activator rails

Changed crafting recipe.
Now includes two sticks and a redstone torch, as well as the 6 iron ingots.
Dinnerbone stated the previous crafting recipe was not intended.
Chests

Large/double chests can now release a redstone signal when connected to a comparator depending on the number of stacks within.
Before, double chests would only emit a signal depending on how full each individual chest was (a double chest with only the top half full would emit a full signal from the side that the items were in).
Hoppers

Powering the block now also stops it from taking in items (see comparison between 13w02a and 13w02b).
Trapped chests

Can now transmit signal downward through one block. This does not apply horizontally (through walls).
