MINECON Earth 2017 Skin Pack is a celebratory skin pack for MINECON Earth 2017 that includes 8 skins themed around MINECON. In addition to the skin pack's release on the storefronts of Bedrock Edition and Legacy Console Edition, a ZIP archive containing the skins for Java Edition was available on Minecraft.net.
