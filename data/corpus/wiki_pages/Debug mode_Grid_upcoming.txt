This page shows the debug mode layout for development versions 26.2-snapshot-1.
There are 180 lines with up to 181 block states. The last line has 122 block states. Of these 179, those that appear at the end of a line are repeated at the beginning of the next. Therefore, there are 32,521 block states in total, with 32,342 unique block states.
