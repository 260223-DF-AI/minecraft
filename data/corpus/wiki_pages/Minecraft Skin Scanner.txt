Minecraft: Skin Scanner is an app for iOS developed by 57Digital which was initially released on August 21, 2013. It allows the user to turn a colored skin template into a real Minecraft skin.


== Purchase and availability ==
Minecraft: Skin Scanner is available to buy on App Store for US$3.99.
The app is available on the following platforms:


== Functional ==
The app allows the user to turn a drawing into a Minecraft skin. To do this, the user must color the printed skin template, then take a photo of it and the app will turn the drawing into a texture for the skin. This skin can be ported to Minecraft: Bedrock Edition.
