1.99 was an update for PlayStation 4 Edition released on December 10, 2019, alongside Bedrock Edition 1.14.0. It is accessible through the Editions button in Minecraft for PlayStation 4.


== Changes ==
Added "Launch new Minecraft" to the title screen.
Added "What's new Minecraft" to the options screen.
