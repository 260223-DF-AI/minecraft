Minecraft x Dragon Ball Z is a merchandise collaboration between Minecraft and the Dragon Ball Z series, which is part of the Dragon Ball Japanese multimedia franchise.


== Background ==


== Marketplace releases ==


=== Dragon Ball Z ===

Developed by Cyclone, a special collaboration world was released on November 4, 2025, featuring many characters and scenarios from the franchise.


== Cinematics ==


=== Minecraft x Dragon Ball Z Tease ===

During Minecraft LIVE  September 2025, a special trailer was released announcing the collaboration. The video depicts Efe gathering the dragon balls, summoning Shenron to become a Super Saiyan.

== Trivia ==
Many years prior to the collaboration, Ash Davis joked about the dragon balls in a Minecraft.net article centering on a build challenge.
Much of the Dragon Ball mythology, such as Goku and Oolong, were heavily inspired by the classic Chinese novel Journey to the West. Coincidentally, this also received a prior skin pack.
