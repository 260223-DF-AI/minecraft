2.5.9 is an update to the launcher released on June 27, 2023.

Accessibility updates and bug fixes.
Fixes to Launcher open/closed setting control.
Various build distribution updates & fixes.
Linux build bug fixes.

MCL-23411 - Minecraft Error message strings displayed within the Launcher are untranslatable
