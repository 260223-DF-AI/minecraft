The modified jungle or jungle mutated was a rare variant of the jungle that no longer generates since Caves & Cliffs: Part II, and was removed in Java Edition.


== Description ==
The modified jungle featured much more mountainous terrain, being taller and steeper than jungle hills. Overhangs, ravines, floating islands, and large cliffs were very common here. The heights, combined with the thick foliage, rendered the ground below almost entirely out of sight. The folliage was similar to the jungle biome. Ocelots, parrots, and pandas spawned in this biome, but jungle pyramids didn't generate here.
With Vibrant Visuals, modified jungles use a strong humid volumetric fog setting, which fades the distance in an orange tint. Modified jungles use warmish and color grading, which makes the biome look more humid and warm. The night sky is slightly brighter and moonlight colored more vibrant blue.


== Mobs ==
In Java Edition, modified jungles used the same mob spawning chances as jungle hills for hostile and ambient categories, and had different chances for passive categories, while in Bedrock Edition, modified jungles used the same mob spawning chances as jungles.
