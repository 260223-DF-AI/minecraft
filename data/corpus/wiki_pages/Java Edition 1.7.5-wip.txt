1.7.5-wip was a development testing version for Java Edition 1.7.5, shown in a video on the official Minecraft YouTube channel on February 18, 2014.


== Additions ==
Realms

Added a Minigames feature, which can be enabled temporarily by admins. At the moment only 5 minigames are available.
Cake Defense 2, by FVDisco.
Blocks vs. Zombies, by SethBling.
The Walls, by Hypixel.
Autospleef, by SethBling.
Dash of Doom, by Dragnoz.
It's at v1.1, instead of v1.0.
