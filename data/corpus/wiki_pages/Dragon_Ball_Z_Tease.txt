Minecraft x Dragon Ball Z Tease is a special trailer released during Minecraft LIVE  September 2025, announcing the Dragon Ball Z collaboration.


== Summary ==
Climbing up a mountain, Efe discovers six of the seven dragon balls, illuminating a bright light. Placing down the four-star ball, Efe successfully summons Shenron into the Overworld. Efe proceeds to ask an inaudible wish, which Shenron happily grants, first turning Efe into a super saiyan, before being replaced with Goku. After taking a look at himself, several of the other Z Fighters appear.
