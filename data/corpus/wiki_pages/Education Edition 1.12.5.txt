1.12.5, dubbed the Library Update, is a minor update to Education Edition, released on November 12, 2019.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Library

The Library menu has been updated based on user feedback to bring lesson content in-game, providing lesson plans and categories to simplify navigating the library of lessons, worlds, build challenges, and tutorials.
Demo Lesson

Added a demo lesson for users without an Office 365 Education account. Users with an Office 365 Education account can access the world used in the demo lesson from the world library.
