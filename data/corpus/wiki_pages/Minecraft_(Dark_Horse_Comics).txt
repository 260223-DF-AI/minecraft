Minecraft is a comic series based on the video game of the same name, published by Dark Horse Comics in partnership with Mojang Studios.


== Publication history ==
On September 24, 2018, Dark Horse Comics announced a partnership with Mojang Studios and Microsoft to produce a series of Minecraft graphic novels.
