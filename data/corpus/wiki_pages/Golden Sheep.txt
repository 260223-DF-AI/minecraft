A golden sheep, or golden fleece, is a Greek-themed variant of sheep spawned by Poseidon in Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Appearance ==
Golden sheep look like their regular counterpart, but have a yellow wool coat with swirling patterns, as well as a smaller mouth.


== Biography ==
When Poseidon stole the show from CommuniKate during an attempted collaboration by the latter, he repeatedly bragged about his divine status and "unlimited powers". To prove it, he used his trident to summon a herd of golden sheep around his new contestants, as well as taking away their inventory. However, the golden sheep posed no threat or even played a role, except that they instantly despawned when Poseidon brought his ocean monument from the seafloor.
