Beta 1.13.0.6 is the fifth beta version for Bedrock Edition 1.13.0, released on August 14–15, 2019, which fixes bugs.


== Fixes ==
Crashes

Fixed several crashes that could occur during gameplay.
Fixed a crash that could sometimes occur when previewing structure blocks with structure voids.
Fixed an issue that was preventing the game from loading in correctly on some Android devices. (MCPE-48313)
General

In-game beta patch notes now show correctly when launching the game after an update.
Setting the y-rotation when teleporting a player now sets it correctly to 0 degrees instead of -180 degrees.
