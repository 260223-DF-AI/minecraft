== v0.9.0 alpha build 2 ==
v0.9.0 alpha build 2 is the second build version released for v0.9.0 alpha.


=== Additions ===
Sounds

Added sounds to villagers, slimes and wolves.


=== Changes ===
Creative inventory

Additions:
Monster Spawner
Villager spawn egg
Endermen

Now teleport out of the way of arrows.
Monster Spawners

Now have breaking particles.
Spawners placed by the player are now empty spawners instead of pig spawners.
Can be configured to spawn the desired mob by using a spawn egg on one.
Render distance

If the player's render distance is set to a value below the minimum available in the options screen, their render distance is automatically set to the minimum available value when the game is opened.
Zombies

Are now neutral towards Creative mode players.
Now they attack the villagers
The AI and mechanics was changed, made much more horde-like.


=== Fixes ===
17 bugs fixed

Worlds no longer get stuck during loading.
Fixed a subtle crash and world corruption.
Fixed a possible crash on quit.
Removed the gray quad on split controls.
Crops, glass panes, and iron bars no longer render their back sides.
Added name to podzol.
Desert villages are now made of sandstone.
Fire dies out faster in the jungle and roofed forests to prevent runaway fires.
Fixed the chunk section at 0,0,0 having a black border around it.
Red sand does not transform anymore into normal sand when falling.
All entities are no longer the same color as the sky when the render distance is under 72 blocks.
Glowstone and snow layers no longer randomly rotate the textures of certain surrounding blocks.
Brown mushrooms do not glow anymore.
Diorite, granite, and andesite now drop the correct resource type.
Dark oak, acacia leaves, and trunks now catch fire.
You can now place torches on mob spawners.
The contents of mob spawners are saved now.
