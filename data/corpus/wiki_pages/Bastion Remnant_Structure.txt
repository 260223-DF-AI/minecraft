A bastion remnant is a structure that generates in all Nether biomes except for the basalt deltas. It has four variants: Bridge bastions, Hoglin stables bastions, Housing units bastions, and Treasure bastions. Its mobs are piglins, piglin brutes, and hoglins. It is the only place to find the snout banner pattern, Pigstep music disk, gilded blackstone, snout armor trim smithing template, the magma cube monster spawner, and the netherite upgrade smithing template.


== Bridge ==
The bridge leads to a building designed to resemble a piglin head, with its mouth serving as the entrance.


== Hoglin stables ==
The hoglin stables consist of a three-part rampart with damaged hoglin stables on either side. Hoglin stables' pieces are contained in the subfolder hoglin_stable. They contain the hoglin stable chests.


== Housing units ==
The housing unit consists of multiple ruined two-part ramparts arranged around a central courtyard with nether wart growing in the middle. Housing unit pieces are contained in the subfolder units.


== Treasure ==
The player can get many great drops from the chests such as netherite scraps, ancient debris, enchanted diamond tools, and enchanted diamond armor.
It is also very likely that you will find in generic chests spectral arrows ‌[Java Edition  only] (11.2%), arrows (54.6%), magma cream (44.1%), iron chains (24.4%), bone block (24.4%), gold ingot (19%), block of gold (19%), and the pigstep music disc (2.27%)
