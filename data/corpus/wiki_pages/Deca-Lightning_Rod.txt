The Deca-Lightning Rod (10, Jubai Hiraishin?) is a massive Lightning Rod, created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Giant Spyglass resembles a regular Lightning Rod, but significantly larger.


== Functionality ==
Like a regular Lightning Rod, the Deca-Lightning Rod can be used to attract and direct lightning. However, due to its large, it amplifies any lightning that hits it, causing the damage of 10 lightning strikes.
Additionally, because of its nail-like shape, it can be used to skewer mobs in place.


=== Crafting recipe ===


== History ==
While accompagnying a goat back to its owner, Nico White's party saw him get struck by lightning, triggered a skeleton trap and spawning skeleton jockeys. They rushed to help him, and Nico crafted the Deca-Lightning Rod, skewering the skeletons with it and causing lightning to strike them, defeating them.
