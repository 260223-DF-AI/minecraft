Elizabeth "Lizzie" Batty, better known by her username LDShadowLady, is an English YouTuber known for her Minecraft playthroughs.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==

In 2016, she performed as a fictionalized version of herself in Minecraft: Story Mode, taking part in a murder mystery storyline alongside several other YouTubers. Several years later, she would participate in the Ender Cup, under the team Aqua Axolotls.
To celebrate Minecraft content reaching one billion views, a skin of her was included in the in the YouTuber Creators Skin Pack. This same design was reused for her depiction in Caves & Cliffs Explorers map, where she appeared as an NPC.
In 2025, she made a cameo appearance in A Minecraft Movie alongside several other creators as Auction Attendees at an auction for Steve's belongings. Prior to being officially cast in the film, she posted about taking a trip to New Zealand.


== Trivia ==
In A Portal to Mystery, she is not credited by her full name but instead as "LDSHADOWLADY as Herself", unlike other cast members.
