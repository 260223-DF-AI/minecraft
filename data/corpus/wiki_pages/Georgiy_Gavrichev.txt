Georgii 'Fry' Gavrichev is a Software Engineer at Mojang Studios who works on Minecraft: Java Edition. He was announced as a new Mojang team member working on Java Edition on October 6, 2017.
He has been known for addressing technical lighting issues, maintaining Data Fixer Upper and working on Blaze 3D.
Before he joined Mojang Studios, he was known for working on a forge API for modded models.
