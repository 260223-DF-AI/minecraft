Amos Roddy is a musician based in Portland, Oregon. He composed the entire soundtrack for Minecraft's Chase the Skies game drop, which contains the music disc Tears.
He is also responsible for composing the soundtrack of other indie games, such as Citizen Sleeper, Citizen Sleeper 2: Starward Vector, In Other Waters, Cloud Gardens, and Kingdom series.
