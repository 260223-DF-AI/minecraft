Leather armor is a type of armor made from leather. It is the weakest armor, right below copper.

=== Mob loot ===
In Normal and Hard difficulties, zombies, husks, zombie villagers, skeletons, strays, and bogged have a chance to spawn with armor, with a 23.60% chance of it being leather armor. If killed by a player, there is an 8.5% chance of dropping the armor. The dropped armor has a random durability and retains any enchantments it has.


=== Trading ===

Leather armor can be bought from leatherworkers.


=== Fishing ===
Leather boots, often badly damaged, can be obtained as a junk item from fishing.


== Usage ==

Wearing leather armor reduces damage taken from most damage types and provides immunity to freezing.


=== Statistics ===
When a full set of leather armor is worn, the set provides 7 () armor points.


=== Damage reduction ===

Wearing armor reduces incoming damage according to two values: armor, and armor toughness. Each piece of armor provides a given armor and armor toughness value, and the values from each armor piece are added together when calculating damage reduction.
The strength of incoming damage also determines the damage reduction of worn armor, as armor does not protect in a linear fashion.


=== Dyeing leather armor ===

In Java Edition, leather armor can be dyed by crafting it together with 1 or more dyes.
In Bedrock Edition, leather armor can be dyed by using it on a cauldron filled with dyed water. This consumes 1 layer of water but does not change the color of the water.
Dyed leather armor also be undyed by using it on a cauldron with undyed water. This changes the water's color to the color of the armor's dye, and changes the leather armor to have no dye color.
Default color hex code for leather armor is  #A06540.


=== Equipping ===
Players can equip leather armor by placing it in their inventory, using the item, or via a dispenser. When equipped, the armor is rendered. Some other humanoids can also render the armor, pick it up, equip it, and be equipped via a dispenser; see the table below.

In Java Edition, with the exception of the allay, dolphin, fox, and panda, all other mobs with the CanPickUpLoot nbt tag true (1b) can equip armor from a dispenser (except happy ghast), and pick up and equip if are without armor or have worse armor (except ender dragon). All living entities can equip armor using commands, like /item or /data.


=== Powder snow ===
Players and mobs do not freeze in powder snow while wearing at least one piece of leather armor. They also do not sink in powder snow while wearing leather boots, and the player can move through the powder snow like scaffolding.
