A version of Indev 0.31 was released on December 31, 2009, at 22:55 UTC. It was announced on The Word of Notch at 22:57 UTC. This is the final version released in 2009.


== Additions ==


=== Non-mob entities ===
Drops

Re-added.


=== General ===
Controls

Items can now be dropped with Q.


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
Mob

Can now take damage.


== Unconfirmed changes ==
The following changes happened in this version at the latest; as the immediately preceding versions have not been archived, there currently exists no way to confirm for certain whether the changes mentioned here were made in one of said versions.


=== User interface ===
The item forms of most blocks are now lit differently from before. The left and right sides of blocks are no longer lit the same as they were previously; the left has been brightened and the right darkened. If a block used a solid white texture, the top face would use the color  #F4F4F4, the left face  #9E9E9E and the right face  #666666.
Saplings, dandelions, roses, red mushrooms and brown mushrooms are unchanged from their previous appearances.
