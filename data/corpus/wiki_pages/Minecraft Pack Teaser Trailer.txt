Minecraft Pack Teaser Trailer is a trailer promoting the release of Minecraft content in Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds. The video serves as a tongue-in-cheek prologue, explaining how the Minecraft representatives made it into the game.


== Summary ==
One day, Steve is riding in a minecart across from a village, but is distraught when they realize it's slower than a pig. The minecart comes to a screeching halt in despair, until Steve has an idea. Steve begins building, using an iron axe and the apparently still alive head of the ender dragon, constructing a scaffolding rollercoaster. At the end is a giant travel ring, constructed out of gold. Alex watches, expressing some ambiguous emotion over this development, as Steve launches off a ramp into another world, in the process destroying the scaffolding.
