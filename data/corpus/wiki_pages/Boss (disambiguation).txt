A boss is a type of mob that is significantly stronger than standard mobs. They can usually be identified by displaying their health as a boss bar when fought, however some games like Minecraft: Story Mode never display boss bars. Bosses typically represent a significant event or the conclusion of a prior challenge or level.
A mini boss, called a powerful mob in Minecraft Dungeons, is a weaker version of a boss that usually does not have a boss bar.
