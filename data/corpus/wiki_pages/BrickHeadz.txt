Lego BrickHeadz is a Lego theme of stylized statues in the image of characters, typically in the form of pop culture characters. Among the licenses featured in the series is Minecraft.


== Set information ==
BrickHeadz are similar to a Funko Pop, being a small collectible with a comically large head. BrickHeadz differ, being made in the LEGO system, with different options for alternate builds and accessories. Characteristic of the line are the proportions; the head and body being roughly the same size and cubic shape, with "cutesy" black dots for eyes. Unlike typical depictions of the characters, the Minecraft figures have round pupils represented by a 1x1 circular tile, much like other BrickHeadz. Only creepers use a square tile for the eyes, fitting the game's art style.

== Trivia ==
The trader llama figure was the 200th produced in the whole line.
Many other intellectual properties featured in the theme have also collaborated with Minecraft, such as Sonic the Hedgehog and Star Wars.
