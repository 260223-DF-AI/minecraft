2.1.12266 (macOS), 2.1.12269 (Windows), or 2.1.12273 (Linux) is an update to the launcher released on February 20, 2020. This beta version added a section to sign up for the Minecraft Dungeons beta.

Section Minecraft Dungeons
Contains a sign-up button and a few videos from Minecraft Dungeons.
