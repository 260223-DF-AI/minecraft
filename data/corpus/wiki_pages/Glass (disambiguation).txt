Hardened Glass, a harder version of glass, exclusive to Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Education.
Hardened Stained Glass, dyed harder version of glass, exclusive to Bedrock Edition and Education Edition.
Hardened Glass Pane, a thinner version of hardened glass, exclusive to Bedrock Edition and Education Edition.
Hardened Stained Glass Pane, dyed thinner version of hardened glass, exclusive to Bedrock Edition and Education Edition.
