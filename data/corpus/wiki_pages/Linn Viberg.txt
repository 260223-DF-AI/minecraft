Linn Viberg is a Creative Writer at Mojang Studios.


== Biography ==

Viberg joined Mojang Studios as a Creative Writer in March 2023, prior to working at Mojang, she worked with London-based entertainment company Secret Cinema. She has worked on game drops including Spring to Life and The Copper Age as well as on the joke update 25w14craftmine and Minecraft Live 2023.
