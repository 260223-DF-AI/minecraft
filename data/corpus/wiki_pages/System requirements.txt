System requirements refers to the hardware and software specifications that a device must meet in order to play a video game.


== Minecraft ==
The system requirements for Minecraft vary between different editions on the game. The requirements for each edition are explained on that edition's page. Here is a list of links to the "System requirements" section of each edition's page:

Bedrock Edition
Simply titled Minecraft. This edition is available on most platforms.
Java Edition
Titled Minecraft: Java Edition, this edition is only available on personal computers.
Minecraft Education
An educational edition of Minecraft designed for classroom use.
China Edition
A release of Minecraft that is only available in mainland China.
Legacy Console Edition
This edition doesn't have any system requirements, because it was released with specific editions designed for specific console systems.
