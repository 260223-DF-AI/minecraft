Superman Skin Pack is a skin pack consisting of 5 skins of characters from the popular Superman franchise. The skin pack's launch coincided with the release of the 2025 Superman movie.

== Trivia ==
In a pop-up prompting the player to get the skin pack, it was teased that there will be a Superman DLC coming in the near future. The Superman map then came to the Marketplace on September 8, 2025.
