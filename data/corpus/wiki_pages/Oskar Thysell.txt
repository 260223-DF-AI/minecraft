Oskar Thysell is a Content Manager, Java Creators Team Lead and Producer at Mojang Studios. He is in charge of the Java Realms Content Creator Program.


== About ==
Oskar manages community-made content that is published on Minecraft Realms for Java Edition. This includes reviewing and publishing maps, writing monthly blog posts about new Realms content on minecraft.net, bridging the communication between mapmakers and technical developers, and developing the program itself.
He joined Mojang in 2018 as a Content Coordinator for Java Realms. In March 2025 he was promoted to Producer "for the Java Creators team".
