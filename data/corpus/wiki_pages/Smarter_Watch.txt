The smarter watch was an offhand item present in the April Fools' snapshot 1.RV-Pre1.


== Obtaining ==


=== Mob loot ===
Zombies, zombie villagers and skeletons could spawn with it in their offhand slot. They had a chance[more information needed] to drop it when killed by the player.


== Usage ==
Smarter watches were "equipped" by holding them in the offhand slot. While equipped, they displayed the current time above the hotbar, in minutes and seconds, with one minute of in-game time being 5/6 of a real-time second, and one in-game hour being 5/6 of a real-time minute or 50 seconds.
The smarter watch also notified the player of statistics milestones and taken damage.
When the player had achieved a statistic, the message said:
Notice! <Statistic> is now <number>!
When the player had taken damage, the message said:
Notice! You recently took <amount of damage taken> half hearts of damage!

== Issues ==
Issues relating to "Smarter Watch" are not maintained on the bug tracker because it is an April Fools' joke, and is therefore not in the newest stable version or snapshot. Issues reported there are closed as "Invalid".


== Trivia ==
The ankle monitor's curfew starts at 18:30 and ends at 05:30.
The player wakes up from a bed at 06:00.
/time set day sets the time to 07:00.
/time set night sets the time to 19:00.
The damage notification shows how guardian laser beams deal 1 point of magic damage and the rest of melee damage by having it as 2 separate notification.
Some statistic notifications do not differentiate between different items.
All stained glass blocks show up as [White Stained Glass].
Wet sponges show up as dry sponges.
Different mobs killed all show up as [stat.entityKill].
[Picked Up] doesn't say the item name.
Some wooden items incorrectly show up as oak or acacia.
All wool colors show up as [Wool].
