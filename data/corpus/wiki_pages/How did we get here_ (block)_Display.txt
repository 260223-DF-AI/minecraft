== Trivia ==
When the item property displays a block, its model appears rotated and cull facing still applies as if it was not rotated.
The only block that is not affected by this is the How did we get here? block, but only when the item property is set to 0.
Tint blocks and items like the grass block and spawn eggs always appear with a white tint when displayed by the block.
Translucent blocks and items are opaque when displayed by the block.
Some block entities don't display by the block, such as chests, beds, heads, etc...
The enchanted glint does not appear on items that have the enchanted glint when displayed by the block.
Because of that, enchanted golden apple when displayed by the block looks like normal golden apple.
