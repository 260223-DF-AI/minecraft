1.00  is a version of PlayStation Vita Edition released on October 14, 2014.  This was the first version of the PlayStation Vita Edition.
[edit]


== Features ==

Based on PlayStation 3 Edition 1.08.[verify]
Worlds saved onto cloud storage from PlayStation 3  can be uploaded to PlayStation 4 or PlayStation Vita editions.

Render distance is 5 chunks as opposed to 10 chunks on PlayStation 3, and world processing distance was reduced.
