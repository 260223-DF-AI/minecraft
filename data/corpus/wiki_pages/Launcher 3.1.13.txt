3.1.13 is an update to the Launcher released on September 17, 2024.


== Changes ==
Minecraft Pixel Remixes chiptune video now plays under the Java and Bedrock art.


== Fixes ==
4 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.21.2

MCL-20282 – Minecraft for Windows's uninstall warning is reused from Dungeons.
MCL-24498 – GiftingButton speech bubble extension detaches from base after switching tabs.
MCL-24661 – Launcher for Windows 10/11 is referred as "Launcher Bedrock Edition".
Other
Various bug fixes and under the hood improvements.
