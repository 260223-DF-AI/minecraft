Leaderboards are a Legacy Console Edition exclusive where one can keep track of their progress with their friends or other players on the same console type as theirs.
There are six types of leaderboards:​[more information needed]

Traveling Leaderboard, which tracks the distance players have traveled, calculated in meters. It also tracks the distance traveled using minecarts and boats, and the highest distance players have fallen.[verify]
Kills Leaderboard, which tracks how many entities players have killed. The entities tracked are mostly monsters, such as zombies, skeletons, spiders, spider jockey, zombie pigmen, and slimes.
Mining Blocks Leaderboard, which tracks how many blocks players have mined. The blocks tracked are dirt, cobblestone, sand, stone, gravel, clay, and obsidian.
Farming Leaderboard, which tracks how many farm items players have collected. The items tracked are eggs, wheat, mushrooms, sugar cane, milk buckets, and pumpkins.
Glide Leaderboard, which can be filtered with score and time attack. ​[more information needed]
Leaderboards' players can be filtered by "Overall" (which show global player leaderboard), "Friends", and "My Score".​[more information needed]
