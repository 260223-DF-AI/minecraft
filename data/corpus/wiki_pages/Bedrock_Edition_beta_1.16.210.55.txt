Beta 1.16.210.55 is the fifth beta version for Bedrock Edition 1.16.210, released on January 13, 2021, which fixes bugs.


== Fixes ==
Stability and Performance 

Fixed a crash that could occur when changing language settings.
General 

Fixed an issue that was causing the Ender Dragon animation to appear jittery (MCPE-105892).
Fixed a bug that prevented Actors from initializing their animation component.
