A sulfur pool is a generated feature found in sulfur caves.


== Description ==
Sulfur pools consist of a body of water surrounded by sulfur blocks and contain potent sulfur at the bottom. Potent sulfur creates bubbles and yellow particles which inflict the Nausea effect for 3 seconds when entering.
