The Cobalt Cape is a cape that was given out to players who won competitions for the game Cobalt, which was developed in collaboration with Mojang.


== Distribution ==
This cape was given to participants/winners of the Cobalt Workshop Challenge level-making competitions and the Cobalt League tournaments in 2016.

== Trivia ==
The design of the cape's elytra texture is based on a wasp enemy from Cobalt.
In the Steam version of Cobalt, Radwasps can no longer spawn in the color featured for the Cobalt Cape's Elytra. It's unclear if this is a bug or intended behavior as neither Oxeye Game Studio or Mojang publicly track issues for Cobalt.
