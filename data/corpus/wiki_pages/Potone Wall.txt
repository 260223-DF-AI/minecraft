A potone wall is a potone variant of a wall, only present in 24w14potato.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
The correct tool for breaking potone walls is the hoe.

Potone wall drops itself when destroyed.


=== Natural generation ===
Potone walls naturally generate in colosseums.
