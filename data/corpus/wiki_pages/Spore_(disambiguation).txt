== Minecraft ==


=== Blocks ===

=== Mobs and entities ===

=== Structures ===
Spore Tower, a tree-like piglin structure whose foliage occasionally slams down to cause knockback and damage.


=== Other ===
Horde of the Spore, one of the three piglin hordes.
