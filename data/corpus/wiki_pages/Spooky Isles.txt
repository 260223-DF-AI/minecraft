The Spooky Isles are an archipelago where CommuniKate's reality show took place throughout the first season of Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Description ==
The Spooky Isles are an archipelago consisting of four major islands: Birch Island, Eerie Island, Scatter Island, and Arena Island.


== Points of interest ==


=== Birch Island ===

The Birch Island is a forested island where the five teams were summoned given the first two challenges. The first involved fighting monsters at night, and the other took place in an abandoned mineshaft.


=== Eerie Island ===

Eerie Island is a dense, haunted jungle and the second destination of the participants. There is a large clearing in the jungle where the farm and the well were built.


=== Scatter Island ===

Scatter Island is an island occupied on the shores by hordes of endermen transporting random blocks from place to place, hence the island's name. The Ender Cave is also a notable location inhabited by endermen, in addition to The Enderall. At higher altitudes are Scatter Island's frozen mountains where the Challenge of Ice and Magma took place.


=== Arena Island ===

Arena Island is a desert island housing its namesake arena, and the final destination of the participants. It houses the Desert Maze where the first of two Arena Island challenges took place. It also houses the obsidian cage where eliminated team members were banished until the remaining participants rescued them illegally.


== History ==

CommuniKate arranged her game show to take place in the Spooky Isles, putting five teams to a series of nine challenges across the archipelago.
