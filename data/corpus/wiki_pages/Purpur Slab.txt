A purpur slab is a decorative slab variant of purpur that generates in End cities and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Purpur slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Purpur slabs generate as part of end cities and end ships.

=== Note blocks ===
Purpur slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
