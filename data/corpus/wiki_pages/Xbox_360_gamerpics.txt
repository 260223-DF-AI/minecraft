Several Minecraft-themed gamerpics (or gamer pictures) were available for purchase on the Xbox 360 Marketplace. They were sold in packs of 5, each costing US$1.00 (originally  80 Microsoft Points). Additionally, a Steve gamerpic and a Creeper gamerpic can be obtained as free awards for completing certain in-game tasks. These two gamerpics were not purchasable on the Marketplace.
