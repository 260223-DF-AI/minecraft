2.13.7 is an update to the launcher released on November 8, 2023.


== Changes ==
Now you can give the gift of Minecraft more easily than before! Gift icons added to each game's page. Just in time for the holidays.
Bug fixes around Bedrock update flow.
Bug fixes around Realms access.
UI enhancements & bug fixes around Launcher.


== Fixes ==
MCL-23837 - Twitter's rebranding to X since July 2023 is not reflected in the launcher.
MCL-23857 - Selected menu highlighting not properly centered.
MCL-22543 - Accessing the Minecraft Live section of the Minecraft Launcher at least one day before voting begins or ends from 12:00 to 13:00 EDT fails to insert a space between the days left count and the minutes left count.
MCL-23825 - Minecraft live vote countdown days,hours,minutes appears in reverse order on hebrew.
MCL-23143 - Broken links under the "Third-party licenses" section of the Minecraft Launcher.
