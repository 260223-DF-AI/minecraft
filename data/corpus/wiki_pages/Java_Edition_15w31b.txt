15w31b is the second snapshot for Java Edition 1.9, released on July 30, 2015 to fix some bugs and crashes from 15w31a, including an important one regarding books and signs.


== Changes ==


=== Items ===
Splash water bottles

Can be used to put out fires, temporarily creating a little water puddle.


=== Command format ===
/clear 

Now clears the offhand slot.


=== Gameplay ===
Status effects

Levitation:
Can no longer induce people from being kicked from servers.
Glowing:
The details on item models are now highlighted completely while held, instead of being just a box.
Worn armor and held items get highlighted with the corresponding team color, instead of overriding them.
