Potato oil is a joke potion that provides It's very slippery when used.


== Obtaining ==
Potato oil can be obtained via commands, and is also available in the Creative inventory.[verify]


=== Brewing ===
While the base potato oil cannot be obtained via brewing,[verify] it can still be brewed into a splash potion or a lingering potion.
