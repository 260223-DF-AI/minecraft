A version of Indev 0.31 was released on February 4, 2010, at 20:27 UTC. It was announced on The Word of Notch at 20:34 UTC.


== Changes ==


=== Items ===
Bows

Are now fully functional.​[more information needed]


=== Mobs ===
Creepers

Are now hostile again.
Only spawn in dark areas.
Now can attack by approaching and exploding near players.
Pathfinding tweaked so that they don't look like they break their necks every time the player moves one block.
Pigs

Now only spawn in bright areas.
Sheep

Now only spawn in bright areas.
Skeletons

Are hostile again.
Only spawn in dark areas.
The purple arrows that skeletons throw have been removed and have been replaced by regular arrows.
Pathfinding tweaked so that they don't look like they break their necks every time the player moves one block.
Spiders

Are now hostile again.
Only spawn in dark areas.
Pathfinding tweaked so that they don't look like they break their necks every time the player moves one block.
Zombies

Are now hostile again.
Only spawn in dark areas.
Pathfinding tweaked so that they don't look like they break their necks every time the player moves one block.
