A Chain Sickle (Japanese: 鎖鎌, Hepburn: Kusarigama?) is a weapon consisting of a sickle on a metal chain. They can be created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
Chain Sickles are composed of a central chain, with a sickle on one end and a heavy weight on the other.


== Functionality ==
Chain Sickles can be used for close- and medium-range combat. As they can be swung to increase momentum and damage, they can be effective against other spinning techniques, like Yamabuki's Kamaitachi.
