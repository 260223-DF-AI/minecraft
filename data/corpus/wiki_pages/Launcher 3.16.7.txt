3.16.7 is an update to the Minecraft Launcher that was released on June 17th, 2025.


== Changes ==
Changed the background of the home button to include a animation of a happy ghast. The background constantly changes time between day and night.
Changed the background of the main play section to include a picture of a happy ghast.
The loading screen of the launcher has now a video of flying happy ghasts.
Slightly adjusted the text font on Java Edition and Bedrock Edition section.
The "Creator Tools" page on the Bedrock Edition section has now been updated.
