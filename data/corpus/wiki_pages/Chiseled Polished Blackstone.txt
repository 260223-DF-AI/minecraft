Chiseled polished blackstone is the chiseled variation of polished blackstone. It has a piglin nose figure.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Chiseled polished blackstone needs to be broken using a pickaxe, otherwise the block drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Chiseled polished blackstone can generate in bastion remnants and nether ruined portals.
