Gareth Coker is a British composer who created the soundtracks for the minigames in Legacy Console Edition and some mash-up packs. Outside of Minecraft, he has created music for Ark: Survival Evolved, Ori and the Will of the Wisps, Halo Infinite and more.


== Original Minecraft soundtracks ==
Coker has created six soundtracks for Minecraft:

Minecraft: Battle & Tumble (Original Soundtrack) (December 21, 2016)
Minecraft: Greek Mythology (Original Soundtrack) (December 21, 2016)
Minecraft: Chinese Mythology (Original Soundtrack) (December 21, 2016)
Minecraft: Norse Mythology (Original Soundtrack) (December 19, 2017)
Minecraft: Egyptian Mythology Soundtrack (May 29, 2018)
Minecraft: Glide Mini Game (Original Soundtrack) (December 22, 2020)
