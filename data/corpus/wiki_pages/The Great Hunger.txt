Mob C, or "The Great Hunger" was a proposed mob featured in the Mob Vote during MINECON Earth 2017, alongside the barnacle, the phantom and the wildfire.
It was a lizard-like creature that sinks into the ground, camouflages itself and consumes mobs and items with its huge mouth. It has a "great appetite for enchanting powers" and Jeb says that viewers should vote for Mob C because players may be able to use the mob's abilities to add or remove enchantments for players' own items. The ability to remove enchantments was later added in the form of the grindstone block.
The great hunger received 26% of the votes in the first round and moved onto the second, receiving 35%	votes, but then lost with 48% of the votes in the final.

== Trivia ==
Upon being asked, Jens Bergensten revealed on Bluesky that The Great Hunger was the only mob in the 2017 Mob Vote that did not have an actual name.
The mob is referenced in Terra Swoop Force, one of the death messages joking about the player voting for it.
