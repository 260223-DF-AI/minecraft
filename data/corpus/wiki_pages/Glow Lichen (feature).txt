A glow lichen is a feature that consist of several (1-3) glow lichen blocks, not diagonally within each feature.


== Generation ==
Glow lichen feature can be found in all the Overworld cave biomes. It generates on the four sides and lower surface of stone-like blocks including stone, andesite, diorite, granite, dripstone block, calcite, tuff, cinnabar, sulfur and deepslate. It generates only in air and water.
