1.6.4 Build 15 is the fifteenth build for MinecraftEdu 1.6.4, released on April 8, 2014. It is based on Java Edition 1.6.4.


== Additions ==
Added alias protection for servers.
Disabled by default.
You can then password protect aliases in the server as you login with your client.
List of protected aliases and their matching encrypted passwords are stored in the servertool/settings/reserved_aliases.ini file.
Passwords are encrypted with MD5.
Idle kick functionality.
Disabled by default. It can be enabled by modifying an existing server's idle-kick parameters in serverwizardsettings.ini
