Minecraft Indev refers to 17 versions of Indev released from February 6 to February 23, 2010. These versions added armor, farming, furnaces, and a day-night cycle.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
Farmland

Can have crops placed on it.
Furnace

Used to smelt items.


=== Items ===
Hoes

Used to till dirt into farmland.
Bread

Can be eaten.
Crafted from wheat.
Seeds

Can be placed on farmland.
Grow into wheat.
Wheat

Can be crafted into bread.
Armor

Can be worn.
Used for protection.


=== Non-mob entities ===
Paintings

Added 19 new paintings by the artist Kristoffer Zetterstrand.


=== World generation ===
Map themes

Paradise
Beaches expanded, flowers are more common, and trees are less common with a bright sky.
Woods
No flowers, trees generate commonly and the sky is dimmer.


=== General ===
Daylight cycle

The sun and moon move across the sky.
Farming

The player can plant seeds which can be grown into wheat and crafted into bread.
Third-person view

Toggled by pressing F5.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Log

Now drops itself instead of planks.
Cobblestone

Smelts into stone when placed in a furnace.


=== Mobs ===
Skeletons

Now burn in sunlight.
Drop arrows.
Zombies

Now burn in sunlight.
Drop feathers.
Pigs

Drop raw porkchops.
Creepers

Drop sulphur.
Spiders

Drop string.


=== General ===
Sounds

Switched the sound system to use OpenAL.
