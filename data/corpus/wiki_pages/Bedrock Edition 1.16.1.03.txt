1.16.1.03 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released only for PlayStation 4 on July 8, 2020, to try to fix an issue where the game would get stuck on the loading screen and potentially crash.


== Fixes ==
General

Implemented a workaround button combination on startup to clear the player's download cache on PS4. (MCPE-84790)
The button combination is  +  +  +  +  + , and must be held until the initial splash screen is removed.
