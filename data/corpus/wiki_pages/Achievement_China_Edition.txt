Achievements are a system that record the activity and play of players. Players earn achievements after meeting certain conditions in the game or in the launcher.


== Mobile edition ==
The pocket edition achievements include five categories, namely: career, game, social, collection, and paradise, a total of 48 achievements. After earning an achievement, the player receives a certain amount of achievement points. After reaching 3000 points, one can receive the "Hero Road" avatar frame reward. Each achievement has up to four quality levels, which are: primary achievement, advanced achievement, excellent achievement, and legendary achievement, with a value of 10, 30, 50, and 100 achievement points respectively. When the player earns an achievement, the game will instantly display a prompt message at the bottom of the screen.
Players can choose to display three achievement icons on the personal homepage interface.
Achievement points are related to the quality of achievement. Achievements of the same type will only calculate the highest quality achievement points, and players can participate in the friend ranking. On the personal homepage - friend ranking - achievement points, one can view the ranking of achievement points between players.


== Desktop edition (needs in-game testing) ==
There are 24 achievements in the desktop edition, of which 20 achievements are divided into multiple levels.
The achievement interface lists all the achievements. The achievement that has reached the maximum level uses a purple background; other achievements use a gray background, and the current level and the progress bar to the next level will be displayed. Clicking on the achievement will display its detailed information, including description, the requirements of the level that has been met, and the requirements of the next level (if any).
The achievement interface of the desktop edition can only be entered through the prompt box when the achievement is obtained.
