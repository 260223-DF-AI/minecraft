1.16.210.05 is a major update to the Bedrock Dedicated Servers, released on March 9, 2021, that updates them for Bedrock Edition 1.16.210 and fixes bugs.


== Changes ==
Bedrock Dedicated Servers
Updated for 1.16.210 clients.


== Fixes ==
When view-distance is <5 clients are unable to connect. (BDS-8855)
Unexpected or invalid characters in server-name breaks the server. (BDS-6825)
Typo in HTML help file. (BDS-2341)
