Shazboots! are a joke item added in 25w14craftmine. They appear identical to chainmail boots when worn, despite having a unique item texture.


== Obtaining ==
Shazboots! are unlocked when the player opens the player unlock tab: Shazboots!, but this only works in the mine worlds.


== Usage ==
Shazboots! can be worn by the player and gives the player the Shazboots! status effect, which will boost the player while holding the space button on the keyboard. Walking on soul sand will prevent the boost from charging up. Fall damage is negated while worn by the player.

== Trivia ==
The name and description are a reference to the 2001 video game Tribes 2.
"Skiing" refers to a game mechanic in Tribes 2 where one can hold the jump key to slide downhill and launch uphill at great speeds
"Shazbot" is a curse word euphemism originally from the show Mork & Mindy, later referenced throughout the Starsiege: Tribes series.
VGS is the series of keys to say "Shazbot!" in chat through the game's voice command menu.


== Issues ==
Issues relating to "Shazboots!" are not maintained on the bug tracker because it is an April Fools' joke, and is therefore not in the newest stable version or snapshot. Issues reported there are closed as "Invalid".
