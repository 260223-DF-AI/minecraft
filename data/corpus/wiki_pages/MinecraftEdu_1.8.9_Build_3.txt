1.8.9 Build 3 is the third and final build for MinecraftEdu 1.8.9, released on April 5, 2016, and the final version of MinecraftEdu before it was replaced with Minecraft Education. It is based on Java Edition 1.8.9.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Updated CCEdu to version 1.79.
