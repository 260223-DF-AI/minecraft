A mason is a villager that trades polished stones, terracotta, clay, glazed terracotta and quartz. Masons wear a black apron and black gloves.


== Spawning ==
An unemployed villager turns into a mason when an unclaimed stonecutter is nearby.


=== Natural generation ===
The workstation of masons, the stonecutter, generates in the mason house in villages.


== Drops ==
In Java Edition, a mason can drop clay by throwing it as a gift towards a nearby player with the Hero of the Village effect.
