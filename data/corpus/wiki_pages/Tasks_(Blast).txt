Tasks are one of the main gameplay features within Minecraft Blast and is placed alongside the levels button with the home screen. Tasks use up energy in order for the player to progress through the story. Tasks like building or decorating have three different options, once the player has decided which one of the three pre-set builds they want, clicking accept makes the companion build the chosen option (you can still change to other options whenever you want), while tasks like breeding, gathering and destroying have no options (building-related tasks can still have no options).
Tasks are grouped into chapters with each chapter coming with its own story and set of tasks. There are currently only three chapters in the game, with the journey screen teasing a fourth.
