Jonathan Minecraft was a character on the official Minecraft YouTube channel. He was a minor character in How We Make Minecraft, gradually having a larger role until he became the main antagonist of Around the Minecraft World in 80 Biomes along with Mobbo. He is also allegedly the full name of Minecraft.


== Appearance ==
Jonathan Minecraft slightly resembles Narrator, who has several items from the Character Creator, including the "Short Slick" and the old "Tired-Brows" however with darker hair. Unlike Narrator, he uses the default player model rather than the slim one. His dark tuxedo has a pink area above his neck.


== Biography ==

Sometime in the past, Jonathan Minecraft allegedly married Narrator and broke up with him. Eventually, he was hired by Mojang Studios, but was briefly fired because his ideas were simply too good. Enraged, he sought his revenge, including breaking into Mobbo and his ex-husband's wedding. At an unknown point, he acquired the Minefinity Gauntlet. He then leaked his plans for Minecraft in an ancient city, which Narrator read but was killed by a warden by the orders of Mobbo. Jonathan then went to the feature addition machine in Mojang's secret headquarters. However, Narrator was revived, where he brought M.A.R.I.L.L.A. and chased Jonathan to the machine. However, he and Mobbo entered Minecraft first. Jonathan had also acquired Mega-Mobbo at some point. At the end, however, Mobbo betrayed Jonathan Minecraft and sacrificed themself to drown him in lava, seemingly killing him and Mobbo forever.


== Personality ==
Jonathan Minecraft's goal is to "replace Minecraft with [his] perfect image." His traits resemble a stereotypical villain, like angrily yelling at a failed plan.
