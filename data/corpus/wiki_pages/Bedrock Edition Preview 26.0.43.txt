Preview 26.0.43 is an unreleased developer build of Bedrock Edition Preview 26.0.23, recorded on December 2, 2025, and shown in part of the "ALL ABOUT THE SPEAR" Dev Diaries episode on the official Minecraft YouTube channel.


== Changes ==
Differences between this version and Bedrock Edition Preview 26.0.23 are unknown.
