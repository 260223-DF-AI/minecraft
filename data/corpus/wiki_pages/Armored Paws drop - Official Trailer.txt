Armored Paws drop - Official Trailer is a trailer for the release of Armored Paws.


== Summary ==
An armadillo is wandering a badland biome, while Noor is on top of a hoodoo scouting the area. Using a spyglass, Noor spies a ruined portal. Rushing out to find it, Noor and a pack of tamed wolves from every biome leap over a lush cave, and then overwhelm the armadillo. As it peaks out, Noor and the wolves are gone. Breathing a sigh of relief, the armadillo walks off, but not before shedding a scute.
