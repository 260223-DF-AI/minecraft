== Issues ==
Issues relating to "Potato Sign" are not maintained on the bug tracker because it is an April Fools' joke, and is therefore not in the newest stable version or snapshot. Issues reported there are closed as "Invalid".


== Trivia ==
Potato signs have a maximum stack size of 23, and are the first item in any version of the game to have this maximum stack size.
