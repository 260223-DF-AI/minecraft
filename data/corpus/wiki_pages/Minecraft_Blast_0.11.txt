0.11 is the first version of Minecraft Blast, released on November 10, 2025.


== Features ==


=== Gameplay ===
 Levels

Levels are the main gameplay features within Minecraft Blast and is placed within the home screen.
 Tasks

Tasks are one of the main gameplay features that the player must do in Minecraft Blast.
 Blocks

Blocks are the main components of each level of Minecraft Blast. They are used to destroy, clear or collect blockers by tapping on matching blocks that are next to the blockers.
