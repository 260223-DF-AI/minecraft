"Ki" is an album-exclusive song composed by C418 that only appears on Minecraft - Volume Beta.


== Track description ==
"Ki" is a reprise (a similar, but slightly different version of a previous track) of "Key" from Minecraft - Volume Alpha, both with the same pronunciation. It opens with an echoey version of "Key", but then adds new layers of electronic ambient music, with a more darker tone.
The bass notes at 0:38 can also be heard in "Concrete Halls".
