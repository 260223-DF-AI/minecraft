The Realms MapMaker Cape is a cape given to certain contributors in the Java Realms Content Creator Program.


== Appearance ==
The design is based on the logo for the Atlas submission portal, the web interface used by invited creators to upload their maps for review.

This cape is given to contributors who meet the criteria listed here.
