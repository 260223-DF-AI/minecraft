Insomnia is a mechanic in Minecraft that allows phantoms to spawn around players that have not entered a bed or died for three in-game days (1 hour or 72000 ticks). In Java Edition, this value is specifically defined by the "Since last rest" statistic and in both editions it can be disabled with the command /gamerule spawn_phantoms false in Java Edition or /gamerule doinsomnia false in Bedrock Edition."
Insomnia may refer to:

Phantoms – phantoms may only spawn when a player has insomnia.
Sleeping – sleeping or entering a bed are methods to reset the insomnia counter.
/gamerule spawn_phantoms‌[Java Edition  only] or /gamerule doinsomnia‌[Bedrock Edition  only] – a game rule that enables or disables insomnia, and thus, phantom spawning.
