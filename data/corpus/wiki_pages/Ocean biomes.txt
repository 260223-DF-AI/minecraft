== Description ==

Oceans are vast expanses of water from sea level to the ocean floor, which is at a depth of roughly Y=45 for normal oceans and Y=30 for deep variants. The seafloor is fairly hilly, featuring multiple peaks and valleys.
All oceans together are the largest biomes by area, covering anywhere around 28–29% of the surface area of the Overworld when factoring in all of its variants. 


=== Common generation ===
All oceans are vast bodies of water that usually take up a lot of space. They all generate with ocean ruins, which are either there warm or cold variant. All oceans have a deep variant except for warm oceans. Ocean Monuments generate in these deep variants.


=== Ocean ===

Oceans generate with seagrass and kelp along the floor, which is made of sand or gravel. The floors of non-deep oceans can generate patches of dirt and clay.


=== Frozen Ocean ===

Frozen oceans do not generate with seagrass or kelp. The surface of frozen oceans are covered with ice, but not deep frozen oceans. Both frozens oceans and deep frozen oceans generate large icebergs.


=== Cold Ocean ===

Cold oceans have less seagrass compared to other ocean biomes, while deep cold oceans have more amounts of large seagrass. Cold ocean ruins generate in these biomes. Cold ocean floors mainly consist of gravel.


=== Lukewarm Ocean ===

Lukewarm oceans have more seagrass compared to other ocean biomes, and deep lukewarm oceans have more amounts of large seagrass. Warm ocean ruins generate in these biomes. Lukewarm ocean floors mainly consist of sand. Pufferfish can spawn in these biomes.


=== Warm Ocean ===
Warm ocean floors mainly consist of sand, similarly to lukewarm oceans. Coral reefs and sea pickles are exclusive to this biome. Zombie nautili that spawn in this biome spawn as coral zombie nautiluses. Warm oceans do not have a deep variant, so ocean monuments can't spawn here.
