The Wild Update is a major update released on June 7, 2022, as Java Edition 1.19 and Bedrock Edition 1.19.0, themed around "scary things" and natural wilderness. It introduces the deep dark and mangrove swamp biomes; ancient cities; mobs such as the allay, the frog, the tadpole, and the warden; as well as new items obtainable only in these new biomes.

On Java Edition, the first publicly-available build containing The Wild Update content is Deep Dark Experimental Snapshot 1, which was released on February 17, 2022. The first pre-release for The Wild Update was released on May 18, 2022 and the first release candidate was released on June 2, 2022.
On Bedrock Edition, experimental content for The Wild Update has been released as part of 1.18.0, 1.18.10, and 1.18.30 prior to the finalized release.

Sculk family of blocks:

Blocks that are found in the deep dark biome.
Can be instant mined using a hoe.
 Sculk
Drops Experience when broke.
 Sculk catalyst
 Mobs killed near a catalyst will cause nearby blocks to either be converted to sculk or have sculk veins grow.
Occasionally spawn a sculk sensor or an inert sculk shrieker.
 Sculk shrieker
Causes a shriek sound and particle effect to be made when activated by the player or nearby sculk sensors.
Has a warning system where the player can only activate the sculk shrieker three times; upon the fourth shriek, a warden will spawn.
 Sculk shriekers placed by the player or generated by sculk catalysts cannot spawn a warden.
 Sculk sensor
Detects any vibrations within a 8-block radius.
Vibration detection can be dampened with wool or carpet.
Emits a redstone signal depending on vibration frequency and close proximity.
 Sculk vein
Up to 6 can be put on the same block space, merging into one block.
Does not drop experience when broken.

=== Enchantments ===
 Swift Sneak

Used on leggings to provide a quicker sneaking speed.


=== Effects ===
 Darkness

Occurs upon activating a sculk shrieker or being in close proximity with a warden.
Similar to blindness, but with a pulsating darkness limiting visibility with 14 blocks.


=== Mobs ===
 Allay

A passive flying mob found in pillager outpost cages or woodland mansion jail cells.
Follows the player around when holding an item.
In that state, the owner is unable to harm the allay unless its related to its surroundings.
Will collect any item close by and drop it to either an activated note block or the player.
Can be duplicated using amethyst shards when playing to a jukebox.
 Frog

A passive semi-aquatic mob that spawns in swamps and mangrove swamps.
Can jump 8 blocks height.
Occasionally prefer to land on lily pads or big dripleaves.
Eats small slimes and magma cubes, the latter produces froglights.
Can be bred using slime balls, which laids frogspawn.
Has three variants:
 Temperate frog: A variant that spawns in temperate biomes.
 Cold frog: A variant that spawns in cold biomes.
 Warm frog: A variant that spawns in warm biomes.
 Tadpole

A baby version of the frog that hatches from frogspawn.
Will mature to a frog; its type depending on the biome they are matured in.
Can be moved with a water bucket.
Like fish mobs, tadpoles suffocate when on land, and being attacked by axolotls.
 Warden

A blind, powerful, yet evadable hostile mob, with the most health out of any mob.
Spawns in from above after enough sculk shriekers have been activated.
Being blind, it can only detect players and mobs using vibration, smell, and touch.
Upon finding a target, the warden roars and pursues the target.
If it cannot reach the target, the warden does a sonic boom attack that bypasses armor, enchantments, shields, and other mob-related armor.
If not aggravated for 60 seconds, the warden will burrow down and despawn.
Drops a sculk catalyst when killed.

=== World generation ===


==== Biomes ====
Deep dark

Found deep underground, commonly under mountainous areas.
Generates with sculk, sculk veins, sculk catalysts, sculk sensors, and sculk shriekers.
Mangrove swamp

Generates with mangrove trees, mud, dead bushes, and lily pads.
Frogs, bees and tropical fishes can be found here.


==== Features ====
 Ancient city

An underground structure found in the deep dark.
Has a higher chance of finding enchanted golden apples than any other structures prior.
Three music discs, disc fragments, echo shards, and an enchanted book with a random level of Swift Sneak can be found here.
 Mangrove tree

A tree that generates on mangrove swamp.

"Aerie", "Firebugs", & "Labyrinthine"

== Trivia ==
While this update introduces mud as a block, mud has existed in the franchise as a fluid in Minecraft Earth. The properties of mud in this update are identical to a block.
Fireflies were originally planned for this update, but were ultimately scrapped as some species of fireflies are poisonous to some species of frogs in real life. This decision received heavy criticism from the community.
They were later added as part of firefly bushes.
To promote The Wild Update, Mojang released a free choose your own adventure game on minecraft.net called Craft Your Own Adventure: Wild Tales.
Kingbdogz stated in a tweet that the souls in the warden's chest is incredibly important to its lore.
The official artwork and trailer for the update is notable for marking the first appearance of Sunny and Noor, before their official introduction into the game.
Similarly, Efe's first appearance was in the What Will You Uncover trailer.
So far, this is the last major update that has the word "Update" in its name. All subsequent major updates and game drops (e.g. Trails & Tales, Armored Paws, Tricky Trials, The Garden Awakens, Chase the Skies, etc.) do not have the word "Update" in their names. The first named major update whose name did not include "Update" was Village & Pillage, released in 2019.
