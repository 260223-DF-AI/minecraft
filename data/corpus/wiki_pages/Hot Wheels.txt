From 2017 to 2022, the toy brand Hot Wheels released a special line of cars based on Minecraft characters as part of their Character Cars series, namely the Creeper, Enderman, Skeleton, Iron Golem, Spider and Zombie. In 2020, a new car was added to the roster featuring the Ocelot, and, in 2021, another new car was released, featuring the Redstone Monstrosity from Minecraft Dungeons.
Between 2016 and 2018 there were also several Minecraft-themed Hot Wheels track sets featuring cars resembling Minecraft minecarts containing mobs and players.
