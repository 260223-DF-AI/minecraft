Armero is a Minecraft mapmaker known for working closely with the Java Realms Content Creator Program, particularly as the program's first and only Deputy Reviewer. He joined the program in June 3, 2024.


== Contribution to the Program ==
Armero joined the program during the map review drought, and participated in the community reviewer program. He became Deputy Reviewer in September 2025, and works with reviewing maps in the program's Reddit queue.
In 2025, Armero pitched the idea of a Realms award ceremony to Nadja, who had also been thinking of similar ideas. He was the main coordinator and organizer throughout the entire project, but his involvement was anonymous until January 2026. He worked closely with Nadja and Oskar, until it was discovered that Nadja's contract (as content moderator) would not be renewed, at which point Oskar took over her role and the project pivoted to a surprise tribute to her. This would go on to become the Realms Mapmaker Awards on February 4th, 2026.
