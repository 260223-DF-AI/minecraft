The Ultra-Long Iron Sword (超ロング鉄の剣, Chō Rongu Tetsu no Ken) is a very long iron sword, created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Ultra-Long Iron Sword resembles a regular iron sword, except with a very long blade.


== Functionality ==
As a sword, the Ultra-Long Iron Sword can be used to slash at enemies; due to its length, it can be used against large swarms of enemies at once with ease.
