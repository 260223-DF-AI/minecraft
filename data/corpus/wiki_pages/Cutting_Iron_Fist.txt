The Cutting Iron Fist (, Yaiba no Tekken) is a giant ball with iron spikes that can be used as a fist weapon. It is created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Cutting Iron Fist is a large ball with numerous iron spikes protruding from it. It has a handle at its base for its wielder to hold.


== Functionality ==
By punching forward while holding the Cutting Iron Fist from its handle, the user can launch it forward, causing it to smash into any enemies they're facing.
