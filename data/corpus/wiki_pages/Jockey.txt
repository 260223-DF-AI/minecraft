A jockey refers to a naturally-spawned mob riding another mob. The mob on the top often controls the mob on the bottom in Java Edition, but sometimes does not control it in Bedrock Edition.
Most jockeys are named based on the mob being ridden. The exceptions to the "jockey" naming scheme are the skeleton horseman and zombie horseman.


== List of jockeys ==
Camel Husk Jockey; a camel husk ridden by a husk wielding a spear and a parched passenger.
Chicken Jockey; a baby zombie, zombie villager, drowned, husk, or zombified piglin riding a chicken or most adult mobs[BE  only]
Hoglin Jockey; a baby hoglin ridden by one to three baby piglins.
Ravager Jockey; a ravager ridden by an illager, most commonly being a pillager.
Skeleton Horseman; a skeleton horse ridden by a skeleton with an enchanted iron helmet.
Spider Jockey; a spider ridden by a skeleton, stray, bogged, parched, or wither skeleton.[BE  only]
Cave Spider Jockey; a cave spider ridden by said skeletons.[BE  only]
Strider Jockey; a strider ridden by a zombified piglin or baby strider.
Zombie Horseman; a zombie horse ridden by a zombie wielding a spear.
Zombie Nautilus Jockey; a zombie nautilus ridden by a drowned wielding a trident.


=== Removed ===
Giant jockey; a chicken ridden by a giant.[JE  only]
Most jockeys spawn in similar conditions to their respective mobs, except for baby zombie (variant) riders in Bedrock Edition, which can mount any mob.


== Mounting ==

In Bedrock Edition, 15% of naturally spawned baby zombies, baby husks, and baby drowned can mount other mobs when spawned. This includes baby zombies summoned by monster spawners and spawn eggs. Such a mob can be summoned with the minecraft:as_baby_jockey spawn event. 
The following mobs can be mounted:

Due to a bug, baby zombified piglins jockeys don't spawn properly.
Due to another bug, baby zombie villagers cannot mount mobs as jockeys.


== Summoning ==

Jockeys can also be manually summoned using commands:
Chicken Jockey
In Java Edition: /summon minecraft:chicken ~ ~ ~ {IsChickenJockey:1,Passengers:[{id:"minecraft:zombie",IsBaby:1}]}
In Bedrock Edition, it requires two commands: 

/summon chicken ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:spawn_adult
/ride @e[type=chicken] summon_rider zombie minecraft:as_baby
Variants of zombies or ridden mobs can be summoned by replacing chicken and zombie respectively.
Camel Husk Jockey

In Java Edition: /summon minecraft:camel_husk ~ ~ ~ {Passengers:[{id:"minecraft:husk",equipment:{mainhand:{id:"minecraft:iron_spear",count:1}}},{id:"minecraft:parched",equipment:{mainhand:{id:"minecraft:bow",count:1}}}]}
In Bedrock Edition: /summon camel_husk ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:spawn_with_rider
Cave Spider Jockey

In Java Edition: /summon minecraft:cave_spider ~ ~ ~ {Passengers:[{id:"minecraft:skeleton",HandItems:[{id:"minecraft:bow",count:1}]}]}
In Bedrock Edition: /summon cave_spider ~ ~ ~ ~  ~ minecraft:entity_spawned_with_default_jockey
Hoglin Jockey

In Java Edition: /summon minecraft:hoglin ~ ~ ~ {Age:-12000,Passengers:[{id:"minecraft:piglin",IsBaby:1}]}
In Bedrock Edition, it requires two commands:
/summon hoglin ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ spawn_baby
/ride @e[family=hoglin_baby] summon_rider piglin spawn_baby
Ravager Jockey
In Java Edition: 
/summon minecraft:ravager ~ ~ ~ {Passengers:[{id:minecraft:pillager,HandItems:[{id:crossbow,Count:1b}]}]}

This command summons a ravager with a pillager rider.
/summon minecraft:ravager ~ ~ ~ {Passengers:[{id:minecraft:vindicator,HandItems:[{id:iron_axe,Count:2b}]}]}

This command summons a ravager with a vindicator rider.
/summon minecraft:ravager ~ ~ ~ {Passengers:[{id:minecraft:evoker}]}

This command summons a ravager with an evoker rider.
In Bedrock Edition:
/summon ravager ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:spawn_with_pillager_rider

This command summons a ravager with a pillager rider.
/summon ravager ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:spawn_with_vindicator_rider

This command summons a ravager with a vindicator rider.
/summon ravager ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:spawn_for_raid_with_evoker_rider

This command summons a ravager with an evoker rider.
/summon ravager ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:spawn_for_raid_with_pillager_rider

This command summons a ravager with a pillager rider, but the pillager drops special loot upon death.
/summon ravager ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:spawn_for_raid_with_vindicator_rider

This command summons a ravager with a vindicator rider, but the vindicator drops special loot upon death.
/summon ravager ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:spawn_with_pillager_captain_rider

This command summons a ravager with a pillager rider, but the pillager is a raid captain.
/summon ravager ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:spawn_with_vindicator_captain_rider

This command summons a ravager with a vindicator rider, but the vindicator is a raid captain.
Skeleton Horseman
Skeleton horsemen can be manually summoned by both the regular method and by spawning a skeleton trap.
A skeleton trap spawns 4 skeleton horsemen.

In Java Edition: /summon minecraft:skeleton_horse ~ ~ ~ {SkeletonTrap:1}
In Bedrock Edition: /summon skeleton_horse ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:set_trap
Spider Jockey 

In Java Edition: /summon minecraft:spider ~ ~ ~ {Passengers:[{id:"minecraft:skeleton",HandItems:[{id:"minecraft:bow",count:1}]}]}
In Bedrock Edition: /summon spider ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:entity_spawned_with_default_jockey. Note: It only works during nighttime or underground.
The following command summons a skeleton variant 80% of the time when in a biome with skeleton variants: /summon spider ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:entity_spawned_with_biome_specific_jockey
Strider Jockey

Zombified piglin riding strider:
In Java Edition: /summon minecraft:strider ~ ~ ~ {Saddle:1,Passengers:[{id:"minecraft:zombified_piglin",HandItems:[{id:"minecraft:warped_fungus_on_a_stick",count:1}]}]}
In Bedrock Edition: /summon strider ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ spawn_adult_piglin_jockey
Baby strider riding adult strider:
In Java Edition: /summon minecraft:strider ~ ~ ~ {Passengers:[{id:"minecraft:strider",Age:-1200}]}
In Bedrock Edition: /summon strider ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ spawn_adult_parent_jockey
Zombie Horseman

In Java Edition: /summon minecraft:zombie_horse ~ ~ ~ {Passengers: [{id:"minecraft:zombie",equipment: {mainhand:{id:"minecraft:iron_spear",count:1}}}]}
In Bedrock Edition: /summon zombie_horse ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:spawn_adult_with_rider
Zombie Nautilus Jockey

In Java Edition: /summon minecraft:zombie_nautilus ~ ~ ~ {Passengers: [{id:"minecraft:drowned",equipment:{mainhand:{id:"minecraft:trident",count:1}}}]}
In Bedrock Edition: /summon drowned ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ minecraft:as_rider
