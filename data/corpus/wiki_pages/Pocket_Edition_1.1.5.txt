1.1.5 was an update for Pocket Edition and its derived editions that added new and updated existing Marketplace content as well as fixing several bugs. This update is the last version for Pocket Edition (except for iOS, its last version would be 1.1.7) and all editions derived from it, which, aside from Apple TV Edition, would all eventually be merged into Bedrock Edition. It made it possible for additional content to be added to the Marketplace without the need for a full update.
This is also the final release of Pocket Edition that can be played on Android devices that do not own the game in the Google Play Store.


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Marketplace

Updates to PureBDCraft.
All Marketplace-related modules were moved into a separate library.
Future additional content to the Marketplace will not require new releases of Pocket Edition.


== Fixes ==
Fixed clock and compass textures becoming corrupted after reconnecting to the world with a resource pack.
Fixed the texture of the tripwire string.
Optimized the skin picker and all skins will now be displayed.


== Trivia ==
Despite being the final update to Apple TV Edition, it remained downloadable for another 413 days, and was finally discontinued on September 24, 2018.
