cheapsh0t's Cape is a personal cape.


== Appearance ==
The cape is almost identical to the Translator Cape, but depicts a Japanese flag on its underside.


== Distribution ==
Unlike the similar-looking Translator and Chinese Translator capes, this cape was given to cheapsh0t for helping the Mojang Studios team when they visited Tokyo, Japan in September 2013.
