The snowy taiga mountains or cold taiga mutated was a rare variant of the snowy taiga that no longer generates since Caves & Cliffs: Part II, and was removed in Java Edition.


== Description ==
The very rare snowy taiga mountains featured much steeper terrain than the snowy taiga hills, but not as mountainous as the windswept hills. Similarly to the taiga mountains, this variant reached high elevations. The steep elevations made this biome difficult for survival. Structures didn't generate here. Although this was a snowy biome, rivers generated inside snowy taiga mountains were not replaced by frozen rivers.
Because snowy taigas were already very rare, this biome variant was the third rarest in the game, behind the modified badlands plateau and the modified jungle edge.
With Vibrant Visuals, snowy taiga mountains use a coolish humid volumetric fog setting, which slightly fades the distance in an orange tint. Snowy taiga mountains have strong cold color grading which tints the environment with an icy blue color, and cold sunlight which appears slightly brighter at dusk and dawn.
