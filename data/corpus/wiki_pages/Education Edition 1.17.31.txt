1.17.31 is a minor update to Education Edition, released for Windows, macOS, and ChromeOS on December 14, 2021, and for iOS and iPadOS on December 21, 2021, which fixes some bugs.


== Fixes ==
Fixed proxy configurations preventing login on Windows.
Fixed client hanging when downloading resources.
Fixed Windows disconnecting multiplayer sessions when app is minimized.
Fixed non-functional arrows being visible while using Code Builder.
Fixed blocks getting stuck in the material reducer UI.
Fixed not being able to close the "There was a problem loading this world" screen.
Fixed old boards not being editable and old NPCs not having correct skins.
Additional crash fixes.
