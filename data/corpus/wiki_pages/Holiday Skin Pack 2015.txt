Holiday Skin Pack 2015 is a holiday-themed skin pack in celebration of Christmas in 2015, and has 33 skins to choose from. It was the first Bedrock Edition skin pack to include extra geometry on the skin model and includes all skins that were previously part of the Festive Skin Pack for Legacy Console Editions.
