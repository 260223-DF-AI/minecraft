Chase the Skies with Vibrant Visuals (stylized in all caps) is an official trailer for Chase the Skies, including Vibrant Visuals.


== Summary ==
Steve leads two horses and a ghastling over a cliff, only to discover Noor and Kai riding a happy ghast. The ghast flies high up into the clouds, as Makena and Ari watch from their own ghast, above a build of the name Minecraft. The duo head down, gracing an ocean Alex and Sunny are camping at. Makena and Ari dock as Sunny rehydrates a dried ghast, while Kai and Noor arrive at a village being raided. The conquest is momentarily stopped as the illagers become perplexed by the appearance of a ghast.
Later, Sunny, Alex, Makena, and Ari share a ghast, as they check in on Zuri and Efe at a swamp. They also have a happy ghast, but are prioritizing slime blocks. In a bamboo jungle, pandas are playing around and become memorized by the ghast, Kai holding up a cake. To Kai's right is Steve, fending off a random goat, who launches Steve off a cliff and onto a happy ghast with Zuri and Efe.
