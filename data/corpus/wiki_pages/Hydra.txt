The Hydra is a three-headed lizard-like mythological creature that appeared in Minecraft Mini-Series as minor antagonist in The Twist of the Plot.


== Appearance ==
The Hydra looks like a lizard, but unlike lizards it has two legs and three heads located on long necks. Its belly, teeth and parts of its neck are colored yellow, and the rest of its body is green, and its pupils are red.


== Biography ==
After Durango, Zed, Quinn and CommuniKate dug into the cave with the chest, the Hydra appeared and began to attack the heroes. The heroes decided to split up, and Quinn began to distract the Hydra, and the latter ran after her, and then the Hydra began to charge a strong attack, but Durango and CommuniKate jumped on her back and hit the Hydra in the neck, and then when shot, the Hydra exploded from inside.


== Powers and abilities ==
As an amphibious creature, the Hydra can live both on land and underwater without drowning or dehydrating, as evidenced by it emerging from its underground lake. It can charge and shoot explosive fireballs from its mouths, but all of its three heads face one direction while targeting. It also boasts enough strength to knock any mob far away with its tail.


== Trivia ==
The Hydra is called the Lernaean Hydra in Greek Mythology in reference to its lair being the lake of Lerna in the Argolid.
