Mud brick stairs are a decorative stairs variant of mud bricks that generates in trail ruins and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Mud brick stairs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Mud brick stairs generate as part of trail ruins.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Mud brick stairs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
