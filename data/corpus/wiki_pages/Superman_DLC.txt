== Trivia ==
The DLC was teased in a pop-up prompting the player to get the Superman skin pack, which released on July 8, 2025.
Throughout the DLC, many subtle references to regular Minecraft are made.
Surrounding the Daily Planet globe are carvings of creepers.
Banners throughout Metropolis appear to show chickens.
The shot of Superman standing in front of the sun is a reference to the cover of the first issue of Superman volume 5, which became a popular internet meme.
