A pet cat is a pet variant of the cat introduced in 25w14craftmine.


== Spawning ==
A pet cat spawns next to the player on mine start when the "Pet Cat" Player Unlock is active.


== Drops ==
Pet cats drop nothing when killed.


== Behavior ==
The behaviors between pet cat and the cat in regular versions are almost the same, but there are still some differences:

The player's melee attack can't damage the pet cat.
Creepers and pet creepers don't escape from the pet cat.
Raw cods and raw salmons can't be used on the pet cat.
