Mighty Morphin Power Rangers is a skin pack crossing over with Mighty Morphin Power Rangers, the first season of the long-running Power Rangers tokusatsu franchise, based off of the Japanese show Kyoryu Sentai Zyuranger of the Super Sentai franchise. The skin pack was delisted on March 31, 2020.
