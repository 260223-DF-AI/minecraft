1.88 was an update to PlayStation 4 Edition released on February 26, 2019, which added more features from the Village & Pillage update. This update is equivalent to Bedrock Edition 1.9.0, only missing the Experimental Gameplay features, such as pillagers.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
Flowers
Added new flowers: Lily of the Valley and Cornflower.
Signs
Added new sign variants: spruce, birch, jungle, acacia, and dark oak.
Smooth blocks
Added smooth stone, smooth sandstone, smooth red sandstone, and smooth quartz block.
Shulker Boxes
Added an undyed variant of the shulker box. All previous purple shulker boxes are now undyed shulker boxes.
The purple shulker box texture has been changed to the new color palette.
Slabs, stairs, and walls
Added new slab, stairs, and wall variants:
Stone slabs and stone stairs.
Andesite slabs, andesite stairs, and andesite walls.
Polished andesite slabs and polished andesite stairs.
Diorite slabs, diorite stairs, and diorite walls.
Polished diorite slabs and polished diorite stairs.
Granite slabs, granite stairs, and granite walls.
Polished granite slabs and polished granite stairs.
Mossy stone brick slabs, mossy stone brick stairs, and mossy stone brick walls.
Mossy cobblestone slabs and mossy cobblestone stairs.
Sandstone walls and red sandstone walls.
Smooth sandstone slabs and smooth sandstone stairs.
Smooth red sandstone slabs and smooth red sandstone stairs.
Cut sandstone slabs and cut red sandstone slabs.
Smooth quartz slabs and smooth quartz stairs.
Brick walls.
Stone brick walls.
Prismarine walls.
Nether brick walls.
Red nether brick slabs, red nether brick stairs, and red nether brick walls.
End stone brick slabs, end stone brick stairs, and end stone brick walls.


=== General ===
Mash-up packs
Added Steven Universe Mash-up pack.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Cauldrons
Can now hold lava.
Scaffolding
Can now be placed on top of glass blocks.
More blocks can be placed on scaffolding, including mob heads, and flower pots.


=== Mobs ===
Pandas
Now panic when set on fire.


=== General ===
Tamed animals no longer need to be renamed with a name tag to send death messages to their owner.
Added "Immediate Respawn" host option.
Added several new loading screen tips and trivia.
Added several new splash texts to the main menu.
