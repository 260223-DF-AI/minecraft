The Minecraft Experience Cape‌[JE  only] or MC Experience Cape‌[BE  only] is a cape given to Minecraft Experience: Villager Rescue attendees.


== Distribution ==
Players can receive this cape by redeeming a code rewarded after completing the experience. The codes must be redeemed no later than 30 days after the event closes in the tour city where they were obtained.
The code is distributed using one of two methods; which method is used varies by tour city. In some cities, codes are sent by email after visiting. In other cities, codes are distributed on printed cards when the Orb of Interaction is returned at the end of the experience. Guests will not receive code emails from cities that use the printed card method; in those cities, the code must be collected from the experience.


== History ==


== Trivia ==
The cape was designed by Craig Leigh, a Mojang employee. He took inspiration from Two-Face, a villain appearing in Batman comics.
