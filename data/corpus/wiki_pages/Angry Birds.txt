Minecraft x Angry Birds is a merchandise collaboration between Minecraft and the Angry Birds franchise.


== Marketplace releases ==


=== Angry Birds ===

Released on April 26, 2022, Angry Birds is a DLC map based on the franchise of the same name. The map concerns a conflict between the Angry Birds and Bad Piggies, with several different birds being playable over the course of the story, each with unique abilities. Included in the map are ten skins based off the Angry Birds: Summer Madness television series, featuring both existing characters and new ones made for the program.


=== Angry Birds Pack ===

Continuing off the release of the DLC world, the Angry Birds Pack is a content bundle featuring it, eight character creator items, and  1,720 Minecoins.
