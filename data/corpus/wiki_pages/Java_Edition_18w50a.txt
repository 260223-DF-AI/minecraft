18w50a is the twelfth snapshot for Java Edition 1.14, released on December 12, 2018. It adds a new type of cat, a villager profession, a new statistic, functionality to barrels, blast furnaces, smokers, giants and more. This is the final snapshot released in 2018.


== Additions ==


=== Mobs ===
 Cats

Added Jellie, the winner of the community competition.
 Villagers

Added mason profession.
Currently has no trades.


=== Gameplay ===
Sounds

Added bell sound.
Added raid horn sounds.
Statistics

New "Barrels opened" statistic.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Barrels

Added functionality.
Now acts as a storage unit.
Works in tight spaces, even if there is a block above it, unlike a chest.
Can now be crafted using 6 planks and 2 wooden slabs.
Beds, cobblestone, emerald blocks, end stone, grass, jigsaw blocks, mossy cobblestone, nether quartz ore, quartz pillars, red nether bricks, red sandstone, sandstone, spruce planks

Changed texture.
Bells

Added sounds.
Has a shaking animation when hit or right-clicked.
Based on the position and facing, bells can only be hit in some certain directions, and only shake in those directions.
Changed texture.
Changed model for ceiling bells and added models for wall bells.
Can now be placed on walls.
Changed block states face=ceiling|floor|wall to attachment=ceiling|double_wall|floor|single_wall.
Blast furnaces

Added functionality.
Able to smelt ores twice as fast as the normal furnace.
Cannot smelt anything other than ores.
Can now be crafted using 3 smooth stone, 1 furnace, and 5 iron ingots.
Enchanting tables

Changed obsidian side and bottom textures.
Smokers

Added functionality.
Able to smelt food twice as fast as the normal furnace.
Cannot smelt anything other than food.
Can now be crafted using 4 logs and 1 furnace.
Stripped jungle logs

Changed top texture.


=== Items ===
Baked potatoes, fermented spider eyes, all horse armor, potatoes, poisonous potatoes, rabbit stew

Changed texture.


=== Mobs ===
Cats

Now spawn naturally in villages.
Giants

Now use zombie AI.
Now have all NBT that zombies have.

Villagers and zombie villagers

Now have seven skin types which correspond to the following biomes:
Plains
Desert
Savanna
Taiga/mountain
Snowy tundra/snowy taiga
Jungle
Swamp
Villagers now have five profession levels, showing how many trade tiers they've unlocked by a badge of a varying material on their suit.
The first trade tier is represented by a stone badge, the next iron, then gold, emerald and finally diamond.
Some of the professions have less than five tiers in total.
If a villager summoned by a command has a higher level than its natural highest level, its badge can still be seen, however, the player wouldn't be able to interact with it.
Cured zombie villagers retain their trades when they are converted back into regular villagers.
Witches

Are now neutral mobs.
Changed appearance.
Both of these changes were unintentional.


=== Non-mob entities ===
Boat

Changed texture.
Thrown eggs, thrown ender pearls, thrown bottles o' enchanting, eyes of ender, ghast fireballs, blaze fireballs (fire charge), and snowballs 

Now have an optional Item tag that can specify a different item stack to render.


=== World generation ===
Villages

Updated taiga and desert villages.
Updated village pieces from plains, savanna and snowy villages.
New loot chests for fletcher houses: can contain emeralds, arrows, feathers, eggs, flint, sticks.


=== Gameplay ===
Raids

Patrol leaders now give 13 Bad Omen levels when killed. Pillager outpost patrol leaders always give 1 level only.
Patrol leaders can now spawn at outposts.
Illager beasts start spawning at wave 2, witches at wave 4, and evokers at wave 10.
