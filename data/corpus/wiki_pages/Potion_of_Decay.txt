A potion of Decay is a potion that provides the Wither effect when used.


== Obtaining ==
Potions of Decay can be obtained via commands, and is also available in the Creative inventory, obtained as a tipped arrow rarely in fletcher trades in chances of 0.1%[1]


=== Brewing ===
While the base potion of Decay cannot be obtained via brewing, it can still be brewed into a splash potion or a lingering potion.
