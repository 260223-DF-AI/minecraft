Rooted Together is a  Marketplace map developed by Everbloom Games, designed to raise awareness for mangrove swamp preservation.


== Summary ==

Flora will tell the player all about mangrove forests and why they need to be saved. Then, the player will be prompted to use the Seed Cannon to plant a tree. The player will be explained about the Carbon Observer which detects carbon dioxide, which can be used to see clouds of carbon dioxide. Then, the player will be taught about harvesting seeds to refill the Seed Cannon. The player can now freely plant trees.
Every 10% of carbon dioxide cleared, a radio message will be sent about facts about mangrove forests.


== Animals ==


=== Unlocking animals ===
Every time the player gets near an animal, it is unlocked and the player will receive a book about the animal.
