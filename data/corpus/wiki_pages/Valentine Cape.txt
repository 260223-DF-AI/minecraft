The Valentine Cape is a personal cape that was briefly given to two players.


== Distribution ==
This cape was given to LolFoxy2 by a Mojang employee, Ekkelangelo who started to become friends with LolFoxy2 and resolved their migration problems with their alternative account. It was promptly removed twenty minutes later after discovering its rarity.
It was also given to the player JoinGrave, presumably by accident, and then removed two days later.
