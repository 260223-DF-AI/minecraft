14w34d is the fifty-second and final snapshot for Java Edition 1.8, released on August 20, 2014, which fixes some bugs and crashes from 14w34a, 14w34b, and 14w34c. It was announced that it would likely be the last snapshot before the 1.8 pre-release. This is also the final snapshot released in 2014.
