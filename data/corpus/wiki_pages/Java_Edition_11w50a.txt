11w50a (mislabeled as 11w49a in-game) is the fourth snapshot for Java Edition 1.1, released on December 15, 2011, which adds new splashes and makes more music discs obtainable in Survival, more specifically dropped by creepers. This is the final snapshot released in 2011.

== Changes ==


=== Items ===
 Music discs

All of the music discs, except for "11", can now be obtained when a skeleton kills a creeper.


=== Mobs ===
 Baby sheep

Baby sheep now eat faster.


=== General ===
Debug features

Pressing B no longer changes the player's gamemode.
