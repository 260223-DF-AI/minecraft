Potone redstone ore is a type of ore found in the Potato dimension, only present in 24w14potato.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Potone redstone ore must be mined with an iron hoe or better, otherwise it drops nothing.
Breaking the ore drops 4-5 redstone dust, unless mined with a Silk Touch hoe. It is affected by Fortune enchantment, dropping up to 8 redstone dust, or 6 redstone dust on average with Fortune III.


=== Natural generation ===
Potone redstone ore generates in patches through the Potato dimension in place of potone.
