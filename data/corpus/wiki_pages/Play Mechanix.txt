Play Mechanix is an American arcade game entertainment company based in Skokie, Illinois, best known for the Big Buck Hunter series of arcade games. The company is responsible for developing Minecraft Dungeons Arcade alongside Mojang Studios.


== Games developed ==
Play Mechanix has developed the following arcade games:

Aliens: Extermination (2008)
Big Buck Safari (2008)
Wheel of Fortune (2009)
Big Buck Hunter Pro: Open Season (2010)

Big Buck World (2010)
Big Buck HD (2012)
Aliens: Armageddon (2013)
Barrel of Monkeys (2013)
Big Buck HD Wild (2015)

The Walking Dead (2017)

Big Buck Hunter: Reloaded (2019)
