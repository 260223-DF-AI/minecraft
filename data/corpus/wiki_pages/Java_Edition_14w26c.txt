14w26c is the thirty-first snapshot for Java Edition 1.8, released on June 26, 2014, to fix some critical bugs and a major world-save corrupting problem in the 14w26a and 14w26b snapshots.
It is strongly advised not to use a world that had been last saved with 14w26a or 14w26b; to avoid problems it is advised to use a world backup that was saved with a pre-14w26a version.
A potential means of recovering corrupted worlds using MCEdit was made available by a Reddit user.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Hay bale

Rebalanced how they heal and accelerate the growth of horses.
Jack o'lanterns

Cannot be used to build golems.


=== Mobs ===
Villagers

Now have a head slot where armor or blocks can be put, using the /replaceitem command.


=== General ===
Block models

Items can now specify rotation and translation in their file.
