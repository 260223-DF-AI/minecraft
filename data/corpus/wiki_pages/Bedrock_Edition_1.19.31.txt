1.19.31 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on October 4, 2022, which fixes bugs.


== Additions ==


=== Controls ===
New mobile touch controls: Under Settings > (Controls) > Touch there is a new toggle entitled, Enable New Touch Controls Schemes. When toggled ON, a new Interaction Model dropdown menu will appear, allowing players to use the new Touch scheme, the new Crosshair scheme, or the original Classic scheme.


== Changes ==
Performance upgrades on Xbox


== Fixes ==
Fixed several crashes that could occur during gameplay.
Fixed terrain on maps getting replaced with black pixels. (MCPE-162421)
Fixed a bug that unbound the inventory key when using the Full Keyboard Gameplay toggle.
Fixed players sometimes spawning under the obsidian platform and falling into the void after entering an end portal for the first time after the 1.19.30 update on Realms. (MCPE-162482)
