Programmer Art (in Java Edition) and the Minecraft Classic Texture Pack (in Bedrock and PlayStation 4 editions) are resource packs that primarily use classic Minecraft textures from before the Texture Update. The name in Java Edition refers to the fact that these textures were created by the programmers of the game, rather than a dedicated art team; the term programmer art is common for referring to such assets in video games.
The equivalent resource packs are built into Java Edition and PlayStation 4 Edition, and available as a free download on the Marketplace in Bedrock Edition.
