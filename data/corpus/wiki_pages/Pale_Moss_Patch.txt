Pale moss patch is a feature that grows in the pale garden biome. A large patch consisting of pale moss blocks, pale moss carpets, and grass. A pale moss patch can be created by using bone meal on pale moss blocks. When pale moss blocks are grown with bone meal, they never contain dirt, pale oak saplings, flowers, or either kind of eyeblossoms.
