=== Piglins ===


===== Horde of the Bastion =====

A horde of piglins that likes fortifying their outposts with many walls, thus having the most fortified outposts compared to the other two hordes.

Portal Guard,
Brute


===== Horde of the Spore =====

A horde of piglins that is less fortified than the Horde of the Bastion at their outposts as they don't use walls but they like building their outposts on the high ground.

Lava Launcher,
Sporeback


===== Horde of the Hunt =====

A horde of piglins that doesn't use any fortification in their outposts at all, but their outposts are larger than the others nonetheless. This horde has and uses a lot of bones for their outposts.

Pigmadillo,
Warboar


===== Others =====
Runts, small piglins that make up the bulk of the piglins' forces in all their hordes.
Mace Runt, runts that wields maces.
Blaze Runt, runts that wields blaze rods.
Spore Medic, runts that spreads netherspores which heal wounded piglins.
Piglin Builder, runts that build piglin structures and throw grenades.
Seekers, a piglin within the Horde of the Hunt and Horde of the Spore that carries an explosive fungus which it uses to damage nearby enemies by charging at them and exploding, damaging itself in the process.
Clangers, piglins within all hordes that spawn in higher level outposts and portals, carrying 2 cymbals, which they slam together to stun enemy units.
