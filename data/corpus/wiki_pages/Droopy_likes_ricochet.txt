"Droopy likes ricochet" is an album-exclusive song composed by C418 that only appears on Minecraft - Volume Alpha.


== Track description ==
"Droopy likes ricochet" is composed of a muffled, reverbed synth drums that begins playing a mid-tempo beat before fading into a fuller sound, beginning a calm, cruising synth piece with an echoing "meow" sound and chopped up samples of a robotic voice.
"Droopy likes ricochet" continues into "Droopy likes your face".


== Trivia ==
Droopy likes ricochet is also from C418's previous album, life changing moments seem minor in pictures.
Although never included in the game, "Droopy likes ricochet" was used in a video by Vareide that was featured on minecraft.net.
A remixed mash-up of "Droopy likes ricochet" and "Droopy likes your face" by C418 was released as "Droopy Remembers" for the album 148.
