Dryads are mythological fairy-like monsters that work alongside the Chimera as scorekeepers in Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Appearance ==
Dryads are female humanoid creatures with yellow skin and green hair, eyes, shirt, and sandals. They wear a brown headband with blue and red flowers, as well as a belt around their waist and golden bracelats on both hands. They also have a pair of blue and pink butterfly-like wings.


== Behavior ==
Dryads work as scorekeepers for the Chimera, granting points for those who passed one of their master's challenges. They are capable of flight using their wings.


== Trivia ==
Dryads in Greek mythology are spirits who live in trees and take the form of a beautiful young woman to protect nature.
The dryad in the Minecraft Mini-Series is based on the "Dryad" from Mattel which in turn was based on a skin with the same name in the Greek Mythology Mash-up pack.
