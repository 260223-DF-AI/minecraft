Polished blackstone is the stone-like polished variation of blackstone.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Polished blackstone needs to be broken using a pickaxe, otherwise the block drops nothing.

Polished blackstone has a slightly higher hardness (thus takes slightly longer to break) than its chiseled variant.


=== Natural generation ===
Polished blackstone can generate as part of ruined portals in the Nether.
