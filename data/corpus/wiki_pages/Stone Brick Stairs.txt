Stone brick stairs are a decorative stairs variant of stone bricks that generate in overworld structures and are used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Stone brick stairs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Stone brick stairs generate as a part of cold ocean ruins, ruined portals in the Overworld, and strongholds.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Stone brick stairs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
