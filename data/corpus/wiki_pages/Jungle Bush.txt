A jungle bush is a variant of the jungle tree that generate along the ground in jungle biomes. It cannot be grown from jungle saplings.


== Generation ==
Unlike normal jungle trees, jungle bushes can generate in all jungle biome variants: jungle, bamboo jungle, and sparse jungle.
Jungle bushes generate as 1–2 jungle logs surrounded by oak leaves. They are typically 1–3 layers tall and cover the floor of jungle biomes.
