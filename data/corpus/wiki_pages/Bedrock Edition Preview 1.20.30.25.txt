Beta 1.20.30.25 (Android) or Preview 1.20.30.25 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox) is the tenth and final beta, and ninth and final Preview version for Bedrock Edition 1.20.30, released on August 31, 2023, which fixes bugs.


== Fixes ==
Fixed an issue where a boat wouldn't be placed on the water if the block underneath the water was highlighted.
Fixed a bug in Education mode that prevented summon NPC commands from working if the users did not have worldbuilder permission when the base version of the world was before 1.19.80.
