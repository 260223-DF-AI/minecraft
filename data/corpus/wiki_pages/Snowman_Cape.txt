The Snowman Cape is a personal cape.


== Distribution ==
This cape was given to JulianClark as a reward for making a meme about how he brought Ray Cokes to Notch because of a joke splash text that was in Minecraft at the time. Ray apparently is "his godfather."


== History ==

The account that originally had the snowman cape (JulianClark) was lost and presumably deleted due to not migrating during the Mojang account migration period.
