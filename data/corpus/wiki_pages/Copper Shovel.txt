A copper shovel is a variant of shovel that is crafted from copper ingots. It is used to break dirt, sand, gravel and other related blocks faster, to create dirt paths from dirt-related blocks, and to extinguish campfires. It is the fourth-highest tier of shovel, having higher mining efficiency, durability, and enchantability than a stone shovel.

== Usage ==


=== Digging ===
A copper shovel can be used to break dirt and soil-type materials faster. When breaking a block, it uses 1 durability. For blocks that break instantly, it uses 0 durability.


=== Mining snow ===
A copper shovel can be used to break snow blocks or snow to obtain snow balls without Silk Touch.


=== Dirt paths ===
A copper shovel can be used to create a dirt path by using (not digging) the shovel on any one of the following blocks, losing one durability point:

Grass Block
Dirt
Coarse Dirt
Rooted Dirt
Podzol
Mycelium


=== Campfire ===
A copper shovel can be used to extinguish a campfire.


=== Weapon ===


==== Java Edition ====
Copper shovels have an attack speed modifier of -3, meaning they take 1 second to recover. Copper shovels deal 3.5HP × 1.75 damage.


==== Bedrock Edition ====
Copper shovels always attack instantly and do 4HP damage.


=== Repairing ===


==== Combining ====


==== Unit repair ====

A copper shovel can be repaired in an anvil by adding copper ingots, with each ingot restoring 62 durability points (25% of maximum durability). Two copper shovels can also be combined in an anvil. Both methods preserve the shovel's enchantments.
