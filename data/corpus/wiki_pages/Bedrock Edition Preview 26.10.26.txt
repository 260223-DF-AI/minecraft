Beta 26.10.26 (ChromeOS, Android) or Preview 26.10.26 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox, PlayStation) is the sixth beta and sixth and final Preview version for Bedrock Edition 26.10, released on March 4, 2026, which fixes bugs.


== Changes ==


=== Gameplay ===
Trading

Re-added the master librarian level, where they will now offer red and yellow candles for three emeralds each.


== Fixes ==
4 issues fixed
From the 26.10 development versions

MCPE-236650 – Librarians cannot level up to Master due to having no master-level trade
MCPE-236656 – Villager movement is choppy and extremely fast
Other

Fixed the baby sheep texture causing layers to turn invisible when dithering was enabled.
Mob and player heads are now scaled correctly when worn by humanoid baby mobs.
