1.21.132 (Windows) or 1.21.132.1 (iOS), dubbed Copper, Collaborate & Compete, is a minor update to Minecraft Education released on February  17, 2026. It adds features from The Copper Age and Mounts of Mayhem game drops.


== Additions ==

Added features and items from Bedrock Edition 1.21.93, to Bedrock Edition 1.21.132, The Copper Age and Mounts of Mayhem.
Added the "Lava Chicken" music disc from Bedrock Edition 1.21.93.
Added Dedicated Servers.


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Python Notebooks has been removed as a supported IDE.


== Fixes ==
Further improvements in performance and product stability.
Bugfixes and stability improvements.
