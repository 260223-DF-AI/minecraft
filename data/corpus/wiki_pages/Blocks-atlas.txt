blocks-atlas was a texture atlas used by the game to store textures for blocks and fluids, following the deprecation of terrain.png
It was eventually merged with items-atlas to form textures-atlas.

==== Anisotropic Filtering ====
When toggled on, this option would add eight pixels to the edge of each texture, taken from the other side. This results in atlases which look considerably different, and with different dimensions.


==== Mipmapped versions ====
Up to four mipmapped versions of each atlas would also be generated by the game from 13w38a onwards, depending on the current values in Video Settings. The textures would be shrunk to 8×8, 4×4, 2×2 and 1×1, and would render instead of the normal textures at far distances or sharp angles of incidence.
