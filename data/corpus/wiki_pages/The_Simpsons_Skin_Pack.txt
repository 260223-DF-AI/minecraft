The Simpsons Skin Pack is a skin pack containing 24 skins of characters from the animated sitcom The Simpsons and was available on Legacy Console Editions until May 30, 2017. Due to the Better Together Update, it was added in Bedrock Edition on September 12, 2017, although it is only available to players who owned it already on Xbox consoles when they migrated from Legacy Console Edition.
