Spark Universe is a game development and animation studio, responsible for developing many add-ons and maps for the Marketplace. Among their work, they are known for making many releases promoting A Minecraft Movie. They also jointly created the resource pack for the A Minecraft Movie Live Event, along with Mojang.

== Trivia ==
Several of the studio's projects have been directly sponsored by Mojang Studios.
Realms Plus included Planes, Lucky Block: Summer, Zombies, Monsters++, and Lucky Blocks.
Marketplace Pass included InsaneCraft Pack 1.6, Spark Portals, and the character creator items Flame Samurai Chest and Dark Monster Mask.
Their map Realism Craft was used to promote the introduction of dynamic worlds, even receiving a special Marketplace button.
