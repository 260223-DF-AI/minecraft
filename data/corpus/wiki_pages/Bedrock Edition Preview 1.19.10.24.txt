Beta 1.19.10.24 (Android) or Preview 1.19.10.24 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox) is the fifth and final beta/Preview for Bedrock Edition 1.19.10, released on June 23, 2022, which fixed some bugs. 


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
Allay

Allay duplication now has a 5-minute cooldown.


=== General ===
Experiments

Removed the Vanilla Experiment and Wild Update toggle since it has no active experimental content.


== Fixes ==
Mobs

Fixed a bug that caused an empty trader llama Spawn Egg to appear in the Creative menu in pre 1.19.10 worlds.
Stability and Performance

Fixed a crash when assigning an invalid entity to monster spawners.
Touch Controls

Fixed touch control's right d-pad button not working when the d-pad area overlapped with the hotbar area (MCPE-156103).
Commands

Improved the performance of the locate biome command to mitigate stalls on the server while searching for a biome that is far away (MCPE-157609).
