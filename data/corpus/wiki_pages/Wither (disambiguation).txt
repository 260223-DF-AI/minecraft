Wither may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Mobs ===
Wither - a boss that can be created by players.
Wither Spawn Egg
Wither Skeleton - a Nether variation of the skeletons similar to the wither.
Wither Skeleton Spawn Egg


=== Items ===
Wither Rose - a flower which gives the Wither status effect, created after the wither slays a mob.
Wither's totem painting - a painting of the recipe to create a wither.
Nether Star - an item that can be dropped by a wither.


=== Blocks ===
Chiseled Red Sandstone - a chiseled block with a wither image on it.
Wither Skeleton Skull - a rare drop from the wither skeletons.
Soul Sand - a block that can be used to summon the wither.
Soul Soil - a block that can be used to summon the wither.


=== Effect ===
Wither - an effect that can be caused by either wither or wither skeleton.


=== Jokes ===
Pink Wither - a joke variation of the wither in an April Fools' version.

== Minecraft: Story Mode ==


=== Mobs ===
Wither Storm - the main antagonist from The Order of the Stone to A Block and a Hard Place


=== Items ===
Withered Nether Star - a variant of the Nether star obtained from the wither storm.
Wither Skull - a skull obtained from wither skeletons.


=== Illnesses ===
Wither - an illness contracted from wither mobs, including the wither storm. Also referred to as "wither sickness".
Mad Wither - an illness contracted from the wither storm that causes retrograde amnesia without inducing wither effects.


=== Events ===
Wither storm disaster, an unnatural disaster caused by the wither storm


== Minecraft Dungeons ==


=== Mobs ===
Wither Skeleton - a melee Nether variation of the skeletons.
Wither Skeleton Archer - an archer Nether variation of the skeletons.


=== Armors ===
Wither Armor - an armor made by the parts of slain wither skeletons.


=== Enchantments ===
Withering - an enchantment exclusive for the stone swords and the red snakes of the wither skeletons.


=== Effects ===
Wither - an effect exclusive to wither skeletons.
