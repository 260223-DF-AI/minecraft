A fisherman is a villager who sells fishing items and campfires. Fishermen wear fisher hats and have a blue fish as well as a net on their trousers secured with a strap.


== Spawning ==
An unemployed villager turns into an fisherman when an unclaimed barrel is nearby.


=== Natural generation ===

The workstation of fishermen, the barrel, generates in fisherman houses in villages.


== Drops ==

In Java Edition, a fisherman can drop various items by throwing them as a gift towards a nearby player with the Hero of the Village effect. The gift is randomly selected from the following list:

Raw Cod
Raw Salmon
