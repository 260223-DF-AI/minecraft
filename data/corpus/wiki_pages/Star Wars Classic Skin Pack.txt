Star Wars Classic Skin Pack is a skin pack that includes 55 skins of characters from Star Wars: Episode IV – A New Hope (originally just Star Wars), Star Wars: Episode V – The Empire Strikes Back, and Star Wars: Episode VI – Return of the Jedi, the movies that form the Star Wars original trilogy.
