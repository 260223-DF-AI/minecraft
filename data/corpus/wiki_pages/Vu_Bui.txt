Vu Bui is the Media Director of Mojang Studios and has held this position since October 2012. He is well-known as a host in many Minecraft shows such as Minecraft Now (in some episodes), and especially Minecraft Monthly. And for Minecraft Live, he is known as the Host of the Deep Dig After Show, the voice of Tiny Vu, a miniaturized pixelated doppelganger of himself featured in several promotional videos for the Mob Vote and such, and for being previously the Biome Vote/Mob Vote Correspondent before the dynamic's cancelation in 2024. He's also one of the Producers of A Minecraft Movie alongside Torfi Frans Olafsson.
Besides his work on Minecraft, Vu has also been the president of the charity group Block by Block since October 2015. He shares a YouTube channel about photography with his wife Lydia Winters.
He is known to be of Vietnamese descent and has a brother named Lan Bui through his Instagram and Twitter posts.
