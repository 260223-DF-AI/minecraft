Adventure Time is an animated television series that aired on Cartoon Network from 2010-2018, telling the story of Jake the Dog and Finn the Human in the Land of Ooo.


== About ==
Adventure Time is an action-adventure comedy series, known for its distinctive "noodley" art style and nonchalant sense of humor. The show is set in the post-apocalyptic Land of Ooo, a continent where anything could happen. The heroes are a young boy named Finn and his friend, a shapeshifting dog named Jake, as they go on various weird adventures. Across their journeys, they regularly come into conflict with the Ice King, a fiendish villain who kidnaps princesses, most commonly the scientist Princess Bubblegum. Other characters include Princess Bubblegum's pet Lady Rainicorn, Finn and Jake's sentient game console BMO, Lumpy Space Princess, and Marceline the Vampire Queen.
