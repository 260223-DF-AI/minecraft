The termite was a proposed mob that was featured in the Biome Chooser at MINECON Earth 2018, alongside the ostrich, meerkat and fox. It was shown to be tiny and crawling on a mound in savannas; however, proper mechanics were never established.
It, along with the rest of the biome, made it to the second round with 27.6%, but lost with 34.7%. Unlike other mobs featured in Mob Votes and similar events, it would not be featured in the 2023 Mob Vote Event.
