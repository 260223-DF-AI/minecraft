A wind wand is an item introduced in 25w14craftmine. It uses the breeze rod texture with an enchantment glint. It has its other counterpart, the fire wand.


== Obtaining ==
The wind wand is only obtainable without commands by getting the upgrade "Heart of the Oak", but by default this upgrade is obfuscated. Killing a breeze makes this upgrade readable.


== Usage ==
The wind wand shoots out a wind charge when used.


== Issues ==
Issues relating to "Wind Wand" are not maintained on the bug tracker because it is an April Fools' joke, and is therefore not in the newest stable version or snapshot. Issues reported there are closed as "Invalid".
