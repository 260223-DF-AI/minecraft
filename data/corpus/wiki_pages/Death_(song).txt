"Death" is an album-exclusive song composed by C418 that appears only on Minecraft - Volume Alpha, and is the shortest track on the album.


== Track description ==
"Death" is composed of a piano piece that plays in tangent with sound effects. At the beginning, a player can be heard traversing during the night, as watery sounds are made. As the player approaches, a chicken is heard clucking. The player then charges their bow at the chicken and shoots, killing the chicken. Quickly, the player runs to grab their drop.


== Trivia ==
While "Death" uses Minecraft sound effects, most of the sounds featured are original and did not appear in the game when the album was released.
