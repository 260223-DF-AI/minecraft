2.0.1049 (Windows) or 2.0.1051 (macOS) is an update to the launcher released on May 8, 2018.


== Changes ==
Removed the "Allow Mojang to collect user data" toggle.
Changed access to Minecraft versions.


== Fixes ==
MCL-8432: Attempting to use "Forgot Password" on windows doesn't open the link in the default browser.
MCL-9178: "Need Assistance?" link in skins tab does not work.
