Strength is a status effect which increases attack damage.


== Effect ==
In Java Edition, it increases melee damage by 3HP × level.
In Bedrock Edition, melee damage under the Strength effect can be found through the equation BaseDamage×1.3level+1.3level−10.3, where BaseDamage is the default damage of whatever weapon or tool is used to attack. Example for an iron sword:

Normal: 7HP
Level 1: 10.1HP × 5.05
Level 2: 14.13HP × 7.065


== Exceptions ==
Strength does not affect the damage of spear charge attacks in Java Edition or the damage of ranged attacks.


== Causes ==


== Immune mobs ==
Withers and ender dragons are immune to Strength.
Any mob that cannot attack or only attacks with projectiles is unaffected by Strength.
