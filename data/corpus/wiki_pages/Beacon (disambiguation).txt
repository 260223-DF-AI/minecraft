Beacon may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Blocks ===
Beacon - A block that grants various effects to nearby players.


=== Advancements and Achievement ===
Beaconator - An achievement/goal advancement when a player fully powers and activates a beacon.
Bring Home the Beacon - An advancement when a player activates a beacon of any size.


== Minecraft: Story Mode ==
Beacontown, a large town led by Jesse (formerly) or Radar (determinant).


== Minecraft Dungeons ==
Beacon, an interactable object found in missions
Corrupted Beacon, an artifact that uses souls


== Minecraft Legends ==
Night Beacon, a unique kind of piglin base encountered during the first act of the Campaign
