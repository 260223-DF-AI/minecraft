The well is a haunted location used as the setting of the second half of The Well Wisher in Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Description ==
The well is located at a proximity of the farm. Outside, it resembles a generic well made of cobblestone, logs, and a fence on top. Inside, the well is made up of cobblestone and its mossy variant. A small cavity is open on a wall, at a distance from the bottom of the well. On the other side, four stands, each dedicated to a team, are placed next to each other, and each stand has sixteen item frames placed on the wall, used for the puzzle. Chests are scattered beneath the stands, sometimes close to the floor. Between the two walls is the scoreboard for the teams. Inside the cavity, there is a lever used by CommuniKate to make the waters rise.
