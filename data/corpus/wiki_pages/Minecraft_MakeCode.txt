Minecraft MakeCode is a version of Microsoft MakeCode designed for Minecraft Education. It serves primarily as an educational tool, used to taught programming in Blocks, Python and JavaScript.
Minecraft MakeCode also offers subject kits, including a computer science kit.
Minecraft MakeCode provides several tutorials, ranging from position and building, to programming with the agent and making mini games, with additional videos, projects and extensions being able to be watched, saved and imported.
