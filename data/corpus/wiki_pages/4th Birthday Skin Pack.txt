4th Birthday Skin Pack is a skin pack that includes 20 skins of Steve and Alex wearing mob-themed t-shirts, celebrating the fourth anniversary of Xbox 360 Edition's release.
It was available from May 9 to May 16, 2016 and May 9 to May 21, 2017 for Xbox 360 and Xbox One editions. 
Due to the Better Together Update, it was added in Bedrock Edition on September 12, 2017, although it is only available to players who owned it already when they migrated from Legacy Console Edition.
