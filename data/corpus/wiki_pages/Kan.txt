Kan is a character in the novel Minecraft: The End. He lives in the End and is the friend of protagonists Fin and Mo.


== Appearance ==

Kan is described as having green eyes, for which he was bullied in the past.


== Personality ==

Kan loves playing music on his note block. His hubunits dislike this sound, which is why he has a sour relationship with his hubunits, especially Karshan. He frequently visits the End ship of his friends Fin and Mo. He practices in the Enderdome, but does not like it, especially because Taskmaster Owari keeps telling him to "get stronger" whenever he complains.


== Biography ==

Kan is introduced having left his hubunits and entered the End ship of Fin and Mo. Shortly after that, his hubunits come in and search the ship. In the meeting on the central island, Karshan reveals Kan as a human and berates him for this, throwing away his note block. Kan cries to this, and is healed by a golden apple.
Later, it is revealed that Fin and Mo "broke the cycle" by deciding to save him. He eventually proves instrumental in the battle against the Ender Dragon when his music distracts the dragon long enough to be slain.
