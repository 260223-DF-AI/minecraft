19w03c is the sixteenth snapshot for Java Edition 1.14, released on January 18, 2019, which fixes one major bug.


== Fixes ==
5 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.14

MC-114779 – Stairs render faces that cannot be seen normally
MC-130154 – Checkerboard Buffet worlds incorrectly generate ocean and rivers.
From the previous development version

MC-142521 – Players are invisible and do not interact with the world normally.
MC-142560 – Chests sometimes remain open after closing the GUI.
MC-142577 – Farmland glitches player.
