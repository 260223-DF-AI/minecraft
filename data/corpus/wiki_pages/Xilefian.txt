Felix Jones, also known as Xilefian ( /zalfin/ ZY-leh-FEE-n; derived from his first name backwards), is a Minecraft Technical Lead at Mojang Studios and the tech lead for Minecraft Bedrock Edition gameplay. He joined the Minecraft team in January 2020.


== Trivia ==
Xilefian has been playing Minecraft since alpha.
Xilefian had a fun moment while deciding how to do the eyes on his Minecraft player skin.
His skin makes a cameo in the video Minecraft Celebrates the Community, being blown up by a creeper.
