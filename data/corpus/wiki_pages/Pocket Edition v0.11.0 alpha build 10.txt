v0.11.0 alpha build 10 is the tenth build version released for v0.11.0.


== Additions ==


=== Skin packs ===
Version exclusive: City Folk Skin Pack.
Version exclusive: Town Folk Skin Pack.


== Changes ==
Third person interactions are only available in front of the player.
Increased creative mode interaction range.


== Fixes ==
13 bugs fixed

Clients connecting to the server would teleport the host when spawning.
Hotbar won’t shift to the left anymore.
Players are affected by knockback on servers.
Players can be pulled by a fishing rod.
Chat, PotionEffect, and Similar buttons will only open when tapping (not dragging).
Increased amount of gravel on Extreme Hills Mutated biome.
World opening errors are now logged (for future fixes).
From the 0.11.0 development versions

MCPE-8215 - Sand block disappearing when falling (worlds before 0.11 only).
MCPE-8285 - All leaves are not named correctly.
MCPE-8305 - Game crash when breaking Nether Reactor Core.
From the previous development version

MCPE-8399 - Eating food gives incorrect particles.
MCPE-8429 - Stone Brick bug.
MCPE-8436 - Farming wheat, potatoes and carrots doesn't work.
