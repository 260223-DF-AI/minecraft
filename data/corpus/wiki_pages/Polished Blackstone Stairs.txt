Polished blackstone stairs are a decorative stairs variant of polished blackstone that generates in ruined portals and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Polished blackstone stairs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Polished blackstone stairs generate as part of ruined portals in the Nether.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Polished blackstone stairs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
