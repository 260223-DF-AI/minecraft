A scrape pottery sherd is a pottery sherd that depicts an axe. It can be found in trial chambers.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Breaking a decorated pot causes it to drop all of the bricks and pottery sherds used to craft it, meaning it drops each of the scrape pottery sherds that were used to craft it.


=== Natural generation ===
Decorated pots with scrape pottery sherds on their sides naturally generate in trial chambers. For a randomly generated pot in trial chambers, there is 1⁄13 chance that is has one scrape pottery sherd. 


== Usage ==


=== Crafting ingredient ===
Scrape pottery sherds can be used to craft decorated pots.
