A smooth sandstone slab is a decorative slab variant of smooth sandstone that generates in desert villages and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Smooth sandstone slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Smooth sandstone slabs generate as part of desert villages.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Smooth sandstone slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.


== Sounds ==
In Bedrock Edition, when a smooth sandstone slab is combined into a double slab, the block's use sound is played.
