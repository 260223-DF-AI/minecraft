The swaggiest stairs ever is an April Fools' joke block. It is a netherite block variant of stairs, added in Java Edition 20w14.


== Obtaining ==
The swaggiest stairs ever is available in the Creative inventory, and naturally generates in the "missing" dimension. It is also seen in other dimensions, one of them being the "gallery" dimension.


=== Breaking ===
The swaggiest stairs ever drops itself if mined with a diamond pickaxe or better. If mined with any other tool, it drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
The swaggiest stairs ever naturally generates in the "missing" dimension as the stairs leading up to the chest containing the footprints. 7 stairs generate here.

== Issues ==
Issues relating to "Swaggiest stairs ever" are not maintained on the bug tracker because it is an April Fools' joke, and is therefore not in the newest stable version or snapshot. Issues reported there are closed as "Invalid".


== Trivia ==
Despite being an April Fools joke block, it appears in Minecraft Dungeons on various structures.
Internally, including the command, it is known as "Netherite Stairs".
