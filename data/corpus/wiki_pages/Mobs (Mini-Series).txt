In Minecraft Mini-Series, a mob refers to any creature that appear throughout the story.
In the series, mobs can be passive, neutral, or hostile, and while some are essential to the story and interact with the characters, others serve as background elements or temporary obstacles. Unlike in the original Minecraft.
Some of the mobs may have given names, defined personalities, and roles in the story, effectively making them characters.
There are several kinds of mobs exclusively found in Minecraft Mini-Series that are not present in Minecraft, the original game.
