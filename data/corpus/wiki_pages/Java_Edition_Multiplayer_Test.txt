Multiplayer Test refers to 38 versions of Java Edition Classic released between May 31 and July 11, 2009. As the name suggests, these versions were released mainly to implement and develop multiplayer.


== Additions ==

Blocks
Sponges, which were a temporary fix to stop water from flooding until finite water was implemented. It soaked up water in a 555 volume around it.
See-through glass.
Wool (Labeled as "Cloth" in this version). Colors include aqua-green, black, blue, cyan, green, gray, indigo, lime, magenta, orange, pink, purple, red, violet, white, and yellow.
Dandelion and rose.
Red and brown mushrooms.
Block of gold.
Multiplayer
Players' names show above their heads.
List logged-in players by pressing  Tab.
Added custom skins.
Chat
Players can chat to other players on the server.
Commands
/ban, /banip, /broadcast (Renamed to /say), /deop, /kick, /op, /teleport alias /tp, /unban, /setspawn, and /solid.
Admin-only.
Inventory
Accessed by pressing B.
Hotbar can display 9 blocks or items in each slot.
Sounds
Added multiple .ogg sound files including block footsteps and breaking blocks.
