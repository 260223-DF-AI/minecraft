The Enderall is a large hostile enderman-like creature with an "otherworldly origin" that appeared as a skin in the Campfire Tales Skin Pack and later as the main antagonist of The Ender Cave in Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Appearance ==
The Enderall resembles a distorted version of an enderman, more humanoid in nature. Its body is more detailed, with what looks to be smooth flesh, but a stretched face merged with its torso, the jaw being where the waist would be. Instead of a regular enderman's dislocated jaw, the inside of the Enderall is a glowing pink, same as its eyes.
In Minecraft Mini-Series, the Enderall is redesigned to a more toyetic style, with the body being simplified to a cuboid-like shape with thinner arms. The glowing parts are noticeably more fuchsia in color, with dark purple eyes.  It has a purple weak spot on its back.


== Powers and abilities ==
Within Mini-Series, the Enderall has teleporting abilities like an enderman, along with the ability to suction mobs, objects, and lightweight materials into its portal-like mouth. It can also launch blocks or items it sucked in back out as a ranged attack.


== Biography ==


=== Mini-Series ===
The two teams of a reality game show make their way to Scatter Island, which is home to numerous endermen, and are informed that they have to kill the endermen and collect ten of their ender pearls. After this is done, the host declares that only they can say who won and unleashes The Enderall from behind a wall of stone. The Enderall reveals its threat by sucking up an enderman. Team Stone Beach then goes up against it, and come out victorious, win the challenge, and are rewarded with two bread, two pumpkin pie, and two cooked chicken, which they split upon the remaining teams. This sharing becomes upsetting to the host.
