The Kraken is a giant squid-like creature that appears in the Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Appearance ==
The Kraken looks similar to a Squid, but his shape of head are different, he has red eyes with angry eyebrows and is paler in color, and also has large mouth with a nether portal inside.


== Biography ==
After the contestants of the first season of the game show accidentally teleported themselves to the Greek Isles because of CommuniKate, she tried collaborating with Poseidon as her co-host for the second season. However, he took over the show without her consent and ordered her, Zed, Quinn, and Durango to find the five enchanted eyes of ender. Promising the "ultimate treasure" after collecting all the eyes, Poseidon also threatened them that the other contestants, who were kidnapped on sand pillars above the sea, would get eaten by the Kraken and banished to the Nether if his players didn't pay the ransom on time, which is the eyes themselves.
When the team managed to gather all five enchanted ender eyes, Poseidon revealed that the "ultimate treasure" was actually a hoax and teleported them altogether on a sand pillar near the Kraken. Later, River and Toby found a use for the fish Theseus gave them during their quest. They threw them on the water to distract the Kraken before other contestants flee from the ocean to get revenge on Poseidon.


== Powers and abilities ==
Being a giant version of a squid, the kraken has the ability to use its eight tentacles as a means of attack strong enough to take down a slab from a pillar of sand in one hit. Its mouth has a Nether portal inside, making it practically impossible to eat prey, yet this can cause them to be banished in the Nether with no escape.
