== Trivia ==
Hoglin meat was previously an unused item found in the code of Bedrock Edition, presumably deemed redundant in favor of porkchops.
The item green ham and eggs is a reference to the children's book Green Eggs and Ham, reordered presumably due to copyright concerns.
Coincidentally, a map featuring works of Dr. Suess was previously produced.
