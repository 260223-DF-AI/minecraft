Raw salmon is a food item that can be eaten by the player or cooked to make cooked salmon.

=== Generated loot ===


=== Fishing ===
Raw salmon can be obtained from fishing, with a 21.3% chance per attempt.


=== Villager gifts ===
In Java Edition, Fisherman villagers throw raw salmon at players under the Hero of the Village effect.


== Usage ==


=== Smelting ingredient ===


=== Food ===
Raw salmon restores 2 () hunger and 0.4 saturation.


=== Cats ===
Raw salmon can be used to tame cats with 13 chance of success, get cats off of chests, and beds, breed cats, and make baby cats grow up faster by 10% of the remaining time. Additionally, raw salmon can be used to heal cats by 2HP.


=== Ocelots ===
Raw salmon can be used to gain ocelot trust, breed ocelots, and make baby ocelots grow up faster by 10% of the remaining time.


=== Dolphins ===
A dolphin can be fed raw salmon to increase its trust of the player and cause it to interact with the player more often. However, unlike most other animal mobs, this does not cause them to breed. Additionally, raw salmon can be used to make baby dolphins grow up faster by 10% of the remaining time.
Dolphins swim to the nearest chest in a shipwreck or underwater ruin after they are fed raw salmon. If the chest in the nearest structure is broken, they swim to another structure with a chest.


=== Trading ===


=== Wolves ===
Raw salmon can be used to feed wolves, healing them by 4HP. However, in Bedrock Edition, unlike other wolf food, raw salmon cannot be used to speed up the growth of baby wolves nor used to breed them and can only be fed to a wolf that is not at full health.
