The Default Skin Pack is a skin pack consisting of 18 skins included by default in Legacy Console Edition. Most of the skins from this pack were later released in Bedrock Edition as part of the Legacy Skin Pack.
In Legacy Console Edition, if a player has not manually selected a skin to use, they are automatically assigned one of these skins, depending on the order they joined a game (player 1 is Steve, player 2 is Tennis Steve, player 3 is Tuxedo Steve, and so on).
In Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, Tennis Steve, Tennis Alex, Scottish Steve, and Swedish Alex appear as playable alternative costumes of Steve and Alex.
