0.24_SURVIVAL_TEST is a version of Java Edition Classic and the first version of Survival Test, released on September 1, 2009, at 16:54 UTC.


== Additions ==


=== Mobs ===
 Creepers

Hostile mob.
Has 20HP × 10 health points.
Flashes when at low health
Returns to full brightness when hit.
Explodes on death, dealing damage and destroying the blocks around it and dropping them as items.
Pursues and attacks the player, dealing melee damage.
Deals 2HP to 6HP when attacking and up to 12HP × 6 when exploding on death.[verify for Java Edition]
 Pigs

Passive mob.
Has 20HP × 10 health points.
Wanders aimlessly around the world.
 Skeletons

Hostile mob.
Has 20HP × 10 health points.
Pursues and attacks the player, dealing melee damage.
Deals 1HP to 6HP damage.
At the time, skeletons did not shoot arrows, instead, they attack the player directly.
 Zombies

Hostile mob.
Has 20HP × 10 health points.
Pursues and attacks the player, dealing melee damage.
Deals 1HP to 6HP damage.


=== Non-mob entities ===
  Plate armor

Visual feature only.
Provides no protection.
Can only be worn as a helmet or chestplate by zombies and skeletons.
  Chain armor

The model and texture are not used.
Visual feature only.
Provides no protection.
 Arrows

Can be shot by pressing ↹ Tab.
Deals 3HP damage.
Does not deal damage after flying for more than 1 second.
Drops

Blocks and mobs are now able to drop items:
Most blocks drop themselves, with exceptions:
Leaves drop saplings (1⁄10 chance).
Grass block drops dirt.
Logs drop 3-5 planks.
Player

Added the player's fist in the lower right corner.
 Signs

Added as an entity.
Can be spawned by pressing B.
Cannot be edited.
Always says: "This is a test of the signs. Each line can be 15 chars!".


=== Gameplay ===
Damage

Mobs and players jump back and flash white when taking damage and have a dying animation.
Taking damage causes the player's camera to tilt towards the damage source, acting as a directional damage indicator.
Enemies fall over when killed.
Damage after falling

The players and mobs take fall damage after falling more than 3 blocks.
At the same time, they take about 1HP damage for every block fallen over their safe fall distance.
Drowning

The player can drown and has limited breath indicated by bubbles.
A player who runs out of air underwater begins drowning, taking 2HP damage per second.
 Health

Has 20HP × 10 health points.
Each heart is 2 health points.
When the player reaches 0HP health points, they die and are shown a "Game over!" screen with the option to generate a new level.
Particles

Re-added sapling particles, although they are now darker than before.[verify for Java Edition]
Score points

Added score.
Score can only be seen after death.
Killing zombies gives 100 points.
Killing skeletons gives 100 points.
Killing creepers gives 250 points.
Killing pigs gives 10 points.
Survival mode

Players can not respawn and the level needed to be restarted.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Dandelion

No longer decays in darkness.
Grass block

Now drops dirt when destroyed.
Lava

Now deals damage.
Rose

No longer decays in darkness.
Sapling

Now grows into a tree if there is enough space and if it's placed on grass.
No longer decays in darkness.


=== Items ===
Brown mushroom

Heals 5HP when eaten.
No longer decays in darkness.
Now will decay if not on stone, cobblestone, or gravel. Previously they had to be on dirt or grass blocks.
Red mushroom

Depletes 3HP when eaten.
No longer decays in darkness.
Now will decay if not on stone, cobblestone, or gravel. Previously they had to be on dirt or grass blocks.


=== Mobs ===
 Mob

Texture changed from , adding a second layer of hair.
Can no longer be spawned by pressing G.


=== World generation ===
General

More cliffs.
Longer and narrower caves.
The deeper down in the world, the bigger the caves are.
A layer of lava now generates above the bedrock layer.


=== Gameplay ===
Controls

Right-click to build and left-click to mine.
The player can auto-jump again.
Player reach reduced by 1 block.
Sky

Changed color from  #7FCCFF to  #99CCFF.


=== General ===
Options menu

Bobbing and the "View Bobbing" option added.
Anaglyph 3D added.
Level size options under "Generate New Level" are now closer together.


== Technical ==
Performance

Faster rendering.


== Removals ==


=== Blocks ===
General

liquid raising. Sand and gravel now fall through liquids instead of sitting on top of them.


=== Gameplay ===
Creative mode

Removed.


=== General ===
Controls

White block selection box removed.
Pressing R key no longer sends the player back to spawn point.
Removed the ability to spawn mobs by pressing G key.


== Bugs ==
11 bugs reported

If a creeper blows up a liquid, the liquid drops itself.
Cracking animation only shows on one side of a block.
Skeletons can enter 1-block high holes.[verify for Java Edition]
Minecraft crashes upon loading for some users.
OpenGL invalid value bug.
Arrows do not deal damage after flying for more than 1 second.
Players cannot damage themselves with arrows.
Middle-clicking a block (pick-block) adds it into the player's inventory even when they do not yet have that block.
Only works on blocks that could previously be copied.
Doing so replaces the block type of whatever you are holding, but not its amount.
Placing a block at the build limit consumes the block, destroying it without placing anything.
Looking at glass no longer makes glass textures directly behind it disappear.[verify for Java Edition]
Walking on the very edge of a block causes the amount of sounds required to play to build up. Once the player stands on a block, those sounds play every 0.125 seconds.
