A version of Minecraft Infdev was released on June 18, 2010, marking the first Seecret Friday update. This is the only version of Infdev available in the launcher.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Rails

Crafted with 6 iron ingots and a stick.


=== Non-mob entities ===
  Minecarts

Crafted with 5 iron ingots.

Used as portable storage.
Right-clicking minecarts opens them like a chest (with the container called "Minecart"). Filling them up makes the "dirt" layer inside of them rise.
 Falling blocks

Called "FallingSand".
Appears when sand or gravel lose their base of support.
Сan now be dropped by falling onto a torch or a slab.


=== Other ===
