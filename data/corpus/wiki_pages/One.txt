One (stylized as one on Bandcamp) is the soundtrack released for Minecraft: The Story of Mojang by C418, which features all of the songs in the documentary. It was released on December 23, 2012 on Bandcamp, Deezer, and YouTube Music (back then known as Google Play Music), followed by a release on January 2, 2013 on Spotify, Apple Music, and Amazon Music. A physical edition was also released by Fangamer, containing four remixes.
