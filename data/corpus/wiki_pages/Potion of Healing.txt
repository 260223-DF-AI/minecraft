A potion of Healing is a potion that provides Instant Health when used.
The potion effect heals all non-undead mobs and entities, for undead mobs it deals damage.


== Obtaining ==


=== Brewing ===


=== Mob loot ===
A witch has an 8.5% chance to drop a potion of Healing if it dies while drinking the potion.


=== Generated loot ===
In Bedrock Edition the cauldron in a swamp hut has a 25% chance to contain a potion of Healing.
Two Instant Health II potions can be found in the brewing stand on End ships in End cities.


== Usage ==


=== Effect ===


==== Undead mobs ====

The effect of potions of Healing are reversed on undead mobs. It deals damage instead of heals them.


=== Base potion ===
Potion of Healing is a base for brewing potions of Harming.
