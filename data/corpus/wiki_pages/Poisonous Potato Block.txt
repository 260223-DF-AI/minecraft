A poisonous potato block is a compacted block made of poisonous potatoes in 24w14potato.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
A poisonous potato block can be broken using anything, but a pickaxe is the fastest. Poisonous potato blocks always drop themselves when mined.

Note: No version exists with both copper tools and this block, but the breaking time can be calculated from the block's hardness.


=== Natural generation ===
Poisonous potato blocks generate naturally as part of potato geodes.
