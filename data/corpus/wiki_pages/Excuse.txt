"Excuse" is an album-exclusive song composed by C418 that only appears on Minecraft - Volume Alpha.


== Track description ==
"Excuse" is a sparse, somewhat eerie track that consists of a piano playing a sad piece with slightly discordant chords that echo as if in a cave (sometimes with a vibrato effect applied). The song ends with vinyl warping sounds.


== Trivia ==
"Excuse" was originally supposed to play in the Nether, but then C418 decided when he started creating the album that he wanted the album to be "more than just carbon copies of whats in the game".
