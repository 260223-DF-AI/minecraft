Mod armor is an item exclusive to China Edition, used for add-on development, which has now become obsolete.


== Obtaining ==
The item can be obtained through the use of addons and modifying NBT data, but cannot be obtained through the /give command.


== Usage ==
In the early versions of China Edition, mod_armor was used for custom equipment and an item frame that allows customization and modification of equipment settings (only for the equipment slot area).
When worn, mod_armor provided 2 () armor points. The item was not worn on the head, but on the feet. It had 0 armor toughness by default.
The item used the old golden_helmet texture and did not have an accurate name (the name was "item.mod_armor.name").
