A sandstone slab  is a decorative slab variant of sandstone that generates in desert structures and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Sandstone slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Sandstone slabs generate as part of desert pyramids, desert villages, and desert wells.

=== Note blocks ===
Sandstone slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
