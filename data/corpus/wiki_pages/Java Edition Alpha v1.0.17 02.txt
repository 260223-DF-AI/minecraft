Alpha v1.0.17_02 is a version of Java Edition Alpha released on August 20, 2010 to attempt to fix a bug. This version is available in the launcher.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Fence

Can now be crafted with 6 sticks.


=== Items ===
Milk bucket

Milk buckets can no longer be stacked; they previously stacked up to 64.
