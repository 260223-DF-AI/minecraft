A potion of Water Breathing is a potion that provides Water Breathing when used.

=== Brewing ===


=== Mob loot ===
A witch has an 8.5% chance to drop a potion of Water Breathing if it dies while drinking the potion.


=== Generated loot ===

In Bedrock Edition, the cauldron in a swamp hut has a 10% chance to contain a potion of Water Breathing.
