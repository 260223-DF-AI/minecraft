Steak is a food item obtained by cooking raw beef. Steak and cooked porkchops restore the most hunger out of all stackable foods.

=== Cooking ===
Raw beef can be cooked in a furnace, smoker, or campfire. Each steak removed from a furnace output slot gives 0.35 experience (22.4 experience per stack).


=== Villager gifts ===
In Java Edition, butcher villagers may give players steak if they have the Hero of the Village status effect.


== Usage ==


=== Food ===
To eat steak, press and hold use while it is selected in the hotbar. Eating one restores 8 () hunger and 12.8 hunger saturation.


=== Wolves ===
Steak can be used to breed and heal tamed wolves, lead them around, and make baby tamed wolves grow up faster by 10% of the remaining time.
