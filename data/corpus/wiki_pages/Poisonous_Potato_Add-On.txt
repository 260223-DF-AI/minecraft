Poisonous Potato Add-On is an official add-on developed by Jigarbov Productions released alongside Java Edition 24w14potato as part of the Poisonous Potato April Fools' Day event in 2024.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
 Poisonous Potato Bundle
Same capacity as a regular bundle.
There is no opened poisonous potato bundle texture.
 Baked Poisonous Potato
Obtained by cooking poisonous potato in a furnace.
Not compatible with smoker.
Can be eaten to restore 5 () hunger and gives 3 to 4 random positive effects. The positive effects are always level 5 and last for 30 seconds.
 Explosive Poisonous Potato
If used, it is thrown and generates a colossal explosion.
Crafted from 1 poisonous potato, 4 gunpowder and 4 redstone dust.
 Poisonous Potato Nubs
Crafted from a poisonous potato.
When used, it is thrown and infects entities with poison.
 Explosive Poisonous Potato Nubs
Crafted from 7 poisonous potato nubs, 1 redstone dust, and 1 gunpowder.
When used, it is thrown and creates a small explosion.
  Sprouted Poisonous Potato Nubs
 Sprouted Explosive Poisonous Potato Nubs
Poisonous Potato Armor
 Poisonous Potato Helmet
 Poisonous Potato Chestplate
 Poisonous Potato Leggings
 Poisonous Potato Boots
Each piece grants double the armor points of diamond/netherite armor, with the helmet and boots granting 6 () points each, the chestplate 16 (  8), and the leggings 12 (  6).[more information needed]
When the full set is equipped, every mob within a radius[more information needed] of the player are inflicted with a high level of Fatal Poison[more information needed]. Can be enchanted.
 Poisonous Potato Guide
Guides the player on how to smelt baked poisonous potatoes and craft poisonous potato blocks, poisonous potato powered wooden swords, poisonous potato powered armor, and poisonous potato powered minecarts.
Gives the credits for the add-on.
 Poisonous Potato Powered Sword
Has wooden, stone, iron, golden, diamond, and netherite variants.
Has twice the attack damage from normal swords, and can be enchanted.
Inflicts a high level of Fatal Poison[more information needed]
 Poisonous Potato Stew
Restores hunger and adds status effects.[more information needed]
 Wrench
Part of all packs by Jigarbov.[more information needed]
Adds +1 attack damage.

==== Mobs ====
 Mutato Mutato
A neutral mob, which can be both crafted or spawned by breaking potato crops with 2% chance.
Its spawn egg is crafted from 1 poisonous potato, 1 potion of Regeneration and 7 vines.
Mutato have 60HP  30 health points and 7HP damage points + Poison effect on 15 seconds
Player can tame the mutato with a  potato or poisonous potato.
Has a chance of dropping a Jumbo Poisonous Potato.
Passive if the player is wearing poisonous potato armor (more information needed)
Tamed ones can sit down or stand up.
Can change haircut with shears.
 Tuber Trekkers Tuber Trekkers
Crafted from 2 poisonous potatoes and 4 vines.
Ridable.
Has very high speed and jump height.
Drops its spawn egg upon death.
 Mashacre Mashacre
Hostile and bigger version of mutato.
Has 100HP  50 health points and 20HP  10 damage points.
Attacks mutatos.
Has a chance of dropping a Jumbo Red Poisonous Potato.
