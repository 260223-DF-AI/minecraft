The Minecraft x LUSH collection is a merchandise collection and collaboration between Mojang Studios and British cosmetics retailer LUSH. The collection was announced on August 1, 2024, released online on August 6, 2024, and began rolling out to LUSH stores on August 12, 2024. Twelve products were released as part of the collection.
