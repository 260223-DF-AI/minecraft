Jonatan 'eldrone' Poljo is a former 3D artist at Mojang, who previously assisted with the development of 0x10c until its indefinite shelving. Prior to joining Mojang on September 28, 2012, Poljo worked on a number of major titles for AAA developers, including Capcom Entertainment, Inc.
In addition to his game development, Poljo was already recognized by the Minecraft community for his custom texture pack, the Eldpack, which won third place in an official texture pack competition by Mojang.
He announced on December 15, 2016 that he would be leaving the company at the end of the year, and handed in his badge on December 29. After several years of freelancing, Poljo was hired as the art director of Nightdive Studios, restoring & enhancing older games.
