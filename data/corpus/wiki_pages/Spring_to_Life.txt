Spring to Life is a game drop that released on March 25, 2025, as Java Edition 1.21.5 and Bedrock Edition 1.21.70. It focuses on nature related features such as warm and cold variants to various passive farm animals, new foliage blocks for different biomes, and ambience features such as sounds and falling leaf particles.
During development, the update was referred to as Spring Drop 2025 and Drop 1 2025 as the name of the Bedrock Edition experiment. The final name and release date of the update were revealed during Minecraft LIVE  March 2025, on March 22, 2025.
Experimental features for the update were been released prior to the finalized release as part of Bedrock Edition 1.21.60.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Bush

Generates in the following biomes:
Plains
Windswept Hills
Windswept Gravelly Hills
Windswept Forest
River
Frozen River
Forest
Birch Forest
Old Growth Birch Forest
 Cactus flower

A new type of flower which can grow on cacti.
Can only be placed on full blocks.
Can be crafted into pink dye.
 Short and Tall dry grass 

Generates in the desert and all types of badlands.
Can be used as fuel, smelting 0.5 items per dry grass.
 Firefly bush

Generates in swamps and near rivers.
 Produces firefly particles when at a light level of 13 or lower, up to 5 blocks away.
Make buzzing sounds at night.
 Leaf litter

A new type of decorative block which can be found in the forests, dark forests and wooded badlands.
Four can be placed in one block.
Can be used as fuel, smelting 0.5 items per leaf litter.
Can be smelted from any type of leaf block.
 Wildflowers

A new type of flower which grow in birch forests, old growth birch forests and meadows.
Four can be placed in one block.
Can be crafted into yellow dye.

 New Explorer maps

Jungle Explorer Map

=== Mobs ===
There are now cold and warm variants of pigs, cows, and chickens.

The variant of naturally spawned mobs is determined by the biome they spawn in.
When bred by a player, a baby of these mobs will not be a variant chosen by the current biome, but instead randomly be selected from one of its parent's variants.
The pre-existing variants are now called temperate.
The temperate mobs spawn by default wherever the cold and warm variants do not.
Pig

 Cold pig: A variant that spawns in cold biomes.
 Warm pig: A variant that spawns in warm biomes.
Cow

 Cold cow: A variant that spawns in cold biomes.
 Warm cow: A variant that spawns in warm biomes.
Chicken

 Cold chicken: A variant that spawns in cold biomes.
 Warm chicken: A variant that spawns in warm biomes.


=== World generation ===
Fallen tree for Java Edition

A new decorative variant of trees.
Fallen trees come in five different variants:
 Oak
 Birch
 Tall Birch
 Spruce
 Jungle
Sometimes generate as tree stumps when there is no space to generate a tree trunk.
Fallen trees are sometimes decorated with mushroom or vines.
Fallen trees can be found in all biomes where their standing tree variants grow, except the following:
Meadow
Bamboo Jungle
River
Grove
Flower Forest
Has fallen birch trees but not fallen oak trees.

Sand, Red sand, Terracotta and Stained terracotta
Played when surrounded by any one of them on at least 3 sides 8 blocks away.

Wolf

Now has sound variants:
Big
Cute
Puglin
Angry
Grumpy
Sad
The already existing wolf sounds are now part of the Classic sound variant.

== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
 Creaking heart

Now has a unique dormant texture.
Lodestone

Can now be found in ruined portals.
The crafting recipe was altered to now require an iron ingot instead of a netherite ingot.

Leaves

 All leaf blocks now have a chance of spawning falling leaf particles.


=== Items ===
Bundle

Now generates in village chest loot.
 Spawn eggs

Overhauled all spawn egg textures to be a caricature of the mob in question.


=== Mobs ===
Cartographer

Cartographers now offer the following items:
Sell 7 new explorer maps pointing to each village type as well as jungle temples and witch huts.
Different biome cartographers will have different selections of these maps, available at Apprentice level.
Cartographers from different village types sell a different range of maps and colored banners.
Prices and quantities for some trades have been adjusted.
Trial chamber and ocean monument maps remain available at Journeyman level.
Colored banners are sold at Expert level, with the range varying by biome.
A woodland mansion map is guaranteed at Master level, along with the globe banner pattern.
Camel

Now spawns everywhere in deserts, not just in desert villages.
  Cow and Mooshroom

Changed texture and extend the snout by one pixel.
 Sheep

Changed the rules for sheep coloring to be based on which biome they spawn in:
Temperate biomes (all non-warm, non-cold biomes):
Common:  White
Uncommon:  Black, Gray, Light Gray, and Brown
Rare:  Pink
Cold biomes:
Common:  Black
Uncommon:  Gray, Light Gray, White, and Brown
Rare:  Pink
Warm biomes:
Common:  Brown
Uncommon:  Gray, Light Gray, White, and Black
Rare:  Pink
Sheep colors for temperate biomes is unchanged from current behavior.
In a parity update with Bedrock Edition, sheep in Java Edition now display the correct wool color on their undercoat, and eat ferns.
Now eat dry grass.

Wandering trader

Improved prices, more trade options, and larger stock amounts for many items.
New items the wandering trader can sell:
Logs (Oak, Spruce, Birch, Jungle, Acacia, Dark Oak, Cherry, Pale Oak)
Enchanted Iron Pickaxe
Potion of Invisibility
The wandering trader now offers to buy two items from the following list:
Water Bottle
Water Bucket
Milk Bucket
Fermented Spider Eye
Baked Potato
Hay Bale
