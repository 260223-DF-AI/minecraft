Reinforced deepslate is a block that generates exclusively in ancient cities. It has high hardness, is immune to explosions, and cannot be pushed by pistons or obtained in Survival mode.


== Obtaining ==
Reinforced deepslate is not obtainable in Survival, even with Silk Touch, and is only available through the Creative inventory, pick block, or commands.


=== Breaking ===
Reinforced deepslate has no tool associated with it, meaning it is mined at the exact same speed regardless of the tool used.
Reinforced deepslate drops nothing when destroyed. It also cannot be destroyed by the ender dragon or wither.


=== Natural generation ===
56 blocks of reinforced deepslate are found in the central part of each ancient city, creating a frame-like structure.


== Usage ==
Reinforced deepslate has no uses besides decoration. It is also unique in that it can not be destroyed by withers.


=== Piston interactivity ===
Reinforced deepslate cannot be pushed by pistons or pulled by sticky pistons.

== Trivia ==

In China Edition, a variant of reinforced deepslate exists containing a hole in its texture.
According to Brandon Pearce, reinforced deepslate's purpose is to add mystery.
Despite being unobtainable in-game, in Craft Your Own Adventure: Wild Tales there is a path where the player is able to mine and obtain it.
Reinforced deepslate is the hardest block in Minecraft, with 55 hardness.
