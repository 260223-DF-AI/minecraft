Beta 1.21.20.24 (ChromeOS, Android) or Preview 1.21.20.24 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox, PlayStation) is the ninth and final beta/Preview version for Bedrock Edition 1.21.20, released on July 25, 2024, which fixes bugs.
