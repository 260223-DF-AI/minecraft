Swamp may refer to:


== Minecraft ==
Swamp – a wetland biome characterized by shallow pools, gray-green water, and patches of lily pads, dead bushes, firefly bushes, and blue orchids.
Mangrove Swamp – a variant of the swamp biome, mangrove swamps are filled with mud and dense mangrove trees, as well as green-blue water. They also include many features from the swamp biome.
Swamp Hut – also known as witch huts, these small structures can spawn in the swamp biome and have the ability to spawn witches and black cats.
Swamp Oak a variant of the oak tree that spawns in swamps.
Mangrove a tree that spawns in mangrove swamps.
Swamp Hills – a hilly variant of the swamp biome that was removed in the Caves & Cliffs update.
