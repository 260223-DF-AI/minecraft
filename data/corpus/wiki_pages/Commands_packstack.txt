Display resource packs and behavior packs data in the chat bar.


== Syntax ==
/packstack <stackType: stackType> [verbose: verbore] [exclude-vanilla: exclude-vanilla]
Display resource packs and behavior packs data in the chat bar.


== Arguments ==
stackType: stackType: Type of pack. client is resources pack, server is behavior pack.
verbose: verbore: Show details.
exclude-vanilla: exclude-vanilla: Exclude the vanilla package.
