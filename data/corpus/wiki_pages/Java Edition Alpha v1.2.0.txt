Alpha v1.2.0, also known as the Halloween Update, is a major update to Java Edition. It was released with server 0.2.2 on October 30, 2010, at 20:53 UTC. It primarily added the Nether along with new Nether-exclusive mobs and blocks. It also added fishing, a new terrain generator, and other changes and fixes.
Alpha v1.2.0 was re-uploaded immediately after release to prevent the game still saying "do not distribute" in the main menu.
This version is listed in the launcher, but instead downloads Alpha v1.2.0_02.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Netherrack

Generates in the Nether.
Sustains fire indefinitely.
 Soul sand

Generates in the Nether.
Decreases the player's walking speed.
 Glowstone

Generates in the Nether.
Emits a light level of 15.
Drops 1 glowstone dust per block.
Crafted with 9 glowstone dust.

 Pumpkin

New plant found in patches in the overworld.
Can be worn by the player.
 Jack 'o' lantern

Emits a light level of 15.
Can be worn by the player.
Crafted with 1 pumpkin and 1 torch.

 Nether portal

New block used to transport players to and from the Nether.
Emits a light level of 11.


=== Items ===
 Clock

New item that tells the time.
Crafted with 1 redstone dust and 4 gold ingots.

 Glowstone dust

Obtained by mining glowstone, one piece per block.
 Raw fish

Obtained by fishing.
New food item, which restores 2HP.
 Cooked fish

New food item, which restores 5HP.
Obtained by cooking raw fish in a furnace.


=== Mobs ===
 Ghast

Large, white mob that floats around in the Nether.
Shoots fireballs at the player.
The fireballs use the snowball texture when shot.
Has 10HP.
Drops 0-2 sulphur.

 Zombie pigmen

Neutral mob found in the Nether.
When one zombie pigman is attacked, all nearby pigmen retaliate and attack the player, becoming permanently hostile.
Holds a golden sword.
Has 10HP.
Drops 0-2 cooked porkchops.


=== Non-mob entities ===
 Ghast fireball

It is a projectile from a ghast.
It has a straight trajectory unaffected by gravity and a theoretically infinite range.
It creates an explosion upon impact with an entity or block, setting fire to nearby blocks.
It uses the same snowball texture.


=== World generation ===

New terrain generator, featuring 12 new biomes:
Desert

A warm biome consisting of sand and cacti. No trees generate.
Forest

A biome with many trees.
Hell

A biome found in the Nether. Consists of netherrack and soul sand.
To get there, you'll have to build an obsidian portal frame and set it on fire in the center .
One block passed in this dimension is equal to eight blocks passed in the Overworld.
Water cannot be placed here.
Ice Desert

Unused biome. Same as the desert, except the ground is covered in snow and no cacti generate.
Plains

Flat, open biome with only the occasional tree.
Rainforest

Very wet forested biome, with a 1 in 3 chance of trees being big, rather than 1 in 10 in all other biomes.
Savanna

Mostly flat biome with occasional trees. Only difference from the plains biome is the grass color.
Seasonal forest

Very similar to the forest, with the only difference being a lower-density canopy.
Shrubland

Very similar to plains, with the only difference being the grass color.
Swampland

Indentical to Shrubland, with only a different grass color and the occasional patch of snow.
Taiga

Snowy forested biome.
Tundra

A cold biome with snow, ice, and very few trees.
