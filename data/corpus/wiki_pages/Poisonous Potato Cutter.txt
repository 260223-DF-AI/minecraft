A poisonous potato cutter is a joke block added in 24w14potato. It functions like a stonecutter, except it works only on poisonous potatoes, cutting them into poisonous potato slices or poisonous potato sticks.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
A poisonous potato cutter can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, it drops nothing.


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==


=== Cutting ===
The poisonous potato cutter can be used to craft poisonous potatoes into poisonous potato slices and poisonous potato sticks.
