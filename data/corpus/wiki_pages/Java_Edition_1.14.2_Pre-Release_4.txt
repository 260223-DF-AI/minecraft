1.14.2 Pre-Release 4 is the fourth and final pre-release for Java Edition 1.14.2, released on May 24, 2019, which fixes a lighting issue and a bug with entity clipping.
1.14.2 Pre-Release 4 was reuploaded on May 27, 2019, to remove the splash text "Woo, minecraftforum!", because the forum announced they would be shutting down, although this announcement was later retracted.


== Changes ==


=== Gameplay ===
Lighting

All light is now re-calculated the first time a world saved in a previous version is opened.


=== General ===
Splashes

Re-upload: Removed "Woo, minecraftforum!" as the forum announced it would soon be shutting down.


== Fixes ==
1 issue fixed
From 1.14

MC-147715  Entities clip through blocks after traveling through an end gateway.
