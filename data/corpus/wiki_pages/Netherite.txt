Netherite is a metal alloy from the Nether, used primarily to upgrade diamond gear. Netherite items are more powerful and durable than diamond, and in dropped item form are resistant to fire or lava. Blocks of netherite are also unbreakable by the strongest explosions from normal gameplay, with the exception of blue wither skulls.


== Lore ==
According to lore, pure netherite ore no longer exists naturally because piglins have mined it all out, leaving behind only ancient debris.


== Blocks and items ==
Many blocks and items make netherite, are made with netherite, are used to upgrade gear to netherite, or are directly stated to be netherite. They are:

Ancient Debris - the blast-resistant ore form rarely found at low altitudes in the Nether, or as loot in bastion remnants (which may also contain netherite scraps and ingots).
Block of Netherite - the mineral block form of netherite ingots.
Netherite Scrap - an item obtained by smelting ancient debris, used to craft netherite ingots.
Netherite Ingot - used for upgrading diamond gear to netherite.
Netherite Upgrade - a smithing template, found as loot in bastion remnants, used for upgrading diamond gear to netherite.


== Gear and tools ==
