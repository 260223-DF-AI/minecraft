The E3 2014 Build was the first known developmental version of PlayStation 4 Edition, and also the first known pre-1.0 version. It was never publicly released but was playable at the E3 2014 event and given to many individuals for private testing and showcasing.


== Features ==
Besides it being a developmental build, all the features were identical from the original release.
