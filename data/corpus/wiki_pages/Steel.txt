Steel is a metal that has been referenced multiple times early on in the Minecraft's development. Notch had stated that it could be made using coal and iron ore, and that it could be used for armor and weapons more powerful than iron. In-game, flint and steel is crafted using iron ingots, considering the lack of steel and the need of a “fire striker”, despite the name of the former. Steel can refer to the following topics.
