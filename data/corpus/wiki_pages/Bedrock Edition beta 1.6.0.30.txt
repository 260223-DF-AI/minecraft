Beta 1.6.0.30 is the fifth and final beta version for Bedrock Edition 1.6.0, released on August 8, 2018, which fixes bugs.


== Fixes ==
Fixed mobs and players missing several limbs on some Android devices in previous 1.6 beta versions. (MCPE-34385)
Fixed several crashes that occurred during gameplay.
Fixed a crash that occurred when joining a game and downloading Greek Mythology textures.
Quickly moving a stack of items in the inventory with a controller no longer leaves items behind. (MCPE-34694)
