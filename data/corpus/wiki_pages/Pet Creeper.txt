The pet creeper is a pet variant of the creeper introduced in 25w14craftmine.


== Spawning ==
A pet creeper spawns next to the player on mine start when the "Pet Creeper" Player Unlock is active.


== Drops ==
Pet creeper drops nothing when it's killed.


== Behavior ==
The pet creeper is similar to the regular creeper, but there are still some differences:

The player's melee attack can't damage the pet creeper.
The pet creeper doesn't chase players in Survival mode.
The pet creeper doesn't explode when it is near a target.
The pet creeper doesn't escape from cats, ocelots, or pet cats.
The pet creeper doesn't become a charged creeper when it is struck by lightning.
