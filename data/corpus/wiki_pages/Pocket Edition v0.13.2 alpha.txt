v0.13.2 alpha is a minor update to Pocket Edition and the Windows 10 Edition Beta released on February 3–4, 2016, which fixes bugs in v0.13.1 alpha and adds a new skin pack. This is the first version released in 2016.
An unstable build of v0.13.2 alpha was accidentally uploaded to the Google Play Store on January 29, 2016. This build was known to cause numerous crashes.
This version got reuploaded.


== Additions ==


=== Skin packs ===
Version exclusive: Journey to the West Skin Pack.


== Fixes ==
Zombie's and skeleton's armor renders correctly again.
Zombie pigmen's armor still does not, however.
Zombie pigmen's hat layer now renders correctly.
Ghasts now open their mouths when attacking.
Wolves' collars now show their correct colors.
Arm is now animated with items correctly.
Fixed lag in the Nether.
