The Halloween Texture Pack is a texture pack that overhauls the game with Halloween-themed textures. It was first available for free on Xbox 360 Edition from October 25, 2013 to November 3, 2013; it was then re-released for a limited time on Xbox 360 Edition and Xbox One Edition starting from October 29, 2014, on PlayStation 3 Edition in North America starting from October 31, 2014, and on PlayStation 3 Edition, PlayStation 4 Edition, and PlayStation Vita Edition worldwide starting from November 20, 2014.
This texture pack was later used as the basis for the Halloween Mash-Up Pack.
