Tiny Vu is a miniaturized doppelganger of Vu Bui, who typically appeared during Community Votes. Although similar in personality and appearance to "Giant Vu," Tiny Vu was shown consistently to be a separate entity with many secrets.


== Appearance ==
Tiny Vu closely resembles Vu Bui's typical persona, wearing a brimmed hat and gray shirt, just with altered proportions. His head is enormous, while his legs are small stumps and arms replaced by "spherical" nubs that can serve as hands.


== Personality ==

Tiny Vu closely follows Vu Bui's typical persona, being very energetic and providing exposition. However, he is noticeably dimmer, apparently unaware of what a calendar is and possessing infrequent memory lapses.


== Abilities ==
When first introduced during MINECON Live 2019, Tiny Vu boasts that he can be scalable to any size, due to being "just a bunch of pixels." From there he is also shown appearing in several different biomes instantaneously, implying he has some sort of teleportation ability During a video promoting the 2023 Mob Vote, Tiny Vu is electrocuted by a wire, so powerful that it causes him to be sent flying backwards. He possesses no noticeable injuries afterwards, showing a high tolerance to pain. During a short preceding Minecraft LIVE  March 2025, he is also shown to possess enhanced reflexes, being able to peddle a bike using his entire body, jumping between both peddles without any issue.


== Biography ==

First appearing during MINECON Live 2019, Vu Bui introduced Tiny Vu to demonstrate the Biome Vote, however the two would quickly get into a debate over their relative size.
