Happy Ghast Music Video (stylized in all caps), also called the Happy Ghast Song, is a music video about the happy ghast played during Minecraft LIVE – September 2025. The number revolves around Efe raising a happy ghast, going on several one to two second long misadventures in the process.


== Summary ==
Efe is mining copper ore, only for a happy ghast to appear. In a library Efe hugs the ghast, but gets squished up against bookshelves. The ghast creeps behind a cherry tree, and then unknowingly begins hitting Efe with tentacles. The two begin flying up into the sky, over a badlands biome, and then into a village where Efe steals a bed for no apparent reason. Two warm animals look up at the sky, while two horses race by it, and then Efe gets mobbed by a clutter of spiders in a mineshaft.
In a birch forest, Efe crafts a harness and equips it on the ghast, allowing their friends Kai, Noor, and Alex to fly with them. From there Steve and Alex get their own ghasts, while Efe begins carrying several mobs in boats "for a party." On the ground a villager flees from the boat, and ends up being caught in one against their will. The happy ghast flies with bees, and then serves as an umbrella for Efe, before Efe gets knocked off a mountain by a screaming goat. An armadillo is startled by the suddenly appearing ghast, and then Zuri leaps into water for an unstated reason. From there Efe fights against illagers in a burning forest, again without an apparent context.
The scene cuts to Efe running with a dried ghast from a regular ghast and piglins, before returning to the Overworld, where Efe is ready to kill several cow. Makena tethers a bee to the happy ghast, alongside several other mobs, before they suddenly disappear when Efe flies through an arch into the sky. Suddenly, all nine heroes appear, on their own happy ghasts. Using crossbows, they aim up towards the clouds, and set off a show of fireworks. Efe and the happy ghast fly through, setting off more fireworks, and then return to the arch to fly through it again.

== Trivia ==
In a Twitter post, it is claimed that the song may cause people to hum it for hours.
There is a reference in the song to the game drop that brought the Happy Ghast, Chase the Skies.
This is the fourth musical-based video, after The Super Duper Minecraft Musical, Caves & Cliffs: The Musical, and A Warden's Song.
Prior to the video's release, Marc Watson jokingly gave lyrics to a similar song, also about ghasts.


=== Errors ===
During a shot with three happy ghasts, at 0:35, two separate Alexes can be seen. One on Efe's ghast, the other on their own, opposite to Steve's.
