Daniel "Kappische" Kaplan was Mojang's business developer from October 2010 to July 2017.


== Biography ==
Kaplan was born and raised in Skovde, Sweden. He founded ludosity.com.  After leaving Mojang, he worked at Coffee Stain Studios.


== Work ==
Games:

Garden Gnome Carnage - Tetherball Tower Defense
Mama & Son: Clean House - Two player shooter
Bob Came in Pieces - Puzzle Game
Loot, Steal 'n Destroy - 2-4 player party game
On-Scene Commander - Simulator
Lean Bicycle Factory Demonstration Game - Manage a Bicycle factory
