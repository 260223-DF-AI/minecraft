v0.10.0 alpha is a major update to Minecraft: Pocket Edition that was released on November 18, 2014. It adds several new features such as the day/night cycle in Creative, 64-bit hardware support, new fence types, and other changes and fixes.
Compared to previous updates, 0.10 was more known as containing more changes than new features, generally due to bringing the game up-to-date with newer devices and running smoother.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Day/night cycle added in creative mode.
Adaptation for 64-bit hardware/software, making the game faster for 64-bit users.
Support for the resolutions of the iPhone 6.
