Console Edition may refer to:

Bedrock Edition, the edition of Minecraft that supports crossplay on Xbox, Nintendo Switch, and PlayStation.
Legacy Console Edition, the discontinued edition of Minecraft developed by 4J Studios for the following consoles:
 Xbox 360 Edition
 Xbox One Edition (Replaced with Bedrock Edition)
 PlayStation 3 Edition
 PlayStation 4 Edition (Replaced with Bedrock Edition)
 PlayStation Vita Edition
 Wii U Edition
 Nintendo Switch Edition (Replaced with Bedrock Edition)
 New Nintendo 3DS Edition, the discontinued edition of Minecraft for the New Nintendo 3DS/2DS systems.
