The cursor is a joke block introduced in Java Edition 20w14∞ with an animated texture that changes its color every second, toggling between lime and black concrete.


== Obtaining ==
Cursors are available in the Creative inventory, and naturally generate in the "terminal" dimension.


=== Breaking ===
Cursor can be mined using a pickaxe, in which case it drops itself. If mined without a pickaxe, it drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
The block naturally generates in the "terminal" dimension as the blinking "_" next to a "C:\" made of lime concrete. It appears to be cycling between black and lime concrete in appearance, which is what the rest of the "terminal" dimension is made of.
