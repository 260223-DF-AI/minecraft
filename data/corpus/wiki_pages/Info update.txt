info_update and info_update2 are debugging or placeholder blocks exclusive to Bedrock Edition. They previously generated in place of blocks with invalid IDs.


== Obtaining ==
These blocks cannot be obtained via /give or /setblock, and as such can only be obtained through inventory editors or add-ons.


=== Breaking ===
No tools can accelerate the breaking process of info_update and info_update2.

info_update and info_update2 drop themselves when they're destroyed.
