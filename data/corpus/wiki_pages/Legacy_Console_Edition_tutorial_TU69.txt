The TU69 tutorial world is the eighth and final distinct tutorial for the Legacy Console Edition of the game, released on all platforms that received the Update Aquatic.


== Areas ==


=== Spawn ===

When creating a new tutorial world, the player will always spawn in this enclosed area. They are given tutorial pop-ups that can be skipped or progressed through. There is a partially built house, made of oak planks. At the back, there is a tunnel with stairs that appears to be caved in. Towards the front, there is a broken bridge that, when either rebuilt or traversed around, leads to a room with a bridge suspended over water, with creeper faces made of dark prismarine on either side. The bridge leads to a stairway that brings the player out into the main tutorial area, greeted by a giant stone Minecraft logo above head.


=== Tutorial areas ===


==== Farming (crops) ====
Just past the big Minecraft logo, there is a farm that contains every crop. Inside this fenced farm, there is a chest that contains the following:

Wheat Seeds 5
Melon Seeds 5
Pumpkin Seeds 5
Beetroot Seeds 5
Carrot 10
Potato 10
Stone Hoe
Bone Meal 10
The farm area to the right of the chest has some untilled grass, and farmland with nothing planted on it, for use in the tutorial.


==== Trading ====
Directly after the farm there are two more fenced areas, with the one on the right being for trading. There are 3 houses that somewhat resemble the village houses of the time. Inside the biggest house there are two villagers behind a counter, and there are two more who can roam freely within the fenced area. There is a butcher, an armorer, a cleric, and a librarian. There is a chest containing 32 paper and 11 emeralds. 


==== Repairing ====
Across from the trading pen is another fenced area containing a blacksmith-style building and an anvil. There are two villagers, a weapon smith and a tool smith. There is a chest containing the following:

Wooden Pickaxe with low durability
Golden Shovel with even lower durability
Iron Shovel with almost full durability
Iron Ingot
Gold Ingot
Stone Sword
Enchanted Book with Fire Aspect II
Bottle o' Enchanting 10
Name Tag 5
Lava Bucket


==== Farming (animals) ====
To the left of the farming and repairing areas is a stable containing pens of chickens, cows, sheep, pigs, rabbits, wolves, and ocelots. In the middle there is a chest containing the following:

Wheat 10
Seeds 10
Carrot 10
Raw Beef 5
Raw Cod 10
Bone 10
These items are to be used to breed and tame the penned animals.


== Music discs ==

Most of the Legacy Console Edition tutorial worlds included music discs hidden throughout the world, and the TU69 world is one of them. There are 12 discs total; with those discs being 11, 13, blocks, cat, chirp, far, mall, mellohi, stal, strad, wait, and ward.
