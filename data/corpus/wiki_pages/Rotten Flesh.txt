Rotten flesh is a food item that can be eaten by players, but may inflict Hunger. It can also be safely fed to tamed wolves or sold to cleric villagers.

=== Fishing ===
Rotten flesh can be obtained as a junk item while fishing. See fishing for more details.


=== Cat gifts ===

Tamed cats have a 70% chance of giving the player a gift when they wake up from a bed, and the gift has a 16.13% chance to be a rotten flesh.


== Usage ==


=== Food ===

To eat rotten flesh, press and hold use while rotten flesh is selected in the hotbar.
Eating rotten flesh restores 4 () hunger and 0.8 saturation, but has an 80% chance of inflicting Hunger (effect) for 30 seconds. Note that the duration does not accumulate when eating multiple pieces. However, if more is consumed and the Hunger status effect is inflicted, the duration resets to 30 seconds.


=== Wolves ===
Rotten flesh can be used to breed and heal tamed wolves, lead them around, and make baby tamed wolves grow up faster by 10% of the remaining time.
Wolves are immune to the Hunger effect.
