1.14.1 Pre-Release 2 is the second and final pre-release for Java Edition 1.14.1, released on May 7, 2019, which fixes several bugs.


== Changes ==
Campfires

Can now be lit by flaming arrows.


== Fixes ==
7 issues fixed
From 1.14

MC-150218  Spawning algorithm stops at chunk borders.
MC-150785  Villagers and iron golems count towards the passive mob cap.
MC-150847  Can load crossbow with air.
From the previous development version

MC-151057  Redstone components sometimes do not get updated properly.
MC-151068  Crash when breaking a work station: Ticking entity.
MC-151097  Villagers cannot use doors.
MC-151126  Villagers will not detect beds when doors are in the way.
