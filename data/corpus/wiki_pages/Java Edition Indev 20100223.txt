A version of Minecraft Indev was released on February 23, 2010. This was the final version of the Indev phase of development.


== Additions ==


=== Entities ===
 Paintings

New cosmetic entity.
Added 19 new paintings by the artist Kristoffer Zetterstrand:

Crafted with planks and light gray cloth.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Cobblestone

Can now be smelted into stone.
Log

Now drops itself instead of planks when broken.
Planks

Now crafted with log.

Water

Improved performance.


=== Items ===
Armor

Can no longer be stacked.
Bread

Now crafted with 3 wheat instead of 6.


=== Mobs ===
Sheep

Sheep now only drop light gray cloth when punched.
