1.20.32 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on October 5, 2023, which fixes bugs.


== Fixes ==
4 issues fixed
From the 1.20.x versions

MCPE-174480  Special characters all delete text behind them when typed after [on iOS and iPadOS]
MCPE-175072  Unable to type any text after writing on a sign and pressing Esc
MCPE-175119  Buttons and non-full blocks do not prevent spiders from spawning
Others

Fixed purchased content not transferring to a Microsoft account when logging in for the first time.
