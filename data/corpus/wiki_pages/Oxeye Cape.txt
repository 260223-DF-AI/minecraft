The Oxeye cape is a personal cape based on Oxeye Game Studio, a game development company co-founded by Jens Bergensten and authors of games such as Cobalt. Its existence was revealed when it was randomly given to a player.


== Appearance ==
The cape's design mimics the logo of the aforementioned studio: a black background with a circular white center.


== Distribution ==
This cape was randomly given to the player iSxckk, presumably by accident, then removed the next day.
