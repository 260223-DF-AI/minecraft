Within the Nether, a replica of Woodsword Middle School was constructed.


== Location ==
The structure is located in what appears to be a Nether wastes biome, close enough to the roof for it to be visible, evoking the shape of storm clouds. It's settled on top of either a hill or plateau, surrounded by several unnaturally high fires, and a river of lava on the ground below, in the shape of an arch.


== Architecture ==
The replica stays very close to the appearance of the real Woodsword school, with many of the same details. The building is the same height, at a minimum of two floors with a clocktower, with the same number of windows, carvings, and flagpole. The most glaring difference is that it is all made out of blocks, albeit none recognizable. The sign on the top once again merely reads School, with the griffins rendered illegible, resembling the shape of a lily of the valley.
