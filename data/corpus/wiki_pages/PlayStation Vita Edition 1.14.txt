1.14  is a version of PlayStation Vita Edition released on February 11, 2015.  
[edit]


== Changes ==
Increased minecart limit.
Crafting a map now produces an empty map.


== Fixes ==
Fixes for redstone.
Stopped tamed horses despawning when they are in a fenced-off area.
Fixed a crash with a note block.
Fixed an issue with village generation.
Fixed an issue with witch huts allowing other mobs to spawn in them after a reload.
Fixed an issue where blaze spawners and chests do not appear in the Nether after a Nether reset.
Fixed a crash with minecarts traveling through Nether portals.
Fixed a problem causing clouds to look 2D.
