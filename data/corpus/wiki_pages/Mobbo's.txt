Mobbo's is an apparent restaurant owned and themed after Mobbo; in truth, a front to capture their enemies.


== Layout ==
On the outside, the building resembles a hastily made pastiche of Mobbo's head, made out of wool. In the middle of their mouth is a single oak door, while above their eyes is a neon sign, labeling the establishment "a real restaurant." The roof is a simple oak stairs arrangement, with a pink M made of pink wool on top.
The interior of the building is even more cheaply constructed, being a shapeless mass of cobblestone with no lighting. When the building appears at night, however, several torches appear surrounding it. To the right side of the building is a long railway, and from that angle it can be seen that the roof is more of a trapazoidal sign on the front.
Later, upon entering Mobboland, a second Mobbo's is established to exist. Although visually identical on the outside, it's never made clear if it has any practical differences.


== Biography ==
During a conflict wherein Jonathan Minecraft sought to take over Minecraft, M.A.R.I.L.L.A. and Narrator attempted to investigate, inserting themselves into the game. Upon arriving, they began to search for Mobbo, and assumed after a while that they had already been defeated. To celebrate, M.A.R.I.L.L.A. pointed towards a newly opened restaurant, Mobbo's. Unbeknownst to them, it was a trap made specifically for them.
Approximately three seconds upon entering, the duo were overwhelmed by mobs of skeletons and zombies, unprotected by the darkness inside M.A.R.I.L.L.A. Fearing for their lives, the two began to bicker and mock each other. However, it turned out that Mobbo had actually hired a villager to work inside, and was gullible enough to ask for orders. Thinking fast, Narrator managed to convince them to provide an escape, in the form of a minecart.
