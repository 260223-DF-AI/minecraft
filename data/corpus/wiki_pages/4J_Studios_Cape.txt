The 4J Studios Cape is a cape that was designed for 4J Studios developers on Minecraft: Xbox 360 Edition.


== Distribution ==
This cape was presumably given out to 4J Studios developers on Xbox 360 Edition through the "Title Managed Storage" developer feature. However, it may simply be an unused asset, as no reliable sources have acknowledged the cape outside of the data dump that revealed it.
