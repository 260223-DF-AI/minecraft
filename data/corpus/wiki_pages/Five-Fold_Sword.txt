The Five-Fold Sword (5, Gobai Ken?) is a fan-like sword composed of five blades, created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Five-Fold Sword resembles a wide sword, with five blades protruding in a fanning shape out from its hilt.


== Functionality ==
Nico used the Five-Fold Sword in an attempt to reflect the Curve-Ball Ghast's fireballs back at it, though even the sword's wider range was unable to counterract the fireball's curving.


=== Crafting recipe ===


== History ==
When exploring the Nether, Nico White's party ran into the Curve-Ball Ghast. Nico tried to deflect the ghast's fireballs, but was unable to due to its Curve Ball Mod Power. He tried creating the Five-Fold Sword to use as a bigger area for reflecting the fireballs, but still failed, and discarded the sword.
