Bush may refer to:


== Minecraft ==
Bush  a decorative block.
Firefly Bush  a decorative block that emits firefly particles.
Dead Bush - a brown, "dead-looking" block found in desert, badlands, swamp and mangrove swamp, as well as old growth taiga biomes.
Sweet Berry Bush  a block found in taigas and snowy taigas which can be used to harvest sweet berries when grown.
Rose Bush  a type of flower found in forests, birch forest, and dark forest biome variants.
Leaves  blocks that grow as a part of trees, which have a "bush-like" look and are thus sometimes used to simulate bushes when building.
Azalea  a decorative block that look like a bush and has a flowering and flowerless version.
 Shrubs  two variants of grass, one of which has the same appearance as a dead bush and the other the same appearance as normal grass.[Legacy Console Edition  only]
Green Shrub - a variant of the shrub
Jungle Bush  a bush-like tree in jungles.
 Saplings  blocks that can be grown into a tree; formerly internally known as "bushes."
Shrubland - a removed biome.
