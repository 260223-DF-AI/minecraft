A couple of development versions of Beta 1.4 were developed between March 15 and March 31, 2011 to test the addition of wolves.


== Wolf Test (March 15, 2011) ==


=== Additions ===
 Wolves

Have a texture completely different from the release.
Their appearance resemble a wolf with dark, grey skin.


== Sit Test (March 16, 2011) ==


=== Changes ===
 Wolves

Now have a different texture, resembling a wolf with white skin and grey patches.


== Tame Test (March 18, 2011) ==


=== Changes ===
 Wolves

Changed texture once again, now being close to the release.


=== Trivia ===
Version number still says Beta 1.3.
