Overworld Adventures is a series of narrative chapters on Minecraft.net that are written by and starring Sofia Dankis.


== Installments ==


=== Roundtrip to the Nether ===


==== Summary ====
Following the release of A Minecraft Movie, Sofia Dankis decides to go on a vacation, going back to the Nether after a previous visit. She prepares a portal beside a lake, going in as the sun sets. Aware of the dangers, she decides to act unaware, only to run away from a troop of piglins with her eyes closed. Running into a soul sand valley, she trips on a random block, and realizes it's a dried ghast. As the piglins close in, she decides to grip it "like a security blanket," and runs back to the portal.
Back at her chicken coop, she recognizes the thing as a ghastling, and began to study it. As it began to rehydrate and grow, she noticed its change in demeanor, until it matured into a full happy ghast. Recognizing its angelic qualities, she decided to take it out on a road trip, promising to write letters to chickens. With that, the two ventured out into the Overworld.

=== Causing a Cold Commotion ===


==== Summary ====
As Sofia embarks on her quest, she feels the digestion of suspicious stew, filling her with determination. Riding across the happy ghast, her locator bar identifies someone in the distance, traveling to the snowy taiga biome to investigate. She discovers a grove of poppies, and keeps a mental note to look for crafting tables, hoping to dye her harness red. She begins to write a postcard "before [her] feet even touched the ground."
Discovering an igloo, Sofia runs into none other than Sunny inside, who was about to gather dyes for a project. Chasing them into the woods, Sunny discovers another patch of poppies, Sofia stealing some behind their back. Suddenly, Sofia decides to prioritize earning a Nobel Prize for "Arts & Minecrafts speech," Sunny going out to a frozen lake. Curious, Sofia ends up racing them to it, sliding into Sunny on the ice.
From there they decide to go on a trek together, enjoying the sun. Sofia is inspired to tell Sunny a Japanese word, only to overhear a strange noise. Thinking it was an echo from the ghast, Sofia began clearing her throat to sing a minor key, only to realize it's actually a hungry wolf. Sofia watches as it hunts a fox, before returning to the ghast. Borrowing Sunny's crafting table, she finally dyes her harness, and finishes the postcard as she leaves.

=== Simply the Best ===


==== Summary ====
Likening herself to Superman and calling herself a "Sky Skribe," Sofia continues voyaging across a happy ghast. Finding the prior adventure "harrowing yet thrilling," she thinks back to her fancy new, dyed harness. She likes the idea of matching outfit, before getting distracted by ponies. Before leaving to admire them, she remembers to start another postcard.
Setting out to tame a horse, she quickly locates a field of cornflowers, and then hoof sounds. As she approaches the horses, she discovers a troop of pillagers, stationed at an outpost. Finding it too late to run away, she decides to take the horses head-on, escaping the pillage. Finding the prairie flat and calming, she ends up falling off into a ravine. As she picks herself back up, she exclaims the importance of lawnmowers.
Venturing back to the surface, she decided to check her locator bar, and was completely alone. She becomes horribly distraught by the loneliness, but manages to find a fork in the ravine. One led to a pool of lava, the other a cave, which she decides to spelunk into. Fending off many spiders, she eventually escapes to the surface, and claims a bunch of cornflowers. Deciding to give the ghast a new blue harness, she finishes her postcard before leaving.

=== Dreamscape Escape ===


==== Summary ====
Having been in the skies for several days now, Sofia struggles to find a fourth pitstop. Using the locator bar, she pinpoints the location of Noor and arranges a rendezvous. Apparently, they hadn't seen each other since an expedition to find pottery sherds, Sofia never getting her brush back from them. Landing in a bamboo jungle, Sofia realizes Noor isn't there, and begins investigating.
Marching between bamboo, Sofia discovers pandas playing, and gets offended by a sneeze. Her eye almost gets impaled by a slimeball. From there she discovers a cluster of azalea trees, and decides to take a quick break from searching. However, she realizes there's a camp belonging to Noor nearby, but verifies Noor still isn't quite there. She decides to venture down into a lush cave.
Entering into the underground, Sofia is quite impressed by the mineshaft and beautiful plants. Plucking several glow berries, she discovers many mobs, including glow squids and axolotls hunting tropical fish. Sofia decides to rest to preserve energy, but believes she goes through a surreal dream, where a skeleton drags her out of the cave by the foot and steals a second brush. She finds herself again lying on podzol, surrounded by parrots. Hearing a parrot make a skeleton sound, she comes to the conclusion that the parrots had a psychic connection with her. Looking back at the path, she realizes it's grown back while she was in the cave, stranding her, only for her to just go back to the ghast anyway. Deciding to wait to meet up with Noor, she gets the idea for her next destination, and leaves.

=== The Magic of Mud ===


==== Summary ====
Now several weeks into the trip, Sofia notices the sight of a large fossil in a mangrove swamp, already perfectly excavated. Immediately, Sofia decides to jot down on a new postcard and perform a dance. However, in the process she ends up spinning into a bee nest, angering a swarm of bees. Before she could be saved, she gets pulled into the murky waters, only for her to then get distracted by a frog.
After waiting it out, the two swam to shore, the savior revealed to be Kai. It's explained Kai went on an expedition to this area and uncovered the fossil, having previously discovered froglights. Conversing over a meal, Kai is said to be exhausted from wandering the Overworld on foot, and sheds a tears upon being told of happy ghasts. 
Very soon Sofia decides to part with Kai, but realizes the tadpoles wanted to join her on her quest. After scooping up the tadpoles with a bucket, Kai decides to tag along as well, hoping to do more archeology.

== Trivia ==

Overworld Adventures is the first editoral series to use photos taken with Vibrant Visuals.
Throughout the series, many articles are linked to. Several of these include existing series such as Mob Menagerie, Meet the Mob, and Block of the Week. However, there are also just regular articles promoting recent releases, recent announcements, skits, or personalized builds.
In the first installment, nine pages are linked to, including editorials, skits, and news. The article Take a vibrant test flight is linked to three separate times.
In Dreamscape Escape, pottery sherds are mispelled as shards, a common mistake.
For unknown reasons, the sixth chapter of the series went unpublished, and could only be found through the URL. In spite of this, the seventh chapter makes mention of its events.
