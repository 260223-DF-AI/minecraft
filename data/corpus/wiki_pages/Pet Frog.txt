The pet frog is a pet variant of the frog introduced in 25w14craftmine.


== Spawning ==
A pet frog spawns next to the player on mine start when the "Pet Frog" Player Unlock is active.


== Drops ==
Pet frog drops nothing when it's killed.


== Behavior ==
The behavior of the pet frog is similar to frog in regular versions, but there are still some differences:

The player's melee attack can't damage the pet frog.
The pet frog doesn't seek slime, magma cube and pet slime.
The pet frog doesn't inflate its float sac.
The pet frog can drown.


== Sounds ==
Java Edition:
Frogs use the Friendly Creatures sound category for entity-dependent sound events.


== Issues ==
Issues relating to "Pet Frog" are not maintained on the bug tracker because it is an April Fools' joke, and is therefore not in the newest stable version or snapshot. Issues reported there are closed as "Invalid".
