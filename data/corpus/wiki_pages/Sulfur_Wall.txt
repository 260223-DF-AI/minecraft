A sulfur wall is a decorative wall variant of sulfur used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Sulfur walls drop as an item if mined by any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Crafting ===


=== Stonecutting ===


== Usage ==

Unlike most other walls, mobs can be leashed to sulfur walls similar to fences.[Bedrock Edition  only]
