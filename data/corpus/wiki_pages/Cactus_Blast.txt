The Cactus Blast (, Saboten Hanabi) is an explosive device created by Icco White using the Imagination II Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Cactus Blast's appearance is unknown, as Icco detonated it immediately.


== Functionality ==
The Cactus Blast is an explosive device, designed around launching cacti when triggered. By smashing it into the ground, Icco created a burst of cacti around him, blowing back mobs and allies alike.
