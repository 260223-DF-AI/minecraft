Sandstone stairs are a decorative stairs variant of sandstone that generates in overworld structures and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Sandstone stairs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Sandstone stairs generate as part of desert pyramids, desert villages, and warm ocean ruins.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Sandstone stairs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
