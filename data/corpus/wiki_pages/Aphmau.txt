Jessica Bravura, better known by the pseudonym Aphmau, is an American YouTuber known for her roleplaying and animated content.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
In 2021, Aphmau made an appearance in One Trillion Minecraft Views on YouTube and Counting, a special cinematic celebrating the Minecraft community amassing a trillion views. Although her cameo was silent, she promoted the video on social media, regarding it as an honor.
In 2024, she joined MC Championship to participate in the Ender Cup, under the team the Aqua Axolotls. During the competition, she played alongside jojosolos, Shubble, and LDShadowLady. Later in the year, she was interviewed by Time magazine for the 15th anniversary of the game.
In 2025, she made a cameo in A Minecraft Movie along with LDShadowLady, DanTDM and Mumbo Jumbo, appearing as an attendee of Daryl's auction, while Garrett inspects and ravages it. Prior to being officially announced for the film, she alluded to traveling to New Zealand during filming.


== Trivia ==
Although unofficial to Minecraft, she has partnered with the developers Night Studios to release several skin packs to the Marketplace.
