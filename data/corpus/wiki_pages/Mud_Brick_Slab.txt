A mud brick slab is a decorative slab variant of mud bricks that generates in trail ruins and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Mud brick slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Mud brick slabs generate as part of trail ruins.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Mud brick slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.


== Sounds ==
In Bedrock Edition, when a slab is combined into a double slab, the block's step sound is played.
