A cobblestone slab is a decorative slab variant of cobblestone that generates in overworld structures and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Cobblestone slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Cobblestone slabs generate as part of plains villages, pillager outposts, and woodland mansions.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Cobblestone slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.


== Sounds ==
In Bedrock Edition, when a cobblestone slab is combined into a double slab, the block's use sound is played.
