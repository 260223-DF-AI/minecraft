Minecraft Explorer, also known as Minecraft Explorer Pro, Minecraft Explorer Pro HD, and Minecraft Explorer Lite, was an app for iOS developed by 57Digital which was initially released in April 12, 2011 as a Lite version. It served as an official companion guide for the user, allowing to look up hundreds of item recipes, search for items by name, check mobs, and overall had a similar function to the Minecraft Wiki.


== Purchase and availability ==
Minecraft Explorer Pro was available to buy on the App Store for US$2.99, but it has been taken down.
Minecraft Explorer Pro HD used to be on the App Store for US$3.99, but it has been taken down.
Minecraft Explorer Lite used to be on the App Store for free, but it has been taken down.
