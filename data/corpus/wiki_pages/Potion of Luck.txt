A potion of Luck is a potion that provides Luck when used.


== Obtaining ==
Potions of Luck can be obtained via commands and are also available in the Creative inventory.


=== Brewing ===
While the base potion of Luck cannot be obtained via brewing, it can still be brewed into a splash potion or a lingering potion.
