1.21.51 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on December 9, 2024 for ChromeOS and Android, December 10 for iOS, iPadOS, and Fire, December 11 for all other platforms, and December 16 for Nintendo Switch. It fixes bugs and introduces a new Play screen. It is compatible with 1.21.50 servers. This is the final version released in 2024.
This version was reuploaded on Android on December 11 with the internal version number 1.21.51.02 to re-enable RenderDragon for ARMv8 devices after it had been mistakenly disabled in the original release.


== Changes ==


=== World generation ===
Pale Garden

Creaking Hearts now generate more often in naturally generated Pale Oaks.


=== General ===
Play Screen

A new "Play screen" is now introduced which includes the following tabs:
Worlds tab: Access saved local and LAN worlds
Realms tab: Access Realms you own or have joined
Servers tab: Join multiplayer featured servers, and add or access custom servers
Create from template: Create from, and rate, your owned templates, and view Marketplace content
Friends: Connect with friends (they now live in the top right hand corner of the screen)
"These updates are being rolled out incrementally, so they might not be available right away."   .


== Fixes ==
5 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.21.91

MCPE-187337 – Generated Pale Oak trees with Creaking Hearts are rarer than in Java Edition
MCPE-188547 – Magma Cube texture broken with some marketplace resource packs
Other

Fixed several crashes that could occur during gameplay.
Fixed an issue where some shaped recipes weren't parsing properly.
Fixed an issue where legacy data driven block didn't apply the render layer from the material instances component.
