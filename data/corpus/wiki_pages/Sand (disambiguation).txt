Sand may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Blocks ===
Sand, a gravity-affected block found in many Overworld biomes
Red Sand, red variety of sand
Sandstone, a block found under sand and in several desert structures
Red Sandstone, red variety of sandstone
Soul Sand, a block found in the Nether
Suspicious Sand, a block found in desert wells and desert pyramids
Gravtater, a potato version of sand for April Fools' 2024


==== Mentioned ====
Sand Layer, a retexture of snow used for testing

Desert, an arid biome composed mostly of sand
Desert Hills, a removed variation of deserts
Desert Lakes, an unused variation of deserts
Soul Sand Valley, a ghastly biome composed mostly of soul sand

Falling Block, formerly known as FallingSand, an entity created by other gravity-affected blocks blocks when falling.
