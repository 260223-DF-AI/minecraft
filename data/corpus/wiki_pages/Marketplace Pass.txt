Minecraft Marketplace Pass is an official subscription-based service that gives players in-game content that is available in the Marketplace. Hosted by Mojang Studios, Marketplace Pass provides 150+ worth of monthly in-game content from the Marketplace, including worlds, skins, add-ons, texture packs and more. Marketplace Pass is also bundled with Realms Plus.


== Requirements ==
Marketplace Pass is a service that can only be used by players that meet these requirements:

The player must have purchased the full version of Bedrock Edition and must be signed in with a Microsoft account.
The player must have a stable internet connection.
The player must have a supported version of Bedrock that has access to Marketplace.
Marketplace Pass cannot be purchased on Fire tablets, but it is still possible to access Marketplace Pass content if subscribed to it on a different platform.


== Subscription ==
Marketplace Pass is a subscription-based service, consisting of content from the in-game marketplace (US$3.99 per month).
All players can have a 30-day free Marketplace Pass trial, providing they have not had a free trial on their Microsoft account before.


== Usage ==
When the player has an active Marketplace Pass subscription and access to the Marketplace, they can freely download and use all content currently available in the Marketplace. This bypasses the normal purchasing system, but only works as long as the content is available in the Marketplace Pass, or the subscription is active. Players can also purchase Marketplace Pass content with a discount to permanently own it.
Character Creator items from the Marketplace Pass will stay available for the player even if it is no longer accessible.


== Content ==
All content is selected by the Minecraft Content Team.
