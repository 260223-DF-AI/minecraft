1.1.3 build 2 (also known as alpha 1.1.3.1) is the second and final build released for 1.1.3. This is the final alpha released in 2017.


== Fixes ==
Fixed for crashes and stability


== Trivia ==
This is the last alpha build for a version as alpha builds were replaced by beta builds upon the release of beta 1.2.0.2 on July 31, 2017.
