Netherite leggings are a late-game variant of leggings obtained by upgrading diamond leggings using a netherite ingot and netherite upgrade at a smithing table. They are the highest tier of leggings, having higher armor toughness, durability, and enchantability than diamond leggings. Like all netherite armor, netherite leggings provide 10% knockback resistance when worn, are immune to burning from fire and lava in item form, and take no durability damage from lava when worn.

== Usage ==
When worn, netherite leggings provide 6 () armor points, 3 () armor toughness, and 1 (10%) knockback resistance.


=== Repairing ===


==== Combining ====
Two damaged pairs of netherite leggings can be combined in a crafting grid, a grindstone or an anvil. The durability of the two pairs is added together, plus a bonus 5% of the maximum durability.
Crafting or using a grindstone does not preserve enchantments on the leggings.

Using an anvil preserves enchantments on the leggings, but costs experience.


==== Unit repair ====
Netherite leggings can also be repaired in an anvil by adding netherite ingots, with each ingot restoring 25% the leggings maximum durability, rounded down. This preserves the leggings enchantments, but costs experience.

=== Armor trimming ===
It is possible to upgrade armor with trims. This requires a smithing template, leggings, and an ingot or crystal (iron ingot, copper ingot, gold ingot, netherite ingot, emerald, redstone dust, lapis lazuli, amethyst shard, nether quartz, diamond, or resin brick).
These trims have no effect on the gameplay or strength of the armor.
