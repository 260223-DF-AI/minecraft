A Nether brick is an item made by smelting netherrack in a furnace, and is used to craft the Nether bricks block and its variants.


== Obtaining ==


=== Smelting ===
Nether brick can be smelted from netherrack.


=== Bartering ===
Piglins may barter 2 to 8 Nether brick items with a chance of ~8.53% (40469) when given a gold ingot. Apart from netherrack that generates on floating portals in Bedrock Edition, bartering is the only renewable source of Nether bricks.
