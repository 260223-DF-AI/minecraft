The Copper Age is an official trailer for The Copper Age, revolving around Sunny discovering the many uses and quirks of copper golems.


== Summary ==
On a dark and stormy night, Sunny begins work on a copper golem, cautiously placing a pumpkin on a block of copper. Sure enough, a golem appears, eager to work, and tempts Sunny to place items into its chest. Some time passes, Sunny bringing in a lantern, copper bulb, and copper ingot for the golem, but a zombie appears. Terrified, Sunny runs away, which confuses the golem.
Later, Sunny emerges in the base exhausted from an apparent fight, covered in several arrows. They place a golden apple onto a shelf, and empty out precious goods into the chest nearby. Later again, Sunny enacts an experiment with TNT, and the copper golem accidentally walks right into it, setting fire to the base. After that problem is presumably resolved, Sunny tends to a brewing stand, and realizes the copper golem is oxidizing. Distraught that it's become a statue, Sunny brings it outside to be put to rest at a memorial, but realizes it can be brought back to life using a copper axe. The copper golem springs to life once again, and the two hug, ending the story.
