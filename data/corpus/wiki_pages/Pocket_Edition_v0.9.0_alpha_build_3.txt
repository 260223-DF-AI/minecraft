v0.9.0 alpha build 3 is the third build version released for v0.9.0 alpha.


== Additions ==
Added sounds for endermen.


== Changes ==
Lily pads are now brighter in the inventory
Sheep now eat tall grass
No more village skyscrapers
Added dye recipes for all flowers
The low memory render distance now has an additional tick
Bone meal can now be applied to grass and ferns


== Fixes ==
13 bugs fixed

Zombies not targeting players
Crash when generating lava
Lava lighting
Water intersecting by caves would be stuck in air
Spiders would instantly attack when you hit them
Savanna and dark oak could be crafted into each other's planks
Top of doors not opening and closing
Potential crash when placing blocks at y 127
Emerald blocks were put into decorations
The smooth stone variants now require the correct stone type
Top snow is now destroyed when the block underneath is destroyed
Granite, diorite and andesite drops again
Mineshafts no longer regenerate on world reload
