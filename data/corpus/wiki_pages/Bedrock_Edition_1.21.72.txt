1.21.72 is a hotfix for Bedrock Edition released on April 8, 2025, which fixes some bugs.


== Fixes ==
4 issues fixed
MCPE-194195  Text in renamed book and quill is not saved when closing the book and quill
MCPE-194959  Sea Pickles no longer replace blocks when being placed on a partial block
Other

Fixed several crashes that could occur during gameplay
Fixed some cases of players' locations being massively rewound during long server stalls
