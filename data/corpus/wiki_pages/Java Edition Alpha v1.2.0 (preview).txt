A special preview of Alpha v1.2.0 was privately given to PC Gamer and IndieGames.com. Multiple differences between this preview and the officially-released Alpha v1.2.0 can be seen in the released footage.


== Differences from the full release ==
The Nether

Referred to as the Slip.
 Zombie pigman

Has the original blood texture, instead of the slime/decay.
Runs around making pig noises, and makes zombie noises when hurt.
Does not have their arms up like a zombie, and do not wield a golden sword.
Glowstone

Drops itself instead of glowstone dust.
Ghast

Does not have any sound effects.
Leaves

Leaf decay is still enabled as opposed to the official release of Alpha 1.2.0.
