A start platform is a structure generated when a player enters a mine in the 25w14craftmine snapshot. It comes ready with a crafter and lever, enabling the player to craft items early game.


== Generation ==
This structure spawns in mines at the player's spawn point. There is only one start platform per mine. 


== Structure ==
Start platforms match the style of mine exits, using deepslate tiles, stripped pale oak wood, and resin bricks in the shape of a small hut. Four deepslate pillars hold up the ceiling with a shroomlight on top to light up the structure.
