Fouge Tempro is a character from the novel Minecraft: The Voyage, written by Jason Fry. He is the leader of the raiders who ruined Stax Stonecutter's life.


== Appearance ==
Fouge is a man with hypnotic bright blue eyes that are always in motion, his armor is seemingly random: Golden chestplate, diamond leggings, leather boots, and a way too big belt that holds his diamond sword. The clothes he wears are completely messed up as well - some are too big and some are too tight for him, they all are supposedly stolen from his victims.


== Personality ==

Fouge is a cruel pirate leader with unique villain philosophy.
