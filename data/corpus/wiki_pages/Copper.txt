Copper is a metal found in the Overworld that is used to craft copper armor, copper tools, building blocks and other items, as well as to create copper golems. Most blocks made from copper oxidize slowly over time, changing their appearance.
Copper generates naturally as copper ore, which drops raw copper that can be smelted into ingots. Additionally, copper blocks - many of which are fully oxidized - can be found abundantly in trial chambers.


== Items ==
Raw Copper – an item dropped from mining copper ore.
Copper Ingot – an item created by smelting raw copper or by killing drowned and copper golems.
Copper Nugget – obtained by smelting copper tools and armor.
Bolt Armor Trim – an item that is duplicated using a block of copper.

=== Minecraft Education ===
Copper – an element in  Minecraft Education and Bedrock Edition.


== Mob ==
Copper Golem – a buildable mob that takes items from copper chests and sorts them into regular chests.
 It also oxidizes over time.


== Structure ==
Trial Chambers – Overworld underground structures made out of waxed blocks of copper and their oxidized variants, containing combat challenges in the form of trial spawners.
