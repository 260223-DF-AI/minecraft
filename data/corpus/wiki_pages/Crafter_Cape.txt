The Crafter Cape is an upcoming cape that will be given to TwitchCon 2026 attendees.


== Appearance ==
The cape's design depicts an iron pickaxe on a crafting table background. It is similar to the Inverted Crafter Cape, but the pickaxe is right side up.


== Distribution ==
This cape will be given to those who attend the in-person TwitchCon Europe 2026 event in Rotterdam, which takes place on May 30-31, 2026.


== History ==


== Trivia ==
The design layout is similar to the teaser poster for A Minecraft Sequel.
