Chiseled Nether bricks are a decorative variant of Nether bricks, crafted from Nether brick slabs.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Chiseled Nether bricks can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.
