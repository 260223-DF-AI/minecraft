MINECON 2015 Skin Pack is a celebratory skin pack for MINECON 2015 that includes 8 skins of Steve and Alex wearing MINECON capes. It was available for free on Legacy Console Editions until July 15, 2015. Due to the Better Together Update, it was added in Bedrock Edition on September 12, 2017, although it is only available to players who owned it already when they migrated from Legacy Console Edition. All skins in this pack were later part of the MINECON 2016 Skin Pack.
