1.8.13 is an update for the New Nintendo 3DS Edition released on November 20–21, 2018, which added new downloadable content and fixed bugs.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
New DLC available for purchase:

Steampunk Texture Pack
Strangers - Biome Settlers 3
Moana Character Pack
Mighty Morphin Power Rangers skin pack
Magic: The Gathering Skin Pack
Halloween Costumes Skin Pack
Festive Mash-up 2016


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Improved stability and performance.


== Fixes ==
Fixed a bug where the player could sprint while sleeping in a bed.
Numerous bug fixes from previous builds.
