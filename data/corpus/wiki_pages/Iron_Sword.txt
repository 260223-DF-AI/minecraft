An iron sword is a variant of sword crafted from iron ingots. It is the third-highest tier of sword, dealing more damage and having more durability than stone and copper swords.


== Obtaining ==


=== Mob loot ===

A vex never drops its iron sword, because its main hand's HandDropChances value is 0. This is not affected by the Looting enchantment.
In Bedrock Edition, pillagers and vindicators that spawn from raids have a 4.1% chance (or a 5.12% chance on Hard difficulty) of dropping a damaged iron sword. This sword has a 50% chance of being enchanted.[needs testing]
In both editions, zombies, zombie villagers, and husks have a random chance to spawn with an iron sword. The zombie has an 8.5% chance to drop the sword; this chance is also affected by Looting, with each level increasing the chance by 1% (11.5% with Looting III).


=== Generated loot ===


=== Crafting ===
An iron sword is crafted using 1 stick and 2 iron ingots.

== Usage ==


=== Breaking ===

A sword can also be used to destroy certain blocks 50% quicker, sometimes much quicker than with fists. Using a sword to destroy any block that doesn't break instantly by hand decreases its durability by 2; this includes bamboo, despite that the sword is the fastest tool for breaking it.
If a sword is enchanted with Silk Touch, either using Creative or commands, cobwebs the sword destroys drop the cobwebs themselves instead of the usual string. This is due to the sword being classified as the proper tool for cobwebs.


==== Creative mode ====
In Creative mode, swords are unable to break blocks. However, they can still break placed entities such as minecarts, paintings, item frames,[Java Edition  only] and armor stands.


=== Attacking ===

An iron sword is used to deal damage to mobs and entities by attacking them. The iron sword has 6HP base damage in Java Edition and 7HP base damage in Bedrock Edition, and can be enchanted to deal more damage. ==== Anvil repair ====

An iron sword can be repaired in an anvil by adding iron ingots, with each repair material restoring 25% the sword's maximum durability, rounded down. Two iron swords can also be combined in an anvil. Both methods preserve the sword's enchantments.

=== Smelting ingredient ===
An iron sword can be smelted into an iron nugget using a furnace or blast furnace.


== Sounds ==

Iron swords make a sound when used to attack an entity. The sound depends on how the attack is landed. Their sounds are shared with other types of swords.
