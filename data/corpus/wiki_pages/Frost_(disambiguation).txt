Frost may refer to:


== Minecraft ==
Freezing, damage taken by entities staying for a long time in powder snow.
Frost Walker, an enchantment that grants the player the ability to turn water walked on into frosted ice.
Frosted Ice, quick-melting ice produced by the Frost Walker enchantment.
