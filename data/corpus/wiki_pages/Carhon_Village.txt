Carhon Village (, Karon-mura?) is a village appearing in Minecraft: The Manga. It was attacked by the Giant Spider, and saved by Nico's party.


== Layout ==
Carhon Village's layout is largely unknown, but it appears to resemble a typical village, consisting of small houses built out of stained terracotta and wood and all connected by a pathway. It is located near a forest.


== History ==
Some time relatively recently, the Giant Spider took up residence in the forest near Carhon Village. It began to attack the village's livestock, continually growing larger due to its "Growth" Mod Power, until it eventually kidnapped 100 animals in a single night.
Desperate to kill the monster harassing the village, Lily, the mayor's daughter, approached any adventurers she could find for help. However, they all turned her down, except a duo who attempted to run with her advance payment. Overhearing the issue, Nico White decided to investigate himself, and began to look around the village for clues about the monster.
That night, while patrolling the village, Lily was abducted by the Giant Spider herself, who had decided to move onto eating humans. She screamed as she was kidnapped, allowing Nico and Gray to track down the spider and kill it before it ate her. Elsewhere in the forest, Yamabuki apprehended the two adventurers she had attempted to hire, leaving them hanging from a tree for the other villagers to find.
With the threat to the village eradicated, Lily thanked Nico's party, who moved on to continue their adventure.


== Points of interest ==
Carhon Village is home to a tavern, but no other points of interest are otherwise known.
