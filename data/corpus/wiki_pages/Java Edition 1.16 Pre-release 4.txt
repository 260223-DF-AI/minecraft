1.16 Pre-release 4 (known as 1.16-pre4 in the launcher) is the fourth pre-release for Java Edition 1.16, released on June 11, 2020, which tweaks some spawn rates and fixes bugs.


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
Skeletons

Increased skeleton's spawning weight in soul sand valleys from 2 to 10.


=== Command format ===
/datapack

Changed the chat output when enabling/disabling a data pack from "Enabled/Disabled data pack <datapack>" to "Enabling/Disabling data pack <datapack>".


=== Gameplay ===
Spawning

Tweaked hostile spawn rates in warped forests and soul sand valleys.
