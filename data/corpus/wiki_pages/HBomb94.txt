Liam Heneghan, also known as HBomb94, is an American gaming YouTuber, known for his work on Minecraft content.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
In 2024, Heneghan participated in the Ender Cup, under the team Red Rabbits. To promote the event, he appeared in the MCC x Minecraft 15th Anniversary Party, voicing himself as an NPC.
