A shepherd is a villager that trades shears, wool, dyes, paintings and beds. Shepherds wear a brown hat and a white coat.


== Spawning ==
An unemployed villager turns into an shepherd when an unclaimed loom is nearby.


=== Natural generation ===

The workstation of shepherds, the loom, generates in shepherd houses in villages.


== Drops ==

In Java Edition, a shepherd can drop a random color of wool by throwing it as a gift towards a nearby player with the Hero of the Village effect.
