v0.7.6 alpha is a minor update to Minecraft: Pocket Edition released on October 11, 2013 for Android and on October 16, 2013 on iOS. It improves servers and fixes some of the bugs and crashes in v0.7.5.


== Changes ==
Changed icon from  to  on iOS.
New keyboard design for iOS 7.
Realms servers are now displayed in alphabetical order.
The way to access Realms was moved from the "Play on Realms" button in the main menu to the "Invited to Realms Alpha?" section in the options menu.
Despite being removed, the "Play on Realms" button still can be selected with the Xperia Play controller.
The "Disconnected from server" kick message has been replaced with a proper kick screen.


== Fixes ==
4 issues fixed
From 0.7.0

MCPE-3159  It does not say "Stone Slab"
From 0.7.1

MCPE-4142  While using chat box and get damage, the chat box will lose and the keyboard not disappear.
Other

Items with an invalid ID are now automatically removed from chests.
Fixed a crash when a chest or furnace containing air is opened.
