Joseph Mark Garrett, better known as Stampylonghead, Stampylongnose, Stampy Cat, or simply Stampy, is an English YouTuber, actor, and author known for his Minecraft playthroughs.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==


=== Minecraft Story Mode ===


==== Season 1 ====

Within the game, he portrays himself in the episode A Portal to Mystery, alongside several other popular creators. His character becomes intertwined with a series of murders connected to the villain the White Pumpkin, where his role in the story can change depending on the player's choices.


==== Season 2 ====
He reappears as himself alongside some builds from his Lovely World series. Being featured in the title screen and in-game, his tower-like structure with a rainbow-striped dome is used as his domestic household in-universe with his Love Garden beside it.


== Trivia ==
In contrast to other Minecraft content creators, Stampy doesn't use a custom Minecraft skin. Instead he uses a skin based on Fidget from Dust: An Elysian Tail which was originally part of the Summer of Arcade Skin Pack and later re-released in Skin Pack 4, as well as the Legacy Skin Pack.
Although unofficial to Minecraft, he has partnered with 4J Studios to release the world from his long-time YouTube series Stampy's Lovely World on the Marketplace.
