Alpha v1.0.4, also known as Seecret Friday 4, is the fourth seecret update and the second of Java Edition Alpha, released on July 9, 2010, which added winter mode, snowfall, and two new blocks. This is the oldest Alpha version to be available in the launcher.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Ice

Slippery when stepped on.
Generates when water is exposed to the sky on world generation.
It can be melted by fire or lava.
 Snow

Spawns on top of most blocks when exposed to snowfall.
Cannot be obtained yet.
It can be melted by fire or lava.


=== World generation ===
Winter mode

Added a new "Winter mode" map type, featuring:
Snowfall.
Blocks exposed to sky generate with a thin layer of snow and those exposed to snowfall slowly get covered by it.
Water exposed to snowfall generates as ice.
However, water found on gravel beaches is not entirely frozen upon generation.
Upon world creation there is a 25% chance for it to become a winter world.
