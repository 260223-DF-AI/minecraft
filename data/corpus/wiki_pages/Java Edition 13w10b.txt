13w10b is the fifteenth and final snapshot for Java Edition 1.5, released on March 6, 2013 to fix some of the bugs and crashes in the 13w10a snapshot.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Dispensers and droppers

Now only fire once when they receive a redstone signal.
To fire again, the signal must be turned off and on again. (Previously they would fire whenever they had a signal and a nearby block changed)
