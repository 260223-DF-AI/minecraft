1.2.6.1 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released only for Android on December 7, 2017.


== Fixes ==
Crashes/Performance

Fixed android specific crashes.
General

MCPE-27409 – Certain numbers are censored like swear words
MCPE-29434, MCPE-29499, MCPE-29428, MCPE-29457, MCPE-29495 – Chat filter with hashtags
Graphics/Audio

MCPE-17100 – Eating sounds/particles are missing when feeding sheep, pigs, chickens, ocelots, wolves, and rabbits.
