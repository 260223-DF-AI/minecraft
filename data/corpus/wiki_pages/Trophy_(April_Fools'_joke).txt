A trophy is a block exclusive to 25w14craftmine.


== Obtaining ==
The Mega Spud trophy is obtained by eating 100 poisonous potatoes for the "Certified Spud Chomper" advancement.
The "Close But No" Medal is obtained by using a trial key or an ominous trial key on a shimmering door.

== Issues ==
Issues relating to "Trophy (April Fools' joke)" are not maintained on the bug tracker because it is an April Fools' joke, and is therefore not in the newest stable version or snapshot. Issues reported there are closed as "Invalid".


== Trivia ==
The Mega Spud Trophy is a reference to the Mega Spud from the previous year's April Fools' update, the Poisonous Potato Update (24w14potato).
