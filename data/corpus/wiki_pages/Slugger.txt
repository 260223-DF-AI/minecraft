Slugger is a character in the Minecraft Mini-Series and part of Team Mooshroom with Mo.


== Appearance ==
Slugger has purple hair, black eyebrows, brown eyes, and tan skin. She wears a dull lavender shirt with three gray buttons, mushroom mitts, gray pants, a black belt, dirty feet, and an anklet.


== Biography ==
Before the series began, Slugger was mushroom brawler.
Slugger was once teleported along with Mo by CommuniKate using a command block. In the second trial, she saves Toby from the Cave Spiders, but still loses, and then Mo triggered her own death by explosion to save her friends, which led to Slugger's elimination from the show. Then she with other heroes was saved from obsidian prison and their sabotage succeeds, but instead of going home, they are teleported to the Greek Isles. Poseidon teleported Slugger to a sand pillar in the Kraken sea along with the other heroes, and she spent most of the season there. Then at the end, all the heroes escape and try to stop Poseidon. After a while, Zeus comes, stops s Poseidon, and asks heroes to help him. After helping the god, Slugger and Mo apparently returned home.


== Personality ==
Slugger is energetic and boisterous. She enjoys punching anything but prefers to punch hostile mobs.


== Relationships ==


=== Mo ===
Apparently, Slugger and Mo were friends before the series began, and maintained this relationship until the end of the series.


=== Quinn ===
They've been friends since Episode 2, always helping each other out during challenges and eventually stopping CommunitKate's gameshow


== Trivia ==
Slugger is based on the "Mushroom Brawler" from Mattel which in turn was based on a skin by the same name in the Biome Settlers Skin Pack 2.
