A deepslate tile slab is a decorative slab variant of deepslate tiles that generates in ancient cities and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Deepslate tile slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Deepslate tile slabs generate as part of ancient cities.

=== Stonecutting ===


== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Deepslate tile slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.


== Sounds ==
In Bedrock Edition, when a deepslate tile slab is combined into a double slab, the block's use sound is played.
