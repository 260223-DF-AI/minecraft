A cinnabar brick wall is a decorative wall variant of cinnabar bricks.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Cinnabar brick walls drop as an item if mined by any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Crafting ===


=== Stonecutting ===


== Usage ==

Unlike most other walls, mobs can be leashed to cinnabar brick walls similar to fences.[Bedrock Edition  only]
