Skin Pack 3, named Skin Pack 3 - Classic in Bedrock Edition, is a skin pack that includes 44 skins (45 in Legacy Console Edition) referencing various characters from Minecraft and other popular video games.


== Skins ==
Bedrock Edition includes the theme name in front of their respective skin's name e.g. Half-Life Gordon Freeman
