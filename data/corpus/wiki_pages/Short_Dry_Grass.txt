Short dry grass is a one-block tall foliage block that generates frequently in deserts and all types of badlands. It grows on sand and terracotta. It can also be eaten by sheep.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Short dry grass is immediately destroyed when broken.

Short dry grass drops nothing when destroyed using a tool without Silk Touch or not using shears.
Short dry grass drops itself when destroyed using a tool with Silk Touch or shears.


=== Natural generation ===
Short dry grass generates in deserts and badlands.


=== Post generation ===
Using bone meal on tall dry grass places a short dry grass on a neighboring block if possible.


== Usage ==
Short dry grass can be used for aesthetic decoration. Short dry grass can be placed on any of the following blocks:

In Bedrock Edition, short dry grass can be snowlogged.
When a sheep eats short dry grass, the block is removed, and a sheared sheep regrows its wool. Baby sheep graze grass 20 times more often than adults and mature 1 minute faster when grazing.


=== Fuel ===
When used in a furnace as fuel, a piece of short dry grass lasts 5 seconds (smelting half an item).


=== Composting ===
Placing short dry grass into a composter has a 30% chance of raising the compost level by 1, the lowest possible chance.


=== Bone meal ===
Bone meal can be used on short dry grass to turn it into tall dry grass.


=== Ambience ===
If placed on sand, red sand or a terracotta block of any kind with at least one more corresponding block underneath it, short dry grass occasionally produces ambient sounds reminiscent of wind howling. These ambient sounds can play anywhere if the given conditions are met, and naturally play in the badlands and desert biomes due to the abundance of dry grass on top of said blocks.
