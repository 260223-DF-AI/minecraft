The Harpy is a half-woman half-eagle mythic legend appearing as the main antagonist of Clash in the Canal in Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Appearance ==
The harpy has a brown bird-like body and wings, as well as yellow legs. Instead of a bird's head, the harpy has a human head with red eyeballs, maroon pupils, and large brown eyebrows. The harpy's hair is medium and brown.


== Biography ==
The Harpy appeared in Episode 4 of Season 2 of the Minecraft Mini-Series, when Quinn and Zed went to the cave to get the enchanted eye of Ender. They were met by Arachne in the cave, but when the heroes approached the Harpy's cave, the Arachne convinced them of fear. When they entered the cave, the Harpy attacked them, and when the heroes were almost killed, CommuniKate and Durango came and caught and held the harpy on a fishing rod. Then the heroes took the eye and leaved the cave.


== Power and Abilities ==
As shown in the series, Harpy's main power is flight. She has high movement speed, combat speed, strength and durability. She can strike with her paws at the sane time accelerating to high speed.
