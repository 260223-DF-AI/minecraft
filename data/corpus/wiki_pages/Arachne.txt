Arachne is a human-spider hybrid monster based on the character from Greek mythology, appearing as a minor antagonist of Clash in the Canal in Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Appearance ==
Arachne has a red abdomen with four holes on top. Instead of a spider's cephalothorax, she has a human head with red eyes and a body, and her legs have white stripes.


== Personality ==
Arachne is known for sneaking on intruders in the Harpy's Cave, lurking and then surprising them. However, she shows a deep fear of the Harpy.


== Powers and Abilities ==
Arachne, being a hybrid of a spider and a human, can jump far, run fast and weave webs. She can also stand with a few legs in the air.
