1.6 is the first pre-release for Java Edition 1.6.1, released on June 25, 2013.
1.6 was reuploaded just two hours after its release to fix a crash bug with brewing potions.


== Changes ==
Ghasts and zombie pigmen no longer visibly catch fire when going into fire or lava.
Potion of Weakness now reduces damage by one-half heart, instead of one heart as it was before.
