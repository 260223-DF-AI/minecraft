A tuff wall is a decorative wall variant of tuff that does not generate naturally and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Tuff walls drop as an item if mined by any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Tuff walls can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
