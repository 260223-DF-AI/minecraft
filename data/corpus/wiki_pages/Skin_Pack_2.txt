Skin Pack 2, named Skin Pack 2 - Classic in Bedrock Edition, is a skin pack that includes 43 skins (originally 45) referencing various characters from Minecraft and other popular video games.


== Skins ==
Bedrock Edition includes the theme name in front of a skin's name e.g. Minecraft - Enderman
