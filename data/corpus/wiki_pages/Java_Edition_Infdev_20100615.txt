A version of Minecraft Infdev was released on June 15, 2010.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
 Bucket

Crafted with 3 iron ingots.

Collects water and lava.
 Water bucket

Spills water.
Cannot be placed onto a block already containing a liquid to replace it; only non-liquid blocks can be used to replace liquids.
 Lava bucket

Spills lava.
Cannot be placed onto a block already containing a liquid to replace it; only non-liquid blocks can be used to replace liquids.


=== General ===
Options

Fast/fancy graphics toggle.
This option toggles the clouds between 2D and 3D. Fast clouds are identical to the old clouds. Fancy graphics are preset by default.
Entity shadows become invisible when fast graphics are enabled.

Daylight cycle

Days are now slightly longer than nights. Day lasts around 9 minutes 15 seconds, night lasts around 8 minutes.
