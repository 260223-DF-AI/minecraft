A Nether brick slab is a decorative slab variant of Nether bricks that does not generate naturally and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Nether brick slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Crafting ===


=== Stonecutting ===


== Usage ==

Despite their appearance, top and double Nether brick slabs do not qualify as Nether brick blocks for the purposes of Nether fortress mob spawning in Java Edition. 


=== Crafting ingredient ===


=== Note blocks ===
Nether brick slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.


== Sounds ==
In Bedrock Edition, when a Nether brick slab is combined into a double slab, the block's use sound is played.
