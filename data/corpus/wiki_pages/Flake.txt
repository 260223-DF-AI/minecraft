"Flake" is a song composed by C418 that plays in the Festive Mash-up pack for Legacy Console Edition and New Nintendo 3DS Edition.


== Track description ==

"Flake" starts with calm, echoing bells backed by a synth, and then a scratchy beat is introduced. Near the end, the scratching beat glitches, with the synth and bells gradually fading out.
