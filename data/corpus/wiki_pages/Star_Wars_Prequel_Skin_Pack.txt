Star Wars Prequel Skin Pack is a skin pack that includes 52 skins of characters from  Star Wars: The Phantom Menace, Star Wars: Attack of the Clones, Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith, and the 2008 animated series Star Wars: The Clone Wars.
