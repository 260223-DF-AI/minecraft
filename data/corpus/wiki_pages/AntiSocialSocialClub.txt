AntiSocialSocialClub (also referred to as ASSC) is a clothing company that collaborated with Mojang Studios on a limited-time Minecraft-themed collection. The collection was revealed on October 15, 2025 and released on October 18, 2025. The collaboration was featured in the official Minecraft booth at the TwitchCon in San Diego from October 19 to October 20.
