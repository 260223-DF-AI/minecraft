A version of Minecraft Infdev was released on March 25, 2010, at 15:45 UTC.


== Additions ==


=== World generation ===
Caves

Have been re-implemented and now generate naturally.


=== General ===
Particles

Re-added.
Now generated at the edges of water.
Block ticks

Re-implemented, allowing for crops to grow, fire to spread, grass to spread to dirt, and more.
Can no longer place fire on the sides of non-flammable blocks.
Flowers pop off when not exposed to sunlight.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Leaves

Leaves now decay.
Furnaces

Now work like they did before Infdev.
Stone slabs

Now make double slabs when possible.


=== World generation ===
Ores

Ores now generate in ore blobs.


== Removals ==


=== General ===
Removed duplicate login detection because it caused problems.


== Fixes ==
1 bug fixed

Due to the removal of the duplicate login detection, the game no longer randomly crashes with the "session conflict" screen.


== Bugs ==
4 bugs reported

Cave generation sometimes drops performance.
The game crashes on sunset.
This is caused by flower drops in darkness.
The game progressively degrades in performance, becoming unbearably slow within minutes of loading a world. (This is caused by flowers that are generated in places where they break as soon as they appear, resulting in an increase in the number of entities. In rare cases, a flower or chunk breaks in such a way to spawn insane amounts of entities, bringing the game to a grinding halt in seconds.)
This can cause extreme lag spikes happening to the game, and this will sometimes result in the game freezing.
Above-ground cave entrances cut into trees and brick pyramids, resulting in half-destroyed trees and brick pyramids containing caves. The entrances also do not turn dirt into grass, which results in patches of dirt strewn across the terrain near cave entrances.
