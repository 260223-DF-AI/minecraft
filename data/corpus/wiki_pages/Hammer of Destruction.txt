The Hammer of Destruction (大破壊ハンマー, Dai Hakai Hanmā) is a large iron hammer, created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Hammer of Destruction is a giant rectangular hammer with an X-mark drawn across its head. It has spikes sticking out of either side of its head, and a long handle for the wielder to grip.


== Functionality ==
As a giant hammer, the Hammer of Destruction can be used to destroy things. When Nico White uses it, he refers to swinging with it as "Shatter" (大粉砕, Dai Funsai, lit. "Large Crushing").
