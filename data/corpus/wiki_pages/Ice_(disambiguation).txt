Ice may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Blocks ===
Ice, a translucent block that generates naturally on frozen rivers and yields a water source when melted.
Frosted Ice, a variant of ice that generates temporarily with the Frost Walker enchantment.
Packed Ice, an opaque variant of ice that does not melt and generates in frozen peaks and ice spikes biomes, as well as in icebergs.
Blue Ice, an ice variant that generates in icebergs and is more slippery than ice and packed ice.


=== Item ===
Ice Bomb, Minecraft Education item used to freeze water into ice.


=== Features ===
Iceberg, feature that generates on frozen oceans that consists of snow, packed ice and blue ice.
Ice Spike, feature that consists of ice and packed ice.
Ice Patch, feature that generates in Ice Spikes biome.
Ice Pile, a feature that generates only in villages.


=== Biomes ===
Ice Spikes, snowy plains biome variant that contains many ice spikes.
Ice Desert, deleted biome in Java Edition.


=== Tag ===
minecraft:ice, a tag that includes all ice block variants.


== Minecraft: Story Mode ==
Icy Ender Creeper, hostile mobs that are a hybrid of endermen and creepers.
Icy Golem, an icy cold golem variant
Icy Spider, an icy variant of the spider.
Larger Icy Golem, a boss variant of the icy golem.
Icy Palace of Despair, a structure built by Romeo.


== Minecraft Earth ==
Ice, a block found in many levels.
Packed Ice, Ditto.


== Minecraft Dungeons ==
Icy Creeper, a variant of creeper in the Creeping Winter DLC that freezes mobs in its blast radius.
Frozen Zombie, a variant of zombie in the Creeping Winter DLC that freezes hit targets.
Iceologer, an illager in the Creeping Winter DLC able to summon falling ice chunks above the player's head.
Ice Chunk, an entity sumomned by an iceologer.


=== Item ===
Ice Wand, an artifact that can summon an ice block that crushes and stuns mobs.


=== Locations ===
Ice Cabin, an optional sub dungeon within the secret mission Lost Settlement.
Ice Cabin Backroom, a part of the Ice Cabin optional sub dungeon
Ice Caves, an optional sub dungeon within the secret mission Lost Settlement.
Ice Dungeon, an optional sub dungeon within the secret mission Lost Settlement.


== Minecraft Legends ==
Frost Tower, a structure that freezes enemies with large blocks of ice.
Ice Trap, a structure that freezes enemies when they walk over it.
