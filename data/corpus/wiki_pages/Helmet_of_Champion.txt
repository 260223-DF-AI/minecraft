A Helmet of Champion is a unique variant of golden helmet, trimmed with five diamonds. It appears in the episode "The First Night" in the Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Appearance ==
The Helmet of Champion looks like a normal gold helmet, but is decorated with diamonds of various shapes on all sides.


== Properties ==
The Helmet of Champion is used as a head ornament, similar to the Crown and as a means of head protection, however the Helmet of Champion is likely to have the same durability as its unadorned counterpart.


== Biography ==
The Helmets of Champion, two in number, were apparently created before the series began. They were intended to be a reward for the team that passed all the challenges and won the show. When Durango and River pass the first challenge, Zed shows them the helmets, and CommuniKate says that they are a reward for the winning team. Later, when teams teleported to the Greek Islands, Zed, standing next to CommuniKate, puts on helmet.
