1.21.101 is a hotfix for Bedrock Edition released on August 13, 2025 on most devices and on August 15, 2025 on Xbox, which fixes some bugs.


== Fixes ==
8 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.21.111

MCPE-223910 – Grass color in the Swamp and Mangrove Swamp biomes is now much more green than it should be
MCPE-225267 – Firefly Bush particles spawn offset/displaced from their intended position
MCPE-226072 – Firefly bush generation is too rare in worlds created after 1.21.100
MCPE-226102 – c=-1 target selector is broken
Other

Fixed several crashes that could occur during gameplay.
Fixed a crash that could occur when treatment packs were removed while loading game or loading into a world.
Fixed an issue with items not being placed into bundles correctly when interacting with hoppers.
Fixed an issue on PlayStation 5 where downloading large packs when joining Realms and multiplayer worlds would sometimes fail.
