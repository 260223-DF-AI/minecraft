These values apply to blocks placed in the world in the remake of Classic 0.0.23a_01. IDs in  light blue cannot be legitimately obtained in the player's inventory in the game; they can only be obtained by inventory editors.


== IDs ==
Inventory sprites are used where they exist. For certain blocks (air, water, lava, bedrock, gold ore, iron ore, coal ore) no sprite is defined, so an isometric render is used instead. Some of these match the sprites used in 0.0.23a_01 proper, however many have subtle differences.
