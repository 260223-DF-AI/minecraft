An upcoming animated television series based on the video game of the same name is being produced by Mojang Studios, WildBrain Studios, and Flying Bark Productions. It will debut exclusively on Netflix.


== Plot ==
According to Netflix, the series “will tell an original story with new characters and reflect the world of Minecraft in a new light”.


== Production ==


=== Development ===
The series was announced to be in production on May 30, 2024, alongside a teaser trailer. WildBrain Studios - the Canadian studio behind Carmen Sandiego, Ninjago: Dragons Rising, and Sonic Prime - are responsible for the series' CG-animation, while Netflix and Mojang Studios produce it.
On April 23, 2025, Netflix announced that an "in-depth look" at the series would be shown at their animated series studio focus at the 2025 Annecy International Animation Film Festival on June 12, 2025.
On June 12, 2025, Nexus Point News reported on X that Netflix revealed that the series will have detailed animation and "will be authentic to the game while offering new adventures". It was reported by Variety that Netflix and Mojang Studios desired to "create a universe that is a new experience for fans" showcasing "a trio of images that evoked audible gasps from the crowd". It was also announced that Kevin Adams and Joe Ksander would serve as showrunners while A.C. Bradley would serve as head writer.
