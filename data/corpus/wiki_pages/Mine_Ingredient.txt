The mine ingredient is an item introduced in 25w14craftmine that can be used to create a mine and add effects and features to it.


== Obtaining ==
Mine Ingredients can be obtained in Survival mode by doing certain tasks. Exiting a mine with a Mine Ingredient in the inventory unlocks it in the mine crafter menu.
If a Mine Ingredient is obtained in the Hub (through commands or using the rooms behind shimmering doors), it can be obtained by right-clicking the Mine Crafter while holding an ingredient.
The mine crafter interface shows a list of all the ingredients that the player hasnt unlocked, labeled Undiscovered, and every ingredient shows a hint on how to obtain them.
Specific ingredients can be obtained with this command: 
/give @s minecraft:mine_ingredient[minecraft:world_modifiers={effects : [<mine_ingredient_identifier>], include_description:1b}, minecraft:world_effect_unlock={}] 
where <mine_ingredient_identifier> is the identifier of the desired ingredient.


== Usage ==
The mine ingredient is used as an ingredient in the mine crafter. Each ingredient added to a mine using the Mine Crafter will alter the generation of the mine in the described way.
It is not possible to add mine ingredients to special event mines.
