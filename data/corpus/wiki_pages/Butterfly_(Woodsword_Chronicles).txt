A butterfly is a type of insect found within the computer lab of Woodsword Middle School, as well as a modded mob created by Theo Grayson.


== Appearance ==
Butterflies are a purple modded mob, with a body consisting of a cube head and three cuboids for a body. On the front of the head is what appears to be a mouth or lips, with eyes on both sides, and gangling antenna making a swirl shape on top. Their wings are flat, and come in many different colors, with patterns resembling circles or hearts.
