1.21.71 is a hotfix for Bedrock Edition released on March 28, 2025 on most platforms and March 31, 2025 for Fire tablets and Nintendo Switch, which fixes some bugs.


== Fixes ==
3 issues fixed
From the previous development version

MCPE-191525  Entity death animation plays too fast compared to previous versions.
Other

Fixed an issue that prevented buttons for external links from working correctly on iOS and iPadOS devices.
Fixed client and server desynchronizing on entities the client erroneously thought were dead.
