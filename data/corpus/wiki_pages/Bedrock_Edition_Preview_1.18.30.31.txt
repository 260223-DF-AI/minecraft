Beta 1.18.30.30 (Windows, Android, Xbox) or Preview 1.18.30.31 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox) is the tenth beta/Preview version for Bedrock Edition 1.18.30, released on March 25, 2022, which fixes a critical bug regarding corrupted chunks.


== Fixes ==
Addressed an issue from the last beta that caused chunks below y=0 to become corrupted. (MCPE-153316)
The chunks will no longer become corrupted but any chunk that was affected will revert to its original world generation.
