The Music update was an update for Java Edition that added 17 new music tracks by Minecraft composer C418, released on November 15, 2013. 16 tracks had been previously released on the album Minecraft - Volume Beta, and the 17th was a new original piece.


== Additions ==
Four tracks that play at the title screen: "Mutation", "Moog City 2", "Beginning 2", and "Floating Trees".
Six tracks that play in Creative mode: "Biome Fest", "Blind Spots", "Haunt Muskie", "Aria Math", "Dreiton", and "Taswell".
Four tracks that play in the Nether: "Concrete Halls", "Dead Voxel", "Warmth", and "Ballad of the Cats".
One track that plays in the End: "The End".
One track that plays during the credits: "Alpha".
One track that plays during the fight with the ender dragon: "Boss".
