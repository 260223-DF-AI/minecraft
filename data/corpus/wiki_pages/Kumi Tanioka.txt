Kumi Tanioka (谷岡 久美, Tanioka Kumi) is a Japanese composer and pianist who composed tracks for the Caves & Cliffs and Tricky Trials soundtracks. Outside of Minecraft, she works extensively with Square Enix, most notably on the Final Fantasy Crystal Chronicles series. She has also contributed arrangements to the Super Smash Bros. series.


== Official Minecraft soundtracks ==
Minecraft: Caves & Cliffs (Original Game Soundtrack) (October 20, 2021)
Minecraft: Tricky Trials (Original Game Soundtrack) (April 26, 2024)
