The first version of Minecraft 4k was released on December 2, 2009. This version only featured two blocks: air and the XOR Block.


== Features ==


=== Blocks ===
 Air
 XOR Block[unofficial name]

Is the only non-air block available.
Uses a XOR fractal as a texture.


=== World generation ===
The world generation is an endless scattering of the XOR Block but there is an ending not too far from the worlds spawn point/origin.


=== General ===
Controls

Moving the mouse while holding right, middle, or left click turns the camera.
Fog

Obscures the player's view distance.
