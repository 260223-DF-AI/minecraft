"komorebi" is a song composed by Kumi Tanioka that plays in the Overworld and when the player is in Creative mode in Java Edition.


== Locations ==


== Track description ==
A relaxing melody with what sounds like chimes jingling.

=== Raw music files ===
In Java Edition, the music files can be found in .minecraft/assets/objects.
In Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Education, it can be found in:

Mobile: com.mojang/resource_packs/music/vanilla_music/sounds/music/game/
Windows: %PROGRAMFILES%\WindowsApps\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP_<version>_x64_8wekyb3d8bbwe\data\resource_packs\vanilla_music\sounds\music\game
All music files are in Ogg Vorbis.

== Trivia ==
The word "komorebi" (木漏[こも]れ日[び]) means "sunlight filtering through trees" in Japanese.
komorebi uses the Comforting Memories leitmotif, also made by Kumi Tanioka from the Minecraft: Caves & Cliffs (Original Game Soundtrack), and the latter 2 (Pokopoko and Yakusoku) all sound similar.
