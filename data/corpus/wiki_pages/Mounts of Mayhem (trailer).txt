Mounts of Mayhem (stylized in all caps) is an official trailer for Mounts of Mayhem.


== Summary ==
In a badlands biome, Alex is seen riding a horse, scaring an armadillo as they rush by it. They are seen chased by a group of five camel husk jockeys, and a parched attempts shooting an arrow at Alex only to miss. Alex then blows their goat horn and stops near a wooded biome, not knowing what to do while the jockeys are coming to them. Soon, they hear the blaring of a horn, unsheath their diamond sword and ride toward the jockeys, with their friends (Efe, Kai, Makena and Sunny) by their side, all the four wielding iron spears. Between the sides, a pig gets panicked as both sides start fighting. Efe takes down the husk and parched on a camel husk with their spear, and tells Makena to do the same on another camel husk jockey. Soon after, Efe notices the sun setting as five zombie horsemen arrive to attack the two riders. Kai falls from their horse and runs away from a zombie horseman chasing them. A gliding Noor then rushes by and instantly takes down the zombie rider. Alex on their horse passing by quickly causes Kai to fall into a lake, finding themselves in front of five zombie nautilus jockeys preparing to attack them. Kai then finds a nautilus and rides it to come back to safety, petting it before coming back to the surface and again falling into the lake by a running camel husk. The camera then shifts to the other riders continuing the fight before Kai spots a lone zombie horse and the pig from earlier, offering them a potato only for the animals to refuse. All the riders meet at one point, including Kai on the pig, before facing down an army of camel husk jockeys, zombie horsemen, spider jockeys, and chicken jockeys. Alex then blows their goat horn and all the group starts fighting the monster army.
