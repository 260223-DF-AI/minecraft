Minecraft Five is a font. It is used on a few Minecraft.net articles and on a few clickable buttons of the Minecraft Experience: Villager Rescue website.


== Definition ==
Minecraft-Fivev2.ttf
A TrueType font, named "Minecraft Five". All letters using this font are always in lowercase.
