Marvel Avengers Skin Pack is a skin pack containing 35 skins of characters from the Avengers superhero comic book series by Marvel Comics and was available on Legacy Console Editions for Xbox consoles until December 21, 2015. Due to the Better Together Update, it was added in Bedrock Edition on September 12, 2017, although it is only available to players who owned it already when they migrated from Legacy Console Edition.
