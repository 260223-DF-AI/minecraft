Rachel Jessica Te Ao Maarama House is a New Zealand actress and director, known for her roles in films by Taika Waititi.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
In 2025, House voiced Malgosha, the main antagonist of A Minecraft Movie.


== Trivia ==
Prior to appearing in Minecraft, a character House had voiced, Gramma Tala from Moana, appeared as a skin in the Moana Character Pack and Moana 2.
