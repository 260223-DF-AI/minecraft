Shazboots! is a joke status effect in Java Edition 25w14craftmine.


== Effect ==
This effect causes the player to begin to slide around on any blocks like ice and the speed increases to around a level equivalent to Speed 15. In addition, when the player jumps, a blue bar appears that acts like a jump bar for a horse, and while flying, the player slows down a lot. The amount of time that the effect is given for is so short that it doesn't have much of an effect.
