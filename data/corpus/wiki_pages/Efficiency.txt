Efficiency is an enchantment applied to tools that increases mining efficiency.


== Obtaining ==
Tools of any material except stone or diamond can receive up to Efficiency V through the enchanting table. Stone and diamond tools can only receive up to level IV through the enchanting table, but can be given Efficiency V by combining 2 items with Efficiency IV in an anvil. Diamond tools with Efficiency V can also be found in end city, ancient city, and bastion remnant chests.
Enchanted books enchanted with a random level of Efficiency can be found in loot chests in the following structures: desert pyramids, strongholds, ocean ruins, ancient cities, woodland mansions, pillager outposts, mineshafts, monster rooms, jungle pyramids, and from vaults in trial chambers.
Enchanted books and tools with a random Efficiency level can be obtained through trading with villagers.


== Usage ==

Efficiency adds 2 mining efficiency for the first level, 5 for the second level, 10 for the third level, 17 for the fourth level, and 26 for the fifth level. The proper tool for a block must be used in order to receive the speed benefit, as the enchantment has no effect if the block is mined with the incorrect tool.


== Mining efficiency ==
Each level above zero gives (Level^2)+1 Mining Efficiency, added on to the Base Mining Efficiency.
