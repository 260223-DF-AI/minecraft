3.2.13 is an update to the Launcher released on October 7, 2024.


== Changes ==
Updated the left navigation to make it easier to know what page you're on & to find your way around the Launcher.
Added link in Help menu to Minecraft's WhatsApp channel.
Offline Mode bug fixes to make offline play more accessible & reliable.
