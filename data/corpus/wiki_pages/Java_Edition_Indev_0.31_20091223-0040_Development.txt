Several unreleased builds of post-Classic/pre-Indev showcased by Notch on his blog are labelled as 0.30. These are different from the Classic 0.30 Creative and Survival versions and show the testing of features to be included in Indev.


== MD3 mob models test (December 7, 2009) ==

Word of Notch post; YouTube video Archive 

MD3 player model test.
In Survival mode.
MD3 Test Mobs added, using models from the game Quake 3 Arena as placeholders.
The player's fist no longer displays.
The top layer of water is no longer fully bright.


== Shadow test (December 19, 2009) ==

Word of Notch post

Added circular shadows underneath mobs and players.
Rana first seen, presumably with working animations likely due to this version being uncompiled.
When playing in Survival, the player now spawns with 99 stone, 99 water and 99 lava.[verify] This persists into early Indev.


== Finite water (December 19, 2009) ==

Word of Notch post

Added a primitive form of finite water.
Water would only spread sideways if the entire layer below it was a solid lake.
Later removed due to buggy behavior.
Water now has an animated side texture.


== Dynamic lighting testing (December 19, 2009) ==

Word of Notch post

Early work started on dynamic lighting.
Per-block light value implemented, but blocks cannot yet emit or reflect light.
Issue: Fog in caves appears white.


== Wall-hack protection (December 19, 2009) ==

Word of Notch post

Implemented a form of wall-hack protection.
Server checks if there is enough space to walk through an area.
Due to latency issues, the player gets teleported back sometimes if the player runs past another player who managed to place a tile in front of the player.


== Dynamic lighting (December 22, 2009) ==

Dynamic lighting added.
The player can die in Creative mode.
Rana spawning has apparently been disabled.
