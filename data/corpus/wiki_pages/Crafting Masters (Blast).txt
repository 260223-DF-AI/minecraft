Crafting Masters, also known as  Craft Masters, is a tiered reward system within Minecraft Blast that requires players to break a certain number of yellow blocks in order to unlock a single reward. Once a reward has been unlocked, the player can break more blocks to unlock more booster rewards. A time limit (2 days) is tied into this reward system, not breaking enough yellow blocks within the allotted time frame prevents players from achieving the grand prize which is a chest that gives various rewards.
As of now Crafting Masters resets to tier 1 once all rewards have been obtained, allowing the player to continuously obtain rewards until the time limit runs out.
