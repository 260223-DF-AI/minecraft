Alpha v1.0.1, also known as Seecret Friday 3, is the second version and first Java Edition Alpha version of the Seecret Updates, released on July 2, 2010, in which, various items added and changes were made. It is the first version truly named "Alpha" as Alpha v1.0.0 was only called "Alpha" retroactively upon the release of v1.0.1.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Iron door

Can only be controlled by redstone power.
Crafted with iron ingots.

 Lever

Can be switched to provide redstone power.
Crafted with a stick and cobblestone.

  Redstone

Replaces existing gears in a world as they share the same numeric ID of 55.
Redstone as a placed block has two power appearances; either completely on, or completely off.
At the time, it was not given a name.
 Redstone ore

Redstone ore mined using an iron pickaxe or higher drops 4 or 5 redstone dust.
The mining time of redstone ore takes far longer compared to other ores (4.5 seconds with iron pickaxe).
  Redstone torch

Can provide redstone power.
Redstone torches emit flame particles when lit.
Crafted with redstone dust and a stick.

 Button

Can be pushed to provide redstone power.
Crafted with stone.

 Stone pressure plate

Can be pushed to provide redstone power.
Can detect only players and mobs.
Crafted with stone.

 Wooden pressure plate

Can be pushed to provide redstone power.
Can detect all entities.
Crafted with oak planks.


=== General ===

Added reddust and largeexplode.
