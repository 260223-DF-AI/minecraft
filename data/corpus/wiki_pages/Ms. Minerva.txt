Ms. Minerva is a teacher at Woodsword Middle School, and a supporting character in Minecraft: Woodsword Chronicles. She spends much of the story introducing various subjects to the heroes, her students, but gradually gets involved in the overarching conflict.


== Appearance ==
Minerva is a light skinned woman of average height. Her hair appears to be blonde and curly, tied up into a fluffy ponytail. Her wardrobe consists of a flannel jacket, a T-shirt with a lightning bolt, ballerina shoes, a long skirt, and several small bracelets on her left wrist.
Using a pair of VR goggles, she is able to take on the persona of the Librarian. This avatar resembles a villager version of herself, with similar hair and glasses, but a longer (if noticeably thinner) nose and the attire of a plains villager.
During her time on vacation in Greece, she wore Greek cultural attire.​[more information needed]
