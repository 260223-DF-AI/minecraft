0.1 is the first released version of MinecraftEdu. It is based on Java Edition Beta 1.6.6.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Steppe dirt

Steppe dirt blocks are used for the feature Desertification.
 Steppe sand

Steppe sand blocks are used for the feature Tropification.


=== Gameplay ===
Desertification.
Afforestation.
Tropification.


=== General ===
Ability to change the language between Finnish and English.
Ability to pick a username and gender before playing a world.
Added a direct option to decide between WASD and Arrow keys.
