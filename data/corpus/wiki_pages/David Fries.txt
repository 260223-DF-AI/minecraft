David Fries is the quality manager at Mojang Studios in Washington, U.S.


== About ==
David is a Microsoft veteran, with 15 years of experience in testing and quality assurance across 8 different products and 2 countries. He has 5 years of leadership experience, and is fluent in Japanese with certificate.
Previously between September 2016 and January 2018, for 1 year and 5 months, Fries worked as a senior software engineering lead.
