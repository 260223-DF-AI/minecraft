0.0.22a is a version of Java Edition Classic released on June 29, 2009, at 21:56 UTC, which added sounds and music by C418.
0.0.22a was reuploaded at 21:58 UTC (2 minutes after release) to fix players not being able to connect to servers.


== Additions ==


=== Gameplay ===
Sounds

Grass, gravel, stone, and wood sounds. Used as footsteps and breaking sounds.
Grass sounds were used for dirt, leaves, sponge, cloth.
Gravel sounds were used for sand, gravel.
Stone sounds were used for stone, cobblestone, gold ore, iron ore, coal ore. High pitched stone sounds were used for glass, block of gold.
Wood sounds were used for planks, wood.
Added unused wood click.ogg sound.
Music

Added music by C418: calm1.ogg, calm2.ogg, calm3.ogg, calm4.ogg (duplicate of calm2.ogg)
