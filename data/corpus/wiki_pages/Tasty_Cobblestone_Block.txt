The Tasty Cobblestone Block (Japanese: , Hepburn: Oishi Maruishi Burokku?) is a giant, food-filled cobblestone block. It is created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Tasty Cobblestone Block resembles a regular block of cobblestone.


== Functionality ==
The Tasty Cobblestone Block is designed as a trap for silverfish, particularly the Replicating Silverfish's swarm. Since silverfish naturally love cobblestone, the block's appearance draws them in, where they discover the food inside and can't help but crowd around to eat it.
