Minecraft Campfire Tales Skin Pack is a trailer for the Campfire Tales Skin Pack, hosted by Logdotzip.


== Summary ==
At night in a taiga, Logdotzip invites the audience around a campfire to listen to campfire tales he is about to tell. He warns them that the stories are "rather grim" while informing them of the skin pack. He then starts telling the backstory of various characters in the form of poetry.
In Crisp's tale, Logdotzip warns that "baking is not a race". At the same time, a baker crafts multiple recipes in his bakery, before accidentally entering his own furnace and catching fire. He soon gets his face covered in soot, resulting in Crisp. As Alex is seen crafting "three cookies in a row", Crisp vengefully appears behind them and pushes them in a hole where they get "bur[ied] in dough" as they are surrounded by dispensers shooting cookies.
In Bedlam's tale, Jesse sleeps in their bed, before Bedlam somehow emerges and breaks all the torches in the base. It slowly approaches Jesse as the latter's vision distorts in order to claim their soul.
In The Headless Haunter's tale (incorrectly refered to as "Headless Hunter"), Steve in royal clothes watch in envy as their friend builds a complex redstone contraption. Soon, a bunch of TNT explodes in front of Steve, leaving them headless. On the following rainy night, Logdotzip then warns that The Headless Haunter is told to seek out prideful players to "put [them] in the ground" as a revenge for making them jealous. At the same time, The Headless Haunter is seen filling a grave with stone in a taiga graveyard.
In The Sea-Swallowed Captain's tale, a large ship in the distance sinks into the sea after lightning struck it during a storm. The ship is told to house The Sea-Swallowed Captain, who drowned after the wreckage and was reborn to seek out people to join her crew only to drown along.
In the Lumbering Jack's tale, Steve and Alex enter their base in the taiga. The Lumbering Jack then rises out of the ground to seek vengeance with his axe to anyone, specifically the owners of the base.
In The Well Wisher's tale, a girl throws a penny (a gold nugget) into a well to make a wish as she was told before. She soon falls in after Steve pushes her, and transforms into The Well Wisher, a vengeful spirit who seeks out other souls as an appeasement.
In the Arisen Rose's tale, it is unknown about what happened to her before she died, but all that is known is that a rose bush grows on her grave, as shown by her standing in a field of roses. Whenever her specter is sighted, her bones are entwined in roses.
In Ol' Diggy's tale, the sound of his pickaxe is always heard across caves as he still mines the ores. However, whenever there is a light source, Ol' Diggy disappears, but he is always searching for his "haul".
In Rancid Anne's tale, a starving Anne searches for pigs and cows as a source for food across a desert, to no avail. With her limbs growing weak, she finds rotten flesh in a desert pyramid and finds it rather tasty in her viewpoint. With her newfound love of rotten flesh, she keeps seeking out rot and waste for food.
