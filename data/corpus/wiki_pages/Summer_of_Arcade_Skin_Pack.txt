The Summer of Arcade Skin Pack was a free skin pack, featuring 15 skins of characters from video games that were part of the 2012 Summer of Arcade event on the Xbox Live Arcade. It was available exclusively in Xbox 360 Edition for a limited time between July 18 and August 22, 2012 and can no longer be downloaded. During the aforementioned period, the skin pack could be downloaded through a promotion on the Xbox 360's dashboard.
