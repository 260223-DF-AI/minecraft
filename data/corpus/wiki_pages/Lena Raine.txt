Lena Raine (/ˈleɪ.nə/ LAY-nuh or /ˈlɛ.nə/ LEN-uh), also known as Lena Chappelle or kuraine, is a composer, producer, and video game developer who composed tracks for the Nether Update, Caves & Cliffs Part II, The Wild Update and Tricky Trials. She also made the music discs, Pigstep, otherside, Creator and Creator (Music Box). All of these songs are featured in her albums Minecraft: Nether Update (Original Game Soundtrack), Minecraft:  Caves & Cliffs (Original Game Soundtrack), Minecraft:  The Wild Update (Original Game Soundtrack), and Minecraft: Tricky Trials (Original Game Soundtrack). Outside of Minecraft, Raine is known for creating the soundtracks of games such as Celeste, Guild Wars 2, and Chicory: A Colorful Tale.


== Biography ==
Lena Raine was exposed to music at a young age through her participation in a youth choir and her father, who was a violinist. She was introduced to MIDI arrangement through a Sonic fan community, where she began recreating songs she already knew and then eventually creating original music. She attended Cornish College of the Arts and got a degree in music composition. Raine is well known for her work on the soundtracks of Celeste, Deltarune, Guild Wars 2 and  Chicory: A Colorful Tale. In 2019, she was nominated for a British Academy of Film and Television Arts award and won the American Society of Composers, Authors and Publishers Video Game Score of the Year. She is transgender.
