16w39b is the eighth snapshot for Java Edition 1.11, released on September 29, 2016 to fix some bugs and crashes from 16w39a.


== Changes ==
Flower pots

Player can now remove the potted plant from the pot, by pressing use.
Llamas

Drops are now affected by Looting.
