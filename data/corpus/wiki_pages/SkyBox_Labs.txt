SkyBox Labs is a Canadian game development studio based out of Burnaby, British Columbia, Canada. The studio was brought on to assist with bringing Bedrock Edition to Nintendo Switch and PlayStation 4, as well as implementing VR support to the latter.
On January 6, 2023, NetEase Games announced that they had acquired SkyBox Labs for an undisclosed amount.


== Games ==
SkyBox Labs has published the following original games:

TASTEE: Lethal Tactics (PC, 2016)
Stela (PC/ONE/NSwitch/iOS, 2019-2020)
They have also ported/remade/co-developed the following games: 

Age of Empires II: The Forgotten (PC, 2013)
Age of Mythology: Extended Edition (PC/OSX, 2014)
Rise of Nations Extended Edition (PC, 2014)
EA Sports UFC (ONE/PS4/iOS/Android, 2014-2015)
Project Spark (PC/ONE, 2014)
Protocol Zero VR (GearVR, 2015)
Grandia II Anniversary Edition (PC, 2015)
Age of Empires II HD: The African Kingdoms (PC, 2015)
Halo 5: Guardians (ONE, 2015)
Age of Mythology: Tale of the Dragon (PC/OSX, 2016)
Halo 5: Forge (PC, 2016)
Age of Empires II HD: Rise of the Rajas (PC, 2016)
Minecraft: Bedrock Edition (2017)
Fallout 76 (PC/ONE/PS4, 2018)
Magic: The Gathering Arena (PC/macOS, 2019-2020)
Halo Infinite (PC/Xbox, 2021)
Destiny 2: Lightfall (PC/PlayStation/Xbox, 2023)
