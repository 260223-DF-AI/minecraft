Tall dry grass is the tall variant of dry grass. Unlike other tall variants of foliage, this block is only one-block tall. It generates frequently in deserts and all types of badlands, and looks like a more full version of short dry grass. It also grows on sand and terracotta, and can be eaten by sheep.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Tall dry grass can be broken instantly with any item or by hand. If broken with shears or any tool enchanted with Silk Touch it drops itself, otherwise it drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Tall dry grass generates in deserts and badlands.


=== Post generation ===
Using bone meal on short dry grass turns it into tall dry grass.


=== Trading ===
Wandering traders may sell 1 tall dry grass for 1 emerald.


=== Mob loot ===
In Bedrock Edition, wandering traders have a chance of 8.5% (increased by 1% per level of Looting) to drop 1 tall grass when killed while showing the item as trade offer, if the player holds an emerald.


== Usage ==
Tall dry grass can be used for aesthetic decoration. Tall dry grass can be placed on any of the following blocks:

In Bedrock Edition, tall dry grass can be snowlogged.
When a sheep eats tall dry grass, the block is removed, and a sheared sheep regrows its wool. Baby sheep graze tall dry grass 20 times more often than adults and mature 1 minute faster when grazing.


=== Fuel ===
When used in a furnace as fuel, a piece of tall dry grass lasts 5 seconds (smelting half an item).


=== Composting ===
Placing tall dry grass into a composter has a 30% chance of raising the compost level by 1, the lowest chance possible. On average, a stack of tall dry grass yields 2.74 bone meal.


=== Bone meal ===
Bone meal can be used on tall dry grass to place a short dry grass on a neighboring block if possible.


=== Ambience ===
If placed on sand, red sand or a terracotta block of any kind with at least one more corresponding block underneath it, tall dry grass occasionally produces ambient sounds reminiscent of wind howling. These ambient sounds can play anywhere if the given conditions are met, and naturally play in the badlands and desert biomes due to the abundance of dry grass on top of said blocks.
