Sarah Boeving is an artist at Mojang Studios working on Minecraft.


== Work on Minecraft ==
Her earliest known work was on the textures for the wolf variants introduced in Armored Paws. Boeving created five of the paintings introduced in Tricky Trials, those five being "Meditative", "Prairie Ride", "Baroque", "Humble", and "Unpacked". Following this, she designed the pale oak door texture, and the "Dennis" painting introduced in Chase the Skies.
