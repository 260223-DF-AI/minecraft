18w32a is the fourth snapshot for Java Edition 1.13.1, released on August 8, 2018, which adds dead coral and some other features, changes some existing features, optimizes performance, and fixes many bugs.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Dead coral

Dead variants of corals.
When the live coral is placed on land it'll now turn dead.
Can be placed on top of blocks.


=== Gameplay ===
Statistics

New statistics:
"Damage Dealt (Absorbed)"  damage_dealt_absorbed
"Damage Dealt (Resisted)"  damage_dealt_resisted
"Damage Blocked By Shield"  damage_blocked_by_shield
"Damage Absorbed"  damage_absorbed
"Damage Resisted"  damage_resisted
"Shulker Box Cleaned"  clean_shulker_box


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Coral

Can now be placed in air.
Unless adjacent to water, they turn into dead coral after a short moment.
Purpur stairs and slabs

Can now be crafted using purpur pillars.


=== Items ===
Bone meal

When used in water of warm ocean biomes, it now has a chance to grow coral fans.


=== General ===
Performance has been optimized.
