Beta 1.11.0.10 is the eighth and final beta version for Bedrock Edition 1.11.0, released on April 11, 2019, which optimizes performance around villages and fixes bugs.


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Optimizations

Optimized the use of target selectors.
Made various performance optimizations around villages.
Seed picker

Removed most content in the seed picker with 7 original seeds remaining, added 11 more.


== Fixes ==
3 bugs fixed

Fixed villagers wandering constantly when there is no village nearby. (MCPE-43958)
Fixed a crash that sometimes occurred after opening the skin picker.
Fixed local multiplayer games not being playable between Nintendo Switch devices after playing a regular multiplayer game.
