The MINECON 2016 Cape is a cape given to MINECON 2016 attendees.


== Distribution ==


=== Java Edition ===
In Java Edition, this cape was given to MINECON 2016 attendees who redeemed an emailed link, provided they have scanned their ticket at the venue.


=== Bedrock Edition ===

In Bedrock Edition, this cape was available in skin packs. These skin packs featured Steve and Alex skins with all of the MINECON capes. These capes are locked to the skin pack and cannot be equipped with other skins, so they are not considered to be fully available on this edition.
From September 23, 2016 to October 3, 2016 in Legacy Console Edition and from September 22, 2016 to October 7, 2016 in Bedrock Edition, the MINECON 2016 Cape was available for free with the MINECON 2016 Skin Pack.
