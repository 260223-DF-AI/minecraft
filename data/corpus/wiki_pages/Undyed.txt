Some dyeable materials in the game have specific, dedicated undyed variants, in addition to being dyeable in 16 colors.


== Minecraft ==
Terracotta – Undyed variant of Stained Terracotta
Glass – Undyed variant of Stained Glass.
Glass Pane – Undyed variant of Stained Glass Panes.
Shulker Box – Undyed variant of the Shulker Box.
Candle – Undyed variant of Candles.
Bundle – Undyed variant of Bundles.
Hardened Glass‌[edu  only] – Undyed variant of Hardened Stained Glass.
Hardened Glass Pane‌[edu  only] – Undyed variant of Hardened Stained Glass Panes.
