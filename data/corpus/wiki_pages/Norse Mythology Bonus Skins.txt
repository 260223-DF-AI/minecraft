Norse Mythology Skins is a skin pack that includes 4 skins themed around Nordic mythology and was created by 4J Studios. It is an addition to the Norse Mythology Mash-Up, which launched earlier in the same month, and was released as the fifth gift in the "12 Days of Minecraft" Marketplace promotion.
