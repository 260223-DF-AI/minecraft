"Floating Trees" is a song composed by C418 that plays on the main menu.


== Locations ==


== Track description ==

"Floating Trees" is a warm, ethereal, and calming ambient track mostly played on an organ, accompanied by bright synths and backed by low strings, all playing on the same key as "Minecraft".
There are some very quiet chiming sounds in the background near the end. The ending, which is softly played on a distant, echoing piano, is a callback to the ending melody of "Subwoofer Lullaby".

=== Raw music files ===
In Java Edition, the music files can be found in .minecraft/assets/objects.
In Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Education, it can be found in:

Mobile: com.mojang/resource_packs/music/vanilla_music/sounds/music/menu/
Windows: %PROGRAMFILES%\WindowsApps\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP_<version>_x64_8wekyb3d8bbwe\data\resource_packs\vanilla_music\sounds\music\menu
All music files are in Ogg Vorbis.
