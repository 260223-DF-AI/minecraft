Cameron Manning (born February 22, 2004), better known by his username camman18, is an American YouTuber and Twitch streamer known for his vertical-oriented videos about Minecraft. Manning made an apperance in Minecraft Live  March 2026.


== Background ==
Manning makes short-form videos uploaded to YouTube and TikTok about the game. He uploads consistently with a video every day. In these videos, Manning makes videos of the following:

Rarities of certain features (e.g. rarest item, rarest mob, etc.).
Challenges, often speedrunning a certain item with custom world modifications, or verbal challenges such as his most popular video as example, "Minecraft, But I Can't Use The Letter O"
New features added, or speculation for future updates
Obscure facts about the game, or debunking features
Discussion of community-made creations
Odd seed generations and community challenges regarding seed searching
Playing challenges with his community
Manning is known for his constant suggestion for faster minecarts. When interviewed in 2025, Manning asked Anna Lundgren regarding minecarts and the possibility of change with the Minecart Improvements experiment. Lundgren responded his big awareness of his suggestion in the community and said anything is possible, though she could not reveal anything at the moment.
In 2026, during snapshot releases of Tiny Takeover, Manning made a video introducing the golden dandelion. In the video, Manning says, "Then we got the Golden Dandelion, which is a golden dandelion". The quote became a meme across social media, primarily TikTok. During The Deep Dig in Minecraft Live  March 2026, Vu Bui asked Manning about the meme. 


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
In 2025, Manning uploaded the video "I Interviewed Mojang...", where he interviewed Anna Lundgren, the senior product manager of Mojang Studios. In the interview, Manning asked Lundgren questions about their game philosophy, such as feature rarity, the line between realism and fantasy in game design, difficulty balancing for casual and complex players, balancing quantity over quality in updates. Manning also asked about the nautilus and whether it was planned during or after the addition of the nautilus shell. Manning also asked about the possibilty of faster minecarts.


=== Events ===
In late 2024, Manning was invited as a VIP in opening of the Minecraft Experience: Villager Rescue event in Plano, Texas.
In 2026, Manning was invited to be a special guest in Minecraft Live  March 2026 during The Deep Dig. He was revealed as a guest from a TikTok posted by Minecraft revealing all the guests. Manning additionally teased himself on his channel with the title, "i have a secret... #MinecraftPartner".
During the aftershow, Manning played the Chaos Cubed game drop with Vu Bui, Agnes, and Jeb, playing mini-games using the sulfur cube.
