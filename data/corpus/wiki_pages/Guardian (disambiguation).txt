=== Mobs ===
Guardian, an aquatic hostile mob.

Elder Guardian Ghost, an invisible mob in Bedrock Edition used to render the elder guardian particle
Warden, a mob that spawns in in the deep dark and dispite it's name having a similar definition, the warden is not related to guardians
