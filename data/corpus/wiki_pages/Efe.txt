Efe is one of the nine default skins players can use in Minecraft, and the fifth to be introduced. They were first introduced in the cinematic What Will You Uncover? with Sunny, and has since become one of the main protagonists of the franchise.


== Appearance ==
Efe is typically depicted with slim arms, and brown skin. They have a distinctive purple haircut, white eyes, garnet pupils, and brown lips. Notably, Efe is adorned with many gold trimmed items, including a gold earring, gold bracelet, gold belt buckle, and gold trimmed boots. Their outfit is a bluish-white cardigan on top of a black blouse, with high raised dark purple leather pants.
In the character creator, Efe has approximately eight items, the most of any default character.

== Trivia ==
In the YouTube short WHAT WOULD YOU DO?, Efe appears to have a voice, speaking with a masculine, young voice.
In another short, FIRST TIME IN THE DEEP DARK, Efe is depicted with a gruff voice.
Despite not being usable as a skin in the map, Planet Earth III's marketing uses Efe.
In June 2025, Efe appeared on a button for the Marketplace, promoting the introduction of the dynamic world Better on Bedrock to Marketplace Pass.
Efe is one of two default skins for which Mojang uses the pronoun "they" in the descriptions of images on minecraft.net. The other one is Noor.
