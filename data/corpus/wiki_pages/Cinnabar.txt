Cinnabar is a decorative stone variant block that naturally generates in sulfur caves alongside sulfur.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Cinnabar drops as an item if mined by any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, it drops nothing.


== Usage ==


=== Crafting ingredient ===
Cinnabar can be crafted into several decorative variants.

== Trivia ==
In real life, cinnabar is a toxic red ore of mercury and sulfur, historically used as a primary ore for mercury and as a red dye.
