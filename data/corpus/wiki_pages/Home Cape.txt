The Home Cape is a cape that was given out as a promotion for A Minecraft Movie, alongside the Menace Cape and the Yearn Cape.


== Appearance ==
The cape's design depicts the Earth portal, as shown in A Minecraft Movie, with a grass base and a bright blue backdrop.
The elytra design depicts the movie's version of Steve, as portrayed by Jack Black.


== Distribution ==
Players could receive this cape by redeeming a code, which could be obtained by watching any Minecraft livestream on Twitch for three minutes. This could be done between March 18, 2025 and April 6, 2025.
This task could not be completed normally in regions where either Twitch or its rewards program were unavailable. Players in these regions could obtain the cape by using a proxy/VPN, traveling to regions where they were available, or receiving a code from another player.
