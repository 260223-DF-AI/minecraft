1.6.4 Build 10 is the tenth build for MinecraftEdu 1.6.4, released in 2014. It is based on Java Edition 1.6.4.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Read/Write test when starting Launcher to ensure everything will work.

== Fixes ==
2 bugs fixed

Empty tooltip showing in mods GUI.
Students couldn't always use some blocks or items like turtles and mailboxes. Now they are usable with an empty hand.
