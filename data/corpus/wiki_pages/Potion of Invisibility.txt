A potion of Invisibility is a potion that provides Invisibility when used.

=== Brewing ===


=== Mob loot ===
Wandering traders have an 8.5% chance (increased by 1% per level of Looting) of dropping a potion of Invisibility if they die while they are drinking the potion, or holding the potion as a trade offer.‌[Bedrock Edition  only]


=== Trading ===
Wandering traders can sell extended potions of Invisibility for 5 emeralds.
