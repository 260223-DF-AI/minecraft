A potion of Weakness is a potion that provides Weakness when used.


== Obtaining ==


=== Brewing ===


=== Thrown projectile ===
A witch has a 25% chance of throwing a splash potion of Weakness if a player is 3 or less blocks away and does not already have the Weakness effect. Also, the player's health has to be at 8HP or less, or the player has to have the Poison effect.
The thrown potion cannot be picked up, but it can cause the Weakness effect.


=== Generated loot ===

One splash potion of Weakness can be found in the brewing stand inside every igloo basement.
In Bedrock Edition, the cauldron in a swamp hut contains 1 to 2 bottles of a random potion. There is a 10% for it to be a potion of Weakness.

=== Curing zombie villagers ===

A golden apple and a splash potion of Weakness can be used to convert a zombie villager into a regular villager.
