The Frostgate (氷河期の門, Hyōgaki no Mon?) is a large door that releases a torrent of snow and ice when opened, created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Frostgate is a large door with a pointed top. The door itself has a snowflake design across it, and ice grows from the ground where it is placed.


== Functionality ==
When opened, the Frostgate releases a blast of snow and ice, even in the Nether. This freezing blast was enough to instantly freeze the Blue-Flame Blaze, who was covered in lava at the time.


=== Crafting recipe ===


== History ==
Nico White crafted the Frostgate when battling the Blue-Flame Blaze, once they had cooled the blaze down with lava. It instantly froze the mob, killing it and causing its Blaze Rods to drop.
