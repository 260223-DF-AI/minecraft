v0.11.1 alpha is a minor update to Minecraft Pocket Edition released to fix bugs regarding world crashes and unavailable skin packs.
This version was reuploaded in an unknown date.[more information needed]


== Changes ==


=== Gameplay ===
Health

Can no longer be filled by exiting and entering the game.


== Fixes ==
5 issues fixed
 From released versions before 0.11.0

MCPE-7879  Missing Items after save and reduced file size.
MCPE-7929  No warning when a creeper is about to explode on LAN
 From the 0.11.0 development versions

MCPE-8349  Other players in local multiplayer cannot sail in a boat.
MCPE-8656  Health restored to full every time the player loads their game.
MCPE-8773  Players other than the host spawn at the highest point of the spawn when on multiplayer.
Other

Fixed bugs regarding world crashes and unavailable skin packs.
