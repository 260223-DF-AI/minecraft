A granite wall is a decorative wall variant of granite that generates in desert villages and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Granite walls can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===

Each desert village armorer building generates with 2 granite walls as the top part of its chimney.

=== Note blocks ===
Granite walls can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
