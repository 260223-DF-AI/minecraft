MINECON 2010 was a gathering of Minecraft fans on a Java Edition Classic server from May 30 to June 1, 2010, to celebrate the first anniversary of the game's initial Classic release.


== Event ==
The event was organized through a Minecraft Forum thread, which served as the unofficial hub for the server. Activities included:

A spleef tournament.
A building competition.
An obstacle course race (a planned griefing competition was canceled).
A signature wall where 167 participants, including Notch, left their marks.
The server world was made available for download after the event and is compatible with Classic server 1.10.1 and Classic 0.30c.
