2nd Birthday Skin Pack, named 2nd Birthday Skin Pack - 2015 in Bedrock Edition, is a free skin pack that includes 24 party-themed skins celebrating the second anniversary of Legacy Console Edition's release on PlayStation consoles. It was originally available on PlayStation 3,  PlayStation 4, and PlayStation Vita editions from December 18 to December 29, 2015.
Due to the release of Bedrock Edition on PlayStation 4, it was added to the Marketplace on December 9, 2019, although it is only available to players on PlayStation 4 who owned it already when they migrated from Legacy Console Edition.
