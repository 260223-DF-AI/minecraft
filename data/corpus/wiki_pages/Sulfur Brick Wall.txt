A sulfur brick wall is a decorative wall variant of sulfur bricks.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Sulfur brick walls drop as an item if mined by any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.

=== Stonecutting ===


== Usage ==

Unlike most other walls, mobs can be leashed to sulfur brick walls similar to fences.‌[Bedrock Edition  only]
