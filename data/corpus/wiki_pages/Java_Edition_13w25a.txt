13w25a is the fifteenth snapshot for Java Edition 1.6.1, released on June 17, 2013.


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
General

Mobs named "Dinnerbone" or "Grumm" are now flipped upside down.


=== Items ===
Golden apples

Absorption time has been increased from 1 minute and 30 seconds to 2 minutes.
