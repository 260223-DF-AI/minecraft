The Potato, also called the Poisonous Potato Dimension, Potato Dimension or the MegaSpud dimension, is a joke dimension added in the 24w14potato snapshot.


== Accessing ==
To access the Potato, player must enter a potato portal. This can be done in Survival by finding a pedestal. Ruined portatols, located by throwing potato eyes in the Overworld, have a pedestal, and strongholds always have a pedestal in them. Many villages also generate with pedestals.
Using a poisonous potato on the pedestal creates a potato portal, if the block above the pedestal is air or a replaceable block, like water or snow.


== Traits ==

The Potato is composed entirely of large floating islands that can generate 5 different potato-based biomes.
Blocks can exist between Y=0 and Y=256. Just like in the End, there is no floor or ceiling, and players can easily fall into the void if not careful enough. When that happens, a special version of the end poem is displayed.

When the poem finishes (or is skipped), the player launches high above their normal spawn point, wearing poisonous pota-toes.
Any other entity falling into the void doesn't take void damage, and keeps falling indefinitely instead.
Daylight cycle in the Potato occurs as normal (clocks can be used), but the sun has a different texture, slightly resembling a poisonous potato. Clouds and weather exist as well: it never snows, but rain and thunder can occur.  Rain has green tint and it is visible only below Y=110, in all biomes except the hash biome. Rain is poisonous in the wasteland biome.
Both water and lava behave just like in the Overworld, with the exception of wasteland biome, where water is poisonous. Beds work normally, while respawn anchors explode, also mimicking the Overworld. However piglins, piglin brutes and hoglins don't get zombified in the Potato, unlike the Overworld.

=== Terrain features ===
All types of Overworld trees, as well as huge mushrooms, can generate in this dimension. There also exists a tree native only to this dimension, the potato tree. Normal potato plants can be found commonly in any biome (especially in fields biome), growing on peelgrass, corrupted peelgrass, poison farmland or gravtater. Most flowers also can generate in the Potato.
Amethyst geodes and potato geodes can generate too, as well as monster rooms and hash wells, which are variants of desert wells.
Besides the normal clouds, a cloud-like blobs of floatato blocks generate between the islands. Rarely a tall spire of terre de pomme, known as "large potatostone", appears on the surface of this dimension.


=== Mobs ===
Many Overworld mobs can spawn in the Potato, with most of them having a different potato-like texture variant, specific to this dimension. These include skeletons, strays, creepers, endermen, spiders, villagers, cows, chickens, sheep, pigs, bees, and armadillos.
Other Overworld mobs spawn, but don't have a potato-like texture: these include cats, horses, iron golems, bats, witches, zombies, phantoms, slimes, and glow squids.
There are also mobs native to the Potato that are separate entities: poisonous potato zombies, batatos, toxifin slabs, plaguewhale slabs, and the mega spud, which is the main boss of this dimension.
Piglin brutes, ghasts, and giants can spawn during the battle with mega spud. Giants spawned in this dimension have the skin of Mojang Studios developer Jay Wells.
For more details on potato-themed mobs, see Potato Mob.
