This page lists the unused content in the Super Mario Mash-up.


== Unused content ==


=== Illusioner ===
While the illusioner is absent in Bedrock Edition, they still have a texture resembling a Magikoopa.


=== Spectral Arrow ===
While the spectral arrow is absent in Bedrock Edition, it still has a texture.


=== Killer Bunny ===
While the killer bunny is absent in Bedrock Edition, they still have a texture resembling a Nabbit.


=== Knowledge book ===
While the knowledge book is absent in Bedrock Edition, it still has a texture.


=== Portfolio ===
While the portfolio is exclusive to Minecraft Education, it still has a texture.


=== Ruby ===
While the ruby is unused, it still has a texture resembling Bowser's icon.


== Changed content ==


=== Ender Dragon ===
The ender dragon was at one point textured to resemble Hooktail instead of Gobblegut.


=== Pig ===
The pig was at one point textured to resemble Midbus instead of a model exclusive to the pack.


=== Horse ===
The horse was at one point textured to resemble Yoshi instead of a horse.


=== Ender Chest ===
The ender chest was at one point textured to resemble Mr. I instead of a Shy Guy.


=== Shulker ===
The shulker is textured to resemble the Piranha Creeper, but had at one point a different texture.
