A stone brick wall is a decorative wall variant of stone bricks that generates in ruined portals and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Stone brick walls can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Stone brick walls may generate in ruined portals in the Overworld.

=== Stonecutting ===


== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Stone brick walls can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
