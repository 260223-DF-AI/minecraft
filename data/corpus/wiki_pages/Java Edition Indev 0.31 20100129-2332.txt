A version of Indev 0.31 was released on January 29, 2010, at 23:32 UTC, which added new tools and items.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
  Workbench

Pressing use on a workbench opens a 3×3 crafting grid.
Broken faster by swords.
Crafted with 4 planks.


=== Items ===
 Bowl

Used to make mushroom stew.
Crafted with 3 planks.

 Golden swords, axes, pickaxes and shovels

Only the golden sword is craftable, with 2 gold ingots and a stick.

 Mushroom stew

New food item.
Restores 8HP health.
Crafted with a bowl, a brown and a red mushroom.


=== Gameplay ===
Crafting

Added 2×2 crafting grid to the player inventory.


== Changes ==

The crafting recipe has been changed; it now requires 9 diamonds instead of 4.

== Technical ==
