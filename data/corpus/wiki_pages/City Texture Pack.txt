The City Texture Pack is a texture pack that customizes the game with new textures that are themed around a modern city. Along with the Plastic Texture Pack, it was one of the first two texture packs released for Bedrock and New Nintendo 3DS editions. It is included in the Wii U and Nintendo Switch editions by default.

== Trivia ==
The “Siamese” cat in this texture pack has been changed to resemble a Sphinx cat, while “silverfish” resemble cockroaches.
The textures from this texture pack were used to create the Alien Invasion demo world which was showcased at E3 2016.
