== Usage ==
Despite being crafted with a melon slice, a glistering melon slice cannot be eaten, unlike a golden apple or golden carrot.


=== Brewing ingredient ===


=== Piglins ===
Piglins run toward any glistering melon slice item on the ground and inspect it for 6 seconds in Java Edition or 8 seconds in Bedrock Edition before putting it in their inventory.
