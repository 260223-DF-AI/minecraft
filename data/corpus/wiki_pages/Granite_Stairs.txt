Granite stairs are a decorative stairs variant of granite that generates in desert villages and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Granite stairs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Granite stairs generate as part of desert villages.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Granite stairs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
