A swamp oak is a variant of oak only found in swamps. Unlike regular and fancy oaks, swamp oaks only generate naturally and cannot be grown from oak saplings.


== Appearance ==
Swamp oaks have round, flat-topped canopies. They tend to be covered in vines that trail down to the ground. Compared to regular oaks, their canopies are 2 blocks wider. They are also generally slightly taller than regular oaks, with their trunks ranging from 5 to 8 blocks tall.


== Generation ==
Swamp oaks generate naturally only in the swamp biome. They can be found on land or in water up to 2 blocks deep.


== Blocks and items ==
These items can be obtained from a swamp oak:

Oak Log (harvested with any tool including hands)
Oak Leaves (harvested with shears)
Stick (chance of dropping when breaking leaves)
Oak Planks (crafted from a log in the inventory crafting grid or a crafting table)
Stripped Oak Log (created by using an axe on a log)
Oak Sapling (chance of dropping when breaking leaves)
Apple (chance of dropping when breaking leaves)
Vines (harvested with shears)
