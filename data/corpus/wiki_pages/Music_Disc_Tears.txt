"Tears" refers to both a song composed by Amos Roddy, and a music disc that plays the song when inserted in a jukebox. The music disc is obtained by killing ghasts by a player-deflected ghast fireball.


== Obtaining ==


=== Mob loot ===
The music disc "Tears" is dropped by ghasts when killed by a player-deflected fireball.


== Track description ==

The entire track features ghast sounds remixed.


== Usage ==
Like all music discs, using the "Tears" music disc on a jukebox causes the jukebox to play the song.
If the player places a redstone comparator beside a jukebox playing "Tears", it will emit a redstone signal of 10.
