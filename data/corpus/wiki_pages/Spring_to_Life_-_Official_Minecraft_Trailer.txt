Spring to Life - Official Minecraft Trailer is an official trailer for Spring to Life, demonstrating many of its features' mechanics.


== Summary ==
In a swamp full of fireflies, three heroes emerge riding pigs of different backgrounds. Zuri is leading, before Steve and Makena go their separate ways, Zuri going into a snowy taiga village. While there, they meet several cold mobs, and a cold chicken lays a blue egg on a cold cow's head. Several cows fight for Zuri's pig's attention. before Zuri receives a map from a cartographer.
Meanwhile, Makena is in a desert, looking down at their own map. A warm chicken jockey and several other husks try to ambush them, while a warm cow and pig admire a cactus flower. Steve is having less trouble, building a tent while it rains in an old growth birch forest, before overhearing a wandering trader. Suddenly, lightning strikes, destroying Steve's tent. In spite of this loss, Steve's pig decides to happily skedaddle about, intercut with Zuri and Makena's pigs doing the same.
Zuri ends up falling off a cliff, while Makena runs away from monsters emerging from a cave. Steve actually manages to fight several monsters in a swamp, before their friends appear. Zuri appears to get overwhelmed in the chaos, watching as several monsters attack Steve and Makena, all while the three pigs don't care at all.
