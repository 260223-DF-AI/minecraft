The Bacon Cape is a personal cape that was briefly given out.


== Distribution ==
This cape was given to Miclee by Notch for his idea of adding pigmen. The cape was made by Miclee himself.
The cape, however, was revoked due to a large influx of players asking Notch for their own capes.
