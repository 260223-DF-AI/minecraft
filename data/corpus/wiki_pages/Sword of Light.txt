The Sword of Light (光の剣, Hikari no Ken?) is a sword capable of stopping a Monster Spawner from working, created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Sword of Light is an extremely long sword that is constantly glowing.


== Functionality ==
When stabbed into a Monster Spawner, the Sword of Light prevents monsters from spawning. However, it has limited durability, and will eventually shatter.


== History ==
Some time in the past, Icco White stopped in a village with a Monster Spawner beneath it. Because of the Spawner's "Tenacity" Mod Power, it spawned monsters endless, and could not be broken; as a temporary fix, Icco crafted the Sword of Light, sticking into the Spawner and halting new monster spawns.
Eventually, Nico White made his way to the same village, as the Sword was nearly broken. While he contemplated a new solution, it finally broken, shattering into pieces and allowing hordes of monsters to spawn once again.
