Callum "Cal" Knight is an English YouTuber known for his Minecraft content, under the alias of Seapeekay.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
In 2018, he was one of the guests for MINECON Earth 2018 Pre-Show, alongside LaurenZside.
In 2024, Knight participated in the Ender Cup, under the team Cyan Coyotes. To promote the event, he appeared in the MCC x Minecraft 15th Anniversary Party, voicing himself as an NPC. The following year, he appeared at another edition of MC Championship, Colour the World, once again playing under the team Cyan Coyotes. In 2025, he would participate in The Copper Crown, under the team Yellow Yaks.
