Diamond armor is a type of armor made from diamonds. It is the second strongest armor, between iron and netherite.

=== Mob loot ===
Zombies, husks, zombie villagers, skeletons, strays, and bogged that spawn with armor have an 8.5% chance of dropping their armor when killed by the player. The dropped armor has a random durability and retains any enchantments it has.
There is a 0.04% chance of that armor being diamond armor.

== Usage ==

Wearing diamond armor reduces damage taken from most damage types.


=== Statistics ===
When a full set of Diamond armor is worn, the set provides 20 (  10) armor points and 8 () armor toughness.


=== Damage reduction ===
Wearing armor reduces incoming damage according to two values: armor, and armor toughness. Each piece of armor provides a given armor and armor toughness value, and the values from each armor piece are added together when calculating damage reduction.
The strength of incoming damage also determines the damage reduction of worn armor, as armor does not protect in a linear fashion.


=== Upgrading ===


=== Equipping ===
Players can equip diamond armor by placing it in their inventory, using the item, or via a dispenser. When equipped, the armor is rendered. Some other humanoids can also render the armor, pick it up, equip it, and be equipped via a dispenser; see the table below.

In Java Edition, with the exception of the allay, dolphin, fox, and panda, all other mobs with the CanPickUpLoot nbt tag true (1b) can equip armor from a dispenser (except happy ghast), and pick up and equip if are without armor or have worse armor (except ender dragon). All living entities can equip armor using commands, like /item or /data.
