A cinnabar wall is a decorative wall variant of cinnabar used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Cinnabar walls drop as an item if mined by any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.

== Usage ==

Unlike most other walls, mobs can be leashed to cinnabar walls similar to fences.‌[Bedrock Edition  only]
