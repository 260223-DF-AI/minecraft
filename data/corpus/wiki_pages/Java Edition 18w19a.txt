18w19a is the thirty-third snapshot for Java Edition 1.13, released on May 8, 2018, which added the Dolphin's Grace effect and changed some IDs, mainly to biomes.


== Additions ==


=== Gameplay ===
Effects

 Dolphin's Grace.
Acquired by swimming near dolphins.
Makes the player swim faster.


=== Fluids ===
Logic

Separated some of the logic for blocks and fluids.
Current fluids:
minecraft:empty
minecraft:flowing_water
minecraft:water
minecraft:flowing_lava
minecraft:lava
The fluid at a location is currently dependent on the block at the location; currently there still are water and lava blocks.
Added two new fluid tags: minecraft:lava and minecraft:water.
This system is mostly internal; it cannot be directly interacted with yet.


=== World generation ===
Buffet worlds

New generation type: "Caves".
Creates Nether-like generation.

Swimming

All undead mobs (except for zombie horses) now sink.

Phantoms

Now flap their wings smoother and faster.
Now drop experience.
