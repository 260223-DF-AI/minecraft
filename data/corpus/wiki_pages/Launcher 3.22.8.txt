3.22.8 is an update to the Minecraft launcher released on October 28, 2025, which added "Tricks" for Halloween.


== Additions ==
Added new "Trick" button.
Is set to be "On" and "Sound on" by default.
Upon being enabled, an enderman will appear on the screen, making an enderman sound depending if sound is on or off, and will pick elements from the launcher and place them in random positions.
If the player moves their mouse or presses a keyboard key, the enderman will disappear from the screen.
If set to "Sound off", the enderman will make no sounds. If set to "Off", the enderman will not appear.
