1.9-pre4 is the fourth and final pre-release for Java Edition 1.9, released on February 26, 2016.


== Changes ==


=== Non-mob entities ===
Projectiles

Changed scaling.


=== Command format ===
Target selectors

Changed handling of r= in entity selectors.
Now based on the position of the entity and not on the center of the block anymore - meaning that r=0 only selects entities at the exact same position.


=== Gameplay ===
Traveling

Made players invulnerable during dimension changes.


== Fixes ==
4 issues fixed
From the 1.9 development versions

MC-85135  Projectiles are smaller as projectiles than they are on the ground.
MC-90711  Marker armor stands cannot be selected within r=0.
MC-96254  Player grace period missing for damage.
MC-97316  Target selector arguments r, rm are counting distance from the middle of the bottom face of the block, instead of from the position of the entity running the command.
Player entities ticking twice on servers.
Fixed an issue that can affect sound positions.
