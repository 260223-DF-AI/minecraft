== Trivia ==
To create the sounds that the dolphins and pandas make in the game he went to Kolmården Wildlife Park and Chimelong Safari Park.
To create phantoms' sounds he used the voice of his child crying.
To make the sounds for the drowned, he gurgled milk.
His in-game skin resembles American guitarist Buckethead.
