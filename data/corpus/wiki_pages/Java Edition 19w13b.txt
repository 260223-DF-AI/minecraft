19w13b is the thirtieth snapshot for Java Edition 1.14, released on March 29, 2019, which revamps the trading interface.
19w13b was reuploaded for unknown reasons with no changes except for a later build time.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Bells

Hitting a bell during a raid applies the Glowing effect to raiding illagers within 32 blocks of it.
A new sound for ringing bells during raids has been added.
Blocks of quartz

Texture changed from  to .
Stonecutters

Added recipe for chiseled stone bricks.


=== Mobs ===
Villagers

Revamped trading UI.
Available trades are listed in a left sidebar, similar to Bedrock Edition.
When players have the required materials, clicking on one of the trades puts the items into the slots automatically.


=== General ===
Entity tags

Added new raiders tag for all illagers including witches and illusioners (despite them being unused).
