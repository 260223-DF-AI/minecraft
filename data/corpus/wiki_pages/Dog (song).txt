"Dog" is a song composed by C418 that plays as part of an extension with the music disc "cat" on Legacy Console Edition.


== Track description ==
"Dog" continues off from where "cat" ends, using the same composition and melody of "cat", but with more focus on the chiptune-like synths.
"Dog" can only be heard on Legacy Console Edition once the music disc "cat" fades out. It plays for an extra two minutes, making the Legacy Console Edition version of "cat" the second longest music disc in the game, behind "blocks".
The album version fades into "Danny".
