The Mojang Studios Cape is a cape given to employees of Mojang Studios.


== Appearance ==
The cape's design reflects the logo change that came with the rebrand from Mojang to Mojang Studios. It features some of the white "mojangs" that make up their logo against a red backdrop.
The cape was designed by Johan Aronson.


== Distribution ==
This cape is given to those employed by Mojang Studios on or after July 25, 2021. The cape is kept indefinitely even if the employee leaves Mojang, unless manually revoked.


== History ==


== Trivia ==
Unlike the previous Mojang capes, this one is also given to Mojang employees on Bedrock Edition.
