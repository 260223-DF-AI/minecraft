Scott Major, better known as Smajor or Dangthatsalongname, is a Scottish YouTuber and Twitch streamer, known for co-founding MC Championship.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
In 2019, he was invited to attend the Minecraft Creator Summit. Later in the year was one of the co-hosts for MINECON Live 2019, alongside MasuoTV, Marielitai Gaming, and Shubble. Following this, he and Noxcrew would create MC Championship event, which would eventually host the Ender Cup, Colour the World, and The Copper Crown. He appears in almost every MCC event as a co-organizer.
In 2022, Smajor participated in a promotional stream for a collaboration between Lacoste and Minecraft.
In 2025, Smajor appeared as a guest at the green carpet premiere of A Minecraft Movie.


=== Events ===
In 2024, he participated in the Ender Cup, a special edition of MC Championship made for the 15th anniversary of Minecraft. To promote the event, Smajor voiced a fictionalized version of himself in the event server MCC x Minecraft 15th Anniversary Party.
