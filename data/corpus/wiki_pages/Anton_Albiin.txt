Anton Albiin is a former member of the Mojang Studios customer support team. He left the company in March 2013.


== Career ==
Customer Service Team since February 2012.
Analyst/Project Manager for a video game from July to December 2011.
Employee for events, performing various tasks according to customer demand (IT sector) from October 2010 to November 2011.
Sports coach from June 2010 to August 2010.
Studied at Stockholm University, and previously at Stockholm International College.


== Trivia ==
His Twitter handle Ett Glas Vatten means A glass of water in Swedish, and his Twitter avatar is a ghost of Pac-Man in a glass of water.
He's trilingual: he speaks Swedish, English and Spanish.
He loves video games and often shows snapshots of his acquisitions PlayStation 1 on his Twitter (deleted).
