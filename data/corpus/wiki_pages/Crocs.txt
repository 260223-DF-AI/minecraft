Crocs is an American footwear company known for producing a line of foam "clogs." In 2023, a collaboration with Minecraft released, consisting of a line of Minecraft themed shoes and a tie-in game.


== Products ==


== Game ==

On May 19, 2023, a game with two modes was released to promote the collaboration. When scanning your Minecraft Crocs, the game will start and you will be able to choose between two modes: Crocs Creator and Crocs Crossing. In Crocs Creator, the player can build their own structures from blocky Jibbitz on a small platform. In Crocs Crossing, the player must run along a straight path, collecting Jibbitz and dodging creepers and lava.


== Giveaways ==


=== Chase the Skies ===
As part of the promotion for the Chase the Skies drop, a giveaway was hosted, with one of the prizes being the Minecraft Crocs.
