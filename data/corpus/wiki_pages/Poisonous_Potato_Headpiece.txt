A poisonous potato headpiece is a joke block, added in 24w14potato.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
A poisonous potato headpiece can be mined using any item and drops itself when broken.

When a poisonous potato headpiece is pushed by a piston, it breaks off as an item.


=== Mob loot ===
Java Edition:Bedrock Edition:
A poisonous potato headpiece does not drop when a poisonous potato zombie is killed by a charged creeper's explosion; instead, a normal zombie head is dropped.


== Usage ==
Player can wear poisonous potato headpieces in the head armor slot, like a helmet. Poisonous potato zombies are neutral toward players that wear this item: they don't attack first, but they do retaliate if attacked.
