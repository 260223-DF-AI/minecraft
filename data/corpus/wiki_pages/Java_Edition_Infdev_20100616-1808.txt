A version of Minecraft Infdev was released on June 16, 2010, at 18:08 UTC.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
Springs

Now generate naturally.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Liquids

Flowing liquids now take on the appearance of smooth slopes with a flowing texture on top.
Still liquids now have a vertex offset applied, meaning that the corners of some liquid blocks can reach up to a full block in height.
Buckets can now pick up full liquids which are behind a flowing liquid block.
 Lava

Texture[verify for Java Edition] changed from .
Now generates naturally in caves, replacing all air below Y=12.
Lava now only spreads 4 blocks instead of 8.
 Water

Changed texture[verify for Java Edition] from .
Downward flowing water now pushes the player out.
Bedrock

Now generates at the bottom 5 layers of the world (Besides the bottom-most, where the infinite lava sea still generates).
Roses

Now generate again.
Dandelions

Now generate again.
 Leaves

Setting the graphics mode to Fast makes leaves appear and behave as opaque solid blocks .
With fast graphics, blocks such as Torches, Doors and Levers can be placed on leaves and mobs can spawn on top of leaves.
Red Mushrooms

Now generate again.
Brown Mushrooms

Now generate again.


=== World generation ===
General

Coastlines are now more gradual, making oceans generally smaller.
Trees no longer have logs inside of leaves.
Caves

Re-implemented caves.
Regional caves.
Some areas have more caves than others now.


== Fixes ==
1 bug fixed

The sides of the blocks no longer have a flowing water texture when viewed underwater.
