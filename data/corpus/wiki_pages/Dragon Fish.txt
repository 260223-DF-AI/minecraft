=== Raw music files ===
In Java Edition, the music files can be found in .minecraft/assets/objects.
In Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Education, it can be found in:

Mobile: com.mojang/resource_packs/music/vanilla_music/sounds/music/game/water/
Windows: %PROGRAMFILES%\WindowsApps\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP_<version>_x64_8wekyb3d8bbwe\data\resource_packs\vanilla_music\sounds\music\game\water
All music files are in Ogg Vorbis.

== Trivia ==
According to C418's manager Patrick McDermott, "Dragon Fish", along with the other Update Aquatic songs "Shuniji" and "Axolotl" have their IP rights owned by Microsoft, making them the only C418-composed songs not licensed for Minecraft.
"Dragon Fish" was featured in A Minecraft Movie, playing in a scene featuring pandas sharing a peaceful moment before being interrupted by Steve, Garrett, and Henry crashlanding with a water bucket.
