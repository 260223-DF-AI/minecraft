A fletcher is a villager that trades bows, crossbows, arrows, and archery ingredients. Fletchers wear a hat with a feather and have a quiver on the back.


== Spawning ==
An unemployed villager turns into a fletcher when an unclaimed fletching table is nearby.


=== Natural generation ===

The workstation of fletchers, the fletching table, generates in fletcher houses in villages.


== Drops ==

In Java Edition, a fletcher can drop various items by throwing them as a gift towards a nearby player with the Hero of the Village effect. The gift is randomly selected from the following list:

Arrow
Arrow of Fire Resistance
Arrow of Harming
Arrow of Healing
Arrow of Invisibility
Arrow of Leaping
Arrow of Night Vision
Arrow of Poison
Arrow of Regeneration
Arrow of Slowness
Arrow of Strength
Arrow of Swiftness
Arrow of Water Breathing
Arrow of Weakness
