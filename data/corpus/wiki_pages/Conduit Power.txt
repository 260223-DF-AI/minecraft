Conduit Power is an area-of-effect status effect given by active conduits to players touching water or rain. It grants a combination of Water Breathing, Haste, and Night Vision.


== Effect ==
Players under the effect of Conduit Power receive all of the benefits of Water Breathing, Night Vision, and Haste. This means that their breath meter does not decrease, it gets filled up even underwater, they can sleep in a waterlogged bed‌[Bedrock Edition  only], they are able to see underwater, and their mining speed increases.
Higher levels of Conduit Power can be obtained only by using commands, in which case Conduit Power X provides the same effect as Haste X, and the other two abilities are unaffected by level.
Despite the effects, Conduit Power does not stack with Water Breathing, Night Vision, or Haste. The strongest Haste effect is used, and all timers continue to count down.
In Bedrock Edition, because the Conduit Power effect lasts for 12 seconds above water, night vision is activated but does not show borders.

== Immune mobs ==
Withers and ender dragons are immune to Conduit Power.
All mobs that can breathe underwater are unaffected by Conduit Power.
