The first version of 0.25 SURVIVAL TEST is a version of Java Edition Classic released on September 3, 2009, at around 16:13 UTC. It was announced on The Word of Notch at 16:19 UTC.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Added proper saving and loading of worlds for Survival.


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
General

Hostile mobs are now more dangerous.
Mobs are now faster.
Mobs can now see the player from further away.
Mobs now anger toward the player if attacked, regardless of the distance between the player and mob (evident when shooting arrows).
Mobs now respawn over time.
Lone mobs lose health over time and eventually die.
Hostile mobs can now fight each other.
For example, if a skeleton shoots another hostile mob, that mob fights back that skeleton.
Skeletons do not fight other skeletons.
Pigs

Now drop 0–2 brown mushrooms.
Skeletons

Now fire  purple arrows.
Killing a skeleton now makes 4–9 arrows drop with random velocities around the skeleton's corpse.


=== Non-mob entities ===
 Arrows

Texture changed from .
Now it's flying on.
Player

Now spawns with 20 arrows upon world generation.
Can now damage themself with arrows.


=== Gameplay ===
Score points

Score is now displayed in the top-right corner.


== Fixes ==
2 bugs fixed

Arrows now deal damage after flying for more than 1 second.
Fixed being able to hit monsters through walls.


== Bugs ==
1 bug reported

Creeper explosions are now less powerful.


== Trivia ==
This version, same as Indev 0.31 20091223-1457, was only available for 2 minutes, what makes them the shortest lived versions across all Minecraft editions.
