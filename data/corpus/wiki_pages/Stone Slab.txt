A stone slab is a decorative slab variant of stone that generates in ruined portals and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Stone slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Stone slabs can generate in some Overworld ruined portals.

=== Note blocks ===
Stone slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
