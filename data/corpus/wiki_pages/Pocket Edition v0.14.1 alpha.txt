v0.14.1 alpha is a minor update to Pocket Edition and the Windows 10 Edition Beta released on April 5-7, 2016, which introduced a skin pack comprised of characters from Minecraft: Story Mode. This is the last version of Pocket Edition to support iOS 5.


== Additions ==


=== Skin packs ===
Version exclusive: Minecraft Story Mode Skin Pack.


== Changes ==
Loading in a world no longer displays the message "Generating World". Only the "Building Terrain" message is now displayed.
