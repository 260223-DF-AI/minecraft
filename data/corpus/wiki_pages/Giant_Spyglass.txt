The Giant Spyglass (, Kyodai Boenkyo?) is a massive spyglass, created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Giant Spyglass resembles a regular spyglass, but significantly larger.


== Functionality ==
Using a technique called "Solar Beam" (, Sora Bimu?), which reflects and focuses sunlight, the Giant Spyglass can be used to burn undead mobs that are underwater.

== History ==
While exploring a cave, Nico White and Gray fell into an underground lake. They were swarmed by a group of Drowned, but managed to escape due to the help of a nearby axolotl; to pay the axolotl back, Nico had Platinum punch a hole to the surface and crafted the Giant Spyglass, using it to focus the sunlight and burned the Drowned.
