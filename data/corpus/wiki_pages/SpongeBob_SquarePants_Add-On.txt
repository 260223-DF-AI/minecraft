==== Bosses ====
 Mecha Karen
Boss mob
Attacks with a chum blaster and by waving its hands around itself
Drops treasure chest which drops Cassette Plankton or Chum Blaster
 King Jellyfish
Boss mob
Spawns multiple angry jellyfish
Attacks by rising up and sharply falling down, creating a shock wave around itself
Drops treasure chest which contains a Jellyfish Glider or King Jellyfish Crown
 DoodleBob
Boss mob
Spawns doodle minions
Stunned when hit with a regular sword, during the stun phase a magic pencil appear which can be used to deal damage to Doodlebob
 The Flying Dutchman
Boss mob
Attacks with cannonballs
Can fly
