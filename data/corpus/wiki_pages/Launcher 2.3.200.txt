2.3.200 is an update to the launcher released on May 24, 2022.


== Changes ==
Lots of under the hood stability improvements.


== Fixes ==
More work on fixing user entitlement & offline mode/time-out issues.
Fixes to variety of other bugs, access problems, user & data issues.
MCL-21249 - Loading icon dark background is restrained to the size of the loading icon, rather than covering the entire window.
MCL-21215 - What's New page is blank when opening without an Internet connection.
MCL-21454 - Launching 22w19a and above gives the error message: "Failed to download file. Java runtime manifest is missing the desired component for this platform"
