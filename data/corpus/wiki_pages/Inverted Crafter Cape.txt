The Inverted Crafter Cape is an upcoming cape.


== Appearance ==
The cape's design depicts an iron pickaxe on a crafting table background. Similar to the Crafter Cape, but the pickaxe is upside down. The cape's design is similar to the teaser poster for A Minecraft Sequel.


== Distribution ==
Players can receive this cape by redeeming a code through watching Minecraft livestreams, presumably on Twitch. It will presumably be distributed online as a more accessible counterpart to the Crafter Cape (which requires in-person attendance to TwitchCon 2026), but full details of the distribution method are not yet known.
This task could not be completed normally in regions where either Twitch or its rewards program were unavailable. Players in these regions could obtain the cape by using a proxy/VPN, traveling to regions where they were available, or receiving a code from another player.
