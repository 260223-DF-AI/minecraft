Ashley Suzanne Johnson is an American actress, beginning her career as a child actress. She is best known for portraying Chrissy Seaver on the sitcom Growing Pains. As an adult, she became a prolific voice actor, portraying characters such as Ellie in The Last of Us and Gwen Tennyson in the Ben 10 franchise.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
In 2015, Johnson was announced to be voicing Petra in Minecraft: Story Mode, the first spin-off game in the Minecraft franchise. In 2017, she would return for Minecraft: Story Mode - Season Two, and the following year reprised the role a third time, providing a unique tutorial sequence for the Netflix version of the original game.
