Potatiesh, Greatstaff of the Peasant or just potato staff is an item dropped from mega spuds used to create potato portals in 24w14potato.


== Obtaining ==
Mega spud drops one potato staff if killed by a player or a tamed wolf.


== Usage ==
Players that have either completed the Non Plus Ultra advancement or are in Creative can use potato staffs on a block to create potato portals. If a player in Survival that has not completed the Non Plus Ultra advancement attempts to create a potato portal, an explosion of power 5 occurs at the player's position, and the following message is displayed in chat:

<player> was unworthy of Potatiesh, Greatstaff of the Peasant
Potato staffs can create portals in any dimension and on any side of a block.
Additionally, using the staff on a composter always fills it up by one layer and grants the Good Plant advancement.

== Issues ==
Issues relating to "Potatiesh, Greatstaff of the Peasant" are not maintained on the bug tracker because it is an April Fools' joke, and is therefore not in the newest stable version or snapshot. Issues reported there are closed as "Invalid".


== Trivia ==
The item name is likely a reference to Atiesh, Greatstaff of the Guardian, a weapon in World of Warcraft.
The item texture is a blaze rod with a compressed poisonous potato layered on top of it, causing it to look out of place because of the bluriness.
