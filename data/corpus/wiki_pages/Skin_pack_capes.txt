This is a list of capes included in skin packs on Bedrock Edition. Unlike regular capes, which can be freely equipped with any skin, skin pack capes can only be equipped with their associated skin. As a result, they are considered separate from standard capes, and statements about capes on this wiki will exclude skin pack capes by default.
Many of these capes' textures do not represent capes in the traditional sense, but rather hair or other accessories represented by using capes with many transparent pixels. All skin pack capes do not have an elytra counterpart.

== MINECON skin pack capes ==

The capes from previous MINECON events were included in some commemorative skin packs. The MINECON skin packs include only the (outdated) Steve and Alex skins, combined with the different MINECON capes. Like other skin pack capes, the capes can only be equipped with their associated skins; as such it is not possible to use this skin pack to equip the capes to a skin that is not Steve or Alex.


=== MINECON 2015 Skin Pack ===

This skin pack includes the MINECON 2015 cape, as well as all capes from previous years' MINECON events. The skin pack was made available to every Bedrock Edition player in 2015 regardless of whether they attended any of the events.


=== MINECON 2016 Skin Pack ===

This skin pack includes the MINECON 2016 cape, as well as all capes from previous years' MINECON events. The skin pack was made available to every Bedrock Edition player in 2016 regardless of whether they attended any of the events.
