Seed may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Item ===
Seeds that are grown on farmland:

Wheat Seeds
Melon Seeds
Pumpkin Seeds
Beetroot Seeds
Torchflower Seeds
Pitcher Pod
Plants that function as their own seeds:

Carrots (grows on farmland)
Potatoes (grows on farmland)
Nether Wart (grows on soul sand)
Cocoa Beans (grows on jungle logs)


=== World generation ===
World seed, the number used for world generation.
Seed Templates, a menu in Bedrock Edition with a list of seeds (with captions) for the player to select from.
Starter Seeds, predefined seeds in Bedrock Edition for the trial or first created worlds.


=== Command ===
/seed, the command which displays the world seed.‌[Java Edition  only]
