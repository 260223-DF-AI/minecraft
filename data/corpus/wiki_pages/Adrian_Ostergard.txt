Adrian Ostergard is a producer of Minecraft: Java Edition at Mojang Studios.


== Biography ==
Adrian was educated at Alands Lyceum. His primary competencies are telecommunications, management projects, and management. He started his job as a customer support agent at Mojang from May to November of 2015 with the help of a recruitment company, Academic Work.
After working as a customer support agent since December 2015, he became a project manager for Mojang starting from August of 2016. After 2 years, he worked as a producer and a developer for Java Edition. Since September 2018, he became a product owner of Java Edition. He announces Java Edition snapshots on Twitter and minecraft.net.
