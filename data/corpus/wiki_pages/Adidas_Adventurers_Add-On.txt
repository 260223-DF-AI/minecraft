=== Entities ===
File:Adidas Companion.png adidas Companion
A humanoid mob that spawns upon activating the add-on and follows the player around.
The companion will emit a soft glow during the night.
The companion will alternate their look periodically.
Will go after creepers if player ends up in close proximity and unaware.
Once player engages with a hostile mob will spawn in alternative characters to also engage and assist either with attacking the same mob, or also going after others that are near by.
When interacting with the companion, a menu appears listing nine options: Quest List, Show Ores, Death Recall, Set Home, Teleport Home, Request Items, Throw, Structure Locator, and closing the menu.
