Cristina Anderca is a communications manager at Mojang Studios.


== Education ==
In 2011, Cristina Anderca enrolled in an English Language and Literature/Letters program at the West University of Timisoara, participating in "The Symposium of Students in English" and the "International Conference on British and American Studies", as a Student Organizer in both groups.
In 2014, she graduated, but stayed for a duration of two more years in WUT to get a Master's Degree in Advertising and Sales Promotion. During this period, Anderca collaborated at her university in late 2014 through early 2015, helping establish connections with local institutions and representing the university at cultural events, among other things. She also interned at Morningstar Sustainalytics a few months after.


== Career ==
After graduating in late 2015, she entered Sustainalytics as a Junior Analyst, where she mainly conducted research centered around discovering controversies in companies within the chemicals and transportation sectors.
She left her position in December of 2016 to join inTech Dynamics as a Copywriter. She was promoted to Senior Copywriter in February of 2019, after spending three years in the company. Though her responsibilities evolved, she eventually left her post at inTech. 
Three months after leaving inTech Dynamics, she joined Mojang Studios' game development block as a Creative Writer. After 3 years of working in this field, she was promoted to Senior Creative Writer, a position she actively holds today.
