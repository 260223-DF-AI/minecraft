A tuff brick slab is a decorative slab variant of tuff bricks that does not generate naturally and is commonly used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Tuff brick slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.

=== Note blocks ===
Tuff brick slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.


== Sounds ==
In Bedrock Edition, when a tuff brick slab is combined into a double slab, the block's use sound is played.
