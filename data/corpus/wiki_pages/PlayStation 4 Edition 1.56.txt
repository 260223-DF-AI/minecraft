1.56  is a version of PlayStation 4 Edition released on August 29, 2017.  
[edit]


== Additions ==
Added the Glide Myths Track Pack; Drift through the clouds of Icarus, dive into conflict in Excalibur and ride with the Kelpies in Celts.
Added the Strangers - Biome Settlers 3 skin pack.


== Changes ==
Endermen and endermites are now always hostile to each other.
Polar bears no longer attack the player when in Peaceful mode.
Wither skeletons no longer fear daylight and will attack anything that lives.
Skeleton horses can now be tamed and leashed.
Skeletons no longer attack iron golems.
Strays now flee from wolves.
Wolves no longer become hostile against mobs that they cannot directly see.
Crafting any kind of boat now requires a wooden shovel.
String can now be crafted from cobwebs.
Purpur stairs and slabs can now be crafted using purpur pillars.
Stone slabs, redstone repeaters, and redstone comparators can now be crafted from granite, andesite, diorite, and their polished variants.
Squid now produce a cloud of floating black ink particles when attacked.
Sugar canes are no longer affected by biome coloring.
Observers now register changes to item frames, doors, and flower pots.
Observers no longer output extra signals for fire block state changes or multiple signals when lava or water is placed in front of them.
Pistons can now push daylight sensors.
Potion durations have changed:
Extended potion of Poison, extended potion of Regeneration had their durations increased from 1:30 back to 2:00.
Enhanced potions of Poison have their duration increased from 0:21.6 back to 0:22.5.
Splash potions no longer have the same duration as drinkable potions. They only have 75% of the duration once again.
Taking damage that is reduced by unenchanted armor while blocking with a sword now makes the shield blocking sound.
Coordinate display of maps no longer has spaces.
Looking at a parrot while holding a cookie now shows the "feed" tooltip.
