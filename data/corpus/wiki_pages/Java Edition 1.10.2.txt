1.10.2 is a minor update to Java Edition released on June 23, 2016, which fixes bugs. It is compatible with 1.10 and 1.10.1 servers.


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
 Witch

The splash potion throwing sound is marked as Hostile Creatures instead of Friendly Creatures.


=== Gameplay ===
 Speed effect

Now works on a ridden pig.
