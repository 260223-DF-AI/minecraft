== Village Generation ==
Villages usually spawn with multiple farms and houses. The rarest buildings are weaponsmiths, butchers, temples, and libraries. Small villages may not even have one of each, but large villages can spawn with multiple of each. The "Medium House 1" is rare in post-1.14 versions, but abundant in pre-1.14.
Streets are made with dirt path and grass blocks, or planks if replacing water or lava. Desert villages use sandstone instead. Streets are 3 blocks wide and lead away from the well in any of the 4 cardinal directions.  All streets are straight with intersections at right angles.  Streets dont need to be flat across.


==== Biome Variants ====
Villages have 5 types:

Plains
Desert
Savanna
Taiga
Snowy Plains
Snowy Villages and Taiga Villages use spruce wood, Savanna villages use acacia wood. There are acacia logs instead of cobblestone in savanna villages except the weaponsmith, church, and around the well. Desert villages have sandstone based variants of all the buildings substituting regular sandstone for cobblestone or wooden logs, and smooth sandstone for wooden planks. Wooden doors and fence posts are still used despite the lack of wood sources. Desert weaponsmiths still use stone slabs on the roof. Also cobblestone in the church, the weaponsmith and around the well is not replaced with sandstone.


==== Farm Crops ====
Each farm spawns as one of four types of crops, chosen between wheat, beetroot, carrot, or potato. In Java Edition the crops that generate on a village farm spawn as seeds, while in Bedrock Edition they spawn as full-grown crops.


==== Abandoned Villages ====
In Abandoned villages, cobwebs sporadically replace any block besides chests, bells, crafting tables, carpets, water, and lava source blocks; doors and torches are destroyed; and all non-stained glass panes are turned into their respective brown stained variant. All villagers that spawn in the village will instead spawn as zombie villagers. Animals and livestock spawn like normal villages. Cobblestone also has a higher chance of turning into mossy cobblestone than normal villages.
