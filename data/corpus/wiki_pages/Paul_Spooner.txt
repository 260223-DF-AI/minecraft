Paul Spooner is a 3D artist notable for his contributions to the global environmental elements of Minecraft, specifically the large trees. 


== Contributions ==

After being disappointed with his inability to build a tree fort in the small trees, he wrote a map modding tool which was then partially included in the base game. 


== Trivia ==
Unlike Daniel Rosenfeld, Spooner's in-game character does not have a Mojang cape.
