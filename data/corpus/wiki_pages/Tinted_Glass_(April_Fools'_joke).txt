Tinted glass is a dyed variant of the glass block added in Java Edition 2.0. It was later added as stained glass in 1.7.2.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Tinted glass drops itself only if it is broken with a tool enchanted with Silk Touch. Otherwise, it drops nothing.


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==
Tinted glass is a colored version of normal glass.
