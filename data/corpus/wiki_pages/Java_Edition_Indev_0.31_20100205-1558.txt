A version of Indev 0.31 was released on February 5, 2010, at 15:58 UTC, adding giants.


== Additions ==


=== Mobs ===
 Giants

Oversized zombies.
Can walk up to the player.
Hostile mob.
Can only attack the player if the player is standing on top of it.


=== General ===
Difficulty

New settings.
Peaceful, Easy, Normal, and Hard.
Inventory screen

Right-clicking a stack cuts the stack in half.
It is now possible to continually craft more of an item, without having to put it in the inventory, if there are materials left in the crafting table.
Changed block shading for a fourth time during Indev.
The player model is now shown in the inventory GUI.


== Technical ==
Performance

Performance improvements.


== Bugs ==
2 bugs reported

Pigs and sheep now walk backwards and glide around.
Performance drops sometimes still occur in the game; this will not be fixed until Indev 20100206-2034.
