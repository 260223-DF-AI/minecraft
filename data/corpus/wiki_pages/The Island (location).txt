The Island is the titular setting of Minecraft: The Island, the first Minecraft novel. A mysterious location that Guy washes up on, where he has to fight the rules of Minecraft to survive.

The Island as it appears in both tie-in maps are actually one and the same. If one returns to the site of the opening cinematic in The Mountain, an inactive version of the area can even be found, with nonfunctional features.
