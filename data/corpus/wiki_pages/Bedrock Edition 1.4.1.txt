1.4.1 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released initially for Android and Fire OS on May 17, 2018, which fixes one bug. It is compatible with 1.4.0 servers.
This is the first digital release of Bedrock Edition on Nintendo Switch, and was also available as a day one patch for the physical version.


== Fixes ==
Purchased content is now available on Android platforms after updating from 1.2.13.
