A totem of undying is an item that can prevent players or mobs from dying when they take fatal damage. It is dropped by evokers, which spawn in woodland mansions and raids.


== Obtaining ==


=== Mob loot ===
Evokers always drop one totem of undying upon death, which is not affected by Looting.

== Usage ==

If a player or a mob is holding a totem of undying in the off-hand or main hand slot and receives otherwise fatal damage, the totem prevents their death. The totem of undying must be held in the user's hand for it to work  it does not work if it is in the hotbar, unless selected. Totems of undying prevent a game over in Hardcore mode in the same way they prevent a death in Easy, Normal, and Hard mode.
A totem can be used only once, and disappears when the effect is activated. It does not prevent death caused by void damage or the /kill command. In Java Edition, it also does not prevent death caused by an arrow of harming if the arrow kills the holder with just the arrow's physical damage, as the arrow's Instant Damage effect is applied after the totem has activated, effectively killing the player a second time. 
When a totem of undying is activated, it restores 1HP, removes all existing status effects and grants several positive status effects:

Regeneration II (1HP every 25 ticks, HP  0.4 per second) for 45 seconds (long enough to heal HP  18)
Fire Resistance I for 40 seconds
5 seconds of Absorption II (providing HP)
When activated, multiple green and yellow particles are displayed around the player that held the totem. A screen effect also plays, showing the totem hovering in front of the player's screen, similar to the animation seen when a player receives the Raid Omen effect or the Hero of the Village effect in Bedrock Edition, or enters the same area as an elder guardian.
Any mob that can hold a totem of undying (in a hand or in the mouth) is revived if killed while it is holding the totem. The totem functions exactly as it does with players, although undead mobs are not affected by the Regeneration effect.

== Trivia ==
In the New Nintendo 3DS Edition, the totem of undying can be put in any hotbar slot to activate it, due to the lack of an off-hand slot in that version.
Being able to obtain totems of undying in raids without exploring woodland mansions is considered a "massive game play design flaw" by Mojang.
Totems of undying are the only renewable resource that are made non-renewable in a difficulty other than Peaceful, as raids do not last long enough to spawn evokers in Easy difficulty.
