Town Folk Skin Pack is a skin pack that includes 20 skins themed around being citizens of a medieval town. Together with the City Folk Skin Pack, this is one of the first two skin packs released for Bedrock Edition.
The skins included in this pack were designed by Garry Lucken. Some of these are based on skins included in Skin Pack 3 and Skin Pack 4.
