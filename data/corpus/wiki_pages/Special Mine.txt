== Trivia ==
The Kuiper World is a reference to the Kuiper Belt, a region of the outer Solar System consisting mainly of comets and other icy objects.
Spooky Scary Skeletons and its flavor text are references to the memetic 1996 Halloween novelty song of the same name by Andrew Gold.
