15w36c is the twenty-first snapshot for Java Edition 1.9, released on September 2, 2015 to fix a crash involving endermen, as well as beacon beam rendering, and shulker's appearance while in a monster spawner.


== Fixes ==
4 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.9

MC-4465  Floating ghast bombs/wither skulls/fire charges
From the 1.9 development versions

MC-85007  Beacons are 2D.
MC-85928  The mini-mob of a shulker spawner rotates outside of the spawner cage.
MC-87881  Crash when looking at enderman.
