A version of "Minecraft alpha" (Java Edition Classic private alpha) was released on May 16, 2009, at 16:25 UTC, which fixed some bugs.


== Fixes ==
2 bugs fixed

Fixed texture issues.
Fixed mouse invert not working.


== Bugs ==
1 bug reported

A couple of issues with this version reported by the three testers were low sensitivity and low FPS. Notch also reported having issues with mouse looking.
