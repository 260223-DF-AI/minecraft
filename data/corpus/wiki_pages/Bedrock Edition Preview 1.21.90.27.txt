Beta 1.21.90.27 (ChromeOS, Android), or Preview 1.21.90.27 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox, PlayStation) is the sixth beta/Preview version for Bedrock Edition 1.21.90, released on May 27, 2025, which fixes bugs.


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
Happy Ghast

Updated ghastling speed to slow it down.
The happy ghast's player detection area has been reverted to a single box: it now extends 2 blocks above its top, 1 block below, and 1 block to each side.
The happy ghast no longer jitters when a player is detected at the edge of its detection area.


=== Graphical ===
Vibrant Visuals

Temporarily reverted weather-related changes to sky color in Vibrant Visuals while some issues are sorted out.


=== Technical ===
Blocks

alpha_test_to_opaque, alpha_test_single_sided_to_opaque, and blend_to_opaque will not shift to opaque in the distance.

MCPE-220039 – Squid's lead hold position is incorrect.
MCPE-220040 – Player can not leash other mobs to a glow squid.
MCPE-220041 – Snow golem's lead hold position is incorrect.
From the previous development version

MCPE-220308 – Vibrant Visuals: Everything appears green on Android.
Other

Fixed ambient lighting levels in the Nether in Vibrant Visuals.
Fixed fog settings in warm and humid biomes in Vibrant Visuals.
Fixed black screen issues on some Android devices when in Vibrant Visuals.

Fixed the default control schemes for all cameras.
Fixed the leash attach positions for the parrot and the glow squid.
