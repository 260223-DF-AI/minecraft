1.14.30.2 is a major update for the Bedrock Dedicated Servers, released on February 6, 2020, that updates the servers for Bedrock Edition 1.14.30 and fixes bugs.


== Changes ==
Bedrock Dedicated Servers
Updated for 1.14.30 clients.


== Fixes ==
Added ops.json and whitelist.json back to the default BDS packs. (BDS-43)
Previously created worlds can now be loaded to a server once again. (BDS-948)
Bees' AI now works on Bedrock Dedicated Servers. (BDS-2170)
