Toby is a character in the Minecraft Mini-Series and part of Team Stone Beach with Quinn. He helps her in trying to escape CommuniKate's game show and then Poseidon's game show after he takes over in the Greek Isles.


== Appearance ==
Toby has neat brown hair, green eyes, glasses, and dark-tan skin. He wears a light blue button up shirt, slate colored pants, and dark dray shoes.


== Biography ==
Before the series began, Toby is shown to be a stone beach miner when in-between interview clips.
Toby was once teleported from his mesa home along with the others by CommuniKate using a command block on the show. He and Quinn won the some challenges, and in the final challenge, he and the others sabotaged their way back home, and Toby teleporting friends to their homes. But were teleported to the Greek Isles. Poseidon teleported Toby to a sand pillar in the Kraken Sea along with the other heroes, and he spent most of the season there. Then at the end, all the heroes escape and try to stop Poseidon. After a while, Zeus comes, stops Poseidon, and asks heroes to help him. After helping the god, Toby and Quinn apparently returned home.


== Personality ==
Toby is helpful, analytical, and methodical. He is kind-hearted towards his friends and nervous towards opposition.


== Relationships ==


=== Quinn ===
Despite the fact that Toby and Quinn represent the same biome, they have a rather weak relationship, at the level of communication.


== Trivia ==
He is similar to Radar in many aspects.
