A granite slab is a decorative slab variant of granite that does not generate naturally and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Granite slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Granite slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.


== Sounds ==
In Bedrock Edition, when a granite slab is combined into a double slab, the block's use sound is played.
