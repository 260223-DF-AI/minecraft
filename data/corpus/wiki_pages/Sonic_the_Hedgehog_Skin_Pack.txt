Sonic the Hedgehog Skin Pack is a free skin pack, developed by Hive Games as part of the Minecraft x Sonic the Hedgehog collaboration to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the video game franchise.

== Trivia ==
The skin pack was actively promoted on The Hive featured server with idle NPC displays on either ends of the game selection area. These displays had a rotating selection between the two skins.
