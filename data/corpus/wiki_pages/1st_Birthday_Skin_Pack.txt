1st Birthday Skin Pack is a skin pack that includes 22 party-themed skins (originally 23) celebrating the first anniversary of Xbox 360 Edition's release.
It was originally available on Xbox 360 Edition from May 9 to May 31, 2013 and from May 9 to May 18, 2014. From May 7 to May 17, 2015, May 9 to May 16, 2016, and May 9 to May 21, 2017 the skin pack was available again for Xbox 360 and Xbox One editions.
For a limited time after December 17, 2014, as well as for a short period after December 19, 2017, the skin pack was available for Legacy Console Editions on PlayStation consoles.
Due to the Better Together Update, it was added in Bedrock Edition on September 12, 2017.
