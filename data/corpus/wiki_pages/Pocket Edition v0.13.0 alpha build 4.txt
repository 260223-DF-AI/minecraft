v0.13.0 alpha build 4 is the fourth build version released for v0.13.0.


== Additions ==


=== Skin packs ===
Version exclusive: Biome Settlers Skin Pack 1.


== Changes ==
Block lag significantly reduced
Updated the textures of all doors in the inventory
Ghasts and slimes now have a higher spawn rate
Tripwire outputs power when broken with anything other than shears


== Fixes ==
Localized the "New" button
Skeletons now burn during daytime
Fixed apples inflicting poison
Minecarts placed on a powered rail next to an opaque block will now move in the right direction
Fixed detector rails saving their ON/OFF state incorrectly
