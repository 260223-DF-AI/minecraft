1.18 experimental snapshot 7 is the seventh and final experimental snapshot for Java Edition 1.18, released on September 8, 2021, which introduces changes to terrain generation and tweaks to elytra due to various server chunk loading issues. This is the final experimental snapshot released in 2021.


== Changes ==


=== Items ===
Elytra

No longer take durability damage when gliding, only when boosting with firework rockets.
Decreased the firework rocket boost on an elytra from 30 blocks per second to 26 blocks per second.
Now break when the item reaches 0 durability.


=== World generation ===
Caves

Noodle caves are no longer capped at Y=130, allowing cave openings and cracks in mountain peaks.
General

Slightly smoothened the terrain by lowering the noise.
