Noor is one of the nine default skins players can use in Minecraft, and the third to be introduced. First appearing in promotional material for The Wild Update, they've since become a major recurring character, serving as a mascot for releases such as Armored Paws and Trial Chamber Legends.


== Appearance ==
Noor is typically depicted with thin arms, and reddish brown skin. They have long, brown dreadlocks hanging down both shoulders, white eyes, brown pupils, noticeable eyelids, and brown lips. Their jacket is a distinct shade of red, with a black shirt underneath and baggy green pants, as well as brown shoes.
In the character creator, Noor has approximately six unique items.

== Trivia ==
Noor makes an appearance in the painting Prairie Ride.
More than anyone else, Noor will often be paired with Sunny, such as in the Wild Update key art and the promotions for Trial Chamber Legends, where they are otherwise not playable.
In a meme image posted by Minecraft social media accounts, the character Fry from the animated comedy television series Futurama is represented wearing Noor’s jacket.
Noor is one of two default skins for which Mojang uses the pronoun "they" in the descriptions of images on minecraft.net. The other one is Efe.
