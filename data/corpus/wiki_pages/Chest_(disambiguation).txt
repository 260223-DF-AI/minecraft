Chest may refer to:


== Minecraft ==
Chest, a block which can store items.
Trapped Chest, a redstone component which sends redstone signals when opened.
Ender Chest, a chest whose contents are the same universally per player.
Minecart with Chest, a chest in a minecart.
Boat with Chest, a chest in a boat.
Copper Chest, a variant of the chest that can be crafted with copper ingots.
Bonus Chest, a structure which consists of a chest surrounded with torches.
Shulker Box, a portable chest.
Dyed Shulker Box, a dyed variant of the shulker box.
Barrel, a chest-like block.
Buried treasure, an underground structure which consists of a chest buried underground.
Shelf, a block that that can hold and display up to three stacks of items. It has variants in all wood types.


=== April Fools' jokes ===
Locked chest, an April Fools' joke where items were (not actually) sold with real money.
Golden Chest, an April Fools' joke chest with golden texture.


=== Technical ===
Command storage format
"Chest" is also the name of the armor slot in which chestplates and elytra can be equipped. This exists as one of the slots of players, mobs, armor stands, and all living entities.
