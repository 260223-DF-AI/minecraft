Beta 1.8.1 is the last version of Java Edition Beta, released on September 15, 2011, to fix some of the bugs and crashes in Beta 1.8.


== Changes ==


=== World generation ===
Mineshaft

Support beams in mineshafts now generate with fences instead of planks.


== Fixes ==
2 bugs fixed

 Shift+ clicking into a full chest/inventory crashing the game fixed.
 Shift+ clicking items in and out of a dispenser crashing the game fixed.


== Bugs ==
1 bug reported

The player can hit mobs and other players from far away.


== Trivia ==
Beta 1.8.1 was the last update in the Beta stage of the game's development.
The prereleases for 1.0.0 prior to RC1 were named Beta 1.9, as the final name had not been decided on yet. However, since they are prereleases for 1.0.0, they are not generally considered to be part of the Beta stage.
