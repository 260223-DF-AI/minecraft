1.21.43 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on October 25-30, 2024, which fixes some bugs.


== Fixes ==
4 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.21.2

MCPE-167791 – Campfires no longer use fire damage sound
From 1.21.40

MCPE-187188 – Trying to use an item while aiming at copper blocks prevents the item from being consumed/used
Other

Fixed an issue that prevented some Trophies from being unlocked on PlayStation 5.
To ensure players receive all missing Trophies, certain objectives that didn't unlock previously may need to be completed again.
Fixed an issue that prevented some notifications from appearing on Xbox.
