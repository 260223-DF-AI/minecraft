Uno Minecraft is a spin-off of the card game Uno by Mattel. The deck features 112 cards with Minecraft characters and mobs, with one card being called the "Creeper card" which is unique to Uno as it forces a player to draw three cards. Like regular Uno, 210 players are required and the recommended player age is 7 years old and older.  
The card game can be purchased in either a regular card box or in a storage tin.
