Tuff bricks are a brick variant of tuff found abundantly in trial chambers.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Tuff bricks drop as an item if mined by any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, it drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Tuff bricks generate naturally in trial chambers as the primary ceiling and wall block of many rooms.
