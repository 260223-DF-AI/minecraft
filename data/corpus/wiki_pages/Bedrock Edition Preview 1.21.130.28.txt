Beta 1.21.130.28 (ChromeOS, Android) or Preview 1.21.130.28 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox, PlayStation) is the sixth and final beta and seventh and final Preview version for Bedrock Edition 1.21.130, released on November 18, 2025, which fixes bugs.


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
 Zombie Horse

No longer spawns on Peaceful difficulty.


== Fixes ==
7 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.21.130

MCPE-228176 – Restarting the game after signing out signs the user back in when signed in before
MCPE-229154 – Vibrant Visuals setting is not available on Android devices using newer Adreno 722 GPUs
From the 1.21.130 development versions

MCPE-230871 – Neither Netherite Horse Armor nor Netherite Nautilus Armor provide knockback resistance
MCPE-231485 – Game crashes on launch when the "Improved Input Response" option is enabled
MCPE-231518 – Repeated activation of LT and RT trigger buttons on Xbox
Other

Fixed an issue where IME Candidates would appear in the wrong monitor or misplaced in relation to text field.
Fixed blocks with the tick component not ticking when placed via a Jigsaw structure.
