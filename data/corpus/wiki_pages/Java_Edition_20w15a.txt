20w15a is the eleventh snapshot for Java Edition 1.16, released on April 8, 2020, which adds a new Nether biome, new music tracks, new blocks, and other changes and fixes.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Blackstone

Generates in basalt deltas.
Can be used in place of cobblestone to craft furnaces and stone tools.
Has a slab, stair, and wall variant.
 Blackstone slab, blackstone stairs, blackstone wall

Decorative blocks crafted from blackstone.
 Polished blackstone

Crafted with 4 blackstone.
Has a chiseled, slab, stair, and wall variant.
Can be crafted into buttons and pressure plates.
 Polished blackstone slab, polished blackstone stairs, and polished blackstone wall

Decorative blocks crafted from polished blackstone.
 Polished blackstone bricks

Crafted with 4 polished blackstone.
Has a cracked, slab, stair, and wall variant.
 Polished blackstone brick slab, polished blackstone brick stairs, polished blackstone brick wall

Decorative blocks crafted from polished blackstone bricks.
 Polished blackstone button and polished blackstone pressure plate

Redstone blocks crafted from polished blackstone.
Work like their stone counterparts.
 Chiseled Nether bricks

New variant of Nether bricks.
Crafted from 2 Nether brick slabs.
 Chiseled polished blackstone

Decorative block crafted from polished blackstone.
 Cracked Nether bricks

New variant of Nether bricks.
Obtained by smelting Nether bricks in a furnace.
 Cracked polished blackstone bricks

Decorative blocks crafted from polished blackstone.
 Gilded blackstone

Has a chance of dropping 25 gold nuggets (increased with Fortune), or it drops itself instead.
Currently only available via the Creative inventory or by using commands.
 Quartz bricks

A decorative block crafted from 4 blocks of quartz.
 Soul campfire

Has a blue flame instead of orange.
Has a light level of 10.
Does the same amount of damage as a regular campfire.
Crafted with soul sand or soul soil instead of coal.
Piglins are repelled by soul campfires.


=== Items ===
 Piglin banner pattern

New pattern for banners, the "Piglin".
Used in the loom to apply the piglin pattern to the banner.
Currently unobtainable in Survival mode.


=== World generation ===
Biomes

Basalt deltas
A volcanic biome in the Nether. Fog effects have flakes of volcanic ash.
Filled with pillars of basalt and large areas of blackstone.
Ghasts, magma cubes, and striders spawn in this biome.


=== General ===
Accessibility

Chat line spacing can now be adjusted.
A chat delay can be set between 0 (default) and 6 seconds.
Music

Added 3 new soundtracks to the Nether, composed by Lena Raine: "Rubedo", "Chrysopoeia" and "So Below."
"Rubedo" plays in the Nether wastes biome.
"Chrysopoeia" plays in the crimson forest biome.
"So Below" plays in the soul sand valley and basalt deltas biomes.
Sound

Added 31 ambient sounds for the basalt deltas.
Tags

Added campfires block tag.
Contains campfires and soul campfires.
Bees, parrots, and turtles view blocks in this tag as dealing fire damage, and pathfind accordingly.
Campfires use this tag to determine if they are lit or not.
Flint and steel uses this tag to determine if it can light campfires.
Added guarded_by_piglins block tag.
Contains container blocks (chests, trapped chests, ender chests, barrels, the shulker_boxes block tag) and gold blocks (blocks of gold, gilded blackstone, and the gold_ores block tag).
Piglins become hostile toward a player that destroys or use a block in this tag.
Added furnace_materials item tag.
Contains cobblestone and blackstone.
Items in this tag can be used to craft furnaces and unlock the recipe in the recipe book.
Added stone_tool_materials item tag.
Contains cobblestone and blackstone.
Items in this tag can be used to craft stone tools and unlock the recipes in the recipe book.
Added soul_fire_base_blocks item tag.
Parallels the block tag.
Contains soul sand and soul soil.
Items in this tag can be used to craft soul fire torches and soul campfires and unlock the recipes in the recipe book.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Dispensers

Can now saddle pigs, striders, and tamed horses, donkeys and mules.
Can now put horse armor on tamed horses.
Can now put carpets on tamed llamas.
Can now put chests on tamed llamas, donkeys, and mules.
Can now shear mooshrooms and snow golems.
Furnaces

Can now be crafted with blackstone.
Soul fire torches

Can now also be crafted with soul sand.
Walls

Now connect to the bottom and sides of glass panes and iron bars.
Now connect to the bottom of sea pickles.


=== Items ===
Stone axes, hoes, pickaxes, shovels, and swords

Can now be crafted with blackstone.
Hoes

Are now the default tool for mining leaves.
Shears are still used for collecting leaf blocks.
Nether fungus, Nether sprouts, crimson roots, warped roots, Nether wart, Nether wart blocks, warped wart blocks, twisting vines, and weeping vines

Can now be composted.


=== Mobs ===
General

Mobs now avoid walking on magma blocks and lit campfires.
Hoglins

Baby hoglins no longer drop loot (but still drop experience).
Magma cubes

Spawn commonly in basalt deltas.
Piglins

Now avoid soul campfires.
Now become hostile toward any player that breaks a chest, trapped chest, ender chest, barrel, gold ore, Nether gold ore, gilded blackstone, or a gold block.
Striders

Now turn blueish-purple outside of lava.
When outside lava, their facial expression becomes a more prominent frown.
Can now only spawn if the block above the lava is air.
Baby striders ride on top of bigger striders.
Zoglins

Are now considered undead.


=== General ===
Tags

Added polished blackstone, and (where applicable) blackstone blocks and items to the buttons, slabs, stairs, and walls block and item tags.
Added soul campfires to the piglin_repellents block tag.
Added the #signs block tag and removed lanterns and soul fire lanterns from the wall_post_override block tag.


=== Textures ===
Strider

Changed strider texture from  to  to remove an unused fin. The fin itself was not vieweable in-game.

== Trivia ==
The word "Rubedo" is Latin for "redness", it was adopted by alchemists to refer to the final stage of creating the philosopher's stone, it is also likely associated with the red-colored environment of the Nether.
The word "Chrysopoeia" is an alchemical term that describes the creation of the philosopher's stone, or the transmutation of a given object into gold.
