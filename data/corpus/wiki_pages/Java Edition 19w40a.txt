19w40a is the eighth snapshot for Java Edition 1.15, released on October 2, 2019, which fixes many rendering issues.


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
Parrots 

Can now sit on a player's shoulder even when the player is riding an entity.


=== General ===
Block models

Lighting of blocks in the inventory is once again darker on the right, as it was before 19w39a; however, inventory models are now brighter than before.
