Poisonous potato chips are a joke food item, added in 24w14potato, obtained by smelting poisonous potato slices.


== Obtaining ==


== Usage ==
To eat poisonous potato chips, press and hold use while it is selected in the hotbar. Eating one restores 8 () hunger and 10 saturation. Unlike unprocessed poisonous potato, chips do not cause Poison.
