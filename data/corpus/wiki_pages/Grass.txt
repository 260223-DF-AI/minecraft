Grass may refer to:


== Minecraft ==
Short Grass, a non-solid block that spawns abundantly in the Overworld, formerly known as "Grass".
Tall Grass, its tall variant.
Grass Block, a block with grass on top of it.
Dirt Path, a path block used for villages, formerly known as "Grass Path".
Seagrass, a sea variant.
Tall Seagrass, a tall seagrass variant.
Seagrass (feature), a feature which generates seagrass.
Short Dry Grass, a desert variant of grass.
Tall Dry Grass, its tall variant.
Fern, a non-solid block found only in certain biomes.
Large Fern, a 2-block tall fern.
Bush, a non-solid block with a similar texture as short grass.
Firefly Bush, a variant of the bush.
