Crafty Crew, also known as Minecraft x Reebok Crafty Crew Skin Pack, is a skin pack that includes 21 skins promoting the collaboration between Minecraft and footwear and clothing brand Reebok. It is available through a promo code obtained by purchasing a Minecraft-themed Reebok hoodie or T-shirt at Walmart before January 31, 2025. The promo codes are redeemable until August 15, 2025.
