Why You Can't Miss Minecraft Live - March 2026! (stylized in all caps) is a promotional video for Minecraft Live – March 2026. As with prior similar videos, it follows the tone and iconography of Minecraft Monthly, though it is noticeably smaller in scope with only one shot.
