1.4.3 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released only for iOS on June 5, 2018, which fixes an account issue. It is compatible with 1.4.2 servers and did not include any code changes.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Marketplace

New map called Kingdom Survival by Blockception.
Can be downloaded for free for a limited time only (1 month) starting from June 5, 2018.


== Fixes ==
Fixed an issue with kid accounts being unable to play over LAN.
