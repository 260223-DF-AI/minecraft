Copper ingots are metal ingots obtained from smelting raw copper and from killing drowned or copper golems.


== Obtaining ==


=== Crafting ===


=== Smelting ===
Copper ingots can be obtained by smelting raw copper in a furnace or blast furnace, as well as the ore itself if mined using Silk Touch.


=== Mob loot ===
When killed by a player or a tamed wolf, a drowned has a 11% (11100) chance of dropping a copper ingot. With the Looting enchantment, the chance can be increased to 13% (13100) with Looting I, 15% (320) with Looting II, and 17% (17100) with Looting III. Copper golems also drop 1-3 copper ingots when killed, unaffected by Looting.

=== Repairing ===
Copper ingots are the repair items for the copper tier and thus can be used to repair the following items in an anvil:

Copper Helmet
Copper Chestplate
Copper Leggings
Copper Boots
Copper Sword
Copper Pickaxe
Copper Axe
Copper Shovel
