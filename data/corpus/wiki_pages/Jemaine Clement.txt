Jemaine Atea Mahana Clement is a New Zealand actor, comedian, musician, and filmmaker. He is a part of the musical comedy duo Flight of the Conchords, and has appeared in several films and television series, such as What We Do in the Shadows, Men in Black 3, Moana, Rio, and Harold and the Purple Crayon.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
In 2025, Jemaine played a supporting role in A Minecraft Movie, portraying the eccentric auctioneer Daryl. He also voiced a minor animated character, the piglin Bruce, pivotal to Malgosha's backstory.


== Trivia ==
Prior to being cast in A Minecraft Movie, one of Clement's prior roles, Tamatoa, appeared in the Moana Character Pack and Moana 2.
