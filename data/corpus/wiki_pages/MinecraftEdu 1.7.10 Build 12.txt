1.7.10 Build 12 is the twelfth build for MinecraftEdu 1.7.10, released on December 19, 2014. It is based on Java Edition 1.7.10. This is the first hosting version and final build in 2014.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Ability to login with MinecraftEdu hosting account and connect to your Hosting servers.


== Fixes ==
3 bugs fixed

Bats not appearing even when animals were enabled.
World name showing as "null" in server window if started a world from the World Library.
Possible fix for some intel graphics cards not being able to run MinecraftEdu at all (1280 GL ERROR).
