Minecraft Now was a series of online livestreams produced by Mojang Studios, where development information regarding Minecraft was presented, alongside community creations, live gameplay, and a Q&A with the developers. The livestreams began with the first edition on December 9, 2021, and concluded on November 24th, 2022, being succeeded by Minecraft Monthly.
