TU63  is a version of Xbox 360 Edition released on March 27, 2018.  
[edit]


== Additions ==
Added the Star Wars Sequel Skin Pack.

Added chat message to inform users once they reach the maximum number of End crystals in a world.


== Changes ==
The "where are we now" music disc has been renamed to "wait".
Chests, trapped chests, and ender chests can no longer be opened when an entity is standing on top of it. Shulker boxes cannot be opened when an entity is inside the area that its collision box would extend to when opened.
