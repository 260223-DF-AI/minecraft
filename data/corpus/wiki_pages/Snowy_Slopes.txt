Snowy slopes are snowy biomes that often generate on the sides of mountains. Unlike the snowy plains, this biome is made out of stone covered with one or more layers of snow blocks and occasional powder snow patches.


== Generation ==
Snowy slopes generate next to groves, most snowy plains, ice spikes, plains, sunflower plains, and flower forests.


== Description ==
Snowy slopes are similar to groves except that no trees generate. It is a mostly barren biome covered in snow, multiple layers of snow blocks and strips of powder snow, with steep sides to the north or east also having stone cliffs covered with snow layers. Goats spawn in this biome alongside rabbits. This is the only mountain biome where igloos can generate. Polar bears can spawn in this biome.[BE  only]
With Vibrant Visuals, snowy slopes use default environmental settings with a coolish color grading, making the biome appear colder blue. They use cold sunlight, which is slightly brighter at dusk and dawn.
