Resin brick stairs are a decorative stairs variant of resin bricks that does not generate naturally and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Resin brick stairs can be mined using a pickaxe. When mined without a pickaxe, it drops nothing.

=== Note blocks ===
Resin brick stairs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
