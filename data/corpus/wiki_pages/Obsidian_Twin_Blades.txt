The Obsidian Twin Blades (Japanese:  , Hepburn: Koku Yoto?) are a set of two obsidian swords created by Icco White using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Obsidian Twin Blades resemble regular swords with black blades. They have some type of jewel embedded in their hilts.


== Functionality ==
The Obsidian Twin Blades function as regular swords. Due to being made of obsidian, they are particularly tough, enough to break Diamond Swords after a few hits, though they are also much heavier than average swords.


=== Techniques ===
Yamabuki uses the Obsidian Twin Blades to perform various techniques in combat.

Serpent of Eight Heads (, Yamata no Orochi?): A series of eight slashes in quick succession. This technique is named after Yamata no Orochi, an eight-headed serpent from Japanese mythology.
Kamaitachi (, Kamaitachi?): Yamabuki holds each sword outward, and begins to spin incredibly fast, creating a tornado of slashes. This technique is named after the Kamaitachi, a "sickle weasel" yokai.

== History ==
Ten years ago, Icco White passed through the ninjas' hidden village. He crafted the Obsidian Twin Blades while there, leaving them behind when he moved on. The twin blades were eventually inherited by Yamabuki, who took them with him when he was exiled from the village.
After his exile, Yamabuki began to challenge other adventurers to combat, aiming to become the strongest in the world. He used the Obsidian Twin Blades to achieve 999 victories, finally facing his first defeat against Nico White and Gray. Motivated by his loss, Nico decided to discover the end of the world, bringing his twin blades as his primary weapons. Throughout his journey, he occasionally ran into Nico's party, continuing to wield his twin blades the whole time.
Later on in his journey, Yamabuki passed through Metys Village. While there, Don Gold tricked him into handing over the twin blades, claiming them for himself and imprisoning Yamabuki. Don Gold's ownership of the twin blades lasted for an unknown amount of time until Nico's party reached the village, who stole the blades from Don Gold and returned them to Yamabuki.
