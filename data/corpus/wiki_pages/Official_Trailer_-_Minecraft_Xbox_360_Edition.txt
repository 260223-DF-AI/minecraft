Official Trailer - Minecraft Xbox 360 Edition, also known as  is the official trailer for Minecraft Xbox 360 Edition.


== Summary ==
Wielding a stone pickaxe, a player looks over the Overworld. Suddenly, several more players begin to join the game, spawning right behind the viewer in a dirt castle. The players split off, demonstrating splitscreen, frolicking through plains, digging for coal, hunting mobs, and building a wooden house. The screen changes to four player splitscreen, all crafting different items.
The trailer then returns to the castle, which appear to be in a shouting match with a similar mound across from it. Apparently, they engage in combat over bridges, one player clearing a blockade. Eight player splitscreen is then show on a block, with the promise of "8 friends, on Xbox Live!" More players dig in a cave, one obtaining diamonds, before another opens a nether portal. The adventure continues in the Nether, one player burning to death. They are then shown building together in the Overworld, doing various activities.
