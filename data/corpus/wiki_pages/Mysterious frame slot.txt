mysterious_frame_slot was an unused block in Bedrock Edition.


== Obtaining ==
mysterious_frame_slot could only be obtained using third-party editors.


=== Breaking ===

mysterious_frame_slot dropped nothing when broken.


== Behavior ==
mysterious_frame_slot had no behavior, and thus defaulted to the unknown block behavior. It broke instantly, and was able to be placed. Like ice, it had low friction.
