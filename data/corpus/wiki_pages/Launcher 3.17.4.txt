3.17.4 is an update to the Minecraft Launcher that was released on July 23, 2025.


== Changes ==
Changed the font.
Reverted the home animation from Chase the Skies.
A bunch more stability, accessibility & user experience improvements & bug fixes.
A few important fixes to how things work on the Realms pages, the Marketplace Pass page & beyond.


== Fixes ==
MCL-24242 - The drop down menus on the Home page of the launcher are overlapped by the play/pause looping news button.
