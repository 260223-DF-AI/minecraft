1.9.19, the release of the Discovery Update, is the final update for New Nintendo 3DS Edition, released on January 15–16, 2019. It added many new features primarily focused on exploration (similar to Java Edition 1.11), such as woodland mansions and explorer maps. It also added some features revolving around the color palette of the game (similar to Java Edition 1.12), such as concrete and glazed terracotta. Other new features included new mobs, such as llama and vexes.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Concrete

Comes in the 16 traditional dye colors.
Created when concrete powder comes into contact with water.
 Concrete powder

Comes in the 16 traditional dye colors.
Gravity affected (like sand and gravel).
When it touches water, it turns into a concrete block.
Can be crafted from 4 sand and 4 gravel.
 Glazed terracotta

Comes in the 16 traditional dye colors.
Can be obtained by smelting terracotta.
 Dyed beds

Can be crafted with colored wool or by dyeing a white bed.
 Shulker box

Works similarly to a chest, but keeps its content when broken.
If it has any contents inside, it will drop itself in Creative mode as well.
Cannot be placed inside another shulker box.
Can be crafted by using a chest and two shulker shells.
Can be dyed in any of the available dye colors.
 Frosted ice

Created when using Frost Walker.
Cannot be obtained in inventory, even when using Silk Touch.
 Magma block

Can be crafted using four magma cream.
Can generate in the Nether.
Mobs and players take 1HP damage every second while touching it, similar to a cactus.
It emits as much light as it receives from other sources.
 Bone block

Can be crafted from 9 bone meal.
Can be crafted back into 9 bone meal.
 Nether wart block

Can be crafted from 9 nether wart.
It cannot be crafted back into nether wart.
 Red nether brick

Can be crafted from 2 nether wart and 2 nether bricks.


=== Items ===
 Explorer map

Can only be obtained from cartographer villagers.
Can be used to locate some structures.
 Totem of undying

Can be obtained from killing evokers.
Holding it in a hotbar slot when the player dies will activate it and save their life.
Gives player the Absorption and Regeneration effects when activated.
 Iron and gold nuggets

Can be smelted from iron and gold tools and armor.
Can be also obtained from iron ingots or gold ingots.
 Spawn eggs for evokers, llamas, vexes and vindicators

Used to spawn evokers, llamas, vexes or vindicators.


=== Mobs ===
 Llamas

Spawns in hills and savanna biomes.
Attacks by spitting.
Neutral to mobs and players.
 Vindicators

Hostile toward players and villagers.
Holds an iron axe.
Spawn in the woodland mansions upon generation. They will not respawn.
May drop emeralds and an iron axe upon death.
 Evokers

In battle, they will summon vexes and fangs to attack.
Hostile toward players and villagers.
Will always drop a totem of undying upon death. May drop emeralds too.
 Vexes

Spawn only when summoned by an evoker.
Hostile toward players and villagers.
Holds an iron sword in their main hand.
Cannot drop their iron sword.
 Cartographer villagers

White-robed villagers (librarians) that can sell explorer maps.


=== World generation ===
 Woodland mansion

Illagers spawn here.
Can be located with a woodland explorer maps.
 Underground fossils

Generate in the underground.
Natural magma block generation in the Nether
