Biome Settlers Skin Pack 1 is a skin pack themed around the inhabitants of the tundra, forest and desert biomes. In January 2026, the skin pack was delisted from the Marketplace in Bedrock Edition, though it is still available in Legacy Console Edition on some platforms. The skin pack can still be obtained on Bedrock Edition if purchased on Legacy Console Edition.
