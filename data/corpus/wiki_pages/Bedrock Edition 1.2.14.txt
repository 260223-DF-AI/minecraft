1.2.14 is a hotfix for Bedrock Edition released exclusively to iOS devices. The update removed the developer commands that were accidentally introduced in 1.2.13. It is compatible with 1.2.13 servers. Although two beta versions (1.2.14.2 and 1.2.14.3) were released under the same name as this version, they were actually for 1.4.0.


== Changes ==
Removed the debug commands that were accidentally included in the previous update.
