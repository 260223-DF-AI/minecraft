"Équinoxe" is an album-exclusive song composed by C418 that only appears on Minecraft - Volume Alpha.


== Track description ==
Continuing from "Oxygène" and sharing some of the same melodies, "Équinoxe" is a lighthearted piano melody accompanied by synths from the previous track and ending in trilling arpeggios.


== Trivia ==
The name "Équinoxe" is a reference to the album of the same name by Jean-Michel Jarre.
