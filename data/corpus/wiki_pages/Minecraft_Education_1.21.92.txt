1.21.92 is a minor update to Minecraft Education, released on August 20, 2025, which fixes some bugs.


== Fixes ==
A few folders in Library always appeared in English no matter if you changed the language, which are now correctly localized.
Fixed an issue with connecting to long running multiplayer sessions.
Fixed old worlds being corrupted when saving.
Additional improvements to performance and product stability.
