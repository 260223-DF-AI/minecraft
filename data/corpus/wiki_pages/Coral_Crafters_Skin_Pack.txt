Coral Crafters Skin Pack is a skin pack that includes 8 ocean-themed skins, celebrating the release of the Update Aquatic. As part of Mojang's Coral Crafters campaign to help the oceans, net proceeds of the skin pack's sale were contributed to The Nature Conservancy, a non-profit environmental organization. At some point, the skin pack was delisted.
