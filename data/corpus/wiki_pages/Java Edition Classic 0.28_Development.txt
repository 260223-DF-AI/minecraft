Classic 0.28 refers to three internal development versions made by Notch made during Survival Test. They are versioned under "0.27", but are indeed early versions of 0.28.


== Sheep (October 25, 2009) ==
  

Word of Notch post

Sheep are shown, and drop red cloth.


== Bleached Sheep (October 25, 2009) ==

Word of Notch post; YouTube Video

Sheep now drop white cloth.


== Creative (October 26, 2009) ==

Word of Notch post

Reimplemented Creative mode.
Only works in singleplayer.
Has higher fps than Survival.
