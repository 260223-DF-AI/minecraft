Prismarine stairs are a decorative stairs variant of prismarine that does not generate naturally and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Prismarine stairs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Crafting ===


=== Stonecutting ===


== Usage ==

Like its parent block, the normal prismarine stairs are uniquely useful for decoration as it has an animated texture. The cracks in prismarine stairs slowly cycle in color through light blue, green, purple, and dark blue. The full animation for the prismarine stair texture changes color 22 times in an irregular sequence before the whole pattern repeats taking 5 minutes and 30 seconds.


=== Note blocks ===
Prismarine stairs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
