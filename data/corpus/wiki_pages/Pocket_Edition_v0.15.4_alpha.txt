v0.15.4 alpha is a minor update to Pocket Edition, Gear VR Edition, and the Windows 10 Edition Beta released on July 28-29, 2016 to fix some of the bugs and crashes in v0.15.3. For all non-Android platforms, this includes bug fixes from v0.15.3.
This version was the base for the first release of New Nintendo 3DS Edition.


== Additions ==
Animation for Signing in to Xbox Live accounts


=== Skin packs ===
Version exclusive: Villains Skin Pack.


== Changes ==
More localization fixes.
Fixed the HUD moving when riding an animal in VR.
Fixed a few texture issues.
Some tweaks to Gear VR Edition performance & experience.
Increased draw distance for Windows 10 Edition.
