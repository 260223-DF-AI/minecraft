15w34c is the twelfth snapshot for Java Edition 1.9, released on August 21, 2015 mainly to extend features added in 15w34a and 15w34b.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Particles

New sweepAttack particle.
Used for the new defensive "sweep" attack.


== Changes ==


=== Items ===
Axes

New offensive attack.
Hitting another player who is blocking with a shield has a chance to disable the player's shield for 5 seconds.
25% base chance, plus 5 percentage points for each level of Efficiency on the axe, plus 75 percentage points if sprinting.
This is visible on the shield icon through a "rolling curtain" animation.
Chorus fruit & ender pearls

Now have a cooldown every time they are used.
Have the same animation as the shields.
Chorus fruits can no longer teleport the player on top of the Nether roof.
Swords

New defensive attack while crouching or standing and having a full damage bar.
Mobs near the one hit (bounding box expanded by 1 block horizontally and 0.25 vertically) and within 3 blocks of the attacker are knocked back without damage. Knockback is 80% of that done by Knockback I and is not affected by the Knockback enchantment.
Creates a sweep attack particle.


=== Gameplay ===
Combat

Critical hits and sprint-knockback attacks now require an attack cooldown percentage of 84.8% or greater.
Added the attack speed tooltip.
When an attack is blocked by a shield, the attacker may be knocked back.
Different attack speeds and damages for different tools.
Hoes are the fastest, but only slightly more powerful than before, axes are much more powerful, but are also the slowest.


== Fixes ==
3 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.9

MC-69108 – Command `/time query daytime` not resetting after day-night cycle.
MC-78761 – Noteblock doesn't remember that it is powered on unloading a chunk.
From the 1.9 development versions

MC-84198 – Chorus fruit can teleport the player to the roof of the Nether.
