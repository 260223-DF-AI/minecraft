== Enchantments ==


=== New ===
Between snapshot 19w02a (13th for Java Edition 1.14) and 1.14.3 Pre-Release 1 (including both bounds) all different type Protection enchantments [Protection, Projectile Protection, Fire Protection, and Blast Protection] were not mutually exclusive. It means that any peace of armor could have them all at once (added with anvil). Obtained this way armor saves all its properties even after 1.14 Pre-Release 2 when Protection enchantments became mutually exclusive again.
