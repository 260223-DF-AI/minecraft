Ask Mojang, also referred to as Ask Mojang Home Edition, Ask Mojang Midsummer, and Ask Mojang Anything is a video series on the Minecraft YouTube channel, where Mojang employees answer any questions asked by Minecraft players, mostly on Twitter and YouTube posts that use the hashtag "#askmojang".
Created in July 2, 2019, the series' playlist currently counts with 28 videos.
