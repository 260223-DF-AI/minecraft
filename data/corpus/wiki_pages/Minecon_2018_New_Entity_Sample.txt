Minecon 2018 New Entity Sample is an official add-on sample to show the capabilities of adding custom entities without replacing the existing ones. This add-on was showcased on MINECON Earth 2018.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
 Duck Spawn Egg
Spawns a duck when used.
 Scorpion Spawn Egg
Spawns a scorpion when used.
 Skeletaur Spawn Egg
Spawns a skeletaur when used.
 Spider Illager Spawn Egg
Spawns a spider illager when used.
 Vile Spider Spawn Egg
Spawns a vile spider when used.


=== Mobs ===
  Duck
A passive mob similar to chickens but with two textures, one for its baby form and another for adult.
Instead of being fed and bred with seeds, it uses bread.
 Scorpion
A mob that lives in deserts.
It can have different sizes.
It is neutral.
 Skeletaur
A faster variant of skeletons.
When the player is far the skeletaur shoots arrows like a regular skeleton. When the player gets close, it kicks the player.
 Spider Illager
An illager similar to the evoker but instead of summoning vexes or evoker fangs, it summons vile spiders.
Affected by the Bane of Arthropods.
 Vile Spider
A smaller variant of spiders that can only be spawned by the spider illager,
It inflicts Poison when it attacks.
It makes a unique particle effect when walking.

== Trivia ==
Ducks were later added to Minecraft Dungeons as a cosmetic pet.
Skeletaurs seem to have a bug that occasionally made their bow glow or simply not render properly.
Scorpions would later reappear in an unreleased adventure map, demonstrated at Minecraft Live 2020.
