Beta 1.14.0.6 is the fifth and final beta version for Bedrock Edition 1.14.0, released on November 6, 2019, which fixes bugs.


== Fixes ==
Crashes

Fixed several crashes that could occur during gameplay.
Fixed a crash that could sometimes occur when throwing lingering potions.
General

Fixed capes not rendering in some skin packs. (MCPE-54181)
Added an extra fix that prevents skins taking too long to load in. (MCPE-45038)
Fixed misspelled splash text.
Clearing the player's inventory with commands now works more consistently in certain Marketplace maps.
Gameplay

Fixed a bug that was preventing redstone signals from traveling across some chunk borders.
The player's hands no longer rotate off-screen when riding a horse and holding a map.
Carved pumpkins once again create iron golems when dispensed.
Mobs

Bees can now pass through open doors.
