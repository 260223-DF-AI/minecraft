Cooldown may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Mechanics ===
Attack cooldown - the melee attacking mechanic exclusive to Java Edition
Attack speed - the attribute which determines the attack cooldown of a weapon
Charge time - the time it takes to charge a ranged weapon before firing it
Use cooldown - the mechanic used by certain items like ender pearls
