Minecraft comes to Xbox Game Pass! is a trailer announcing the addition of Minecraft to Xbox Game Pass, featuring an assortment of characters from various skin packs.


== Summary ==
Inside a furnished household, Composer and Trapper rush over to a couch. After pulling a lever, a sign saying Xbox Game Pass opens up, revealing a sunset. They rush out into the outside, and immediately run into Forest Hunter and Tinkerer. They converge and head over to a village, where Alex is looking over from the top of a church, before climbing down a ladder. Alex and Steve get to work building a town square out of various materials at unusually fast speeds, before the others arrive, and see even more players coming in. The video zooms out on the reveal of a ginormous build of the Game Pass logo, which the video was set inside of at an impossible angle all along.
