Beta 1.19.30.25 (Android) or Preview 1.19.30.25 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox) is the fifth and final beta, and sixth and final Preview version for Bedrock Edition 1.19.30, released on September 1, 2022, which fixes bugs.


== Fixes ==
Graphical

Fixed an issue with flowing lava and water textures not animating on some iOS devices
Stability and Performance

Fixed a crash that would occur on some devices when loading in worlds with texture packs applied (MCPE-160800)
