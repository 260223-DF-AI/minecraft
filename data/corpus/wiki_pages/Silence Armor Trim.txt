Silence armor trim is a type of smithing template used in smithing tables to add a decorative trim to armor. It is consumed when used, but can be duplicated using an existing template, cobbled deepslate, and diamonds.


== Obtaining ==
This item is found in ancient cities. Once obtained, it can be duplicated using the crafting recipe below.

The silence armor trim appears to have sculk creeping onto / infecting it.
