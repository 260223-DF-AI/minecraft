Eerie Island is a haunted island and part of the Spooky Isles in Minecraft Mini-Series. It is the second island where the game show took place.


== Description ==
Eerie Island is mostly a jungle with abundant vegetation. However, some parts of the island are plains that lack any trees. The plains house the farm and the well, as well as the bed challenge and a scoreboard.


== Points of interest ==


=== Farm ===

The farm is where the first challenge on Eerie Island took place. During daytime, it is inhabited solely by farm animals, but when night falls, they transform into farm monsters, chickens become hostile, and the evil scarecrow Cropsy comes to life.


=== Well ===

The well is where the second challenge on Eerie Island took place. It is fabled to be haunted by The Well Wisher, a vengeful spirit that drags anyone approaching beneath the water.


=== Bed challenge ground ===
The third and final challenge on Eerie Island took place on this ground, made of wooden pillars with beds on top. The challenge consisted of collecting the most feathers from the top of slime blocks using beds. The beds later turn out to be haunted, as two of them turned into Shams but didn't cause any harm, only startling Rudi and Durango.
