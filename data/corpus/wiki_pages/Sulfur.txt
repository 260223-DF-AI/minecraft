Sulfur is a block that naturally generates in sulfur caves alongside cinnabar, in sulfur pools, and as part of sulfur springs.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Sulfur drops as an item if mined by any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, it drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Sulfur generates throughout sulfur caves and as part of sulfur springs.
