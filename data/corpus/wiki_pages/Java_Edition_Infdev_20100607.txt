A version of Minecraft Infdev was released on June 7, 2010. This release ended a spell of a month and a half of no updates to the game.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Doors

Crafted with planks.

 Ladders

Crafted with sticks.

 Signs

Crafted with planks and sticks.

Are very big, have collision, take a very long time to mine, cannot be placed on the sides of blocks, and drop one wooden plank when mined.

== Bugs ==
9 bugs reported

Placing doors on farmland causes the bottom part of the door to disappear and the door is now unable to be interacted with.
Placing a door in a 1 block tall space causes the door to delete the block occupying the topmost door space.
Placing doors on glass, slabs, ladders, and signs cause the doors to immediately break and drop two doors, allowing for door duplication.
Trees can grow into the top door block, causing only a bottom half door to remain.
While mining signs, they give out grass block particles.
When placing a sign, it is rotated by 1 increment more than supposed to.
Placing two non-opaque blocks (ex. glass, slabs) next to each other and placing a sign on top of each causes the signs to break, the game to crash, and the player will be left with a 0-stacked sign in the inventory which can then be duplicated.
Placing ladders on any non-solid block makes ladders invisible. The ladders will have same data value as the last "correctly" placed ladder. Upon reloading the world they will take up the space of a full block and are unable to be climbed.
Jumping in water may crash the game.
