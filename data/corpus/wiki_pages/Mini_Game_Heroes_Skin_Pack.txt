Mini Game Heroes Skin Pack is a skin pack that builds upon the Mini Game Masters Skin Pack and is themed around several teams of three characters, each embodying a mob partaking in some sort of sport. This skin pack was released in promotion of the mini games, which were included in the Legacy Console Editions, and was part of the Glide Track Pack Season Pass.
