Remove may refer to:


== Control ==
Q, a control that removes item(s) from the inventory of a player.


== Enchantment ==
Curse of Vanishing, an enchantment that makes items disappear upon the owner's death.


== Commands ==
/clear, a command that removes all the items from the inventory of a player.
/effect clear, a command that can remove all the effects of a player.
/item, a command that removes items by replacing them.‌[Java Edition  only]
/kill, a command that removes all the entities in a range, or a specific entity like the closest one.
/remove, the Minecraft Education equivalent of /kill.
/replaceitem, the Bedrock Edition equivalent of /item replace.
