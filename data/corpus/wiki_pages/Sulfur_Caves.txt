The sulfur caves is a cave biome composed of sulfur and cinnabar blocks, where sulfur cubes and cave spiders spawn. Sulfur pools with potent sulfur generate here, in addition to sulfur spikes.


== Generation ==
Sulfur caves generate underground at the same depth as lush caves and dripstone caves, although they are much smaller in size and more fragmented. Sulfur springs sometimes generate at the surface, indicating there are sulfur caves below.
The biome generates only at low weirdness values ( -0.45). This means that it commonly borders any other cave biome, and above-ground are usually mountains or plateaus, and never generates close to rivers, although it can generate under the ocean. However, if sulfur caves generate at higher erosion, there may also be more middle biomes, desert biomes, and shattered biomes.
Rarely, sulfur caves can generate above ground at weirdness values above 1.0, which occurs inside mountain rings.


== Description ==
The main landscape is composed of sulfur and cinnabar layers in approximately same amount of blocks, with frequent sulfur pools possessing a unique acid-green color of water. Potent sulfur is found only on the bottom of these pools, creating noxious gas with white bubbles particles in and on top of the water. Nausea is inflicted to mobs and players when they come in contact with this noxious gas. The gas can be removed by destroying the potent sulfur under the water.
Other terrain blocks such as deepslate and stone variants generate between the sulfur and cinnabar, and glow lichen is noticeably more common.[BE  only]
Ores rarely generate here, because sulfur and cinnabar replace most stone and deepslate blocks.
With Vibrant Visuals, sulfur caves use warmish color grading, which tints the biome to look more humid and warm.
