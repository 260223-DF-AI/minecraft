1.21.41 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on October 22, 2024, which fixes some bugs.


== Fixes ==
3 issues fixed
MCPE-186720 – Dyed bundles aren't translated
MCPE-186928 – Chunks in all dimensions have their biome replaced with an Ocean biome upon reloading in updated older worlds created prior to 1.17.40
Others

Fixed an issue where game mode and difficulty were not being preserved after uploading or replacing world in Realms Slot. It should now be safe to play Hardcore on Realms, regardless of what kind of world the Hardcore world is replacing.


== Trivia ==
This hotfix was released within two and a half hours after 1.21.40, making it the shortest-lived update.[verify for Bedrock Edition]
This update is the first hotfix for the PS5.
