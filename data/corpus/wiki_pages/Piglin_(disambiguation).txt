== Minecraft Legends ==


=== Mobs ===
Horde of the Bastion, a horde of piglins that likes fortifying their outposts with many walls, thus having the most fortified outposts compared to the other two hordes.
Portal Guard, Horde of the Bastion-exclusive piglins which have a flail arm which they use to attack.
Brute, elite piglins that wield two jagged blades that are used to deal damage.
Horde of the Spore, a horde of piglins that is less fortified than the Horde of the Bastion at their outposts as they rarely use walls, but they station their outposts on the high ground.
Lava Launcher, ranged, mounted and massive hoglins that have a magma loaded spring-cannon.
Sporeback, elite ranged piglin units that throw explosive mushrooms from their back.
Horde of the Hunt, a horde of piglins that doesn't use any fortification in their outposts at all, but their outposts are larger than the others nonetheless. This horde has and uses a lot of bones for their outposts.
Pigmadillo, large, elite piglin units that bowl through their enemies.
Warboar, hoglins used by the piglins to attack enemies.
Runt, small piglins that make up the bulk of the piglins' forces in all their hordes.
