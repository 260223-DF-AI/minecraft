CU59 is a version of Xbox One Edition released on April 9, 2024. This version was released more than 4 years after the previous update to Xbox One Edition.


== Changes ==
Removed the "Download the latest version of Minecraft for FREE!" button from the options menu.
Reset any skins that players had selected as their favorite but did not own.
Updated all splashes to match with Bedrock Edition.
