Warner Bros. Pictures is an American film production and distribution company that serves as the motion picture arm of the Warner Bros. Motion Picture Group division of Warner Bros., which are both ultimately owned by the mass media conglomerate Warner Bros. Discovery. It is currently one of the five live-action film studios within the Warner Bros. Motion Picture Group, the others being New Line Cinema, Castle Rock Entertainment, and a minority stake in Spyglass Media Group.
Warner Bros. Pictures is the co-production company and worldwide distributor of the 2025 live-action animated film A Minecraft Movie and its sequel.
