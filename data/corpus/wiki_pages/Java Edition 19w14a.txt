19w14a is the thirty-first snapshot for Java Edition 1.14, released on April 3, 2019, which changes player positioning mechanics, allowing players to crawl in 1-block-tall areas.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Debug screen

Now has a new "Sounds" section, listing how many sounds are currently playing near the player out of 247, and streaming sounds (jukeboxes and ingame music) out of 8.
Splashes

Added "It came from space."


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
Cats

Now naturally spawn in villages properly instead of only spawning during world generation.
Pillagers

Can no longer open doors.
Villagers

Nitwits and jobless villagers now bobble their head and grunt if the player tries to trade with them.


=== World generation ===
Pillager outposts

No longer can spawn inside of villages.
Can now be toggled in custom-preset superflat worlds via "pillager_outpost".
Villages

Instead of oak saplings, sticks now generate in the chest of savanna village houses.
The Nether

Soul sand and gravel now generate at lava sea level, instead of between Y level 60 and 65.


=== Gameplay ===
Advancements

Monsters Hunted
No longer requires polar bears.
Monster Hunter
Killing polar bears no longer triggers this advancement.
Player positioning

The player's position now adjusts to the gap they are within:
1.5 blocks: sneaking posture.
<1 block: swimming animation.
Being forced into an area under two blocks high causes the player to adopt the appropriate posture.
