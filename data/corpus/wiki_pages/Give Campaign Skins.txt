Give Campaign Skins are four skin packs, namely Brody's Give Campaign Skin, Claire's Give Campaign Skin, Joslyn's Give Campaign Skin and Lucy's Give Campaign Skin, that include 1 skin each. Each skin pack was given to a Microsoft employee who won it during a "Give Campaign" auction, an auction held internally each year at Microsoft to raise funds for non-profit organizations.
