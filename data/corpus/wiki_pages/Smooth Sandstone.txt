Smooth sandstone is a variant of sandstone, obtained by smelting sandstone.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Smooth sandstone can be mined with any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, the block drops nothing.

Despite regular sandstone having hardness and blast resistances of 0.8, smooth sandstone has a blast resistance of 6 and hardness of 2. This is likely due to historical reasons, specifically as a result of hardness being inherited from slab blocks, however the "correct"/"expected" values were changed to the "incorrect" values in Bedrock Edition.


=== Natural generation ===
Smooth sandstone generates in desert villages.


=== Smelting ===


== Usage ==
Unlike sand, smooth sandstone is never affected by gravity.
