16w39c is the ninth snapshot for Java Edition 1.11, released on September 30, 2016 to fix some bugs and crashes from 16w39a and 16w39b.


== Changes ==
Dispensers

Now dispense boats facing the same way as the dispenser, instead of toward the dispenser.
Shulkers

Can now be spawned on the sides and bottoms of blocks through the usage of spawn eggs.
