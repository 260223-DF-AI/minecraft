Fatal Poison inflicts damage to entities over time, and can kill both undead and non-undead mobs, similar to Wither. The effect is actually called "Poison," but its ID distinguishes it from the other Poison effect, which cannot kill. In Survival mode, the effect applies only to parrots (when feeding them a cookie) and is not obtainable by players.

== Causes ==


== Immune mobs ==
Withers and ender dragon are immune to fatal poison.
Witches are 95% resistant to damage from fatal poison.


== Death message ==
If the player is killed by Fatal Poison, the death message "<player> was killed by magic" appears, as when killed by a potion of Harming.
