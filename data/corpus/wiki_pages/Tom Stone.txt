Tom Stone is a freelance scriptwriter, writer and editor. He wrote scripts for videos on the Minecraft YouTube channel, and was was the former head of editorial for Minecraft.net at Mojang Studios until February 2020. He also joined Mojang on January 26, 2017, when he was a creative communications assistant, and announced his departure from Mojang in an article on Minecraft.net, but he continued writing articles and videos as a freelance worker.
