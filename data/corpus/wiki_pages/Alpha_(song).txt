"Alpha" is a song composed by C418 that plays during the end credits sequence.


== Locations ==


== Track description ==

"Alpha" is an emotional orchestral reprise of some tracks from Minecraft - Volume Alpha. The song begins with a soft, piano chords playing "Minecraft" (0:00 - 2:59) with a gradual build-up of strings. Later, a wind instrument plays the main melody of "Mice on Venus"(3:00), before a full-string orchestra plays out the remainder of the song. Mid-way through, the orchestra slows and stops, while a piano plays another part of "Mice on Venus". The string orchestra then returns to finish out the remaining portion of the song.
A piano once again plays parts of "Mice on Venus" as the song rises into Battlemode 2 from Legacy Console(4:56 - 6:06) which is then transitioned over to a Moog synthesizer playing "Moog City" (6:07). The song gradually builds up the Moog synthesizer and piano chords, before a drop causes the Moog synthesizer to become quiet. The piano chords once again build up, joined together by strings. After a final piano drop, the Moog synthesizer plays quietly in the background, as the piano plays the beginning notes of "Sweden" (8:14). An individual can then be heard getting on a piano and playing the main melody of "Sweden". The song then fades out, as an echoey Moog-like synthesizer plays repeatedly.

Windows: %PROGRAMFILES%\WindowsApps\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP_<version>_x64_8wekyb3d8bbwe\data\resource_packs\vanilla_music\sounds\music\game\end
All music files are in Ogg Vorbis.
