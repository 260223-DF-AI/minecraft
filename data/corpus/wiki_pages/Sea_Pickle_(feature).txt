Sea pickle is a feature that places a cluster of sea pickle blocks in the warm ocean biome.


== Generation ==
Sea pickle colonies generate on the bottom of warm oceans and can also be found on top of coral blocks in coral reefs. Each chunk has 16 chance to generate sea pickle colonies. They can be found in groups of 1 up to 4.
