1.31  is a version of PlayStation 4 Edition released on July 26, 2016.  
[edit]


== Additions ==
Added Battle Map Pack 2 (Atlantis, Ruin and Siege).
Added the Redstone Specialists Skin Pack.


== Changes ==
Disabled the previous Birthday and Minecon skin packs for players who had them.

Made changes to some item textures in the City Texture Pack, Fantasy Texture Pack, and Greek Mythology Mash-up Pack, so that they are more easily identifiable in Battle mini games.
Fixed various bugs with Battle:
Fix to display player names above crouching players when in Showdown mode.
Fix to allow thrown potions to go through non-solid blocks such as vines or tall grass.
Fix to stop non-solid blocks obstructing player attacks.
Fixed a couple of holes in the Cove Battle mini game map.
Fix to remove all mob heads from the player's inventory except any that are currently being worn when transitioning from the lobby to a map.
Fix to automatically replace mob heads in the armor slot when a helmet is collected.
Disabled "Take Everything" in the lobby.
Added "Select All" and "Deselect All" tooltips to the "Select Maps" menu.
Fix for an issue with hills generation.
Fix for an issue causing some blazes to not perform their attacks after becoming aggressive towards the player.
Fix to allow renamed items to be traded with a Villager.
Fixed a duplication bug.
Fix to remove the redundant option for creating white wool within the crafting menu using one white wool to create.
Fix for an issue where a pig struck by a lightning turns into a zombie pigman without a golden sword.
Fix for an incorrect death message when the player is killed by lightning.
Fix for some issues with achievements (including the Passing The Time achievement).
