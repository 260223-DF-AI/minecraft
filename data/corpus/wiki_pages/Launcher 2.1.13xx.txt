2.1.1351 (Windows), 2.1.1350 (macOS), or 2.1.1349 (Linux) is an update to the launcher released on July 26, 2018.


== Additions ==
When installing a Minecraft version, the launcher now checks if the computer meets the requirements. If it does not, a message is displayed.
The launcher now shows the cause of a game crash.

MCL-9498: .dll files don't exist error.
