14w34a is the forty-ninth snapshot for Java Edition 1.8, released on August 18, 2014, which makes changes to rabbits.


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
Killer rabbit

Texture of the killer rabbit changed from  to .
It is now more bloody and has darker and differently shaped eyes, making it much more distinct from the white textured rabbit.
Automatically spawns with a name tag 'The Killer Bunny'.
No longer tries to attack the player in Creative mode.
No longer spawns naturally.
Changed the black and white mottled fur rabbit's eye color from pink to black.
Rabbits

Now use the AI of regular passive mobs.
They cannot be tamed, and do not flee from the player.
When hit, they leap in random directions.
