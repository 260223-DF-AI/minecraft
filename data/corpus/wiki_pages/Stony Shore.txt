A stony shore or stone beach is a coastal biome that is almost entirely made of stone and gravel. Stony shores replace beach biomes in some mountainous areas. 


== Generation ==
A stony shore replaces normal beaches in areas with mountainous terrain at low erosion values.


== Description ==
Stony shores are made entirely of stone, with long strips of gravel. They are usually a steep drop into the water they border. Stony shores may generate at high enough elevations to have snow on their peaks (above Y=120±8), even when bordering warm biomes. Water and lava springs frequently generate on the sides of stony shores. In Bedrock Edition, buried treasure can sometimes generate at high altitudes, and can even generate at Y=90; In Java Edition, it doesn't generate here.
The steep drops into the ocean below makes this variant dangerous, especially if the player is unaware of their surroundings.
