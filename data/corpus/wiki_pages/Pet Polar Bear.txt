A pet polar bear is a pet variant of the polar bear introduced in 25w14craftmine.


== Spawning ==
A pet polar bear is spawned next to the player on mine start when the "Pet Polar Bear" Player Unlock is active.


== Drops ==


== Behavior ==
The behaviors between the pet polar bear and the polar bear in regular versions are almost the same, but there are still some differences:

The player's melee attack can't damage the pet polar bear.
The pet polar bear doesn't attack the player when he or she approaches a baby bear.
The pet polar bear doesn't attack the fox and pet fox.
