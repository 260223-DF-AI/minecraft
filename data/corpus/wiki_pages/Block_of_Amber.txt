A block of amber, internally known as an amber block, is a storage block made of amber gems in 24w14potato. They closely resemble blocks of gold in coloration.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Mining blocks of amber requires an iron pickaxe or better.

== Usage ==


=== Crafting ingredient ===


=== Beacons ===
Blocks of amber can be used to "power" a beacon. The beacon can be powered by a 33 square of amber blocks underneath it, and can optionally also include a 55, 77, and 99 layer in the shape of a pyramid under the original layer to increase the effects from the beacon. The layers do not have to be entirely blocks of amber, as they may also contain blocks of iron, blocks of gold, blocks of diamond, blocks of netherite or blocks of emerald.
