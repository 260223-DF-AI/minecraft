== About ==
Moesh's job consisted of assisting with content management for the Minecraft franchise and adding new Realms maps. Moesh has a background of creating custom levels for games such as Quake II and Star Wars Jedi Knight: Dark Forces II, and as such is also responsible behind the level design of structures like the bastion remnant, the ancient city, and the trial chambers.
