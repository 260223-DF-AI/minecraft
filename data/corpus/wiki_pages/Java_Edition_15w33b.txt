15w33b is the eighth snapshot for Java Edition 1.9, released on August 12, 2015, to fix some bugs and crashes from 15w33a.


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Pick block

Now works on Survival for items that are in the player's inventory, but not in their hotbar.
