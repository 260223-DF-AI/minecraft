The Titan's Blazing Sword (巨大の光炎剣, Kyodai no Kōen Ken?) is a giant sword capable of destroying any Monster Spawner, created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Titan's Blazing Sword is a massive glowing sword.


== Functionality ==
When stabbed into a Monster Spawner, the Titan's Blazing Sword instantly destroys it. This works even on Spawners with the "Tenacity" Mod Power, which makes them otherwise unbreakable, even to Platinum's Block Piercer.


=== Crafting recipe ===


== History ==
While adventuring, Nico White stopped in a village with a Monster Spawner beneath it. Because of the Spawner's "Tenacity" Mod Power, it spawned monsters endless, and could not be broken; some time in the past, Icco White had solved this using the Sword of Light, but it was now near broken. While Nico was in the village, the Sword of Light finally broke, and monsters began to spawn in hordes again.
Having learned that Icco had entrusted the village's safety to him, Nico realized that it must be possible to permanently stop the Spawner using a combination of their crafts, and crafted the Titan's Blazing Sword using his materials and the Sword of Light's shattered pieces. He then thrust the sword into the Spawner, destroying it.
