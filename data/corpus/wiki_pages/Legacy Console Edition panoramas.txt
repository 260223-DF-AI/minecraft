Compared to other editions of Minecraft, Legacy Console Edition uses different panoramas exclusive to that edition. These were usually taken from inside the most recent tutorial world. This page documents all of them.


== Build 0016 ==

The panorama was introduced in Xbox 360 Edition 1.66.0016.0. This one was to Xbox 360 Edition 1.66.0035.0.


== Build 0054 ==

In Xbox 360 Edition 0.66.0054.0, the panorama was changed to the tutorial world. A night version of the panorama was also added.
These panoramas were used for versions from Xbox 360 Edition 0.66.0054.0 to Xbox 360 Edition TU4.


== TU5 ==

In Xbox 360 Edition, these panoramas were used for versions TU5 and TU6.


== TU7 ==

In Xbox 360 Edition, these panoramas were used for versions from TU7 to TU11.


== TU12 ==

In Xbox 360 Edition, these panoramas were used for versions from TU12 to TU19.


== TU20 ==

In Xbox 360 Edition, these panoramas were used for versions from TU20 to TU30.


== TU31 ==

In Xbox 360 Edition, these panoramas were used for versions from TU31 to TU45.


== TU46 ==

In Xbox 360 Edition, these panoramas were used for version from TU46 to TU68.


== TU69 ==

In Xbox 360 Edition, this panorama is used from TU69 onward.


== Trivia ==
In the panorama of Build 0016, a second castle can be seen in the far distance.
