A stone brick slab is a decorative slab variant of stone bricks that generates in overworld structures and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Stone brick slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Stone brick slabs generate as part of ruined portals in the Overworld as well as in strongholds.

=== Note blocks ===
Stone brick slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
