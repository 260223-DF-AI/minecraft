Mounts of Mayhem is a game drop that was released on December 9, 2025 as Java Edition 1.21.11 and Bedrock Edition 1.21.130.  Its theme is a combination of both "mounts" and "combat". It introduces spears, netherite horse armor, nautiluses, nautilus armor, parched, zombie nautiluses, camel husks, zombie horses spawning naturally, and the ability for most mounts to swim. It was announced at Minecraft LIVE  September 2025.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
         Nautilus armor

A new tiered armor type for equipping a tamed nautilus or zombie nautilus.
Cannot be crafted.
Copper, iron, gold, and diamond variants can be found in ocean structure chests.
Netherite variant can be obtained by upgrading the diamond variant in a smithing table.
 Netherite horse armor

A new type of horse armor.
Provides 19 (  9.5) armor points, 3 armor toughness, and 1 knockback resistance.
Obtained by upgrading diamond horse armor using a smithing table with a netherite upgrade template.
Spawn eggs:

 Camel husk spawn egg
 Nautilus spawn egg
 Parched spawn egg
 Zombie nautilus spawn egg

      Spear

A new tiered weapon.
Has a longer attack range than the sword or any other weapon.
Includes two main attacks: jab and charge.
The faster the player moves while charging, the more damage is done to an entity.
Can be crafted using 2 sticks and its corresponding tier item or by upgrading from diamond to netherite using a smithing table.


=== Enchantments ===
 Lunge

Spear exclusive enchantment with 3 levels.
Only works with the jab attack.
When doing a jab attack with Lunge, it propels the player horizontally in the view direction.
The view angle needs to be perfectly leveled horizontally to achieve maximum lunge distance.
Consumes saturation and hunger points at an amount dependent on the enchantment level, as well as 1 durability point.
Can only be used if the player has 6 or more hunger points.


=== Effects ===
 Breath of the Nautilus

Freezes the entity's oxygen bar.
Applied when riding on a nautilus or zombie nautilus.


=== Mobs ===
 Camel husk

An uncommon undead variant of the regular camel that spawns in deserts at night.
 Spawns with two riders: a husk wielding an iron spear and a parched, forming a camel husk jockey.
The husk rider has a chance to drop a rabbit's foot when killed.
Counts as a hostile mob until ridden by a player, despawning like other hostile mobs.
Does not burn in sunlight.
Behaves similarly to a regular camel, except it cannot be bred.
Can be tempted and healed using a rabbit's foot.
Drops rotten flesh like any other zombie-type mob.
 Nautilus

An aquatic neutral mount that spawns in oceans.
Can dash attack at any mob if provoked.
Occasionally hunts pufferfish.
Immune to poison.
Can be tamed and bred with pufferfish and buckets of pufferfish, though it can be fed any type of fish or fish bucket afterward.
 Baby nautiluses have a unique model and sounds.
Rideable with a saddle, controlled with mouse/joystick movements, similar to the happy ghast.
Has a dash movement that can be used while riding it, similar to the camel.
When ridden, the rider is given the Breath of the Nautilus effect, freezing their oxygen bar.
When on land, the nautilus becomes dehydrated.
Has a 5% chance to drop a nautilus shell when killed.
 Parched

A variant of the skeleton that spawns in deserts at night.
Does not burn in sunlight, similarly to the husk.
Shoots arrows of Weakness.
Immune to Weakness.
Has a slower rate of fire compared to a normal skeleton, similar to the bogged.
 Zombie nautilus

A rare undead variant of the regular nautilus that spawns in oceans and dripstone caves.
 Can also spawn as a coral variant in a warm ocean.
 Spawns being ridden by a drowned wielding a trident, forming a zombie nautilus jockey.
Counts as a hostile mob while untamed, despawning like other hostile mobs.
Behaves similarly to a regular nautilus, except it cannot be bred.
Swims 10% faster than regular nautiluses when swimming.
Doesn't dehydrate when on land.
Burns in sunlight.
Nautilus armor prevents the zombie nautilus from burning in sunlight.
Drops rotten flesh like any other zombie-type mob.

Donkey, camel, horse, mule, and zombie horse

Piglin, husk, zombie and zombified piglin

Changed texture.
Now spawns naturally in plains and savanna biomes at night.
 Spawns being ridden by a zombie wielding an iron spear, forming a zombie horseman.
The zombie rider has a chance to drop a red mushroom when killed.

== Promotions ==


=== Dungeon Descent ===

Released on January 12, 2026, Dungeon Descent is a promotional add-on expanding on the drop, with features such as dungeons, charms, and decorative dungeon and horse stable themed blocks.


=== Challengers Tournament ===

The Mounts of Mayhem: Challengers Tournament, also referred to as the Mayhem Tournament, is a series of articles on Minecraft.net written to promote the Mounts of Mayhem drop.


=== Reward Drops ===

Three special reward drops are available to promote the release of the game drop. One is available through TikTok, and the other two are available through Twitch.


=== Community Challenge ===

The Mounts of Mayhem Community Challenge was a community challenge promoting the release of the game drop. Announced on December 10, 2025, the challenge began on December 12 and concluded on December 15, 2025.
