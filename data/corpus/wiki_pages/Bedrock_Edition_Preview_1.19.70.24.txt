Beta 1.19.70.24 (Android), Preview 1.19.70.24 (Windows, Xbox) or Preview 1.19.70.25 (iOS, iPadOS) is the fifth beta/Preview version for Bedrock Edition 1.19.70, released on February 23-24, 2023, which fixes bugs.


== Changes ==


=== Gameplay ===
Sneaking

Reverted the changes related to 1.5 gap sneaking.


== Fixes ==
Gameplay

Players now stop sneaking in Powder Snow after letting go of the sneak button. (MCPE-166518)
Marketplace

Fixed an error that could occur when clicking on a Minecoin bundle.
