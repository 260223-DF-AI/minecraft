1.8.1-pre4 is the fourth pre-release for Java Edition 1.8.1, released on November 6, 2014.


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Options

Off/fancy/fast clouds.
Off/on entity shadows.
Fast/fancy now only controls leaves and rain particles.
Performance

Many optimizations.
