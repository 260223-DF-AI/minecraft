Skin Pack 3 is a skin pack that includes 45 skins referencing various characters from Minecraft and other popular video games. It is exclusive to the PlayStation 3,  PlayStation 4, and PlayStation Vita editions of Minecraft, as well as Bedrock Edition on PlayStation 4.
