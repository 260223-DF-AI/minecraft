1.11.4 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on May 23, 2019, which fixes bugs.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Mash-up packs

Added the Toy Story mash-up pack.


== Fixes ==
Fixed several crashes on Nintendo Switch that could occur during gameplay, especially surrounding villager job site blocks.
Fixed issues with Marketplace maps so villager_v2 works in command selectors again, targeting both old and new villagers.
