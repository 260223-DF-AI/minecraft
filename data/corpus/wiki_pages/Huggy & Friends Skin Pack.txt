== Trivia ==
Prior to the release of the skin pack, an add-on was made for the same property, also released by Spark Universe. However, it is not considered official to the Minecraft franchise.
In a promotional image for the skin pack, Doey has a noticeably different texture, with the clay hands resembling claws and a tentacle. Boxy Boo's texture also appears reversed in the image.
