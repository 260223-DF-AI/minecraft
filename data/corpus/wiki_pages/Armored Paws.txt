Armored Paws is a game drop released on April 23, 2024, as Java Edition 1.20.5 and Bedrock Edition 1.20.80. It focuses on adding content voted for during Minecraft Live 2023 by adding armadillos, wolf armor, and 8 new biome variants of wolves.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
 Armadillo scute

Obtained as an occasional drop from armadillos, or from using a brush on an armadillo.
 Armadillo spawn egg

Used to spawn an armadillo.
 Wolf armor

Crafted using six armadillo scutes shaped like a lowercase h.
Using it on wolves grants them immunity from most damage sources.
Gradually breaks with each attack.
Can be repaired using armadillo scutes.
Can also be removed by using shears.
Like leather armor and leather horse armor, wolf armor can be dyed.


=== Mobs ===
 Armadillo

A passive mob that spawns in badlands and savanna biomes.
 Rolls up when hurt or scared by undead mobs or by players sprinting, mounted, or on a vehicle.
Reduces physical damage in that state.
 Occasionally peeks out from its shell to see if a threat is still near.
Will roll up to detect a mob or player that has attacked it recently.
Cannot roll up when it's in a minecart, boat, leashed or in water.
Occasionally drops an armadillo scute every 5 to 10 minutes.
Can also use a brush to drop an armadillo scute, using its durability.
Can be bred with spider eyes.
The wolf now has 9 variants, including the original wolf:

 Ashen wolf
Spawns in snowy taiga.
 Black wolf
Spawns in old growth pine taiga.
 Chestnut wolf
Spawns in old growth spruce taiga.
 Pale wolf
Spawns in taiga.
 Rusty wolf
Spawns in sparse jungle.
Can also appear in jungle or bamboo jungle if spawned using spawn eggs, monster spawners, commands, or through a wolf pack bordering the biome.
 Snowy wolf
Spawns in grove.
 Spotted wolf
Spawns in savanna plateau.
Can also appear in savanna or windswept savanna if spawned using spawn eggs, monster spawners, commands, or through a wolf pack bordering the biome.
 Striped wolf
Spawns in wooded badlands.
Can also appear in badlands and eroded badlands if spawned using spawn eggs, monster spawners, commands, or through a wolf pack bordering the biome.
 Woods wolf
Spawns in forest.
All existing wolves in a world continue to be pale wolves.

== Changes ==


=== Items ===
Turtle scute

Renamed from "Scute" to "Turtle Scute" to distinguish from the armadillo scute.


=== Mobs ===
Spider and Cave spider 

Are now scared of armadillos that are not in a rolled up state.
Wolf

Now spawns in sparse jungle, windswept savanna, and wooded badlands.
 Changed tamed wolf's collar texture.
Increase maximum health from 20HP × 10 to 40HP × 20. Preexisting Tamed Wolves need to be fed to reach the new maximum health.
Doubled the health restored by each item when it is fed.
