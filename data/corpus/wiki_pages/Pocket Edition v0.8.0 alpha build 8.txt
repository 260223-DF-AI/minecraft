v0.8.0 alpha build 8 is the seventh and final build version released for 0.8.0, as well as the final build released in 2013.


== Changes ==
Falling blocks

Now drop their item form when falling onto blocks they cannot replace, such as torches.
Creative inventory

All wool and carpet variants are now sorted based on their color rather than their block data.
