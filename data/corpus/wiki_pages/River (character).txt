River is a character in the Minecraft Mini-Series and part of Team Mesa with Durango.


== Appearance ==
River has light skin, yellow-blond hair in two pigtails or buns, and brown eyes. She wears a purple shirt with a ocelot face, green pants, and brown shoes.


== Personality ==
River is kind, helpful, agreeable, and sees the best in others. She likes flowers and animals; and dislikes the dark and hostile mobs. She is a friend to most, notable ones being Durango, Toby, and Quinn.


== Biography ==
Before the series began, River was shown to be a mesa farmer in-between interview clips.
River was once teleported from her home along with the others by CommuniKate using a command block on the show. She and Durango won the first and sixth challenges, and in the final challenge, she and the others sabotaged their way back home, but were teleported to the Greek Isles. Poseidon teleported River to a sand pillar in the Kraken Sea along with the other heroes, and she spent most of the season there. Then at the end, all the heroes escape and try to stop Poseidon. After a while, Zeus comes, stops Poseidon, and asks heroes to help him. After helping the god, River and Durango apparently returned home.


== Relationships ==


=== Durango ===
At first, Durango was cold with River, wanted to do everything himself. However, during the series, he goes through a personal journey and becomes her friend, and begins to save her from dangers.


== Trivia ==
River's outfit closely resembles the ocelot T-shirt skin from the 4th Birthday skin pack, being both purple and possessing an ocelot face.
In 2026, another character named River would appear in Minecraft Mash-Up series. It is unclear if there is an intended connection.
