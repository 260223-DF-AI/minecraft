1.6.11 is an update for the New Nintendo 3DS Edition released on July 2425, 2018, which added new DLC packs to the store.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
New DLC available for purchase:
Candy Texture Pack
Battle & Beasts Skin Pack
Star Wars Classic Skin Pack
Star Wars Rebels Skin Pack


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Improved stability and performance.


== Fixes ==
Several bug fixes from previous versions.
