26.10, also known as 1.26.10, the release of Tiny Takeover, is a game drop for Bedrock Edition released on March 24, 2026, which adds new textures and models for baby animals, adds the golden dandelion, makes name tags craftable, and fixes some bugs.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Golden dandelion

Crafted with a dandelion and 8 gold nuggets:

Can also be bought from wandering traders.
When used on a baby mob, it will stop aging; if used again on the same mob, it will resume aging.
Cannot be used on monsters (including undead mounts) and villagers.
Can be placed and potted like other flowers.


=== General ===
Settings

Added an option on Windows to disable pausing when the application loses focus.
Added closed captions. However, the toggle has not been added to the settings, but they can be enabled from options.txt.
Controls

Added a keybind, F4 by default, to open the social drawer in-game.
Added a key binding for Identify, a feature that displays with text or text to speech which block or mob is in front of the player.
Game Menu

The small buttons now show a tooltip similar to hotbar tooltips on hover.
Play screen

Hardcore worlds now show the amount of days played if the game rule showdaysplayed is enabled.


=== Sounds ===
Note block

Added the trumpet instrument.
It can be heard when a note block is placed on top of a block of copper.
The sound is different based on the oxidation level of the copper block.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Dead bush

Can now be placed on farmland.
Stonecutter

Deepslate can now be directly crafted into its cobbled, polished, brick, and tile variants in the stonecutter.
Stone can now be directly crafted into its cobblestone variants in the stonecutter.


=== Items ===
Name tag

Are now craftable.

Can now be sold by wandering traders as a common trade for 1 emerald with a 7% chance, and 5 trades in stock.
Can no longer be found in chests in woodland mansions and ancient cities.


=== Mobs ===
Baby mobs

Added new sounds for baby wolves, cats, pigs, horses, and chickens.
Updated the models and textures of the following baby mobs:
Armor on baby wolves and saddles on baby pigs will no longer render.
Added missing textures beneath baby chicken wings.
Updated the bone hierarchy and renamed the bones of baby donkeys and baby mules to match the horse hierarchy and bones.
Updated the UVs for the rear legs of both baby donkeys and baby mules.
Cat, Chicken, Cow, and Pig

Added distinct sound variants similar to wolves, in addition to the original "classic" variant:
Cats have a new "royal" variant.
Pigs have new "mini" and "big" variants.
Cows have a new "moody" variant.
Chickens have a new "picky" variant.
Horse

Changed the texture of horses with black dots.
Librarian

No longer offers name tags as a Master level trade.
Now offers red and yellow candles for the price of three emeralds each at the Master level.
 Rabbit

Their models, animations, and textures have been updated.
The new rabbit models and textures are now version sliced.
Textures are prefixed with rabbit_.
  Now has an idle animation.
Ghast

The texture of it shooting fireballs has been updated for pixel consistency.


=== General ===
Experiments

Removed the "Drop 1 of 2026" experimental toggle and implemented all features in regular gameplay.
Graphical

Added static/colorized block light shading to particles in Vibrant Visuals.
Game Menu

The Marketplace button has been moved to the small buttons below when playing on realms.
How to Play

The main How to Play button has been moved from the settings to the game menu.
Panorama

Updated the panorama to reflect Tiny Takeover.
Play screen

Custom servers are now always available on the "Servers" tab even when no featured servers could be found.
Realms

The "world_replica_based_backup" flag and the old Realms backup system have been removed, and now the new backups system, Realms Saves, is now enabled at all times.

Updated the inventory and SDL screens to properly reflect purchases made when navigating back to it from a related page.
 Updated the interact button design for touch controls.
Settings

Updated the settings screen with Ore UI, excluding subscriptions, storage, creator, pack tabs, and settings in the game menu on Realms.

Voxel shapes are currently only used for occlusion/culling.

Blocks	

Added culling_shape field to minecraft:geometry component behind the VoxelShapes toggle.
The value should be an identifier of a voxel shape and can refer to either a custom voxel shape file, minecraft:unit_cube, or minecraft:empty.
If the VoxelShapes experiment is enabled and a shape is not specified, minecraft:empty will be used by default if the block does not use minecraft:geometry.full_block.
Blocks can handle the following entity events in scripting through the onEntity handler:
Custom named events:
on_home from minecraft:behavior.go_home
on_escape from minecraft:behavior.avoid_block
on_reach from minecraft:behavior.move_to_block
on_place from minecraft:behavior.place_block
on_take from minecraft:behavior.take_block
Unlike the current Vanilla blocks that handle those events, custom blocks may receive those events up to one tick after they were emitted, depending on how late in the tick the event was emitted.
Added block trait minecraft:multi_block behind the "Upcoming Creator Features" experiment.
Added minecraft:chest_obstruction component under the Upcoming Creator Features toggle.
It defines how a block placed above a chest or ender chest should obstruct their opening.
obstruction_rule specifies how the block is evaluated during opening if placed above; shape is the default when not specified.
always: Will always obstruct a chest from opening.
never: Will never obstruct a chest from opening.
shape: Will use the Blocks AABB shape to determine if the chest is obstructed from opening.
Blocks prior to 26.10 will behave as expected based on their defined geometry and material instances.
