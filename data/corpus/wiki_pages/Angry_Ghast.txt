Angry ghast is an angry version of the ghast only found in Java Edition 25w14craftmine. They shoot three fireballs instead of one at a time.


== Spawning ==
One angry ghast spawns in the Angry Ghast special mine, which unlocks after the player defeats a ghast by deflecting its fireball at it.


== Drops ==
Angry ghasts drop 4XP experience orbs when killed by a player or a tamed wolf.


== Behavior ==
The angry ghast is often more dangerous than the normal ghast. Similar to a blaze, it shoots 3 fireballs at the same time, but they can instantly kill a player if very close to the explosion. They can also cause high amounts of block damage when shooting fireballs. Fireballs have an ExplosionPower of 3.


== Sounds ==
Java Edition:
Angry ghasts use the Hostile Creatures sound category for entity-dependent sound events.
