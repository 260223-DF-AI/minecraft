City Folk Skin Pack is a skin pack that includes 20 skins themed around the citizens and inhabitants of medieval cities. Together with the Town Folk Skin Pack, this is one of the first two skin packs released for Bedrock Edition.
The skins included in this pack were designed by Garry Lucken. Some of these are based on skins included in Skin Pack 3 and Skin Pack 4.
