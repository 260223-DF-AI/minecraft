Furnace may refer to:


== Minecraft ==
Furnace  a block used to smelt items.
Minecart with Furnace  an entity which can boost minecart speed.[Java Edition  only]
Blast Furnace  a block that can only smelt metal and ore but twice as fast.
Smoker  a block that can cook food twice as fast as a furnace.
Campfire  a block that can cook food up to four items at a time without using any additional fuel.
Soul Campfire  soul variant of campfire.


== Minecraft Earth ==
Furnace Golem  a variant of an iron golem.
