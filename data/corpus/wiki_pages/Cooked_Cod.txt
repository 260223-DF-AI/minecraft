=== Cooking ===
Raw cod can be cooked in a furnace, smoker, or campfire. Each piece of cooked cod removed from a furnace output slot gives 0.35 experience (22.4 experience per stack).


=== Trading ===


== Usage ==


=== Food ===
To eat cooked cod, press and hold use while it is selected in the hotbar. Eating one restores 5 () hunger and 6 hunger saturation.


=== Wolves ===
Cooked cod can be used to feed a wolf not at full health, healing by 10HP. However, in Bedrock Edition, unlike other wolf food, cooked cod cannot be used to speed up the growth of baby wolves nor used to breed them and can only be fed to a wolf that is not at full health.
