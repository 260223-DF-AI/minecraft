1.21.44 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on October 31 through November 5, 2024, which fixes some bugs.


== Fixes ==
3 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.21.2

MCPE-66809 – Frost Walker sometimes generates ice when Falling
Other

Fixed all flavors of the skull block being turned into skeleton_skull when opening a world-template version locked below 1.21.40, it does not fix already overwritten chunks.
Fixed a crash on several platforms when navigating back to Realms Stories after inviting a player on the manage members screen.
