"Concrete Halls" is a song composed by C418 that plays in the Nether.


== Locations ==


== Track description ==

"Concrete Halls" is an ominous, rhythmic track that consists of dark, relatively dissonant piano chords supported by cold ambient synths, abrasive, heavily reverbed percussion, and distant ghast sounds.

=== Raw music files ===
In Java Edition, the music files can be found in .minecraft/assets/objects.
In Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Education, it can be found in:

Mobile: com.mojang/resource_packs/music/vanilla_music/sounds/music/game/nether/
Windows: %PROGRAMFILES%\WindowsApps\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP_<version>_x64_8wekyb3d8bbwe\data\resource_packs\vanilla_music\sounds\music\game\nether
All music files are in Ogg Vorbis.
