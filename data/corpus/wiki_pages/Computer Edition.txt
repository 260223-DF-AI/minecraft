Computer Edition may refer to the following Minecraft editions:

Java Edition, the original version of Minecraft for Windows, macOS, and Linux.
Bedrock Edition, the version of Minecraft that supports cross-platform play between different platforms, including Windows and ChromeOS.
Minecraft Education, the education version of Minecraft for Windows, macOS, ChromeOS, Android, iOS, and iPadOS.
China Edition, the mainland China version of Minecraft for Windows, Android, iOS, iPadOS, and macOS.
Minecraft 4k: the under 4 kibibytes version of the game made for the 2010 Java 4K contest.
MinecraftEdu, the discontinued education version of Minecraft for Windows, macOS, and Linux.
 Pi Edition, the discontinued version of Minecraft for the Raspberry Pi.
 Windows 10 Edition, the former title of Bedrock Edition on Windows.
