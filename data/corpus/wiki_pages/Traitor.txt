A traitor is a villager in 25w14craftmine that trades mine ingredients. It has a pillager body but still has the villager overlay.


== Spawning ==
An unemployed villager turns into a traitor when a unclaimed lodestone is nearby.

Issues relating to "Traitor" are not maintained on the bug tracker because it is an April Fools' joke, and is therefore not in the newest stable version or snapshot. Issues reported there are closed as "Invalid".
