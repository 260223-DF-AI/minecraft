La baguette is a joke weapon from Java Edition 23w13a_or_b resembling the iconic French bread of the same name. Its stats are identical to a wooden sword, though la baguette cannot be used to fuel furnaces.


== Obtaining ==
One tricolore and one baguette is given to all players when French mode gets activated.


== Usage ==
La baguette functions identically to a wooden sword. Despite appearances it is not a food item and is unrelated to regular bread.


=== Attacking ===
It can be used to damage mobs for 4HP health points. La baguette has an attack speed of 1.6 and takes 0.6 seconds to recover.


=== Breaking ===
La baguette, just like other swords, can be used to break certain blocks 50% faster. See Sword#Sword breaking times for more.
