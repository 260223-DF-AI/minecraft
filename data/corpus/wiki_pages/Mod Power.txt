Mod Powers (Japanese: MOD能力, Hepburn: Moddo Nōryoku?), also called Cursed Mods (呪いのMOD, Noroi no Moddo?) by Gray, are intrinsic special abilities appearing in Minecraft: The Manga. They appear to be hereditary, allowing the users to manipulate the world around them in ways impossible within typical iterations of Minecraft.


== Symptoms ==
Those affected by Mod Powers have Mod Runes (MODの紋章, Moddo no Monshō?) somewhere on their bodies, which differ in appearance and placement for each ability.


== Types ==


=== Imagination ===
Imagination (創造力, Sōzō-ryoku?), used by Nico and Icco White, allows the user to craft in variable sized crafting grids, even without the use of a crafting table. In addition to normal items (such as a wooden sword), it can be used to craft items invented by the user; due to the versatility of what it creates, it can even be used to mimic enchantments. The size of the crafting grid that each person can use is defined by the level of the Mod Power. With Imagination I, a 5x5 crafting grid can be used, while Imagination II grants a 6x6 crafting grid. Additionally, users of Imagination can combine their crafting grids to form larger, rectangular grids. Both Nico and Icco have access to Imagination II, though when Nico first awakens his Mod Power he is limited to Imagination I.


=== Zombie Curse ===
The Zombie Curse (ゾンビ化の呪い, Zonbi-ka no Noroi?) is a Mod Power that transforms people into zombies, in both body and mind. Gray and his village are afflicted by it.


=== Growth ===
Growth (成長期, Seichō-ki?) is a Mod Power that allows the user to become stronger, more intelligent, and larger the more creatures they eat. It was used by a Giant Spider that attacked Carhon Village, eating enough of the village's livestock to grow to 30 times the size of an average spider and to gain enough intelligence for basic speech.


=== Evolution ===
Evolution (道化しんか, Shinka?) is a Mod Power used by Navy. It grants superior intelligence that rivals or surpasses that of humans, as well as extremely strong legs and blast protection, allowing them to survive explosions unharmed.


=== Blizzard ===
Blizzard (吹雪, Fubuki?) is a Mod Power used by the Blizzard Stray. It creates a constant blizzard around the user, spanning many kilometers.


=== Mirror Seed ===
Mirror Seed (コピーシード, Kopī Shīdo?) is a Mod Power used by Don Gold. By touching others' Mod Runes, the user can create a seed that grants their Mod Power for 10 minutes when eaten.
Don Gold has used Mirror Seed to copy the following Mod Powers:

Iron Nugget Gun (鉄快弾, Tetsukai-dan?): The ability to shoot iron nuggets.


=== Mob Tamer ===
Mob Tamer (魔物使い, Mamono Tsukai?) is a Mod Power used by Hyena. It allows the user to control any mob they can see, provided the mob is weaker than themselves; in Hyena's case, this includes Zombies and Skeletons. Due to the user's control over the mobs, they seem very resistant to damage, though they actually just ignore any damage they incur to prioritize the user's orders.


=== Block Piercer ===
Block Piercer (ブロック貫通, Burokku Kantsū?) is a Mod Power that allows the user to instantly punch through any block, even those that are generally unbreakable. It is used by Platinum.
When attacking through an iron wall created by Nico, Platinum refers to her punch as Iron Punch (アイアンパンチ, Aian Panchi?).


=== Clairvoyance ===
Clairvoyance (千里眼, Senrigan?) is a Mod Power that allows the user to see anywhere in the world that they've been before. It also grants the user the ability to see through walls, floors, and other matter, though the extent of it is limited. It is used by Owl.


=== Replication ===
Replication (増殖, Zōshoku?) is a Mod Power that allows the user to replicate themselves endlessly. It is used by the Replicating Silverfish who, due to their limited intelligence, replicating themselves into a swarm that was stuck constantly searching for more food to feed itself.


=== Cat King ===
Cat King (猫の王, Neko no Ō?) is a Mod Power that grants its users heightened senses and super strength. It is used by Hunter, a member of Don Gold's Crew.


=== Barrier Block ===
Barrier Block (バリアブロック, Baria Burokku?) is a Mod Power that allows its user to create barriers at will. It is used by Ruby, a member of Don Gold's Crew.
While Barrier Block is generally impossible to avoid due to barriers' unbreakable nature, this can be nullified by Block Piercer.
Ruby has one named technique, Ghost Cage (ゴーストケージ, Gōsuto Kēji?), in which she suddenly surrounds an opponent with barriers.


=== Curve Ball ===
Curve Ball (変化球, Henkakyū?) is a Mod Power that allows its user to control the trajectory of long-range attacks. It was used by the Curve-Ball Ghast.


=== Inferno ===
Inferno (超高温, Chō Kōon?) is a Mod Power that raises the body temperature of its user to extreme temperatures, even hotter than lava. It was used by the Blue-Flame Blaze, and named by Platinum.


=== Tenacity ===
Tenacity (頑強, Gankyō?) is a Mod Power used by a Monster Spawner. It causes the Spawner to function in any light level, spawn monsters extremely fast, and be nigh-unbreakable, even able to resist Platinum's Block Piercer. The only known ways to counter the Mod Power are by using the Sword of Light, which temporarily halts spawning until it breaks, or the Titan's Blazing Sword, which is capable of destroying the Spawner entirely.


=== The King's Mod ===
The King's Mod (王のMOD, Ō no Moddo?) is a Mod Power that the Evil King's Gang want to use to destroy the world.


=== Teleport Command ===
The Teleport Command (テレポートコマンド, Terepōto Komando?) is a Mod Power that allows the user to use the /tp command to teleport themselves or others (through direct contact) to specified coordinates. It is used by Calamity.


=== Enlargement ===
Enlargement (広域化, Kōiki-ka?) is a Mod Power stored in a wither skeleton skull. It was one of three used by Abyss when creating the ultimate Wither.


=== Power Amplification ===
Power Amplification (威力增大いりょくぞうだい, Iriyokuzōdai?) is a Mod Power stored in a wither skeleton skull. It was one of three used by Abyss when creating the ultimate Wither.


=== Forced Fusion ===
Forced Fusion (強制融合, Kyōsei Yūgō?) is a Mod Power stored in a wither skeleton skull that allows the user to forcibly fuse others. It was one of three used by Abyss when creating the ultimate Wither, which he created by fusing a Mod Power-infused Wither, himself, and Icco White. The one who activated the fusion appears to remain in control once fused, as Abyss remained in control of the ultimate Wither. The only way to unfuse somebody is to kill them; however, they are still eligible to be revived with a totem of undying, and will remain unfused afterwards.


=== One Block ===
One Block (ワンブロック, Wan Burokku?) is the ability to create a palm-sized pocket dimension, containing a single grass block, and to trap others inside. It is used by an unnamed pickpocket.
