21w17a is the nineteenth snapshot for Java Edition 1.17, released on April 28, 2021, which tweaks the textures of raw metal blocks, adds noodle caves and fixes bugs.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Tags

Added the small_dripleaf_placeable block tag.
Contains clay and moss blocks.
Small dripleaf can be placed on blocks in this tag.


=== Experimental ===
Changes have been made to the official Caves & Cliffs world generation preview data pack, which gives early access to the world generation coming in Java Edition 1.18.
Noodle caves

A thinner, squigglier, tighter variant of spaghetti caves.
These are an improved version of the mesh caves announced around Minecraft Live.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Amethyst cluster

Removed unused 'lit' blockstate.
Blocks of raw metal

Tweaked the textures of raw iron blocks, raw copper blocks, and raw gold blocks.
Copper ore and deepslate copper ore

Now drops 23 raw copper when mined, or more when using a tool enchanted with Fortune.
Deepslate coal ore and deepslate copper ore

Now can spawn naturally.
Block of copper

Is now crafted from 9 copper ingots, instead of 4.
Hanging roots

Can now be waterlogged, to match the behavior in Bedrock Edition.
Small dripleaf

Can now also be placed on moss blocks.


=== Mobs ===
Wither

Is now immune to freezing.


=== World generation ===
Ore

Emerald ore can now replace granite, andesite, diorite, tuff, and deepslate upon generating.
If coal ore or copper ore replace tuff or deepslate, they become their deepslate variants.


=== Gameplay ===
Recipe book

The recipe for crafting mossy stone bricks from moss blocks or vines is now unlocked with moss blocks or vines, respectively, rather than mossy cobblestone.
The recipes for dyed candles are now grouped.
The recipes for smelting and blasting ores into their respective materials are now grouped by ore type (i.e. smelting coal ore and deepslate coal ore are grouped).
For iron, gold, and copper, the recipes for smelting and blasting their raw materials into ingots are also grouped with their respective ore smelting and blasting recipes.
The recipes for crafting copper ingots from copper blocks and waxed copper blocks are no longer grouped.
The recipes for mossy stone bricks are now grouped, as are the recipes for mossy cobblestone.
Many of the waxed copper block recipes were grouped together.


=== General ===
Tags

Added the wither to the freeze_immune_entity_types entity type tag.


=== Experimental ===
Changes have been made to the official Caves & Cliffs world generation preview data pack, which gives early access to the world generation coming in Java Edition 1.18.
Ore veins

Snake veins are now slightly rarer and slightly smaller.
Ores are clumpier and less evenly distributed.
Now have a chance to rarely contain raw metal blocks.
