Scout is a character appearing in the Minecraft: Official Magazine and several annuals.


== Appearance ==


== Personality ==
Normally, Scout is a very mellow figure. For an adventurer, Scout is very passionate, bordering on addicted, and will often rush into danger without a second thought, even if she lacks any context. In spite of her eccentricities, she is a very talented and competent warrior.
