Bear is a character appearing in the 2017 iteration of Minecraft: Official Magazine, as well as several annuals.


== Appearance ==
Being a survival expert, Bear has a very messy appearance, with a face appearing to scream. Their teeth are noticeably tall, with an upraised, long nose, and high blackish blue eyes under a headband. His hair is long, with long braids or bangs, a leaf and flower covering his left side, using the same color scheme as face paint on his cheeks. His clothes are similarly covered by leaves, with a leaf wristband on his right arm. Below are a tattered white shirt, ripped leather colored pants, and brown shoes.


== Personality ==

A very abrasive, paranoid figure, Bear thrives in the wilderness and is known to go on extreme challenges.
