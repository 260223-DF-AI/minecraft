19w12a is the twenty-seventh snapshot for Java Edition 1.14, released on March 20, 2019, which adds two new slabs.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Cut sandstone slab and Cut red sandstone slab

Crafted from cut sandstone and cut red sandstone.
It's no longer possible to make regular sandstone slabs out of cut sandstone.


=== General ===
Splashes

Added "<playername> IS YOU"


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Banners

Illager banners can no longer be copied using a loom or crafting table.
Glass

Now acts more like most solid blocks.
Redstone dust and torches can now be placed on top of glass.
Mobs now suffocate inside glass.
Grass path, mycelium, and podzol

Now have random orientations when placed, similar to grass blocks.
Sandstone

Cut sandstone and cut red sandstone are now available through stonecutters.


=== Items ===
Totems of undying

The texture and model of the totem shown when used now has the same CustomModelData value as the item.
Resource packs which use this tag thus now have the correct model when activated.


=== Mobs ===
Iron golems

Tweaked spawning in villages.
Also only spawn now if villagers in a village have worked and slept before a meeting.
Villagers

Improved AI.
Baby villagers now play in villages.
Nitwit and unemployed variations of villagers for snowy and zombie snowy villages changed.
General

Updated hitboxes and eye positions of many entities.​[more information needed]
Player height while sneaking is now 1.5 meters.


=== Gameplay ===
Advancements

Two by Two
Now also requires breeding foxes.
Sneaking

Now reduces the player's height to 1.5 blocks, allowing them to walk through 1.5-block-tall gaps.
Trading

General improvements.
Trades are now locked from 6–8 trades instead of just 4.


=== General ===
Creative inventory

Wall blocks are now found in the "Decoration Blocks" tab.
Sounds

Rewrote sound system.
Reduced stuttering when playing sounds.
Global sounds now always play at full volume. Previously they were just played at the player's position, which made it possible for them to become quieter or stop being audible if the player moved while it was playing.
Splash

Removed "Hobo humping slobo babe!"
World creation

Worlds with names already being used now prepend a number in parentheses to the folder name instead of adding hyphens.
For example, New World (3) instead of New World---.

== Trivia ==
The splash "<player name> IS YOU" is a possible reference to the indie puzzle game Baba is You, which was released 7 days prior.
19w12a is the first server snapshot released in the 12th week of a year.
19w12a is also the first snapshot released on the 12th week of a year to follow regular naming conventions.
