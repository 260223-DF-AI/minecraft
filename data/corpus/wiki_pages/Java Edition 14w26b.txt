14w26b is the thirtieth snapshot for Java Edition 1.8, released on June 25, 2014, to fix a major bug pertaining to the lack of chunk rendering in multiplayer introduced in 14w26a.
It was strongly advised to make a backup of a world before using this snapshot, because of block-corrupting problems that were not corrected until 14w26c.
A potential means of recovering corrupted worlds using MCEdit is available.
