Follow Me is a joke item added in 26w14a.


== Obtaining ==
It is automatically obtained and appears in the hotbar when the player starts up a world in the Herdcraft Update. It also appears in the hotbar after the player dies.


== Usage ==
Right-clicking living blocks with this item will add them to a group, and left-clicking will leash all the blocks in the group.
