14w28b is the thirty-fifth snapshot for Java Edition 1.8, released on July 10, 2014, to some of bugs and crashes in 14w28a.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
 Monster Spawners

Can be right-clicked with a spawn egg in hand, to change what the spawner produces.
