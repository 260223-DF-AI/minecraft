Beta 1.20.0.25 (Android) or Preview 1.20.0.25 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox) is the sixth and final beta/Preview version for Bedrock Edition 1.20.0, released on May 18, 2023, which fixes bugs.


== Fixes ==
5 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.20.0

MCPE-168409 – BlockPermutation.matches() and blockPermutation.withProperty() methods do not work with custom blocks. Script API.
MCPE-168908 – Minecraft for chromebook cursor sensitivity.
From the 1.20.0 development versions

MCPE-169504 – Aiming in circle mode is not properly focused.
MCPE-170070 – Teleporting players to unloaded chunks while flying causes the fly mode to get disabled.
MCPE-170643 – Cherry Grove biome option is missing
