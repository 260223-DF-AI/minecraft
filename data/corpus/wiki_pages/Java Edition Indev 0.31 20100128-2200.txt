A version of Indev 0.31 was released on January 28, 2010, at 22:00–22:02 UTC, which added new tools, blocks and items. It was announced on The Word of Notch at 22:31 UTC.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
  Block of emerald

Currently unobtainable without world editors.
Not in the starting house chest.
  Emerald ore

Drops 1–3 emeralds.
 Gear

Can only be obtained by inventory editing and initially invisible in the inventory.
A gear's sprite consists of two parts; the center rod and the animated gear itself.
When hacked into the game, a gear can be placed anywhere, but would show nothing in its block space and would be invisible unless horizontally adjacent to one or more blocks.
The placed gear would then show a gear sprite on the near side of each of those blocks.
A placed gear cannot be destroyed by mining a block that its gear sprite is shown on, because the placed gear is actually adjacent to that block.
If the block a gear sprite is shown on is destroyed, the adjacent placed gear would no longer show that sprite.
Removing all blocks adjacent to a placed gear would remove all of its visible gear sprites, leaving the placed gear invisible.
Placed gears cannot be destroyed directly by the player; attempts to do so would phase through to the block behind it, much like water.
A placed gear can only be removed by explosions.
Later replaced by redstone dust.


=== Items ===
 Coal

1–3 are dropped from coal ore.
 Emerald

1–3 are dropped from emerald ore.
 Gold ingot

There are 100 of them in the starting house, but is otherwise unobtainable.
 Iron ingot

There are 100 of them in the starting house, but is otherwise unobtainable.
 Emerald axe, pickaxe, shovel and sword
 Stone axe, pickaxe, shovel and sword
 Wooden axe, pickaxe, shovel and sword


=== Gameplay ===
Crafting

Pressing B opens the "Crafting" menu.
Currently unobtainable in this version.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Gold ore

Now drop themselves.
Iron ore

Now drop themselves.


=== Items ===
General

Items now show as 3D voxel sprites.
Swords

Swords mine 1.5 times faster than by hand.


=== World generation ===
Starting house

Size of starting item stacks in starting house chests increased from 99 to 100.
Items added to the tool chest.
Iron sword.
All 16 new item types.


=== Gameplay ===
Hotbar

Fullscreen mode no longer affects the hotbar.
Inventory

Fullscreen mode no longer affects GUIs or displayed text.
Armor sprites no longer display in their respective slots even if the level is reloaded.


=== General ===
Controls

Right-clicking on a stack picks up or puts down a single item.
Left-clicking on a stack picks up or puts down the entire stack.
It does not deposit more items than would create an item stack larger than the maximum stack size.
The remainder remains hovering at the pointer.
The player can no longer walk while having a chest open.
Maximum item stack size changed from 99 to 50 for items in the inventory, and 100 for items inside chests.


== Fixes ==
