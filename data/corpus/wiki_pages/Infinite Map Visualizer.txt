Infinite Map Visualizer, also known as level previewer, was a feature which was added in Java Edition Infdev 20100616-2210 as a replacement of Indev's isometric screenshot maker, but later removed in Alpha v1.1.1. It was exclusive to the browser version of Minecraft.
The level previewer was hosted on http://minecraft.net/infdev/preview.jsp (archive), and was removed between September 14 and September 26, 2010. However, it remained in the game's source code until 12w18a.
