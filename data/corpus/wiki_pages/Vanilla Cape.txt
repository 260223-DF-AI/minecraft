The Vanilla cape‌[JE  only] or One Vanilla Cape‌[BE  only] is a cape given to players who owned both Java Edition and Bedrock Edition for Windows before June 6, 2022 prior to both editions merging into one bundle.
The cape resembles a grass block on top and bedrock on the bottom, representing both editions of the game.


== Distribution ==
Players were able to obtain the cape under the condition that both Java Edition and Bedrock Edition for Windows are owned in the same Microsoft account before June 6, 2022.

== Trivia ==
The Vanilla Cape is the first cape to be fully available on both editions (aside from the staff-only Mojang Studios Cape).
The Pan Cape and the Founder's Cape existed before the Vanilla Cape and were later given to Java Edition. At the time, however, they were only on Bedrock Edition.
Back in 2015, Java Edition players were able to obtain a free code for the Minecraft: Windows 10 Edition Beta on the now-defunct Mojang website. Players who obtained Bedrock Edition via this method were eligible for the cape.
