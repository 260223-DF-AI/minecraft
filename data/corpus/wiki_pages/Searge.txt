Michael "Searge" Stoyke (/srd/ SARJ) is a technical lead working at Mojang Studios. He has been working on Minecraft since January 2, 2014; his employment at Mojang Studios was announced on November 2, 2013 at the "Future of Minecraft" panel at MINECON 2013. He is most well known for founding the Mod Coder Pack, as well as being the founding member of ModJam and his "secret feature" game where he would add secret features, mainly command block ones, to the game in secret and wait for users to find them and report them on Twitter. On February 13, 2015, Searge announced on Twitter that the "secret feature" game would stop when snapshots were released for Java Edition 1.9, claiming that it was getting "a bit old".


== Trivia ==
During the snapshots for Java Edition 1.9 there used to be a written book in the End ships. It reads "We will have something else here soon", referring to elytra, and was signed by Searge.
There is a secret signature for him, JAPPA and Chiwi in the Armor Stand texture.
