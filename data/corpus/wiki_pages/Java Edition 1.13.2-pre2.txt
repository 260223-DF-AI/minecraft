1.13.2-pre2 is the second and final pre-release for Java Edition 1.13.2 released on October 18, 2018, which fixed a few bugs. This is the final pre-release released in 2018.


== Fixes ==
7 issues fixed
From released versions before 1.13.2

MC-136526 – Crash when generating near top of world.
MC-136888 – Game crashes when shift-right-clicking a shulker box while holding bone meal.
MC-137229 – Book corruption when 1.8 world is loaded in 1.13.
From the previous development version

MC-137300 – ConcurrentModificationException thrown when upgrading world.
MC-137314 – Launching Minecraft 1.13.2 on Mac lags the whole system.
MC-137331 – Hostile and neutral mobs rarely spawn in positive Z coordinates.
Private issues

MC-136759 – Spawn eggs do not make sure that the entity is valid for the egg, allowing for arbitrary command execution in certain cases.

== Trivia ==
1.13.2-pre2 is the last pre-release which uses the naming convention, version-pren. 1.14 started naming pre-releases as Pre-Release, with the R may or may not being capitalized.
