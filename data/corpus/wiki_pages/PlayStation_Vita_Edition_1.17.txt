1.17  is a version of PlayStation Vita Edition released on July 1, 2015.  
[edit]


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Enabled USB keyboard use for book and quill.

Added the LittleBigPlanet Mash-up.
Added the Minecon 2015 Skin Pack.


=== Blocks and items ===
Acacia and dark oak wood planks, wood, stairs, slabs, fences, fence gates and doors
Only obtainable by modding the game.
Iron trapdoor, inverted daylight sensor, book and quill, stained glass, stained glass panes, and spruce, birch and jungle fences, fence gates and doors


=== General ===
Options

Added custom superflat UI, allowing players to customize superflat world generation, being able to change layers and blocks.
Added new in-game options to allow changing game mode, difficulty setting, time of day and spawn position per player and disable or enable ambient cave sounds and weather settings.
Added classic crafting option.


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Split load/join interface into create/load/join.
Changed game mode selection to a slider.
Names of mobs that are renamed using a name tag are now only visible when looked at up close again.


=== Blocks and items ===
Rose now uses poppy texture.
Quick equip for armor.
Door recipe now makes 3 doors instead of one.
Fence recipe now uses 4 planks and 2 sticks instead of 6 sticks.
Beacon beams now change colors when stained glass or stained glass panes are placed over the beam.
Wooden fences now connect to Nether brick fences.
The distance how far splash potions and bottles o' enchanting can be thrown has been massively reduced to match Java Edition.
