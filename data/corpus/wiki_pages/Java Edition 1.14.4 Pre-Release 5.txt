1.14.4 Pre-Release 5 (known as 1.14.4-pre5 in the launcher) is the fifth pre-release for Java Edition 1.14.4, released on July 11, 2019, which fixes some bugs.


== Fixes ==
6 issues fixed
From 1.14

MC-149880 – Villager trades wrong book.
MC-156349 – Cannot press Enter on direct connect.
From 1.14.1

MC-151282 – Villager trade GUI does not show the correct price on servers if trade demand is high.
From the 1.14.4 development versions

MC-156042 – Villager demand never goes down over time unless traded with.
Other

Fixed debug reports in worlds with a dot in their name.
Fixed servers freezing when villagers fall into the void.
