20w16a is the twelfth snapshot for Java Edition 1.16, released on April 15, 2020, which adds bastion remnants, ruined portals, chains, and a new music disc.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Chains

Generate in bastion remnants and ruined portals.
Crafted using 2 iron nuggets and 1 iron ingot in a vertical line formation.
Texture connects to those of the lantern and its soul variant.


=== Items ===
 Music discs

Added the "Pigstep" music disc, composed by Lena Raine.
Can only be found in bastion remnants.
Has a red disc color instead of black.
When played in a jukebox, a redstone comparator output produces a signal strength of 13.


=== World generation ===
 Bastion remnants

Generate in all Nether biomes, except basalt deltas.
Four types of structures generate: bridge, hoglin stables, housing units, treasure room.
Are the only source of the "Pigstep" music disc and piglin banner pattern.
Piglins and hoglins spawn here.
Spawners that spawn magma cubes can be found in bastion remnants.
A barrier block was accidentally left in the treasure room type.
 Ruined portals

Shattered remains of nether portals.
Generate in the Overworld and the Nether.
Can be buried underground or underwater.
A loot chest can be found beside the portal.


=== General ===
Tags

Added new creeper_drop_music_discs item tag.
Contains all music discs except "Pigstep".
Used to determine what music discs creepers can drop.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Chiseled nether bricks

Changed the texture of this block; the center now more closely resembles a skeleton or wither skeleton skull.
Crying obsidian and obsidian

Can now be found in ruined portals and bastion remnant loot chests, making crying obsidian obtainable without bartering.
Gilded blackstone

Can now be found in bastions, making it obtainable in Survival.
Jigsaw blocks

Added a button in the GUI that generates a jigsaw structure starting from the jigsaw block, using a given generation depth.
Quartz bricks

Changed texture of the block; the bottom row of bricks has been shifted to the right about 14 of a block.
Structure blocks

Expanded the maximum size per axis from 32 to 48.


=== Items ===
Banner patterns

The piglin banner pattern can now be found in bastions, making it obtainable in Survival.


=== World generation ===
Biomes

Increased the amount of lava pools found in basalt deltas.


=== General ===
Miscellaneous

Added a JMX MBean to monitor dedicated server tick times.[more information needed]
Tags

Changed the music_discs item tag to use the creeper_drop_music_discs item tag and the Pigstep music disc.
