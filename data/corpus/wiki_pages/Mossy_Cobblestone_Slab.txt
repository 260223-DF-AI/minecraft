A mossy cobblestone slab is a decorative slab variant of mossy cobblestone that generates in pillager outposts and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Mossy cobblestone slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Mossy cobblestone slabs generate as part of pillager outposts.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Mossy cobblestone slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.


== Sounds ==
In Bedrock Edition, when a mossy cobblestone slab is combined into a double slab, the block's use sound is played.
