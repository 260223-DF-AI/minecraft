A version of Indev 0.31 was released on January 24, 2010, at 21:19 UTC, which added chests.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
  Chests

Can be used to store items.
 Placing two chests next to each other creates a large chest with with double as much storage.
Triple chests can now be generated by placing two chests with air in between, then placing a chest in the open space in between.


=== World generation ===
 Starting house

New generated structure.
Player would spawn with chests full of every accessible block and tool in the game, except for dirt, water, lava, fire, and double slabs.
Made out of moss stone.


== Changes ==


=== Items ===
General

The player can now drop whole stacks of items instead of one at a time.
Stacked blocks limit changed back to 99 items per stack.
Now spin when dropped on the ground.[verify for Java Edition]
Arrows

Now have a new sound effect.
 Axes

Changed texture from .
The axe has been repositioned to the left instead of the right.
