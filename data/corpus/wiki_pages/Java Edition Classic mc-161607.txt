A version of Minecraft (Java Edition Classic private alpha) was released on May 16, 2009, at 16:07 UTC, referred to by Notch as "minecraft alpha". This is the first version of Minecraft ever given to players, being released for private testing. It is thus the first version in the Classic phase of development. The changes in this version are unknown.


== Bugs ==
1 bug reported

Issues reported about this version were bad mouse movement and a lack of a Y-axis invert.


== Trivia ==
The URL for Minecraft was given to users dock and fartron for testing.
