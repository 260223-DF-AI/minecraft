1.8.1 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released for Nintendo Switch on December 17, 2018, and for the other platforms on January 8, 2019, which fixes two bugs. This is the final version released in 2018 for the Nintendo Switch, and the first version released in 2019 for all other platforms.


== Fixes ==
Fixed an issue with saving (Nintendo Switch only).
Fixed worlds not loading when downloaded from Realms (excluding Nintendo Switch).
