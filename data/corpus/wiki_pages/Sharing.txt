Sharing is a joke effect from Java Edition 15w14a which replaces the data value of the Weakness effect.


== Effect ==
Sharing causes mobs to drop random items in a random amount of time. The loot table used is the same as that of a Mineshaft chest. The items are:

Bread
Coal
Diamonds
Gold Ingots
Iron Horse Armor
Iron Ingots
Iron Pickaxes
Lapis Lazuli
Melon Seeds
Pumpkin Seeds
Rails
Redstone
Saddles
The effect can be removed by drinking milk.
