Skin Pack 1, named Classic Skin Pack 1 in Legacy Console Editions for PlayStation systems and Skin Pack 1 - Classic in Bedrock Edition, is a skin pack that includes 44 skins (originally 45) referencing various characters from Minecraft and other popular video games.


== Skins ==
Bedrock Edition includes the theme name in front of their respective skin's name e.g. Banjo-Kazooie Banjo

== Trivia ==
A version of Skin Pack 1 was released on PlayStation 3 on March 27, 2014, on the PlayStation 3 specific version of the Skin Pack all licensed skins tied to Xbox were replaced with skins themed around PlayStation franchises.
The skin pack can still be bought via the Microsoft Store or the Xbox app. As of January 2026, Minecraft for Windows lists Skin Pack 1 as available DLC.
