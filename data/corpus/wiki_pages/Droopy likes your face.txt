"Droopy likes your face" is an album-exclusive song composed by C418 that only appears on Minecraft - Volume Alpha.


== Track description ==
Continuing from "Droopy likes ricochet", "Droopy likes your face"  contains more drums and a wind instrument. Around 0:39, there are samples of the voice storybook The Droopy Dragon, with the lines "Droopy looks in a cave", "No one is there", "Droopy looks under a tree", "Droopy is enormous" repeating throughout the song.


== Trivia ==
“Droopy likes your face” is also from C418's previous album, life changing moments seem minor in pictures.
According to C418, he doesn't know what the speech samples in “Droopy likes your face” are actually saying. C418 got the speech samples from a Kontakt sample library and they originate from "a toy you could buy in the 80’s or 90’s which says random phrases about itself". The two track titles may be C418's guesses as to what the sounds are saying.
It has since been discovered that these sounds are from the Texas Instrument Magic Wand Reader reading "The Droopy Dragon", as seen here.
A remixed mash-up of "Droopy likes ricochet" and "Droopy likes your face" by C418 was released as "Droopy Remembers" for the album 148.
