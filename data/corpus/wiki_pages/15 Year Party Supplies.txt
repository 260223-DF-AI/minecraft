15 Year Party Supplies is an official add-on developed by Oreville Studios released to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Minecraft. Multiple features from this add-on are used in the 15 Year Journey map.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
 15 Year Balloon 
Spawns balloons in a "15" shape attached to the block below when used.
While balloons can be placed in the air, they don't float upward, unlike balloons in Minecraft Education.
 15 Year Candle 
Used to craft 15 year cakes
Crafted using one string, one honeycomb, and one gold ingot.
Cannot be placed on its own or on cakes, it is only a crafting ingredient.
Has the Common Rarity, but uses a orange text in-game.
15 Year Party Supplies Handbook 
Guides player on how to craft a 15 year cake, and the various items party villagers give.
 Black Party Horn 
Shows an animation of the party horn being blown and extending when used.
Has a short cooldown.
Each party horn plays a different sound, to add variety.
 Blue Balloon
Spawns a blue balloon attached to the block below when used.
 Blue Party Horn 
Shows an animation of the party horn being blown and extending when used.
Has a short cooldown.
 Bubble Machine 
Spawns a bubble machine when used on a block.
Continuously blows bubble particles in one direction.
Destroyed when damaged.
Can be toggled off by using it.
 Bubble Wand 
Blows bubbles in front of the player when used.
Has a short cooldown.
Candy 
Dropped from pinata creepers.
Can be eaten to restore 1 () .
Grants Jump Boost for a short duration.
 Confetti Cannon 
Creates confetti particles in one direction when used.
Has a short cooldown.
 Confetti Machine 
Spawns a confetti machine when used on a block.
Continuously shoots confetti particles in multiple directions.
Destroyed when damaged.
Can be toggled off by using it.
 Creeper Balloon 
Spawns a creeper-themed balloon attached to the block below when used.
 Cupcake 
Grants Speed for two seconds.
Can be eaten to restore 2 () .
If the player eats too many cupcakes or candy within a short amount of time, the player gets Slowness instead of the food's respective effect.
Party Villager Spawn Egg 
Spawns a party villager when used, just like any other Spawn Egg.
Has the Epic Rarity.
 Pinata Creeper 
Spawns a pinata creeper when used.
Can be hung by tying it to a fence or wall in the air.
The only source of Candy.
 Red Balloon
Spawns a red balloon attached to the block below when used.
 Red Party Horn 
Shows an animation of the party horn being blown and extending when used.
Has a short cooldown.
 Yellow Balloon
Spawns a yellow balloon attached to the block below when used.
Cannot be obtained from Party Villagers, only from the Creative Inventory.
 Yellow Party Horn 
Shows an animation of the party horn being blown and extending when used.
Has a short cooldown.


=== Blocks ===
 15 Year Banner 
A banner with the 15th anniversary logo design.
Cannot be placed on the sides of blocks.
Doesn't need a block for support.
 15 Year Cake 
Crafted using one 15 Year Candle and one cake.
Summons a party villager when lit, who consumes the cake upon being spawned.
Has the Epic Rarity.
All of the cakes added in this addon (except cupcakes) cannot be eaten by players.
 Bunting Green 
A bunting used for decoration.
Can be placed like a Hanging Sign, but has no Collision Box.
Doesn't need a block for support.
 Bunting White 
A bunting used for decoration.
Can be placed like a Hanging Sign, but has no Collision Box.
Doesn't need a block for support.
 Cake Slice 
A slice of cake with a small berry on top.
 Confetti 
Can be placed in patches of 1, 2, 3, or 4 on a block, similar to Pink Petals, Wildflowers, and Leaf Litter.
They connect to fences and walls. this is a known issue with most addon/ mashup items.
 Giant Cake Center 
The center of Giant Cakes.
 Giant Cake Corner 
The corner of Giant Cakes.
 Giant Cake Edge 
The edge of Giant Cakes.
 Green Present 
A green present with a white wrapping.
 Pile of Presents 
A pile of three presents, these include a Red Present, A Green Present, and a Purple Present.
 Purple Present 
A purple present with a pink wrapping.

 Red Present

 White Present

=== Mobs ===
 Party Villager
Spawns when a 15 year cake is lit using flint and steel, consuming the cake upon being spawned. This happens even if there are already party villagers in the world.
Gives individual anniversary items, similar to trading but without any currency, they give you the items upright.
Cannot be hurt or killed, and doesn't despawn. Instead the player can be despawn it on command in the bottom of the item UI.
