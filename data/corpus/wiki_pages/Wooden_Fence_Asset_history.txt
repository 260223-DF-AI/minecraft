== Notes ==

Other notes
From Alpha v1.0.17 to 14w08a, unconnected fence posts would contain two small cuboids, completely interior to the fence block. These could only be seen by clipping inside of the fencepost itself. This did not change the outwards appearance of unconnected fences.
The following reported issues may change the appearance of fences in future if fixed:
MC-109478 - Fence model texture doesn't connect correctly
Apparently, fixing this bug would not affect unconnected fences, or fences facing south, west or south-west. But only when the camera is facing towards north-west.
MC-267281 - Fence multipart model system performance optimization
