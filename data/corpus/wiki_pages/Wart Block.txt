Wart blocks are blocks that generate as part of huge fungi in the Nether. There are two variants, nether wart blocks, a craftable variant found in crimson forests, and warped wart blocks, an uncraftable variant found in warped forests.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===


=== Natural generation ===
Each variant generates as part of huge fungi in their respective biomes.


== Usage ==


=== Composting ===
Both blocks can be fed into a composter with an 85% chance of leveling up the pile.
