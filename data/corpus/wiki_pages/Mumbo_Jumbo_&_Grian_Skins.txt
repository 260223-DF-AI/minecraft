Mumbo Jumbo & Grian Skins is a skin pack that includes the skins of the Minecraft web video creators Mumbo Jumbo and Grian. It was exclusively given to Mumbo Jumbo and Grian for use in the production of their "Minecraft Earth Road Trip" video series which debuted at MINECON Live 2020. The release of the skins as a skin pack was necessary due to Minecraft Earth lacking an option to import custom skin files.
