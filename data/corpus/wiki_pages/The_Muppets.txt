The Muppets are an ensemble cast owned by Disney.


== About ==


== Animated content ==


=== The Muppets Mayhem ===
During the episode Virtual Insanity of The Muppets Mayhem, the Electric Mayhem perform a virtual concert within Minecraft. This section of the episode was produced by Noxcrew.


== Allusions ==


=== The Game Awards ===

During several installments of The Game Awards, various Muppet stars appear to perform various video game-themed skits and routines.  The Game Awards 2024 saw Gonzo briefly show a picture of a chicken, while  The Game Awards 2025 had Rowlf casually mention the game during a number.


== Trivia ==
One of the proposed designs for the phantom was shot down for having a jaw reminiscent of a Muppet.
The world featured in Virtual Insanity is an actual in-game world, using the seed 120.
