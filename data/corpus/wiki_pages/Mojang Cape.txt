The Mojang Cape and the Classic Mojang Cape are capes that were given to employees of Mojang before it rebranded into Mojang Studios.


== Distribution ==
Neither of these capes were given out on Bedrock Edition.


=== Classic Mojang Cape ===
The Classic Mojang Cape was given to those employed by Mojang at any point between December 20, 2010 and October 7, 2015.
This cape was also given out to Mojang employees on Xbox 360 Edition.


=== Mojang Cape ===
The Mojang Cape was given to those employed by Mojang at any point between October 7, 2015 and July 25, 2021.
