A rabbit's foot is a brewing and tempting item obtained from rabbits and camel husk husk riders.


== Obtaining ==


=== Mob loot ===
Each rabbit has a 10% chance to drop a rabbit's foot when killed by the player. This chance can be increased by 3% per level using a sword enchanted with Looting.
A fox sometimes spawns with a rabbit's foot in its mouth, which always drops upon death. Alternatively, the player can drop a food item, which causes the fox to drop the rabbit's foot.

Java Edition:Bedrock Edition:


=== Cat gifts ===

A tamed cat has a 70% chance of giving the player a gift when the player wakes up from a bed, and the gift has a 16.13% chance to be a rabbit's foot, totaling to a cumulative percentage of 11.291% every time the player wakes up.

=== Camel Husk ===
Rabbit's feet can be used to tame and heal camel husks. Additionally, camel husks will follow any player holding a rabbit's foot.
