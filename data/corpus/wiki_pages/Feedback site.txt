The Minecraft Feedback Site is the official website location for players to share their feedback for Minecraft. Similar to the bug tracker, the site is moderated by the community team. The feedback site is located here. There is also an official Minecraft Feedback Discord server.

== Trivia ==
There was a 27th category about what to name the Piglin Beast, announced at MINECON Live 2019. It was decided on the name hoglin, and the category was removed, no longer having a use.
The site does not show commenter and suggestor names, but rather as “Registered User”, and they cannot be clicked on. It is unknown when or if this will be fixed.
Most users have been unable to log into the website since before September 2023 due to WEB-6665.
