"Mutation" is a song composed by C418 that plays on the main menu.


== Locations ==


== Track description ==

"Mutation" is a variation on "Minecraft" from Minecraft - Volume Alpha, played on a piano, with dramatic orchestral embellishments.

=== Raw music files ===
In Java Edition, the music files can be found in .minecraft/assets/objects.
In Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Education, it can be found in:

Mobile: com.mojang/resource_packs/music/vanilla_music/sounds/music/menu/
Windows: %PROGRAMFILES%\WindowsApps\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP_<version>_x64_8wekyb3d8bbwe\data\resource_packs\vanilla_music\sounds\music\menu
All music files are in Ogg Vorbis.
