From May 15 to May 29, 2024, several events and announcements occurred to celebrate Minecraft's 15th anniversary. These 15 days were known as the 15 Days of Minecraft. Alongside other announcements, a free character creator item was released each day, with each one referencing a year of Minecraft's history, which can be claimed in the Marketplace.
This page only details events that were a part of the 15th anniversary celebration; other occurrences over the 15 days, such as development version releases, are not detailed.


== Announcement ==
The event was teased on May 1, 2024, in a video on the official Minecraft YouTube channel titled: "Minecraft - Shape your world".

The event was officially announced on May 14, 2024 in another video titled: "Minecraft is turning 15 | Come celebrate with us!".


== May 15 ==

The following events occurred on day one (May 15, 2024).


=== Discounts ===
Until June 22, 2024, Minecraft on consoles and desktop, and all editions and/or DLCs of Minecraft Dungeons and Minecraft Legends were discounted by 50%. Minecraft on mobile devices was discounted by 70%.


=== Collaborations ===


==== Twitch ====
In collaboration with Twitch, the Purple Heart Cape and Glitch Mask were released from May 15 to May 31, 2024. The Purple Heart Cape was released as a Twitch Drop, which could be obtained by watching 15 minutes of any Twitch Partner or Twitch Affiliate streaming Minecraft (while logged into Twitch) and then redeeming a provided code at https://minecraft.net/redeem. The Glitch Mask could be obtained by redeeming a code obtained by subscribing to a Twitch Partner or Affiliate who is livestreaming in the Minecraft category. Twitch Prime subscriptions did not provide the code. There was also an exploding creeper emote that could be obtained by watching 5 minutes of a Minecraft stream on Twitch from May 25 through May 31. Once the emote was obtained, users could earn an exclusive pickaxe badge by using the emote in chat on May 28 through May 31.


==== Google Delight ====

A Minecraft-themed Google Delight was released. After searching "Minecraft" on Google, a grass block icon appears on the bottom, which can be clicked to activate a Minecraft-like mini-game where parts of the search results page can be broken with a fist, revealing parts of a Minecraft world.


==== Microsoft Solitaire Collection ====
A Minecraft theme was added to Microsoft Solitaire Collection on Windows, Android, iOS and iPadOS.


==== YouTube Shorts filter ====
In collaboration with YouTube, a Minecraft-themed filter for YouTube Shorts called "Minecraft Spring" was added.


=== Minecraft website ===
The Minecraft.net website could be interacted with during the celebration. Players could dig and mine using the pickaxe to get rewards. There were two rewards, which consist of the special 15th anniversary wallpaper which could be downloaded, and the link leading to the Minecraft Classic website to play for free.


=== Minecraft Shop ===
The Minecraft Shop released its 15th Anniversary Collection, which included 10 pieces of clothing as merch.


==== Minecraft Shop Sales ====
The shop also had a sale of 15% off for all merchandise when using the code 15BIRTHDAY until May 26, 2024. Additionally, using the code BOGO50 until May 17 granted a 50% discount on one apparel item if purchased along with another apparel item.


==== 15th Anniversary Sweepstakes ====
From May 15 to May 29, 2024, U.S. residents could enter the 15th Anniversary Sweepstakes. The grand prize was a $500 gift card, and four runner-up winners were given a $100 gift card instead.


=== Minecraft Launcher ===
Various 15th anniversary decorations were added to the Minecraft Launcher.


=== Xbox Game Pass Ultimate perk ===
Subscribers to Xbox Game Pass Ultimate were able to claim 500 Minecoins in the Perk section of the Game Pass menu.


=== 15th Anniversary Celebration announcement ===
The 15th Anniversary Celebration interactive event was announced to occurr on May 18, 2024, both in-person at Alexandra Palace, London, and online.


=== Character Creator ===
May 15's free character creator item was the "Cave Game Hoodie". It references the Classic versions of Minecraft released in 2009, as well as the original name of Minecraft, "Cave Game".


== May 16 ==

The following events occurred on day two (May 16, 2024).


=== Pringles ===
A special "Minecraft Spicy TNT" flavor of Pringles potato chips was announced exclusively in the United States and was available for a limited time.

To celebrate the 10th anniversary of Java Realms, a free map created by "100+ map makers" was released.


=== Character Creator ===
May 16's free character creator item was the "Crafting Cap". It references crafting, which was added in Indev 0.31, released in 2010.


== May 17 ==

The following events occurred on day three (May 17, 2024). This day was the actual 15th anniversary of Minecraft's release.


=== 15th Anniversary Cape ===

The 15th Anniversary Cape was released. It was given to players who clicked a button on the 15 Year anniversary page on minecraft.net from May 17, 2024 to June 21, 2024.


=== TikTok cosmetics ===
In collaboration with TikTok, the Follower's Cape was released and could be obtained by participating in a TikTok LIVE stream in the Minecraft category with Game Rewards enabled. To obtain the code, participants had to use the TikTok mobile app to watch for three minutes or send a message in chat containing the word "Minecraft", depending on the specific LIVE stream​[more information needed]. Progress for either action could be monitored by tapping the TikTok Game Rewards logo in the top left of the stream. The creator had to be from the viewer's geographic region to initiate the timer. Once obtained, the code could be redeemed at https://minecraft.net/redeem. The code could only be obtained until June 18, 2024.

A TikTok diamond helmet profile frame was also available from May 18 to May 31, 2024 by using "Create post" to share a video on TikTok lasting one minute or more using the hashtag #MinecraftBadge in a post.


=== Crafting kick-axe memories since '09 ===
A YouTube short featuring Jason Momoa and Jack Black, two of the actors set to be in A Minecraft Movie, celebrating the 15th anniversary was released. The video features real life recreations of various Minecraft features, such as a cake, a sword, and cherry leaves.


=== Forza Horizon 5 cosmetics ===
In collaboration with Playground Games, Creeper and Enderman mob head helmets were announced to be available from May 23, 2024 to the end of the year. They were obtainable for free from the Message Center.


=== Minecraft Shop Sales ===
The shop also had a sale of 15% off for all merchandise when using the code 15BIRTHDAY until May 26, 2024. Additionally, using the code BOGO50 until May 17 granted a 50% discount on one apparel item if purchased along with another apparel item.


=== Character Creator ===
May 17's free character creator item was the "Ender Hood". It references The End, which was added in the second part of the Adventure Update, released in November 2011.


== May 18 ==

The following events occurred on day four (May 18, 2024). Note that while on this day the Twitch and TikTok cosmetics were officially announced, they had already been released on May 15 and May 17 respectively.


=== 15th Anniversary Celebration event ===
The 15th Anniversary Celebration event occurred both online and at Alexandra Palace, London. It featured a large blank cube. Two sides of the cube were decorated by visitors using magnets. The magnets had a number on them to be placed on the structure to a corresponding number in a large grid. The end result was a mosaic featuring a pile of cookies, and creepers in the shape of Big Ben.


=== Minecraft Shop ===
The Minecraft Shop had a sale of 25% off for all warden merchandise when using the code WARDEN25 until May 19, 2024.


=== Character Creator ===
May 18's free character creator item was the "Emerald Expert". It references emeralds and desert villages, both of which were added in Java Edition 1.3.1, released in 2012.
