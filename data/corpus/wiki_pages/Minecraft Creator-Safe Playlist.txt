The Minecraft Creator-Safe Playlist is a playlist that includes royalty-free music from various Minecraft games and official collaborations. This playlist is designed for creators to use in their content (commercial and non-commercial) provided it abides by the Minecraft Usage Guidelines, as using the music will not violate copyright protections.


== Official description ==


== Contents ==
This playlist contains all musics and official remixes from Minecraft, including Battle and Tumble, Glide Mini Game, and official Marketplace content music, except Grid Runners, all musics and remixes from Minecraft Dungeons, and the multiple DLC, all musics and remixes from Minecraft Legends, the music from Minecraft Earth, all the Minecraft: Soothing Scenes, all the music used for Minecraft Live, Minecraft: Shape Your World, and the soundtrack for Caller's Bane.
