Minecraft Monthly is a series of normally monthly news videos hosted by Vu Bui, aimed at giving a comedic rundown of recent and upcoming features regarding the Minecraft franchise. It is the successor of the livestream series Minecraft Now. The show usually features developers discussing upcoming features, explaining how certain ideas are developed, and previewing content that may appear in future updates. It also sometimes highlights creations from players and provides announcements related to events like Minecraft Live.
