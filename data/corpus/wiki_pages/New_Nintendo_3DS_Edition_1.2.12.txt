1.2.12 is an update for the New Nintendo 3DS Edition released on December 22, 2017, which adds achievements and fixes bugs.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Achievements

Added Achievements.
GUI

Added effect icons and timers to the HUD.


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Various UI fixes and tweaks.[more information needed]
Performance and stability improvements.[more information needed]


== Fixes ==
Fixed hardlock that occurred sometimes with sleep mode.


== Trivia ==
This was the only New Nintendo 3DS version that was released on the same day in both North America and Japan.
