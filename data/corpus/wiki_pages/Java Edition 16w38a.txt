16w38a is the sixth snapshot for Java Edition 1.11, released on September 20, 2016.


== Additions ==


=== Gameplay ===
Gamerules

doWeatherCycle
Determines whether the weather changes.
Defaults to true.
maxEntityCramming
Defaults to 24.
If one entity tries to push more than 24 other entities, it starts to suffocate. (Note that pushing entities like minecarts also count towards this, although minecarts don't suffocate, mobs or players who push one, do suffocate)
The death message is (playername) suffocated in a wall.
Setting to 0 disables the rule.
Affects mobs and players only.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
 Cobblestone Walls and Mossy Cobblestone Walls

Are now in the "Decoration Blocks" tab in the Creative inventory rather than the "Building Blocks" tab.
 Farmland

Using pick block gives the player farmland itself instead of dirt.


=== Command format ===
Target selectors

Invalid selectors (such as @e[Type=creeper] instead of @e[type=creeper], @e[asdf] or @e[foo=nonexistent]) now generate an error instead of being silently ignored.
Removed implicit target selectors (such as @e[167,28,454]).


=== Gameplay ===
Chat

Messages can now be 256 characters long instead of 100.
Fishing

Ink sacs are now caught in stacks of 10 with a weight of 1, rather than in stacks of 1 with a weight of 10.


=== General ===
Sounds

Walking underwater no longer produces a step sound for the block the player is walking on.


=== Items ===
 Potions

Potions that have missing or invalid potion-effect tags now default to the uncraftable potion, which now has no glint, and is now magenta in color.
This also applies to lingering potions, splash potions and tipped arrows.
Due to the water bottle without tags being changed to uncraftable potion, the brewing icon is also changed to uncraftable potion in the Creative inventory.
 Elytra

Visible when equipped on skeletons, zombies and armor stands.


=== Mobs ===
 Guardians and Elder Guardians

Their common drop of raw fish will now be cooked fish if they die while on fire.
Their rare drops from player kills, which match fishing loot and are sometimes fish, are unaffected.
 Rabbits

Their raw rabbit drop is now affected by the Looting enchantment.
 Skeleton Horses and Zombie Horses

Their bone and rotten flesh drops (respectively) now yield 0–2, and are now affected by Looting.
