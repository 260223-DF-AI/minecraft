Melon may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Blocks ===
Melon, a block grown from melon seeds.
Melon Stem, the block that is planted on farmland when melon seeds are used on it.
Attached Melon Stem


=== Items ===
Melon Slice, a food item obtained by breaking a melon block.
Melon Seeds, an item that can be planted on farmland to grow melons.
Glistering Melon Slice, a craftable item used in brewing.


=== Feature ===
Melon Pile, a feature that generates only in villages.


== Minecraft Earth ==
Melon Golem, a snow golem with a melon head.
Melon Seed Projectile, a projectile shot by melon golems.


== Minecraft  Dungeons ==
Melon Slice, a food item from Jungle Awakens DLC.
