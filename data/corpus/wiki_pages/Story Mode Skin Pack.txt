Story Mode Skin Pack, also referred to as Minecraft Story Mode Skin pack, is a skin pack that includes 27 skins referencing various characters from Minecraft: Story Mode from the first four episodes of the game.

== Trivia ==
The skin pack is included as one of the default skin packs in Minecraft Education, being usable in the demo version. As a result it is often used in promotional material for the game.
The key art for the map was used on the thumbnail for the event Server Test.
Despite showing up in A Block and a Hard Place, there is no official Lukas Armored skin.
