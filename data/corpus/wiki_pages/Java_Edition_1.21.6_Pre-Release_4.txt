1.21.6 Pre-Release 4 (known as 1.21.6-pre4 in the launcher) is the fourth and final pre-release for Java Edition 1.21.6, released on June 10, 2025.


== Fixes ==
4 issues fixed
From 1.21.2

MC-279417  Luring mobs onto blocks at Y=-64 will instead make them pathfind to the highest elevation.
From the 1.21.6 development versions

MC-298446  The "Thunder roars" sound is no longer affected by the master volume.
MC-298510  Happy ghasts stay still for a few seconds when you get off them.
From the previous development version

MC-298653  Master volume slider no longer affects /playsound sounds with high volume.
