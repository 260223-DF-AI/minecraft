The vulture was a proposed mob that was featured in the Mob Vote at MINECON Live 2019, alongside the frog and goat. It was a bird mob that would spawn in badlands, and mark where a player had died by circling their drops. Despite some confusion, it would not interact with the drops themselves, only flying above them.
It, along with the rest of the badlands features, lost with only 14% of the vote. Although the swamp features were later added in the Wild Update, the badlands have not.
