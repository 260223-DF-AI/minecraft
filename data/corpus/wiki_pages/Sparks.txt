Sparks is a character appearing in the 2017 iteration of Minecraft: Official Magazine and several annuals.


== Appearance ==


== Personality ==

Sparks loves building fortified bases and redstone contraptions. Her speech is usually very enthusiastic. She seems to have a fear of the dark and the mobs that live within. She likes large lava features in bases.


== Powers and abilities ==
Sparks is consistently shown to be incredibly intelligent, with skills ranging from construction to quantum entanglement. Using her qualifications, she has been able to create impressive inventions, including ones capable of the destruction of the universe.


== Biography ==
At an unknown time, Sparks created the Togetherness Machine, intended to bring about a merger of different consoles that play Minecraft. Using the combined energies of charged creepers, she took a massive gamble, risking her own existence being destroyed. Nonetheless, she pushed forward, sending an excavator out to unite the platforms. This paid off, with dozens of heroes now together building in a circular biome.
