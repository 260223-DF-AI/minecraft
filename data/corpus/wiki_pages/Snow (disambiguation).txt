Snow may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Blocks ===
Snow - A layer of snow that accumulates during snowy weather.
Snow Block - A full block of snow.
Powder Snow - A type of snow that slows entities and inflicts freezing damage.
Powder Snow Cauldron - A cauldron full of powder snow


=== Items ===
Snowball - An item harvested from snow and snow blocks, which can be thrown.
Powder Snow Bucket - An item that holds powder snow.
Snow Golem Spawn Egg - A spawn egg that spawns a snow golem


=== Weather ===
Snowfall - A type of weather that occurs in snowy biomes, and at high altitude in cold biomes.


=== Entities ===
Snow Golem - A golem made of snow
Snowball - A thrown snowball
Snow Fox - A version of the fox found in grove and snowy taiga biomes
Snowy Wolf - A version of the wolf found in grove biomes


=== Biomes ===
Snowy Beach - A snowy version of a beach
Snowy Plains - A snowy version of a plains
Snowy Slopes - A snowy mountainous biome
Snowy Taiga - A snowy version of a taiga
Frozen Peaks - A biome formerly known as the snowcapped peaks
