v0.7.3 alpha is a minor update to Minecraft: Pocket Edition that was released on August 15, 2013. This version adds support for the daylight cycle and Splashes, and fixes some of the bugs and crashes in v0.7.2.
Support for iOS 4.3 was dropped with this version, with iOS 5.0 now being the minimum supported version. Users running iOS 4.3 can still play v0.7.2 and earlier.


== Additions ==


=== Gameplay ===
Sun, moon, stars, sunrise and sunset added.
Improved title screen
Splashes
Added 309 splashes. See /Splashes.
Panorama
The menu panorama uses seed 2151901553968352745 in Java Edition Beta 1.7.3 at coordinates (61, 75, -68).
Panorama pictures is directly blurred instead of overlay on Java Edition
Pocket realms improvements:
Realms can be reset


=== Blocks ===
 Double chests
When chests are put together, items previously in the left chest appear at the bottom half. Items from the right chest will also appear at the top. This may cause a loss of items.
Chests now have an open/close animation.
The player can open chests in creative, but not look inside; they will immediately close.
Quartz slabs


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Added more friction when flying in Creative Mode (iOS).
Stopped building the game with typeinfo enabled (iOS).
Wooden slab material changed to wood instead of stone. Wooden Slabs stored from before 0.7.3 are tagged as Fake Wood Slab and drop regular wooden slabs when mined.
When creating a new world, the default name shows "My World".


=== Mobs ===
Mobs now light on fire under sunlight.
