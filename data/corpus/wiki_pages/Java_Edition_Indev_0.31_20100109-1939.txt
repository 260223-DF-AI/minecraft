A version of Indev 0.31 was released on January 9, 2010, at 19:39 UTC, which added fire. It was announced on The Word of Notch at 19:57 UTC.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Fire

Can burn certain types of blocks (wool, logs, leaves, planks, bookshelves and TNT).
99 are given to the player in the sixth hotbar slot.
Does not render in the hotbar due to it being an animated texture.


=== World generation ===
Inland type

Replaced the original type.
Is like the original type, but has grass blocks instead of water around the map.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===

=== General ===
Light

There are now 16 light levels, instead of just 9.
Now reaches further.
