Cerberus is a three-headed dog-like creature that resides in the Maze of the Minotaur in the Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Appearance ==
Resembling the wither, Cerberus has a purple skeleton body with no feet, hence floating in the air. He has three dog-like heads, each with fully red eyes, pale lavender eyebrows and cheeks, and three sharp fangs. A chain appears to surround his chest.


== Biography ==
After the team managed to get through the Maze of the Minotaur with the help of creepers chasing them, they immediately got overwhelmed and Durango attempted scaring them away. While he was successful, it was revealed that the creepers were scared of the Minotaur Golem, who came out of his lair before a cyclops and Cerberus joined him. The Minotaur Golem and the cyclops soon fell into a ditch Zed dug beneath them. Outnumbered, Cerberus quickly flew away out of fear. Later, when CommuniKate attempted executing a command to self-destroy Poseidon's command block statue, she accidentally summoned the Minotaur Golem's trio in front of her friends, so she quickly despawned them out of fear.


== Behavior ==
While the series does not show his powers, Cerberus is presumed to have immense power like the Minotaur Golem and cyclops. However, he is overdependent of his allies and often feels afraid when he gets outnumbered.


== Trivia ==
In Greek mythology, Cerberus is a multi-headed dog used by Hades to prevent the dead from leaving the underworld.
Cerberus in the Minecraft Mini-Series is based on the "Wither" minifigure in series 12 from Mattel which in turn was based on a texture for the wither in the Greek Mythology Mash-up pack.
