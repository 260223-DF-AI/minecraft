15w31c is the third snapshot for Java Edition 1.9, released on July 31, 2015 to fix some bugs and crashes from 15w31a and 15w31b.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
 Grass Path

The collision height has been changed to 1516 of a block.


=== Items ===
Models for chest-like blocks

Chests, trapped chests, and ender chests were reverted back, to be consistent with the rest of blocks.
It is believed the previous change was made to make it easier to distinguish between regular and trapped chests.


=== Mobs ===
 Ender Dragon

No longer produces any more eggs.


=== World generation ===
The Nether

Re-added the nether fog.


=== Gameplay ===
Offhand slot

Can now stack whatever item the player is holding by picking them from the ground.
Status effects

The Levitation effect no longer overrides the fall damage reduction of the Jump Boost effect.
