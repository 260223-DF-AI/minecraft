Choose Your Mode was an online augmented reality (AR) video game created in collaboration with Crocs. The game is split between two modes, Crocs Creator and Crocs Crossing. As of May 12, 2025 it is no longer possible to play the game.


== Purchase and availability ==
The game is available on its website. The game itself is free, but it's required to scan the creeper face pin from the Minecraft x Crocs collection crocs to play it.


== Gameplay ==


=== Crocs Creator ===
In this game, the player can build structures using Minecraft themed Jibbitz, acting as blocks. An image of the finished build can be downloaded.


=== Crocs Crossing ===
There are 4 playable characters in the game: Ari, Efe, Sunny, and Zuri (renders of characters can be viewed in  Renders section). The characters are wearing Crocs, which are also available in the Bedrock Edition marketplace. The player runs across a long stone road spread across three blocks and surrounded by lava. They can jump by pressing the up arrow, or move horizontally between the three blocks by pressing the left and right arrows. There are holes in the road and creepers waiting to explode. Falling into the lava holes or getting exploded by a creeper by running into it ends the game. Minecraft themed Jibbitz spawn on the road, awarding points when collected.
Different Jibbitz award different amounts of points:

Grass block: 5 points
Zombie: 10 points
Creeper: 15 points
Diamond ore: 20 points
Obsidian: 25 points
Enderman: 50 points
After the game ends, an image with the results, including the score and a breakdown of the collected Jibbitz, can be downloaded.
