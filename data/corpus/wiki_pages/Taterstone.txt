Taterstone is a common block found in the Potato dimension. It is dropped when mining potone in 24w14potato.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
The best tool to break taterstone is the hoe, resulting in one taterstone being dropped. If not broken with a hoe, the block drops nothing.


=== Potone ===
Potone drops taterstone if broken with a non-Silk Touch hoe.


=== Natural generation ===
Taterstone naturally generates underground in the Potato dimension, in the form of blobs. It also generates in structures: colosseums, ruined portatols, and potato villages.
