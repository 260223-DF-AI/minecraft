A block of raw gold, internally known as a raw gold block, is a raw metal block equivalent to nine raw gold.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Blocks of raw gold can be mined only with an iron pickaxe or better.


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==
Blocks of raw gold are a compact way to store raw gold. They cannot be smelted into a block of gold.


=== Crafting ingredient ===


=== Piglins ===
Piglins run toward any block of raw gold item on the ground and inspect it for 6 seconds in Java Edition or 8 seconds in Bedrock Edition before putting it in their inventory. They become enraged if the player breaks a block of raw gold near them.
