13w47e is the fifth snapshot for Java Edition 1.7.4, released on November 22, 2013 to fix some of the bugs and crashes in the 13w47a, 13w47b, 13w47c, and 13w47d snapshots.


== Fixes ==
5 issues fixed
From the 1.7.4 development versions

MC-40512  Stained glass panes are opaque in 3rd person mode.
MC-40527  Twitch broadcast: Unknown error.
MC-40537  13w47c fails to launch.
MC-40603  13w47c crashes at startup on Mac.
MC-40627  Glass blocks in hotbar toggle backface culling on/off when selecting/deselecting a stained glass block.


== Trivia ==
13w47e is one of only three fifth-in-a-week snapshots to ever be released (alongside 12w30e and 15w35e), and the only one for version 1.7.4.
13w47e was previously removed from the launcher for some time, but was added back in September 2018.
