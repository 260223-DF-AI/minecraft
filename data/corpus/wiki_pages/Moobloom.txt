The moobloom was a proposed mob featured in the Mob Vote during Minecraft Live 2020, alongside the iceologer and glow squid. It would have been a buttercup variant of the mooshroom, and had some form of interaction with bees, possibly a symbiotic one that involved the flowers of its head and back. 
The moobloom lost in the first round with 28.3% of the votes.

== Trivia ==
Sprites of mooblooms appeared as an easter egg in the 2022 Mob Vote Event, 2023 Mob Vote Event, and the Eerie Mojang Office Party server.
