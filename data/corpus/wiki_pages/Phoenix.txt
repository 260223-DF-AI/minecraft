The phoenix is a summoned mythological bird featured as a minor antagonist in Minecraft Mini-Series.


== Appearance ==
The phoenix resembles a parrot in body shape, but is orange-colored with a yellow crest, eyes, and wing tips, and tail. It has a small, gray beak, as well as red pupils and three red marks for eyebrows. Its neck is surrounded by red feathers.


== Biography ==
In order to prove his power despite being a mortal, Poseidon used his trident statue to spawn a phoenix against the contestants. Thinking the fight will be easy, Durango throws the trident he stole from Poseidon at the phoenix, who fell to the ground but immediately came to life and threw the trident away. It then shot multiple fireballs at the contestants and Greeks until Durango managed to ride it using his trident. Angered, Poseidon executed a command to despawn the phoenix, causing Durango to hang on a cliff.


== Powers and abilities ==
As shown in the series, the phoenix can shoot explosive fireballs from its beak, causing fires on impact. It also has the ability to repeatedly come back to life after being killed.
