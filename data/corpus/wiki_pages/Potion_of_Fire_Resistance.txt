A potion of Fire Resistance is a potion that provides Fire Resistance when used.


== Obtaining ==


=== Brewing ===


=== Mob loot ===
A witch has an 8.5% chance to drop a potion of Fire Resistance if it dies while drinking the potion.


=== Generated loot ===

The cauldron in a swamp hut has a 5% chance to contain a potion of Fire Resistance.


=== Bartering ===
When given a gold ingot, piglins have a 8469 (~1.71%) chance to barter a potion of Fire Resistance, and an equal chance to barter a splash potion of Fire Resistance.
