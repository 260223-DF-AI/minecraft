Breath of the Nautilus is a status effect that is granted while riding a nautilus or zombie nautilus which prevents the rider's oxygen bar from depleting.


== Effect ==
The effect prevents an entity from drowning underwater by freezing their oxygen bar. If the entitiy's oxygen bar is already empty, the effect prevents them from taking drowning damage.


== Causes ==


== Immune mobs ==
Withers and ender dragons are immune to Breath of the Nautilus.
