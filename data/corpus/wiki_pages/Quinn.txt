Quinn is a character in the Minecraft Mini-Series and part of Team Stone Beach with Toby. She helps everyone to try and escape CommuniKate's game show, winning a few minigames, and ultimately winning Season 1. She then teamed up with Durango, CommuniKate, and Zed in Season 2, The Greek Isles after Poseidon takes over.


== Appearance ==
Quinn has red hair in a ponytail, blue eyes, and tan skin. She wears a black jacket, a pink shirt with a white lighting bolt on it, light blue wristbands, dull tan pants, and brown shoes.


== Biography ==
Before the series began, Quinn was shown to be a stone beach crafter in between interview clips.
Quinn was once teleported from her home along with the others by CommuniKate using a command block on the show. She and Toby won some challenges, and in the final challenge, she met Mo and they, along with others, decided to sabotage. Their sabotage was successful, but, they were teleported to the Greek Isles. There, Quinn, along with Zed, Durango and CommuniKate, were forced by Poseidon to bring him the enchanted eyes of Ender. After he got the eyes, the god decided to get rid of the heroes with a kraken, but Quinn and the others escape and try to stop Poseidon, and soon Zeus comes, stops Poseidon and asks the heroes to help him. Apparently after helping Zeus, Quinn and Toby returned home.


== Personality ==
Quinn is independent and determined. She likes solitude and dislikes bossy, authoritative, or cocky people but learns to work with everyone throughout the show.


== Relationships ==


=== Toby ===
Despite the fact that Toby and Quinn represent the same biome, they have a rather weak relationship, at the level of communication.


=== CommuniKate ===
Quinn strongly hates CommuniKate and wants to stop her from deleting players, but is forced to work with her in order to save their friends in season 2.


=== Durango ===
She and Durango started becoming rivals throughout the show but later on, in Episode 7, they put their differences aside and became friends to defeat CommuniKate and go back home.


=== Mo ===
They've been friends since Episode 2, always helping each other out during challenges and eventually stopping CommuniKate's gameshow.
