21w14a is the sixteenth snapshot for Java Edition 1.17, released on April 7, 2021, which adds raw metals and the ability to wax oxidized copper and cut copper, and fixes some bugs and crashes.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Blocks of Copper

Added waxed variants of oxidized copper, cut copper, and their variants.


=== Items ===
 Raw Metal

Added raw iron, raw copper and raw gold.
Allows Fortune to have an effect on these ores.
Drop from their respective ore.
The ores can still be obtained with Silk Touch.
Can be smelted into an ingot.
Piglins attempt to pick up raw gold.


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Tags

Added waxed oxidized cut copper stairs and slabs to the stairs and slabs block and item tags, respectively.
Added raw gold to the piglin_loved item tag.
