A blackstone slab is a decorative slab variant of blackstone that generates in bastion remnants and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Blackstone slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Blackstone slabs generate as part of bastion remnants.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Blackstone slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.


== Sounds ==
In Bedrock Edition, when a blackstone slab is combined into a double slab, the block's use sound is played.
