26.1 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on February 19, 2026 on most devices and on February 23, 2026 on iOS and iPadOS, which fixes two bugs.


== Fixes ==
2 issues fixed
MCPE-169958 – Relic Music Disc emits redstone signal strength of 15, instead of 14
MCPE-234792 – The UI looks small even if GUI Scale is set to max
