Mr. Warner is the father of Jett and Dri, serving as the deuteragonist of Minecraft: The Tournament.


== Appearance ==

When introduced, Mr. Warner is established to wear a blazer and slacks.


== Personality ==

Mr. Warner is a very passionate father, often at the expense of his children's dignity. Immediately upon being introduced, he fist pumps his son, and blows a kiss to his wife. He likes to put strange toppings on pizza, often surprising his kids with specific recipes of his own design, such as Pineapple Sardine Sadness.


== Powers and abilities ==

During his children's infancy, Mr. Warner took an interest in Minecraft.
