v0.4.0 alpha rev 2 is a minor update to Minecraft: Pocket Edition that was released on September 14, 2012. This version was exclusively released on iOS to add support for the iPhone 5 resolution and to fix some of the bugs and crashes in v0.4.0. It was reuploaded on September 17, 2012 as v0.4.0 alpha rev 3 to fix more bugs and crashes.


== Changes ==
Added support for iPhone 5 resolution
Creative mode additions:
TNT
Cannot be activated due to absence of flint and steel.
Trapdoors
Stone Bricks


== Fixes ==
1 bug fixed

Explosions now destroy the ground.


== Reupload fixes ==
Fixed further bugs and crashes on iOS devices.
