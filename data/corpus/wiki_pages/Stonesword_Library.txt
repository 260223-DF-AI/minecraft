Excalibur County Library and Media Center, better known as Stonesword Library, is a major location in the Minecraft: Stonesword Saga series of books.


== Architecture ==
The building is a moderate sized brick settlement, similar in shape and architecture to Woodsword Middle School. Much like the school, its entrance is a set of stairs under a portico supported by two pillars. Unlike Woodsword Middle School, the building is more proportionate, though still with larger than average doors. The building appears to be around three or four stories, with windows on every side of the first, below the roof, with the above floor only having four, and the roof showing either a shed or a stairway.
Surrounding the school are several trees, with a table in place of the bushes in front of Woodsword. In front of the school is the titular Excalibur, a statue of the legendary sword in the stone. The stone resembles a tall, spiraling formation with no visible cracks, placed on top of a brick podium. The sword itself sticks out at a slight angle, and bears a noticeable resemblance to a stone sword, with the same hilt and pommel.
