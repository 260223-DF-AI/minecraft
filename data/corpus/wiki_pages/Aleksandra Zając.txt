Aleksandra 'MissMarzenia' Zając is a people operations manager at Mojang Studios. From April 2013 to September 2015, she was a customer support agent.


== Biography ==
Missmarzenia was engaged to Nathan Adams in 2013. Nathan announced on August 24, 2016, that the two of them were expecting a baby boy. The baby was later born on October 2, 2016.  In some time between 2016 and 2021 they broke up.
