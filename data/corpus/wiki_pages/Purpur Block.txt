Purpur blocks are decorative blocks that can be crafted with popped chorus fruit and are naturally generated in End cities and End ships.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
The suitable tool to break purpur block is pickaxe. Purpur block needs to be broken using a pickaxe, otherwise the breaking time will increase and the block drops nothing.

Purpur block drops itself when it's destroyed.


=== Natural generation ===
Purpur blocks can be found naturally as part of End cities and End ships.


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==
Purpur blocks are primarily decorative blocks, similar to other brick-type blocks. They can also be used to craft different variants of the block.
