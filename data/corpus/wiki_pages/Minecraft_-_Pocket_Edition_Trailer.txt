The Minecraft - Pocket Edition Trailer is a trailer for Pocket Edition, produced by the YouTube channel Hat Films. The trailer is largely live action, and attempts to sell the concept of a touchscreen by emphasizing mobility and thumbs.


== Summary ==
The video begins by showing a pair of "Thumbs". They are said to have a "greater purpose," in the form of playing Minecraft on Android devices. The trailer then expounds on this, stressing that the game can be played anywhere, in particular the restroom, promising that "you can always Craft!" The video then segues into a short lapse of gameplay, showing an unseen player build a statue of the Android mascot out of lime wool.
The video ends on a black screen with a logo for the game, as the Android waves.
