v0.10.5 alpha is a minor update to Minecraft: Pocket Edition released on January 11, 2015 for Android and on January 8, 2015 for Fire OS. It fixes some minor bugs in v0.10.4. For Fire OS, this version includes changes from 0.10.1, 0.10.2, 0.10.3 and v0.10.4. This version was not released for iOS or Windows Phone. This is the first version released in 2015.
