2.12.2 is an update to the Launcher released on October 20, 2023.


== Changes ==
Changed the Java Edition update process.
Changed some customer service and management processes.


== Fixes ==
MCL-23198 - Formatting issue with text strings for operating systems and age rating within the upsell section for Java Edition.
MCL-23694 - Microsoft Store is incorrectly referred to as "Windows Store" in four launcher strings.
