57Digital is a mobile application development company based in Sheffield, England.   It is run by Jamie Chapman and Luke Dixon, two software engineers from Sheffield.
57Digital is responsible for creating an official Minecraft companion guide, Minecraft Explorer, along with several other applications previously available for iOS devices.


== Minecraft apps ==


=== Official ===
Minecraft Explorer
Minecraft: Skin Studio
Minecraft: Papercraft Studio
Add-Ons Studio for Minecraft
Minecraft Skin Scanner
