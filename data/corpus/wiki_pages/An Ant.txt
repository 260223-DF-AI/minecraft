An ant is a joke block introduced in Java Edition 20w14∞ that moves around when placed on top of black or white concrete.


== Obtaining ==
An ant cannot be obtained as an item. It can be placed using commands such as /fill or /setblock, and naturally generates in the "ant" dimension. It cannot be mined or exploded.


=== Natural generation ===
A single ant naturally generates in the "ant" dimension, where it begins moving immediately.


== Usage ==
An ant simulates Langton's ant. If placed on top of black or white concrete, it moves in a deterministic pattern, turning the concrete underneath it into its other variation.
If two ant blocks collide, it merges into a single block. It cannot be pushed by pistons, and can replace bedrock. If the concrete beneath it is broken, it stops moving. If a block is in its way, it either pushes the block or destroys it.
