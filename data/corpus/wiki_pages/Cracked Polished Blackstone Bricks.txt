Cracked polished blackstone bricks are the cracked variant of polished blackstone bricks.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Cracked polished blackstone bricks needs to be broken using a pickaxe, otherwise the block drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Cracked polished blackstone bricks generate as part of bastion remnants and ruined portals in the Nether.
