Potato pedicules are chain-like blocks that generate naturally in the Potato dimension in 24w14potato.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Potato pedicules can be broken with any tool and drop themselves as an item.


=== Natural generation ===
Potato pedicules generate naturally as part of potato trees, potato mineshafts, and potato villages.


== Usage ==
Potato pedicule can be used as a decorative block in a similar manner to chains.
