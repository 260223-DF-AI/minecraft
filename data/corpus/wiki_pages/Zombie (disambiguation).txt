Zombie Horse, a zombie hostile variant of the horse

== Minecraft Dungeons ==


=== Mobs ===
Zombie, a basic melee mob found in most places.
Grim Guardian, an ancient variant of a zombie found within Grim Guardian Ancient Dungeon.
Baby Zombie, a small and faster zombie variant.
The Tiny Scourge, an ancient variant of a baby zombie found within The Tiny Scourge Ancient Dungeon.
Chicken Jockey a baby zombie that spawns riding a chicken.
Chicken Jockey Tower a chicken jockey variant that features multiple baby zombies stacked on top of each other.
The Tower, an ancient variant of a chicken jockey tower found within The Tower Ancient Dungeon.
Husk, a zombie variant found only in desert missions.
Frozen Zombie a snowy zombie variant that attacks with snowballs in the Creeping Winter DLC.
Jungle Zombie, a mossy zombie that can inflict poison in the Jungle Awakens DLC.
Drowned, an ocean zombie variant that appears in Hidden Depths DLC.
Vengeful Mariner, an ancient variant of a trident drowned found within Vengeful Mariner Ancient Dungeon.
Baby Drowned, a small variant of drowned.
Drowned Necromancer, a boss variant of drowned.
Zombified Piglin, a mob that attacks with a sword and has more piglin-like sounds than in Minecraft.
Zombified Piglin Hunter, a ranged zombified piglin variant that shoots targets with a crossbow.
Zombified Fungus Thrower, a mob that throws Blue Nethershrooms which produce blue poison clouds.
Zombified Baby Pig, a pet baby pig which is zombified.
