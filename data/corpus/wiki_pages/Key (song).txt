"Key" is a song composed by C418 that plays in the Overworld and when the player is in Creative mode in Java Edition.


== Locations ==


== Track description ==

"Key" is composed of a quiet, distant, sparse melody played on an echoing piano.
The album version of "Key" continues with a single, more heavily echoed note, leading directly to be paired with "Door", a bright remix of "Minecraft" played with multiple instruments.

=== Raw music files ===
In Java Edition, the music files can be found in .minecraft/assets/objects.
In Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Education, it can be found in:

Mobile: com.mojang/resource_packs/music/vanilla_music/sounds/music/game/
Windows: %PROGRAMFILES%\WindowsApps\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP_<version>_x64_8wekyb3d8bbwe\data\resource_packs\vanilla_music\sounds\music\game\
All music files are in Ogg Vorbis.
