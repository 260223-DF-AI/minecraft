15w40a is the twenty-ninth snapshot for Java Edition 1.9, released on September 30, 2015.


== Changes ==


=== Mobs ===
General

Changes to mob pathfinding.
Mobs now avoid cacti and fire.
Blazes don't avoid fire.
Passive mobs following a player holding food now go around water, if possible.
Entities panicking and on fire preferentially run toward water within 5 blocks horizontally and 4 blocks vertically, even if they cannot actually reach it.
Shulkers

No longer attack on Peaceful difficulty.
Shulker bullets despawn on Peaceful, but not shulkers themselves.


=== Gameplay ===
Health

Players regenerate 1HP every half-second instead of 1HP every 4 seconds, when saturation is not empty.
Regenerating health increases exhaustion by 4 points rather than 3.
