Monty is a character appearing in the Minecraft: Official Magazine and several books. The resident mobologist of his group, what Monty lacks in brawn he excels in brains, capable of extensive research into the undead.


== Appearance ==


== Personality ==
Monty loves animal and plants, and is unaware of the dangers around him.
