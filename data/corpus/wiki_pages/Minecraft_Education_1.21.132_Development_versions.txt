This is a list of Minecraft Education 1.21.132 development versions.


== Preview 1.21.110.1 ==
1.21.110.1 (Windows, ChromeOS, Android) or 1.21.112.1 (iOS), is the first Preview version for Minecraft Education 1.21.132, released for Windows, ChromeOS, and Android on October 1, 2025, and for iOS on December 8, 2025, which adds features from The Copper Age drop.


=== Additions ===

Added features and items from Bedrock Edition 1.21.100 and Bedrock Edition 1.21.111, The Copper Age.
Added the "Lava Chicken" music disc from Bedrock Edition 1.21.93 and the Dedicated Servers and Cross Tenant hosting.


=== Changes ===


==== General ====
Python Notebooks has been removed as a supported IDE.


=== Fixes ===
Further improvements in performance and product stability.


== Preview 1.21.131.1 ==
1.21.131.1 (ChromeOS, Android, iOS) or 1.21.131.2 (Windows), is the second and final Preview version for Minecraft Education 1.21.132, released for iOS, ChromeOS, and Android on December 18, 2025, and for Windows on December 23, 2025, which adds features from the Mounts of Mayhem drop.


=== Additions ===

Added features and items from Bedrock Edition 1.21.120 and Bedrock Edition 1.21.130, Mounts of Mayhem.
Except the UWP to GDK migration.


=== Changes ===


==== General ====
Dedicated Server updates.


=== Fixes ===
Bugfixes and stability improvements.
