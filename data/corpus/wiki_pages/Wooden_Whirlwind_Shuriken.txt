The Wooden Whirlwind Shuriken (, Ki no Kazama Shuriken) is a very long stone sword, created using the Imagination Mod Power in Minecraft: The Manga.


== Appearance ==
The Wooden Whirlwind Shuriken is a large, wooden shuriken with a plus-shaped grip in its center.


== Functionality ==
The Wooden Whirlwind Shuriken can be used as a fan by spinning it at high speeds, allowing it to deflect arrow shots. Additionally, it can thrown at enemies, slashing into them upon contact.
