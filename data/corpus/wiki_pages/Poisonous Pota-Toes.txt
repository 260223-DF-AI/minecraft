Poisonous pota-toes are a joke type of boots added in 24w14potato, the 2024 April Fools snapshot.


== Obtaining ==


=== Crafting ===


=== Void ===
After falling into the void in Potato dimension, the player spawns high above their world spawn point in the Overworld, wearing poisonous pota-toes. If the player is already wearing boots, the boots will be destroyed and replaced by poisonous pota-toes. 


== Usage ==
Poisonous pota-toes can be equipped in the fourth armor slot like any other boots. They don't provide any armor points, but instead protect the player from falling. Poisonous pota-toes break when the player would receive any fall damage, and place poisonous mashed potato around the player, negating all the damage. One layer of poisonous mashed potato is always placed directly below the player.
Poisonous pota-toes can be stacked, and are used up one by one if the player has multiple equipped.
