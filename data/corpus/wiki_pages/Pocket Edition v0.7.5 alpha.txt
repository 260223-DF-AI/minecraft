v0.7.5 alpha is a minor update to Minecraft: Pocket Edition released on September 4, 2013 for Android and on September 12, 2013 for iOS. It fixes some of the bugs and crashes in v0.7.4.


== Additions ==
Crafting can now be closed with the menu button on the Xperia Play.
