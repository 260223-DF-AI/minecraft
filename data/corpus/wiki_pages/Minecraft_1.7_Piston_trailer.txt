Minecraft 1.7 Piston trailer is a trailer, promoting the then-upcoming release of Java Edition Beta 1.7 showcasing the new pistons and their various uses. It was produced by the YouTube channel Hat Films and uploaded to the official Minecraft channel on June 30, 2011.


== Summary ==
The video shows djh3max wandering a long platform showcasing many different piston contraptions in the background.
