Blockstates definition (also known as block state model dispatch) are used to render blocks there are several different variants of them (like doors, which can be open or closed). Each block has its own blockstates definition file, which lists all the existing variants and links them to their corresponding model. Blocks can also be composed of several different models at the same time, called "multipart". The models are then used depending on the block state of the block.
The blockstates definitions are stored as JSON files in a resource pack in the assets/<namespace>/blockstates folder.
