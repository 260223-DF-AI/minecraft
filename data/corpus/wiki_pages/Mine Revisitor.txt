The mine revisitor is a block added in 25w14craftmine. It resembles a re-textured sculk shrieker.


== Obtaining ==
The mine revisitor is unobtainable in Survival mode, however it generates at the hub upon creating a world and is in the Creative mode inventory.


== Usage ==
Can be used to re-visit mines the player has already generated.
Once used, the player will be put in Adventure mode and will be transported to the mine they want to re-visit.
The player will also be given an eye of exit once transported to the mine they are re-visiting.
