A block of resin is a block equivalent to nine resin clumps.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Blocks of resin can be broken instantly, regardless of held items, or when under the Mining Fatigue effect. When broken, it drops itself.


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==
Because blocks of resin can be broken instantly without any tool, and do not have unusual interactions like slime block, honey block, and TNT, they can be used as a temporary building block that is easy to place and break.
