Chapters is the main story progression found within Minecraft Blast and can be found within the journey screen. Chapters are linked with the tasks, each chapter containing different tasks that correspond to the story. Completing all the tasks found within a chapter progresses the game into the next chapter.


== List of Chapters ==


=== Chapter 1 ===
Chapter 1 follows the story of the companion spawning into the Overworld and exploring around the area whilst mining, crafting and building themselves a home. Halfway the chapter, night falls, triggering a cutscene of the companion meeting their demise to a group of zombies after which the companion respawns, builds a bridge, and meets a villager. The villager suggests tasks for the companion to do.
Completing chapter 1 will earn you:

 2000 Emeralds
 Infinite Hearts for 15 minutes
 3 Rockets
 3 TNT
 3 End Crystals
 1 Pickaxe


=== Chapter 2 ===
In chapter 2, the companion suggests the villager could use a castle, a fortress or a palazzo but the villagers seem to not want any of these and suggests that they build a farm next. The companion then suggests an animal wellness retreat and the villager agrees. They then travel to a new area to build a farm for the villager's village.
Completing chapter 2 will earn you:

 2000 Emeralds
 Infinite Hearts for 15 minutes
 3 Rockets
 3 TNT
 3 End Crystals
 1 Shovel


=== Chapter 3 ===
In chapter 3, the companion enjoys building a farm for the villager's village and hears the sounds of the ocean. The day turns to night, and the companion heads off to the beach. There we are introduced to Blockbeard the pirate, where we find his ship wrecked onto a nearby island. The companion then suggests building a lighthouse to Blockbeard to prevent anymore shipwrecks from happening, Blockbeard then agrees, but warns of potential bloodthirsty sharks lurking around (which in reality are just a group of turtles).
Completing chapter 3 will earn you:

 2000 Emeralds
 Infinite Hearts for 15 minutes
 3 Rockets
 3 TNT
 3 End Crystals
 1 Sword


=== Chapter 4 ===
Not much is known about chapter 4 but it can be seen being teased within the journey screen. Completing all the chapters shows the player a "Completed!" pop-up screen which shows artwork of a chicken with a question mark above it looking at what appears to be a Nether portal.
