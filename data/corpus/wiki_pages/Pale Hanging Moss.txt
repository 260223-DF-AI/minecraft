Pale hanging moss is a block found under the logs and leaves of pale oaks. When attached to a pale oak log or leaves, it emits ambient sounds.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Pale hanging moss can be broken instantly with any tool or by hand. It drops itself only when mined with shears or a tool enchanted with Silk Touch, otherwise it drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Pale hanging moss generates under the leaves and logs of pale oaks in pale gardens. Pale hanging moss does not grow naturally after generating or being placed. Pale hanging moss does not generate as part of pale oaks grown from saplings.


=== Post-generation ===
By applying bone meal to pale hanging moss, the plant will grow one block longer at the bottom.


=== Trading ===
Wandering traders may sell 3 pale hanging moss for 1 emerald.


=== Mob loot ===
In Bedrock Edition, wandering traders have a chance of 8.5% (increased by 1% per level of Looting) to drop 3 pale hanging moss when killed while showing the item as trade offer, if the player holds an emerald.


== Usage ==


=== Ambient sounds ===
Pale hanging moss emits subtle atmospheric sounds when it is attached to pale oak leaves or a pale oak log.


=== Composting ===
Placing pale hanging moss into a composter has a 30% chance of raising the compost level by 1.
