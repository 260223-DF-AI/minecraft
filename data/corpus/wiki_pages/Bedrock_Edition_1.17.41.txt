1.17.41 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released for most platforms on October 27, 2021, and released for Fire tablets on October 29, 2021, which fixes bugs. This version is still compatible with all Bedrock platforms running on 1.17.40.


== Fixes ==
Fixed a crash that could occur when starting a split screen session while playing on PlayStation
Fixed a problem that caused certain worlds to drastically increase in size, causing high memory usage (MCPE-143156)
