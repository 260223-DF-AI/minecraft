The Sonic Add-On is an add-on developed by Gamemode One, which adds blocks, items and morphs of characters from the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise.


== Additions ==


=== Items ===
 Sonic Add-On Book
Contains explanations and recipes for the blocks and mechanics included in the add-on
Appears in the inventory upon starting the map.
 Amy Life
Turns player into Amy Rose.
 Knuckles Life
Turns player into Knuckles.
 Shadow Life
Turns player into Shadow the Hedgehog.
 Sonic Life
Turns player into Sonic the Hedgehog.
 Tails Life
Turns player into Tails.
 Ring
Used as currency in Tails Tinker's Table.
