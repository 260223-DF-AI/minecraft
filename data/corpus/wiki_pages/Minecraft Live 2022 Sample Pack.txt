Minecraft Live 2022 Sample Pack is an official add-on developed to demonstrate the capabilities of the Minecraft Entity Wizard, a feature in Blockbench.

=== Entities ===

 Biceson
Developed by Benton Brost and Connor @ Minecraft.
Described as a relative of the American Bison.
Uses the polar bear AI as a basis, with the added ability to charge, throw snowballs and icicles, and use frost walker.
 Black Bear
Developed by Beth B. and a full class.
Based off of the mascot for Austin High School.
 Boomfly
Developed by Jackson F.
A "fun take" on phantoms, with the ability to explode, likened to wardens in terms of threat.
 Booshroom
Developed by Chloe @ Minecraft
Appears during night, similar to phantoms
Drop beds when killed instead of phantom membrane
 Butterfly
Developed by Olivia.
Shares AI with bees.
 Eyeder
Developed by Rob @ Minecraft.
 Flying Cow
Developed by Trip B.
 Frost Moose
Developed by Kayla @ Minecraft.
Described as a "mystic animal at home in the snow."
 Ghost
Developed by Isah.
Flies around the world like a vex.
 Hornbill
Developed by Stephen @ Minecraft.
Distinctly not a toucan.
 Lilydog
Developed by Reagan A.
Made in the image of the developer's own dog, Lily.
Created to add wolf variety, later fulfilled in Armored Paws.
 Mimic
Developed by Jaylon B.
Referred to as the "Smick Mimic" by its creator, being a shulker that adapted to copy chests.
 Nardolphle
Developed by Anjelika @ Minecraft.
Uses the dolphin model but turtle AI.
 Narwhal
Developed by Bo P.
Created to raise awareness for real narwhals.
Pearrot
Developed by Rob @ Minecraft.
Can be tamed with apples.
Come in five different colors: green, orange, pink, and two shades of yellow.
 Phoenix
Developed by Blake G.
An "attack pet" that cannot die, turning into a baby upon being killed.
Functions similarly to a totem of undying when tamed.
 Raccoon
Developed by Dylan C.
Spawns in any forest biome and sometimes plains.
Designed to be ambience, the creator likening them to a "source of entertainment" for the player.
Can climb trees.
 Robot
Developed by Sebastian S.
A "synthetic mob," designed to be challenging.[needs testing]
  Sea Chicken
Developed by Chloe @ Minecraft.
Made using salmon as a base, even still dropping salmon. The creator was noted as finding this humorous.
 Sheepomelon
Developed by Rob @ Minecraft.
Drop melon slices when sheared.
 Snow Wizard
Developed by Chloe @ Minecraft.
Made using AI of snow golems.
Doesn't throw splash potions.
 Spooky Golem
Developed by Jack S.
Created to celebrate Halloween, the creator's favorite holiday.
"Somehow still moving around from beyond the grave."
Uses the snow golem model with the AI of a small slime .
 Wraith
Developed by Jordan J.
A resurrected evoker, created from it using the totem of undying.
Intended to be stronger than regular evokers.
Uses the evoker AI and model as a base .
