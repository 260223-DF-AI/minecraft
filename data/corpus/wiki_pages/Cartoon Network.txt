Cartoon Network is a television network created to air and promote exclusively animated series, such as Dexter's Laboratory, The Powerpuff Girls, Edd, Edd n' Eddy, Codename: Kids Next Door, and The Amazing World of Gumball. Several properties made by the network have collaborated with Mojang Studios.
