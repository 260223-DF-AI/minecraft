Mega-Mobbo, also known as Mobbozilla, is a ginormous golem made in the image of Mobbo, created by Jonathan Minecraft as part of his quest to bring about Moboocraft.


== Appearance ==
Mega-Mobbo resembles Mobbo, however presented in grayscale and about 70-80 blocks tall, apparently constructed out of stone. Inside the mouth is what appears to be a control room or booth, where passengers can ride inside of it.


== Abilities ==
Being incredibly large, Mega-Mobbo possesses great strength, being able to squish the wither, a ravager, and an elder guardian with a mere stomp of the foot. In it possession is a ginormous stone sword, however it is never shown using it.


== Biography ==


=== Around the Minecraft World in 80 Biomes ===


=== Future ===
In its first appearance, Mega-Mobbo was seen in a future ruled by the Squid Overlords, towering over a city. As Narrator was transported into this reality through the time moosheen. While there, he assumed it was the 1980s.
