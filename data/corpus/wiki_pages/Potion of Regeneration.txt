A potion of Regeneration is a potion that provides Regeneration when used.


== Obtaining ==


=== Brewing ===


=== Mob loot ===
A witch has an 8.5% chance to drop a potion of Regeneration if it dies while drinking the potion.


=== Generated loot ===
Each trial chambers supply chest can generate with 2 to 10 potions of Regeneration  in it.


== Usage ==
Potions of Renegeration allow the player to quickly regenerate health. Splash and lingering potions of Regeneration work on most mobs and heal those mobs just as much as they heal players.
