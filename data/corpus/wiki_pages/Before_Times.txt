The Before Times were an era pivotal to the story of Minecraft: The Outsider. A historical period predating recorded history, where humanity was forced to fend for themselves in an empty world overrun with monsters.


== Biography ==

The Before Times consisted of several centuries, so many that the Before Times' beginning "can't be counted". At some point, humans emerged in the Overworld, lacking any inherent resources. As they scavenged and created, they came into conflict with several hostile mobs, such as zombies, slimes, creepers, and endermen.


== Aftermath ==
At an unknown time, many humans came together and created the City, a solution to many of the problems faced during the Before Times. In the centuries that followed, their original struggles became a distant memory, many in doubt they ever had to deal with such hurdles. Many children became fascinated by these stories, some trying to read up on them at the City library, others pretending they were alive at the time.
