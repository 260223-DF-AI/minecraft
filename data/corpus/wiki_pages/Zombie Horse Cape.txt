The Zombie Horse Cape is a cape that was given out to promote Mounts of Mayhem.


== Appearance ==
The cape's design is based on the pelt of a zombie horse. In particular, it resembles the part on the side with the partially exposed bones.


== Distribution ==

The Zombie Horse Cape could be obtained as a reward for the "Spear Mayhem" challenge, which ran from December 12, 2025 at 16:00 UTC to December 15, 2025 at 18:00 UTC. The challenge involved killing 5 mobs using a spear in Survival mode in Bedrock Edition during the time period. The five mobs could be killed separately, and any neutral or hostile mob would count. In addition, going from Creative to Survival mode was allowed.
Distribution of the cape began on December 18, 2025, with all eligible players receiving the cape within approximately 30 days after the promotion end date (i.e. before January 14, 2026).
