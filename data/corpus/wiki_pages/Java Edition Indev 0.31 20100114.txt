A version of Indev 0.31 was released on January 14, 2010, at or around 00:18 UTC.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
  Water spawner

When placed, the block would create water blocks which would replicate infinitely to fill up specific volumes.
Added 99 of it to the inventory.


=== Gameplay ===
Inventory

It is now possible to pick up more blocks after filling up a stack, however, any new items each form separate stacks in the inventory.
Picking up any more items will cause them to go into your armor slots.
Rana is no longer visible in the inventory screen, as nothing is in it now.


== Fixes ==
1 bug fixed

Water now flows much faster.
