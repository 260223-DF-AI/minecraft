El Fin the Archmage or Fin is a character in the novel Minecraft: The End. He lives in the End with his sister Mo.


== Appearance ==


== Personality ==

Fin is cautious and reserved, acting conservative towards to the concept of adventure, but he is generally open to visitors (Kan). He can become horrified and enraged at times, as exemplified by his actions when he learns the true nature of his friends.


== Biography ==

A long time ago, an adventurer named El Fin the Archmage defeated the Ender Dragon, but decided to stay in the End. Since then, he has been through 1000 such cycles.
He lives in "an end ship crammed with junk snatched up from anywhere we could find it" with his sister Mo and his pet shulker Grumpo. After humans enter his ship, he is triggered to investigate his true nature. This culminates when he chooses to read enchanted books to learn about his past. By the end of the book, Fin is happy to remain in the end ship.
