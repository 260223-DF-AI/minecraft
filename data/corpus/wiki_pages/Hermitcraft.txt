Hermitcraft, also written as HermitCraft, is a private Minecraft whitelisted server created for and run by several YouTubers, referred to as Hermits, such as Mumbo Jumbo and Grian. It was founded by Generikb on April 12, 2012, and has continued growing with many vanilla and modded seasons, the most recent one being Season 11. The server is currently administered by Xisumavoid, one of the earliest members of the server. Its members, called Hermits, are popular Minecraft players who have been recognized by other Hermits and have been invited to join.

== Seasons ==
Since 2012, there have been 11 seasons of Hermitcraft. Some of them are Vanilla Minecraft, while others are modded Minecraft. The first season started in 2012 and the latest vanilla season in 2025, modern ones lasting about 2 years.


== Events and minigames ==
The Hermits regularly create minigames during the course of a season in order to keep things fun and interesting. Some of them have been so popular that they are now played throughout Minecraft. For example, the game 'Tag!’ was invented by Grian in Season 6, where one tries to get rid of a tag as fast as possible if they receive it. Another example is Tegg, where the objective of the game is to hide a dragon egg.
They also host server-wide events and activities for the Hermits to participate in and the audience to enjoy. For example, in The Prank War of Season 6, two sides of the Hermitcraft server fought due to pranks that were made before the war. Another example is the Big Moon Incident in Season 8, in which the Moon crashed on the world, ending the season.
Alongside that, they have also invented their own gameshows. Zedaph in particular created "Is That Sheep Looking At Me?" where three contestants played games involving sheep. This was played in Season 5, Season 6, and Season 10.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==


=== Events ===

Several segments dedicated to Hermitcraft have been held at various Minecraft events over the years. At MINECON 2016, there was a panel where members of the server offered advice to aspiring creators. A similar subject was covered at MINECON Earth 2018. During MINECON Live 2019, a community panel was held to discuss playing together as a team.
From April 12 to April 13, 2025, a charity stream was held by the Hermits. A joint-collaboration between the server, Mojang Studios, and the Gamers Outreach Foundation, the event was hosted inside the Mojang headquarters in Redmond, Washington. At TwitchCon San Diego 2025, as part of the awards show in the half-time break of The Copper Crown, the Hermits received the "Heartcrafter" award by Mojang.


=== Marketplace content ===
In 2024, a copy of the world used for Season 9 of Hermitcraft was released on the Marketplace as a free map, in collaboration with "the Marketplace people." The following year, the worlds from Season 8 and Season 10 were also released.
On October 7, 2025, Hermitcraft released a paid add-on for their trading card game on the Marketplace.


== Trivia ==
Current Mojang employees Nathan 'Dinnerbone' Adams and Marc 'Marc_IRL' Watson are former members of the Hermitcraft server.
