Tiny Lydia is a miniaturized doppelganger of Lydia Winters, who appeared in the 2023 Mob Vote Event.


== Appearance ==

Much like the other Tiny characters, Tiny Lydia is a caricature of a real person, with an oversized head and downsized body. Her appearance closely matches her real life counterpart, with brown hair and a fade, blue eyes, and warm skin.
