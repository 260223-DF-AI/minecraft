1.18.11 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released only for the Nintendo Switch on February 11, 2022, which fixes bugs. This version is still compatible with all Bedrock platforms running on 1.18.10.


== Fixes ==
Fixed a crash that could occur during gameplay.
Fixed players not being directed to minigames on some Featured Servers.
Fixed NPC dialogue prompts closing if there was a dialogue open command on a button.
