Polished diorite is the polished version of diorite.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Polished diorite can be mined using any pickaxe. If broken without a pickaxe, it drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Polished diorite can generate in warm ocean ruins.

=== Stonecutting ===


=== Trading ===


== Usage ==
Polished diorite is currently used only for decoration. It has the same blast resistance as stone, meaning it can be used as a substitute for stone when building.
