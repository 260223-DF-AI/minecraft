Stone stairs are a decorative stairs variant of stone used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Stone stairs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.

=== Note blocks ===
Stone stairs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
