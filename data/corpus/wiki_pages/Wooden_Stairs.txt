Wooden stairs are a wooden variant of stairs, crafted from their respective planks.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Wooden stairs can be broken with anything, but axes are the fastest.


=== Natural generation ===
Oak stairs generate as part of:

Plains villages
Woodland mansions
Shipwrecks
Spruce stairs generate as part of:

Igloo basements
Witch huts
Shipwrecks
Taiga, snowy taiga, and snowy tundra villages
Birch stairs generate as part of:

Woodland mansions
Shipwrecks
Jungle stairs generate as part of shipwrecks.
Dark Oak stairs generate as part of:

Woodland mansions
Shipwrecks
Pillager outposts
Acacia stairs generate as part of:

Shipwrecks[BE  only]
Savanna villages


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==


=== Fuel ===
Overworld wooden stairs can be used as fuel in furnaces, smelting 1.5 items per block.
