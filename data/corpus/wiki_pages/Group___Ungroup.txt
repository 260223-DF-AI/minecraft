Group / Ungroup is a joke item added in 26w14a.


== Obtaining ==
It is automatically obtained and appears in the hotbar when the player starts up a world in the Herdcraft Update. It also appears in the hotbar after the player dies.


== Usage ==
The Group/Ungroup item allows a player to group living blocks/items for any of the players shenanigans.
