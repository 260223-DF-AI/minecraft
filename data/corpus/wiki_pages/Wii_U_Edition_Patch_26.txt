Patch 26  is a version of Wii U Edition released on July 25, 2017.  
[edit]


== Additions ==
Added "Canyon", a free Glide track.
Added the Biome Settlers 2 skin pack.
Added Terracotta and Glazed Terracotta layers to Tumble.


== Changes ==

Improved performance in Solo Glide when restarting the level (particularly with split-screen spectators).
Totem of Undying now also applies Fire Resistance II for 40 seconds.
The duration of the Regeneration II effect given by the totem of undying is now reduced from 45 seconds to 40 seconds.
Death screen has a slight delay before buttons become active, to avoid accidentally clicking buttons when killed.
Firework rocket crafting recipe now produces 3 fireworks instead of 1.
Elder guardians now drop dry sponges instead of wet sponges again.
Pick blocking farmland now gives dirt again.
Changed the minimum and maximum values for structure spacing in the custom superflat menu from 512m-864m to 256m-1600m.
