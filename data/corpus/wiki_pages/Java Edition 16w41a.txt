16w41a is the eleventh snapshot for Java Edition 1.11, released on October 13, 2016 mainly to fix a few bugs with observers and pistons.


== Changes ==
Item durability bar

The colors of the durability bar, which is displayed on damaged items in the hotbar and inventories, have been changed.
The color is now bright during the entire length of the bar, when previously it faded through a set of not so bright oranges and yellows, it now fades through clear yellow and orange.
Also, the background of the durability bar is now completely black, where it used to be transparent.
Elytra

Now have their own sound when equipped.
Llamas

The spawn rate has been drastically reduced.
