Mo is a character in the Minecraft Mini-Series and part of Team Mooshroom with Slugger.


== Appearance ==
Mo has a red and white mushroom hat covering her black colored eyes, brown hair, light skin, gray mycelium colored tunic, a red belt, brown wrist pads, dark gray pants, and bare feet.


== Biography ==
Before the series began, Mo was mushroom hunter.
Mo was once teleported along with Slugger by CommuniKate using a command block. During the challenges, she dug many tunnels under the islands, and then triggered her own death by explosion to save her friends. Mo then appears in the finale and discusses a sabotage plan to return home with the other heroes. Their sabotage succeeds, but instead of going home, they are teleported to the Greek Isles. Poseidon teleported Mo to a sand pillar in the Kraken Sea along with the other heroes, and she spent most of the season there. Then at the end, all the heroes escape and try to stop Poseidon. After a while, Zeus comes, stops Poseidon, and asks heroes to help him. After helping the god, Mo and Slugger apparently returned home.


== Personality ==
Mo is fairly reserved and quiet. She enjoys helping people and finding alternative ways to get out of situations or fix problems and mistakes.


== Relationships ==


=== Slugger ===
Apparently, Mo and Slugger were friends before the series began, and maintained this relationship until the end of the series.


=== Quinn ===
They've been friends since Episode 2, always helping each other out during challenges and eventually stopping CommunitKate's gameshow.


== Trivia ==
Mo is based on the "Mushroom Hunter" from Mattel which in turn was based on a skin by the same name in the Biome Settlers Skin Pack 2.
