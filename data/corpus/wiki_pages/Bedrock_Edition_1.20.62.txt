1.20.62 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on February 15, 2024, which fixes some gameplay crashes. It is also the last version which supported iOS 11, iOS 12, Android 5.x Lollipop, 6.0 Marshmallow and 7.x Nougat.


== Fixes ==
Fixed several crashes that could occur during gameplay.
