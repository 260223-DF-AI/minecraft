The deep lukewarm ocean is a deep variant of lukewarm oceans.


== Description ==

The deep lukewarm ocean generates in deep waters. Since they are a deep ocean variant, they can generate ocean monuments and have more tall seagrass. With Vibrant Visuals, deep lukewarm oceans use a coolish humid volumetric fog setting, which slightly fades the distance in an orange tint.

== Sounds ==
When in any ocean biome, unique ocean ambience play randomly. Normal overworld tracks also play, alongside "Axolotl", "Dragon Fish" and "Shuniji", as the requirements for those are merely to be underwater rather than biome-determined.
