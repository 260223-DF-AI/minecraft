Patton Peter Oswalt (born January 27, 1969) is an American stand-up comedian and actor. He is best known for his roles in media such as Ratatouille, Bojack Horseman, 22 Jump Street. As a stand-up comedian, Oswalt has appeared in six stand-up specials. He has won two Emmy Awards, one Grammy Award, and various miscellaneous awards for his acting and stand-up comedy work.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
Oswalt voices the male variant of Jesse in Minecraft: Story Mode and Minecraft: Story Mode - Season Two.
