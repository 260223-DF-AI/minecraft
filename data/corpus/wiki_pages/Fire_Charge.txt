A fire charge is an item that can be used as a single-use version of a flint and steel or shot as a small fireball from a dispenser.


== Obtaining ==
Despite firing small fireballs, blazes do not drop fire charges.

=== Crafting ===


=== Bartering ===
When given a gold ingot, piglins have a ~8.53% (40469) chance to barter a single fire charge.


=== Natural occurrence ===

During ominous trials, each ominous trial spawner has a 15 chance to dispense a fire charge on top of either a player or a mob it spawns. The fire charge is directly dispensed into the world and cannot be obtained in the inventory.


== Usage ==

Fire charges can be used as ammunition in dispensers or as a single-use substitute for a flint and steel.
When used, it instantly places a fire, similar to flint and steel. But, unlike flint and steel, the fire charge is consumed in the process. It can prime TNT and light nether portals, campfires, candles, cakes with candles, and creepers[Java Edition  only].
When a fire charge is fired from a dispenser, it shoots a small fireball. However, a player cannot simply use a fire charge while holding it to shoot a small fireball.
