The mushroom field shore or mushroom island shore was a technical biome that represented both the shores and the rivers of the mushroom fields.


== Description ==
The mushroom field shore generated when a river cut through mushroom fields as well as when it bordered an ocean, unless the ocean was a deep variant, in which case a steep cliff generated instead. The terrain of this biome was much flatter and shallower in elevation, similar to beaches, though it was equal to the mushroom fields in every other way. Buried treasures and shipwrecks generated here‌[Bedrock Edition  only].
Like mushroom fields, mushroom field shores have a unique sky color with Vibrant Visuals, which is yellow at the horizon and gray at zenith. At sunset, the horizon turns bright red, while at sunrise it is more purple/pink. At night, there is a dim purple light at the horizon. Mushroom field shores also have slightly stronger volumetric fog, tinted by a unique purple sunlight.
