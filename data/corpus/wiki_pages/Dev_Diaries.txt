== Trivia ==
The Dev Diaries episodes "ALL ABOUT THE SPEAR" and "ALL ABOUT THE NAUTILUS" are the only proof of existence of the unreleased developer version Bedrock Edition Preview 26.0.43 and an early build of Bedrock Edition 1.21.130 respectively.
In THE HORRORS OF MINECRAFT, several add-ons are shown to demonstrate horror culture in the fandom. All of these had previously received promotions on Minecraft.net.
This continued after the video's release, with them being featured on the front page.
Alongside the release of Why Is That Squid Flying? was an article touching on many of the same topics.
