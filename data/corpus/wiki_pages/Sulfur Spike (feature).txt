Sulfur spike features are a feature in sulfur caves. There are two types of sulfur spike features: sulfur spike and sulfur spike cluster.


== Description ==
Sulfur spike features generate in sulfur caves as well as inside of sulfur pools. 
There are two types of sulfur spike features:

The first type is the "sulfur spike", which can generate with a couple sulfur blocks as the base with and at least one sulfur spike that can reach up to two blocks tall.
The second is the "sulfur spike cluster" which generate with many sulfur blocks as the base and multiple clusters of sulfur spikes that can reach up to six blocks tall.
