A pet armadillo is a pet variant of the armadillo introduced in 25w14craftmine.


== Spawning ==
A pet armadillo spawns next to the player on mine start when the "Pet Armadillo" Player Unlock is active.


== Drops ==
Pet armadillos drop nothing when killed.


== Behavior ==
The behaviors between pet armadillo and armadillo in regular versions are almost the same, but there are some differences:

The player's melee attack can't damage the pet armadillo.
The pet armadillo doesn't roll up.
Spider eyes and brushes can't be used on the pet armadillo.
