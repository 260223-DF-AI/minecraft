The Universal Event was an event server made in collaboration with Universal Pictures. There are six districts in the map, which are designed as a theme park. Players could complete two sets of quests to obtain two character creator items. The map contained 6 minigames and 3 parkour courses.


== Tickets ==
Tickets were a currency obtained by completing parkour, playing minigames, finding golden clappers, and completing achievements. They were used to purchase items from various shops around the server.


== Shot List ==
The shot list was a list of achievements which could be completed by each player on the server. 


== Golden Clappers ==
There were 12 golden clappers in hidden places around the server. For finding all of them, the player was rewarded with the Clapperboard static back character creator item.


== Districts ==

The server was divided into 6 main districts: the entrance at the bottom of the map, Courthouse Square in the center, and Jaws Lake, Shrek's Swamp, Wall Street, and Western Street around it. Each district has a distinct theme as well as unique shops, parkour, and minigames. The quick travel menu could be used to view a map of the server as well as teleport to each area.


=== Entrance ===
The entrance was the location at which players spawn. In the center was a replica of the rotating Universal globe fountain found at Universal Studios in real life. Behind the spawn point was the theme park's exit, blocked by a gate. To the left was the entrance to Wall Street, to the right was the entrance to Western Street, and directly forward was Courthouse Square on the other side of a road.
Players in the 7x7 area around the spawn point were given Invisibility for 99,999 seconds. Players who moved out of this area had the effect removed.

== Parkour ==


=== Swamp Parkour ===
The parkour course Swamp Parkour is an easy-difficulty parkour that begins in a cave in the Shrek's Swamp district. It has 8 checkpoints.


=== Rooftop Run ===
The parkour course Rooftop Run is a medium-difficulty parkour that starts on a building near a cutout, in the Wall Street district. It has 8 checkpoints.


=== Western Street ===
The parkour course Western Street is a hard-difficulty parkour that starts on a building near the food cart in the Western Street district. It has 8 checkpoints.

== Trivia ==
This is not only the first collaborative event server (the closest previous being a promotion for Minecraft Legends), but the first to be released to celebrate a holiday as opposed to a specific Minecraft release.
In one of the promotional images, a character resembling Zuri can be seen in the background, the only difference being noticeably different hair.
The Wall Street and Western Street cutouts appear to reuse spritework from the Trails & Tales Event; in particular, Efe and a camel.
Notably, the map correctly names the shark from Jaws as Bruce instead of attributing the title to him, as is common.
