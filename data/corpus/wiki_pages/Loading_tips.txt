Loading tips are informative pieces of text frequently shown on the loading world screen in Bedrock Edition. There are three categories of tips based on player progression, and exclusive sets for Creative mode, Realms, or add-ons.
Loading tips are defined in the loading_messages.json file via a resource pack and can be defined either by a localization key or by an untranslatable raw string.


== List of tips ==
Note that the in-game text does not have links and styling. It is plain text, and every tip is treated as one line that is wrapped as necessary in the in-game text box. The links and additional explanations in this article have been added for the reader's convenience.


=== Beginner tips ===
These tips are always displayed when loading Survival worlds and no loading tip progression has been made.


=== Mid game tips ===
These loading tips are displayed when loading Survival worlds when the player has completed one full in-game day, tracked by day_one_experience_completed in options.txt.


=== Late game tips ===
Late game tips show when the player has entered the Nether at least once in Survival mode. This is tracked by nether_reached in options.txt.


=== Fluff/trivia ===
These tips can always show on the loading screen, regardless of player progression or specific circumstances.


=== Creative mode tips ===
These tips only appear instead of the other tips when the world's default game mode is Creative mode, regardless of the personal game mode.


=== Add-on tips ===
These tips are shown when resource packs or behavior packs are enabled for the world.


=== Realms tips ===
These tips are shown while connecting to a Realm.


=== Beta tips ===
These loading tips are shown in the beta version of the game.


=== Preview tips ===
These loading tips are shown in Minecraft Preview.


=== Minecraft Education tips ===
These loading tips are shown in Minecraft Education.
