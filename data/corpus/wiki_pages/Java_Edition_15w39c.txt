15w39c is the twenty-eighth snapshot for Java Edition 1.9, released on September 23, 2015 to fix several bugs and improve performance.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Dispensers

Now equip pumpkins and mob heads onto the heads of players, mobs and armor stands.


=== General ===
Performance

Redstone and piston contraptions visibly update much faster.
Better performance on older computers.
