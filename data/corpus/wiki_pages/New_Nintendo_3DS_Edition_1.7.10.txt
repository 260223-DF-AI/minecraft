1.7.10, the first release of the Ender Update, is a major update to New Nintendo 3DS Edition that was released on September 1920, 2018. Major additions included the End, new achievements, and new mobs including the ender dragon, shulkers, and polar bears.
This is the first version to be released outside of North America and Japan.
