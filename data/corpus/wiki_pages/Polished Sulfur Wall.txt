A polished sulfur wall is a decorative wall variant of polished sulfur.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Polished sulfur walls drop as an item if mined by any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.

=== Stonecutting ===


== Usage ==

Unlike most other walls, mobs can be leashed to polished sulfur walls similar to fences.‌[Bedrock Edition  only]
