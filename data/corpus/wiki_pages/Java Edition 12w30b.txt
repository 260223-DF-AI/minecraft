12w30b is the sixteenth snapshot for Java Edition 1.3.1, released on July 23, 2012, and fixes some of the bugs and crashes in the 12w30a snapshot.


== Fixes ==
3 bugs fixed

Fixed a null pointer exception.
Fixed being unable to right-click on blocks.
Fixed chests stacked on top of other chests not opening.


== Trivia ==
This version's article is incorrectly rotuled as "12w30a" in the URL. It also has a misspelling "mincraft".
