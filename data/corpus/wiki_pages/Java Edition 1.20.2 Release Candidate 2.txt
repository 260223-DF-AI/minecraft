1.20.2 Release Candidate 2 (known as 1.20.2-rc2 in the launcher) is the second and final release candidate for Java Edition 1.20.2, released on September 18, 2023, which fixes a bug.


== Fixes ==
1 issue fixed
Other

Fixed villagers and wandering traders suffocating when travelling up slopes in minecarts.
