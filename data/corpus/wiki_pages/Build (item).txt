Build is a joke item added in 26w14a.


== Obtaining ==
It is automatically obtained and appears in the hotbar when the player starts up a world in the Herdcraft Update. It also appears in the hotbar after the player dies.


== Usage ==
The Build item can be used to command certain living blocks to go to the targeted location and turn into normal blocks. It is the only way to build.
