Coreweb, also known as coreweb GmbH, is a German software development company based in Pfarrkirchen, Germany. It is responsible for the development of the Oreo x A Minecraft Experience video game.


== Games developed ==
Coreweb GmbH has developed and published one augmented reality videogame, Oreo x A Minecraft Experience, through collaboration between A Minecraft Movie and Oreo.
