2.1.12874 (Windows), 2.1.12872 (Linux), or 2.1.12871 (macOS) is an update to the launcher released on March 16, 2020.


== Changes ==
Fix potential crash when Launcher is closed during a download.
Fix bug where the Launcher state is sometimes not persisted.
Launch Minecraft Dungeons launcher from Minecraft Java launcher, if it is installed.
Added a Tooltip on "Open Launcher" button.
Entitlement polling.
