A red sandstone slab is a decorative slab variant of red sandstone that does not generate naturally and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Red sandstone slabs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.

=== Note blocks ===
Red sandstone slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.


== Sounds ==
In Bedrock Edition, when a red sandstone slab is combined into a double slab, the block's use sound is played.
