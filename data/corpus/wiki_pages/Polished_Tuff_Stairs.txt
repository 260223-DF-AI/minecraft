Polished tuff stairs are a decorative stairs variant of polished tuff that does not generate naturally and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Polished tuff stairs can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.

=== Note blocks ===
Polished tuff stairs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
