Hibiscus House is the name of a fortress on the Ever Realm server, inhabited by a gang of players who frequent it.


== Location ==
The building is situated on a large hill, on the north end of four split paths decorated with lamp posts. To the south end of the intersection is a large sign, pointing towards the House, as well as three other areas, with the House's sign being noticeably carved and covered in vines and a lantern.


== Architecture ==
The house is constructed out of a white inorganic material, presumably quartz or diorite, in a modern architecture style. From the west side, it resembles a conglomeration of six blocks, one rotated, with three balconies. Each of the three have noticeable railings and green banners, with wooden beams supporting the one closest to the ground. On the top of the roofs are what appears to be moss, with vines and hanging moss growing out of the bottom side. 


== Layout ==
From the west of the building, two doors can be seen, one on the front and the other on a balcony patio. The front door leads into a room with walls made of the same material as the exterior, with magenta carpeting and an assortment of trinkets. Closest to the door is a stylized poster of a creeper, as well as a second poster colored like a map. Across from there is a table, decorated with a diamond sword, a water bottle, and a stack of papers. Beneath are a diamond pickaxe, lapis ore, a crate of apples, and a carpet decorated with skulls and what appear to be slime eyes.
Above the room is a support beam, where a chicken is shown watching, and three unidentified objects built into the wall. Nearby is a set of three chests, and an armory of various gear, including a trident and diamond armor. Beside the gear are the heads of several mobs, including a creeper, wither skeleton, ghast, zombie pigman, and three eyes of ender. Beneath the heads is a lavender elevation, which hosts a table and a cardboard box of what appears to be gold.
