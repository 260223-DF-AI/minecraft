Due to the large number of Atan2 and Sine operations, the nether portal frames are not generated in real time; instead, 32 frames of animation (at a resolution of 1616) are generated once at startup and stored into an internal animation strip. The random shimmer is the same every time, as the game always uses a random number generator with a seed of 100.
