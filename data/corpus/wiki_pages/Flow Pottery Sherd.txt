A flow pottery sherd is a pottery sherd that depicts a spiral or a wind charge. It can be found in trial chambers.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Breaking a decorated pot causes it to drop all of the bricks and pottery sherds used to craft it, meaning it drops each of the flow pottery sherds that were used to craft it.


=== Natural generation ===
Decorated pots with flow pottery sherds naturally generate in trial chambers. For a randomly generated pot in trial chambers, there is 1⁄13 chance that is has one flow pottery sherd.


== Usage ==


=== Crafting ingredient ===
Flow pottery sherds can be used to craft decorated pots.
