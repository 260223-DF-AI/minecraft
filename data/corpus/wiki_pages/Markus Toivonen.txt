Markus 'Junkboy' Toivonen is the creative brand director for Mojang Studios. He is best known for designing the original version of the Minecraft logo.
He is quite secretive about his identity, and has only appeared on a video as a blue-haired pixelated face. In the Scrolls documentary, his face is blurred, but can be made out as bald. Junkboy's name also appears in the Legacy Console and Bedrock editions as "No one at Mojang has ever seen Junkboy's face." while on the world loading screen.
