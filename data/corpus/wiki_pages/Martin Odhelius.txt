Martin Odhelius is a former web developer who worked at Mojang Studios from January 2013 to March 2017.


== Biography ==
Odhelius is a graduate of the Royal Institute of Technology in mechanical engineering. He began his career at Lund University working as a developer from May 2000 to October 2000. Later at Dotify from October 2000 to December 2004, then at Creuna from February 2005 to December 2012.
