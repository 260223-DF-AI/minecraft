1.89 was an update to PlayStation 4 Edition released on March 7, 2019, to fix bugs.


== Fixes ==
6 issue fixed
Fix for a zombie texture issue in Steven Universe Mash-Up Pack.
Fix for chests splitting apart on chunk boundaries; split chests will need to be broken and placed.
Fixed old shulker boxes loading as purple shulker boxes instead of undyed.
Fix for dispensers not placing shulker boxes.
MCCE-8829  Nether brick fences required the nether brick wall recipe to be crafted.
MCCE-8832  Stone slabs can be used to craft purpur pillars.
