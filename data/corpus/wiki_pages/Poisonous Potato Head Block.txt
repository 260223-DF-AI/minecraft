A poisonous potato head block is a joke block added in 24w14potato.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
A poisonous potato head block can be mined using any item and drops itself when broken.


=== Piston interactivity ===
If a poisonous potato head block is pushed by a piston, it breaks off as an item. It can't be pulled by sticky pistons.
