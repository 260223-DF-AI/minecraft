Jennifer Coolidge is an American actress, known for her roles in the American Pie and Legally Blonde film franchises.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==

Coolidge was announced to be appearing in A Minecraft Movie on January 17, 2024. Within the film, she plays a high school vice principal, still dealing with the fallout of a divorce. On a fateful day, she encounters a nitwit and falls in love, engaged to him by the end of the film.


== Trivia ==
Director Jared Hess claimed to have envisioned Coolidge for the part as he pitched the film.
