Chunker is a free and open-source tool developed by Hive Games which can convert worlds between Java Edition and Bedrock Edition. Its development is funded by Mojang Studios to make an open-source version of the tool available. It is also recommended as tool for world conversion for mapmakers in the Bedrock Edition creator documentation, and in the Minecraft Launcher.
