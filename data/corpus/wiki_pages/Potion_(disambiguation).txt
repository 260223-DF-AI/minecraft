Potion may refer to:


== Minecraft ==
Potion, a brewed potion giving the user an effect.
Splash Potion, a throwable potion.
Lingering Potion, a throwable potion that creates an effect cloud.


=== April Fools ===
Potion of Big, a potion that enlarges the user.
Potion of Small, a potion that shrinks the user.
Potion of Sticky, a potion that allows the user to scale walls.
Potato Oil, an unused potion that makes all blocks slippery for the user.
Poisonous Potato Oil, a poisonous version of Potato Oil.


== Minecraft: Story Mode ==
Ivor's Potion, the potion intended to be used as a fail-safe for the wither storm.
Potion, regular potions that are seen throughout both seasons of the series.
Smoke Bomb, a lingering potion that produces large amount of smoke.
Splash Potion, a weapon used by witches and Ivor.


== Minecraft Dungeons ==
Burning Brew, a potion that grants the hero the burning effect.
Dense Brew, a potion that grants the hero a resistance to pushbacks and melee damage.
Oakwood Brew, a potion that briefly boosts the hero's defense.
Potion of Water Breathing, a potion that grants the ability to breathe freely underwater for a limited time.
Shadow Brew, a potion that grants invisibility and boosts the hero's next melee attack.
Strength Potion, a potion that briefly boosts damage.
Sweet Brew, a potion that grants a small defense boost and resistance to freezing effects.
Swiftness Potion, a potion that briefly boosts the hero's movement speed.
Health Potion, a potion that is part of the hero's utility bar.
Health Potion (consumable), an unused consumable version of the health potion utility.
Harming Potion, an unused potion.
Invisibility Potion, an unused potion.
Splash Harming Potion, an unused potion.
Splash Slowing Potion, an unused potion.
