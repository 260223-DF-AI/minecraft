1.90 was an update to PlayStation 4 Edition released on April 15, 2019, which added more Village & Pillage features. This update is equivalent to Bedrock Edition 1.10.0, only missing Experimental Gameplay features, e.g. new villages.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
Loom

Used to easily make patterns for banners.
Lectern

Placing a book and quill or written book on the lectern allows multiple players to read it together.
Redstone comparator functionality is not available yet.
Lantern

Acts as a new, hangable light source.
Wood and Stripped Wood

A log block with the bark texture on all 6 sides.


=== Items ===
Crossbow

New weapon.
Crossbows can be enchanted with three new enchantments.
Stronger than bow, but takes longer to recharge.
Banner Pattern

Made with the Loom.
Can be added to Banners to apply designs.
Shield

Sneak while holding a shield in either hand to block.
Banners cannot be applied to shields yet.


=== Mobs ===
Pillager

They are hostile mobs, considered a subset of illagers.
Have 24 health.
They wield crossbows.
Drop arrows and their crossbows when killed (affected by Looting).
They will raid, or take over, villages, killing villagers.
They cannot equip armor of any type.
Only available through Creative mode.
Cats

Added a new skin: Jellie, the community cat contest winner.


=== Gameplay ===
Enchantments

Quick Charge
Used on crossbows to decrease the reload time.
Multishot
Used on crossbows to shoot 3 arrows or fireworks but only consumes 1.
Piercing
Used on crossbows to shoot through mobs.


=== General ===
Texture packs

Added Minecraft Classic texture pack (built-in), which contains pre-Texture Update textures.
Trophies

Added Expansion Pack 9, which contains five new trophies:
(Silver) Atlantis? – Find an underwater ruin.
(Bronze) Organizational Wizard – Rename a Shulker Box with an Anvil.
(Bronze) Fruit on the Loom – Make a Banner with an Enchanted Apple stencil.
(Silver) Plethora of Cats – Befriend twenty stray Cats.
(Gold) Sail the 7 Seas – Visit all Ocean Biomes.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Chorus Flower 

Can now be broken when struck by an arrow.
Crops

Now have placement sounds.
Dead Bush 

Can now be used as furnace fuel.
Dispenser 

Dispensers with shears in them can now shear sheep when activated.
Leaves 

May now drop sticks when decaying.
Sponge 

Placing wet sponge in the Nether and warm biomes will dry it out after some time.
White carpets and undyed glass panes

Can now be dyed by surrounding dye with them in the crafting table.
Regular glass and glass panes have changed textures.
Logs

Renamed from wood.


=== Items ===
Enchanted golden apple

Renamed from "Golden Apple" to "Enchanted Apple" to match Bedrock Edition.
Rabbit Stew and Beetroot Soup 

Now have shapeless crafting recipes.


=== Mobs ===
Cats

Can now be healed by feeding them fish.
Creepers

Can now drop a music disc if they are killed by a stray, as well as a skeleton.
Ghasts

Now have glowing eyes and mouth when the programmer art texture pack is enabled.


=== General ===
Holding light emitting blocks now lights up held items in first person view.
Textures

The Texture Update textures have been added as the default pack.
