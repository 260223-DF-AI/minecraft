Lucky's Minigame Mayhem is a Marketplace map developed by YouTuber Logdotzip, based around a variety of minigames.


== Summary ==
The map begins with the player in an airplane, with a squirrel character ordering to jump out. Once the player jumps, the player descends using a parasol, as the title card appears. Descending down a hole in a building through a flight of stairs, the player will eventually land and meet Lucky, the owner of the theme park the map is set on. Lucky explains the situation; in order to invite guests over to the park, they will need to activate a teleporter in the center of the room, and in order to do that, the player will need to retrieve the power source, trophies, from five attractions.
Once the player is finished speaking with Lucky, a series of doors open, revealing the paths to the five areas of the map. The player is then free to explore all of them in any order, however there are several optional features and details in the hub. Outside the lime door is a poster containing the map's credits, and to the right of that another explaining that the map uses "awesome stream-safe music." Progressing from there will reveal the first golden mob, a chicken, a collectible found throughout the map. 
Further still is the Pet-O-Matic, a prize machine which rewards pets using tickets earned from the minigames. Beside the machine are a set of tables, holding a pair of roller skates, which can be used to increase the player's speed. Past those is a sign linking to Logdotzip's support website, featuring a cameo from his traditional avatar.


=== Dragons ===
The section can be identified by a build of a large, red dragon breathing fire. In the hub, it sits between a birch surrounded by tulips and a stone brick flower bed with peonies and blue orchids, beside several scooters. Descending down a resin-like path, players can pass by orange tulips, and walk into a small mud brick patio with a hedge gate and umbrella table. To the right of the patio is another packed mud path, leading into a large house with an open first floor, containing scarce filled only by a set of barrels, cobwebs, and polished andesite flooring. However, a golden spider can be found underneath the stairs.. Up a flight of stairs is an atmospheric second floor, with a wooden floor, orange carpets, tables, boxes, candles, and a bed.
Returning to the orange path, more and more tulips will build up, until the player ends up at the site of the dragon's fire. There, the player can meet Coach Brimstone, a sports coach who is also a dragon. To his left is the entrance to the Target Practice minigame, in front of another large house, made up of three buildings very close together. In the yard are several rocks of clay and andesite, amongst tall trees. Inside the central building is what looks like a cellar or workshop, with an empty bookshelf, more boxes potted plants. A door beside the stairs lead to a skull on a bone block, while the stairs lead to an open bedroom with a cake.
The leftmost building is similar, with more bookshelves and lecterns immediately visible. The second floor is again a bedroom, but in a circular shape with several bookshelves, plants, and storage units. The rightmost building has a much denser interior, with potted crimson roots and crimson fungus on the first and second floors respectively. To the right of Brimstone is another small neighborhood, with the Ring Glider minigame behind a rock. The building closest to the minigame opens to a large space, with three skulls on soul sand built into a beam, seemingly evoking the Wither. Next to it are two buildings with very similar layouts and details to the others, just with other items potted, such as brown mushroom, and lily of the valley, and warped roots.
Heading straight from Brimstone leads the player under the dragon's fire, and finally to a large, smooth quartz castle structure. The road forks into three paths, two circling the castle and leading to a Pet-O-Matic before returning to the start. The right path takes the player into the castle, with white concrete powder walls and an diorite chandelier. Climbing up the stairs will lead to an area decorated with azure bluets, with two small bedrooms on the right side. On the left, there are two nonfunctional doors, two more can be found on the third floor, reached from the right. Now on a wall, the player can walk underneath the dragon itself, made out of red nether bricks. Crossing the wall will reveal the third minigame, Sky Battle, with the golden endermite at the end in a final bedroom.


==== Target Practice ====
The minigame begins in a lobby, based inside a cramped recreation of a forest with a large mangrove house and several scarecrows, similar to those found at pillager outposts. To begin the minigame players will have to speak to Coach Brimstone, who explains the goal. Players will ride on top of a large dragon, and can shoot projectiles at targets, with the goal of earning 50 points in three minutes. Wooden targets reward 1 point, golden targets reward 3, diamond targets 5, and X targets lose 1 point. Once 50 points are earned or the time runs out, the minigame will end.


==== Ring Glider ====

The minigame begins in a lobby, based on a series of clouds made of snow and wool. To begin the minigame players will have to speak to Coach Brimstone, who explains the goal. Players will ride on top of a large dragon and have the objective of finding and going through 15 golden rings in 5 minutes. Once the 5 minutes are up or the player flew through all the rings, the game will end.
