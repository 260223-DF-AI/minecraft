We Got You is a marketing campaign promoting PC Game Pass, featuring many characters and assets from games interacting with real life players.


== PC Game Pass. We Got You. ==


=== Summary ===
As a player is scrolling PC Game pass in their bedroom, a narrator chimes in to warm them that they're in a commercial. Visibly concerned, the narrator then pivots to asking the player if they want to play Call of Duty: Black Ops 6, which the player agrees to. Suddenly, their screen begins to glitch out, before their room begins to glitch out itself, impressing them. Suddenly, the scene changes to a second player, on a fluffy couch with a Lenovo Legion Go, who is suddenly asked if they want to pilot an Airbus while playing Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024. As they're revealed to be inside a plane, the player exclaims they don't know how to fly, as the narrator concedes they don't either.
In an office building, a stressed person remarks they want a new career, as opposed to being in a cubicle. The narrator introduces them to The Sims 4, which causes the office worker to transform into a rockstar and perform an office party. Abruptly, the scene changes to a fourth player asking for a "great adventure." The narrator asks if they've heard of the Great Circle, which they haven't, as a suitcase of equipment from Indiana Jones himself falls into the room. The narrator then asks "who's next," cutting to a player in a green lit room, who asks to play with "all League of Legends champions." They do, suddenly appearing at a world series alongside them.

== AAA Titles? Goats? We Got You. ==


=== Summary ===
In a kitchen, the narrator asks a player if they "want high quality PC Games?" Asserting that they have, many things in the room begin to transform into Minecraft equivalents, including paintings, a dog becoming an armored wolf, and a zombie. Another player is asked if they would like to be a goat, presumably implying GOAT. Instead, the narrator remarks "we GOAT you" as a pun, and transforms the player into a goat from the game Goat Simulator 3, whose tongue pulls down a librarian's desk.
