The Library is a feature to simplify the process of finding and importing educational content directly in the game. It is available exclusively for teachers and students that uses Minecraft Education. It contains a list of the most popular worlds to import and use.

== Trivia ==
Before the release of the Library Beta, teachers and students were forced to use the full world library on education.minecraft.net to download worlds.
Unlike the Marketplace, all content of the library is free to use.
