Minecraft x Youtooz is an official merchandise collaboration between Minecraft and vinyl figure company Youtooz. The first release (Ender Dragon, Creeper, Wolf) happened on November 3, 2023, the second release (Warden, Bees, Chickens, Pin Set) happened on April 23, 2024, and the third release (Enderman, Zombies, Pin Set 2) happened on September 30, 2024.


== Background ==
Yootooz is a Canadian merchandising company, known for producing vinyl figures, similar to Funko Pops. Their primary focus is on merchandise of content creators from the platforms Twitch and YouTube, such as DanTDM, Stampy, CaptainSparklez, SeaPeeKay, and Technoblade. They have also made merchandise from pop culture franchises, such as Avatar: The Last Airbender, Godzilla, Scream, Hazbin Hotel, and Arcane. For some of their licenses, they will also make merchandise based off popular memes within their communities, such as for SpongeBob SquarePants, Invincible, and Five Nights at Freddy's.
