A resin brick slab is a decorative slab variant of resin bricks that does not generate naturally and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Resin brick slabs can be mined using a pickaxe. When mined without a pickaxe, it drops nothing.

=== Note blocks ===
Resin brick slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.


== Sounds ==
In Bedrock Edition, when a resin brick  slab is combined into a double slab, the block's use sound is played.
