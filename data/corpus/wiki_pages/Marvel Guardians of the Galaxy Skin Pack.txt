Marvel Guardians of the Galaxy Skin Pack is a skin pack containing 24 skins of characters from the superhero film Guardians of the Galaxy by Marvel Studios, as well as the comic series of the same name, and was available on Legacy Console Editions for Xbox consoles until December 21, 2015. Due to the Better Together Update, it was added in Bedrock Edition on September 12, 2017, although it is only available to players who owned it already when they migrated from Legacy Console Edition.
