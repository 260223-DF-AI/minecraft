Beta 1.2.0.11 (also known as 1.2 build 4) is the fourth build released for 1.2.0 that fixed bugs.


== Changes ==
Clicking the left thumb stick on a controller will now switch focus between Recipe Book and the inventory.
Added item animations to the Recipe Book to show which items are being used for crafting.
Default controller sensitivity has been increased to 60.
