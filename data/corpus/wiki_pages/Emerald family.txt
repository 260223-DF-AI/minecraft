Emerald may refer to:


== Minecraft ==
Emerald, the currency used with villagers.
Block of Emerald, a block crafted from emeralds.
Emerald Ore, a rare ore which drops emeralds.
Deepslate Emerald Ore, the deepslate variant of emerald ore.


== Minecraft Dungeons ==
Emerald, the currency used with all camp merchants except the piglin merchant.
Emerald Chest, a type of chest containing emeralds.
