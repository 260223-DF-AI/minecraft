3D is a joke item exclusively introduced in Java Edition 3D Shareware v1.34.


== Obtaining ==


=== Mob drops ===
3D is dropped by Nerd Creepers summoned via the cheat code "NEEEERD".


== Usage ==
Eating the 3D item when the hunger bar is not full shows a picture of the developer cast of Minecraft at the time. Hovering over it in the inventory says "Tasty!" in constantly changing formatting codes.
