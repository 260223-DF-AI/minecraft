TikTok, known in mainland China and Hong Kong as Douyin (Simplified Chinese: ; Pinyin: dou yin; Bopomofo:  ), is a social media and short-form online video platform owned by Chinese Internet company, ByteDance (Simplified Chinese: ; Pinyin: zi jie tiao dong; Bopomofo:   ). It hosts user-submitted videos, which may range in duration from three seconds to 60 minutes. It can be accessed through a mobile app or through its website.
TikTok has collaborated with Minecraft multiple times, typically taking the form of TikTok Game Rewards.


== Rewards drops ==

TikTok users can earn these rewards by participating in any livestream in the Minecraft category with the Game Rewards enabled.


=== Profile Frame ===
A TikTok diamond helmet profile frame was available from May 18 to May 31, 2024 by using "Create post" to share a video on TikTok lasting one minute or more using the hashtag #MinecraftBadge in a post.
