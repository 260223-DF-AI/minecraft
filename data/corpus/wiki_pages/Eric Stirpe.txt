Eric Stirpe is a writer and director. He is known as a writer in Minecraft: Story Mode and Minecraft: Story Mode - Season Two. His other works include Tales from the Borderlands (2014), Batman: The Telltale Series (2016), High on Life (2022), and The Walking Dead: Season Two (2013).


== In the Minecraft franchise ==

He is one of the writers in both seasons of Minecraft: Story Mode. He is often cited as a source for behind-the-scenes information and clarifications about the story, characters, and development choices.


== Trivia ==
Back when Order Up! came out, Eric Stirpe made bottles of Ivor's Eversauce and gave them as gifts to all team members who worked on the game. The bottles' labels were designed by him and the episode's lead designer, Molly Maloney.
