Leaf litter is a thin decorative block resembling fallen leaves. Up to four can be placed in the same block space.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Leaf litter can be mined using any tool or without a tool.

A leaf litter is also removed and drops as an item if:

its attachment block is moved, removed, or destroyed.
water or lava flows into its space.
a piston tries to push it or moves a block into its space.
The amount of leaf litter items dropped is equal to how many were present in the block space beforehand (i.e. a leaf litter block with 3 placed drops 3 leaf litter items when destroyed).


=== Natural generation ===

Leaf litter generates naturally in forests, dark forests, and wooded badlands.


=== Smelting ===
Leaf litter can be created by smelting any type of leaves block.


== Usage ==


=== Placement ===
Leaf litter can be placed on blocks that have a full solid top surface. Up to four pieces of leaf litter can be placed in a single block space. When placed, a leaf litter faces the location of the player placing it.


=== Fuel ===
Leaf litter can be used as fuel for smelting. It burns for 5 seconds, smelting 12 of an item. 1 stack of leaf litter smelts 32 items.


=== Composting ===
Using leaf litter on a composter has a 30% chance to raise the compost level by 1. A stack of leaf litter yields an average of 2.74 bone meal.


=== Color ===

Leaf litter takes on a different color depending on the biome it is placed in. It is colored with different shades of brown in most biomes or gray in pale gardens. The color applied in most biomes is determined by the dry_foliage.png colormap.
