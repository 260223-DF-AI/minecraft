Minecraft: Gear VR Edition was the former title of the version of Bedrock Edition designed for virtual reality, which was available for Samsung Galaxy Note 4 through Galaxy Note 9, and Galaxy S6 through Galaxy S10 through the Samsung Gear VR headset. It had crossplay with Pocket Edition and its derived editions. It was released on April 27, 2016, as a port from v0.14.2 alpha. Although Pocket Edition was released on the Galaxy Store, Gear VR Edition was never made free to owners of Pocket Edition on that platform. Following the Better Together Update, it is no longer named Gear VR Edition.
As of 1.16.40, Minecraft for Gear VR has not received new feature updates, such as Caves & Cliffs (1.17 & 1.18), The Wild Update, Trails & Tales, etcetera, and has been discontinued ever since.
The most recent feature update for Minecraft on Gear VR devices is the Nether Update.
