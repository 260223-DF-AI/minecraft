14w29b is the thirty-seventh snapshot for Java Edition 1.8, released on July 16, 2014, which fixes some of the bugs and crashes in 14w29a and introduces threaded chunk rendering.


== Changes ==


=== General ===
Optimizations

Threaded chunk rendering.
