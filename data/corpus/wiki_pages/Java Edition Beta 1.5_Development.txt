An unreleased development version of Beta 1.5 was shown on April 9, 2011 in a video on the official Minecraft YouTube channel. It shows in-development features later included in Beta 1.5 and later releases.


== Differences from Beta 1.5 ==


=== General ===
Weather

The snowfall looks slightly different.
Inventory menu

Under the inventory itself, there are the "Achievements" and the "Statistics" buttons.
Achievements

There are only the first three achievements: Taking inventory, Getting wood, and Benchmarking.
Only the first words of the achievements' names are capitalized (the "E" of Taking inventory isn't too).
The layering of the icon for Benchmarking is glitched.
The background of the achievements screen resembles dirt instead of stone and ores.
The green lines and the grayed out ones used to connect two achievements that aren't both complete are missing.
There in no Taken! text that usually appears when you complete an achievement, and no period at the end of Taking inventory's description.
In the top right corner there is a map of the whole achievements tree, that's bigger than usual but almost empty.
Under the map there is the description of When Pigs Fly, written like the achievements text in the top left corner.
Statistics

The dirt background is lighter.
The "Distance by Minecart", "Distance by boat", and "Distance by pig" statistics are missing.
Some of the blocks' icons are slightly different.
