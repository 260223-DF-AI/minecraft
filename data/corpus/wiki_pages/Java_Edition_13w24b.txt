13w24b is the fourteenth snapshot for Java Edition 1.6.1, released on June 14, 2013 to fix some of the bugs and crashes in the 13w24a snapshot.


== Additions ==


=== Gameplay ===
Status effects

Added Absorption effect.
Replaces Health Boost effect in golden apples.
Also added to effects of enchanted golden apples.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
 Carpet 

Can now be placed on top of any block.
This includes blocks such as water source blocks, saplings, torches, chests, other carpets, etc.
This is part of the fix for being unable to place carpet on iron bars.


=== Commands ===
The damage dealt by /kill is changed from 1000HP  500 to 3.41038
