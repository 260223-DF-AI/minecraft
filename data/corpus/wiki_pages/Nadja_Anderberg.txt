Nadja Anderberg is a Community Manager at Mojang Studios since March 2026. She began in August 2024 as a Content Moderator for the Java Realms Content Creator Program, with an 18-month contract that ended in February 2026.
During her time as Content Moderator, she is credited for several improvements to the program, such as coming up with the idea of presenting the monthly program updates through an ingame server.
She is also a Twitch streamer and has participated in multiple hardcore SMP servers, most notably Challengers SMP and HomeSMP.
