13w36b is the second snapshot for Java Edition 1.7.2, released on September 6, 2013 to fix some of the bugs and crashes in the 13w36a snapshot.
13w36b was reuploaded twice on September 6, 2013. The first time was to fix issues relating to the inventory key
and the second was to roll back control changes.[more information needed].


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Statistics

Added mob statistics.


== Changes ==


=== Items ===
Armor, bows, swords

Can now get the Unbreaking enchantment from enchantment tables.


=== General ===
Options

Pressing the "Super Secret Settings" button plays a random sound with a deeper pitch.
