The Yearn Cape is a cape that was given out as a promotion for A Minecraft Movie, alongside the Home Cape and the Menace Cape.


== Appearance ==
The cape's design depicts the mine entrance as shown in A Minecraft Movie, with rails leading into the mine. The backside of the cape depicts a view into the outside world from within the mine.
The elytra design depicts the movie's version of Steve, as portrayed by Jack Black.


== Distribution ==

The Yearn cape is obtained by completing all 6 quests in the A Minecraft Movie Live Event, an event server hosted from March 25, 2025 to April 14, 2025 on Bedrock Edition.

== Trivia ==
"Idaho" is a codename used for Marketplace items related to A Minecraft Movie.
The name and design of the cape is a reference to a quote from the film, wherein Steve recounts his origin story of "yearning for the mines", which quickly turned into a meme before the movie had even been released.
This was itself a reference to an internet meme that jokingly suggested Minecraft's popularity among children indicated their desire to partake in child labor.
