v0.15.1 alpha is a minor update to Pocket Edition, Gear VR Edition, and the Windows 10 Edition Beta released on June 26-27, 2016 to fix some of the bugs and crashes in v0.15.0.


== Fixes ==
18 issues fixed
From released versions before 0.15.0

MCPE-7414 – The player cannot cook acacia or dark oak logs in a furnace to get charcoal.
MCPE-8142 – "Stitching" on block edges: graphical glitch.
MCPE-8877 – Custom skins not working on Amazon Fire TV.
MCPE-10858 – Reloading a world while in or ungrounded above lava will place the player at the next available platform.
MCPE-11833 – Top snow cannot be seen in hand.
MCPE-12061 – Cannot join external server over ethernet on Amazon FireTV.
MCPE-12540 – Hopper texture bug.
MCPE-13325 – Dyed leather armor in a dispenser changes to regular leather armor when the dispenser is broken.
MCPE-14279 – Hotbar shifts to the right by very little when inventory is opened.
MCPE-14303 – No damage taken when standing on a cactus.
From the 0.15.0 builds

MCPE-14544 – When a player is next to a button, said player suffocates.
MCPE-15266 – Player spawns in leaves after world creation.
From the 0.15.0 release

MCPE-15638 – Missing audio if Realm is joined before a local world.
MCPE-15724 – Pig inventory GUI closes within a half a second after opening it.
MCPE-15776 – Client player spawns in leaves on multiplayer LAN.

MCPE-15527 – Player cannot leave the bed bug
