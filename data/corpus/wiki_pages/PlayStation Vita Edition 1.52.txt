1.52  is a version of PlayStation Vita Edition released on June 27, 2017.  
[edit]


== Additions ==


=== Blocks and items ===
Observers, totem of undying, explorer maps.
Spawn eggs to the Creative menu for skeleton horses, zombie horses, elder guardians, strays, wither skeletons, husks, and zombie villagers.
15 new bed colors which are now dyeable.


=== Mobs ===
Parrots, llamas, vindicators, evokers, vexes, cartographer villagers, and illusioners.


=== World generation ===
Woodland mansions


=== Gameplay ===
Curses of Binding and Vanishing.
Added display of status effects on the HUD.


=== General ===
Mini Game Heroes Skin Pack
Giants Glide track pack
7 new achievements
Added Biome Scale Slider, Find Balanced Seed, and Auto-Jump options
Added entity cramming
New dye crafting tab
Added unique sounds for when the player takes fire or drowning damage.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks and items ===
Armor

Is now once again duplicated when equipped using the quick equip functionality in Creative mode to match Java Edition (unless it is swapped with another piece).
Armor stands

Can now be equipped with elytra.
Banners

Rearranged the order of colors in the Creative inventory.
Beds

If the player clicks on the bed, but is not right beside it, it now shows the message: "You may not rest now, the bed is too far away".
Previously the player would get no message, but would be unable to sleep.
Now partially reduce fall damage.
The red colored bed has now been renamed from just "Bed" to "Red Bed".
Cobblestone walls and mossy cobblestone walls

Moved to the Decorations tab in the Creative inventory.
Durability bar

The colors of the durability bar, which is displayed on damaged items in the hotbar and inventories, have been changed.
The color is now bright during the entire length of the bar, when previously it would fade through a set of not so bright oranges and yellows, it will now fade through clear yellow and orange.
Also the background of the durability bar is now completely black, where it used to be transparent.
End portal blocks

The starfield effect was changed to match that of the End gateway block. It no longer changes perspective with respect to the player, but now remains static with respect to the game screen.
Farmland

Pick blocking it now gives farmland instead of dirt.
Flower pots

Can now remove the potted plant from the pot, by pressing use.
Iron bars, glass panes, and fences 

Will now visually connect when adjacent to the full-block side of a stair.
Iron trapdoors 

No longer have a right-click action - previously they would result in the hand swinging, and would override other valid right click actions such as snowball throwing.
Magma blocks

Are now able to burn infinitely, like netherrack.
Rails

All sloped rail types' hitbox heights are decreased from a full block to a half block.
Snow layers

Can now be placed on top of upside-down stairs.
Spawn eggs

Updated textures for mule and donkey spawn eggs.
Torches 

Can now be placed on the full-block side of stairs.


=== Mobs ===
Creepers

If a creeper is inflicted with a potion effect, its explosion causes a lingering effect of the potion.
Elder guardians 

Now drop wet sponges again instead of dry sponges.
Now drop cooked fish instead of raw fish if killed while on fire.
Ender dragon and wither

Increased spawn limit from 1 to 5.
Endermen and blazes 

Now take 1HP damage when hit by a splash water bottle.
Guardians

Now drop cooked fish instead of raw fish if killed while on fire.
Strays 

Now have their own sounds instead of having the same sounds as regular skeletons.
Villagers

Nitwits are now able to spawn.


=== Non-mob entities ===
Painting

Now tries to place the largest possible in the available space it has.


=== World generation ===
Improved spawn chances of ocean monuments.


=== Gameplay ===
Reduced the amount of hunger drain that many actions cause, but increased the drain caused by health regeneration.
Players walking backwards now use the same animation as moving forward instead of rotating the model to the side as if they are going sideways.
Multiple bossbars can now show without overlapping.
Bossbars now have a smooth animation when filling up or emptying.
Players can now be pushed by mobs.
Closing the crafting, enchanting table, or anvil interface with items inside no longer drops the items in front of the player, but sends them instead to the inventory.
Sneaking now prevents drops from heights of more than 0.6 blocks; previously, it was 1 full block or greater.
Added a tooltip for pick block.
Mending and Infinity are now mutually exclusive.
