1.21.03 (Windows) or 1.21.03.0 (iOS), the first release of the Cloud Update, is a major update to Minecraft Education, released on July 23, 2024. It adds the ability to backup worlds to OneDrive, as well as introducing new features from the Bats and Pots, Armored Paws, and Tricky Trials updates.


== Additions ==

Cloud World Backup - Upload

Worlds are automatically uploaded to OneDrive upon world close as a backup.
Future preview updates will add the ability to download and open worlds from OneDrive.
IT Admins will need to ensure that the following URLs are allowlisted through the network:
graph.microsoft.com
*.sharepoint.com
"Recipe Unlocking" and Recipe Book search improvements when playing in Survival mode.


== Changes ==


=== Items ===
General

Chemistry items like balloons, compounds, and glow sticks are now available in the Creative inventory.


=== General ===
The "Manage Account" button has been moved to the bottom right of the home screen.


== Fixes ==
Improvements to performance and game stability.
