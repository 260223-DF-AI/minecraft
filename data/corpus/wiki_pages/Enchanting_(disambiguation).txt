Enchanting is the process of applying additonal abilities or properties called enchantments to items.


== Minecraft ==
Enchantments
The outcome of enchanting.


=== Blocks ===
Enchanting Table  A block used to enchant unenchanted items; contains a general overview of enchanting.
Enchanting table mechanics  A technical overview of enchanting.
Anvil  A block that can be used to apply enchantments from enchanted books or other enchanted items.
Anvil mechanics  A technical overview of anvils.


=== Items ===
Enchanted Book  An item that stores enchantments; the enchantments can be applied to an item through an anvil.
Bottle o' Enchanting  An item that can give the player experience points for enchanting.


=== Commands ===
/enchant  A command that can apply enchantments to the selected item in a player's hotbar.
