Gunpowder is an item used for explosion-related recipes, and as an ingredient in potions to create splash potions.


== Obtaining ==


=== Mob loot ===
Java Edition:Bedrock Edition:In Bedrock Edition, wandering traders have a chance of 8.5% (increased by 1% per level of Looting) to drop 4 gunpowder when killed while showing the item as trade offer, if the player holds an emerald.


=== Generated loot ===


=== Trading ===
Wandering traders can occasionally sell 4 gunpowder for 1 emerald.
