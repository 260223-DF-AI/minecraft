A version of Minecraft Infdev was released on April 20, 2010, which changes world generation and fixes bugs.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Sapling

Saplings now grow into big trees.


=== Mobs ===
General

Mobs now spawn less often.
Mob pathfinding and behavior changed.


=== World generation ===
General

The level generator has been updated.
It is now much faster.
The terrain seems to be much less mountainous. Instead, more hills and plains generate and cliffs appear less often.
Due to this change, the Far Lands are now different, with thinner tunnels and sharper walls.


=== General ===
Auto-save of worlds happens more often.
Changed world save format.


== Removals ==


=== General ===
Debug screen

Removed slash from "All" value.
"Counted" value has been removed.


== Fixes ==
2 bugs fixed

Hostile mobs no longer spawn in bright areas.
Fixed lag spikes happening randomly due to high chunk updates.


== Bugs ==
2 bugs reported

While breaking multiple blocks, it is possible to open the inventory. Upon closing it, the player's hand or tool will remain stuck and will not animate until the breaking stops.
When breaking a heap of gravel stacked on top of another from the bottom gravel block, part of the top gravel's texture might get stuck, causing a visual glitch.[more information needed]
The glitch is stopped when a block is placed on the bugged gravel.
The player can look under the world through the glitched gravel block.
It seems that only the third gravel block (from the bottom) on a gravel stack is affected by this bug.
