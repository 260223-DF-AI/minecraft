A mud brick wall is a decorative wall variant of mud bricks that generates in trail ruins and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Mud brick walls can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, they drop nothing.


=== Natural Generation ===
Mud brick walls generate as a part of trail ruins.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Mud brick walls can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.
