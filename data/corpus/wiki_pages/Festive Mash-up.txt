Festive is an official Mash-up pack made by 4J Studios released during Christmas 2014 and later updated and re-released in 2016. It includes 44 skins on Legacy Console Edition, 51 skins on Bedrock Edition, and 46 skins on New Nintendo 3DS Edition.
