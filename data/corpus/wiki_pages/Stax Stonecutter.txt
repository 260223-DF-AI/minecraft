Stax Stonecutter is a character from the novel Minecraft: The Voyage, written by Jason Fry. Stax and his cats were living peaceful life in a house by the sea on the Stonecutter peninsula until his home was destroyed and he was marooned by pirates in the Sea of Sorrows.


== Appearance ==
Stax's appearance wasn't directly described in the novel, but there were a few pages that held some small details hinting on Stax' appearance.
Stax is described as a young man, having a long beard and scruffy clothing. It was mentioned that Stax cut his beard, implying that he has short hair currently.


== Personality ==
Stax Stonecutter is a brave man who was forced to adapt to his difficult situation after being marooned by pirates. Initially, Stax was typical loner who didn't really care about adventures or pretty much anything besides cats and flowers. But, after his adventures, he gained more bravery and a sense of adventure, leading to him deciding to leave Stonecutter peninsula and go on an adventure around the overworld in the end.
