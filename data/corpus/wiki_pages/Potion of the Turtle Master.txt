== Issues ==
Issues relating to "Potion of the Turtle Master", "Splash Potion of the Turtle Master", or "Lingering Potion of the Turtle Master" are maintained on the bug tracker. Issues should be reported and viewed there.


== Trivia ==
The lingering potion of the Turtle Master has the longest item name in Java Edition, at 37 characters long with spaces, or 32 without. It's also composed of 6 words, more than any other item in the game other than items and books named through an anvil.
The longest item names in Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Education are "Hardened Light Blue Stained Glass Pane" and "Hardened Light Gray Stained Glass Pane", both having 38 characters including spaces and 33 without. However, these items are only available when enabling the "Minecraft Education features" cheat setting when outside of Minecraft Education.
