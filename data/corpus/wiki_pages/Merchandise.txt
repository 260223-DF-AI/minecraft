This page lists the various official Minecraft merchandise release by or in collaboration with Mojang Studios.


== Official Minecraft Shop ==

The Official Minecraft Shop features various clothing, plush toys, and other accessories themed around Minecraft.


== LEGO ==

In December 2011, Mojang Studios submitted the concept of Minecraft merchandise to LEGO for the LEGO Ideas (then known as LEGO Cuusoo) program, from which it quickly received 10,000 votes by users, prompting LEGO to review the concept. On January 24, 2012, LEGO Ideas announced the concept was approved and they would develop sets based around Minecraft. In February 2012, the first LEGO Minecraft set of Micro World was showcased and made available for pre-order, with a release set for mid-2012. Since then, various LEGO Minecraft sets have gradually released continuously.


== Books ==

The Official Minecraft Books are a series of guidebooks for Minecraft, written for new and inexperienced players.


== Minecraft: Creeper Crunch ==

Minecraft: Creeper Crunch is a breakfast cereal that is produced by Kellogg's in the United States, consisting of pulverized oats flavored with cinnamon and "Creeper Bit" marshmallows melded into cubes.


== Action figures ==

Mattel manufactured a series of Minecraft action figures.


== Hot Wheels ==

From 2017 to 2022, the toy brand Hot Wheels released a special line of cars based on Minecraft mobs as part of their Character Cars series.


== Higround ==

On May 29, 2024, as part of the 15th anniversary event, various keyboards and other accessories were released in collaboration with Higround.


== Youtooz ==

Two series of vinyl figures based on Minecraft mobs were manufactured and released by Youtooz.


== The Woobles ==

Six crochet kits and four bundles based on Minecraft mobs were released by The Woobles on June 5, 2024.
