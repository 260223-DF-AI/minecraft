1.17.2 is a hotfix to Bedrock Edition released on June 22, 2021, which fixes bugs related to world generation.


== Fixes ==
2 issues fixed
From 1.17.0

MCPE-127555  Diamond ores are very rare.
MCPE-130185  Andesite and diorite are excessively generated throughout the world.
