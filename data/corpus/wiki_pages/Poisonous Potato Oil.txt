Poisonous potato oil is a joke item added in 24w14potato.


== Obtaining ==


=== Refinement ===
Refining a poisonous potato in a potato refinery with a glass bottle in the bottle slot outputs 1 poisonous potato oil. This takes 125 game ticks (6.25 seconds) and grants 0.1XP.


== Usage ==
To drink poisonous potato oil, press and hold use while it is selected in the hotbar. Drinking it gives the player It's very slippery effect for 1 tick and Poison for 10 seconds, and returns a glass bottle.
Poisonous potato oil can not be used in the potato refinery to lubricate items like the non-poisonous oil variant.
