v0.3.0 alpha is a major update to Minecraft: Pocket Edition that was released on April 23, 2012 and April 24, 2012. It adds notable features, such as crafting, new mobs, blocks, items, and tools as well as other changes and fixes.
This version was not playable, as the pre-reupload had a bug where it caused a crash upon launch. This was fixed in the first reupload. It was later reuploaded again that day to add more inventory space and a smaller GUI as well fixing an icon bug.

=== Gameplay ===
New sound effects
New inventory
Crafting

How the player makes blocks and items.
Crafting menu can be accessed by either tapping the "Craft" button on the upper-left of the inventory, or by tapping on a crafting table.
Version exclusive: uses the Minecraft Advanced Touch Technology Interface System (MATTIS) crafting system.
Durability

Most tools, excluding shears, now have a limited number of uses based on their tier.
Wooden tools have 11 uses, and stone tools have 132 uses.
For most tools, breaking any block counts as 1 use.
For swords, this counts as 2 uses.
Hitting a mob currently counts as 0 uses.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
 Coal ore

Now drops coal when mined with any pickaxe.
 Iron ore

Now drops itself when mined with a stone pickaxe.
 Lapis lazuli ore

Now drops 48 lapis lazuli when mined with a stone pickaxe.
 Wooden slab and cobblestone slabs

Now has been obtainable in survival mode.
Can now be crafted with three wooden planks or cobblestone on crafting table.
 Snow blocks

Can now be crafted with four snowballs.
Now drop snowballs when broken.
 Yellow Wool

Now has been obtainable in survival mode.
Can now be crafted with 1 any dyed wool and 1 dandelion yellow.
 Blue Wool

Now has been obtainable in survival mode.
Can now be crafted with 1 any dyed wool and 1 lapis lazuli.
 Snow layers

Now drop snowballs when destroyed with a shovel.
 Sugar cane

Now drops its item form when broken.
