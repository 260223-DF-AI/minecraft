The deep cold ocean is a deep variant of cold oceans.


== Description ==

The deep cold ocean generates in deeper waters. Being twice as deep, ocean monuments can generate, and there are higher amounts of tall seagrass.
With Vibrant Visuals, deep cold oceans use default environmental settings with a coolish color grading, making the biome appear colder blue. They use cold sunlight, which is slightly brighter at dusk and dawn.

== Sounds ==
When in any ocean biome, unique ocean ambience play randomly. Normal overworld tracks also play, alongside "Axolotl", "Dragon Fish" and "Shuniji", as the requirements for those are merely to be underwater rather than biome-determined.
