0.0.19a is a version of Java Edition Classic released alongside server version 1.5 on June 19, 2009, at 22:35 UTC, which added sponge and glass.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Glass

Glass is textured, but is semi-transparent.
 It does not render in the hotbar and when underwater.
 Sponge

Used to dry up a 5-block-wide area of water or lava around the sponge, 2 blocks to each side of it.
Disappears after a while.


=== Gameplay ===
 Hotbar

Visually displays available blocks.
The top texture is rotated such that the "down" (positive Y) direction of the texture is toward the top-left.
Different sides of items are shaded differently. If a fully white block existed, its top face would be fully white ( #FFFFFF), its left side  #999999 and its right side  #CCCCCC:

== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Cobblestone

Removed from the hotbar because of a lack of space.
 Lava

Texture changed from .
Now have animated, and slightly different textures.
Sand

Removed from the hotbar because of a lack of space.
 Water

Texture changed from .
Now have animated, and slightly different textures. However, water outside of the world border keeps the old texture.


=== Gameplay ===
Multiplayer

Generating a new level while on a server, or saving the server level, no longer works.


== Technical ==


=== General ===
Limited frame rate to 100 fps, however, it causes problems in later versions.


== Fixes ==
1 bug fixed

Fixed a client crash bug in chat.


== Bugs ==
7 bugs reported

Sponges sometimes do not soak up water when placed and removed in certain ways, especially when water can flow in from above.
Water cannot be seen through glass.
Glass cannot be seen through water.
Glass is invisible in darkness.
Water has Z-fighting issues.
Mobs have backwards heads.
Pressing pick-block on sand switches to glass and using it on cobblestone switches to sponge.
