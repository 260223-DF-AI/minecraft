15w43b is the thirty-fifth snapshot for Java Edition 1.9, released on October 22, 2015 to fix an issue with multiplayer from the previous snapshot and to get custom drop tables working, as well as add subtitles.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Subtitles

Display sounds for the hard of hearing/deaf.
Mobs have individualized subtitles, for example: "Creeper hissing".
"<" or ">" points in the direction the sound is coming from.


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Water

No longer shows the flowing animation when directly adjacent to glass.


=== Mobs ===
Elder guardians

Drop chances for raw fish or prismarine crystals changed again.
Now it's a 50% chance for fish, 33% for crystals, and 16.7% of nothing, with the amount increased by Looting.
Ghasts

Drops reverted back to 0–2 gunpowder and 0–1 ghast tears, incremented as usual with Looting.
Rabbits

Drop feet again as a rare drop.
Meat drop is no longer affected by Looting.
Skeletons

Drops reverted back to 0–2 arrows and 0–2 bones, incremented as usual with Looting.
Squid

Now drop 0–2 ink sacs (was 1–3), incremented as usual with Looting.
Wither skeletons

Coal drop changed to 0–2 coal, incremented as usual with Looting. This is still an increase over 15w42a.
Bone drop reverted back to 0–2 bones, incremented as usual with Looting.
Zombie pigmen

Drops reverted back to 0–1 rotten flesh and 0–1 gold nuggets, incremented as usual with Looting.


=== World generation ===
Igloos

A golden apple is now always present, and no longer disappears if the chunk is unloaded before the chest is opened.
Made the basement walls less uniform.
