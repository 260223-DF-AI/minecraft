18w11a is the twenty-eighth snapshot for Java Edition 1.13, released on March 13, 2018, which added drowned and shipwrecks.


== Additions ==


=== Blocks ===
 Coral fans

Do not generate naturally yet.
Can be placed underwater on the side of blocks.


=== Items ===
Spawn eggs

 Drowned spawn egg
Spawns drowned.


=== Mobs ===
 Drowned

Spawn in all oceans and rivers.
Zombies morph into drowned after a while if they are in the water.
Drowned can spawn with tridents, allowing players to get them in Survival.
Do not float, but can swim.
All drowned have a melee attack, and ones with tridents have a ranged attack.
Like zombies, drowned attack baby turtles and stomp on and destroy turtle eggs.


=== World generation ===
Shipwrecks

Can be found in oceans and beaches.
Contain 1–3 loot chests containing different types of loot depending on the ship.
Can generate upright, sideways, or upside down.


=== General ===
Controls

F3+C now copies the player's current location to clipboard.
