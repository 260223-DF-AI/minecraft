3.30.43 is an update to the Minecraft Launcher that was released on April 1, 2026.


== Additions ==
Added the "Cute" button.
If turned on, several animations of baby turtles, chicks, wolves and pigs will display on the "Home" and Java/Bedrock Edition page, while also producing their respective sounds if the "Toggle Sound" option is enabled.
