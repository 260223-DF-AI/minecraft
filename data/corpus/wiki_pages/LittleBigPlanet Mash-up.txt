LittleBigPlanet Mash-up (also known as Minecraft: LittleBigPlanet Edition) is a mash-up pack based on the LittleBigPlanet game series developed by Media Molecule. It was released exclusively on PlayStation 3,  PlayStation 4, and PlayStation Vita editions, as well as Bedrock Edition on PlayStation consoles. It contains a skin pack, a texture pack and a world template.


== World template ==


==== Spawn ====
The player spawns inside the pod located underground with 6 trapdoors. The world features levels from the LittleBigPlanet franchise which can be traveled to via minecart. These levels include "Skate to Victory", "Boom Town", "Sensei's Lost Castle", "The Frozen Tundra" from LittleBigPlanet, "Patients Are a Virtue" from LittleBigPlanet 2, & "Stitchem Manor" from LittleBigPlanet 3. The surface features a giant globe which can only be reached outside as it is a one-way entrance to the pod.
The Overworld landscape is based on the planet Earth.


==== Skate to Victory ====
Located at the top left, where the United Kingdom is. (X -219, Y 63, Z -165 at the start point) It appears mostly the same as its counterpart from the game. However, with the removal of one tutorial cinema, no ghosts appear inside the Castle, and one Boot at the entrance of the castle. There is a village surrounding the landscape that features arrows leading back to spawn.


==== Boom Town ====
Located at the bottom right where Mexico is. (X 239, Y 70 Z 193 at the start point) It appears mostly the same as its counterpart from the game.


==== Sensei's Lost Castle ====
Located at the middle right where Japan is. (X 329 Y 64 Z -123 at the start point) It appears mostly the same as its counterpart from the game.


==== The Frozen Tundra ====
Located at the top right. (X 119, Y 70, Z -285 at the start point) It appears mostly the same as its counterpart from the game.


==== Patients Are a Virtue ====
Located at the bottom left where South America is. (X -235 Y 102 Z 261 at the start point) It appears mostly the same as its counterpart from the game.
