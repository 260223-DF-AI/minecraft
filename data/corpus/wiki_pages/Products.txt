Products are a type of block and item in Minecraft Education and in Bedrock Edition that can be crafted using compounds.


== Products ==
Super Fertilizer, an item crafted with Ammonia and Phosphorus.
Ice Bomb, an item crafted with Sodium Acetate.
Bleach, an item crafted with Water and Sodium Hypochlorite.
Underwater Torch, a block crafted with Magnesium.
Underwater TNT, a block crafted with Sodium.
Heat Block, a block crafted with Iron, Water, and Salt.
Colored Torch, a block crafted with Chlorides.
Blue Torch, a colored torch crafted with Cerium Chloride.
Purple Torch, a colored torch crafted with Mercuric Chloride.
Red Torch, a colored torch crafted with Potassium Chloride.
Green Torch, a colored torch crafted with Tungsten Chloride.
Hardened Glass, a block crafted with Aluminum Oxide and Boron Trioxide.
Hardened Stained Glass
Hardened Glass Pane
Hardened Stained Glass Pane
Balloon, an item crafted with Latex and Helium.
Glow Stick, an item crafted with Polyethylene, Hydrogen Peroxide, and Luminol.
Sparkler, an item crafted with Chlorine and Magnesium.


=== Medicine ===

Eye Drops, a medicine brewed with Calcium.
Tonic, a medicine brewed with Bismuth.
Antidote, a medicine brewed with Silver.
Elixir, a medicine brewed with Cobalt.
