A diorite slab is a decorative slab variant of diorite that does not generate naturally and is used for building.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Stone slabs can be mined using any axe. If mined without an axe, they drop nothing.

== Usage ==


=== Note blocks ===
Diorite slabs can be placed under note blocks to produce "bass drum" sounds.


== Sounds ==
In Bedrock Edition, when a diorite slab is combined into a double slab, the block's use sound is played.
