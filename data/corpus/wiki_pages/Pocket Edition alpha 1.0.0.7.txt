Alpha 1.0.0.7 (also known as 1.0.0 build 6) is the sixth and final build version released for 1.0.0, also known as the Ender Update. This is the final alpha released in 2016.


== Fixes ==
From release versions before 1.0

MCPE-16611 – Blacksmith generate wrong loot
MCPE-16889 – Ghasts don't make sounds
MCPE-17379 – Rabbits spawning too frequently
MCPE-19124 – Hopper bug
From 1.0 development versions

MCPE-18246 – Lingering potions have no sounds
MCPE-18623 – Endermites now have 'player/hurt' sounds
MCPE-19135 – Ender charge particles
Fixed a crash that occurred on some Android devices.
