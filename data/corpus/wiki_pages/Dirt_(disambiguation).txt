Dirt may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Overworld ===
Dirt, a block that generates abundantly in the Overworld.
Grass Block, dirt with grass on top of it that spreads to other dirt.
Mycelium, a variant only found in mushroom fields that can spread to other dirt and grow mushrooms.
Coarse Dirt, a variant that grass and mycelium cannot spread to.
Podzol, a variant that does not spread and can grow mushrooms.
Dirt Path, a variant created by using a shovel on any dirt block.
Farmland, a variant created by using a hoe on most dirt blocks, which can grow crops.
Rooted Dirt, a variant that generates below azalea trees, which can grow hanging roots.
Mud, found in mangrove swamps and can be created by using a water bottle on dirt.


=== The Nether ===
Netherrack, "Dirt" in the Nether.
Nylium, "Grass Block" in the Nether.
Soul Soil, found abundantly in soul sand valley.
