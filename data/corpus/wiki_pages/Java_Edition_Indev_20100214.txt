A version of Minecraft Indev was released on February 14, 2010, at 21:43 UTC, which added 2 map themes, "Paradise" and "Woods".


== Additions ==


=== World generation ===
Seeds

Added 2 new map themes, "Paradise" and "Woods".


== Changes ==


=== Blocks ===
Dirt

Any dirt at or below sea level, when exposed to enough light, is turned into grass, upon world generation.


=== General ===
HUD

The player's hand now displays again.
However, the idle animation is shown when the player opens the inventory.
