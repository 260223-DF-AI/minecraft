The barnacle, originally known as Mob A or the "Monster of the Ocean Depths",  was a proposed mob featured in the Mob Vote during MINECON Earth 2017, alongside the great hunger, the phantom and the wildfire.
It was an ocean creature that resembled a gigantic counterpart of the real-world animal, which would have sucked down players down with its tentacle. The mob spawns in the deep ocean biome and has a large mouth that it uses to propel itself through the water and pull players down into the ocean. In a promotional video for the Mob Vote, Jeb encouraged players to vote for 'Mob A' as "the oceans currently don't have that much content, and it would make it more exciting when traveling from island to island." While the barnacle was ultimately scrapped, the concept of a water-based mechanic that pulled players down was later added with the magma blocks being given the ability to create bubble columns.
The barnacle was eliminated after the first poll, receiving only 23% of the votes.

== Trivia ==
The barnacle was originally part of Update Aquatic's feature slate before being moved to the mob vote, as the developers were unsure of how it would have been received by the community.
The barnacle is referenced in Terra Swoop Force, one of the death messages joking about the player voting for it.
