Beta 1.20.60.26 (Android) or Preview 1.20.60.26 (Windows, iOS, iPadOS, Xbox) is the seventh and final beta/Preview version for Bedrock Edition 1.20.60, released on January 18, 2024, which fixes bugs.


== Changes ==


=== Command format ===
/me, /say, /tell, and /title

Reduced max length for output message to 512 characters.
/kill

Reduced max length for reason message to 512 characters.

Others
