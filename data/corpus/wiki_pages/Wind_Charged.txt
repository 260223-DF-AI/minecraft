Wind Charged is a status effect that causes an entity to release a burst of wind upon death.


== Effect ==
Wind Charged causes any entities that have the effect to emit a burst of wind, similar to that caused by a thrown wind charge, at the center[verify for Bedrock Edition] of the entity's hitbox upon its death. Mobs affected by Wind Charged emit wind-like particles.


== Causes ==


== Immune mobs ==
Wither and ender dragon are immune to Wind Charged.
Creakings that spawn from creaking heart are unaffected by Wind charged.
