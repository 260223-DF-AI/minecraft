The first version of Indev 0.31 was released on December 23, 2009 at 00:40 UTC.
This is the first version of the Indev development cycle of Minecraft, and the first paid version of Minecraft (available at €9.95), as Notch released this version for paid users to test out new features.


== Additions ==


=== General ===
Lighting

Added dynamic lighting.
There are 9 different light levels.
Due to this, caves have now been made darker.
Blocks in the inventory and hotbar are affected by lighting - upon generating a new world they appear dark, then go back to normal brightness after a while.
Shadows appear below mobs.
Changed shading on the sides of blocks.

=== Mobs ===
 Mob

Uses a new model and texture, known as "Rana".
Can now spawn again.


=== Gameplay ===
Game modes

This version is in Creative mode.
TNT was removed from the hotbar.
Survival mode

These changes can only be found by modding as the game runs in Creative by default.
As a side effect of the entity removal, blocks no longer drop items when mined and attempting to fire arrows doesn't do anything.
The player only has one bubble of air bubble when breathing underwater, down from 10.
The player now spawns with 99 stone slabs, stone, water blocks and lava blocks.


=== User interface ===
The item forms of blocks are now lit differently and have been rotated such that the top texture now faces the opposite direction. The left and right sides of blocks are now lit the same. If a block used a solid white texture, the top would remain white, whereas the other two sides would be the same shade of grey:  #828282
The inventory sprites for water (still and flowing), lava (still and flowing), saplings, dandelions, roses, and red and brown mushrooms also changed in this version, but are excluded from the following table as they had other visual changes beyond just shading.


=== General ===
New work-in-progress level format.
Level saving/loading no longer works.
The world is now generated as a small size by default.
Grass block no longer spreads.
Leaf particles fall at the normal speed again.[verify for Java Edition]
The size of the .jar file has been changed to 1765K instead of 285K because of 4 new .md3 files.
