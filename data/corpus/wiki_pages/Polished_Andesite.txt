Polished andesite is the polished version of andesite.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Polished andesite can be mined using any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, it drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
One block of polished andesite can be found in igloo basements. Polished andesite can also be found in the "nature", "forge", and "large jail" rooms of woodland mansions and also in trial chambers surrounding the supply chests with chiseled tuff bricks.

== Usage ==
Polished andesite is currently used only for decoration. It has the same blast resistance as stone, meaning it can be used as a substitute for stone when building.
