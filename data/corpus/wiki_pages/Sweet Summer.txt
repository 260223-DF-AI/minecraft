Sweet Summer, also known as Minecraft x Justice Sweet Summer Skin Pack, is a skin pack that includes 15 skins themed around candy. It is available through a promo code obtained by purchasing a product from the "Minecraft x Justice" collection at Walmart before September 1, 2024. The promo codes were redeemable until October 26, 2024.
