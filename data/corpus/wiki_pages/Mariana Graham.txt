Mariana Graham, better known as RazzleberryFox, previously worked at Mojang Studios as Animator, Modeler and Texture Artist since October 17, 2016. She is well known for creating the DecoCraft mod, as well as contributing models and artwork to many other mods. She is a content creator for the Minecraft Marketplace.


== Biography ==
Mariana was born in Mexico City, Mexico. She announced her engagement to Thomas Guimbretière on August 10, 2016, and they were married on September 10, 2016.
