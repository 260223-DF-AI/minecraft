v0.6.1 alpha is a minor update to  Minecraft: Pocket Edition that was released on Android and Fire devices on January 31, 2013[verify for Bedrock Edition] and on iOS devices on February 2, 2013 to fix some of the bugs and crashes in v0.6.0 alpha.
This version was the base for Pi Edition.
