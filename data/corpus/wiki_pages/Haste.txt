Haste is a status effect that increases mining efficiency and attack speed.‌[Java Edition  only]


== Effect ==
Haste increases attack speed by 10% per level‌[Java Edition  only] and increases mining speed by 20% per level‌[Java Edition  only] or (1+0.2n)×1.2n times of mining speed without Haste‌[Bedrock Edition  only] (n represents the level of Haste). Negative levels decrease mining and attack speed, similar to Mining Fatigue.


== Causes ==


== Immune mobs ==
Withers and ender dragons are immune to Haste.
All mobs are unaffected by Haste as they cannot mine blocks.


== Interaction with Mining Fatigue ==
Mining Fatigue III inflicted by elder guardians prevents the player from instant mining most blocks regardless of the Haste effect, along with a huge decrease of mining speed. However, lower levels of Mining Fatigue can be overcome by Haste of level III or greater, with a resultant reduced Haste effect.


== Notes ==
Above levels 5, the player's arm does not move while mining.
Haste decreases the charging time of sweep attack.‌[Java Edition  only]
An alternative method of increasing mining speed is the Efficiency enchantment. Its effects stack with Haste.
