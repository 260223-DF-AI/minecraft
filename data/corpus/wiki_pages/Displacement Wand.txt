A displacement wand is an item introduced in 25w14craftmine. Its description reads: "Jera Isaz Berkanan".


== Obtaining ==
The displacement wand is only obtainable without commands by getting the upgrade "Enigma", but by default this upgrade is obfuscated. Killing a enderman makes this upgrade readable.


== Usage ==
It has different properties from the normal version; instead of following a curved trajectory, it travels in a straight line when fired, and when the player is teleported, they take no damage.


== Issues ==
Issues relating to "Displacement Wand" are not maintained on the bug tracker because it is an April Fools' joke, and is therefore not in the newest stable version or snapshot. Issues reported there are closed as "Invalid".
