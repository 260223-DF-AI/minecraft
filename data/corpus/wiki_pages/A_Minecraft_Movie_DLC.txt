A Minecraft Movie DLC is a Marketplace map centered around playing different minigames in Game Over World.


== Summary ==
The map opens on a start button. Once pressed, the logos for Spark Universe, Warner Bros. Pictures, Mojang Studios, and A Minecraft Movie appear one after each other. Afterwards, the camera pans to the player entering Game Over World, featuring dialogue from Garrett.
