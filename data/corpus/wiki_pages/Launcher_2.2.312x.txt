2.2.3125 (Windows), 2.2.3124 (macOS), or 2.2.3123 (Linux), is an update to the launcher released on June 10, 2021.


== Changes ==
Updated main Java Launcher page to Caves & Cliffs image.
Updated how some buttons work to reduce long localization strings causing UI problems.


== Fixes ==
MCL-17346 - News article images momentarily disappear and show their filename when clearing a search or category.
