Hannah, better known by her username Hannahxxrose, is an American Twitch streamer and YouTuber who specializes in playing Minecraft. Within the community, she is known for her activity in the Hypixel Bedwars minigame.


== In the Minecraft franchise ==
In 2023, Hannah partnered with LEGO to create several builds. In 2024, Hannah released a promotion for the then-recent release of Flippin' Figs, recommending the axolotl. To promote the collaboration with Fossil, she would be invited over to Mojang Studios.
In 2025, Hannah appeared as a guest at the green carpet premiere of A Minecraft Movie.


=== Events ===
In 2023, Hannah appeared in the Minecraft Legends Live Event as one of the two hosts, alongside Jens Bergensten as Jeb.
In 2024, she participated in the Ender Cup, a special edition of MC Championship made for the 15th anniversary of Minecraft. To promote the event, Hannah once again voiced a fictionalized version of herself in the event server MCC x Minecraft 15th Anniversary Party. She would return to participate in Colour the World the following year, and again at The Copper Crown.
In 2025, she would make another appearance at Minecraft Live – March 2026.
