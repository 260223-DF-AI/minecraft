3.11.30 is an update to the Minecraft Launcher that was released on March 25, 2025.


== Changes ==
Updated the main play section to include the Spring to Life update.


=== Additions ===
Added new Leaves option that when turned on, falling leaves will show.
Added Leaves counter on the About section that shows how many leaves the user has clicked.
Added A Minecraft Movie Live Event popup on the Bedrock Edition play section.


== Fixes ==
MCL-23559 - Launcher "Already joined the game as this user" play button bug
MCL-23924 - Non-custom scrollbar appears when collapsing and extending Quick Play selector.
