Dust may refer to:


== Minecraft ==


=== Items ===
Glowstone Dust - an item obtained from mining glowstone blocks, which can be used in brewing or to create glowstone blocks.
Redstone Dust - an item obtained from mining redstone ore, which can be placed as redstone wire, crafted into various redstone-related items or used in brewing.
Dust Particle - a type of particle emitted by blocks, usually redstone related, or displayed through commands, see about on dust section
Dust Particle Configuration - the configuration to display said particle through commands, see on dust section


=== Unimplemented ===
Bluestone Dust - an animation error mistaken for a teaser in Village & Pillage: Official Trailer

Diamond Dust - an unused item used to upgrade the power level of gear
