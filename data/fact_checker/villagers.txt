Minecraft Villagers — Fact-Check Knowledge Document

1. OVERVIEW
Villagers are passive humanoid NPCs in Minecraft that live in villages and participate in a trading system. Their behavior is governed by profession, workstations, schedules, and village mechanics.

Core functions:
- Trading with players
- Using workstations
- Sleeping and working on schedules
- Participating in village mechanics (breeding, gossip, panic)

------------------------------------------------------------

2. VILLAGER TYPES

Villagers have professions based on workstation blocks:

- None (Unemployed)
- Farmer
- Librarian
- Cleric
- Armorer
- Weaponsmith
- Toolsmith
- Fletcher
- Butcher
- Leatherworker
- Mason (Stone Mason)

Nitwit:
- Cannot trade
- Has green robe
- No profession assignment

ASCII:

        [VILLAGER]
             |
   +---------+---------+
   |                   |
[PROFESSION]       [NITWIT]
   |                   |
 TRADING            NO TRADE

------------------------------------------------------------

3. PROFESSION ASSIGNMENT SYSTEM

Villagers acquire professions based on nearby workstation blocks:

Example mapping:
- Composter → Farmer
- Lectern → Librarian
- Blast Furnace → Armorer
- Grindstone → Weaponsmith
- Smithing Table → Toolsmith
- Fletching Table → Fletcher

ASCII FLOW:

[VILLAGER]
     |
     v
+----------------------+
| Detect Workstation   |
+----------------------+
     |
     v
[Assign Profession]

Important rules:
- Must be unclaimed workstation
- Villager must be unemployed or not locked
- Profession locks after first trade

------------------------------------------------------------

4. TRADING SYSTEM

Villager trading is a barter system:

PLAYER → ITEM → VILLAGER → EMERALDS / ITEMS

ASCII:

[PLAYER]
   |
   v
+----------------+
| Trade Menu     |
+----------------+
   |
   v
[Exchange Items]

Example:
- Player gives emerald → receives enchanted book
- Villager gives item → receives emerald

------------------------------------------------------------

5. TRADE LOCKING RULE

Once a trade is used:
- It becomes locked (cannot change trades)
- Experience increases villager level
- New trades unlock over time

Villager levels:
- Novice
- Apprentice
- Journeyman
- Expert
- Master

ASCII:

Novice → Apprentice → Journeyman → Expert → Master
   |         |            |           |         |
 new trades unlock progressively

------------------------------------------------------------

6. WORKDAY SCHEDULE

Villagers follow a daily cycle:

- Morning: Go to workstation
- Day: Work and trade
- Evening: Return home
- Night: Sleep in bed

ASCII:

MORNING → WORK → EVENING → SLEEP
   ↑                         ↓
   +-------------------------+

Important:
- They must reach workstation to restock trades
- They must sleep in a claimed bed

------------------------------------------------------------

7. BREEDING SYSTEM

Villagers can breed if conditions are met:

Requirements:
- Food (bread, carrots, potatoes, beetroots)
- Available beds
- Willingness state

ASCII:

[VILLAGER] + [VILLAGER]
        |
        v
+------------------+
| Willingness OK?  |
+------------------+
        |
        v
     BABY VILLAGER

------------------------------------------------------------

8. GOSSIP SYSTEM (BEHAVIOR AI)

Villagers share “gossip” that affects trading prices:

Types:
- Trading gossip
- Negative gossip (attacks, crimes)
- Positive reputation gossip

Effect:
- Influences emerald prices
- Affects villager trust

ASCII:

[VILLAGER] → GOSSIP → [OTHER VILLAGERS]
      ↓                     ↓
  PRICE CHANGE        REPUTATION UPDATE

------------------------------------------------------------

9. PANIC AND PROTECTION BEHAVIOR

Villagers react to threats:

Triggers:
- Zombie nearby
- Raid event
- Damage sources

Response:
- Run away
- Hide in houses
- Iron Golems spawn during raids

ASCII:

[THREAT DETECTED]
        |
        v
+------------------+
| Panic State      |
+------------------+
   |         |
Run away   Hide

------------------------------------------------------------

10. ZOMBIFICATION MECHANIC

If a villager is killed by a zombie:
- It may convert into a zombie villager

Recovery:
- Splash Potion of Weakness
- Golden Apple

ASCII:

[VILLAGER] → [ZOMBIE KILL] → [ZOMBIE VILLAGER]
                                        |
                                        v
                             CURE PROCESS (Potion + Apple)

------------------------------------------------------------

11. IRON GOLEM RELATIONSHIP

Villagers can cause iron golem spawning:

Conditions:
- Enough villagers
- Enough beds
- Recent panic events

ASCII:

[VILLAGE STATE]

      v
+------------------+
| Spawn Conditions |
+------------------+
      |
      v

Role:
- Village protection
- Hostile mob defense

------------------------------------------------------------

13. KEY RULES

- Villagers require workstations to maintain profession
- Trades are deterministic once locked but cost varies
- Beds define village existence
- AI behavior is schedule-based
- Reputation affects economy significantly
