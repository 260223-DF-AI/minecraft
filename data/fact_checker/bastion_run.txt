Minecraft Bastion Speedrun Guide — Fact-Check Knowledge Document

1. OVERVIEW
A Bastion Remnant is a Nether structure containing Piglins, loot chests, gold blocks, and dangerous terrain. In speedrunning, Bastions are critical for acquiring:
- Gold
- Ender Pearls (via bartering)
- Diamond gear (rare loot)
- Obsidian or utility items (varies by loot type)

Goal of bastion speedrun strategy:
- Enter safely
- Locate treasure quickly
- Secure gold/loot
- Barter efficiently
- Exit without dying

------------------------------------------------------------

2. BASTION TYPES

There are 4 main Bastion variants:

1. Treasure Bastion
- Central treasure room
- Contains magma cube spawner
- Highest-value loot

2. Bridge Bastion
- Large open structure
- Dangerous Piglin Brute patrols

3. Hoglin Stable Bastion
- Contains Hoglins (food + threat)
- Good for food farming

4. Housing Units Bastion
- Multiple rooms
- Moderate loot spread

ASCII (generic bastion layout):

        [STRUCTURE]
     +----------------+
     |  ROOM  | ROOM  |
     |--------+--------|
     |  GOLD  | CHEST  |
     |--------+--------|
     | BRUTES | TREASURE|
     +----------------+

------------------------------------------------------------

3. ENTRY STRATEGY (FIRST 30 SECONDS)

Primary objectives on entry:
- Avoid Piglin Brutes
- Identify chest locations
- Secure safe high ground
- Do NOT open chests immediately if Piglins are nearby

ASCII ENTRY FLOW:

[ENTER BASTION]
       |
       v
+------------------+
| Immediate Scan   |
+------------------+
   |       |      |
Loot   Threat   Exit route

------------------------------------------------------------

4. PIGLIN BEHAVIOR RULES

Piglins:
- Neutral unless provoked
- Aggro if:
  - Player opens chest
  - Player breaks gold
  - Player wears non-gold armor

Piglin Brutes:
- Always hostile
- High damage
- Cannot be bribed with gold armor

ASCII:

[PLAYER]
   |
   v
+----------------------+
| GOLD ARMOR CHECK     |
+----------------------+
   |YES        |NO
   v           v
Neutral     Aggro Piglins

------------------------------------------------------------

5. LOOT PRIORITY SYSTEM

Speedrun loot priority:

1. Gold blocks
2. Treasure chest loot
3. Bartering gold
4. Obsidian / utility items

ASCII:

LOOT VALUE PRIORITY:
GOLD BLOCKS >>> CHESTS >>> BARTER >>> RANDOM ITEMS

------------------------------------------------------------

6. TREASURE CHEST STRATEGY

Steps:
1. Locate central/hidden chest
2. Clear Piglins or isolate area
3. Open chest quickly
4. Grab high-value items first

ASCII:

+------------------+
| CHEST ROOM       |
+------------------+
        |
        v
[OPEN → LOOT → EXIT]

Important:
- Opening chests triggers Piglin aggression
- Always prepare escape path first

------------------------------------------------------------

7. GOLD FARMING INSIDE BASTION

Gold blocks can be mined:
- Used for bartering
- Attract Piglin attention

Strategy:
- Mine gold quickly
- Drop gold to bait Piglins away

ASCII:

[GOLD BLOCK]
     |
     v
+------------------+
| Mine → Drop bait |
+------------------+
     |
     v
[Piglins distracted]

------------------------------------------------------------

8. BARTERING SYSTEM

Bartering with Piglins:

PLAYER gives:
- Gold ingot

Piglin returns random loot:
- Ender pearls (key speedrun item)
- Fire resistance potions
- Obsidian
- Gravel / arrows / junk

ASCII:

[PLAYER GOLD]
       |
       v
+------------------+
| PIGLIN BARTER    |
+------------------+
       |
       v
[RANDOM LOOT POOL]

Key speedrun goal:
- Maximize ender pearl output

------------------------------------------------------------

9. PIGLIN BRUTE MANAGEMENT

Brutes:
- Always hostile
- High damage
- Dangerous in tight spaces

Strategy:
- Avoid direct combat
- Use terrain separation
- Block paths or isolate

ASCII:

[BRUTE]
   |
   v
+------------------+
| DO NOT ENGAGE    |
+------------------+
   |
   v
[BLOCK / ESCAPE]

------------------------------------------------------------

10. MOVEMENT AND PATHING

Efficient movement rules:
- Stay elevated when possible
- Avoid lava floor areas
- Use blocks for bridging gaps
- Minimize vertical drops

ASCII PATH:

SAFE ROUTE:
START → HIGH GROUND → CHEST → GOLD → EXIT

------------------------------------------------------------

11. EXIT STRATEGY

Exit immediately after:
- Obtaining enough gold
- Getting pearls or key loot
- Losing control of area

ASCII:

[LOOT SECURED]
       |
       v
+------------------+
| EXIT BASTION     |
+------------------+
       |
       v
[NETHER TRANSITION]

------------------------------------------------------------

12. SPEEDRUN OPTIMIZATION RULES

- Do NOT fully clear bastion
- Do NOT fight all mobs
- Focus only on:
  - gold
  - bartering
  - treasure chest

Core principle:
SURVIVAL + VALUE EXTRACTION > FULL CLEAR

------------------------------------------------------------

13. COMMON FAILURE MODES

- Opening chest without clearing Piglins
- Fighting Brutes directly
- Falling into lava
- Losing gold control (no bartering fuel)
- Staying too long in structure
